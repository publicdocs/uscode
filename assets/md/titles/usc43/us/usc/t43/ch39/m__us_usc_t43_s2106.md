## § 2106. Relationship to other laws

    (a) __Law of salvage and law of finds__ 

        The law of salvage and the law of finds shall not apply to abandoned shipwrecks to which [section 2105 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s2105] applies.

    (b) __Laws of United States__ 

        This chapter shall not change the laws of the United States relating to shipwrecks, other than those to which this chapter applies.

    (c) __Effective date__ 

        This chapter shall not affect any legal proceeding brought prior to April 28, 1988.

([Pub. L. 100–298, § 7][/us/pl/100/298/s7], Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 434][/us/stat/102/434].)
