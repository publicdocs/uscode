## § 152. Restoration of lands previously withdrawn

    Where under the law the Secretary of the Interior is authorized or directed to make restoration of lands previously withdrawn he may also restrict the restoration as prescribed in [section 151 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s151].

([Sept. 30, 1913, ch. 15, § 2][/us/act/1913-09-30/ch15/s2], [38 Stat. 114][/us/stat/38/114].)
