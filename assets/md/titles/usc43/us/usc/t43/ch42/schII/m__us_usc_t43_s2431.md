## § 2431. Report

    Not later than 1 year after the date on which the eligibility criteria are published in the Federal Register under [section 2422(a) of this title][/us/usc/t43/s2422/a], and every 2 years thereafter, the Secretary shall submit to the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources of the Senate and the Committee on Resources of the House of Representatives a report that describes the implementation of the loan guarantee program under [section 2423 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s2423].

([Pub. L. 109–451, title II, § 212][/us/pl/109/451/s212], Dec. 22, 2006, [120 Stat. 3361][/us/stat/120/3361].)
