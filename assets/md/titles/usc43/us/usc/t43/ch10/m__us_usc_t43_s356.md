## § 356. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789]

    Section, [act Sept. 22, 1922, ch. 400][/us/act/1922-09-22/ch400], [42 Stat. 1012][/us/stat/42/1012], extended time for development of underground water supplies with reclamation grants.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789], provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579] not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579/s701], set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1701].

§§ 357 to 360. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 88–417, § 1__  __][/us/pl/88/417/s1]__  __,__  __Aug. 11, 1964__  __,__  __[__  __78 Stat. 389__  __][/us/stat/78/389]__ 

    Section 357, [act Oct. 22, 1919, ch. 77, § 6][/us/act/1919-10-22/ch77/s6], [41 Stat. 294][/us/stat/41/294], provided for disposition of land not included in patents.

    Section 358, [act Oct. 22, 1919, ch. 77, § 7][/us/act/1919-10-22/ch77/s7], [41 Stat. 295][/us/stat/41/295], provided for payment of proceeds of land sales into reclamation fund.

    Section 359, acts [Oct. 22, 1919, ch. 77, § 8][/us/act/1919-10-22/ch77/s8], [41 Stat. 295][/us/stat/41/295]; [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1][/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1], [42 Stat. 208][/us/stat/42/208]; [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1145][/us/stat/43/1145]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], provided for reservation to the United States of coal and mineral rights, and for disposition of such reserved rights.

    Section 360, [act Oct. 22, 1919, ch. 77, § 9][/us/act/1919-10-22/ch77/s9], [41 Stat. 295][/us/stat/41/295], authorized Secretary to prescribe rules and regulations.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Sections repealed subject to valid rights and obligations existing on Aug. 11, 1964, see [section 1 of Pub. L. 88–417][/us/pl/88/417/s1], set out as a note under sections 351 to 355 of this title.

 __Processing of Pending Applications__ 

    Processing of applications filed prior to Aug. 11, 1964, to be in same manner as if [Pub. L. 88–417][/us/pl/88/417] had not been enacted, see [section 2 of Pub. L. 88–417][/us/pl/88/417/s2], set out as a note under sections 351 to 355 of this title.
