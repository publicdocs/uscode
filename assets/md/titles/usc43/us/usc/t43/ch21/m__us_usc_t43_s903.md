## § 903. Relief of settlers on lands granted in aid of wagon roads

    The provision of [section 888 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s888] and all statutes amendatory thereof or supplementary thereto, including sections 894 to 899 of this title, as modified or supplemented by sections 900 to 902 of this title, shall apply to grants of land in aid of the construction of wagon roads.

([July 1, 1902, ch. 1386][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1386], [32 Stat. 733][/us/stat/32/733].)
