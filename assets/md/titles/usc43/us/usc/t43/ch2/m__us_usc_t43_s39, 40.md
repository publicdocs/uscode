## §§ 39, 40. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 39, [act Feb. 27, 1925, ch. 360, § 1][/us/act/1925-02-27/ch360/s1], [43 Stat. 1011][/us/stat/43/1011], authorized the President to complete a general utility topographical survey of the territory of the United States within a period of twenty years from Feb. 27, 1925.

    Section 40, [act Feb. 27, 1925, ch. 360, § 2][/us/act/1925-02-27/ch360/s2], [43 Stat. 1011][/us/stat/43/1011], related to cooperative agreements with States to expedite completion of topographical survey.
