§§ 1381 to 1383. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 705(a)__  __][/us/pl/94/579/s705/a]__  __,__  __Oct. 21, 1976__  __,__  __[__  __90 Stat. 2792__  __][/us/stat/90/2792]__ 

    Section 1381, [Pub. L. 86–649, title III, § 301][/us/pl/86/649/s301], July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 507][/us/stat/74/507], related to forfeiture of bond or deposit by timber purchaser or permittee.

    Section 1383, [Pub. L. 86–649, title III, § 303][/us/pl/86/649/s303], July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 508][/us/stat/74/508], related to Oregon and California Railroad and Coos Bay Wagon Road Grant lands.
