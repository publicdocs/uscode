## §§ 1361 to 1364. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 705(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s705/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792][/us/stat/90/2792]

    Section 1361, [Pub. L. 86–649, § 2][/us/pl/86/649/s2], July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 506][/us/stat/74/506], defined “public lands”.

    Section 1362, [Pub. L. 86–649, title I, § 101][/us/pl/86/649/s101], July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 506][/us/stat/74/506], authorized the Secretary of the Interior to conduct investigations, etc., for improvement, management, use, and protection of public lands and resources.

    Section 1362a, [Pub. L. 91–429][/us/pl/91/429], Sept. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 885][/us/stat/84/885], authorized contracts for use of aircraft, services, and supplies for protection from fire of public lands administered by the Secretary of the Interior.

    Section 1363, [Pub. L. 86–649, title I, § 102][/us/pl/86/649/s102], July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 506][/us/stat/74/506], authorized cooperative agreements by Secretary of the Interior with respect to improvement, etc., of public lands and resources.

    Section 1364, [Pub. L. 86–649, title I, § 103][/us/pl/86/649/s103], July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 506][/us/stat/74/506], authorized acceptance of contributions by Secretary of the Interior with respect to improvements, etc., of public lands and resources.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 705(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s705/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792][/us/stat/90/2792], provided that the repeal made by section 705(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579] not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579/s701], set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1701].
