## §§ 1100 to 1102g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789]

    Section 1100, acts [Aug. 7, 1946, ch. 772, § 1][/us/act/1946-08-07/ch772/s1], [60 Stat. 872][/us/stat/60/872]; Sept 22, 1950, ch. 983, [64 Stat. 903][/us/stat/64/903], granting of patents to certain lands south of Cimarron base line in Oklahoma and north of north line of Texas.

    Section 1101, [act Aug. 7, 1946, ch. 772, § 2][/us/act/1946-08-07/ch772/s2], [60 Stat. 872][/us/stat/60/872], related to relinquishment by United States of title to townsite plots.

    Section 1102, [act Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 498, § 1][/us/act/1955-08-03/ch498/s1], [69 Stat. 445][/us/stat/69/445], related to management and disposition of lands conveyed to United States by Choctaw Nation.

    Section 1102a, [act Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 498, § 2][/us/act/1955-08-03/ch498/s2], [69 Stat. 445][/us/stat/69/445], authorized certain powers in Secretary to facilitate administration of such lands.

    Section 1102b, [act Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 498, § 3][/us/act/1955-08-03/ch498/s3], [69 Stat. 446][/us/stat/69/446], related to sale or leasing of lands to Oklahoma or other agency.

    Section 1102c, [act Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 498, § 4][/us/act/1955-08-03/ch498/s4], [69 Stat. 446][/us/stat/69/446], related to issuance of quitclaim deeds and reservation of mineral deposits.

    Section 1102d, [act Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 498, § 5][/us/act/1955-08-03/ch498/s5], [69 Stat. 446][/us/stat/69/446], related to granting of easements, leases or permits for nonmineral resources.

    Section 1102e, [act Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 498, § 6][/us/act/1955-08-03/ch498/s6], [69 Stat. 446][/us/stat/69/446], related to acceptance of contributions, donations, etc.

    Section 1102f, [act Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 498, § 7][/us/act/1955-08-03/ch498/s7], [69 Stat. 447][/us/stat/69/447], related to issuance of regulations by Secretary of the Interior.

    Section 1102g, [act Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 498, § 8][/us/act/1955-08-03/ch498/s8], [69 Stat. 447][/us/stat/69/447], related to deposit of moneys received into Treasury.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789], provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579] not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579/s701], set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1701].
