## § 3a. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act June 5, 1942, ch. 336, § 1][/us/act/1942-06-05/ch336/s1], [56 Stat. 312][/us/stat/56/312], provided for assistant or deputy commissioners of the General Land Office and Bureau of Indian Affairs. Insofar as it related to the General Land Office it was superseded by Reorg. Plan No. 3 of 1946, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], set out as a note under [section 1 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1]. Provisions unaffected by the Plan relating to the Bureau of Indian Affairs are set out as [section 2a of Title 25][/us/usc/t25/s2a], Indians.

§§ 4, 5. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)__  __][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a]__  __,__  __Sept. 6, 1966__  __,__  __[__  __80 Stat. 632__  __][/us/stat/80/632]__  __, 641__ 

    Section 4, [act May 22, 1908, ch. 186][/us/act/1908-05-22/ch186], [35 Stat. 225][/us/stat/35/225], which provided for a temporary assistant commissioner of the General Land Office, was superseded by Reorg. Plan No. 3 of 1946, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], set out as a note under [section 1 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1].

    Section 5, R.S. § 447, provided for a recorder of the former General Land Office.
