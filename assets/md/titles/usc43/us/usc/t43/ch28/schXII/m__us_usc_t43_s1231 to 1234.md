## §§ 1231 to 1234. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–646, title II, § 220(a)(1)][/us/pl/91/646/s220/a/1], Jan. 2, 1971, [84 Stat. 1903][/us/stat/84/1903]

    Section 1231, [Pub. L. 85–433, § 1][/us/pl/85/433/s1], May 29, 1958, [72 Stat. 152][/us/stat/72/152], related to payment of moving expenses to owners and tenants of land acquired for developments and to applications for payments.

    Section 1232, [Pub. L. 85–433, § 2][/us/pl/85/433/s2], May 29, 1958, [72 Stat. 152][/us/stat/72/152], related to administration and rules and regulations.

    Section 1233, [Pub. L. 85–433, § 3][/us/pl/85/433/s3], May 29, 1958, [72 Stat. 152][/us/stat/72/152], related to definitions.

    Section 1234, [Pub. L. 85–433, § 4][/us/pl/85/433/s4], May 29, 1958, [72 Stat. 152][/us/stat/72/152], related to availability of appropriations. See section 4601 et seq. of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 2, 1971, see [section 221 of Pub. L. 91–646][/us/pl/91/646/s221], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 4601 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s4601], The Public Health and Welfare.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Any rights or liabilities existing under provisions repealed by [section 220(a) of Pub. L. 91–646][/us/pl/91/646/s220/a] as not affected by such repeal, see [section 220(b) of Pub. L. 91–646][/us/pl/91/646/s220/b], set out as a note under [section 4621 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s4621], The Public Health and Welfare.
