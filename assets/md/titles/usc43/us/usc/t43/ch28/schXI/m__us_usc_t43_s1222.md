## § 1222. Notice of opening of lands to purchase

    Upon the filing of a plat of resurvey under [section 1221 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1221] the Secretary shall give such notice as he finds appropriate by newspaper publication or otherwise of the opening of the lands to purchase under this subchapter.

([Aug. 24, 1954, ch. 900, § 2][/us/act/1954-08-24/ch900/s2], [68 Stat. 790][/us/stat/68/790].)
