## § 1197. Agreements with Indians not affected

    Nothing in this act shall change, repeal, or modify any agreements or treaties made with any Indian tribes for the disposal of their lands, or of land ceded to the United States to be disposed of for the benefit of such tribes, and the proceeds thereof to be placed in the Treasury of the United States; and the disposition of such lands shall continue in accordance with the provisions of such treaties or agreements; except as provided in sections 161 and 162  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title.

([Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 561, § 10][/us/act/1891-03-03/ch561/s10], [26 Stat. 1099][/us/stat/26/1099].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This act, referred to in text, means [act Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 561][/us/act/1891-03-03/ch561], [26 Stat. 1095][/us/stat/26/1095], as amended, which enacted sections 161, 162, 173, 174, 185, 202, 212, 321, 323, 325, 327 to 329, 663, 671, 687a–6, 718, 728, 732, 893, 946 to 949, 989, 1165, 1166, 1181, and 1197 of this title, sections 471, 607, 611, 611a, and 613 of Title 16, Conservation, [section 495 of Title 25][/us/usc/t25/s495], Indians, and sections 30, 36, 44, 45, 48, and 52 of Title 30, Mineral Lands and Mining. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

    Sections 161 and 162 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 702][/us/pl/94/579/s702], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2787][/us/stat/90/2787].

 __Codification__ 

    Section was not enacted as part of [act July 6, 1954, ch. 463][/us/act/1954-07-06/ch463], [68 Stat. 452][/us/stat/68/452], which comprises this subchapter.
