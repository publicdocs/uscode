## §§ 1151 to 1156. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 705(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s705/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792][/us/stat/90/2792]

    Section 1151, R.S. § 2447; [act Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1144][/us/stat/43/1144]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], related to issuance of patents for confirmed claims.

    Section 1152, R.S. § 2448, related to effect of patents to persons dead before issue.

    Section 1153, [act June 6, 1874, ch. 223, § 1][/us/act/1874-06-06/ch223/s1], [18 Stat. 62][/us/stat/18/62], related to confirming titles to lands in Missouri in existence prior to June 6, 1874.

    Section 1154, [act June 6, 1874, ch. 223, § 2][/us/act/1874-06-06/ch223/s2], [18 Stat. 62][/us/stat/18/62], related to unimpairing, etc., rights in existence prior to June 6, 1874.

    Section 1155, [act Jan. 28, 1879, ch. 30][/us/act/1879-01-28/ch30], §§ 1–4, [20 Stat. 274][/us/stat/20/274], 275; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], authorized issuance of certificates of location of private land claims for certain States.

    Section 1156, [act May 30, 1894, ch. 87][/us/act/1894-05-30/ch87], [28 Stat. 84][/us/stat/28/84]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], authorized issuance of patents for locations under certificates made prior to Jan. 28, 1879, under former [section 1155 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1155].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 705(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s705/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792][/us/stat/90/2792], provided that the repeal made by section 705(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579] not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579/s701], set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1701].
