## §§ 678 to 682. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789]

    Section 678, R.S. § 2357, related to acreage price for public lands offered for sale.

    Section 679, [act June 15, 1880, ch. 227, § 3][/us/act/1880-06-15/ch227/s3], [21 Stat. 238][/us/stat/21/238], related to acreage price for alternate sections of railroad lands offered for sale.

    Section 680, [act June 15, 1880, ch. 227, § 4][/us/act/1880-06-15/ch227/s4], [21 Stat. 238][/us/stat/21/238], excepted former [section 679 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s679] from applicability to mineral lands of the United States.

    Section 681, [act Mar. 2, 1889, ch. 381, § 4][/us/act/1889-03-02/ch381/s4], [25 Stat. 854][/us/stat/25/854], related to price of forfeited railroad lands and adjacent lands.

    Section 682, [act Mar. 1, 1907, ch. 2286][/us/act/1907-03-01/ch2286], [34 Stat. 1052][/us/stat/34/1052], authorized sale of public lands for cemetery purposes and set forth the price for such acreage.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789], provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579] not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579/s701], set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1701].
