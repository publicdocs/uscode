## § 1339. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–185, § 8(b)][/us/pl/104/185/s8/b], Aug. 13, 1996, [110 Stat. 1717][/us/stat/110/1717]

    Section, [act Aug. 7, 1953, ch. 345, § 10][/us/act/1953-08-07/ch345/s10], [67 Stat. 469][/us/stat/67/469], related to requirements for refund of excess payments.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 104–185, § 8(b)][/us/pl/104/185/s8/b], Aug. 13, 1996, [110 Stat. 1717][/us/stat/110/1717], provided in part that the repeal of this section is effective Aug. 13, 1996.

 __Applicability of Repeal__ 

    Repeal of section not applicable to any privately owned minerals or with respect to Indian lands, see sections 9 and 10 of [Pub. L. 104–185][/us/pl/104/185], set out as an Applicability of 1996 Amendment note under [section 1701 of Title 30][/us/usc/t30/s1701], Mineral Lands and Mining.
