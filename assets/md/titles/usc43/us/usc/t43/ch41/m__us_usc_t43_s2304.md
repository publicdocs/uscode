## § 2304. Disposal of public land

    (a) __In general__ 

        The Secretary shall establish a program, using funds made available under [section 2305 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s2305], to complete appraisals and satisfy other legal requirements for the sale or exchange of public land identified for disposal under approved land use plans (as in effect on July 25, 2000) under [section 1712 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1712].

    (b) __Sale of public land__ 

        (1) __In general__ 

            The sale of public land so identified shall be conducted in accordance with sections 1713 and 1719 of this title.

        (2) __Exceptions to competitive bidding requirements__ 

            The exceptions to competitive bidding requirements under [section 1713(f) of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1713/f] shall apply to this section in cases in which the Secretary determines it to be necessary.

    (c) __Report in Public Land Statistics__ 

        The Secretary shall provide in the annual publication of Public Land Statistics, a report of activities under this section.

    (d) __Termination of authority__ 

        The authority provided under this section shall terminate 11 years after July 25, 2000.

([Pub. L. 106–248, title II, § 205][/us/pl/106/248/s205], July 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 615][/us/stat/114/615]; [Pub. L. 111–212, title III, § 3007(a)][/us/pl/111/212/s3007/a], July 29, 2010, [124 Stat. 2339][/us/stat/124/2339].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2010—Subsec. (d). [Pub. L. 111–212][/us/pl/111/212] substituted “11 years” for “10 years”.
