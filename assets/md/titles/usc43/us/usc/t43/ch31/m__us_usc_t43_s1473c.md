## § 1473c. Payment of costs incidental to services contributed by volunteers

    Appropriations under this title  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  in fiscal year 1992 and thereafter, may be made available for paying costs incidental to the utilization of services contributed by individuals who serve without compensation as volunteers in aid of work for units of the Department of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 102–154, title I, § 116][/us/pl/102/154/s116], Nov. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 1012][/us/stat/105/1012].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This title, referred to in text, is title I of [Pub. L. 102–154][/us/pl/102/154], Nov. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 990][/us/stat/105/990], known as the Department of the Interior and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 1992. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in the following prior appropriation act:

    [Pub. L. 101–512, title I, § 118][/us/pl/101/512/s118], Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1937][/us/stat/104/1937].
