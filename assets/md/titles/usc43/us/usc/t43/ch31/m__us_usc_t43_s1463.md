## § 1463. Disposition of receipts

    All sums received under the provisions of [section 1460 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1460] shall be deposited in the Treasury to the credit of miscellaneous receipts.

([Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 370, § 6][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch370/s6], [37 Stat. 498][/us/stat/37/498].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 492 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s492] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1][/us/pl/89/554/s1], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
