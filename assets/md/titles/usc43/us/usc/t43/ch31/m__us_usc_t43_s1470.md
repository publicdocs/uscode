## § 1470. Appropriations; availability for certain administrative expenses

    Appropriations for field work of the Department of the Interior shall be available for the hire, with or without personal services, of boats, work animals, and animal-drawn and motor-propelled vehicles and equipment.

([June 25, 1946, ch. 472, § 1][/us/act/1946-06-25/ch472/s1], [60 Stat. 306][/us/stat/60/306].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 692 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s692] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 31, Money and Finance, by [Pub. L. 97–258, § 1][/us/pl/97/258/s1], Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 877][/us/stat/96/877].
