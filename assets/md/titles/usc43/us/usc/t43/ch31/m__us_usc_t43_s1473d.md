## § 1473d. Insurance costs covering vehicles, aircraft, and boats operated by Department of the Interior in Canada and Mexico

    Notwithstanding any other provisions of law, in fiscal year 1992 and thereafter, appropriations in this title  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  shall be available to provide insurance on official motor vehicles, aircraft, and boats operated by the Department of the Interior in Canada and Mexico.

([Pub. L. 102–154, title I, § 107][/us/pl/102/154/s107], Nov. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 1012][/us/stat/105/1012].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This title, referred to in text, is title I of [Pub. L. 102–154][/us/pl/102/154], Nov. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 990][/us/stat/105/990], known as the Department of the Interior and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 1992. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.
