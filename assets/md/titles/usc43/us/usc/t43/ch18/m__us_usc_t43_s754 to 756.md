## §§ 754 to 756. Repealed. [Dec. 16, 1930, ch. 14, § 1][/us/act/1930-12-16/ch14/s1], [46 Stat. 1029][/us/stat/46/1029]

    Section 754, R.S. § 2398, related to binding effect of contracts for surveys.

    Section 755, R.S. § 2399; acts [Oct. 1, 1890, ch. 1262][/us/act/1890-10-01/ch1262], [26 Stat. 650][/us/stat/26/650]; [Aug. 15, 1894, ch. 288][/us/act/1894-08-15/ch288], [28 Stat. 285][/us/stat/28/285]; [Apr. 26, 1902, ch. 592][/us/act/1902-04-26/ch592], [32 Stat. 120][/us/stat/32/120]; [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1144][/us/stat/43/1144], related to various surveying instructions which were to be deemed part of every contract for survey.

    Section 756, R.S. § 2400; [act Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 130, § 1][/us/act/1875-03-03/ch130/s1], [18 Stat. 384][/us/stat/18/384], related to establishment of prices of surveys.
