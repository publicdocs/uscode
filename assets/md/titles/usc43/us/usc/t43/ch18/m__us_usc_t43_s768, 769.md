## §§ 768, 769. Repealed. [Apr. 29, 1950, ch. 134, § 4][/us/act/1950-04-29/ch134/s4], [64 Stat. 93][/us/stat/64/93]

    Section 768, R.S. § 2408, authorized Secretary of the Interior to vary lines of subdivisions from a rectangular form to suit the circumstances of the country in extending the surveys of public lands in Nevada. See [section 770 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s770].

    Section 769, R.S. § 2409, authorized Secretary of the Interior to continue geodetic method of survey in Oregon and California.
