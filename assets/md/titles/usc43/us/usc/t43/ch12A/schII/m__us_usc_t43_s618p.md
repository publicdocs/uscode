## § 618p. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act Oct. 12, 1949, ch. 680][/us/act/1949-10-12/ch680], title I, § 101, in part, [63 Stat. 784][/us/stat/63/784], related to reports to Congressional appropriations committees on Colorado River dam funds, was from the Interior Department Appropriation Act, 1950, and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts. Similar provisions were contained in [act June 29, 1948, ch. 754, § 1][/us/act/1948-06-29/ch754/s1], [62 Stat. 1130][/us/stat/62/1130].
