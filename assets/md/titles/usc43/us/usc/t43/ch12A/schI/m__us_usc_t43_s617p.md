## § 617p. Claims of United States; priority

    Except as provided in title 11, claims of the United States arising out of any contract authorized by this subchapter shall have priority over all others, secured or unsecured.

([Dec. 21, 1928, ch. 42, § 17][/us/act/1928-12-21/ch42/s17], [45 Stat. 1065][/us/stat/45/1065]; [Pub. L. 95–598, title III, § 332][/us/pl/95/598/s332], Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2679][/us/stat/92/2679].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1978—[Pub. L. 95–598][/us/pl/95/598] inserted introductory phrase “Except as provided in title 11”.

 __Effective Date of 1978 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 95–598][/us/pl/95/598] effective Oct. 1, 1979, see [section 402(a) of Pub. L. 95–598][/us/pl/95/598/s402/a], set out as an Effective Dates note preceding [section 101 of Title 11][/us/usc/t11/s101], Bankruptcy.
