## §§ 270–13 to 270–17. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789]

    Section 270–13, [act Mar. 8, 1922, ch. 96, § 3][/us/act/1922-03-08/ch96/s3], as added Aug. 17, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–147][/us/pl/87/147], [75 Stat. 384][/us/stat/75/384]; amended Oct. 3, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–742][/us/pl/87/742], [76 Stat. 740][/us/stat/76/740], authorized sale or other disposition of Alaskan lands containing coal, oil, or gas deposits. Section was formerly classified to [section 377a of Title 48][/us/usc/t48/s377a], Territories and Insular Possessions.

    Section 270–14, [act July 8, 1916, ch. 228, § 3][/us/act/1916-07-08/ch228/s3], formerly § 2, [39 Stat. 352][/us/stat/39/352], renumbered [June 28, 1918, ch. 110][/us/act/1918-06-28/ch110], [40 Stat. 633][/us/stat/40/633], excepted certain lands in Alaska from homestead entry and settlement. Section was formerly classified to [section 378 of Title 48][/us/usc/t48/s378].

    Section 270–15, acts [Apr. 13, 1926, ch. 121, § 1][/us/act/1926-04-13/ch121/s1], [44 Stat. 243][/us/stat/44/243]; [Apr. 29, 1950, ch. 134, § 3][/us/act/1950-04-29/ch134/s3], [64 Stat. 93][/us/stat/64/93], related to claims and rectangular system of surveys and departure for local or topographic conditions.

    Section 270–16, acts [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1][/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1], [42 Stat. 208][/us/stat/42/208]; [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1145][/us/stat/43/1145]; [Apr. 13, 1926, ch. 121, § 2][/us/act/1926-04-13/ch121/s2], [44 Stat. 244][/us/stat/44/244]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], related to additional entries by soldiers.

    Section 270–17, [act Apr. 13, 1926, ch. 121, § 3][/us/act/1926-04-13/ch121/s3], [44 Stat. 244][/us/stat/44/244], related to disposition of deposit of estimated cost of work incident to survey, and promulgation of rules and regulations.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789], provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after tenth anniversary of date of approval of this Act, Oct. 21, 1976.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579] not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579/s701], set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1701].
