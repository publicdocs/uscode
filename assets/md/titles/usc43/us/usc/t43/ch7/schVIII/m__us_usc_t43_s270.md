## § 270. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789]

    Section, acts [May 14, 1898, ch. 299, § 1][/us/act/1898-05-14/ch299/s1], [30 Stat. 409][/us/stat/30/409]; [Mar. 3, 1903, ch. 1002][/us/act/1903-03-03/ch1002], [32 Stat. 1028][/us/stat/32/1028]; [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 387, § 1][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch387/s1], [37 Stat. 512][/us/stat/37/512]; [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1][/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1], [42 Stat. 208][/us/stat/42/208]; [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1144][/us/stat/43/1144]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100]; [Apr. 29, 1950, ch. 137, § 1][/us/act/1950-04-29/ch137/s1], [64 Stat. 94][/us/stat/64/94]; [Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 496, § 1][/us/act/1955-08-03/ch496/s1], [69 Stat. 444][/us/stat/69/444], set forth provisions relating to applicability of homestead laws to Alaska. Section was formerly classified to [section 371 of Title 48][/us/usc/t48/s371], Territories and Insular Possessions.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789], provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579] not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579/s701], set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1701].

§§ 270–1 to 270–3. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 92–203, § 18(a)__  __][/us/pl/92/203/s18/a]__  __,__  __Dec. 18, 1971__  __,__  __[__  __85 Stat. 710__  __][/us/stat/85/710]__ 

    Section 270–1, acts [May 17, 1906, ch. 2469, § 1][/us/act/1906-05-17/ch2469/s1], [34 Stat. 197][/us/stat/34/197]; [Aug. 2, 1956, ch. 891, § 1(a)][/us/act/1956-08-02/ch891/s1/a]–(d), [70 Stat. 954][/us/stat/70/954], authorized making of homestead allotments to native Indians, Aleuts, or Eskimos and provided for conveyance of allotted lands. Section was formerly classified to [section 357 of Title 48][/us/usc/t48/s357], Territories and Insular Possessions.

    Section 270–2, [act May 17, 1906, ch. 2469, § 2][/us/act/1906-05-17/ch2469/s2], as added [Aug. 2, 1956, ch. 891, § 1(e)][/us/act/1956-08-02/ch891/s1/e], [70 Stat. 954][/us/stat/70/954], permitted allotments of land in national forests if land was certified as chiefly valuable for agricultural or grazing uses. Section was formerly classified to [section 357a of Title 48][/us/usc/t48/s357a].

    Section 270–3, [act May 27, 1906, ch. 2469, § 3][/us/act/1906-05-27/ch2469/s3], as added [Aug. 2, 1956, ch. 891, § 1(e)][/us/act/1956-08-02/ch891/s1/e], [70 Stat. 954][/us/stat/70/954], prohibited making of an allotment unless person made satisfactory proof of substantially continuous use and occupancy of land for five years. Section was formerly classified to [section 357b of Title 48][/us/usc/t48/s357b].
