### SUBCHAPTER I—GENERAL PROVISIONS

§§ 161 to 164. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 702__  __][/us/pl/94/579/s702]__  __,__  __Oct. 21, 1976__  __,__  __[__  __90 Stat. 2787__  __][/us/stat/90/2787]__ 

    Section 161, R.S. § 2289; [Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 561, § 5][/us/act/1891-03-03/ch561/s5], [26 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/26/1097], related to entry of unappropriated public lands.

    Section 162, R.S. § 2290; [Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 561, § 5][/us/act/1891-03-03/ch561/s5], [26 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/26/1097]; [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1][/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1], [42 Stat. 208][/us/stat/42/208]; [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1145][/us/stat/43/1145]; 1946 Reorg.     Section 163, R.S. § 2295; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], related to record of application, registration, and return to Bureau of Land Management.

    Section 164, R.S. § 2291; [June 6, 1912, ch. 153][/us/act/1912-06-06/ch153], [37 Stat. 123][/us/stat/37/123], related to issuance, etc., of certificate or patent for entered lands.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 702][/us/pl/94/579/s702], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2787][/us/stat/90/2787], provided that the repeal made by section 702 is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976, except such effective date to be on and after tenth anniversary of date of approval of this Act, Oct. 21, 1976, insofar as homestead laws apply to public lands in Alaska.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579] not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579/s701], set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1701].
