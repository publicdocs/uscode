## §§ 1076 to 1081. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789]

    Section 1076, [act Mar. 3, 1893, ch. 208][/us/act/1893-03-03/ch208], [27 Stat. 593][/us/stat/27/593], authorized the President to withhold from sale or grant to municipal corporations portions of abandoned military reservations.

    Section 1077, acts [Aug. 23, 1894, ch. 314, § 1][/us/act/1894-08-23/ch314/s1], [28 Stat. 491][/us/stat/28/491]; [Feb. 15, 1895, ch. 92, § 1][/us/act/1895-02-15/ch92/s1], [28 Stat. 664][/us/stat/28/664], authorized opening of lands of abandoned military reservations to settlement.

    Section 1078, [act Aug. 23, 1894, ch. 314, § 2][/us/act/1894-08-23/ch314/s2], [28 Stat. 491][/us/stat/28/491], limited applicability of [section 1077 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1077] with respect to provisions of act July 5, 1884, relating to disposition of mineral lands.

    Section 1079, [act Feb. 11, 1903, ch. 543][/us/act/1903-02-11/ch543], [32 Stat. 822][/us/stat/32/822], related to confirmation of indemnity selections by States in lieu of school sections in abandoned military reservations.

    Section 1080, [act Feb. 15, 1895, ch. 92, § 1][/us/act/1895-02-15/ch92/s1], [28 Stat. 664][/us/stat/28/664], extended applicability of provisions relating to settlement and indemnity selection of abandoned military reservation to those abandoned prior to July 5, 1884.

    Section 1081, [act Aug. 23, 1894, ch. 314, § 3][/us/act/1894-08-23/ch314/s3], as added [Apr. 23, 1904, ch. 1496][/us/act/1904-04-23/ch1496], [33 Stat. 306][/us/stat/33/306], authorized patents for homesteads on Fort Abraham Lincoln Military Reservation, North Dakota.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789], provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579] not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579][/us/pl/94/579/s701], set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1701].
