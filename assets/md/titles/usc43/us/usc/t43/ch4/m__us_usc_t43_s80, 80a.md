## §§ 80, 80a. Repealed. [Apr. 24, 1944, ch. 177, § 4][/us/act/1944-04-24/ch177/s4], [58 Stat. 215][/us/stat/58/215]

    Section 80, R.S. §§ 2237, 2240; acts [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1][/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1], [42 Stat. 208][/us/stat/42/208]; [May 21, 1928, ch. 661][/us/act/1928-05-21/ch661], [45 Stat. 684][/us/stat/45/684]; [Aug. 22, 1935, ch. 602][/us/act/1935-08-22/ch602], [49 Stat. 680][/us/stat/49/680]; [Oct. 9, 1942, ch. 584, § 7][/us/act/1942-10-09/ch584/s7], [56 Stat. 779][/us/stat/56/779], provided that from and after Sept. 1, 1935, registers should be paid $2,000 per annum together with fees and commissions limited to $3,600 per annum. See [section 79c of this title][/us/usc/t43/s79c].

    Section 80a, R.S. §§ 2237, 2240, provided that receivers should be paid $500 per annum together with fees and commissions limited to $3,000 per annum. See [section 79c of this title][/us/usc/t43/s79c].
