## §§ 70 to 73. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 632][/us/stat/80/632], 638, 645, 646

    Section 70, [act Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1][/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1], [42 Stat. 208][/us/stat/42/208], consolidated offices of register and receiver.

    Section 71, [act Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1145][/us/stat/43/1145], provided for consolidation of offices of register and receiver, effective July 1, 1925.

    Section 72, R.S. § 2334; acts [Jan. 27, 1898, ch. 10][/us/act/1898-01-27/ch10], [30 Stat. 234][/us/stat/30/234]; [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1][/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1], [42 Stat. 208][/us/stat/42/208]; [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1145][/us/stat/43/1145]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], related to duties of Secretary of the Interior concerning sale of public lands.

    Section 73, [act Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 2][/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s2], [42 Stat. 208][/us/stat/42/208], related to designation of chief clerk to act in case of death, resignation, removal, or disability of register.

 __Prior Provisions__

    [June 5, 1920, ch. 235][/us/act/1920-06-05/ch235], [41 Stat. 907][/us/stat/41/907].

    [July 19, 1919, ch. 24][/us/act/1919-07-19/ch24], [41 Stat. 194][/us/stat/41/194].

    [Act May 24, 1922, ch. 199][/us/act/1922-05-24/ch199], [42 Stat. 557][/us/stat/42/557], abolished land office at Springfield and offices of register and receiver thereat.

    [Act May 2, 1914, ch. 74][/us/act/1914-05-02/ch74], §§ 1, 2, [38 Stat. 371][/us/stat/38/371], 372, abolished office of receiver of public moneys at Springfield, Mo., transferred his duties and custody of books, records, etc., to register, and contained other provisions concerning register’s duties.

    Act. [Mar. 2, 1895, ch. 177, § 3][/us/act/1895-03-02/ch177/s3], [28 Stat. 807][/us/stat/28/807], required duplication of reports and returns of registers and receivers to be prevented by regulations.

    Act. [Oct. 1, 1890, ch. 1269, § 2][/us/act/1890-10-01/ch1269/s2], [26 Stat. 657][/us/stat/26/657], concerned taking of final proofs by remaining officer in case of a vacancy in office of register or receiver.
