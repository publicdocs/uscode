## § 85. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, acts [Mar. 3, 1887, ch. 362][/us/act/1887-03-03/ch362], [24 Stat. 526][/us/stat/24/526]; [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1][/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1], [42 Stat. 208][/us/stat/42/208]; [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1145][/us/stat/43/1145]; [May 21, 1928, ch. 661][/us/act/1928-05-21/ch661], [45 Stat. 684][/us/stat/45/684], which required all fees collected by registers which would increase their salaries beyond a certain amount to be covered into the Treasury, except for certain clerical fees, was superseded by sections 79c and 79d of this title, which require all fees to be covered into the Treasury.
