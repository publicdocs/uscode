## § 90. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

Section, [act July 1, 1946, ch. 529][/us/act/1946-07-01/ch529], [60 Stat. 352][/us/stat/60/352], which required authorization of Commissioner of the General Land Office for expenses chargeable to the Government incurred by registers, was omitted pursuant to Reorg. Plan No. 3 of 1946, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100], set out as a note under [section 1 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1], which abolished the offices of registers and Commissioner of the General Land Office. Section was not repeated in the Interior Department Appropriation Act, 1948, [act July 25, 1947, ch. 337][/us/act/1947-07-25/ch337], [61 Stat. 460][/us/stat/61/460].
