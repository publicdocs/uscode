## § 971d. Effect on valid existing Alaskan rights

    Nothing in sections 971a to 971e of this title shall affect adversely any valid existing rights.

([Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 848, § 4][/us/act/1956-08-01/ch848/s4], [70 Stat. 898][/us/stat/70/898].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 420c of Title 48][/us/usc/t48/s420c], Territories and Insular Possessions.
