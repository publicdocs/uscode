## § 485h–3. “Long-term contract” defined

    As used in sections 485h–1 to 485h–5 of this title, the term “long-term contract” shall mean any contract the term of which is more than ten years.

([July 2, 1956, ch. 492, § 3][/us/act/1956-07-02/ch492/s3], [70 Stat. 484][/us/stat/70/484].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was not enacted as part of the Reclamation Project Act of 1939 which comprises this subchapter.
