## § 392. Payments into reclamation fund of moneys received from entrymen and water right applicants

    All moneys received from entrymen or applicants for water rights shall be paid into the reclamation fund.

([June 17, 1902, ch. 1093, § 5][/us/act/1902-06-17/ch1093/s5], [32 Stat. 389][/us/stat/32/389].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section is comprised of fourth sentence of section 5 of act June 17, 1902. First, second and fifth sentences of such section 5 were classified to sections 439, 431 and 381 of this title, respectively; part of third sentence was classified to [section 476 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s476].

 __Section as Unaffected by Submerged Lands Act__ 

    Provisions of this section as not amended, modified or repealed by the Submerged Lands Act, see [section 1303 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s1303].
