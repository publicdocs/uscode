## § 390h–19. Williamson County, Texas, water recycling and reuse project

    (a) __Authorization__ 

        The Secretary, in cooperation with the Lower Colorado River Authority, Texas, is authorized to participate in the design, planning, and construction of permanent facilities to reclaim and reuse water in Williamson County, Texas.

    (b) __Cost share__ 

        The Federal share of the costs of the project described in subsection (a) of this section shall not exceed 25 percent of the total cost.

    (c) __Limitation__ 

        The Secretary shall not provide funds for the operation and maintenance of the project described in subsection (a) of this section.
