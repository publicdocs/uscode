## § 497. Action to recover operation charge and penalty

    In the discretion of the Secretary of the Interior suit or action may be brought for the amounts of operation or maintenance charges in default and penalties in like manner as provided in [section 481 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s481].

([Aug. 13, 1914, ch. 247, § 6][/us/act/1914-08-13/ch247/s6], [38 Stat. 688][/us/stat/38/688].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section is comprised of third sentence of section 6 of act Aug. 13, 1914. First and second sentences of such section 6 are classified to sections 479, 493, 494, 495, and 496 of this title, respectively.
