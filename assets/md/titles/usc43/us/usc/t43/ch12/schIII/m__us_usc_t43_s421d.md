## § 421d. Effect on existing laws

    Except as otherwise provided in sections 421a to 421h of this title, the provisions of the Federal reclamation laws, and Acts amendatory thereto, are continued in full force and effect.

([July 4, 1955, ch. 271, § 4][/us/act/1955-07-04/ch271/s4], [69 Stat. 245][/us/stat/69/245]; [Pub. L. 92–487][/us/pl/92/487], Oct. 13, 1972, [86 Stat. 805][/us/stat/86/805].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1972—[Pub. L. 92–487][/us/pl/92/487] reenacted section without change.
