## § 485h–2. Amendments to existing contracts

    The Secretary is authorized to negotiate amendments to existing contracts entered into pursuant to subsection (e) of [section 485h of this title][/us/usc/t43/s485h] to conform said contracts to the provisions of sections 485h–1 to 485h–5 of this title.

([July 2, 1956, ch. 492, § 2][/us/act/1956-07-02/ch492/s2], [70 Stat. 484][/us/stat/70/484].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was not enacted as part of the Reclamation Project Act of 1939 which comprises this subchapter.
