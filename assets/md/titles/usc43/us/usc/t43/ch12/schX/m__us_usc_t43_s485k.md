## § 485k. Short title

    This subchapter may be cited as the “Reclamation Project Act of 1939.”

([Aug. 4, 1939, ch. 418, § 19][/us/act/1939-08-04/ch418/s19], [53 Stat. 1198][/us/stat/53/1198].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [act Aug. 4, 1939, ch. 418][/us/act/1939-08-04/ch418], [53 Stat. 1187][/us/stat/53/1187], as amended, which enacted this subchapter, sections 375a, 380a, and 387 to 389 of this title and section 16d of former Title 41, Public Contracts, and enacted provision set out as a note under [section 485j of this title][/us/usc/t43/s485j]. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

 __Short Title of 2013 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 113–24, § 1][/us/pl/113/24/s1], Aug. 9, 2013, [127 Stat. 498][/us/stat/127/498], provided that: 

> “This Act \[amending [section 485h of this title][/us/usc/t43/s485h]\] may be cited as the ‘Bureau of Reclamation Small Conduit Hydropower Development and Rural Jobs Act’.”
