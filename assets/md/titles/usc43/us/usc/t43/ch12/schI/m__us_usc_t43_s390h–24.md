## § 390h–24. Eastern Municipal Water District recycled water system pressurization and expansion project, California

    (a) __Authorization__ 

        The Secretary, in cooperation with the Eastern Municipal Water District, California, may participate in the design, planning, and construction of permanent facilities needed to establish operational pressure zones that will be used to provide recycled water in the district.

    (b) __Cost sharing__ 

        The Federal share of the cost of the project described in subsection (a) shall not exceed 25 percent of the total cost of the project.

    (c) __Limitation__ 

        Funds provided by the Secretary shall not be used for operation or maintenance of the project described in subsection (a).

    (d) __Authorization of appropriations__ 

        There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section $12,000,000.

    (e) __Sunset of authority__ 

        The authority of the Secretary to carry out any provisions of this section shall terminate 10 years after May 8, 2008.
