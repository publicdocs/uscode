## § 525. Covering proceeds into reclamation fund

    The moneys received in pursuance of the contracts authorized by sections 523 and 524 of this title shall be covered into the reclamation fund and be available for use under the terms of the reclamation Act and the Acts amendatory thereof or supplementary thereto.

([Feb. 21, 1911, ch. 141, § 3][/us/act/1911-02-21/ch141/s3], [36 Stat. 926][/us/stat/36/926].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The reclamation Act, referred to in text, probably means [act June 17, 1902, ch. 1093][/us/act/1902-06-17/ch1093], [32 Stat. 388][/us/stat/32/388], as amended, popularly known as the Reclamation Act, which is classified generally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 371 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s371] and Tables.

 __Codification__ 

    The words “the contracts authorized by sections 523 and 524 of this title” substituted in text for “such contracts”.
