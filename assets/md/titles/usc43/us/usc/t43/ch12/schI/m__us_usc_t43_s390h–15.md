## § 390h–15. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated for fiscal years beginning after September 30, 1992, $4,000,000 to carry out the study authorized by [section 390h–14 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s390h–14].

([Pub. L. 102–575, title XVI, § 1633][/us/pl/102/575/s1633], formerly § 1617, Oct. 30, 1992, [106 Stat. 4669][/us/stat/106/4669]; renumbered § 1633 and amended [Pub. L. 104–266, § 2(a)(1)][/us/pl/104/266/s2/a/1], (b)(3), Oct. 9, 1996, [110 Stat. 3290][/us/stat/110/3290], 3294.)

 __Amendments__ 

    1996—[Pub. L. 104–266, § 2(b)(3)][/us/pl/104/266/s2/b/3], made technical amendment to reference in original act which appears in text as reference to [section 390h–14 of this title][/us/usc/t43/s390h–14].
