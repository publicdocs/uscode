## § 1597. Construction of provisions of subchapter

    Except as provided in sections 620d(d)(5), 1543(g)(2), and 1595(b) of this title, with respect to the Colorado River Basin Project Act \[[43 U.S.C. 1501][/us/usc/t43/s1501] et seq.\] and the Colorado River Storage Project Act \[[43 U.S.C. 620][/us/usc/t43/s620] et seq.\], respectively, nothing in this subchapter shall be construed to alter, amend, repeal, modify, interpret, or be in conflict with the provisions of the Colorado River Compact ([45 Stat. 1057][/us/stat/45/1057]), the Upper Colorado River Basin Compact ([63 Stat. 31][/us/stat/63/31]), the Water Treaty of 1944 with the United Mexican States (Treaty Series 994; [59 Stat. 1219][/us/stat/59/1219]), the decree entered by the Supreme Court of the United States in Arizona against California and others (376 U.S. 340), the Boulder Canyon Project Act ([45 Stat. 1057][/us/stat/45/1057]) \[[43 U.S.C. 617][/us/usc/t43/s617] et seq.\], Boulder Canyon Project Adjustment Act ([54 Stat. 774][/us/stat/54/774]; [43 U.S.C. 618a][/us/usc/t43/s618a]) \[[43 U.S.C. 618][/us/usc/t43/s618] et seq.\], section 15 of the Colorado River Storage Project Act ([70 Stat. 111][/us/stat/70/111]; [43 U.S.C. 620n][/us/usc/t43/s620n]), the Colorado River Basin Project Act ([82 Stat. 885][/us/stat/82/885]), section 6 of the Fryingpan-Arkansas Project Act ([76 Stat. 393][/us/stat/76/393]) \[[43 U.S.C. 616e][/us/usc/t43/s616e]\], section 15 of the Navajo Indian irrigation project and initial stage of the San Juan-Chama Project Act ([76 Stat. 102][/us/stat/76/102]) \[[43 U.S.C. 615ww][/us/usc/t43/s615ww]\], the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 \[[42 U.S.C. 4321][/us/usc/t42/s4321] et seq.\], and the Federal Water Pollution Control Act, as amended \[[33 U.S.C. 1251][/us/usc/t33/s1251] et seq.\].

([Pub. L. 93–320, title I, § 207][/us/pl/93/320/s207], June 24, 1974, [88 Stat. 274][/us/stat/88/274].)
