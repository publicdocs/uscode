## § 1101. Definitions

    Section 2101(17a) of title 46 and [section 40102(a) of this title][/us/usc/t49/s40102/a] apply to this chapter. In this chapter, the term “accident” includes damage to or destruction of vehicles in surface or air transportation or pipelines, regardless of whether the initiating event is accidental or otherwise.

([Pub. L. 103–272][/us/pl/103/272], § 1(d), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 746][/us/stat/108/746]; [Pub. L. 106–424][/us/pl/106/424], § 2, Nov. 1, 2000, [114 Stat. 1883][/us/stat/114/1883].)

    A number of the source provisions of the chapter are taken from 49 App.:ch. 20. The text of 49 App.:ch. 20 contains general definitions, some of which are used in those source provisions.

    This section is included to ensure that the identical definitions that are relevant are used without repeating them. The source provisions for the definitions are found in the revision note for section 40102(a) of the revised title.
