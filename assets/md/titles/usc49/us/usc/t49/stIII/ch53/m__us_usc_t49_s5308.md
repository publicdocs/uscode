## \[§ 5308. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–141, div. B][/us/pl/112/141/dB], § 20002(a), July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 622][/us/stat/126/622]\]

    Section, [Pub. L. 103–272][/us/pl/103/272], § 1(d), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 800][/us/stat/108/800]; [Pub. L. 105–178, title III][/us/pl/105/178/tIII], § 3008(a), (c), June 9, 1998, [112 Stat. 348][/us/stat/112/348]; [Pub. L. 105–206, title IX][/us/pl/105/206/tIX], § 9009(f), July 22, 1998, [112 Stat. 855][/us/stat/112/855]; [Pub. L. 109–59, title III][/us/pl/109/59/tIII], § 3010(a), Aug. 10, 2005, [119 Stat. 1572][/us/stat/119/1572], related to a grant program for clean fuel buses.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 2012, see [section 3(a) of Pub. L. 112–141][/us/pl/112/141/s3/a], set out as an Effective and Termination Dates of 2012 Amendment note under [section 101 of Title 23][/us/usc/t23/s101], Highways.

 __National Fuel Cell Bus Technology Development Program__ 

    [Pub. L. 109–59, title III][/us/pl/109/59/tIII], § 3045, Aug. 10, 2005, [119 Stat. 1705][/us/stat/119/1705], which provided for the establishment of a national fuel cell bus technology development program, was repealed by [Pub. L. 112–141, div. B][/us/pl/112/141/dB], § 20002(c)(4), July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 622][/us/stat/126/622].
