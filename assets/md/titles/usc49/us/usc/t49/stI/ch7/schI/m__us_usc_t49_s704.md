## § 704. Annual report

    The Board shall annually transmit to the Congress a report on its activities.

(Added [Pub. L. 104–88, title II, § 201(a)][/us/pl/104/88/s201/a], Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 934][/us/stat/109/934].)
