## § 321. Definitions

    In this subchapter, “aeronautics”, “air commerce”, and “air navigation facility” have the same meanings given those terms in [section 40102(a) of this title][/us/usc/t49/s40102/a].

([Pub. L. 97–449, § 1(b)][/us/pl/97/449/s1/b], Jan. 12, 1983, [96 Stat. 2422][/us/stat/96/2422]; [Pub. L. 98–216, § 2(2)][/us/pl/98/216/s2/2], Feb. 14, 1984, [98 Stat. 5][/us/stat/98/5]; [Pub. L. 103–272, § 5(m)(8)][/us/pl/103/272/s5/m/8], July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1376][/us/stat/108/1376]; [Pub. L. 103–429, § 6(2)][/us/pl/103/429/s6/2], Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4378][/us/stat/108/4378].)
