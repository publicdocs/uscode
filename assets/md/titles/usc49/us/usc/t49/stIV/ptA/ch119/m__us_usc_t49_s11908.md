## § 11908. Relation to other Federal criminal penalties

    Notwithstanding [section 3571 of title 18][/us/usc/t18/s3571], United States Code, the criminal penalties provided for in this chapter are the exclusive criminal penalties for violations of this part.

(Added [Pub. L. 104–88, title I][/us/pl/104/88/tI], § 102(a), Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 852][/us/stat/109/852].)
