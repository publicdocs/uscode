## § 15902. Enforcement by the Board

    The Board may bring a civil action to enforce an order of the Board, except a civil action to enforce an order for the payment of money, when it is violated by a pipeline carrier providing transportation subject to this part.

(Added [Pub. L. 104–88, title I][/us/pl/104/88/tI], § 106(a), Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 927][/us/stat/109/927].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 11702 of this title][/us/usc/t49/s11702] prior to the general amendment of this subtitle by [Pub. L. 104–88][/us/pl/104/88], § 102(a).
