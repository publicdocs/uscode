## § 11327. Supplemental orders

    When cause exists, the Board may make appropriate orders supplemental to an order made in a proceeding under sections 11322 through 11326 of this title.

(Added [Pub. L. 104–88, title I, § 102(a)][/us/pl/104/88/s102/a], Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 843][/us/stat/109/843].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 11351 of this title][/us/usc/t49/s11351] prior to the general amendment of this subtitle by [Pub. L. 104–88, § 102(a)][/us/pl/104/88/s102/a].
