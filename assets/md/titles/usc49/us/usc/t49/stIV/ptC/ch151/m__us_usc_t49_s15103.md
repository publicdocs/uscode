## § 15103. Remedies as cumulative

    Except as otherwise provided in this part, the remedies provided under this part are in addition to remedies existing under another law or common law.

(Added [Pub. L. 104–88, title I, § 106(a)][/us/pl/104/88/s106/a], Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 922][/us/stat/109/922].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 10103 of this title][/us/usc/t49/s10103] prior to the general amendment of this subtitle by [Pub. L. 104–88, § 102(a)][/us/pl/104/88/s102/a].
