## \[§§ 24703 to 24705. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–134, title I][/us/pl/105/134], §§ 103–105(a), Dec. 2, 1997, [111 Stat. 2572][/us/stat/111/2572], 2573\]

    Section 24703, [Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(e)][/us/pl/103/272/s1/e], July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 924][/us/stat/108/924], provided route and service criteria for modifying or discontinuing routes.

    Section 24704, [Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(e)][/us/pl/103/272/s1/e], July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 925][/us/stat/108/925], related to application by States, regional or local authorities, or other persons requesting Amtrak to provide passenger rail service and criteria for decision.

    Section 24705, [Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(e)][/us/pl/103/272/s1/e], July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 926][/us/stat/108/926]; [Pub. L. 104–88, title III, § 308(i)][/us/pl/104/88/s308/i], Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 947][/us/stat/109/947], related to providing service on routes recommended to be discontinued, criteria for deferring Secretary’s recommendation, and providing short haul demonstration routes.
