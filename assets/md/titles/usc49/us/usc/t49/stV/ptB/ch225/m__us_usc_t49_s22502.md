## § 22502. Distribution

    The Secretary shall provide the grants to the State agency or agencies responsible for highway-rail grade crossing safety.

(Added [Pub. L. 110–432, div. A, title II, § 207(a)][/us/pl/110/432/s207/a], Oct. 16, 2008, [122 Stat. 4874][/us/stat/122/4874].)
