## § 41702. Interstate air transportation

    An air carrier shall provide safe and adequate interstate air transportation.

([Pub. L. 103–272][/us/pl/103/272], § 1(e), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1140][/us/stat/108/1140].)
