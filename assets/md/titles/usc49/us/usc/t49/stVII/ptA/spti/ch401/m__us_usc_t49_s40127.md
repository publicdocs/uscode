## § 40127. Prohibitions on discrimination

    (a)  __Persons in Air Transportation__  __.—__ 

    An air carrier or foreign air carrier may not subject a person in air transportation to discrimination on the basis of race, color, national origin, religion, sex, or ancestry.

    (b)  __Use of Private Airports__  __.—__ 

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no State or local government may prohibit the use or full enjoyment of a private airport within its jurisdiction by any person on the basis of that person’s race, color, national origin, religion, sex, or ancestry.

(Added [Pub. L. 106–181, title VII, § 706(a)][/us/pl/106/181/s706/a], Apr. 5, 2000, [114 Stat. 157][/us/stat/114/157].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section applicable only to fiscal years beginning after Sept. 30, 1999, see [section 3 of Pub. L. 106–181][/us/pl/106/181/s3], set out as an Effective Date of 2000 Amendments note under [section 106 of this title][/us/usc/t49/s106].
