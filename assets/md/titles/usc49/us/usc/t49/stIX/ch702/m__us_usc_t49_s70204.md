## § 70204. Savings provision

    Nothing in this subtitle provides additional authority to regulate or direct private activity on freight networks designated under this subtitle.

(Added [Pub. L. 114–94, div. A, title VIII, § 8001(a)][/us/pl/114/94/s8001/a], Dec. 4, 2015, [129 Stat. 1612][/us/stat/129/1612].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Oct. 1, 2015, see [section 1003 of Pub. L. 114–94][/us/pl/114/94/s1003], set out as an Effective Date of 2015 Amendment note under [section 5313 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s5313], Government Organization and Employees.
