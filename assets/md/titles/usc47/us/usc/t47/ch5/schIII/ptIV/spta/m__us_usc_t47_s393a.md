## § 393a. Long-range planning for facilities

    (a) The Secretary, in consultation with the Corporation, public telecommunications entities, and as appropriate with other parties, shall develop a long-range plan to accomplish the objectives set forth in [section 390 of this title][/us/usc/t47/s390]. Such plan shall include a detailed 5-year projection of the broadcast and nonbroadcast public telecommunications facilities required to meet such objectives, and the expenditures necessary to provide such facilities.

    (b) Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–66, title I, § 1021(a)][/us/pl/104/66/s1021/a], Dec. 21, 1995, [109 Stat. 712][/us/stat/109/712].
