## “SEC. 3010. NATIONAL ALERT AND TSUNAMI WARNING PROGRAM.

>     “The Assistant Secretary shall make payments of not to exceed $156,000,000, in the aggregate, during the fiscal year 2007 through 2012 period from the Digital Television Transition and Public Safety Fund established under section 309(j)(8)(E) of the Communications Act of 1934 ([47 U.S.C. 309(j)(8)(E)][/us/usc/t47/s309/j/8/E]) to implement a unified national alert system capable of alerting the public, on a national, regional, or local basis to emergency situations by using a variety of communications technologies. The Assistant Secretary shall use $50,000,000 of such amounts to implement a tsunami warning and coastal vulnerability program.
