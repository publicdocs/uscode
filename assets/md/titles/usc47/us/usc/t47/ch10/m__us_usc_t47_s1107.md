## § 1107. Sunset

    No loan guarantee may be approved under this chapter after December 31, 2006.

([Pub. L. 106–553][/us/pl/106/553], § 1(a)(2) \[title X, § 1009\], Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2762][/us/stat/114/2762], 2762A–140.)
