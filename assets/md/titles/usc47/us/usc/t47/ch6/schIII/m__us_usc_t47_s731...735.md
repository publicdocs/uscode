## §§ 731 to 735. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 731 and 732 ceased to be effective Apr. 15, 2005, pursuant to [section 765d(4) of this title][/us/usc/t47/s765d/4].

    Section 731, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III][/us/pl/87/624/tIII], § 301, as added [Pub. L. 103–414, title III][/us/pl/103/414/tIII], § 304(b)(2), Oct. 25, 1994, [108 Stat. 4297][/us/stat/108/4297], related to the creation of a communications satellite corporation.

    A prior section 731, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III][/us/pl/87/624/tIII], § 301, Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 423][/us/stat/76/423], authorized creation of a for-profit communications satellite corporation not to be a Government agency, subject to this chapter and the District of Columbia Business Corporation Act, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–414][/us/pl/103/414], § 304(b)(2).

    Section 732, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III][/us/pl/87/624/tIII], § 302, as added [Pub. L. 103–414, title III][/us/pl/103/414/tIII], § 304(b)(2), Oct. 25, 1994, [108 Stat. 4297][/us/stat/108/4297], related to laws applicable to the corporation.

    A prior section 732, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III][/us/pl/87/624/tIII], § 302, Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 423][/us/stat/76/423], related to process of organization of the communications satellite corporation, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–414][/us/pl/103/414], § 304(b)(2).

    Sections 733 and 734 ceased to be effective Mar. 17, 2000, pursuant to [section 765d(1) of this title][/us/usc/t47/s765d/1].

    Section 733, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III][/us/pl/87/624/tIII], § 303, Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 423][/us/stat/76/423]; [Pub. L. 91–3][/us/pl/91/3], § 1, Mar. 12, 1969, [83 Stat. 4][/us/stat/83/4]; [Pub. L. 103–414, title III][/us/pl/103/414/tIII], § 303(b)(1), Oct. 25, 1994, [108 Stat. 4296][/us/stat/108/4296], related to the board of directors and officers of the corporation.

    Section 734, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III][/us/pl/87/624/tIII], § 304, Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 424][/us/stat/76/424]; [Pub. L. 97–410][/us/pl/97/410], § 5, Jan. 3, 1983, [96 Stat. 2045][/us/stat/96/2045]; [Pub. L. 103–414, title III][/us/pl/103/414/tIII], §§ 303(b)(2), (3), 304(b)(3), Oct. 25, 1994, [108 Stat. 4296][/us/stat/108/4296], 4297, related to financing of the corporation.

    Section 735, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III][/us/pl/87/624/tIII], § 305, Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 425][/us/stat/76/425], which related to powers of the corporation, ceased to be effective July 18, 2001, pursuant to [section 765d(2) of this title][/us/usc/t47/s765d/2].
