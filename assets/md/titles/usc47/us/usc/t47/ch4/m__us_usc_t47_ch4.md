## CHAPTER 4—RADIO ACT OF 1927

§§ 81 to 83. __Repealed.__  __[__  __June 19, 1934, ch. 652, § 602(a)__  __][/us/act/1934-06-19/ch652/s602/a]__  __,__  __[__  __48 Stat. 1102__  __][/us/stat/48/1102]__ 

    Section 81, [act Feb. 23, 1927, ch. 169, § 1][/us/act/1927-02-23/ch169/s1], [44 Stat. 1162][/us/stat/44/1162], related to regulation of interstate and foreign radio communications and grant of license. See [section 301 of this title][/us/usc/t47/s301].

    Section 82, [act Feb. 23, 1927, ch. 169, § 2][/us/act/1927-02-23/ch169/s2], [44 Stat. 1162][/us/stat/44/1162], related to division of United States into five zones.

    Section 83, acts [Feb. 23, 1927, ch. 169, § 3][/us/act/1927-02-23/ch169/s3], [44 Stat. 1162][/us/stat/44/1162]; [Mar. 28, 1928, ch. 263, § 2][/us/act/1928-03-28/ch263/s2], [45 Stat. 373][/us/stat/45/373]; [Mar. 4, 1929, ch. 701, § 2][/us/act/1929-03-04/ch701/s2], [45 Stat. 1559][/us/stat/45/1559]; [Dec. 18, 1929, ch. 7, § 2][/us/act/1929-12-18/ch7/s2], [46 Stat. 50][/us/stat/46/50], related to creation of Federal Radio Commission, composition, qualifications, appointments, meetings, employees, seal, reports and compensation. See sections 151 and 154 of this title and section 5311 et seq. of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

§§ 83a to 83e. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 83a to 83e were omitted in view of abolition of Federal Radio Commission by [act June 19, 1934, ch. 652][/us/act/1934-06-19/ch652], title VI, § 603, [48 Stat. 1102][/us/stat/48/1102], which was classified to former [section 603 of this title][/us/usc/t47/s603].

    Section 83a, [act June 30, 1932, ch. 314][/us/act/1932-06-30/ch314], pt. II, title V, § 511, [47 Stat. 417][/us/stat/47/417], abolished Radio Division of Department of Commerce and transferred its powers and duties to Federal Radio Commission.

    Section 83b, [act June 30, 1932, ch. 314][/us/act/1932-06-30/ch314], pt. II, title V, § 512, [47 Stat. 417][/us/stat/47/417], transferred officers and employees of Radio Division to Federal Radio Commission.

    Section 83c, [act June 30, 1932, ch. 314][/us/act/1932-06-30/ch314], pt. II, title V, § 513, [47 Stat. 417][/us/stat/47/417], provided that orders, rules, regulations, and laws of Radio Division have continuing application until modified, amended or repealed by Federal Radio Commission.

    Section 83d, [act June 30, 1932, ch. 314][/us/act/1932-06-30/ch314], pt. II, title V, § 514, [47 Stat. 417][/us/stat/47/417], transferred unexpended appropriations from Radio Division to Federal Radio Commission.

    Section 83e, [act Mar. 28, 1934, ch. 102][/us/act/1934-03-28/ch102], title I, § 1, [48 Stat. 513][/us/stat/48/513], related to adjustment of classification or compensation of employees.
