## § 9013. Effective date of chapter

    The provisions of this chapter shall take effect on January 1, 1973.

(Added [Pub. L. 92–178, title VIII][/us/pl/92/178/tVIII], § 801, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 572][/us/stat/85/572].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 9021, added by [Pub. L. 92–178, title VIII][/us/pl/92/178/tVIII], § 801, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 572][/us/stat/85/572], established Presidential Election Campaign Fund Advisory Board, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 93–443, title IV][/us/pl/93/443/tIV], § 408(c), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1297][/us/stat/88/1297]. For effective date of repeal see [section 410(c)(1) of Pub. L. 93–443][/us/pl/93/443/s410/c/1], set out as an Effective Date of 1974 Amendment note under [section 30101 of Title 52][/us/usc/t52/s30101], Voting and Elections.
