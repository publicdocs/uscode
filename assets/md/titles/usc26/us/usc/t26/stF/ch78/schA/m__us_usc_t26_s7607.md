## \[§ 7607. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 320(b)][/us/pl/98/473/s320/b], Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2056][/us/stat/98/2056], and [Pub. L. 98–573, title II, § 213(b)(1)][/us/pl/98/573/s213/b/1], Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2988][/us/stat/98/2988]\]

    Section, added [July 18, 1956, ch. 629][/us/act/1956-07-18/ch629], title I, § 104(a), [70 Stat. 570][/us/stat/70/570]; amended Oct. 27, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–513, title III, § 1102(g)(1)][/us/pl/91/513/s1102/g/1], [84 Stat. 1292][/us/stat/84/1292], set forth additional authority for Bureau of Customs with respect to firearms, warrants, etc.

    Another section 7607 was renumbered [section 7613 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s7613].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 15, 1984, see [section 214(e) of Pub. L. 98–573][/us/pl/98/573/s214/e], set out as an Effective Date of 1984 Amendment note under [section 1304 of Title 19][/us/usc/t19/s1304], Customs Duties.
