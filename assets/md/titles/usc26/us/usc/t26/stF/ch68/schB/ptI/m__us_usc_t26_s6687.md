## \[§ 6687. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–239, title VII][/us/pl/101/239/tVII], § 7711(b)(1), Dec. 19, 1989, [103 Stat. 2393][/us/stat/103/2393]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 92–512, title I][/us/pl/92/512/tI], § 144(b)(1), Oct. 20, 1972, [86 Stat. 936][/us/stat/86/936], related to failure to supply information with respect to place of residence.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal applicable to returns and statements the due date for which (determined without regard to extensions) is after Dec. 31, 1989, see [section 7711(c) of Pub. L. 101–239][/us/pl/101/239/s7711/c], set out as an Effective Date of 1989 Amendment note under [section 6721 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s6721].
