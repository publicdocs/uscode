## § 7326. Disposal of forfeited or abandoned property in special cases

    (a) __Coin-operated gaming devices__ 

        Any coin-operated gaming device as defined in section 4462  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  upon which a tax is imposed by section 4461  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  and which has been forfeited under any provision of this title shall be destroyed, or otherwise disposed of, in such manner as may be prescribed by the Secretary.

    (b) __Firearms__ 

        For provisions relating to disposal of forfeited firearms, see section 5872(b).
