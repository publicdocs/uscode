## \[§ 6424. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–424, title V][/us/pl/97/424/tV], § 515(b)(5), Jan. 6, 1983, [96 Stat. 2181][/us/stat/96/2181]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 89–44, title II][/us/pl/89/44/tII], § 202(b), June 21, 1965, [79 Stat. 137][/us/stat/79/137]; amended [Pub. L. 91–258, title II][/us/pl/91/258/tII], § 207(b), May 21, 1970, [84 Stat. 248][/us/stat/84/248]; [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1906(a)(30), (b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1828][/us/stat/90/1828], 1834; [Pub. L. 95–618, title II][/us/pl/95/618/tII], §§ 222(a)(3), 233(b)(1), (2)(A), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3187][/us/stat/92/3187], 3191; [Pub. L. 97–473, title II][/us/pl/97/473/tII], § 202(b)(13), Jan. 14, 1983, [96 Stat. 2610][/us/stat/96/2610], had provided for payments by the Secretary of an amount equal to 6 cents for each gallon of lubricating oil used in a qualified business use or in a qualified bus to certain ultimate purchasers of the lubricating oil.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal applicable with respect to articles sold after Jan. 6, 1983, see [section 515(c) of Pub. L. 97–424][/us/pl/97/424/s515/c], set out as an Effective Date of 1983 Amendment note under [section 34 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s34].
