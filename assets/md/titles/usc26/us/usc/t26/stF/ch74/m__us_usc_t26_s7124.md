## § 7124. Cross references

    For criminal penalties for concealment of property, false statement, or falsifying and destroying records, in connection with any closing agreement, compromise, or offer of compromise, see section 7206.

([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 850][/us/stat/68A/850], § 7123; [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], § 3(f)(12), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1065][/us/stat/96/1065]; renumbered § 7124, [Pub. L. 105–206, title III][/us/pl/105/206/tIII], § 3465(a)(1), July 22, 1998, [112 Stat. 767][/us/stat/112/767].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1998—[Pub. L. 105–206][/us/pl/105/206] renumbered [section 7123 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s7123] as this section.

    1982—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], § 3(f)(12)(A), struck out heading “Criminal penalties”.

    Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], § 3(f)(12)(B), struck out subsec. (b) which set forth cross reference to R.S. 3469 ([31 U.S.C. 194][/us/usc/t31/s194]) relating to compromises after judgment.
