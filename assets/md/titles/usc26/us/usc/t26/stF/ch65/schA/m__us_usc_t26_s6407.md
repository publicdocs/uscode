## § 6407. Date of allowance of refund or credit

    The date on which the Secretary first authorizes the scheduling of an overassessment in respect of any internal revenue tax shall be considered as the date of allowance of refund or credit in respect of such tax.

([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 793][/us/stat/68A/793]; [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)][/us/pl/94/455/s1906/b/13/A], Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455] struck out “or his delegate” after “Secretary” wherever appearing.
