## § 7484. Change of incumbent in office

    When the incumbent of the office of Secretary changes, no substitution of the name of his successor shall be required in proceedings pending before any appellate court reviewing the action of the Tax Court.

([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 891][/us/stat/68A/891]; [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1906(b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455] struck out “or his delegate” after “Secretary”.
