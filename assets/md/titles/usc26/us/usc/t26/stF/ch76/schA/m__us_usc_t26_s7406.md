## § 7406. Disposition of judgments and moneys recovered

    All judgments and moneys recovered or received for taxes, costs, forfeitures, and penalties shall be paid to the Secretary as collections of internal revenue taxes.

([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 875][/us/stat/68A/875]; [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1906(b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455] struck out “or his delegate” after “Secretary”.
