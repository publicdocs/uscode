## \[§ 6016. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–364, title I, § 103(a)][/us/pl/90/364/s103/a], June 28, 1968, [82 Stat. 260][/us/stat/82/260]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 738][/us/stat/68A/738]; Feb. 26, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–272, title I, § 122(d)][/us/pl/88/272/s122/d], [78 Stat. 29][/us/stat/78/29], Nov. 13, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–809, title I, § 104][/us/pl/89/809/s104](l), [80 Stat. 1563][/us/stat/80/1563], provided for the declaration of estimated income tax by corporations.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective with respect to taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 1967, except as provided by [section 104 of Pub. L. 90–364][/us/pl/90/364/s104], see [section 103(f) of Pub. L. 90–364][/us/pl/90/364/s103/f], set out as an Effective Date of 1968 Amendment note under [section 243 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s243].
