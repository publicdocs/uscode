## \[§ 7443B. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–458, title I][/us/pl/110/458/tI], § 108(l), Dec. 23, 2008, [122 Stat. 5110][/us/stat/122/5110]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 109–280, title VIII][/us/pl/109/280/tVIII], § 856(a), Aug. 17, 2006, [120 Stat. 1019][/us/stat/120/1019], related to the recall of retired special trial judges of the Tax Court.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective as if included in the provisions of [Pub. L. 109–280][/us/pl/109/280] to which the repeal relates, except as otherwise provided, see [section 112 of Pub. L. 110–458][/us/pl/110/458/s112], set out as an Effective Date of 2008 Amendment note under [section 72 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s72].

 __Construction of Amendment by__  __[__  __Pub. L. 109–280__  __][/us/pl/109/280]__ 

[Pub. L. 110–458, title I][/us/pl/110/458/tI], § 108(l), Dec. 23, 2008, [122 Stat. 5110][/us/stat/122/5110], provided that: 

> “Section 856 of the 2006 Act \[[Pub. L. 109–280][/us/pl/109/280], enacting this section\], and the amendments made by such section, are hereby repealed, and the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 shall be applied and administered as if such sections and amendments had not been enacted.”
