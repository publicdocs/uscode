## \[§ 6050C. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–418, title I][/us/pl/100/418/tI], § 1941(b)(1), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1323][/us/stat/102/1323]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 96–223, title I][/us/pl/96/223/tI], § 101(d)(1), Apr. 2, 1980, [94 Stat. 251][/us/stat/94/251]; amended [Pub. L. 99–514, title XV][/us/pl/99/514/tXV], § 1501(d)(1)(E), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2740][/us/stat/100/2740], related to information regarding windfall profit tax on domestic crude oil.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal applicable to crude oil removed from the premises on or after Aug. 23, 1988, see [section 1941(c) of Pub. L. 100–418][/us/pl/100/418/s1941/c], set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 164 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s164].
