## § 6341. Expense of levy and sale

    The Secretary shall determine the expenses to be allowed in all cases of levy and sale.

([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 789][/us/stat/68A/789]; [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1906(b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455] struck out “or his delegate” after “Secretary”.
