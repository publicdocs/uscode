## § 6612. Cross references

        For interest on judgments for overpayments, see [28 U.S.C. 2411(a)][/us/usc/t28/s2411/a].

        For provisions prohibiting interest on certain adjustments in tax, see section 6413(a).

        For other restrictions on interest, see 2014(e)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original.
