## \[§ 7236. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–490, § 3(b)(1)][/us/pl/93/490/s3/b/1], Oct. 26, 1974, [88 Stat. 1466][/us/stat/88/1466]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 860][/us/stat/68A/860], set out acts and penalties for violations of laws relating to filled cheese.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal applicable to filled cheese manufactured, imported, or sold after Oct. 26, 1974, see [section 3(c) of Pub. L. 93–490][/us/pl/93/490/s3/c], set out as a note under sections 4831 to 4834 of this title.
