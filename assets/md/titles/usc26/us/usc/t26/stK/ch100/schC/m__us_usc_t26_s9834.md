## § 9834. Enforcement

    For the imposition of tax on any failure of a group health plan to meet the requirements of this chapter, see section 4980D.

(Added [Pub. L. 110–233, title I, § 103(e)(1)][/us/pl/110/233/s103/e/1], May 21, 2008, [122 Stat. 899][/us/stat/122/899].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section applicable with respect to group health plans for plan years beginning after the date that is one year after May 21, 2008, see [section 103(f)(2) of Pub. L. 110–233][/us/pl/110/233/s103/f/2], set out as an Effective Date of 2008 Amendment note under [section 9802 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s9802].
