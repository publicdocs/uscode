## § 2623. Taxable amount in case of direct skip

    For purposes of this chapter, the taxable amount in the case of a direct skip shall be the value of the property received by the trans­feree.

(Added [Pub. L. 99–514, title XIV, § 1431(a)][/us/pl/99/514/s1431/a], Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2721][/us/stat/100/2721].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section applicable to generation-skipping transfers (within the meaning of [section 2611 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s2611]) made after Oct. 22, 1986, except as otherwise provided, see [section 1433 of Pub. L. 99–514][/us/pl/99/514/s1433], set out as a note under [section 2601 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s2601].
