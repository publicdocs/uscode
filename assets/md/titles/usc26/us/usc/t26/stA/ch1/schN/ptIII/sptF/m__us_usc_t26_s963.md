## \[§ 963. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–12, title VI][/us/pl/94/12/tVI], § 602(a)(1), Mar. 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 58][/us/stat/89/58]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 87–834][/us/pl/87/834], § 12(a), Oct. 16, 1962, [76 Stat. 1023][/us/stat/76/1023]; amended [Pub. L. 88–272, title I][/us/pl/88/272/tI], § 123(b), Feb. 26, 1964, [78 Stat. 29][/us/stat/78/29]; [Pub. L. 90–364, title I][/us/pl/90/364/tI], § 102(b), June 28, 1968, [82 Stat. 255][/us/stat/82/255]; [Pub. L. 91–53][/us/pl/91/53], § 5(b), Aug. 7, 1969, [83 Stat. 95][/us/stat/83/95]; [Pub. L. 91–172, title VII][/us/pl/91/172/tVII], § 701(b), Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 659][/us/stat/83/659], dealt with the receipt of minimum distributions by domestic corporations.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective with respect to taxable years for foreign corporations beginning after Dec. 31, 1975, and to taxable years of United States shareholders (within the meaning of [section 951(b) of this title][/us/usc/t26/s951/b]) within which or with which such taxable years of such foreign corporations end, see [section 602(f) of Pub. L. 94–12][/us/pl/94/12/s602/f], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 955 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s955].
