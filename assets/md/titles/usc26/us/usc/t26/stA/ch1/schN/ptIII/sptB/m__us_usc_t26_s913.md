## \[§ 913. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–34, title I, § 112(a)][/us/pl/97/34/s112/a], Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 194][/us/stat/95/194]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 95–615, title II, § 203(a)][/us/pl/95/615/s203/a], Nov. 8, 1978, [92 Stat. 3100][/us/stat/92/3100]; amended [Pub. L. 96–222, title I, § 108(a)(1)(B)][/us/pl/96/222/s108/a/1/B], (F), Apr. 1, 1980. [94 Stat. 223][/us/stat/94/223], 225; [Pub.  __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal applicable with respect to taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 1981, see [section 115 of Pub. L. 97–34][/us/pl/97/34/s115], set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under [section 911 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s911].
