##### \[PART V—REPEALED\]

\[§§ 860H to 860L. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 108–357, title VIII, § 835(a)__  __][/us/pl/108/357/s835/a]__  __,__  __Oct. 22, 2004__  __,__  __[__  __118 Stat. 1593__  __][/us/stat/118/1593]__  __\]__ 

    Section 860H, added [Pub. L. 104–188, title I, § 1621(a)][/us/pl/104/188/s1621/a], Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1858][/us/stat/110/1858], set forth general rules relating to taxation of a FASIT.

    Section 860I, added [Pub. L. 104–188, title I, § 1621(a)][/us/pl/104/188/s1621/a], Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1859][/us/stat/110/1859], related to gain recognition on contributions to a FASIT and in other cases.

    Section 860J, added [Pub. L. 104–188, title I, § 1621(a)][/us/pl/104/188/s1621/a], Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1860][/us/stat/110/1860], prohibited offset of certain FASIT inclusions by non-FASIT losses.

    Section 860K, added [Pub. L. 104–188, title I, § 1621(a)][/us/pl/104/188/s1621/a], Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1861][/us/stat/110/1861], related to treatment of transfers of high-yield interests to disqualified holders.

    Section 860L, added [Pub. L. 104–188, title I, § 1621(a)][/us/pl/104/188/s1621/a], Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1862][/us/stat/110/1862]; amended [Pub. L. 105–34, title XVI, § 1601(f)(6)][/us/pl/105/34/s1601/f/6], Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 1091][/us/stat/111/1091], defined terms and set forth special rules relating to FASITs.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 1, 2005, with exception for any FASIT in existence on Oct. 22, 2004, to the extent that regular interests issued by the FASIT before such date continue to remain outstanding in accordance with the original terms of issuance, see [section 835(c) of Pub. L. 108–357][/us/pl/108/357/s835/c], set out as an Effective Date of 2004 Amendments note under [section 56 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s56].
