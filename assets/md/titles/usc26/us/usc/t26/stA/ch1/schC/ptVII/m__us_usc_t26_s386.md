## \[§ 386. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–647, title I][/us/pl/100/647/tI], § 1006(e)(8)(A), Nov. 10, 1988, [102 Stat. 3401][/us/stat/102/3401]\]

 __Effective Date of Repeal__

L. 99–514][/us/pl/99/514], to which such amendment relates, see [section 1019(a) of Pub.
