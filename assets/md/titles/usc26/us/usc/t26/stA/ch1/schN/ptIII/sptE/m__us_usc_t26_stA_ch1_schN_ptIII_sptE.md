###### \[Subpart E—Repealed\]

\[§§ 941 to 943. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 108–357, title I, § 101(b)(1)__  __][/us/pl/108/357/s101/b/1]__  __,__  __Oct. 22, 2004__  __,__  __[__  __118 Stat. 1423__  __][/us/stat/118/1423]__  __\]__ 

    Section 941, added [Pub. L. 106–519, § 3(b)][/us/pl/106/519/s3/b], Nov. 15, 2000, [114 Stat. 2424][/us/stat/114/2424], related to qualifying foreign trade income.

    A prior section 941, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 293][/us/stat/68A/293]; Oct. 4, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–455, title X, § 1053(a)][/us/pl/94/455/s1053/a], title XIX, § 1906(b)(1)(A), [90 Stat. 1648][/us/stat/90/1648], 1834, set forth provisions authorizing special deduction for China Trade Act corporations, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–455, title X, § 1053(c)][/us/pl/94/455/s1053/c], (e), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1649][/us/stat/90/1649], effective with respect to taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 1977.

    Section 942, added [Pub. L. 106–519, § 3(b)][/us/pl/106/519/s3/b], Nov. 15, 2000, [114 Stat. 2426][/us/stat/114/2426], defined “foreign trading gross receipts” and set forth economic process requirements.

    A prior section 942, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 294][/us/stat/68A/294], disallowed foreign tax credit authorized by section 901 to any corporation organized under the China Trade Act, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–455, title X, § 1053(c)][/us/pl/94/455/s1053/c], (e), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1649][/us/stat/90/1649], effective with respect to taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 1977.

    Section 943, added [Pub. L. 106–519, § 3(b)][/us/pl/106/519/s3/b], Nov. 15, 2000, [114 Stat. 2428][/us/stat/114/2428]; amended [Pub. L. 107–147, title IV, § 417(14)][/us/pl/107/147/s417/14], Mar. 9, 2002, [116 Stat. 56][/us/stat/116/56], set forth other definitions and special rules for purposes of this subpart.

    A prior section 943, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 294][/us/stat/68A/294]; Oct. 4, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–455, title X, § 1053(b)][/us/pl/94/455/s1053/b], [90 Stat. 1648][/us/stat/90/1648], set forth provisions relating to exclusion from gross income of residents of Formosa or Hong Kong of amounts distributed as dividends by China Trade Act corporations, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–455, title X, § 1053(c)][/us/pl/94/455/s1053/c], (e), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1649][/us/stat/90/1649], effective with respect to taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 1977.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal applicable to transactions after Dec. 31, 2004, see [section 101(c) of Pub. L. 108–357][/us/pl/108/357/s101/c], set out as an Effective Date of 2004 Amendments note under [section 56 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s56].
