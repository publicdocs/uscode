## \[§ 1347. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1951(b)(12)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1840][/us/stat/90/1840]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 349][/us/stat/68A/349]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–866, title I][/us/pl/85/866/tI], § 61(a), [72 Stat. 1648][/us/stat/72/1648]; Dec. 30, 1969, [Pub. L. 91–172, title VIII][/us/pl/91/172/tVIII], § 803(d)(5), [83 Stat. 684][/us/stat/83/684], related to claims against the United States involving acquisition of property.

 __Savings Provision__ 

[Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1951(b)(12)(B), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1840][/us/stat/90/1840], provided that: 

> “Notwithstanding subparagraph (A) \[repealing this section\], if amounts received in a taxable year beginning after December 31, 1976, would have been subject to the provisions of section 1347 if received in a taxable year beginning before such date, the tax imposed by section 1 attributable to such receipt shall be computed as if section 1347 had not been repealed.”
