## \[§ 583. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1901(a)(82)][/us/pl/94/455/s1901/a/82], Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1778][/us/stat/90/1778]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 202][/us/stat/68A/202], related to deductions by certain taxpayers of dividends paid to the United States or any instrumentality thereof exempt from Federal income taxes on the preferred stock of the corporation owned by the United States or such instrumentality.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective with respect to taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 1976, see [section 1901(d) of Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455/s1901/d], set out as an Effective Date of 1976 Amendment note under [section 2 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s2].
