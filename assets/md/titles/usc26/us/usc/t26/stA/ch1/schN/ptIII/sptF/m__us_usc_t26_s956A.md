## \[§ 956A. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–188, title I][/us/pl/104/188/tI], § 1501(a)(2), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1825][/us/stat/110/1825]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 103–66, title XIII][/us/pl/103/66/tXIII], § 13231(b), Aug. 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 496][/us/stat/107/496]; amended [Pub. L. 104–188, title I][/us/pl/104/188/tI], § 1703(i)(2), (3), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1876][/us/stat/110/1876], related to earnings invested in excess passive assets.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 104–188][/us/pl/104/188] applicable to taxable years of foreign corporations beginning after Dec. 31, 1996, and to taxable years of United States shareholders within which or with which such taxable years of foreign corporations end, see [section 1501(d) of Pub. L. 104–188][/us/pl/104/188/s1501/d], set out as an Effective Date of 1996 Amendment note under [section 904 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s904].
