## \[§ 586. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–514, title IX][/us/pl/99/514/tIX], § 901(c), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2378][/us/stat/100/2378]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 91–172, title IV][/us/pl/91/172/tIV], § 431(a), Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 618][/us/stat/83/618]; amended [Pub.
