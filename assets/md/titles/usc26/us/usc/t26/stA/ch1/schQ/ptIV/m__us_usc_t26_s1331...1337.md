## \[§§ 1331 to 1337. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1901(a)(145)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1788][/us/stat/90/1788]\]

    Section 1331, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 343][/us/stat/68A/343], related to war loss recoveries.

    Section 1332, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 343][/us/stat/68A/343], related to inclusion in gross income of war loss recoveries.

    Section 1333, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 344][/us/stat/68A/344], related to tax adjustment measured by prior benefits.

    Section 1334, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 346][/us/stat/68A/346], related to restoration of value of investments referable to destroyed or seized property.

    Section 1335, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 346][/us/stat/68A/346], related to election by taxpayer for application of section 1333.

    Section 1336, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 347][/us/stat/68A/347], related to basis of recovered property.

    Section 1337, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 347][/us/stat/68A/347], related to applicable rules.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

[Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1901(a)(145)(B), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1788][/us/stat/90/1788], provided that: 

> “The repeal by subparagraph (A) \[repealing sections 1331 to 1337 of this title\] shall apply with respect to war loss recoveries in taxable years beginning after December 31, 1976”.
