## \[§ 1056. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–357, title VIII][/us/pl/108/357/tVIII], § 886(b)(1)(A), Oct. 22, 2004, [118 Stat. 1641][/us/stat/118/1641]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 94–455, title II][/us/pl/94/455/tII], § 212(a)(1), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1545][/us/stat/90/1545]; amended [Pub. L. 99–514, title VI][/us/pl/99/514/tVI], § 631(e)(13), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2275][/us/stat/100/2275], related to basis limitation for player contracts transferred in connection with the sale of a franchise.

    A prior section 1056 was renumbered [section 1061 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s1061].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal applicable to property acquired after Oct. 22, 2004, see [section 886(c)(1) of Pub. L. 108–357][/us/pl/108/357/s886/c/1], set out as an Effective Date of 2004 Amendment note under [section 197 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s197].
