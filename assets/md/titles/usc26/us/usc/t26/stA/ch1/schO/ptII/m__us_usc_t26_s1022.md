## \[§ 1022. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–312, title III][/us/pl/111/312/tIII], § 301(a), Dec. 17, 2010, [124 Stat. 3300][/us/stat/124/3300]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 107–16, title V][/us/pl/107/16/tV], § 542(a), June 7, 2001, [115 Stat. 76][/us/stat/115/76], related to treatment of property acquired from a decedent dying after Dec. 31, 2009.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 1022, added [Pub. L. 88–272, title II][/us/pl/88/272/tII], § 225(j)(1), Feb. 26, 1964, [78 Stat. 92][/us/stat/78/92], dealt with the increase in basis with respect to certain foreign personal holding company stock or securities, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1901(a)(126), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1784][/us/stat/90/1784], applicable with respect to stock or securities acquired from a decedent dying after Oct. 4, 1976.

    Another prior section 1022, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 302][/us/stat/68A/302], relating to cross references, was renumbered section 1023.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal of section applicable to estates of decedents dying, and transfers made after Dec. 31, 2009, except as otherwise provided, see [section 301(e) of Pub. L. 111–312][/us/pl/111/312/s301/e], set out as an Effective and Termination Dates of 2010 Amendment note under [section 121 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s121].
