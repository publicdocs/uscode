## \[§ 218. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–600, title I][/us/pl/95/600/tI], § 113(a)(1), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2778][/us/stat/92/2778]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 92–178, title VII][/us/pl/92/178/tVII], § 702(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 561][/us/stat/85/561]; amended [Pub. L. 93–625][/us/pl/93/625], §§ 11(d), 12(b), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2120][/us/stat/88/2120]; [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1906(b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834], related to contributions to candidates for public office.

    A prior section 218 was renumbered [section 224 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s224].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective with respect to contributions the payment of which is made after Dec. 31, 1978, in taxable years beginning after such date, see [section 113(d) of Pub. L. 95–600][/us/pl/95/600/s113/d], set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 24 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s24].
