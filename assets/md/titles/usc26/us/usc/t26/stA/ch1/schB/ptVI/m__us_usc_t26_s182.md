## \[§ 182. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–514, title IV][/us/pl/99/514/tIV], § 402(a), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2221][/us/stat/100/2221]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 87–834][/us/pl/87/834], § 21(a), Oct. 16, 1962, [76 Stat. 1063][/us/stat/76/1063]; amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1906(b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834], authorized deduction of expenditures by farmers for clearing land.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

[Pub. L. 99–514, title IV][/us/pl/99/514/tIV], § 402(c), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2221][/us/stat/100/2221], provided that: 

> “The amendments made by this section \[amending sections 263 and 1252 of this title and repealing this section\] shall apply to amounts paid or incurred after December 31, 1985, in taxable years ending after such date.”
