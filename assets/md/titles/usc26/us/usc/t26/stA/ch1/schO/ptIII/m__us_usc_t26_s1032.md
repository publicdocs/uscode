## § 1032. Exchange of stock for property

    (a) __Nonrecognition of gain or loss__ 

        No gain or loss shall be recognized to a corporation on the receipt of money or other property in exchange for stock (including treasury stock) of such corporation. No gain or loss shall be recognized by a corporation with respect to any lapse or acquisition of an option, or with respect to a securities futures contract (as defined in section 1234B), to buy or sell its stock (including treasury stock).

    (b) __Basis__ 

        For basis of property acquired by a corporation in certain exchanges for its stock, see section 362.

([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 303][/us/stat/68A/303]; [Pub. L. 98–369, div.  __Amendments__ 

    2000—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 106–554][/us/pl/106/554] inserted “, or with respect to a securities futures contract (as defined in section 1234B),” after “an option” in second sentence.

    1984—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 98–369][/us/pl/98/369] inserted provision that no gain or loss shall be recognized by a corporation with respect to any lapse or acquisition of an option to buy or sell its stock (including treasury stock).
