## § 771. Application of subchapter to electing large partnerships

    The preceding provisions of this subchapter to the extent inconsistent with the provisions of this part shall not apply to an electing large partnership and its partners.

(Added [Pub. L. 105–34, title XII, § 1221(a)][/us/pl/105/34/s1221/a], Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 1002][/us/stat/111/1002].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 771, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 253][/us/stat/68A/253], related to the effective date for this subchapter, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1901(a)(94)][/us/pl/94/455/s1901/a/94], Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1780][/us/stat/90/1780].

 __Effective Date__ 

[Pub. L. 105–34, title XII, § 1221(c)][/us/pl/105/34/s1221/c], Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 1008][/us/stat/111/1008], provided that: 

> “The amendments made by this section \[enacting this part\] shall apply to partnership taxable years beginning after December 31, 1997.”

    This part applicable to partnership taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 1997, see [section 1226 of Pub. L. 105–34][/us/pl/105/34/s1226], as amended, set out as an Effective Date of 1997 Amendment note under [section 6011 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s6011].
