## § 139C. COBRA premium assistance

    In the case of an assistance eligible individual (as defined in section 3001 of title III of division B of the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009), gross income does not include any premium reduction provided under subsection (a) of such section.

(Added [Pub. L. 111–5, div. B, title III][/us/pl/111/5/dB/tIII], § 3001(a)(15)(A), Feb. 17, 2009, [123 Stat. 465][/us/stat/123/465]; amended [Pub. L. 111–144][/us/pl/111/144], § 3(b)(5)(B), Mar. 2, 2010, [124 Stat. 44][/us/stat/124/44].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Section 3001 of title III of division B of the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009, referred to in text, is [section 3001 of Pub. L. 111–5][/us/pl/111/5/s3001], div. B, title III, Feb. 17, 2009, [123 Stat. 455][/us/stat/123/455], which is set out as a note under [section 6432 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s6432].

 __Amendments__ 

    2010—[Pub. L. 111–144][/us/pl/111/144] substituted “section 3001 of title III of division B of the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009” for “section 3002 of the Health Insurance Assistance for the Unemployed Act of 2009”.

 __Effective Date of 2010 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 111–144][/us/pl/111/144] effective as if included in the provisions of [section 3001 of Pub. L. 111–5][/us/pl/111/5/s3001] to which it relates, see [section 3(c) of Pub. L. 111–144][/us/pl/111/144/s3/c], set out as a note under [section 6432 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s6432].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section applicable to taxable years ending after Feb. 17, 2009, see [section 3001(a)(15)(C) of Pub. L. 111–5][/us/pl/111/5/s3001/a/15/C], set out as a Premium Assistance for COBRA Benefits note under [section 6432 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s6432].
