## \[§ 179A. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–295, div. A, title II, § 221(a)(34)(A)][/us/pl/113/295/s221/a/34/A], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 4042][/us/stat/128/4042]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 102–486, title XIX, § 1913(a)(1)][/us/pl/102/486/s1913/a/1], Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 3016][/us/stat/106/3016]; amended [Pub. Repeal was executed to this section, which is in part VI of subchapter B of chapter 1, to reflect the probable intent of Congress, notwithstanding directory language of [Pub.  __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Dec. 19, 2014, subject to a savings provision, see [section 221(b) of Pub. L. 113–295][/us/pl/113/295/s221/b], set out as an Effective Date of 2014 Amendment note under [section 1 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s1].
