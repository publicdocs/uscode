## § 5244. Cross references

    (1) For provisions authorizing the withdrawal from the bonded premises of a distilled spirits plant of denatured distilled spirits, see section 5214(a)(1).

    (2) For provisions requiring a permit to procure specially denatured distilled spirits, see section 5271.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201][/us/pl/85/859/s201], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1370][/us/stat/72/1370].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 5244, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 647][/us/stat/68A/647], related to withdrawal of spirits from bonded warehouse on determination of tax, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859]. See [section 5213 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s5213].
