## § 5216. Regulation of operations

    For general provisions relating to operations on bonded premises see part I of this subchapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II][/us/pl/85/859/tII], § 201, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1364][/us/stat/72/1364].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 5216, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 640][/us/stat/68A/640], related to “mash, wort and vinegar; vinegar factories”, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859]. See sections 5178(c)(4), 5222(a)(1), (2)(D), (d), 5501, 5502(a), 5503, 5504(a), (b), 5505(a), (c) and 5601(a)(7), (8), (9)(A) of this title.

    A prior section 5217, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 641][/us/stat/68A/641]; [July 11, 1956, ch. 573][/us/act/1956-07-11/ch573], § 1, [70 Stat. 530][/us/stat/70/530]; July 11, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–517][/us/pl/85/517], [72 Stat. 357][/us/stat/72/357], related to national emergency transfers, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859].
