## § 5560. Other provisions applicable

    All provision of subtitle F, insofar as applicable and not inconsistent with the provisions of this subtitle, are hereby extended to and made a part of this subtitle.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II][/us/pl/85/859/tII], § 201, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1396][/us/stat/72/1396].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those comprising this section were contained in prior section 5557, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 681][/us/stat/68A/681], prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859].
