## § 5411. Use of brewery

    The brewery shall be used under regulations prescribed by the Secretary only for the purpose of producing, packaging, and storing beer, cereal beverages containing less than one-half of 1 percent of alcohol by volume, vitamins, ice, malt, malt sirup, and other byproducts and of soft drinks; for the purpose of processing spent grain, carbon dioxide, and yeast; and for such other purposes as the Secretary by regulation may find will not jeopardize the revenue.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201][/us/pl/85/859/s201], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1389][/us/stat/72/1389]; amended [Pub. L. 91–673, § 3(c)][/us/pl/91/673/s3/c], Jan. 12, 1971, [84 Stat. 2057][/us/stat/84/2057]; [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)][/us/pl/94/455/s1906/b/13/A], Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 5411, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 675][/us/stat/68A/675], consisted of provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859].

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455] struck out “or his delegate” after “Secretary” wherever appearing.

    1971—[Pub. L. 91–673][/us/pl/91/673] struck out requirement of separate facilities for bottling of beer and cereal beverages and permitted use of brewery for packaging and storing beer and other cereal beverages.

 __Effective Date of 1971 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 91–673][/us/pl/91/673] effective on first day of first calendar month which begins more than 90 days after Jan. 12, 1971, see [section 5 of Pub. L. 91–673][/us/pl/91/673/s5], set out as a note under [section 5056 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s5056].

 __Effective Date__
