## § 5357. Premises

    Bonded wine cellar premises, including noncontiguous portions thereof, shall be so located, constructed, and equipped, as to afford adequate protection to the revenue, as regulations prescribed by the Secretary may provide.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201][/us/pl/85/859/s201], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1379][/us/stat/72/1379]; amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)][/us/pl/94/455/s1906/b/13/A], Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 5357, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 664][/us/stat/68A/664], consisted of provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859].

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455] struck out “or his delegate” after “Secretary”.
