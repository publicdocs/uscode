## \[§ 5233. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–39, title VIII, § 807(a)(34)][/us/pl/96/39/s807/a/34], July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 286][/us/stat/93/286]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201][/us/pl/85/859/s201], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1366][/us/stat/72/1366]; amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455], §§ 1905(a)(16), 1906(b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1820][/us/stat/90/1820], 1834, related to the bottling of distilled spirits in bond.

    A prior section 5233, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 644][/us/stat/68A/644], made a cross reference provision to establishment of bottling in bond department, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859].

    Provisions similar to those comprising subsecs. (a) to (d) and (e)(1) of section 5233 added by [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201][/us/pl/85/859/s201], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1366][/us/stat/72/1366], were contained in prior section 5243(a) to (c), (g), [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 645][/us/stat/68A/645], as amended by [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 206(c)][/us/pl/85/859/s206/c], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1431][/us/stat/72/1431].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1980, see [section 810 of Pub. L. 96–39][/us/pl/96/39/s810], set out as an Effective Date of 1979 Amendment note under [section 5001 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s5001].
