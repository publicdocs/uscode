## § 5045. Cross references

    For provisions relating to the establishment and operation of wineries, see subchapter F, and for penalties pertaining to wine, see subchapter J.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201][/us/pl/85/859/s201], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1333][/us/stat/72/1333].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 5045, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 611][/us/stat/68A/611], consisted of provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859].
