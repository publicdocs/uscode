## \[§ 5009. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–39, title VIII, § 807(a)(7)][/us/pl/96/39/s807/a/7], July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 281][/us/stat/93/281]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201][/us/pl/85/859/s201], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1327][/us/stat/72/1327]; amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455], §§ 1905(a)(3), 1906(b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1818][/us/stat/90/1818], 1834, related to drawback on exportation of distilled spirits in casks or packages.

    A prior section 5009, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 603][/us/stat/68A/603], consisted of provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859]. See sections 5205(c)(1), (f), (i)(4) and 5206(c) of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1980, see [section 810 of Pub. L. 96–39][/us/pl/96/39/s810], set out as an Effective Date of 1979 Amendment note under [section 5001 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s5001].
