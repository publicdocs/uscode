## § 5067. Cross reference

    For general administrative provisions applicable to the assessment, collection, refund, etc., of taxes, see subtitle F.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II][/us/pl/85/859/tII], § 201, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1338][/us/stat/72/1338], § 5066; renumbered § 5067, [Pub. L. 91–659][/us/pl/91/659], § 3(a), Jan. 8, 1971, [84 Stat. 1965][/us/stat/84/1965].)
