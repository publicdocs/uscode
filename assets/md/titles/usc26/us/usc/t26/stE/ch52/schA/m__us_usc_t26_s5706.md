## § 5706. Drawback of tax

    There shall be an allowance of drawback of tax paid on tobacco products and cigarette papers and tubes, when shipped from the United States, in accordance with such regulations and upon the filing of such bond as the Secretary shall prescribe.

([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 709][/us/stat/68A/709]; [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 202][/us/pl/85/859/s202], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1419][/us/stat/72/1419]; [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)][/us/pl/94/455/s1906/b/13/A], Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455] struck out “or his delegate” after “Secretary”.

    1958—[Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859] substituted “tobacco products and cigarette papers and tubes” for “articles”.

 __Effective Date of 1958 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859] effective Sept. 3, 1958, see [section 210(a)(1) of Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859/s210/a/1], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 5001 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s5001].
