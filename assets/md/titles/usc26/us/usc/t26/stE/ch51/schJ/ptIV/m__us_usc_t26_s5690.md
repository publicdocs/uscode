## § 5690. Definition of the term “person”

    The term “person”, as used in this subchapter, includes an officer or employee of a corporation or a member or employee of a partnership, who as such officer, employee, or member is under a duty to perform the act in respect of which the violation occurs.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201][/us/pl/85/859/s201], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1413][/us/stat/72/1413].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 5690, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 702][/us/stat/68A/702], consisted of provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859].
