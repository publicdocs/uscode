## § 5213. Withdrawal of distilled spirits from bonded premises on determination of tax

    Subject to the provisions of section 5173, distilled spirits may be withdrawn from the bonded premises of a distilled spirits plant on payment or determination of tax thereon, in approved containers, under such regulations as the Secretary shall prescribe.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II][/us/pl/85/859/tII], § 201, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1362][/us/stat/72/1362]; amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1906(b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834]; [Pub. L. 96–39, title VIII][/us/pl/96/39/tVIII], § 807(a)(27), July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 285][/us/stat/93/285].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 5213, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 639][/us/stat/68A/639], related to return of materials used in the manufacture of distilled spirits, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859]. See sections 5002(a)(6) and 5291 of this title.

    Provisions similar to those comprising this section were contained in prior sections 5194(a), (e) and 5244, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 634][/us/stat/68A/634], 647, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859][/us/pl/85/859].

 __Amendments__ 

    1979—[Pub. L. 96–39][/us/pl/96/39] substituted “Subject to the provisions of section 5173” for “On application to the Secretary and subject to the provisions of section 5174(a)”.

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455] struck out “or his delegate” after “Secretary”.

 __Effective Date of 1979 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 96–39][/us/pl/96/39] effective Jan. 1, 1980, see [section 810 of Pub. L. 96–39][/us/pl/96/39/s810], set out as a note under [section 5001 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s5001].
