## § 3128. Short title

    This chapter may be cited as the “Federal Insurance Contributions Act.”

([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 429][/us/stat/68A/429], § 3125; renumbered § 3126, [Pub. L. 86–778, title I][/us/pl/86/778/tI], § 103(q)(1), Sept. 13, 1960, [74 Stat. 939][/us/stat/74/939]; renumbered § 3127, [Pub. L. 99–509, title IX][/us/pl/99/509/tIX], § 9002(a)(1), Oct. 21, 1986, [100 Stat. 1970][/us/stat/100/1970]; renumbered § 3128, [Pub. L. 100–647, title VIII][/us/pl/100/647/tVIII], § 8007(a)(1), Nov. 10, 1988, [102 Stat. 3781][/us/stat/102/3781].)

 __Effective Date of 1986 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 99–509][/us/pl/99/509], except as otherwise provided, effective with respect to payments due with respect to wages paid after Dec. 31, 1986, including wages paid after such date by a State (or political subdivision thereof) that modified its agreement pursuant to [section 418(e)(2) of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s418/e/2], The Public Health and Welfare, see [section 9002(d) of Pub. L. 99–509][/us/pl/99/509/s9002/d], set out as a note under [section 418 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s418].
