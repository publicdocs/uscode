 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior subchapter B consisted of sections 4111 to 4113, 4121, and 4131 of this title.

    Section 4111, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 485][/us/stat/68A/485]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–859, title I, § 111(a)][/us/pl/85/859/s111/a], [72 Stat. 1277][/us/stat/72/1277], imposed a manufacturers excise tax of 5 percent on household type refrigerators, quick freeze or frozen storage units, or combinations, and a tax of 10 percent on self-contained air-conditioning units, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–44, title II, § 203][/us/pl/89/44/s203], June 21, 1965, [79 Stat. 139][/us/stat/79/139], applicable with respect to articles sold on or after June 22, 1956.

    Section 4112, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 485][/us/stat/68A/485]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 805, § 1(e)][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch805/s1/e], [69 Stat. 689][/us/stat/69/689], defined refrigerator components, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 85–859, title I, § 111(b)(1)][/us/pl/85/859/s111/b/1], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1277][/us/stat/72/1277], effective the first day of the first calendar quarter beginning more than 60 days after Sept. 2, 1958.

    Section 4113, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 485][/us/stat/68A/485], related to exemptions for manufacturers of refrigerator components, prior to repeal by [act Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 805, § 1(d)][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch805/s1/d], [69 Stat. 689][/us/stat/69/689], effective on the first day of the first month beginning more than 10 days after Aug. 11, 1955.

    Section 4121, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 486][/us/stat/68A/486]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–859, title I, § 112][/us/pl/85/859/s112], [72 Stat. 1277][/us/stat/72/1277], imposed a 5 percent tax on electric, gas, and oil household appliances and their accessories, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–44, title II, § 203][/us/pl/89/44/s203], June 21, 1965, [79 Stat. 139][/us/stat/79/139], applicable with respect to articles sold on or after June 22, 1965.

    Section 4131, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 486][/us/stat/68A/486], imposed a 10 percent tax on electric light bulbs and tubes, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–44, title II, § 203][/us/pl/89/44/s203], June 21, 1965, [79 Stat. 139][/us/stat/79/139], applicable with respect to articles sold on or after Jan. 1, 1965.
