## \[§§ 4294, 4295. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1904(a)(10)][/us/pl/94/455/s1904/a/10], (11), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1812][/us/stat/90/1812]\]

    Section 4294, added [Pub. L. 85–859, title I, § 135(a)][/us/pl/85/859/s135/a], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1292][/us/stat/72/1292]; amended [Pub. L. 86–344, § 2(d)][/us/pl/86/344/s2/d], Sept. 21, 1959, [73 Stat. 618][/us/stat/73/618]; [Pub. L. 91–72, title I, § 101(j)(28)][/us/pl/91/72/s101/j/28], Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 529][/us/stat/83/529]; [Pub. L. 91–258, title II, § 205(a)(4)][/us/pl/91/258/s205/a/4], May 21, 1970, [84 Stat. 241][/us/stat/84/241], provided an exemption from tax for services and facilities furnished to a nonprofit educational organization and defined “nonprofit educational organization”.

    Section 4295, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 511][/us/stat/68A/511], § 4295, formerly § 4294, renumbered Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–859, title I, § 135(a)][/us/pl/85/859/s135/a], [72 Stat. 1292][/us/stat/72/1292], related to a cross reference to general administrative provisions.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective on first day of first month which begins more than 90 days after Oct. 4, 1976, see [section 1904(d) of Pub. L. 94–455][/us/pl/94/455/s1904/d], set out as an Effective Date of 1976 Amendment note under [section 4041 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s4041].
