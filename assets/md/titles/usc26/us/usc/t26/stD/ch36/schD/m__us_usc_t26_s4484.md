## § 4484. Cross references

    (1) For penalties and administrative provisions applicable to this subchapter, see subtitle F.

    (2) For exemption for uses by Indian tribal governments (or their subdivisions), see section 7871.

(Added [June 29, 1956, ch. 462][/us/act/1956-06-29/ch462], title II, § 206(a), [70 Stat. 391][/us/stat/70/391]; amended [Pub. L. 97–473, title II, § 202(b)(10)][/us/pl/97/473/s202/b/10], Jan. 14, 1983, [96 Stat. 2610][/us/stat/96/2610].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1983—[Pub. L. 97–473][/us/pl/97/473] designated existing provisions as par. (1) and added par. (2).

 __Effective Date of 1983 Amendment__ 

    For effective date of amendment by [Pub. L. 97–473][/us/pl/97/473], see [section 204(5) of Pub. L. 97–473][/us/pl/97/473/s204/5], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 7871 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s7871].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective June 29, 1956, see section 211 of act June 29, 1956, set out as an Effective Date of 1956 Amendment note under [section 4041 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s4041].
