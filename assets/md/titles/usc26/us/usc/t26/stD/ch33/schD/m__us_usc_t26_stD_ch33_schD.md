#### \[Subchapter D—Repealed\]

\[§§ 4286, 4287. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 89–44, title III, § 304__  __][/us/pl/89/44/s304]__  __,__  __June 21, 1965__  __,__  __[__  __79 Stat. 148__  __][/us/stat/79/148]__  __\]__ 

    Section 4286, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 510][/us/stat/68A/510], imposed a tax equivalent to 10 percent of the amount collected for the use of safety deposit boxes.

    Section 4287, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 510][/us/stat/68A/510], defined safety deposit box.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

[Pub. L. 89–44, title VII, § 701(b)(4)][/us/pl/89/44/s701/b/4], June 21, 1965, [79 Stat. 157][/us/stat/79/157], provided that: 

> “The amendments made by section 304 \[repealing these sections\] shall apply with respect to use periods beginning on or after July 1, 1965.”
