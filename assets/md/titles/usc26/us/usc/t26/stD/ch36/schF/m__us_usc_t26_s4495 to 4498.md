## \[§§ 4495 to 4498. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–34, title XIV, § 1432(b)(1)][/us/pl/105/34/s1432/b/1], Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 1050][/us/stat/111/1050]\]

    Section 4495, added [Pub. L. 96–283, title IV, § 402(a)][/us/pl/96/283/s402/a], June 28, 1980, [94 Stat. 582][/us/stat/94/582], provided for imposition of tax on removal of hard mineral resource from deep seabed.

    Section 4496, added [Pub. L. 96–283, title IV, § 402(a)][/us/pl/96/283/s402/a], June 28, 1980, [94 Stat. 583][/us/stat/94/583], defined terms for purposes of this subchapter.

    Section 4497, added [Pub. L. 96–283, title IV, § 402(a)][/us/pl/96/283/s402/a], June 28, 1980, [94 Stat. 583][/us/stat/94/583]; amended [Pub. L. 99–514, title XV, § 1511(c)(7)][/us/pl/99/514/s1511/c/7], Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2745][/us/stat/100/2745], related to imputed values for commercially recoverable metals and minerals and provided for suspension of tax on minerals held for later processing.

    Section 4498, added [Pub. L. 96–283, title IV, § 402(a)][/us/pl/96/283/s402/a], June 28, 1980, [94 Stat. 584][/us/stat/94/584], provided for termination of tax imposed by section 4495.
