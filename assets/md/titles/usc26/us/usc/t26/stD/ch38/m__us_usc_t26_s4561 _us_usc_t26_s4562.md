###### \[§§ 4561, 4562. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–456, title III][/us/pl/87/456/tIII], § 302(d), May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 77][/us/stat/76/77]\]

    Sections, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 543][/us/stat/68A/543], imposed a tax on animal oils imported into the United States.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective with respect to articles entered, or withdrawn from warehouse, for consumption on or after Aug. 31, 1963, see [section 501(a) of Pub. L. 87–456][/us/pl/87/456/s501/a], title V, May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 78][/us/stat/76/78].
