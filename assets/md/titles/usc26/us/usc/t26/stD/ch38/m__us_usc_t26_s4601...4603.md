## \[§§ 4601 to 4603. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–456, title III][/us/pl/87/456/tIII], § 302(d), May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 77][/us/stat/76/77]\]

    Section 4601, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 548][/us/stat/68A/548]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–859, title I][/us/pl/85/859/tI], § 119(b)(4), [72 Stat. 1286][/us/stat/72/1286], related to applicability of certain tariff provisions.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective with respect to articles entered, or withdrawn from warehouse, for consumption on or after Aug. 31, 1963, see [section 501(a) of Pub. L. 87–456][/us/pl/87/456/s501/a], title V, May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 78][/us/stat/76/78].
