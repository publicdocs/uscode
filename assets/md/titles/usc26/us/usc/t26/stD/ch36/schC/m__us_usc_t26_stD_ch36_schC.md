#### \[Subchapter C—Repealed\]

\[§§ 4471 to 4474. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 89–44, title IV, § 404__  __][/us/pl/89/44/s404]__  __,__  __June 21, 1965__  __,__  __[__  __79 Stat. 149__  __][/us/stat/79/149]__  __\]__ 

    Section 4471, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 532][/us/stat/68A/532], imposed a $20 annual tax upon bowling alleys, billiard tables, and pool tables to be paid by operators of bowling alleys, billiard rooms, and pool rooms.

    Section 4472, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 532][/us/stat/68A/532], defined bowling alley, billiard room, and pool room.

    Section 4473, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 532][/us/stat/68A/532]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–859, title I, § 153(a)][/us/pl/85/859/s153/a], [72 Stat. 1305][/us/stat/72/1305], granted exemptions for hospitals, the armed forces, and certain non-profit and governmental organizations.

    Section 4474, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 532][/us/stat/68A/532], made cross references to chapter 40 and subtitle F for penalties and administrative provisions.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal applicable on and after July 1, 1965, see [section 701(c)(2) of Pub. L. 89–44][/us/pl/89/44/s701/c/2], set out in part as an Effective Date of 1965 Amendment note under [section 4402 of this title][/us/usc/t26/s4402].
