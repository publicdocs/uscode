 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior subchapter B, consisted of sections 4461 to 4464 of this title, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 95–600, title V][/us/pl/95/600/tV], § 521(b), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2884][/us/stat/92/2884], applicable with respect to years beginning after June 30, 1980.

    Section 4461, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 531][/us/stat/68A/531]; Sept. 21, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–344][/us/pl/86/344], § 6(a), [73 Stat. 620][/us/stat/73/620]; June 21, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–44, title IV][/us/pl/89/44/tIV], § 403(a), [79 Stat. 148][/us/stat/79/148], imposed a special tax on persons who maintained for use or permitted use of coin-operated gaming devices and provided an exception from such tax.

    Section 4462, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 531][/us/stat/68A/531]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–859, title I][/us/pl/85/859/tI], § 152(a), [72 Stat. 1304][/us/stat/72/1304]; June 21, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–44, title IV][/us/pl/89/44/tIV], § 403(b), [79 Stat. 149][/us/stat/79/149]; Oct. 4, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–455, title XII][/us/pl/94/455/tXII], § 1208(b), [90 Stat. 1709][/us/stat/90/1709], defined coin-operated gaming devices.

    Section 4463, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 531][/us/stat/68A/531], related to administrative provisions.

    Section 4464, added [Pub. L. 92–178, title IV][/us/pl/92/178/tIV], § 402(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 534][/us/stat/85/534], and amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX][/us/pl/94/455/tXIX], § 1906(b)(13)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834][/us/stat/90/1834]; [Pub. L. 95–600, title V][/us/pl/95/600/tV], § 521(a), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2884][/us/stat/92/2884], related to credit for State-imposed taxes.
