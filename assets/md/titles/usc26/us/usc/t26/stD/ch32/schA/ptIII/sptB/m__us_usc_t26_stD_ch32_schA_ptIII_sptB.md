 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior subpart B, consisting of sections 4091 to 4093, related to taxation of aviation fuel, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 108–357, title VIII][/us/pl/108/357/tVIII], § 853(d)(1), (e), Oct. 22, 2004, [118 Stat. 1612][/us/stat/118/1612], 1614, applicable to aviation-grade kerosene removed, entered, or sold after Dec. 31, 2004.

 __Amendments__ 

    2004—[Pub. L. 108–357, title III][/us/pl/108/357/tIII], § 303(b), title VIII, §§ 853(d)(1), (2)(T), 866(b), Oct. 22, 2004, [118 Stat. 1466][/us/stat/118/1466], 1612, 1614, 1622, redesignated subpart C as B, substituted “Special Provisions Applicable to Fuels Tax” for “Special Provisions Applicable to Petroleum Products” in subpart heading, and added items 4104 and 4105.

    1990—[Pub. L. 101–508, title XI][/us/pl/101/508/tXI], § 11212(e)(3), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1388–432][/us/stat/104/1388-432], added item 4103.

    1986—[Pub. L. 99–514, title XVII][/us/pl/99/514/tXVII], § 1703(b)(2), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2776][/us/stat/100/2776], substituted “Registration and bond” for “Registration” in item 4101.

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–455, title XII][/us/pl/94/455/tXII], § 1202(c)(2), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1686][/us/stat/90/1686], substituted “Inspection of records by local officers” for “Inspection of records, returns, etc., by local officers” in item 4102.

    1965—[Pub. L. 89–44, title VIII][/us/pl/89/44/tVIII], § 802(b)(5), June 21, 1965, [79 Stat. 159][/us/stat/79/159], struck out “and bond” after “Registration” in item 4101.
