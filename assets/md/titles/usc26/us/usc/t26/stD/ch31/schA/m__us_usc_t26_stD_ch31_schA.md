#### \[Subchapter A—Repealed\]

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    This subchapter consisted of part I with subparts A (§§ 4001–4004) and B (§§ 4006, 4007) and part II (§§ 4011, 4012), prior to being amended generally by [Pub. L. 103–66, title XIII][/us/pl/103/66/tXIII], § 13161(a), Aug. 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 449][/us/stat/107/449].

    Another prior subchapter A of chapter 31 was redesignated subchapter B by [Pub. L. 101–508, title XI][/us/pl/101/508/tXI], § 11221(a), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1388–438][/us/stat/104/1388-438].
