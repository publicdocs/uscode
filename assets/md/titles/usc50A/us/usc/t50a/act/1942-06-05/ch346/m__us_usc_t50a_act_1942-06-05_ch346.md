#### [ACT JUNE 5, 1942, CH. 346][/us/act/1942-06-05/ch346], [56 STAT. 323][/us/stat/56/323]

§§ 756 to 759. __Repealed.__  __[__  __July 25, 1947, ch. 327, § 1__  __][/us/act/1947-07-25/ch327/s1]__  __,__  __[__  __61 Stat. 449__  __][/us/stat/61/449]__ 

    Section 756, [act June 5, 1942, ch. 346, § 1][/us/act/1942-06-05/ch346/s1], [56 Stat. 323][/us/stat/56/323], provided for lease or sale of vacant public lands withdrawn or reserved under certain Executive Orders or within a grazing district for use in connection with manufacture of arms, etc.

    Section 757, [act June 5, 1942, ch. 346, § 2][/us/act/1942-06-05/ch346/s2], [56 Stat. 323][/us/stat/56/323], provided that lessee’s interest in public lands leased under sections 756 to 759 should be taxable by States and political subdivisions thereof.

    Section 758, [act June 5, 1942, ch. 346, § 3][/us/act/1942-06-05/ch346/s3], [56 Stat. 323][/us/stat/56/323], provided for rules and regulations.

    Section 759, [act June 5, 1942, ch. 346, § 4][/us/act/1942-06-05/ch346/s4], [56 Stat. 323][/us/stat/56/323], provided a termination date for sections 756 to 759 of this Appendix.
