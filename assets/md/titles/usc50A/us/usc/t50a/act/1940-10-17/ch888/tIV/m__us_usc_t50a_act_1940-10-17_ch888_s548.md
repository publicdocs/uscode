##### § 548. Regulations

    The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall prescribe regulations for the implementation of this title \[sections 541 to 549 of this Appendix\].

([Oct. 17, 1940, ch. 888][/us/act/1940-10-17/ch888], title IV, § 408, as added [Pub. L. 108–189, § 1][/us/pl/108/189/s1], Dec. 19, 2003, [117 Stat. 2854][/us/stat/117/2854].)
