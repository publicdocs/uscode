##### § 1941k. Prior disposal of facilities as unaffected

    The sales, leases, or other dispositions made prior to the enactment of this Act \[Aug. 7, 1953\], pursuant to section 9(b) of the Rubber Act of 1948, as amended \[section 1928(b) of this Appendix\], shall not be affected by this Act \[sections 1941 to 1941y of this Appendix\].

([Aug. 7, 1953, ch. 338, § 13][/us/act/1953-08-07/ch338/s13], [67 Stat. 413][/us/stat/67/413].)
