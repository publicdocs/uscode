§§ 891, 892. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Section 891, acts [July 12, 1950, ch. 460, § 1][/us/act/1950-07-12/ch460/s1], [64 Stat. 336][/us/stat/64/336]; [Mar. 23, 1953, ch. 9, § 1][/us/act/1953-03-23/ch9/s1], [67 Stat. 7][/us/stat/67/7], which authorized free postage to Armed Forces personnel in specified areas, expired on June 30, 1955, pursuant to section 892 of this Appendix.
