#### [ACT MAR. 15, 1949, CH. 19][/us/act/1949-03-15/ch19], [63 STAT. 12][/us/stat/63/12]

§§ 2041 to 2045. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 2041 to 2045 were omitted pursuant to section 2042 of this Appendix.

    Section 2041, [act Mar. 15, 1949, ch. 19, § 1][/us/act/1949-03-15/ch19/s1], [63 Stat. 12][/us/stat/63/12], related to adjustment and settlement of claims.

    Section 2042, [act Mar. 15, 1949, ch. 19, § 2][/us/act/1949-03-15/ch19/s2], [63 Stat. 13][/us/stat/63/13], related to time limitation for presentment of claims under sections 2041 to 2045 of this Appendix and required presentment within one year of Mar. 15, 1949.

    Section 2043, [act Mar. 15, 1949, ch. 19, § 3][/us/act/1949-03-15/ch19/s3], [63 Stat. 13][/us/stat/63/13], related to finality and conclusiveness of Attorney General’s decisions.

    Section 2044, [act Mar. 15, 1949, ch. 19, § 4][/us/act/1949-03-15/ch19/s4], [63 Stat. 13][/us/stat/63/13], related to claims in excess of one thousand dollars.

    Section 2045, [act Mar. 15, 1949, ch. 19, § 5][/us/act/1949-03-15/ch19/s5], [63 Stat. 13][/us/stat/63/13], related to authorization of appropriations for use under sections 2041 to 2045 of this Appendix.
