#### [ACT OCT. 16, 1941, CH. 445][/us/act/1941-10-16/ch445], [55 STAT. 742][/us/stat/55/742]

§§ 721 to 724. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Section 721, acts [Oct. 16, 1941, ch. 445, § 1][/us/act/1941-10-16/ch445/s1], [55 Stat. 742][/us/stat/55/742]; [Mar. 27, 1942, ch. 199][/us/act/1942-03-27/ch199], title VI, §§ 601, 602, [56 Stat. 181][/us/stat/56/181]; [June 30, 1943, ch. 181, § 1][/us/act/1943-06-30/ch181/s1], [57 Stat. 271][/us/stat/57/271]; [June 28, 1944, ch. 307, § 1][/us/act/1944-06-28/ch307/s1], [58 Stat. 624][/us/stat/58/624]; [June 30, 1945, ch. 208, § 1][/us/act/1945-06-30/ch208/s1], [59 Stat. 271][/us/stat/59/271], authorized President, not later than June 30, 1946, to requisition certain military materials needed for defense and provided for payment of just compensation which might be recovered by suits against United States. Authority to requisition under Defense Production Act of 1950 was contained in former section 2081 of this Appendix.

    Section 722, acts [Oct. 16, 1941, ch. 445, § 2][/us/act/1941-10-16/ch445/s2], [55 Stat. 742][/us/stat/55/742]; [June 30, 1943, ch. 181, § 2][/us/act/1943-06-30/ch181/s2], [57 Stat. 271][/us/stat/57/271]; [June 28, 1944, ch. 307, § 2][/us/act/1944-06-28/ch307/s2], [58 Stat. 624][/us/stat/58/624]; [June 30, 1945, ch. 208, § 2][/us/act/1945-06-30/ch208/s2], [59 Stat. 271][/us/stat/59/271], provided for return of property acquired under sections 721 to 724 to the owner not later than Dec. 31, 1946, if owner desired it and paid fair value thereof.

    Section 723, [act Oct. 16, 1941, ch. 445, § 3][/us/act/1941-10-16/ch445/s3], [55 Stat. 743][/us/stat/55/743], related to reports to Congress.

    Section 724, [act Oct. 16, 1941, ch. 445, § 4][/us/act/1941-10-16/ch445/s4], [55 Stat. 743][/us/stat/55/743], related to administration of sections 721 to 724 of this Appendix.
