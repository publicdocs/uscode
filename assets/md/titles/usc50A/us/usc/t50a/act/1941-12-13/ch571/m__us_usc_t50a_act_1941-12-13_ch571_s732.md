##### § 732. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act Dec. 13, 1941, ch. 571, § 2][/us/act/1941-12-13/ch571/s2], [55 Stat. 800][/us/stat/55/800], which extended service periods during any war, was transferred to section 16a of former Title 10, Army and Air Force. Section 16a of former Title 10 was repealed by [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53], [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641], and reenacted as sections 3492 and 8492 of Title 10, Armed Forces. Said sections 3492 and 8492 of Title 10 were repealed by [Pub. L. 90–235, § 1(a)(2)][/us/pl/90/235/s1/a/2], Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 753][/us/stat/81/753]. See [section 671a of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s671a].
