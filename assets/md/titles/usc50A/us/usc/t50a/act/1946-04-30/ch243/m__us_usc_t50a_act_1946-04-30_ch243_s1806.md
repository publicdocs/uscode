##### § 1806. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 243][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch243], title VI, § 601, [60 Stat. 140][/us/stat/60/140], required an executive agreement to be entered into between the Presidents of the United States and the Philippines as a condition precedent for certain private-aid payments under sections 1751 to 1763 of this Appendix which have been omitted from the Code.
