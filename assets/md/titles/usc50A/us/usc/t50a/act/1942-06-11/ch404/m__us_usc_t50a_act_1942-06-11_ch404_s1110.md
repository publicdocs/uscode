##### § 1110. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act June 11, 1942, ch. 404, § 10][/us/act/1942-06-11/ch404/s10], [56 Stat. 356][/us/stat/56/356], which related to insured banks as depositaries of public money, duties and security of these banks, prohibition of discrimination between banks, and to repeal of inconsistent laws, was transferred to [section 265 of Title 12][/us/usc/t12/s265], Banks and Banking.
