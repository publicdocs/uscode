##### § 2163. Territorial application of Act

    The provisions of this Act \[sections 2061 to 2170, 2171, and 2172 of this Appendix\] shall be applicable to the United States, its Territories and possessions, and the District of Columbia.

([Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932][/us/act/1950-09-08/ch932], title VII, § 713, [64 Stat. 821][/us/stat/64/821].)

 __Termination Date__
