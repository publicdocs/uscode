##### TITLE III—MISCELLANEOUS

§§ 941 to 946. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 941 to 946 were omitted as terminated.

    Section 941, [act Jan. 30, 1942, ch. 26][/us/act/1942-01-30/ch26], title III, § 301, [56 Stat. 36][/us/stat/56/36], related to transmission of quarterly reports to Congress.

    Section 942, acts [Jan. 30, 1942, ch. 26][/us/act/1942-01-30/ch26], title III, § 302, [56 Stat. 36][/us/stat/56/36]; [June 30, 1945, ch. 214, § 5(b)][/us/act/1945-06-30/ch214/s5/b], [59 Stat. 308][/us/stat/59/308], contained definitions of terms used in the Emergency Price Control Act of 1942, section 901 et seq. of this Appendix.

    Section 943, [act Jan. 30, 1942, ch. 26][/us/act/1942-01-30/ch26], title III, § 303, [56 Stat. 37][/us/stat/56/37], contained separability provisions.

    Section 944, [act Jan. 30, 1942, ch. 26][/us/act/1942-01-30/ch26], title III, § 304, [56 Stat. 37][/us/stat/56/37], authorized appropriations.

    Section 945, [act Jan. 30, 1942, ch. 26][/us/act/1942-01-30/ch26], title III, § 305, [56 Stat. 37][/us/stat/56/37], related to application of existing law.

    Section 946, [act Jan. 30, 1942, ch. 26][/us/act/1942-01-30/ch26], title III, § 306, [56 Stat. 37][/us/stat/56/37], provided as short title for section 901 et seq. of this Appendix, “Emergency Price Control Act of 1942”.
