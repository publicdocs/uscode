#### [ACT OCT. 10, 1940, CH. 836][/us/act/1940-10-10/ch836], [54 STAT. 1090][/us/stat/54/1090]

§§ 711 to 713. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 711 to 713 were omitted as terminated pursuant to section 713 of this Appendix.

    Section 711, acts [Oct. 10, 1940, ch. 836, § 1][/us/act/1940-10-10/ch836/s1], [54 Stat. 1090][/us/stat/54/1090]; [July 2, 1942, ch. 471, § 1][/us/act/1942-07-02/ch471/s1], [56 Stat. 467][/us/stat/56/467], authorized President to requisition certain military and naval materials, the exportation of which had been prohibited or curtailed by law. Authority to requisition under Defense Production Act of 1950 was contained in former section 2081 of this Appendix.

    Section 712, [act Oct. 10, 1940, ch. 836, § 2][/us/act/1940-10-10/ch836/s2], [54 Stat. 1091][/us/stat/54/1091], provided for compensation for materials requisitioned under sections 711 to 713 of this Appendix. Compensation for property requisitioned under Defense Production Act of 1950 was provided for in former section 2081 of this Appendix.

    Section 713, acts [Oct. 10, 1940, ch. 836, § 3][/us/act/1940-10-10/ch836/s3], [54 Stat. 1091][/us/stat/54/1091]; [July 2, 1942, ch. 471, § 2][/us/act/1942-07-02/ch471/s2], [56 Stat. 468][/us/stat/56/468]; [June 28, 1944, ch. 308][/us/act/1944-06-28/ch308], [58 Stat. 625][/us/stat/58/625]; [June 30, 1945, ch. 207][/us/act/1945-06-30/ch207], [59 Stat. 270][/us/stat/59/270], provided that authority granted in sections 711 to 713 of this Appendix should remain in force only until June 30, 1946.

 __Executive Order No. 8567__ 

    Ex. Ord. No. 8567, Oct. 15, 1940, 5 F.R. 4121, provided for administration of sections 711 to 713 of this Appendix.
