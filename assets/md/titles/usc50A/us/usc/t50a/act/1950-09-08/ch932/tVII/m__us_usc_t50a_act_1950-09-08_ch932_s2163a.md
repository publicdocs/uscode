##### § 2163a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 656][/us/stat/80/656]

    Section, [act Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932][/us/act/1950-09-08/ch932], title VII, § 714, as added [July 31, 1951, ch. 275][/us/act/1951-07-31/ch275], title I, § 110(a) [65 Stat. 139][/us/stat/65/139]; amended [June 30, 1952, ch. 530][/us/act/1952-06-30/ch530], title I, § 121(a) [66 Stat. 306][/us/stat/66/306]; [June 30, 1953, ch. 171, § 10][/us/act/1953-06-30/ch171/s10], [67 Stat. 131][/us/stat/67/131], created Small Defense Plants Administration, and related generally to encouragement and aid to small-business concerns with respect to defense production. It terminated at close of July 31, 1953, by terms of section 2166(a) of this Appendix. For provisions relating to aid to small business, see section 631 et seq. of Title 15, Commerce and Trade.

 __Revolving Fund Continuation__ 

    [Act July 16, 1953, ch. 204, § 1][/us/act/1953-07-16/ch204/s1], [67 Stat. 176][/us/stat/67/176], provided in part that the revolving fund established under the Small Defense Plants Administration was to remain available through July 31, 1953, for payment of obligations and direct costs under contracts entered into during fiscal year 1953.
