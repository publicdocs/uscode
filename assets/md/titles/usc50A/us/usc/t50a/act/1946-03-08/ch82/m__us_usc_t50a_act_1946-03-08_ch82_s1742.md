##### § 1742. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–412, title V, § 501(g)][/us/pl/94/412/s501/g], Sept. 14, 1976, [90 Stat. 1258][/us/stat/90/1258]

    Section, acts [Mar. 8, 1946, ch. 82, § 9][/us/act/1946-03-08/ch82/s9], [60 Stat. 46][/us/stat/60/46]; [Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 1013][/us/act/1956-08-06/ch1013], [70 Stat. 1068][/us/stat/70/1068], made provision for price adjustments on prior sales of surplus war-built vessels to citizens.
