#### [ACT DEC. 3, 1945, CH. 511][/us/act/1945-12-03/ch511], [59 STAT. 590][/us/stat/59/590]

§§ 1731, 1732. __Transferred__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Section 1731, [act Dec. 3, 1945, ch. 511, § 1][/us/act/1945-12-03/ch511/s1], [59 Stat. 590][/us/stat/59/590], which related to an exemption from requirements as to number, position, etc., of lights for vessels of special construction, was transferred to [section 360 of Title 33][/us/usc/t33/s360], Navigation and Navigable Waters, and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(a)][/us/pl/96/591/s8/a], Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435][/us/stat/94/3435].

    Section 1732, [act Dec. 3, 1945, ch. 511, § 2][/us/act/1945-12-03/ch511/s2], [59 Stat. 591][/us/stat/59/591], which related to publication of notice of findings or certification and character and number of lights, was transferred to [section 360a of Title 33][/us/usc/t33/s360a], Navigation and Navigable Waters, and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(a)][/us/pl/96/591/s8/a], Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435][/us/stat/94/3435].
