#### [ACT JULY 10, 1952, CH. 630][/us/act/1952-07-10/ch630], TITLE VII, [66 STAT. 538][/us/stat/66/538]

§§ 2351 to 2356. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 88–132, § 9(b)__  __][/us/pl/88/132/s9/b]__  __,__  __Oct. 2, 1963__  __,__  __[__  __77 Stat. 216__  __][/us/stat/77/216]__ 

    Section 2351, [act July 10, 1952, ch. 630][/us/act/1952-07-10/ch630], title VII, § 702, [66 Stat. 538][/us/stat/66/538], defined terms for purposes of sections 2351 to 2356 of this Appendix.

    Section 2352, [act July 10, 1952, ch. 630][/us/act/1952-07-10/ch630], title VII, § 703, [66 Stat. 539][/us/stat/66/539], related to conditions governing payment of combat pay to members and former members of the uniformed services.

    Section 2353, [act July 10, 1952, ch. 630][/us/act/1952-07-10/ch630], title VII, § 704, [66 Stat. 539][/us/stat/66/539], related to members of the uniformed services killed in action, injured in action, and missing in action.

    Section 2354, [act July 10, 1952, ch. 630][/us/act/1952-07-10/ch630], title VII, § 705, [66 Stat. 539][/us/stat/66/539], related to limitations on combat payments.

    Section 2355, [act July 10, 1952, ch. 630][/us/act/1952-07-10/ch630], title VII, § 706, [66 Stat. 539][/us/stat/66/539], related to regulations for the administration of sections 2351 to 2356 of this Appendix.

    Section 2356, [act July 10, 1952, ch. 630][/us/act/1952-07-10/ch630], title VII, § 707, [66 Stat. 539][/us/stat/66/539], related to determinations of fact, conclusiveness of findings, and availability of appropriations.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1963, see [section 14 of Pub. L. 88–132][/us/pl/88/132/s14], set out as an Effective Date of 1963 Amendment note under [section 201 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s201], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.
