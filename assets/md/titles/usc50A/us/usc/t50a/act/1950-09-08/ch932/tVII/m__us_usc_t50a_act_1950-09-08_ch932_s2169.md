    Section, [act Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932][/us/act/1950-09-08/ch932], title VII, § 720, as added Sept. 30, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–426, § 5][/us/pl/93/426/s5], [88 Stat. 1167][/us/stat/88/1167]; amended Mar. 21, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–9][/us/pl/94/9], [89 Stat. 15][/us/stat/89/15]; Aug. 5, 1975, [Pub.
