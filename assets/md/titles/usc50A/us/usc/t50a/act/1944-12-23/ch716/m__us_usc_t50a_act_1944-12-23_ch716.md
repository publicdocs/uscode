#### [ACT DEC. 23, 1944, CH. 716][/us/act/1944-12-23/ch716], [58 STAT. 921][/us/stat/58/921]

§§ 1705 to 1707. __Transferred__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1705 to 1707, acts [Dec. 23, 1944, ch. 716][/us/act/1944-12-23/ch716], §§ 1, 2, [58 Stat. 921][/us/stat/58/921]; [June 16, 1953, ch. 115][/us/act/1953-06-16/ch115], [67 Stat. 62][/us/stat/67/62], were transferred to sections 492a to 492c, respectively, of former Title 31, Money and Finance, and subsequently repealed by [section 5(b) of Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258/s5/b], Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1076][/us/stat/96/1076], the first section of which enacted Title 31. See [section 3342 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s3342].

    Section 1705 related to check cashing and exchange transactions authorized for official and accommodation purposes.

    Section 1706 related to coverage into Treasury of any gains realized from the operations conducted pursuant to sections 1705 to 1707 of this Appendix.

    Section 1707 related to the issuance of regulation by Secretary of the Treasury to carry out purposes of sections 1705 to 1707 of this Appendix.

 __GENERAL OF MARINE CORPS__
