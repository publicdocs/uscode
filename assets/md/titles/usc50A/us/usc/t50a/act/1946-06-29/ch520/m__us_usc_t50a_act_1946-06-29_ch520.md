#### [ACT JUNE 29, 1946, CH. 520][/us/act/1946-06-29/ch520], [60 STAT. 339][/us/stat/60/339]

§§ 1851 to 1855. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1851 to 1855 were omitted as terminated pursuant to section 1851 of this Appendix.

    Section 1851, acts [June 29, 1946, ch. 520, § 1][/us/act/1946-06-29/ch520/s1], [60 Stat. 339][/us/stat/60/339]; [June 28, 1947, ch. 160][/us/act/1947-06-28/ch160], [61 Stat. 190][/us/stat/61/190]; [Mar. 24, 1948, ch. 141][/us/act/1948-03-24/ch141], §§ 1, 2, [62 Stat. 84][/us/stat/62/84], related to the admission of alien fiancees or fiances of members of the armed forces, on or before Dec. 31, 1948.

    Section 1852, [act June 29, 1946, ch. 520, § 2][/us/act/1946-06-29/ch520/s2], [60 Stat. 340][/us/stat/60/340], related to deportation upon nonoccurrence of marriage.

    Section 1853, [act June 29, 1946, ch. 520, § 3][/us/act/1946-06-29/ch520/s3], [60 Stat. 340][/us/stat/60/340], related to regulations governing functions of diplomatic or consular officers.

    Section 1854, [act June 29, 1946, ch. 520, § 4][/us/act/1946-06-29/ch520/s4], [60 Stat. 340][/us/stat/60/340], related to regulations governing functions of the Immigration and Naturalization Service.

    Section 1855, [act June 29, 1946, ch. 520, § 5][/us/act/1946-06-29/ch520/s5], [60 Stat. 340][/us/stat/60/340], defined the period of World War II.

 __Completion of Processing of Visa Cases__ 

    [Act Apr. 21, 1949, ch. 85][/us/act/1949-04-21/ch85], [63 Stat. 56][/us/stat/63/56], provided for the completion of processing of cases of the alien fiances or fiancees of citizens of the United States pending on Dec. 31, 1948 under sections 1851 to 1855 of this Appendix.
