#### [ACT JUNE 25, 1946, CH. 487][/us/act/1946-06-25/ch487], [60 STAT. 308][/us/stat/60/308]

§§ 1841 to 1845. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1841 to 1845 terminated June 25, 1948, pursuant to section 5 of act June 25, 1946.

    Section 1841, [act June 25, 1946, ch. 487, § 1][/us/act/1946-06-25/ch487/s1], [60 Stat. 308][/us/stat/60/308], related to use of vessels as targets for atomic weapons.

    Section 1842, [act June 25, 1946, ch. 487, § 2][/us/act/1946-06-25/ch487/s2], [60 Stat. 308][/us/stat/60/308], related to disposal of vessels after experiments.

    Section 1843, [act June 25, 1946, ch. 487, § 3][/us/act/1946-06-25/ch487/s3], [60 Stat. 308][/us/stat/60/308], related to number of combatant vessels to be employed.

    Section 1844, [act June 25, 1946, ch. 487, § 4][/us/act/1946-06-25/ch487/s4], [60 Stat. 309][/us/stat/60/309], related to safeguarding of information on results of tests.

    Section 1845, [act June 25, 1946, ch. 487, § 4A][/us/act/1946-06-25/ch487/s4A], [60 Stat. 309][/us/stat/60/309], related to appointment and compensation of an advisory board.
