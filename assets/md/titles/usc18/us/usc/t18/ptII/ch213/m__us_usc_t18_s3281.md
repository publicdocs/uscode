## § 3281. Capital offenses

    An indictment for any offense punishable by death may be found at any time without limitation.

([June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], [62 Stat. 827][/us/stat/62/827]; [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXXIII][/us/pl/103/322/tXXXIII], § 330004(16), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2142][/us/stat/108/2142].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on title 18, U.S.C., 1940 ed., §§ 581a, 581b ([Aug. 4, 1939, ch. 419][/us/act/1939-08-04/ch419], §§ 1, 2, [53 Stat. 1198][/us/stat/53/1198]).

    Sections 581a and 581b of title 18, U.S.C., 1940 ed., were consolidated into this section without change of substance.

 __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–322][/us/pl/103/322] struck out before period at end “except for offenses barred by the provisions of law existing on August 4, 1939”.
