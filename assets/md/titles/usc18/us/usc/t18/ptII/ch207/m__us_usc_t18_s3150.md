## § 3150. Applicability to a case removed from a State court

    The provisions of this chapter apply to a criminal case removed to a Federal court from a State court.

(Added [Pub. L. 98–473, title II][/us/pl/98/473/tII], § 203(a), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 1984][/us/stat/98/1984].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 3150, added [Pub. L. 89–465][/us/pl/89/465], § 3(a), June 22, 1966, [80 Stat. 216][/us/stat/80/216], related to penalties for failure to appear, prior to repeal in the revision of this chapter by [section 203(a) of Pub. L. 98–473][/us/pl/98/473/s203/a].
