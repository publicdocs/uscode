## § 711a. “Woodsy Owl” character, name, or slogan

    Whoever, except as authorized under rules and regulations issued by the Secretary, knowingly and for profit manufactures, reproduces, or uses the character “Woodsy Owl”, the name “Woodsy Owl”, or the associated slogan, “Give a Hoot, Don’t Pollute” shall be fined under this title or imprisoned not more than six months, or both.

(Added [Pub. L. 93–318][/us/pl/93/318], § 6, June 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 245][/us/stat/88/245]; amended [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXXIII][/us/pl/103/322/tXXXIII], § 330016(1)(E), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2146][/us/stat/108/2146].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–322][/us/pl/103/322] substituted “fined under this title” for “fined not more than $250”.

 __Description of “Woodsy Owl” Character__ 

    For description of character of “Woodsy Owl” as referred to in this section, see [section 580p of Title 16][/us/usc/t16/s580p], Conservation.
