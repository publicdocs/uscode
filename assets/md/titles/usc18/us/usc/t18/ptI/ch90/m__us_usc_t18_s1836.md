## § 1836. Civil proceedings to enjoin violations

    (a) The Attorney General may, in a civil action, obtain appropriate injunctive relief against any violation of this chapter.

    (b) The district courts of the United States shall have exclusive original jurisdiction of civil actions under this section.

(Added [Pub. L. 104–294, title I, § 101(a)][/us/pl/104/294/s101/a], Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3490][/us/stat/110/3490]; amended [Pub. L. 107–273, div. B, title IV, § 4002(e)(9)][/us/pl/107/273/s4002/e/9], Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1810][/us/stat/116/1810].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2002—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 107–273, § 4002(e)(9)(A)][/us/pl/107/273/s4002/e/9/A], substituted “this chapter” for “this section”.
