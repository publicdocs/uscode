## § 1793. Trespass on Bureau of Prisons reservations and land

    Whoever, without lawful authority or permission, goes upon a reservation, land, or a facility of the Bureau of Prisons shall be fined under this title or imprisoned not more than six months, or both.

(Added [Pub. L. 99–646][/us/pl/99/646], § 64(a), Nov. 10, 1986, [100 Stat. 3614][/us/stat/100/3614]; amended [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXXIII][/us/pl/103/322/tXXXIII], § 330016(1)(G), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2147][/us/stat/108/2147].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–322][/us/pl/103/322] substituted “fined under this title” for “fined not more than $500”.
