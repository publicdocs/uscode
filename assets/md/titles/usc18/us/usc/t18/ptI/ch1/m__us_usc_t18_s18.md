## § 18. Organization defined

    As used in this title, the term “organization” means a person other than an individual.

(Added [Pub. L. 99–646][/us/pl/99/646], § 38(a), Nov. 10, 1986, [100 Stat. 3599][/us/stat/100/3599]; amended [Pub. L. 100–185][/us/pl/100/185], § 4(c), Dec. 11, 1987, [101 Stat. 1279][/us/stat/101/1279]; [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII][/us/pl/100/690/tVII], § 7012, Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4395][/us/stat/102/4395].)

 __Amendments__
