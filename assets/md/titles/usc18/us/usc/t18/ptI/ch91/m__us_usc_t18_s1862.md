## \[§ 1862. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–200, § 3(c)][/us/pl/95/200/s3/c], Nov. 23, 1977, [91 Stat. 1428][/us/stat/91/1428]\]

    Section, [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], [62 Stat. 789][/us/stat/62/789], imposed a fine of not more than $500 or imprisonment of not more than six months as the penalty for knowingly trespassing upon the reserve known as the Bull Run National Forest in the Cascade Mountains. See note set out under [section 482b of Title 16][/us/usc/t16/s482b], Conservation, for the remainder of [Pub. L. 95–200][/us/pl/95/200], including savings provisions therein, which in addition to repealing this section created the Bull Run Watershed Management Unit, Mount Hood National Forest.
