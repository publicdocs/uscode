## § 1429. Penalties for neglect or refusal to answer subpena

    Any person who has been subpenaed under the provisions of subsection (d) of section 336 of the Immigration and Nationality Act to appear at the final hearing of an application for naturalization, and who shall neglect or refuse to so appear and to testify, if in the power of such person to do so, shall be fined under this title or imprisoned not more than five years, or both.

(Added [June 27, 1952, ch. 477][/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477], title IV, § 402(b), [66 Stat. 276][/us/stat/66/276]; amended [Pub. L. 97–116][/us/pl/97/116], § 18(u)(1), Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1621][/us/stat/95/1621]; [Pub. L. 101–649, title IV][/us/pl/101/649/tIV], § 407(c)(21), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 5041][/us/stat/104/5041]; [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXXIII][/us/pl/103/322/tXXXIII], § 330016(1)(K), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2147][/us/stat/108/2147].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Subsection (d) of section 336 of the Immigration and Nationality Act, referred to in text, is classified to [section 1447(d) of Title 8][/us/usc/t8/s1447/d], Aliens and Nationality.

 __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–322][/us/pl/103/322] substituted “fined under this title” for “fined not more than $5,000”.

    1990—[Pub. L. 101–649][/us/pl/101/649] substituted “an application” for “a petition”.

    1981—[Pub. L. 97–116][/us/pl/97/116] substituted “subsection (d)” for “subsection (e)”.

 __Effective Date of 1990 Amendment; Savings Provisions__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 101–649][/us/pl/101/649] effective Nov. 29, 1990, with general savings provisions, see section 408(a)(3) and (d) of [Pub. L. 101–649][/us/pl/101/649], set out as a note under [section 1421 of Title 8][/us/usc/t8/s1421], Aliens and Nationality.

 __Effective Date of 1981 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 97–116][/us/pl/97/116] effective Dec. 29, 1981, see [section 21(a) of Pub. L. 97–116][/us/pl/97/116/s21/a], set out as a note under [section 1101 of Title 8][/us/usc/t8/s1101], Aliens and Nationality.
