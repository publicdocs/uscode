## \[§§ 2031, 2032. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–646, § 87(c)(1)][/us/pl/99/646/s87/c/1], Nov. 10, 1986, [100 Stat. 3623][/us/stat/100/3623]; [Pub. L. 99–654, § 3(a)(1)][/us/pl/99/654/s3/a/1], Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3663][/us/stat/100/3663]\]

    Section 2031, [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], [62 Stat. 795][/us/stat/62/795], prescribed penalties for commission of rape within special maritime and territorial jurisdiction.

    Section 2032, [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], [62 Stat. 795][/us/stat/62/795], prescribed penalties for carnal knowledge of female under 16 within special maritime and territorial jurisdiction.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 99–646][/us/pl/99/646] and [Pub. L. 99–654][/us/pl/99/654] effective, respectively, 30 days after Nov. 10, 1986, and 30 days after Nov. 14, 1986, see [section 87 of Pub. L. 99–646][/us/pl/99/646/s87] and [section 4 of Pub. L. 99–654][/us/pl/99/654/s4], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 2241 of this title][/us/usc/t18/s2241].
