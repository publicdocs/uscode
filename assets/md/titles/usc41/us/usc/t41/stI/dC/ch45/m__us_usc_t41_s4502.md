## § 4502. Payment

    (a)  __Basis for Payment__  __.—__ 

    When practicable, payments under [section 4501 of this title][/us/usc/t41/s4501] shall be made on any of the following bases:

        (1) Performance measured by objective, quantifiable methods such as delivery of acceptable items, work measurement, or statistical process controls.

        (2) Accomplishment of events defined in the program management plan.

        (3) Other quantifiable measures of results.

    (b)  __Payment Amount__  __.—__ 

    Payments made under [section 4501 of this title][/us/usc/t41/s4501] may not exceed the unpaid contract price.

([Pub. L. 111–350, § 3][/us/pl/111/350/s3], Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3791][/us/stat/124/3791].)
