## § 102. Commercial component

    In this subtitle, the term “commercial component” means a component that is a commercial item.

([Pub. L. 111–350, § 3][/us/pl/111/350/s3], Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3678][/us/stat/124/3678].)
