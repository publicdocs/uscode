## § 134. Simplified acquisition threshold

    In division B, the term “simplified acquisition threshold” means $100,000.

([Pub. L. 111–350][/us/pl/111/350], § 3, Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3682][/us/stat/124/3682].)
