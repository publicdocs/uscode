## § 133. Executive agency

In division B, the term “executive agency” means—

    (1) an executive department specified in [section 101 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s101];

    (2) a military department specified in [section 102 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s102];

    (3) an independent establishment as defined in [section 104(1) of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s104/1]; and

    (4) a wholly owned Government corporation fully subject to chapter 91 of title 31.

([Pub. L. 111–350, § 3][/us/pl/111/350/s3], Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3682][/us/stat/124/3682].)
