## § 1009. Personnel not to receive fees

    An officer or employee of the Postal Service may not receive any fee or perquisite from a patron of the Postal Service on account of the duties performed by virtue of his appointment, except as authorized by law.

([Pub. L. 91–375][/us/pl/91/375], Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 733][/us/stat/84/733].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective July 1, 1971, pursuant to Resolution No. 71–9 of the Board of Governors. See [section 15(a) of Pub. L. 91–375][/us/pl/91/375/s15/a], set out as a note preceding [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t39/s101].
