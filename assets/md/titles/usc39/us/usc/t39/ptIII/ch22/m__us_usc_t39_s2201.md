## § 2201. No postal equipment or supplies manufactured by convict labor

    Except as provided in chapter 307 of title 18, the Postal Service may not make a contract for the purchase of equipment or supplies to be manufactured by convict labor.

([Pub. L. 91–375][/us/pl/91/375], Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 742][/us/stat/84/742].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective July 1, 1971, pursuant to Resolution No. 71–9 of the Board of Governors. See [section 15(a) of Pub. L. 91–375][/us/pl/91/375/s15/a], set out as a note preceding [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t39/s101].
