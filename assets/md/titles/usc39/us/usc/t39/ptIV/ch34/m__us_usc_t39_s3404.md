## § 3404. Unsealed letters sent by blind or physically handicapped persons

    Unsealed letters sent by a blind person or a person having a physical impairment, as described in [section 3403(a)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t39/s3403/a/1], in raised characters or sightsaving type, or in the form of sound recordings, may be mailed free of postage.

([Pub. L. 91–375][/us/pl/91/375], Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 758][/us/stat/84/758].)
