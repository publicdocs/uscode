#### SUBCHAPTER V—POSTAL SERVICES, COMPLAINTS, AND JUDICIAL REVIEW

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior subchapter V was redesignated subchapter VI of this chapter.

 __Amendments__ 

    2006—[Pub. L. 109–435, title II][/us/pl/109/435/tII], § 204(a)(1), Dec. 20, 2006, [120 Stat. 3210][/us/stat/120/3210], redesignated subchapter IV of this chapter as this subchapter and substituted “POSTAL SERVICES, COMPLAINTS, AND JUDICIAL REVIEW” for “POSTAL SERVICES AND COMPLAINTS” in subchapter heading.
