## § 5601. Sea post service

    The Postal Service may maintain sea post service on ocean vessels conveying mail to and from the United States.

([Pub. L. 91–375][/us/pl/91/375], Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 773][/us/stat/84/773].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Chapter effective July 1, 1971, pursuant to Resolution No. 71–9 of the Board of Governors. See [section 15(a) of Pub. L. 91–375][/us/pl/91/375/s15/a], set out as a note preceding [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t39/s101].
