## § 301. Freedom from tolls

    Except as provided in [section 129 of this title][/us/usc/t23/s129] with respect to certain toll bridges and toll tunnels, all highways constructed under the provisions of this title shall be free from tolls of all kinds.

([Pub. L. 85–767][/us/pl/85/767], Aug. 27, 1958, [72 Stat. 912][/us/stat/72/912].)
