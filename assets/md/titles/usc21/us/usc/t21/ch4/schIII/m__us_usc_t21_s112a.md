## § 112a. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act Feb. 7, 1928, ch. 30][/us/act/1928-02-07/ch30], [45 Stat. 59][/us/stat/45/59], amended acts [May 29, 1884, ch. 60][/us/act/1884-05-29/ch60], [23 Stat. 31][/us/stat/23/31]; [Feb. 2, 1903, ch. 349][/us/act/1903-02-02/ch349], [32 Stat. 791][/us/stat/32/791]; and [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1496][/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1496], [33 Stat. 1264][/us/stat/33/1264], to include live poultry in their provisions, added “and/or live poultry” following “live stock” wherever the term “live stock” appeared, and extended the penalties, terms, and provisions of said acts to live poultry. For complete classification of such acts to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 112 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s112] and Tables.
