## §§ 221 to 237. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section 221, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 1, [45 Stat. 1085][/us/stat/45/1085], defined “habit-forming narcotic drug”, “narcotic”, and “addict”. See [section 201 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s201], The Public Health and Welfare.

    Section 222, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 2, [45 Stat. 1085][/us/stat/45/1085], provided for narcotic farms.

    Section 222a, [act June 23, 1935, ch. 725][/us/act/1935-06-23/ch725], § 1, [49 Stat. 1840][/us/stat/49/1840], provided name for narcotic farm at Lexington, Ky.

    Section 222b, [act Mar. 28, 1938, ch. 55][/us/act/1938-03-28/ch55], § 1, [52 Stat. 134][/us/stat/52/134], provided name for narcotic farm at Fort Worth, Texas.

    Section 223, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 3, [45 Stat. 1085][/us/stat/45/1085]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, § 205(b), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1425][/us/stat/53/1425], provided for an annual estimate of expenses of maintenance of narcotic farms.

    Section 224, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 4, [45 Stat. 1086][/us/stat/45/1086], provided for construction of buildings for two of the narcotic farms.

    Section 225, acts [Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 5, [45 Stat. 1086][/us/stat/45/1086]; [June 14, 1930, ch. 488][/us/act/1930-06-14/ch488], § 4(a), [46 Stat. 586][/us/stat/46/586]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424][/us/stat/53/1424], provided for control and management of narcotic farms.

    Section 226, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 6, [45 Stat. 1086][/us/stat/45/1086]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424][/us/stat/53/1424], 1425, provided for care and treatment of addicts.

    Section 227, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 7, [45 Stat. 1086][/us/stat/45/1086], provided for transfer to and from farms of addicts who are prisoners.

    Section 228, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 8, [45 Stat. 1087][/us/stat/45/1087], provided that it was the duty of prosecuting officers to report convicted persons believed to be addicts.

    Section 229, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 9, [45 Stat. 1087][/us/stat/45/1087]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424][/us/stat/53/1424], 1425, provided for employment of addicts.

    Section 230, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 10, [45 Stat. 1087][/us/stat/45/1087], provided for parole of inmates.

    Section 231, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 11, [45 Stat. 1087][/us/stat/45/1087]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424][/us/stat/53/1424], 1425, provided for discharge of addicts.

    Section 232, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 12, [45 Stat. 1088][/us/stat/45/1088]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424][/us/stat/53/1424], 1425, provided for admission of voluntary patients.

    Section 233, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 13, [45 Stat. 1088][/us/stat/45/1088]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424][/us/stat/53/1424], 1425, provided for furnishing of gratuities and transportation to discharged convicts.

    Section 234, act. [Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 14, [45 Stat. 1089][/us/stat/45/1089]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424][/us/stat/53/1424], 1425, provided penalties for introduction of narcotic drugs into a narcotic farm.

    Section 235, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 15, [45 Stat. 1089][/us/stat/45/1089], provided penalties for escape of inmates.

    Section 236, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 16, [45 Stat. 1089][/us/stat/45/1089], provided penalties for procuring of escape by inmates.

    Section 237, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 82][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch82], § 17, [45 Stat. 1089][/us/stat/45/1089], provided for deportation of alien inmates who are entitled to a discharge from narcotic farms.
