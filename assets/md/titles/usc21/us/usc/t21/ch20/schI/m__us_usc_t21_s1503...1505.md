## §§ 1503 to 1505. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title I][/us/pl/100/690/tI], § 1009, Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4188][/us/stat/102/4188], as amended by [Pub. L. 105–20][/us/pl/105/20], § 2(b), June 27, 1997, [111 Stat. 234][/us/stat/111/234]

    Section 1503, [Pub. L. 100–690, title I][/us/pl/100/690/tI], § 1004, Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4184][/us/stat/102/4184], related to coordination between Office of National Drug Control Policy and executive branch departments and agencies. See [section 1704 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s1704].

    Section 1504, [Pub. L. 100–690, title I][/us/pl/100/690/tI], § 1005, Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4185][/us/stat/102/4185]; [Pub. L. 103–322, title IX][/us/pl/103/322/tIX], § 90203, Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1991][/us/stat/108/1991]; [Pub. L. 105–20][/us/pl/105/20], § 2(b), June 27, 1997, [111 Stat. 234][/us/stat/111/234], related to annual development and submission of National Drug Control Strategy by President to Congress. See [section 1705 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s1705].

    Section 1505, [Pub. L. 100–690, title I][/us/pl/100/690/tI], § 1008, formerly § 1003A, as added [Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title X][/us/pl/101/510/dA/tX], § 1011, Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1633][/us/stat/104/1633]; renumbered § 1008 and amended [Pub. L. 103–322, title IX][/us/pl/103/322/tIX], § 90204(a), (b), (c)(2), (3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1993][/us/stat/108/1993], 1994, established Counter-Drug Technology Assessment Center within Office of National Drug Control Policy. See [section 1707 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s1707].

    A prior section 1505, [Pub. L. 100–690, title I][/us/pl/100/690/tI], § 1008, Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4188][/us/stat/102/4188], provided for an executive reorganization study and report to Congress and the President no later than Jan. 15, 1990, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–322][/us/pl/103/322], § 90204(c)(1).

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 30, 1997, see [section 1009 of Pub. L. 100–690][/us/pl/100/690/s1009], as amended, which was formerly classified to [section 1506 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s1506].
