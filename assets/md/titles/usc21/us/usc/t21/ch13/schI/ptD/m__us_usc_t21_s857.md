## § 857. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–647, title XXIV, § 2401(d)][/us/pl/101/647/s2401/d], Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4859][/us/stat/104/4859]

    Section, [Pub. L. 99–570, title I, § 1822][/us/pl/99/570/s1822], Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–51][/us/stat/100/3207-51]; [Pub. L. 100–690, title VI, § 6485][/us/pl/100/690/s6485], Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4384][/us/stat/102/4384]; [Pub. L. 101–647, title XXIV, § 2401(b)][/us/pl/101/647/s2401/b], Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4859][/us/stat/104/4859], related to interstate and foreign sale and transportation of drug paraphernalia.

    Subsec. (a), which related to unlawful acts, was repealed.

    Subsecs. (b) to (f) were redesignated as subsecs. (b) to (f) of section 422 of the Controlled Substances Act by [section 2401(b) of Pub. L. 101–647][/us/pl/101/647/s2401/b] and transferred to section 863(b) to (f) of this title.

 __Effective Date__ 

    [Pub. L. 99–570, title I, § 1823][/us/pl/99/570/s1823], Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–52][/us/stat/100/3207-52], which provided that subtitle O (§§ 1821–1823) of title I of [Pub. L. 99–570][/us/pl/99/570], enacting this section and provisions set out as a note under [section 801 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s801], was to become effective 90 days after Oct. 27, 1986, was repealed by [Pub. L. 101–647, title XXIV, § 2401(d)][/us/pl/101/647/s2401/d], Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4859][/us/stat/104/4859].
