## § 14a. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act June 30, 1906, ch. 3915, § 10A][/us/act/1906-06-30/ch3915/s10A], as added [June 22, 1934, ch. 712][/us/act/1934-06-22/ch712], [48 Stat. 1204][/us/stat/48/1204], and amended, which related to examination of sea food on request of packer, marking of food with results, fees, and penalties, was successively renumbered section 702A and then 706 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act by [act July 12, 1943, ch. 221][/us/act/1943-07-12/ch221], title II, § 201, [57 Stat. 500][/us/stat/57/500], and by [Pub. L. 102–571, title I, § 106(3)][/us/pl/102/571/s106/3], Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4498][/us/stat/106/4498], and was successively classified to section 372a and then 376 of this title.
