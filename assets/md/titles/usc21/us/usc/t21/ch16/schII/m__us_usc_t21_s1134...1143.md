## §§ 1134 to 1143. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–255, title I][/us/pl/92/255/tI], § 104, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 67][/us/stat/86/67]

    Section 1134, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 224, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 71][/us/stat/86/71], provided for establishment of research and development programs by Director.

    Section 1135, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 225, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72][/us/stat/86/72], provided for establishment of a single non-Federal share requirement.

    Section 1136, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 226, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72][/us/stat/86/72], provided for Director to make drug prevention function recommendations to President and to consult with responsible agencies with respect to drug prevention functions.

    Section 1137, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 227, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72][/us/stat/86/72], provided for resolution of agency conflicts with regard to drug prevention functions.

    Section 1138, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 228, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72][/us/stat/86/72], provided for communications and liaison with respect to drug prevention functions by one of Assistant Directors.

    Section 1139, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 229, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72][/us/stat/86/72], provided, with respect to drug prevention functions, coordination of State and local governments, availability of information and other services and activities.

    Section 1140, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 230, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 73][/us/stat/86/73], provided for a management oversight review by a Federal officer.

    Section 1141, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 231, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 73][/us/stat/86/73], provided for convening of a council of officials of Federal agencies responsible for drug prevention functions by Director.

    Section 1142, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 232, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 73][/us/stat/86/73], provided for designation of the Director to represent Government in international negotiations related to drug prevention functions.

    Section 1143, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II][/us/pl/92/255/tII], § 233, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 73][/us/stat/86/73], provided for an annual written report to the President by the Director.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Section 1104 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s1104], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–237][/us/pl/94/237], § 4(c)(1), Mar. 19, 1976, [90 Stat. 244][/us/stat/90/244], provided for repeal of sections effective June 30, 1975.
