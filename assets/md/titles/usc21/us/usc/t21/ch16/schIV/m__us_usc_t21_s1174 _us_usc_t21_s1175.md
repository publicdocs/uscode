###### §§ 1174, 1175. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 1174, [Pub. L. 92–255, title IV][/us/pl/92/255/tIV], § 407, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 78][/us/stat/86/78]; [Pub. L. 94–237][/us/pl/94/237], § 6(a), Mar. 19, 1976, [90 Stat. 244][/us/stat/90/244]; [Pub. L. 94–581, title I][/us/pl/94/581/tI], § 111(c)(2), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2852][/us/stat/90/2852], which prohibited discrimination against drug abusers by general hospitals, was redesignated section 526 of the Public Health Service Act by [Pub. L. 98–24][/us/pl/98/24], § 2(b)(16)(B), Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 182][/us/stat/97/182], and is classified to [section 290ee–2 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s290ee–2], The Public Health and Welfare.

L. 93–282, title III][/us/pl/93/282/tIII], § 303(a), (b), May 14, 1974, [88 Stat. 137][/us/stat/88/137], 138; [Pub. L. 94–237][/us/pl/94/237], § 4(c)(5)(A), (B), Mar. 19, 1976, [90 Stat. 244][/us/stat/90/244]; [Pub. L. 94–581, title I][/us/pl/94/581/tI], § 111(c)(3), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2852][/us/stat/90/2852]; [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX][/us/pl/97/35/tIX], § 973(d), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 598][/us/stat/95/598], which related to confidentiality of patients’ records, was redesignated section 527 of the Public Health Service Act by [Pub. L. 98–24][/us/pl/98/24], § 2(b)(16)(B), Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 182][/us/stat/97/182], and is classified to [section 290ee–3 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s290ee–3].
