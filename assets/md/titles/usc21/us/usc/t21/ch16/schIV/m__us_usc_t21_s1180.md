## § 1180. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 92–255, title IV][/us/pl/92/255/tIV], § 413, Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 84][/us/stat/86/84]; [Pub. L. 96–181][/us/pl/96/181], § 8(a), (b)(1), Jan. 2, 1980, [93 Stat. 1313][/us/stat/93/1313], 1314; [Pub. L. 98–24][/us/pl/98/24], § 2(b)(16)(A), Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 182][/us/stat/97/182], and is classified to [section 290ee–1 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s290ee–1], The Public Health and Welfare.
