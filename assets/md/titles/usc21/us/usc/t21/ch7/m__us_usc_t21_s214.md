## § 214. Previous laws unaffected

    Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as modifying or revoking any of the provisions of sections 191 to 193  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title.

([Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 74, § 13][/us/act/1915-03-03/ch74/s13], [38 Stat. 822][/us/stat/38/822].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Sections 191 to 193 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by [Pub. L. 91–513, title III, § 1101(a)(1)][/us/pl/91/513/s1101/a/1], Oct. 27, 1970, [84 Stat. 1291][/us/stat/84/1291]. See section 801 et seq. of this title.
