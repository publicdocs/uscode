## § 215. “Consul” defined

    The word “consul” as used in this chapter shall mean the consular officer in charge of the district concerned.

([Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 74][/us/act/1915-03-03/ch74], § 12, [38 Stat. 822][/us/stat/38/822].)
