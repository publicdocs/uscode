## § 360ddd–2. Inapplicability of drug fees to designated medical gases

    A designated medical gas, alone or in combination with another designated gas or gases (as medically appropriate) deemed under [section 360ddd–1 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s360ddd–1] to have in effect an approved application shall not be assessed fees under [section 379h(a) of this title][/us/usc/t21/s379h/a] on the basis of such deemed approval.

([June 25, 1938, ch. 675, § 577][/us/act/1938-06-25/ch675/s577], as added [Pub. L. 112–144, title XI, § 1111][/us/pl/112/144/s1111], July 9, 2012, [126 Stat. 1111][/us/stat/126/1111].)
