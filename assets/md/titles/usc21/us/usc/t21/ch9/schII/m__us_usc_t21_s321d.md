## § 321d. Market names for catfish and ginseng

    (a) __Catfish labeling__ 

        (1) __In general__ 

        Notwithstanding any other provision of law, for purposes of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ([21 U.S.C. 301][/us/usc/t21/s301] et seq.)—

            (A) the term “catfish” may only be considered to be a common or usual name (or part thereof) for fish classified within the family Ictaluridae; and

            (B) only labeling or advertising for fish classified within that family may include the term “catfish”.

        (2) __Omitted__ 

    (b) __Ginseng labeling__ 

        (1) __In general__ 

        Notwithstanding any other provision of law, for purposes of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ([21 U.S.C. 301][/us/usc/t21/s301] et seq.)—

            (A) the term “ginseng” may only be considered to be a common or usual name (or part thereof) for any herb or herbal ingredient derived from a plant classified within the genus Panax; and

            (B) only labeling or advertising for herbs or herbal ingredients classified within that genus may include the term “ginseng”.

        (2) __Omitted__ 

([Pub. L. 107–171, title X, § 10806][/us/pl/107/171/s10806], May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 526][/us/stat/116/526].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act, referred to in subsecs. (a)(1), (b)(1), is [act June 25, 1938, ch. 675][/us/act/1938-06-25/ch675], [52 Stat. 1040][/us/stat/52/1040], as amended, which is classified generally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 301 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s301] and Tables.

 __Codification__ 

    Section is comprised of [section 10806 of Pub. L. 107–171][/us/pl/107/171/s10806]. Subsecs. (a)(2) and (b)(2) of [section 10806 of Pub. L. 107–171][/us/pl/107/171/s10806] amended [section 343 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s343].

    Section was enacted as part of the Farm Security and Rural Investment Act of 2002, and not as part of Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act which comprises this chapter.
