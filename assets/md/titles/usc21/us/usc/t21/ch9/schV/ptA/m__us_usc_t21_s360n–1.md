## § 360n–1. Priority review for qualified infectious disease products

    If the Secretary designates a drug under [section 355f(d) of this title][/us/usc/t21/s355f/d] as a qualified infectious disease product, then the Secretary shall give priority review to any application submitted for approval for such drug under [section 355(b) of this title][/us/usc/t21/s355/b].

([June 25, 1938, ch. 675, § 524A][/us/act/1938-06-25/ch675/s524A], as added [Pub. L. 112–144, title VIII, § 802(a)][/us/pl/112/144/s802/a], July 9, 2012, [126 Stat. 1079][/us/stat/126/1079].)

 __Effective Date__ 

[Pub. L. 112–144, title VIII, § 802(b)][/us/pl/112/144/s802/b], July 9, 2012, [126 Stat. 1079][/us/stat/126/1079], provided that: 

> “Section 524A of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act \[[21 U.S.C. 360n–1][/us/usc/t21/s360n–1]\], as added by subsection (a), applies only with respect to an application that is submitted under section 505(b) of such Act ([21 U.S.C. 355(b)][/us/usc/t21/s355/b]) on or after the date of the enactment of this Act \[July 9, 2012\].”
