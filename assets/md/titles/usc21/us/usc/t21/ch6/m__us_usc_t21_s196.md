## § 196. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section, [act June 14, 1930, ch. 488, § 4(b)][/us/act/1930-06-14/ch488/s4/b], (c), [46 Stat. 587][/us/stat/46/587]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424][/us/stat/53/1424], related to studies and investigations by Surgeon General of the Public Health Service. §§ 197, 198. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 91–513, title III, § 1101(a)(4)__  __][/us/pl/91/513/s1101/a/4]__  __,__  __Oct. 27, 1970__  __,__  __[__  __84 Stat. 1291__  __][/us/stat/84/1291]__ 

    Section 197, [act June 14, 1930, ch. 488, § 7][/us/act/1930-06-14/ch488/s7], [46 Stat. 587][/us/stat/46/587], directed Secretary of the Treasury to cooperate with Secretary of State in discharge of international obligations of United States concerning traffic in narcotic drugs.

    Section 198, acts [June 14, 1930, ch. 488, § 8][/us/act/1930-06-14/ch488/s8], [46 Stat. 587][/us/stat/46/587]; [July 18, 1956, ch. 629][/us/act/1956-07-18/ch629], title III, § 302, [70 Stat. 575][/us/stat/70/575], directed Secretary of the Treasury to cooperate with the several States in suppression of abuse of narcotic drugs in their respective jurisdictions.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective on first day of seventh calendar month that begins after Oct. 26, 1970, see [section 1105(a) of Pub. L. 91–513][/us/pl/91/513/s1105/a], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 951 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s951].

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Prosecutions for any violation of law occurring, and civil seizures or forfeitures and injunctive proceedings commenced, prior to the effective date of repeal of these sections by [section 1101 of Pub. L. 91–513][/us/pl/91/513/s1101] not to be affected or abated by reason thereof, see [section 1103 of Pub. L. 91–513][/us/pl/91/513/s1103], set out as a note under sections 171 to 174 of this title.

§§ 198a to 198c. __Transferred__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Section 198a, [act Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 800, § 1][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch800/s1], [69 Stat. 684][/us/stat/69/684], as amended, which related to the authority of Secretary of the Treasury to issue subpenas, administer oaths and compel attendance of witnesses for purpose of any investigation, was transferred to [section 967 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s967].

    Section 198b, [act Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 800, § 2][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch800/s2], [69 Stat. 685][/us/stat/69/685], which related to service of subpenas and proof of service, was transferred to [section 968 of this title][/us/usc/t21/s968].
