## §§ 200 to 200b. Repealed. [Nov. 2, 1951, ch. 666][/us/act/1951-11-02/ch666], § 5(2), [65 Stat. 769][/us/stat/65/769]

    Sections, acts [Aug. 12, 1937, ch. 598][/us/act/1937-08-12/ch598], §§ 1–3, [50 Stat. 627][/us/stat/50/627]; [July 1, 1944, ch. 377][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch377], § 9, [58 Stat. 721][/us/stat/58/721]; [Mar. 8, 1946, ch. 81][/us/act/1946-03-08/ch81], § 8, [60 Stat. 39][/us/stat/60/39], related to punishment for offenses after first offense. See section 801 et seq. of this title.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    [Act Nov. 2, 1951, ch. 666][/us/act/1951-11-02/ch666], § 6, [65 Stat. 769][/us/stat/65/769], provided that any rights or liabilities now existing under former sections 200 to 200b of this title should not be affected by their repeal.
