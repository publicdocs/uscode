## § 202c. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act June 26, 1936, ch. 830][/us/act/1936-06-26/ch830], title V, § 503, [49 Stat. 1965][/us/stat/49/1965], which provided for repeal of former laws, was to have taken effect when a majority of the members authorized to be appointed took office under [section 202b of this title][/us/usc/t27/s202b]. The members, however, were never appointed.
