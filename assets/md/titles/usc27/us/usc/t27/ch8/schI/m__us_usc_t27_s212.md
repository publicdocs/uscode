## § 212. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act June 26, 1936, ch. 830, title V][/us/act/1936-06-26/ch830/tV], § 504, [49 Stat. 1965][/us/stat/49/1965], proposed a transfer of the appropriations authorized for the Federal Alcohol Administration created by [section 202 of this title][/us/usc/t27/s202] to the Administration created by [section 202b of this title][/us/usc/t27/s202b]. It was to have taken effect when a majority of the members authorized to be appointed under [section 202b of this title][/us/usc/t27/s202b] took office. The members, however, were never appointed.
