###### §§ 202a, 202b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 649][/us/stat/80/649]

    Section 202a, [act June 26, 1936, ch. 830, title V][/us/act/1936-06-26/ch830/tV], § 501, [49 Stat. 1964][/us/stat/49/1964], established the Federal Alcohol Administration, and provided for appointment, duties, and pay of its employees.

    Section 202b, [act June 26, 1936, ch. 830, title V][/us/act/1936-06-26/ch830/tV], § 502, [49 Stat. 1964][/us/stat/49/1964], provided for appointment, pay, tenure, and powers of members of the Federal Alcohol Administration.
