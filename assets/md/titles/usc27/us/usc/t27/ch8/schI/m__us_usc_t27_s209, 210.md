## §§ 209, 210. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 209, acts [Aug. 29, 1935, ch. 814][/us/act/1935-08-29/ch814], title I, § 109, formerly § 9, [49 Stat. 987][/us/stat/49/987]; [June 26, 1936, ch. 830][/us/act/1936-06-26/ch830], title V, § 507, [49 Stat. 1966][/us/stat/49/1966]; renumbered title I, § 109, Nov. 18, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–690, title VIII, § 8001(a)(1)][/us/pl/100/690/s8001/a/1], (2), [102 Stat. 4517][/us/stat/102/4517], which related to disposal of forfeited alcoholic beverages, was superseded. See [section 5688 of Title 26][/us/usc/t26/s5688], Internal Revenue Code.

    Section 210, [act Aug. 29, 1935, ch. 814][/us/act/1935-08-29/ch814], title I, § 110, formerly § 10, [49 Stat. 987][/us/stat/49/987]; renumbered title I, § 110, Nov. 18, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–690, title VIII, § 8001(a)(1)][/us/pl/100/690/s8001/a/1], (2), [102 Stat. 4517][/us/stat/102/4517], abolished the Federal Alcohol Control Administration established by Ex. Ord. No. 6474, Dec. 4, 1933; Ex. Ord. No. 6576, Jan. 25, 1934; Ex. Ord. No. 6683, Apr. 19, 1934; Ex. Ord. No. 6788, June 30, 1934; Ex. Ord. No. 6829, Aug. 21, 1934, issued under provisions of [section 702 of Title 15][/us/usc/t15/s702], Commerce and Trade.
