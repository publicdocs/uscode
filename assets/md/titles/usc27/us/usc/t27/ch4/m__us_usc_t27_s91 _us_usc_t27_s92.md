###### §§ 91, 92. Repealed. [Aug. 27, 1935, ch. 740][/us/act/1935-08-27/ch740], title I, § 1, [49 Stat. 872][/us/stat/49/872]

    Section 91, acts [Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 473][/us/act/1929-03-02/ch473], § 1, [45 Stat. 1446][/us/stat/45/1446]; [Jan. 15, 1931, ch. 29][/us/act/1931-01-15/ch29], [46 Stat. 1036][/us/stat/46/1036], set forth maximum penalties that could be imposed in a criminal prosecution for illegal manufacture, sale, transportation, importation, or exportation of intoxicating liquor, as defined in [section 4 of this title][/us/usc/t27/s4].

    Section 92, [act Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 473][/us/act/1929-03-02/ch473], § 2, [45 Stat. 1446][/us/stat/45/1446], provided that [section 91 of this title][/us/usc/t27/s91] did not operate to repeal or eliminate any minimum penalty provided by this title for first or subsequent offense.
