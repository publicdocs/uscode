## § 927. Limitation on recourse

    The holder of a claim payable solely from special revenues of the debtor under applicable nonbankruptcy law shall not be treated as having recourse against the debtor on account of such claim pursuant to [section 1111(b) of this title][/us/usc/t11/s1111/b].

(Added [Pub. L. 100–597][/us/pl/100/597], § 7(2), Nov. 3, 1988, [102 Stat. 3029][/us/stat/102/3029].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 927 was renumbered [section 930 of this title][/us/usc/t11/s930].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Nov. 3, 1988, but not applicable to any case commenced under this title before that date, see [section 12 of Pub. L. 100–597][/us/pl/100/597/s12], set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t11/s101].
