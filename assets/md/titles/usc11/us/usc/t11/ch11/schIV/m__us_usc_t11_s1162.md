## § 1162. Definition

    In this subchapter, “Board” means the “Surface Transportation Board”.

(Added [Pub. L. 104–88, title III, § 302(1)][/us/pl/104/88/s302/1], Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 943][/us/stat/109/943].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 1162, [Pub. L. 95–598][/us/pl/95/598], Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2641][/us/stat/92/2641], defined “Commission”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–88, title III, § 302(1)][/us/pl/104/88/s302/1], Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 943][/us/stat/109/943].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Jan. 1, 1996, see [section 2 of Pub. L. 104–88][/us/pl/104/88/s2], set out as a note under [section 1301 of Title 49][/us/usc/t49/s1301], Transportation.
