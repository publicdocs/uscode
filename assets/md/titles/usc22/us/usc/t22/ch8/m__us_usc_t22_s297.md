## § 297. Acquisition of property by lease

    The authority granted to acquire sites and buildings by purchase or otherwise shall include authority to acquire leaseholds.

([May 7, 1926, ch. 250][/us/act/1926-05-07/ch250], § 6, [44 Stat. 405][/us/stat/44/405]; [June 19, 1952, ch. 446][/us/act/1952-06-19/ch446], § 4, [66 Stat. 140][/us/stat/66/140]; [Pub. L. 95–105, title I][/us/pl/95/105/tI], § 106(b), Aug. 17, 1977, [91 Stat. 845][/us/stat/91/845].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1977—[Pub. L. 95–105][/us/pl/95/105] struck out “of not less than ten years” after “acquire leaseholds”.

    1952—Act June 19, 1952, provided for leaseholds of not less than 10 years.
