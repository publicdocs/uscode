## §§ 1612 to 1612b. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections, [act Oct. 26, 1951, ch. 575, title II][/us/act/1951-10-26/ch575/tII], §§ 201–203, [65 Stat. 646][/us/stat/65/646], were superseded, effective October 1, 1979, by [section 4619(e) of Title 50][/us/usc/t50/s4619/e], War and National Defense. See chapter 56 (§ 4601 et seq.) of Title 50.

    Section 1612 related to regulation of exports other than war materials.

    Section 1612a related to negotiations with recipient countries for control of exports.

    Section 1612b related to termination of assistance upon determination by the President.
