## § 3513. Expiration of statutory authorities

    The authorities contained in this chapter shall expire on September 30, 1984.

([Pub. L. 96–53, title IV, § 414][/us/pl/96/53/s414], Aug. 14, 1979, [93 Stat. 377][/us/stat/93/377].)
