## § 1928c. Report to the Congress

    The United States Group of the NATO Parliamentary Assembly shall submit to the Congress a report for each fiscal year for which an appropriation is made, including its expenditures under such appropriation.

([July 11, 1956, ch. 562, § 3][/us/act/1956-07-11/ch562/s3], [70 Stat. 524][/us/stat/70/524]; [Pub. L. 90–137][/us/pl/90/137], pt. IV, § 401(a)(2), Nov. 14, 1967, [81 Stat. 463][/us/stat/81/463]; [Pub. L. 106–113, div. B, § 1000(a)(7)][/us/pl/106/113/s1000/a/7] \[div. A, title VII, § 701(b)(1)\], Nov. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 1536][/us/stat/113/1536], 1501A–459.)

 __Codification__ 

    This section was not enacted as part of the Mutual Security Act of 1954 which comprises this chapter.

 __Amendments__ 

    1999—[Pub. L. 106–113][/us/pl/106/113] substituted “NATO Parliamentary Assembly” for “North Atlantic Assembly”.

    1967—[Pub. L. 90–137][/us/pl/90/137] substituted “North Atlantic Assembly” for “North Atlantic Treaty Parliamentary Conference”.
