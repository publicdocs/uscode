###### §§ 1781, 1782. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. III, § 642(a)(2), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460][/us/stat/75/460]

    Section 1781, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937], ch. IV, § 521, [68 Stat. 855][/us/stat/68/855]; [July 18, 1956, ch. 627][/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627], § 10(a), [70 Stat. 560][/us/stat/70/560]; Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–141][/us/pl/85/141], § 10(a), [71 Stat. 363][/us/stat/71/363]; June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477][/us/pl/85/477], ch. V, § 501(24), (25), [72 Stat. 271][/us/stat/72/271], empowered the President to delegate his authority and provided for the promulgation of rules and regulations. See [section 2381 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2381].

    Section 1782, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937], ch. IV, § 522, [68 Stat. 855][/us/stat/68/855]; [July 18, 1956, ch. 627][/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627], § 10(b), [70 Stat. 560][/us/stat/70/560]; June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477][/us/pl/85/477], ch. V, § 501(26), [72 Stat. 272][/us/stat/72/272], related to allocation and transfer of funds among Government agencies, utilization of services and facilities, reimbursement for military assistance, commodities, services, and facilities, establishment of accounts, and to charge of expenses to appropriation. See [section 2392 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2392].
