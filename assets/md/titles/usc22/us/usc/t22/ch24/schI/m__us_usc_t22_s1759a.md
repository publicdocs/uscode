    Section, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937], ch. IV, § 549, as added [July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 1(d)][/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s1/d], [70 Stat. 565][/us/stat/70/565]; amended Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub.
