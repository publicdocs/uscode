## §§ 1750 to 1753a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. III, § 642(a)(2), (5), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460][/us/stat/75/460]

    Section 1750, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937, § 2][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937/s2], formerly ch. IV, § 549, as added [July 8, 1955, ch. 301, § 11][/us/act/1955-07-08/ch301/s11], [69 Stat. 289][/us/stat/69/289], renumbered and amended [July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 2][/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s2], [70 Stat. 555][/us/stat/70/555]; July 24, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–108, § 2][/us/pl/86/108/s2], [73 Stat. 246][/us/stat/73/246]; May 14, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–472, § 2][/us/pl/86/472/s2], [74 Stat. 134][/us/stat/74/134], stated the Congressional declaration of purpose for this chapter. See section 2151 et seq. of this title.

    Section 1750a, [Pub. L. 85–477][/us/pl/85/477], ch. V, § 503, June 30, 1958, [72 Stat. 275][/us/stat/72/275], related to strengthening cooperation in the Western Hemisphere.

    Section 1750b, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937], ch. IV, § 552, as added May 14, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–472][/us/pl/86/472], ch. IV, § 401(m), [74 Stat. 140][/us/stat/74/140], prohibited assistance to Cuba. See [section 2370 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2370].

    Section 1751, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937], ch. IV, § 545, [68 Stat. 862][/us/stat/68/862]; [July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 11(b)][/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s11/b], [70 Stat. 565][/us/stat/70/565]; June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477][/us/pl/85/477], ch. IV, § 401(i), ch. V, § 501(31)–(33), [72 Stat. 270][/us/stat/72/270], 272, defined terms used in this chapter. See [section 2403 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2403].

    Section 1752, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937], ch. IV, § 546, [68 Stat. 863][/us/stat/68/863]; Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–141, § 11(c)][/us/pl/85/141/s11/c], 71 Stat 365, related to construction and application of this chapter.

    Section 1753, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937], ch. IV, § 501, [68 Stat. 849][/us/stat/68/849]; [July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 9(a)][/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s9/a], [70 Stat. 560][/us/stat/70/560], authorized transferability of funds. See [section 2360 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2360].

    Section 1753a, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937], ch. IV, § 551, as added July 24, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–108][/us/pl/86/108], ch. IV, § 401(m), [73 Stat. 255][/us/stat/73/255]; amended Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–735, § 4][/us/pl/86/735/s4], [74 Stat. 870][/us/stat/74/870], provided for limitations on the use of the President’s special authority.

 __Transfer of Funds To Carry Out International Educational Exchange Activities__ 

    Section 13 of [act July 18, 1956, ch. 627][/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627], [70 Stat. 565][/us/stat/70/565], was repealed by [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. III, § 642(a)(4). Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460][/us/stat/75/460].
