## §§ 2051 to 2053. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. III, § 642(a)(7), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460][/us/stat/75/460]

    Sections, [Pub. L. 86–108][/us/pl/86/108], ch. VI, §§ 601–603, July 24, 1959, [73 Stat. 256][/us/stat/73/256], 257, which directed the Secretary of State to submit to Congress before Jan. 3, 1960, a plan and program for the establishment in Hawaii of an educational institution, for grants, fellowships, and other payments to outstanding scholars and to qualified students from the nations of the East and West.
