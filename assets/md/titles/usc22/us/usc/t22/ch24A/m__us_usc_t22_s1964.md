## § 1964. Report to Congress

    The President shall whenever appropriate report to the Congress his action hereunder.

([Pub. L. 85–7, § 5][/us/pl/85/7/s5], Mar. 9, 1957, [71 Stat. 6][/us/stat/71/6]; [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. IV, § 705, Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 463][/us/stat/75/463].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1961—[Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195] substituted “whenever appropriate” for “within the months of January and July of each year”.

 __Repeals__ 

    [Section 705 of Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195/s705], cited as a credit to this section, was repealed by [section 401 of Pub. L. 87–565][/us/pl/87/565/s401], pt. IV, Aug. 1, 1962, [76 Stat. 263][/us/stat/76/263], except insofar as section 705 affected this section.
