## § 2695b. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

Section, [Pub. L. 104–208, div. A, title I, § 101(a)][/us/pl/104/208/s101/a] \[title IV\], Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3009][/us/stat/110/3009], 3009–46, which provided that in fiscal year 1998 a system was to be in place that allocated to each department and agency full cost of its presence outside of the United States, was from the Departments of Commerce, Justice, and State, the Judiciary, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 1997, and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts. Similar provisions were contained in the following prior appropriation act:

    [Pub. L. 104–134, title I, § 101][/us/pl/104/134/s101]\[(a)\] \[title IV\], Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321][/us/stat/110/1321], 1321–36; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 104–140, § 1(a)][/us/pl/104/140/s1/a], May 2, 1996, [110 Stat. 1327][/us/stat/110/1327].
