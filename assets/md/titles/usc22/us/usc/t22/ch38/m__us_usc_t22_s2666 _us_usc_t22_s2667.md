###### §§ 2666, 2667. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–93, title I][/us/pl/99/93/tI], § 125(c), Aug. 16, 1985, [99 Stat. 417][/us/stat/99/417]

    Section 2666, acts [June 28, 1955, ch. 199][/us/act/1955-06-28/ch199], § 1, [69 Stat. 188][/us/stat/69/188]; Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–141, title IV][/us/pl/94/141/tIV], § 406, [89 Stat. 770][/us/stat/89/770], authorized security officers of Department of State and Foreign Service to carry firearms. See [section 2709 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2709].

    Section 2667, [act June 28, 1955, ch. 199][/us/act/1955-06-28/ch199], § 2, as added Aug. 27, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–493][/us/pl/88/493], § 4, [78 Stat. 610][/us/stat/78/610], empowered security officers of Department of State and Foreign Service to make arrests without warrant. See [section 2709(a)(5) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2709/a/5].
