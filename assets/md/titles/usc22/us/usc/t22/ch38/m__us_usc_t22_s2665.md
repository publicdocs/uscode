## § 2665. Personal services other than those provided for

    There shall not be employed in the Department of State or in connection with said Department in the District of Columbia any personal services other than those which shall be specifically authorized or appropriated for.

([June 22, 1906, ch. 3514][/us/act/1906-06-22/ch3514], [34 Stat. 402][/us/stat/34/402].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 155 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s155] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
