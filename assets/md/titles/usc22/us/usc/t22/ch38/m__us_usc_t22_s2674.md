## § 2674. Availability of exchange allowances or proceeds derived from exchange or sale of motor vehicles

    The exchange allowances or proceeds derived from the exchange or sale of passenger motor vehicles in possession of the Foreign Service abroad, in accordance with [section 503 of title 40][/us/usc/t40/s503], shall be available without fiscal year limitation for replacement of an equal number of such vehicles.

([Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 841][/us/act/1956-08-01/ch841], title I, § 7, [70 Stat. 891][/us/stat/70/891]; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 97–241, title II, § 202(a)][/us/pl/97/241/s202/a], Aug. 24, 1982, [96 Stat. 282][/us/stat/96/282].)

 __Codification__ 

    “[Section 503 of title 40][/us/usc/t40/s503]” substituted in text for “[section 201(c) of the Act of June 30, 1949][/us/act/1949-06-30/s201/c] ([40 U.S.C. 481(c)][/us/usc/t40/s481/c])” on authority of [Pub. L. 107–217, § 5(c)][/us/pl/107/217/s5/c], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1303][/us/stat/116/1303], the first section of which enacted Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works.

    Section was formerly classified to section 170l of Title 5 prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1][/us/pl/89/554/s1], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
