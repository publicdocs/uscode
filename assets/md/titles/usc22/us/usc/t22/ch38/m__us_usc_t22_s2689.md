## § 2689. American Sections, International Joint Commission, United States and Canada; funds for representation expenses and official entertainment within the United States

    Each fiscal year (beginning with fiscal year 1977), the Secretary of State may use funds appropriated for the American Sections, International Joint Commission, United States and Canada, for representation expenses and official entertainment within the United States for such American Sections.

([Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 841][/us/act/1956-08-01/ch841], title I, § 19, as added [Pub. L. 94–350, title I, § 104][/us/pl/94/350/s104], July 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 824][/us/stat/90/824]; amended [Pub. L. 95–426, title I, § 110(a)][/us/pl/95/426/s110/a], Oct. 7, 1978, [92 Stat. 967][/us/stat/92/967]; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 97–241, title II, § 202(a)][/us/pl/97/241/s202/a], Aug. 24, 1982, [96 Stat. 282][/us/stat/96/282].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1978—[Pub. L. 95–426][/us/pl/95/426] struck out “not to exceed $1,500 of the” after “the Secretary of State may use”.

 __Effective Date of 1978 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 95–426, title I, § 110(b)][/us/pl/95/426/s110/b], Oct. 7, 1978, [92 Stat. 967][/us/stat/92/967], provided that: 

> “The amendment made by this section \[amending this section\] shall take effect on October 1, 1978.”
