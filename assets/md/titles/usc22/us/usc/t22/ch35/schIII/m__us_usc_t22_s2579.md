 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 87–297, title III][/us/pl/87/297/tIII], § 308, formerly § 39, as added [Pub. L. 100–213][/us/pl/100/213], § 4, Dec. 24, 1987, [101 Stat. 1445][/us/stat/101/1445]; amended [Pub.
