    Section 911, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 516, [60 Stat. 1008][/us/stat/60/1008]; [July 28, 1956, ch. 770, § 5][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770/s5], [70 Stat. 704][/us/stat/70/704]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 95–105, title IV, § 405][/us/pl/95/105/s405], [91 Stat. 853][/us/stat/91/853], related to admissions to class 6, 7, or 8 for Foreign Service officers.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].
