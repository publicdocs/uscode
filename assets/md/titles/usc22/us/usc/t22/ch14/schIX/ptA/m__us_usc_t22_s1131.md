## § 1131. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)][/us/pl/96/465/s2205/1], Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/94/2159]

    Section, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title IX, § 901, [60 Stat. 1025][/us/stat/60/1025]; [Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23, § 10][/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23/s10], [69 Stat. 27][/us/stat/69/27]; Sept. 6, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–707, title V, § 511(c)(7)][/us/pl/86/707/s511/c/7], [74 Stat. 801][/us/stat/74/801], related to authorization of representation allowances.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].

    Section 1132, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title IX, § 902, [60 Stat. 1026][/us/stat/60/1026]; [July 28, 1956, ch. 770, § 11][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770/s11], [70 Stat. 705][/us/stat/70/705], authorized the Secretary of State to allot funds to defray the unusual expenses incident to the operation and maintenance of official residences suitable for principal representatives of the United States.
