## §§ 929 to 932. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(6)][/us/pl/96/465/s2205/6], Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160][/us/stat/94/2160]

    Section 929, [Pub. L. 90–464, § 15][/us/pl/90/464/s15], Aug. 20, 1968, [82 Stat. 813][/us/stat/82/813], related to limitation on tenure and extension of appointment for Reserve officers.

    Section 930, [Pub. L. 90–494, § 16][/us/pl/90/494/s16], Aug. 20, 1968, [82 Stat. 814][/us/stat/82/814], related to retirement and tenure for Reserve officers.

    Section 931, [Pub. L. 90–464, § 17][/us/pl/90/464/s17], Aug. 20, 1968, [82 Stat. 814][/us/stat/82/814], related to unlimited tenure for Foreign Service Reserve officers.

    Section 932, [Pub. L. 90–464, § 19][/us/pl/90/464/s19], Aug. 20, 1968, [82 Stat. 814][/us/stat/82/814], provided that sections 929 to 931 of this title not apply to officers and employees of the Agency for International Development, the Peace Corps, and the Arms Control and Disarmament Agency.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].
