## §§ 812 to 814. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 812, acts [May 24, 1924, ch. 182][/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182], § 18, formerly § 11, [43 Stat. 142][/us/stat/43/142]; renumbered § 18 and amended [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276][/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276], § 7, [46 Stat. 1209][/us/stat/46/1209], which related to fees, accounting, and stamps, was transferred to [section 4224 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4224].

    Section 813, [act May 24, 1924, ch. 182][/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182], § 35, as added [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276][/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276], § 7, [46 Stat. 1216][/us/stat/46/1216] and amended, which related to establishment of fiscal districts and district accounting and disbursing offices, was transferred to [section 4225 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4225].

    Section 814, [act May 24, 1924, ch. 182][/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182], § 36, as added [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276][/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276], § 7, [46 Stat. 1216][/us/stat/46/1216], which related to disposition of fees and official monies from diplomatic missions, consular offices and district accounting and disbursing offices, was transferred to [section 4226 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4226].
