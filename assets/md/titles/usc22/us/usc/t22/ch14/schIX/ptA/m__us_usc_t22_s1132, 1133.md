## §§ 1132, 1133. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–707, title V, § 511(a)(1)][/us/pl/86/707/s511/a/1], Sept. 6, 1960, [74 Stat. 800][/us/stat/74/800]

    Section 1132, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title IX, § 902, [60 Stat. 1026][/us/stat/60/1026]; [July 28, 1956, ch. 770, § 11][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770/s11], [70 Stat. 705][/us/stat/70/705], authorized the Secretary of State to allot funds to defray the unusual expenses incident to the operation and maintenance of official residences suitable for principal representatives of the United States. See [section 5913 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s5913], Government Organization and Employees.

    Section 1133, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title IX, § 903, [60 Stat. 1026][/us/stat/60/1026], required the Secretary of State to account for all allowances and allotments.
