## §§ 1079 to 1079c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II][/us/pl/96/465/tII], § 2205(13), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160][/us/stat/94/2160]

    Section 1079, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229][/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229], § 1, [70 Stat. 125][/us/stat/70/125], related to increase of annuities of annuitants retired prior to July 1, 1949.

    Section 1079a, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229][/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229], § 2, [70 Stat. 125][/us/stat/70/125], related to availability of increases provided for by [section 1079 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1079] for officers retired before July 1, 1979, who exercised reduced annuity and restoration clause.

    Section 1079b, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229][/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229], § 3, [70 Stat. 125][/us/stat/70/125], related to increases for widowed annuitants.

    Section 1079c, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229][/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229], § 4, [70 Stat. 125][/us/stat/70/125], related to increases for officers’ wives who become annuitants subsequent to the effective date of sections 1079 to 1079f of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].
