## §§ 961 to 966. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II][/us/pl/96/465/tII], § 2205(1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/94/2159]

    Section 961, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 571, [60 Stat. 1011][/us/stat/60/1011]; [May 26, 1949, ch. 143][/us/act/1949-05-26/ch143], § 5, [63 Stat. 111][/us/stat/63/111]; [Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23][/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23], § 6, [69 Stat. 25][/us/stat/69/25]; June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477][/us/pl/85/477], ch. V, § 502(h), [72 Stat. 273][/us/stat/72/273]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723], § 14, [74 Stat. 833][/us/stat/74/833]; Dec. 16, 1963, [Pub. L. 88–205][/us/pl/88/205], pt. IV, § 404(a), [77 Stat. 391][/us/stat/77/391]; Aug. 17, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–105, title IV][/us/pl/95/105/tIV], § 408, [91 Stat. 854][/us/stat/91/854], set forth provisions relating to assignments of officers or employees to any government agency or international organization.

    Section 962, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV], § 572, [60 Stat. 1012][/us/stat/60/1012], related to compulsory service in the United States.

    Section 963, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV], § 573, [60 Stat. 1012][/us/stat/60/1012], related to assignment for consultation or instruction.

    Section 964, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV], § 574, [60 Stat. 1012][/us/stat/60/1012], related to assignment to trade, labor, agricultural, scientific, or other conferences.

    Section 965, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 575, [60 Stat. 1013][/us/stat/60/1013]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723], § 15, [74 Stat. 834][/us/stat/74/834], related to assignments to foreign governments of officers and employees.

    Section 966, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV], § 576, as added Oct. 26, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–475][/us/pl/93/475], § 9(a), [88 Stat. 1440][/us/stat/88/1440]; amended Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–141, title IV][/us/pl/94/141/tIV], § 401(a), [89 Stat. 763][/us/stat/89/763]; Oct. 7, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–426, title IV][/us/pl/95/426/tIV], § 403(a)(2)–(4), [92 Stat. 978][/us/stat/92/978], related to assignments to public or private nonprofit organizations and Members and officers of Congress.

    A prior section 576 of [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV], [60 Stat. 1013][/us/stat/60/1013], which provided for assignments to international organizations, was repealed by [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723], § 52(3), Sept. 8, 1960, [74 Stat. 847][/us/stat/74/847].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].
