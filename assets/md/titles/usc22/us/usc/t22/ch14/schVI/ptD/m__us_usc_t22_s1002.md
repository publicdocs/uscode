## § 1002. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II][/us/pl/96/465/tII], § 2205(1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/94/2159]

    Section, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title VI, § 632, [60 Stat. 1015][/us/stat/60/1015]; [Aug. 5, 1955, ch. 576][/us/act/1955-08-05/ch576], § 9, [69 Stat. 537][/us/stat/69/537]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723], § 21, [74 Stat. 835][/us/stat/74/835]; July 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–350, title V][/us/pl/94/350/tV], § 519, [90 Stat. 846][/us/stat/90/846], related to separation from service of participants in the Foreign Service Retirement and Disability System who are not career ambassadors.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 17, 1980, see [section 2403(d)(1) of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403/d/1], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].

 __Mandatory Retirement__ 

    [Act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title XI][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tXI], § 1111, [60 Stat. 1034][/us/stat/60/1034], which related to mandatory retirement of certain Foreign Service officers, was repealed by [Pub. L. 96–465, title II][/us/pl/96/465/tII], § 2205(1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/94/2159].
