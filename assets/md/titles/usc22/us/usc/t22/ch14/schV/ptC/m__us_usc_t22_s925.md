## § 925. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–723, § 52(2)][/us/pl/86/723/s52/2], Sept. 8, 1960, [74 Stat. 847][/us/stat/74/847]

    Section, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 525, [60 Stat. 1010][/us/stat/60/1010], authorized Secretary to define active duty period of Reserve officers.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723] effective first day of first pay period which begins more than thirty days after Sept. 8, 1960, see [Pub. L. 86–723, § 56(a)][/us/pl/86/723/s56/a], Sept. 8, 1960, [74 Stat. 848][/us/stat/74/848].
