## §§ 802 to 807. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II][/us/pl/96/465/tII], § 2205(1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/94/2159]

    Section 802, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title I, § 121, [60 Stat. 1000][/us/stat/60/1000]; Oct. 7, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–426, title I][/us/pl/95/426/tI], § 116, [92 Stat. 969][/us/stat/92/969], set forth definitions applicable to provisions of this chapter.

    Section 805, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title X][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tX], § 1003, [60 Stat. 1030][/us/stat/60/1030], related to prohibition on engaging in business abroad.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].
