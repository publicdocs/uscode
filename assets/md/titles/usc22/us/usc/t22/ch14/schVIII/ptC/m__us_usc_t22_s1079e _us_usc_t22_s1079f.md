###### §§ 1079e, 1079f. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II][/us/pl/96/465/tII], § 2205(13), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160][/us/stat/94/2160]

    Section 1079e, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229][/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229], § 6, [70 Stat. 125][/us/stat/70/125], related to restriction on increases under sections 1079 to 1079f of this title.

    Section 1079f, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229][/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229], § 7, [70 Stat. 125][/us/stat/70/125], provided that no annuity currently payable be reduced as a result of sections 1079 to 1079f of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].
