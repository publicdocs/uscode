## § 936. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II][/us/pl/96/465/tII], § 2205(1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/94/2159]

    Section, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 531, [60 Stat. 1010][/us/stat/60/1010]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723], § 12, [74 Stat. 833][/us/stat/74/833], related to appointments for Foreign Service staff officers and employees.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].

 __Transfer of Personnel From Old to New Classes__ 

    [Act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title XI][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tXI], § 1103, [60 Stat. 1034][/us/stat/60/1034], which set forth provisions respecting transfer of personnel from old class to new class, was repealed by [Pub. L. 96–465, title II][/us/pl/96/465/tII], § 2205(1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/94/2159].
