## §§ 1003 to 1009. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II][/us/pl/96/465/tII], § 2205(1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/94/2159]

    Section 1003, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI], § 633, as added [Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23][/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23], § 7, [69 Stat. 25][/us/stat/69/25], related to selection-out process and regulations.

    A prior section 633 of [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI], [60 Stat. 1015][/us/stat/60/1015], which provided that Secretary prescribe maximum period during which Foreign Service officers in classes 2 or 3 remain in force without promotion and for retirement from Service for any officer who did not receive a promotion within such period, was repealed by [act Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23][/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23], § 7, [69 Stat. 25][/us/stat/69/25].

    Section 1004, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI], § 634, as added [Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23][/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23], § 7, [69 Stat. 25][/us/stat/69/25]; amended [July 28, 1956, ch. 770][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770], § 7, [70 Stat. 704][/us/stat/70/704]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723], § 22, [74 Stat. 835][/us/stat/74/835]; July 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–350, title V][/us/pl/94/350/tV], § 520, [90 Stat. 846][/us/stat/90/846], related to selection-out benefits.

    A prior section 634 of [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI], [60 Stat. 1015][/us/stat/60/1015], which provided selection-out benefits to officers in classes 4 and 5 was repealed by [act Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23][/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23], § 7, [69 Stat. 25][/us/stat/69/25].

    Section 1005, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title VI, § 635, [60 Stat. 1016][/us/stat/60/1016]; [July 28, 1956, ch. 770][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770], § 8, [70 Stat. 704][/us/stat/70/704]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723], § 23, [74 Stat. 836][/us/stat/74/836], related to status, etc., of officers in class 7 or 8.

    Section 1006, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title VI, § 636, [60 Stat. 1016][/us/stat/60/1016]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723], § 24, [74 Stat. 836][/us/stat/74/836], related to voluntary retirement.

    Section 1007, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title VI, § 637, [60 Stat. 1016][/us/stat/60/1016]; [July 28, 1956, ch. 770][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770], § 8, [70 Stat. 704][/us/stat/70/704]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub.     Section 1008, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title VI, § 638, [60 Stat. 1016][/us/stat/60/1016]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723][/us/pl/86/723], § 26, [74 Stat. 837][/us/stat/74/837], related to termination of limited appointments of Reserve officers, and staff officers and employees.

    Section 1009, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI], § 639, as added Oct. 7, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–426, title IV][/us/pl/95/426/tIV], § 404, [92 Stat. 979][/us/stat/92/979], related to professional career counseling, advice, and placement assistance.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s2403], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3901].
