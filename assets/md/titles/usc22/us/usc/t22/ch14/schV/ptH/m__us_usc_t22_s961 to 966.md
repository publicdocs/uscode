## §§ 961 to 966. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)][/us/pl/96/465/s2205/1], Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/94/2159]

    Section 961, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 571, [60 Stat. 1011][/us/stat/60/1011]; [May 26, 1949, ch. 143, § 5][/us/act/1949-05-26/ch143/s5], [63 Stat. 111][/us/stat/63/111]; [Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23, § 6][/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23/s6], [69 Stat. 25][/us/stat/69/25]; June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 95–105, title IV, § 408][/us/pl/95/105/s408], [91 Stat. 854][/us/stat/91/854], set forth provisions relating to assignments of officers or employees to any government agency or international organization.

    Section 962, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 572, [60 Stat. 1012][/us/stat/60/1012], related to compulsory service in the United States.

    Section 963, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 573, [60 Stat. 1012][/us/stat/60/1012], related to assignment for consultation or instruction.

    Section 964, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 574, [60 Stat. 1012][/us/stat/60/1012], related to assignment to trade, labor, agricultural, scientific, or other conferences.

    Section 965, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 575, [60 Stat. 1013][/us/stat/60/1013]; Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 15][/us/pl/86/723/s15], [74 Stat. 834][/us/stat/74/834], related to assignments to foreign governments of officers and employees.

    Section 966, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, § 576, as added Oct. 26, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–475, § 9(a)][/us/pl/93/475/s9/a], [88 Stat. 1440][/us/stat/88/1440]; amended Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub.     A prior section 576 of [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957], title V, [60 Stat. 1013][/us/stat/60/1013], which provided for assignments to international organizations, was repealed by [Pub. L. 86–723, § 52(3)][/us/pl/86/723/s52/3], Sept. 8, 1960, [74 Stat. 847][/us/stat/74/847].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__
