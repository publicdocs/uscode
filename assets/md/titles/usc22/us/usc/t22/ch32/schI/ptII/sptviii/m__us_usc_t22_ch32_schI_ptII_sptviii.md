##### subpart viii—southeast asia multilateral and regional programs

§§ 2217, 2217a. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 95–424, title I, § 102(g)(1)(A)__  __][/us/pl/95/424/s102/g/1/A]__  __,__  __Oct. 6, 1978__  __,__  __[__  __92 Stat. 942__  __][/us/stat/92/942]__ 

    Section 2217, [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. I, § 271, as added [Pub. L. 89–583][/us/pl/89/583], pt. I, § 106, Sept. 19, 1966, [80 Stat. 799][/us/stat/80/799], set forth sense of Congress that acceleration of social and economic progress would be served by an expanded effort by countries of southeast Asia and other interested countries in cooperative programs.

    Section 2217a, [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. I, § 272, as added [Pub. L. 89–583][/us/pl/89/583], pt.
