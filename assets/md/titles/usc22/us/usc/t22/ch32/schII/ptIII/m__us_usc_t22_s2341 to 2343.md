## §§ 2341 to 2343. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–629][/us/pl/90/629], ch. 4, § 45(a), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1327][/us/stat/82/1327]

    Section 2341, [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. II, § 521, formerly § 514, as added [Pub. L. 89–583][/us/pl/89/583], pt. II, § 201(f), Sept. 19, 1966, [80 Stat. 803][/us/stat/80/803]; renumbered § 521 and amended [Pub. L. 90–137][/us/pl/90/137], pt. II, § 201(o)(1), (3)–(7), Nov. 14, 1967, [81 Stat. 457][/us/stat/81/457], provided for administration of sales programs involving defense articles and services. Subsec. (a) related to encouragement of regional arms control and disarmament agreements and discouragement of arms races; reimbursable basis of acquisitions; domestic procurement; and considerations. Subsec. (b) related to limitation on military assistance and sales for American Republics; and inclusion of assistance to inter-American military force under control of Organization of American States. Subsec. (c) related to furnishing of defense articles and services; conditions; and report to Congress. For subject matter of subsecs. (a) to (c), see sections 2751 and 2791, 2773, and 2753 of this title, respectively.

    Section 2342, [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. II, § 522, formerly § 507 (a), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 437][/us/stat/75/437]; amended [Pub. L. 87–565][/us/pl/87/565], pt. II, § 201(b), Aug. 1, 1962, [76 Stat. 259][/us/stat/76/259]; [Pub. L. 89–171][/us/pl/89/171], pt. II, § 201(d)(1), Sept. 6, 1965, [79 Stat. 657][/us/stat/79/657]; renumbered § 522 and amended [Pub. L. 90–137][/us/pl/90/137], pt. II, § 201(f), Nov. 14, 1967, [81 Stat. 456][/us/stat/81/456], provided for sales of defense articles from stock and services, manner of payment, price of non-excess defense articles, and value of excess defense articles. See [section 2761 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2761].

    Section 2343, [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. II, § 523, formerly § 507(b), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 437][/us/stat/75/437]; amended [Pub. L. 87–565][/us/pl/87/565], pt. II, § 201(c), Aug. 1, 1962, [76 Stat. 259][/us/stat/76/259]; [Pub. L. 88–633][/us/pl/88/633], pt. II, § 201(c), Oct. 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 1011][/us/stat/78/1011]; [Pub. L. 89–171][/us/pl/89/171], pt. II, § 201(d)(2), Sept. 6, 1965, [79 Stat. 657][/us/stat/79/657]; renumbered § 523 and amended [Pub. L. 90–137][/us/pl/90/137], pt. II, § 201(g), Nov. 14, 1967, [81 Stat. 456][/us/stat/81/456], related to contracts for procurement for sales; undertakings; reimbursements; fixed-price sales agreements; prohibition against sales of articles available from commercial sources; and waiver of restrictions. See [section 2762 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2762].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective July 1, 1968, see [section 41 of Pub. L. 90–629][/us/pl/90/629/s41], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 2751 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2751].

 __Savings Provision__ 

[Pub. L. 90–629, § 46][/us/pl/90/629/s46], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1328][/us/stat/82/1328], provided that: 

> “Except as may be expressly provided to the contrary in this Act \[see Short Title note set out under [section 2751 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2751]\], all determinations, authorizations, regulations, orders, contracts, agreements, and other actions issued, undertaken, or entered into under authority of any provision of law repealed by section 45(a) \[repealing sections 2341 to 2343, 2344(b)(3), 2345, 2394(g), and 2399a of this title\] shall continue in full force and effect until modified by appropriate authority.”
