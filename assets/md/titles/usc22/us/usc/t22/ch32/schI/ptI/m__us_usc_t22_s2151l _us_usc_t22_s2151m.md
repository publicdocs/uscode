###### §§ 2151l, 2151m. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–424, title I][/us/pl/95/424/tI], §§ 102(f), 104(b), Oct. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 942][/us/stat/92/942], 947

    Section 2151l, [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. I, § 114, as added [Pub. L. 93–189][/us/pl/93/189], § 2(3), Dec. 17, 1973, [87 Stat. 716][/us/stat/87/716]; amended [Pub. L. 95–88, title I][/us/pl/95/88/tI], § 109, Aug. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 536][/us/stat/91/536], prohibited use of funds for performance of abortions or involuntary sterilizations.

    Section 2151m, [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. I, § 115, as added [Pub. L. 93–559][/us/pl/93/559], § 20, Dec. 30, 1974, [88 Stat. 1800][/us/stat/88/1800]; amended [Pub. L. 95–88, title I][/us/pl/95/88/tI], § 110, Aug. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 536][/us/stat/91/536], prohibited use of funds available under this part for any countries to which assistance is furnished under part IV of subchapter II of this chapter or under subchapter V of this chapter without specific authorization from Congress.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 605 of Pub. L. 95–424][/us/pl/95/424/s605], set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 2151 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2151].
