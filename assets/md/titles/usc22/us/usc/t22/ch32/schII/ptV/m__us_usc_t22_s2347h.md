## § 2347h. Human rights report

    (a) __In general__ 

        Not later than March 1 of each year, the Secretary of State shall submit to the Speaker of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Foreign Relations of the Senate a report describing, to the extent practicable, any involvement of a foreign military or defense ministry civilian participant in education and training activities under this part in a violation of internationally recognized human rights reported under [section 2151n(d) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2151n/d] subsequent to such participation.

    (b) __Form__ 

        The report described in subsection (a) shall be in unclassified form, but may include a classified annex.

([Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. II, § 549, as added [Pub. L. 107–228, div. B, title XII, § 1212(a)][/us/pl/107/228/s1212/a], Sept. 30, 2002, [116 Stat. 1428][/us/stat/116/1428].)
