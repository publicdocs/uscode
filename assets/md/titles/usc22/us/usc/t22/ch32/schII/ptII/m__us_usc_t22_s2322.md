## § 2322. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. II, § 521, formerly § 514, as added [Pub. L. 89–583][/us/pl/89/583], pt. II, § 201(f), Sept. 19, 1966, [80 Stat. 803][/us/stat/80/803]; renumbered § 521, [Pub. L. 90–137][/us/pl/90/137], pt.
