#### Part IV—Supporting Assistance

 __References to Part IV of Subchapter I Deemed References to Part IV of Subchapter II__ 

    References to part IV of subchapter I of this chapter, or any sections thereof, are deemed references to part IV of subchapter II (§ 2346 et seq.) of this chapter, or to appropriate sections thereof. See [section 202(b) of Pub. L. 92–226][/us/pl/92/226/s202/b], set out as a note under [section 2346 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2346].
