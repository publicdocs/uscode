## § 2431j. Consultations with Congress

    The President shall consult with the appropriate congressional committees on a periodic basis to review the operation of the Facility under this subchapter and the eligibility of countries for benefits from the Facility under this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. V, § 812, as added [Pub. L. 105–214, § 1][/us/pl/105/214/s1], July 29, 1998, [112 Stat. 893][/us/stat/112/893].)

 __Delegation of Functions__ 

    For delegation of functions of President under this section, see Ex. Ord. No. 12163, Sept. 29, 1979, 44 F.R. 56673, as amended, set out as a note under [section 2381 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2381].

    For delegation of functions of President under this section, see section 1 of Ex. Ord. No. 13345, July 8, 2004, 69 F.R. 41901, set out as a note under [section 1738 of Title 7][/us/usc/t7/s1738], Agriculture.
