## § 2399a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–629][/us/pl/90/629], ch. 4, § 45(a), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1327][/us/stat/82/1327]

    Section, [Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. III, § 640, as added [Pub. L. 89–171][/us/pl/89/171], pt. III, § 302(k), Sept. 6, 1965, [79 Stat. 661][/us/stat/79/661]; amended [Pub. L. 90–137][/us/pl/90/137], pt. III, § 302(q), Nov. 14, 1967, [81 Stat. 462][/us/stat/81/462], related to military sales. See [section 2753(a)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2753/a/1].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective July 1, 1968, see [section 41 of Pub. L. 90–629][/us/pl/90/629/s41], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 2751 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2751].

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Determinations, authorizations, regulations, orders, contracts, agreements, and other actions issued, undertaken, or entered into under authority of any provision of former [section 2399a of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2399a] as continuing in full force and effect until modified by appropriate authority, see [section 46 of Pub. L. 90–629][/us/pl/90/629/s46], set out as a note under [section 2341 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2341].
