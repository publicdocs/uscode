## § 2276. “Central American countries” defined

    For the purposes of this part, the term “Central American countries” includes Belize, Costa Rica, El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, Nicaragua, Panama, and regional programs which benefit such countries.

([Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195], pt. I, § 466, as added [Pub. L. 99–83, title VII][/us/pl/99/83/tVII], § 701, Aug. 8, 1985, [99 Stat. 237][/us/stat/99/237].)

 __Codification__ 

    Another [section 466 of Pub. L. 87–195][/us/pl/87/195/s466] is classified to [section 2286 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2286].
