#### Part 3—Internal Taxes

§§ 1271 to 1274. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Section 1271, [act Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244], title II, § 221, [60 Stat. 147][/us/stat/60/147], which related to equality in internal taxes for Philippine products coming into the United States, was omitted on authority of former [section 1345 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1345] which nullified subchapter I of this chapter upon expiration of the revised agreement between the United States and the Republic of the Philippines which occurred on July 4, 1974.

    Section 1272, [act Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244], title II, § 222, [60 Stat. 148][/us/stat/60/148], related to exemption from tax of manila fiber. See note above for [section 1271 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1271].

    Section 1273, [act Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244], title II, § 223, [60 Stat. 148][/us/stat/60/148], prohibited export taxes by the United States on articles exported to the Philippines. See note above for [section 1271 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1271].

    Section 1274, [act Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244], title II, § 224, [60 Stat. 148][/us/stat/60/148], provided an exemption from taxes for articles for official use of the Philippine Government. See note above for [section 1271 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1271].
