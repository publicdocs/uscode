#### Part 5—General Provisions

§§ 1331 to 1334. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Section 1331, [act Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244], title III, § 341, [60 Stat. 151][/us/stat/60/151], which related to rights of United States citizens and business enterprises to natural resources in the Philippines, was omitted on authority of [section 1291(b)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1291/b/1] which provided that this subchapter be effective until the expiration of the revised agreement between the United States and the Republic of the Philippines which occurred on July 4, 1974.

    Section 1332, [act Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244], title III, § 342, [60 Stat. 151][/us/stat/60/151], provided for currency stabilization between the United States and the Philippines. See note above for [section 1331 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1331].

    Section 1333, acts [June 11, 1934, ch. 445][/us/act/1934-06-11/ch445], [48 Stat. 929][/us/stat/48/929]; [Aug. 7, 1946, ch. 809, § 1][/us/act/1946-08-07/ch809/s1], [60 Stat. 901][/us/stat/60/901], which related to deposits of and interest on public moneys, terminated on July 1, 1951, under the provisions of section 2 of act Aug. 7, 1946.

    Section 1334, [act Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244], title III, § 343, [60 Stat. 151][/us/stat/60/151], related to allocation of quotas established by sections 1261, 1262, and 1264 of this title. See note above for [section 1331 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1331].
