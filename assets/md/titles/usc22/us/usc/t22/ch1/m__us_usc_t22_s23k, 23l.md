## §§ 23k, 23l. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 23k, [act May 24, 1924, ch. 182, § 35][/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182/s35], as added [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276, § 7][/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276/s7], [46 Stat. 1216][/us/stat/46/1216], which related to establishment and maintenance of fiscal accounting and disbursing offices, was transferred to [section 4225 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4225].

    Section 23l, [act May 24, 1924, ch. 182, § 36][/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182/s36], as added [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276, § 7][/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276/s7], [46 Stat. 1216][/us/stat/46/1216], which related to fees and official monies from diplomatic missions, was transferred to [section 4226 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4226].
