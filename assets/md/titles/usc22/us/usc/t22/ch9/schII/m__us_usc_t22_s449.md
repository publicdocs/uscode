## § 449. American republics

    This subchapter (except section 452  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title) shall not apply to any American republic engaged in war against a non-American state or states, provided the American republic is not cooperating with a non-American state or states in such war.

([Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 9][/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s9], [54 Stat. 8][/us/stat/54/8].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 452 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s452], referred to in text, was repealed by [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937][/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937], title V, § 542(a)(12), [68 Stat. 861][/us/stat/68/861]. See former section 1934(a) and [section 2778(a) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2778/a].
