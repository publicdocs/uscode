## § 7908. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

([Pub. L. 101–240, title VII, § 738][/us/pl/101/240/s738], as added [Pub. L. 109–58, title XVI, § 1611][/us/pl/109/58/s1611], Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 1117][/us/stat/119/1117].)
