## § 4132. Grievances concerning former members or their survivors

    Within the time limitations of [section 4134 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4134], a former member of the Service or the surviving spouse (or, if none, another member of the family) of a deceased member or former member of the Service may file a grievance under this subchapter only with respect to allegations described in [section 4131(a)(1)(G) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4131/a/1/G].

([Pub. L. 96–465, title I, § 1102][/us/pl/96/465/s1102], Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2143][/us/stat/94/2143].)
