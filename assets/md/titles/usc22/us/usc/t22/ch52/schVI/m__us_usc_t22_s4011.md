## § 4011. Termination of limited appointments

    Except as provided in [section 4010(a)(2) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4010/a/2], the Secretary may terminate at any time the appointment of any member of the Service serving under a limited appointment who is in the Senior Foreign Service, who is assigned to a salary class in the Foreign Service Schedule or who is paid in accordance with [section 3967 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3967] or is a United States citizen paid under a compensation plan under [section 3968 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3968].

([Pub. L. 96–465, title I, § 612][/us/pl/96/465/s612], formerly § 611, Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2099][/us/stat/94/2099]; renumbered § 612 and amended [Pub. L. 103–236, title I][/us/pl/103/236], §§ 180(a)(7), 181(a)(1), Apr. 30, 1994, [108 Stat. 416][/us/stat/108/416].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 612 of Pub. L. 96–465][/us/pl/96/465/s612] was renumbered section 613 and is classified to [section 4012 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4012].

 __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–236, § 180(a)(7)][/us/pl/103/236/s180/a/7], substituted “or who is paid in accordance with [section 3967 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3967] or is a United States citizen paid under a compensation plan under [section 3968 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3968].” for “, or who is a family member of a Government employee serving under a local compensation plan established under [section 3968 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s3968].”
