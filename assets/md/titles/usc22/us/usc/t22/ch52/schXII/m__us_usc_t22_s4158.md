## § 4158. Authority of other agencies

    The heads of agencies other than the Department of State which utilize the Foreign Service personnel system shall perform functions under this subchapter in accordance with regulations prescribed by the Secretary of State under [section 4157 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s4157]. Such agency heads shall consult with the Secretary of State in the exercise of such functions.

([Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2108][/us/pl/96/465/s2108], Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2152][/us/stat/94/2152].)
