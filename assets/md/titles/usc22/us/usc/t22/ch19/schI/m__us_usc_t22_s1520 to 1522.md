## §§ 1520 to 1522. Repealed. [Oct. 10, 1951, ch. 479][/us/act/1951-10-10/ch479], title V, § 503(b)(1), as added [June 20, 1952, ch. 449, § 7(c)][/us/act/1952-06-20/ch449/s7/c], [66 Stat. 144][/us/stat/66/144]

    Section 1520, [act Apr. 3, 1948, ch. 169][/us/act/1948-04-03/ch169], title I, § 122, [62 Stat. 155][/us/stat/62/155], related to termination of program. See [section 2367 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2367].

    Section 1521, [act Apr. 3, 1941, ch. 169][/us/act/1941-04-03/ch169], title I, § 123, [62 Stat. 156][/us/stat/62/156], related to reports to Congress. See [section 2394 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2394].

    Section 1522, [act Apr. 3, 1948, ch. 169][/us/act/1948-04-03/ch169], title I, § 124, [62 Stat. 156][/us/stat/62/156], provided for a Joint Congressional Committee, its composition, vacancies, chairman, functions, committee hearings, attendance of witnesses, cost of stenographic services, penalties, employment of personnel, appropriations and disbursements.
