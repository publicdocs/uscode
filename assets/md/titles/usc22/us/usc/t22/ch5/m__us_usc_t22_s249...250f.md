## §§ 249 to 250f. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 249, [act Aug. 9, 1939, ch. 616][/us/act/1939-08-09/ch616], § 1, [53 Stat. 1290][/us/stat/53/1290], which related to utilization of services of government agencies to promote inter-American relations, was transferred to [section 501 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s501].

    Section 249a, [act Aug. 9, 1939, ch. 616][/us/act/1939-08-09/ch616], § 2, [53 Stat. 1290][/us/stat/53/1290], which related to creation of advisory committees, was transferred to [section 502 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s502].

    Section 250, [act June 15, 1940, ch. 365][/us/act/1940-06-15/ch365], § 1, [54 Stat. 396][/us/stat/54/396], which related to military and naval assistance to governments of American republics, was transferred to [section 521 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s521].

    Section 250a, [act June 15, 1940, ch. 365][/us/act/1940-06-15/ch365], § 2, [54 Stat. 396][/us/stat/54/396], which related to transmission of information relating to implements of war, was transferred to [section 522 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s522].

    Section 250b, [act June 15, 1940, ch. 365][/us/act/1940-06-15/ch365], § 3, [54 Stat. 397][/us/stat/54/397], which related to contract restrictions against disposal of implements of war, was transferred to [section 523 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s523].

    Section 250c, [act June 15, 1940, ch. 365][/us/act/1940-06-15/ch365], § 4, [54 Stat. 397][/us/stat/54/397], which related to information on shipments to be given chairman of National Munitions Control Board, was transferred to [section 524 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s524].

    Section 250d, [act June 15, 1940, ch. 365][/us/act/1940-06-15/ch365], § 5, [54 Stat. 397][/us/stat/54/397], which related to appropriations and dispositions of receipts, was transferred to [section 525 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s525].

    Section 250e, [act June 15, 1940, ch. 365][/us/act/1940-06-15/ch365], § 6, [54 Stat. 397][/us/stat/54/397], which related to protection of patent rights, was transferred to [section 526 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s526].

    Section 250f, [act June 15, 1940, ch. 365][/us/act/1940-06-15/ch365], § 7, [54 Stat. 397][/us/stat/54/397], which related to purchases of implements of war from American republics, was transferred to [section 527 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s527].
