## §§ 233 to 233g. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 233, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VIII][/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVIII], § 3, [40 Stat. 226][/us/stat/40/226], as amended, which related to acting as a foreign agent without notice to Secretary of State, was transferred to [section 601 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s601] and was subsequently repealed by [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], § 21, [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862]. See [section 951 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s951], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

    Section 233a, [act June 8, 1938, ch. 327][/us/act/1938-06-08/ch327], § 1, [52 Stat. 631][/us/stat/52/631], as amended, which related to defining, various terms included in provisions relating to foreign agents and propaganda, was transferred to [section 611 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s611].

    Section 233b, [act June 8, 1938, ch. 327][/us/act/1938-06-08/ch327], § 2, [52 Stat. 632][/us/stat/52/632], as amended, which related to registration statements, was transferred to [section 612 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s612].

    Section 233c, [act June 8, 1938, ch. 327][/us/act/1938-06-08/ch327], § 3, [52 Stat. 632][/us/stat/52/632], as amended, which related to exemptions, was transferred to [section 613 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s613].

    Section 233d, [act June 8, 1938, ch. 327][/us/act/1938-06-08/ch327], § 4, [52 Stat. 632][/us/stat/52/632], as amended, which related to filing and labeling political propaganda, was transferred to [section 614 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s614].

    Section 233e, [act June 8, 1938, ch. 327][/us/act/1938-06-08/ch327], § 5, [52 Stat. 633][/us/stat/52/633], as amended, which related to books and records, was transferred to [section 615 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s615].

    Section 233f, [act June 8, 1938, ch. 327][/us/act/1938-06-08/ch327], § 6, [52 Stat. 633][/us/stat/52/633], as amended, which related to public examination of official records, was transferred to [section 616 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s616].

    Section 233g, [act June 8, 1938, ch. 327][/us/act/1938-06-08/ch327], §§ 12, 13, as added [Apr. 29, 1942, ch. 263][/us/act/1942-04-29/ch263], § 1, [56 Stat. 258][/us/stat/56/258], which related to separability of provisions and effect on existing law, was transferred and set out as a note under [section 611 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s611].
