## § 2141. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 2][/us/pl/104/288/s2], Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3402][/us/stat/110/3402], which stated findings of Congress and purpose of this chapter, was omitted because of the repeal of sections 2141a to 2141d of this title.

§§ 2141a to 2141d. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 104–288, § 7(a)__  __][/us/pl/104/288/s7/a]__  __,__  __Oct. 11, 1996__  __,__  __[__  __110 Stat. 3407__  __][/us/stat/110/3407]__ 

    Section 2141a, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 3][/us/pl/104/288/s3], Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3403][/us/stat/110/3403], related to establishment of United States National Tourism Organization.

    Section 2141b, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 4][/us/pl/104/288/s4], Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3404][/us/stat/110/3404], related to establishment of United States National Tourism Organization Board.

    Section 2141c, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 5][/us/pl/104/288/s5], Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3406][/us/stat/110/3406], related to design and use by Organization of symbols, emblems, trademarks, and names.

    Section 2141d, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 6][/us/pl/104/288/s6], Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3406][/us/stat/110/3406]; [Pub. L. 105–277, div. G][/us/pl/105/277], subdiv. A, title XIII, § 1335(o), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–789][/us/stat/112/2681-789], related to United States Government cooperation.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    For effective date of repeal, see Codification note for former [section 2141e of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2141e].

§§ 2141e, 2141f. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections are omitted from the Code because of the repeal of sections 2141a to 2141d of this title.

    Section 2141e, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 7][/us/pl/104/288/s7], Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3407][/us/stat/110/3407], provided for the repeal of sections 2141a to 2141d of this title if, within 2 years after Oct. 11, 1996, the United States National Tourism Organization Board had not developed and implemented a comprehensive plan for the long-term financing of the United States National Tourism Organization, and further provided that the Board could suspend or terminate the Organization if sufficient private sector and State or local government funds were not identified or made available to continue the Organization’s operations. \[The Board did not develop and implement such a plan within 2 years after Oct. 11, 1996.\]

    Section 2141f, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 12][/us/pl/104/288/s12], Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3410][/us/stat/110/3410], defined the terms “Organization” and “Board” for purposes of this chapter.
