## § 290n. Appointment of members of Border Health Commission

    Not later than 30 days after November 29, 1999, the President shall appoint the United States members of the United States-Mexico Border Health Commission, and shall attempt to conclude an agreement with Mexico providing for the establishment of such Commission.

([Pub. L. 103–400][/us/pl/103/400], § 2, as added [Pub. L. 106–113, div. B][/us/pl/106/113/dB], § 1000(a)(4) \[title V, § 516(1)\], Nov. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 1535][/us/stat/113/1535], 1501A–276.)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 290n, [Pub. L. 103–400][/us/pl/103/400], § 2, Oct. 22, 1994, [108 Stat. 4169][/us/stat/108/4169], related to the establishment of the Border Health Commission, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 106–113, div. B][/us/pl/106/113/dB], § 1000(a)(4) \[title V, § 516(1)\], Nov. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 1535][/us/stat/113/1535], 1501A–276.

 __Short Title__ 

[Pub. L. 103–400][/us/pl/103/400], § 1, Oct. 22, 1994, [108 Stat. 4169][/us/stat/108/4169], provided that: 

> “This Act \[enacting this subchapter\] may be cited as the ‘United States-Mexico Border Health Commission Act’.”
