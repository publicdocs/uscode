## § 290i. Acceptance of membership

    The President is hereby authorized to accept membership for the United States in the African Development Bank (hereinafter in this subchapter referred to as the “Bank”) provided for by the agreement establishing the Bank (hereinafter in this subchapter referred to as the “agreement”) deposited in the archives of the United Nations.

([Pub. L. 97–35, title XIII][/us/pl/97/35/tXIII], § 1332, Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 741][/us/stat/95/741].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this part”, meaning part 3 of subtitle B of title XIII of [Pub. L. 97–35][/us/pl/97/35], Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 741][/us/stat/95/741], known as the African Development Bank Act, which enacted this subchapter and amended sections 262d, 262f, and 276c–2 of this title and [section 24 of Title 12][/us/usc/t12/s24], Banks and Banking. For complete classification of part 3 to the Code, see Short Title note set out below and Tables.

 __Effective Date__ 

[Pub. L. 97–35, title XIII][/us/pl/97/35/tXIII], § 1372, Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 746][/us/stat/95/746], provided that: 

> “This subtitle \[subtitle B (§§ 1311–1372) of title XIII of [Pub. L. 97–35][/us/pl/97/35], enacting this subchapter and sections 262g–1 to 262g–3, 283z–2, 284o, 285w, and 286e–1h of this title, amending sections 262d, 262f, 262g, 276c–2, 283w, 283z–1, 285s, 285t, 285u, 286e–1f, and 286u of this title and [section 24 of Title 12][/us/usc/t12/s24], Banks and Banking, repealing [section 286e–10 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s286e–10], and enacting provisions set out as notes under sections 262c, 262g–2, and 290i of this title\] shall take effect upon its enactment \[Aug. 13, 1981\], except that funds authorized to be appropriated by any provision contained in part 1 \[enacting [section 286e–1h of this title][/us/usc/t22/s286e–1h] and amending [section 286e–1f of this title][/us/usc/t22/s286e–1f]\] or part 4 \[enacting sections 283z–2 and 285w of this title and amending sections 283w, 283z–1, 285s, 285t, and 285u of this title\] shall not be available for use or obligation prior to October 1, 1981.”

 __Short Title__ 

[Pub. L. 97–35, title XIII][/us/pl/97/35/tXIII], § 1331, Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 741][/us/stat/95/741], provided that: 

> “This part \[part 3 (§§ 1331–1342) of subtitle B of title XIII of [Pub. L. 97–35][/us/pl/97/35], enacting this subchapter and amending sections 262d, 262f, and 276c–2 of this title and [section 24 of Title 12][/us/usc/t12/s24], Banks and Banking\] may be cited as the ‘African Development Bank Act’.”
