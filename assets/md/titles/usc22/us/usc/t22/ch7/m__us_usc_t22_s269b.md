## § 269b. Omitted

    Section, acts [May 3, 1928, ch. 489][/us/act/1928-05-03/ch489], [45 Stat. 487][/us/stat/45/487]; [Sept. 21, 1950, ch. 976, § 1(a)][/us/act/1950-09-21/ch976/s1/a], [64 Stat. 902][/us/stat/64/902]; [July 27, 1956, ch. 750][/us/act/1956-07-27/ch750], [70 Stat. 696][/us/stat/70/696]; Feb. 16, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–384][/us/pl/86/384], [74 Stat. 3][/us/stat/74/3]; Oct. 4, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–365][/us/pl/87/365], [75 Stat. 784][/us/stat/75/784], which authorized appropriations for the Department of State for the fiscal years 1963 and 1964, not in excess of $50,000 per fiscal year, to meet the obligations of the United States as a member of the Inter-American Children’s Institute, has been omitted because the authorization has not been extended for later than the 1963 and 1964 fiscal years.
