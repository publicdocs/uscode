## § 287f. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act Oct. 22, 1951, ch. 533, title I][/us/act/1951-10-22/ch533/tI], [65 Stat. 577][/us/stat/65/577], which authorized the Department of State to acquire surplus property for the United Nations and authorized the reduction of the United States contribution to the United Nations by the value of the property acquired and expenses incidental thereto, was from the Department of State Appropriation Act, 1952, and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts. Similar provisions were contained in [act Sept. 6, 1950, ch. 896][/us/act/1950-09-06/ch896], ch. III, title I, [64 Stat. 610][/us/stat/64/610].
