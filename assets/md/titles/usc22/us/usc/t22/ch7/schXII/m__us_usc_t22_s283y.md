## § 283y. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–118, title VII][/us/pl/95/118/tVII], § 702, Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1070][/us/stat/91/1070]

    Section, [Pub. L. 86–147][/us/pl/86/147], § 28, as added [Pub. L. 94–302, title I][/us/pl/94/302/tI], § 103(a)(1), May 31, 1976, [90 Stat. 592][/us/stat/90/592]; H. Res. 5, Jan. 4, 1977, set forth provisions relating to United States participation in financial assistance by Inter-American Development Bank to any country engaging in a consistent pattern of gross violations of internationally recognized human rights. See [section 262d of this title][/us/usc/t22/s262d].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 3, 1977, see [section 1001 of Pub. L. 95–118][/us/pl/95/118/s1001], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 282i of this title][/us/usc/t22/s282i].
