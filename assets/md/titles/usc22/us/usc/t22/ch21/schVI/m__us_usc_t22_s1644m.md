## § 1644m. Applicability of administrative provisions of subchapter I

    To the extent they are not inconsistent with the provisions of this subchapter, the following provisions of subchapter I of this chapter shall be applicable to this subchapter: subsections (b), (c), (d), (e), (h), and (j) of [section 1623 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1623]; subsections (c), (d), (e), and (f) of [section 1626 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1626].

([Mar. 10, 1950, ch. 54][/us/act/1950-03-10/ch54], title VI, § 613, as added [Pub. L. 94–542][/us/pl/94/542], Oct. 18, 1976, [90 Stat. 2512][/us/stat/90/2512].)
