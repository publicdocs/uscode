## § 1439. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–470, title I, § 117][/us/pl/96/470/s117], Oct. 19, 1980, [94 Stat. 2240][/us/stat/94/2240]

    Section, acts [Jan. 27, 1948, ch. 36][/us/act/1948-01-27/ch36], title X, § 1008, [62 Stat. 14][/us/stat/62/14]; Sept. 21, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–256, § 111(a)(2)][/us/pl/87/256/s111/a/2], [75 Stat. 538][/us/stat/75/538]; Oct. 26, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–475, § 7][/us/pl/93/475/s7], [88 Stat. 1440][/us/stat/88/1440]; 1977 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 7(a)(1), 42 F.R. 62461, [91 Stat. 1637][/us/stat/91/1637], required the Director to submit annual reports to Congress on expenditures made and activities carried on under this chapter, including appraisals and measurements, where feasible, as to the effectiveness of the several programs in each country where conducted. [Pub. L. 87–256, § 111(9)(2)][/us/pl/87/256/s111/9/2], Sept. 21, 1961, [75 Stat. 538][/us/stat/75/538], previously repealed this section insofar as it related to educational exchange activities. See [section 2458(b) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2458/b].
