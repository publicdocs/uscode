## § 1480. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 657][/us/stat/80/657], 658

    Section, [act June 20, 1956, ch. 414][/us/act/1956-06-20/ch414], title I, § 107, [70 Stat. 304][/us/stat/70/304], related to maximum rates of per diem in lieu of subsistence payable to foreign participants. See [section 2679 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2679].

    Acts [July 10, 1952, ch. 651][/us/act/1952-07-10/ch651], title I, § 109, [66 Stat. 555][/us/stat/66/555]; [Aug. 5, 1953, ch. 328][/us/act/1953-08-05/ch328], title I, § 108, [67 Stat. 371][/us/stat/67/371]; [July 2, 1954, ch. 456][/us/act/1954-07-02/ch456], title I, § 107, [68 Stat. 418][/us/stat/68/418]; [July 7, 1955, ch. 279][/us/act/1955-07-07/ch279], title I, § 107, [69 Stat. 270][/us/stat/69/270], which contained provision similar to [section 1480 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1480], were repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 657][/us/stat/80/657], 658.
