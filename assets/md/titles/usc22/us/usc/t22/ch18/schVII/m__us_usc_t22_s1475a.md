## § 1475a. Replacement of passenger motor vehicles

    The exchange allowances or proceeds derived from the exchange or sale of passenger motor vehicles used abroad for purposes of this chapter or the Mutual Educational and Cultural Exchange Act of 1961 \[[22 U.S.C. 2451][/us/usc/t22/s2451] et seq.\] shall be available without fiscal year limitation for replacement of an equal number of such vehicles in accordance with [section 503 of title 40][/us/usc/t40/s503].

([Jan. 27, 1948, ch. 36][/us/act/1948-01-27/ch36], title VIII, § 806, as added [Pub. L. 94–350, title II][/us/pl/94/350/tII], § 204, July 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 830][/us/stat/90/830]; amended [Pub. L. 96–60, title II][/us/pl/96/60/tII], § 204(c), Aug. 15, 1979, [93 Stat. 400][/us/stat/93/400].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Mutual Educational and Cultural Exchange Act of 1961, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 87–256][/us/pl/87/256], Sept. 21, 1961, [75 Stat. 527][/us/stat/75/527], as amended, which is classified principally to chapter 33 (§ 2451 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 2451 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s2451] and Tables.

 __Codification__ 

    “[Section 503 of title 40][/us/usc/t40/s503]” substituted in text for “section 201(c) of the Federal Property and Administrative Services Act of 1949” on authority of [Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], § 5(c), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1303][/us/stat/116/1303], the first section of which enacted Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works.

 __Amendments__ 

    1979—[Pub. L. 96–60][/us/pl/96/60] substituted “shall be available” for “are authorized to be made available”.

 __Effective Date of 1979 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 96–60][/us/pl/96/60] effective Oct. 1, 1979, and applicable only with respect to funds appropriated after Aug. 15, 1979, where new authorities provide for expenditures of appropriated funds, see [section 209 of Pub. L. 96–60][/us/pl/96/60/s209], set out as a note under [section 1471 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s1471].
