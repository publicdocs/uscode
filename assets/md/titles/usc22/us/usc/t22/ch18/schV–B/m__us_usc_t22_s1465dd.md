## § 1465dd. Assistance from other Government agencies

    In order to assist the Broadcasting Board of Governors in carrying out the provisions of this subchapter, any agency or instrumentality of the United States may sell, loan, lease, or grant property (including interests therein) and may perform administrative and technical support and services at the request of the Board.

([Pub. L. 101–246, title II][/us/pl/101/246/tII], § 246, Feb. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 62][/us/stat/104/62]; [Pub. L. 105–277, div. G][/us/pl/105/277/dG], subdiv. A, title XIII, § 1325(1), (3), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–781][/us/stat/112/2681-781].)

 __Repeal of Section__ 

    Section repealed upon transmittal of determination by President under [section 6063(c)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s6063/c/3] that democratically elected government in Cuba is in power, see [section 6037(c) of this title][/us/usc/t22/s6037/c].

 __Codification__ 

    Section was enacted as part of the Television Broadcasting to Cuba Act which comprises this subchapter, and not as part of the United States Information and Educational Exchange Act of 1948 which comprises this chapter.

 __Amendments__ 

    1998—[Pub. L. 105–277][/us/pl/105/277] substituted “Broadcasting Board of Governors” for “United States Information Agency” and “the Board” for “the Agency”.

 __Effective Date of 1998 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 105–277][/us/pl/105/277] effective Oct. 1, 1999, see [section 1301 of Pub. L. 105–277][/us/pl/105/277/s1301], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 6531 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s6531].
