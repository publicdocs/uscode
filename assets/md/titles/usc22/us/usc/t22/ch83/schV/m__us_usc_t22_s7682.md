## § 7682. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __In general__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated to the President such sums as may be necessary for the fiscal year 2004 and each fiscal year thereafter to carry out [section 262p–8 of this title][/us/usc/t22/s262p–8].

    (b) __Availability of funds__ 

        Amounts appropriated pursuant to subsection (a) are authorized to remain available until expended.

([Pub. L. 108–25, title V][/us/pl/108/25/tV], § 503, May 27, 2003, [117 Stat. 749][/us/stat/117/749].)
