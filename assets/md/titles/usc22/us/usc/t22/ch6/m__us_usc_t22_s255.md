## § 255. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21], [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section, R.S. § 4062, related to assaulting, etc., a foreign minister. See sections 112 and 1545 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

§§ 255a, 255b. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections, [act Feb. 15, 1938, ch. 29][/us/act/1938-02-15/ch29], §§ 1, 2, [52 Stat. 30][/us/stat/52/30], which related to protection for officers and buildings, jurisdiction of offenses and penalties, and permitted picketing, are of particular application to the District of Columbia.
