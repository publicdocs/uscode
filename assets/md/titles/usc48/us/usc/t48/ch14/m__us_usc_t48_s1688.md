## § 1688. Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands Economic Development Loan Fund

    For the purpose of promoting economic development in the Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands, there is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary of the Interior, for payment to the government of the Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands as a grant in accordance with the provisions of sections 1688 to 1693 of this title, an amount which when added to the development fund established pursuant to [section 3 of the Act of August 22, 1964][/us/act/1964-08-22/s3] ([78 Stat. 601][/us/stat/78/601]), as augmented by subsequent Federal grants, will create a total fund of $5,000,000, which shall thereafter be known as the Trust Territory Economic Development Loan Fund.

([Pub. L. 92–257, § 1][/us/pl/92/257/s1], Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 87][/us/stat/86/87].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 3 of the Act of August 22, 1964][/us/act/1964-08-22/s3], referred to in text, is section 3 of act Aug. 22, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–487][/us/pl/88/487], [78 Stat. 601][/us/stat/78/601], which is not classified to the Code.
