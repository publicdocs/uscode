## § 793b. Repealed. [July 3, 1950, ch. 446, § 5(2)][/us/act/1950-07-03/ch446/s5/2], [64 Stat. 320][/us/stat/64/320]

    Section, [act Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145, § 49b][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145/s49b], as added [Aug. 5, 1947, ch. 490, § 6][/us/act/1947-08-05/ch490/s6], [61 Stat. 772][/us/stat/61/772], and amended [June 24, 1948, ch. 610, § 1][/us/act/1948-06-24/ch610/s1], [62 Stat. 579][/us/stat/62/579], provided for a Coordinator of Federal Agencies in Puerto Rico, his appointment, compensation and duties, and required President to prescribe rules and regulations to carry out provisions of former [section 793 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s793].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective July 25, 1952, see note set out under [section 732 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s732].
