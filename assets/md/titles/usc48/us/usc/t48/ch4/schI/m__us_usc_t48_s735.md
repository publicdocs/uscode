## § 735. Repealed. [July 3, 1950, ch. 446][/us/act/1950-07-03/ch446], § 5(2), [64 Stat. 320][/us/stat/64/320]

    Section, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 57, [39 Stat. 968][/us/stat/39/968]; [May 17, 1932, ch. 190][/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190], [47 Stat. 158][/us/stat/47/158], continued certain Puerto Rican Laws in force and authorized the legislative authority to modify or repeal laws. Section 15 of [act Apr. 12, 1900, ch. 191][/us/act/1900-04-12/ch191], [31 Stat. 80][/us/stat/31/80], formerly cited as a credit to this section, was not repealed by act July 3, 1950.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective July 25, 1952, see Effective Date of Repeal note set out under [section 732 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s732].
