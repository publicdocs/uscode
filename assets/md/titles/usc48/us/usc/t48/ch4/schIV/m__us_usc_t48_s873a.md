## § 873a. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 39, [62 Stat. 992][/us/stat/62/992]

    Section, [act Feb. 12, 1940, ch. 25][/us/act/1940-02-12/ch25], § 1, [54 Stat. 22][/us/stat/54/22], which related to rules governing civil cases. See [section 2072 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s2072], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s1], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
