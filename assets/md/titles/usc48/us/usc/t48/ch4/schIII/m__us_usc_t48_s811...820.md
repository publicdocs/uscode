## §§ 811 to 820. Repealed. [July 3, 1950, ch. 446][/us/act/1950-07-03/ch446], § 5(2), [64 Stat. 320][/us/stat/64/320]

    Section 811, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 25, [39 Stat. 958][/us/stat/39/958]; [May 17, 1932, ch. 190][/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190], [47 Stat. 158][/us/stat/47/158], vested all local legislative powers in Puerto Rico, with certain exceptions, in “Legislature of Puerto Rico”, consisting of a “senate” and a “house of representatives”.

    Section 812, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 26, [39 Stat. 958][/us/stat/39/958]; [May 17, 1932, ch. 190][/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190], [47 Stat. 158][/us/stat/47/158], related to Senate of Puerto Rico, its members, election and powers.

    Section 813, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 27, [39 Stat. 959][/us/stat/39/959]; [May 17, 1932, ch. 190][/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190], [47 Stat. 158][/us/stat/47/158], related to House of Representatives and its members and their election.

    Section 814, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 28, [39 Stat. 959][/us/stat/39/959]; [May 17, 1932, ch. 190][/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190], [47 Stat. 158][/us/stat/47/158], provided for division of Puerto Rico into thirty-five representative and seven senatorial districts.

    Section 814a, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 35, [39 Stat. 963][/us/stat/39/963]; [May 17, 1932, ch. 190][/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190], [47 Stat. 158][/us/stat/47/158], related to qualification of electors.

    Section 815, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 29, [39 Stat. 959][/us/stat/39/959]; [May 17, 1932, ch. 190][/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190], [47 Stat. 158][/us/stat/47/158], provided for time of holding elections and revision of boundaries of senatorial and representative districts and municipalities.

    Section 816, [act Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 32, [39 Stat. 960][/us/stat/39/960], related to powers of senate and house of representatives, including determination of election and qualifications of members.

    Section 817, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 33, [39 Stat. 960][/us/stat/39/960]; [Mar. 4, 1927, ch. 503][/us/act/1927-03-04/ch503], § 5, [44 Stat. 1420][/us/stat/44/1420], provided for holding of annual sessions of legislature and time for convening.

    Section 818, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 33, [39 Stat. 960][/us/stat/39/960]; [Mar. 4, 1927, ch. 503][/us/act/1927-03-04/ch503], § 5, [44 Stat. 1420][/us/stat/44/1420], authorized governor to call special sessions of legislature or senate.

    Section 819, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 30, [39 Stat. 959][/us/stat/39/959]; [May 17, 1932, ch. 190][/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190], [47 Stat. 158][/us/stat/47/158]; [June 1, 1938, ch. 308][/us/act/1938-06-01/ch308], [52 Stat. 595][/us/stat/52/595], related to term of office of senators and representatives, filling of vacancies, and limitations upon appointment to other offices of those legislative members.

    Section 820, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145][/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145], § 31, [39 Stat. 960][/us/stat/39/960]; [Mar. 4, 1927, ch. 503][/us/act/1927-03-04/ch503], § 4, [44 Stat. 1420][/us/stat/44/1420]; [May 17, 1932, ch. 190][/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190], [47 Stat. 158][/us/stat/47/158]; [June 24, 1948, ch. 610][/us/act/1948-06-24/ch610], § 4, [62 Stat. 580][/us/stat/62/580], related to compensation and mileage of members of senate and house of representatives.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal of sections 811 to 820 effective July 25, 1952, see note set out under [section 732 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s732].
