## § 1826. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 95–157][/us/pl/95/157], § 6, Nov. 8, 1977, [91 Stat. 1267][/us/stat/91/1267].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 1694e of this title][/us/usc/t48/s1694e].

 __Effective Date__ 

    For effective date of this section, see [section 1825 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s1825] and Effective Date of Constitution note thereunder.
