## § 1825. Effective date

    This subchapter shall come into force upon its approval or at the time proclaimed by the President for the Constitution of the Northern Mariana Islands to become effective, whichever is the later date.

([Pub. L. 95–157, § 5][/us/pl/95/157/s5], Nov. 8, 1977, [91 Stat. 1267][/us/stat/91/1267].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 1694d of this title][/us/usc/t48/s1694d].

 __Effective Date of Constitution__ 

    For provisions of proclamation of President relating to effective date for Constitution of Northern Mariana Islands, see Proc. No. 4534, Oct. 24, 1977, 42 F.R. 56593, set out as a note under [section 1801 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s1801].
