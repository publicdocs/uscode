## §§ 326 to 330. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 326 to 330, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 326, acts [Feb. 12, 1925, ch. 225][/us/act/1925-02-12/ch225], title II, [43 Stat. 930][/us/stat/43/930]; [June 30, 1932, ch. 320, § 1][/us/act/1932-06-30/ch320/s1], [47 Stat. 446][/us/stat/47/446], authorized incurring of obligations for roads, bridges, and trails in advance of appropriations in certain cases.

    Section 327, acts [June 30, 1921, ch. 33, § 1][/us/act/1921-06-30/ch33/s1], [42 Stat. 90][/us/stat/42/90]; [June 30, 1932, ch. 320, § 1][/us/act/1932-06-30/ch320/s1], [47 Stat. 446][/us/stat/47/446], authorized Secretary of the Interior to accept contributions from Territory or from other sources for use in construction, maintenance, or repair of roads, bridges, ferries, trails, and related works in the Territory.

    Section 328, [act July 9, 1918, ch. 143][/us/act/1918-07-09/ch143], [40 Stat. 863][/us/stat/40/863], related to estimates for work on roads.

    Section 329, acts [Jan. 27, 1905, ch. 277, § 2][/us/act/1905-01-27/ch277/s2], [33 Stat. 616][/us/stat/33/616]; [May 14, 1906, ch. 2458, § 2][/us/act/1906-05-14/ch2458/s2], [34 Stat. 192][/us/stat/34/192], related to expenses of board in addition to salary.

    Section 330, [act Apr. 27, 1914, ch. 72][/us/act/1914-04-27/ch72], [38 Stat. 366][/us/stat/38/366], related to per diem commutation of Army officer member of board.
