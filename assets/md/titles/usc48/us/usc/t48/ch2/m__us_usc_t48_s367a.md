## § 367a. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act Oct. 9, 1942, ch. 584][/us/act/1942-10-09/ch584], § 5, [56 Stat. 779][/us/stat/56/779], which made public land claimants liable for fees, commissions, or purchase money required by law to be paid, was transferred to [section 79d of Title 43][/us/usc/t43/s79d], Public Lands.
