## §§ 461 to 466. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 461, acts [May 14, 1898, ch. 299, § 10][/us/act/1898-05-14/ch299/s10], [30 Stat. 413][/us/stat/30/413]; [Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 323][/us/act/1927-03-03/ch323], [44 Stat. 1364][/us/stat/44/1364]; [May 26, 1934, ch. 357][/us/act/1934-05-26/ch357], [48 Stat. 809][/us/stat/48/809]; Aug. 23, 1958, [Pub.     Section 463, [act May 14, 1898, ch. 299, § 10][/us/act/1898-05-14/ch299/s10], [30 Stat. 413][/us/stat/30/413], which related to several claimants of same tract, was transferred to [section 687a–3 of Title 43][/us/usc/t43/s687a–3], and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)][/us/pl/94/579/s703/a], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789][/us/stat/90/2789].

    Section 464, [act May 14, 1898, ch. 299, § 10][/us/act/1898-05-14/ch299/s10], [30 Stat. 413][/us/stat/30/413], which reserved landing places along water front for natives, was transferred to [section 687a–4 of Title 43][/us/usc/t43/s687a–4], and was subsequently repealed by [Pub.
