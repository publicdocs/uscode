###### §§ 73, 73a. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 73 and 73a, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 73, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 387][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch387], § 5, [37 Stat. 513][/us/stat/37/513]; [Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 363][/us/act/1927-03-03/ch363], § 1, [44 Stat. 1392][/us/stat/44/1392]; [Mar. 26, 1934, ch. 86][/us/act/1934-03-26/ch86], § 1, [48 Stat. 465][/us/stat/48/465], specified time of election of members of the legislature.

    Section 73a, [act Mar. 26, 1934, ch. 86][/us/act/1934-03-26/ch86], § 4, [43 Stat. 466][/us/stat/43/466], empowered legislature to change date of elections.
