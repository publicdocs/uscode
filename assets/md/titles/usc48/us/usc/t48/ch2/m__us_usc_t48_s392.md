## § 392. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act May 14, 1898, ch. 299][/us/act/1898-05-14/ch299], § 13, [30 Stat. 415][/us/stat/30/415], which provided for reciprocity with Canada as to mining rights, was omitted in view of the admission of Alaska into the Union.
