## §§ 46–1 to 46–3. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 46–1 to 46–3, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 46–1, [act July 28, 1956, ch. 772, title I][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772/tI], § 101, [70 Stat. 709][/us/stat/70/709], authorized territorial legislature to enact laws on subject of mental health.

    Section 46–2, [act July 28, 1956, ch. 772, title I][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772/tI], § 102, [70 Stat. 709][/us/stat/70/709], related to jurisdiction, functions and duties of commissioners and courts in carrying out [section 46–1 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s46–1].

    Section 46–3, [act July 28, 1956, ch. 772, title II][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772/tII], § 202, [70 Stat. 711][/us/stat/70/711], prescribed a land-grant program for purpose of [section 46–1 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s46–1].
