###### §§ 310, 311. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 310 and 311, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 310, [act May 26, 1900, ch. 586][/us/act/1900-05-26/ch586], [31 Stat. 206][/us/stat/31/206], allowed conduct of commercial business over military telegraph and cable lines.

    Section 311, [act May 23, 1941, ch. 130][/us/act/1941-05-23/ch130], § 1, [55 Stat. 190][/us/stat/55/190], allowed payment of charges for interconnection between radio-telephone facilities of Alaska Communication System and commercial facilities to be made out of receipts of the Alaska Communication System.
