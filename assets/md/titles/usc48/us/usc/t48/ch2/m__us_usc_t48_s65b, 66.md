## §§ 65b, 66. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 65b, 66, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 65b, [act Apr. 3, 1944, ch. 155, § 1][/us/act/1944-04-03/ch155/s1], [58 Stat. 187][/us/stat/58/187], prescribed salary of secretary of Territory.

    Section 66, [act Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1497, § 1][/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1497/s1], [33 Stat. 1265][/us/stat/33/1265], related to fees of secretary of Territory.
