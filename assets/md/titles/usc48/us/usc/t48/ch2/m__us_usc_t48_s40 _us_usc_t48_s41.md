###### §§ 40, 41. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 40 and 41, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 40, [act June 6, 1900, ch. 786][/us/act/1900-06-06/ch786], § 31, [31 Stat. 332][/us/stat/31/332], related to court rooms and offices of civil government.

    Section 41, acts [Jan. 27, 1905, ch. 277][/us/act/1905-01-27/ch277], § 1, [33 Stat. 616][/us/stat/33/616]; [May 14, 1906, ch. 2458][/us/act/1906-05-14/ch2458], § 1, [34 Stat. 192][/us/stat/34/192]; [Feb. 6, 1909, ch. 80][/us/act/1909-02-06/ch80], § 7, [35 Stat. 601][/us/stat/35/601]; [Mar. 3, 1913, ch. 109][/us/act/1913-03-03/ch109], [37 Stat. 728][/us/stat/37/728], related to deposits to Alaska fund in Treasury Department.
