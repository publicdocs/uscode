###### §§ 292, 293. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 292 and 293, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 292, [act Apr. 13, 1934, ch. 119][/us/act/1934-04-13/ch119], § 2, [48 Stat. 583][/us/stat/48/583], provided for manufacture and sale of intoxicating liquors.

    Section 293, [act Apr. 13, 1934, ch. 119][/us/act/1934-04-13/ch119], § 3, [48 Stat. 584][/us/stat/48/584], ratified and approved act to create board of liquor control, and prescribed penalties for violation of rules and regulations of the board.
