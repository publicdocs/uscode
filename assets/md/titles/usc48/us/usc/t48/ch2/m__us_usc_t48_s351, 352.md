## §§ 351, 352. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 351, [act Mar. 3, 1889, ch. 424, § 1][/us/act/1889-03-03/ch424/s1], [30 Stat. 1098][/us/stat/30/1098], which extended to Territory the system of public land surveys, was transferred to [section 751a of Title 43][/us/usc/t43/s751a], Public Lands.

    Section 352, acts [Mar. 2, 1907, ch. 2537, § 4][/us/act/1907-03-02/ch2537/s4], [34 Stat. 1232][/us/stat/34/1232]; [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1144][/us/stat/43/1144]; [Oct. 9, 1942, ch. 584, § 2][/us/act/1942-10-09/ch584/s2], [56 Stat. 779][/us/stat/56/779], which provided for making of land surveys in Nome and Fairbanks districts, was transferred to [section 751b of Title 43][/us/usc/t43/s751b].
