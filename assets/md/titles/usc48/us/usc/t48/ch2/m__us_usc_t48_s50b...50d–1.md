## §§ 50b to 50d–1. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 50b to 50d–1, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 50b, [act Mar. 7, 1928, ch. 137][/us/act/1928-03-07/ch137], § 1, [45 Stat. 239][/us/stat/45/239], authorized Secretary of the Interior to accept donations for school, medical, and reindeer service.

    Section 50c, [act May 14, 1930, ch. 273][/us/act/1930-05-14/ch273], § 1, [46 Stat. 321][/us/stat/46/321], related to availability of appropriations for education, medical relief, and reindeer.

    Section 50d, [act May 9, 1938, ch. 187][/us/act/1938-05-09/ch187], § 1, [52 Stat. 311][/us/stat/52/311], empowered Secretary of the Interior to authorize officers to incur obligations for benefit of natives prior to appropriation.

    Section 50d–1, [act June 1, 1944, ch. 220][/us/act/1944-06-01/ch220], § 1, [58 Stat. 266][/us/stat/58/266], empowered Secretary to authorize officers to incur obligations for benefit of natives in excess of current appropriations.
