## § 341. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act Mar. 30, 1948, ch. 162][/us/act/1948-03-30/ch162], [62 Stat. 100][/us/stat/62/100], which made provision for occupancy and use of national-forest lands under permit and dealt with period of such permits, size of area allotted, prohibitions, and the termination of permits, was transferred to [section 497a of Title 16][/us/usc/t16/s497a], Conservation.
