## §§ 61 to 64. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 61 to 64, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 61, acts [June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 2][/us/act/1900-06-06/ch786/s2], [31 Stat. 321][/us/stat/31/321]; [May 29, 1928, ch. 901, § 1(109)][/us/act/1928-05-29/ch901/s1/109], [45 Stat. 994][/us/stat/45/994]; [July 25, 1947, ch. 332, § 1][/us/act/1947-07-25/ch332/s1], [61 Stat. 459][/us/stat/61/459], authorized appointment of a Governor for Territory, and detailed his duties.

    Section 62, acts [June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 10][/us/act/1900-06-06/ch786/s10], [31 Stat. 325][/us/stat/31/325]; [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462], [43 Stat. 1181][/us/stat/43/1181]; [Mar. 4, 1931, ch. 516][/us/act/1931-03-04/ch516], [46 Stat. 1530][/us/stat/46/1530]; [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 13][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s13], [62 Stat. 987][/us/stat/62/987]; [Oct. 15, 1949, ch. 695, § 5(a)][/us/act/1949-10-15/ch695/s5/a], [63 Stat. 880][/us/stat/63/880], related to appointment of Governor and his compensation. Acts Mar. 3, 1925, Mar. 4, 1931, and Oct. 15, 1949, were repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 646][/us/stat/80/646], 648, 655.

    Section 63, acts [June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 10][/us/act/1900-06-06/ch786/s10], [31 Stat. 325][/us/stat/31/325]; [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 13][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s13], [62 Stat. 987][/us/stat/62/987], related to expenses of Governor.

    Section 64, [act June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 2][/us/act/1900-06-06/ch786/s2], [31 Stat. 321][/us/stat/31/321], directed Governor to make an annual report to President, and empowered the President to confirm or annul acts of Governor.
