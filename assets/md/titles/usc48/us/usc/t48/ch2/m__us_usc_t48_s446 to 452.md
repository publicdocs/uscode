## §§ 446 to 452. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–252, § 1][/us/pl/86/252/s1], Sept. 9, 1959, [73 Stat. 490][/us/stat/73/490]

    Section 446, [act Oct. 20, 1914, ch. 330, § 11][/us/act/1914-10-20/ch330/s11], [38 Stat. 744][/us/stat/38/744], related to reservation by the United States in leases, entries, etc.

    Section 447, [act Oct. 20, 1914, ch. 330, § 12][/us/act/1914-10-20/ch330/s12], [38 Stat. 744][/us/stat/38/744], related to assignment or subletting of leases.

    Section 448, [act Oct. 20, 1914, ch. 330, § 13][/us/act/1914-10-20/ch330/s13], [38 Stat. 744][/us/stat/38/744], related to possession of lessee as possession of the United States.

    Section 449, [act Oct. 20, 1914, ch. 330, § 14][/us/act/1914-10-20/ch330/s14], [38 Stat. 744][/us/stat/38/744], related to forfeiture or cancellation of leases.

    Section 450, [act Oct. 20, 1914, ch. 330, § 16][/us/act/1914-10-20/ch330/s16], [38 Stat. 745][/us/stat/38/745], related to statements, representations, and reports.

    Section 451, [act Oct. 20, 1914, ch. 330, § 17][/us/act/1914-10-20/ch330/s17], [38 Stat. 745][/us/stat/38/745], related to promulgation of rules and regulations. See [section 189 of Title 30][/us/usc/t30/s189], Mineral Lands and Mining.

    Section 452, [act Oct. 20, 1914, ch. 330, § 15][/us/act/1914-10-20/ch330/s15], [38 Stat. 745][/us/stat/38/745], related to limitation on disposal of coal lands. See [section 193 of Title 30][/us/usc/t30/s193].
