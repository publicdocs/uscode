## §§ 241 to 248b. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 241 to 248b, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 241, acts [June 26, 1906, ch. 3547][/us/act/1906-06-26/ch3547], § 12, [34 Stat. 480][/us/stat/34/480]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736], empowered Secretary to deputize officers and employees of the Department as law enforcement officers.

    Section 242, acts [June 26, 1906, ch. 3547][/us/act/1906-06-26/ch3547], § 14, [34 Stat. 481][/us/stat/34/481]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736], related to jurisdiction of prosecutions for violations of sections 230 to 239, 241, and 242 of this title.

    Section 243, acts [June 14, 1906, ch. 3299][/us/act/1906-06-14/ch3299], § 1, [34 Stat. 263][/us/stat/34/263]; [June 25, 1938, ch. 689][/us/act/1938-06-25/ch689], [52 Stat. 1174][/us/stat/52/1174], prohibited fishing by aliens and permitted sales to aliens.

    Section 244, [act June 14, 1906, ch. 3299][/us/act/1906-06-14/ch3299], § 2, [34 Stat. 264][/us/stat/34/264], prescribed penalties for violations of sections 243 to 247 of this title.

    Section 245, [act June 14, 1906, ch. 3299][/us/act/1906-06-14/ch3299], § 3, [34 Stat. 264][/us/stat/34/264], provided for jurisdiction of prosecutions under sections 243 to 247 of this title.

    Section 246, acts [June 14, 1906, ch. 3299][/us/act/1906-06-14/ch3299], § 4, [34 Stat. 264][/us/stat/34/264]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736]; [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], §§ 1, 20, [63 Stat. 495][/us/stat/63/495], 501, authorized searches and seizures of vessels.

    Section 247, acts [June 14, 1906, ch. 3299][/us/act/1906-06-14/ch3299], § 5, [34 Stat. 264][/us/stat/34/264]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736]; [Jan. 28, 1915, ch. 20][/us/act/1915-01-28/ch20], § 1, [38 Stat. 800][/us/stat/38/800]; [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], §§ 1, 20, [63 Stat. 495][/us/stat/63/495], 561, empowered Secretary to promulgate rules and regulations to carry into effect provisions of sections 243 to 246 of this title.

    Section 248, acts [Aug. 18, 1941, ch. 368][/us/act/1941-08-18/ch368], § 1, [55 Stat. 632][/us/stat/55/632]; [June 29, 1956, ch. 460][/us/act/1956-06-29/ch460], §§ 1, 2, [70 Stat. 372][/us/stat/70/372], provided for protection of walruses.

    Section 248a, [act Aug. 18, 1941, ch. 368][/us/act/1941-08-18/ch368], § 2, [55 Stat. 633][/us/stat/55/633], prescribed duties of law enforcement officers, and for forfeiture of equipment of convicted persons.

    Section 248b, [act Aug. 18, 1941, ch. 368][/us/act/1941-08-18/ch368], § 3, [55 Stat. 633][/us/stat/55/633], defined terms used in sections 248 to 248b of this title.
