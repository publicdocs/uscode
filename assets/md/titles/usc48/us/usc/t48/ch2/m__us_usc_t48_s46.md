## § 46. Repealed. [July 28, 1956, ch. 772][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772], title III, § 301(a)(2), [70 Stat. 712][/us/stat/70/712]

    Section, acts [Feb. 6, 1909, ch. 80, § 7][/us/act/1909-02-06/ch80/s7], [35 Stat. 601][/us/stat/35/601]; [Oct. 14, 1942, ch. 601, § 2][/us/act/1942-10-14/ch601/s2], [56 Stat. 782][/us/stat/56/782]; [July 28, 1956, ch. 722][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch722], title III, § 301(b)(1), [70 Stat. 712][/us/stat/70/712], authorized Governor of Alaska or his designee to contract for care and custody of insane persons.

§§ 46–1 to 46–3. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 46–1 to 46–3, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

    Section 46–1, [act July 28, 1956, ch. 772][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772], title I, § 101, [70 Stat. 709][/us/stat/70/709], authorized territorial legislature to enact laws on subject of mental health.

    Section 46–2, [act July 28, 1956, ch. 772][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772], title I, § 102, [70 Stat. 709][/us/stat/70/709], related to jurisdiction, functions and duties of commissioners and courts in carrying out [section 46–1 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s46–1].

    Section 46–3, [act July 28, 1956, ch. 772][/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772], title II, § 202, [70 Stat. 711][/us/stat/70/711], prescribed a land-grant program for purpose of [section 46–1 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s46–1].
