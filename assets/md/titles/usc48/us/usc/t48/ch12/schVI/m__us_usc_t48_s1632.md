## § 1632. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–496, § 14][/us/pl/90/496/s14], Aug. 23, 1968, [82 Stat. 842][/us/stat/82/842]

    Section, [act July 22, 1954, ch. 558, § 19][/us/act/1954-07-22/ch558/s19], [68 Stat. 505][/us/stat/68/505], authorized Comptroller General of United States to review annually the office and activities of Government Comptroller of Virgin Islands, and report thereon to Governor, Secretary of the Interior, and Congress.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 90–496, § 14][/us/pl/90/496/s14], Aug. 23, 1968, [82 Stat. 842][/us/stat/82/842], provided that the repeal of this section is effective on the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 90–496][/us/pl/90/496], which was approved Aug. 23, 1968.
