## § 2217. Savings clause

    Nothing in this subchapter is intended to change or alter any Federal legal requirements or laws.

([Pub. L. 114–187, title V][/us/pl/114/187/tV], § 507, June 30, 2016, [130 Stat. 603][/us/stat/130/603].)
