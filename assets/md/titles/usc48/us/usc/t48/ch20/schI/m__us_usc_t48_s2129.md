## § 2129. Ethics

    (a) __Conflict of interest__ 

        Notwithstanding any ethics provision governing employees of the covered territory, all members and staff of the Oversight Board shall be subject to the Federal conflict of interest requirements described in [section 208 of title 18][/us/usc/t18/s208].

    (b) __Financial disclosure__ 

        Notwithstanding any ethics provision governing employees of the covered territory, all members of the Oversight Board and staff designated by the Oversight Board shall be subject to disclosure of their financial interests, the contents of which shall conform to the same requirements set forth in section 102 of the Ethics in Government Act of 1978 (5 U.S.C. App.).

([Pub. L. 114–187, title I][/us/pl/114/187/tI], § 109, June 30, 2016, [130 Stat. 563][/us/stat/130/563].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Section 102 of the Ethics in Government Act of 1978, referred to in subsec. (b), is [section 102 of Pub. L. 95–521][/us/pl/95/521/s102], which is set out in the Appendix to Title 5.
