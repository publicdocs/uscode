## § 1908. Transitional immigration rules

    (a) __Citizen of Northern Mariana Islands__ 

        Any person who is a citizen of the Northern Mariana Islands, as that term is defined in [section 24(b) of the Act of December 8, 1983][/us/act/1983-12-08/s24/b] ([97 Stat. 1465][/us/stat/97/1465]), is considered a citizen of the United States for purposes of entry into, permanent residence, and employment in the United States and its territories and possessions.

    (b) __Termination__ 

        The provisions of this section shall cease to be effective when section 301 of the Covenant to Establish a Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands in Political Union With the United States ([Public Law 94–241][/us/pl/94/241]) becomes effective pursuant to section 1003(c) of the Covenant.

([Pub. L. 99–239, title I][/us/pl/99/239/tI], § 108, Jan. 14, 1986, [99 Stat. 1798][/us/stat/99/1798].)
