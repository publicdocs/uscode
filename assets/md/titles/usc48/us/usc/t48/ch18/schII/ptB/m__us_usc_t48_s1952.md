## § 1952. Fiscal procedures assistance

    Upon request of the Government of Palau, the Secretary of the Interior shall provide assistance to the Government of Palau to develop and promulgate regulations for the effective expenditure of funds received pursuant to this joint resolution, Public Laws 99–658 \[[48 U.S.C. 1931][/us/usc/t48/s1931] et seq.\] and 99–239 \[[48 U.S.C. 1901][/us/usc/t48/s1901] et seq., 2001 et seq.\], or any other Act of Congress.

([Pub. L. 101–219, title I, § 102][/us/pl/101/219/s102], Dec. 12, 1989, [103 Stat. 1870][/us/stat/103/1870].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This joint resolution, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 101–219][/us/pl/101/219], Dec. 12, 1989, [103 Stat. 1870][/us/stat/103/1870], which enacted this part and sections 1846 and 1972 of this title, amended sections 1615 and 1933 of this title and [section 3791 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s3791], The Public Health and Welfare, and enacted provisions set out as a note under [section 1905 of Title 44][/us/usc/t44/s1905], Public Printing and Documents. For complete classification of this joint resolution to the Code, see Tables.

    [Public Law 99–658][/us/pl/99/658], referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 99–658][/us/pl/99/658], Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3672][/us/stat/100/3672], as amended, which is classified generally to part A of this subchapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

    [Public Law 99–239][/us/pl/99/239], referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 99–239][/us/pl/99/239], Jan. 14, 1986, [99 Stat. 1770][/us/stat/99/1770], as amended, known as the Compact of Free Association Act of 1985, which is classified principally to part A of subchapter I of this chapter and chapter 19 (§ 2001 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1901 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s1901] and Tables.

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly set out as a note under [section 1681 of this title][/us/usc/t48/s1681].
