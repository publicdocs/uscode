## § 1667. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–576, title I][/us/pl/90/576/tI], § 103, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091][/us/stat/82/1091]

    Section, [Pub. L. 87–688][/us/pl/87/688], § 2, Sept. 25, 1962, [76 Stat. 586][/us/stat/76/586], extended to American Samoa the benefits of the Vocational Education Act of 1946 and authorized an annual appropriation of $80,000 therefor.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 90–576, title I][/us/pl/90/576/tI], § 103, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091][/us/stat/82/1091], provided that the repeal of this section is effective July 1, 1969.
