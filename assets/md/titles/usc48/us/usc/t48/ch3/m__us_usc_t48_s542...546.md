## §§ 542 to 546. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 542 to 546, relating to Territory of Hawaii, were omitted in view of admission of Hawaii into the Union.

    Section 542, acts [Apr. 30, 1900, ch. 339][/us/act/1900-04-30/ch339], § 77, [31 Stat. 156][/us/stat/31/156]; [Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 862][/us/act/1956-08-01/ch862], § 1, [70 Stat. 920][/us/stat/70/920], created posts of auditor and deputy auditor and prescribed their powers and duties.

    Section 542a, [act Apr. 30, 1900, ch. 339][/us/act/1900-04-30/ch339], § 77A, as added [Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 862][/us/act/1956-08-01/ch862], § 2, [70 Stat. 920][/us/stat/70/920], created position of post auditor and set out his duties, term, and powers.

    Section 543, [act Apr. 30, 1900, ch. 339][/us/act/1900-04-30/ch339], § 78, [31 Stat. 156][/us/stat/31/156], prescribed powers and duties of surveyor.

    Section 544, [act Apr. 30, 1900, ch. 339][/us/act/1900-04-30/ch339], § 79, [31 Stat. 156][/us/stat/31/156], prescribed powers and duties of high sheriff and deputies.

    Section 545, [act Apr. 30, 1900, ch. 339][/us/act/1900-04-30/ch339], § 106, as added [July 9, 1921, ch. 42][/us/act/1921-07-09/ch42], § 315, [42 Stat. 121][/us/stat/42/121], and amended Aug. 14, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–650][/us/pl/85/650], § 1, [72 Stat. 606][/us/stat/72/606], prescribed powers and duties of board of harbor commissioners and called for board supervision of moneys appropriated for harbor improvements.

    Section 546, acts [Apr. 30, 1900, ch. 339][/us/act/1900-04-30/ch339], § 80, [31 Stat. 156][/us/stat/31/156]; [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1465][/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1465], § 2, [33 Stat. 1035][/us/stat/33/1035]; [July 9, 1921, ch. 42][/us/act/1921-07-09/ch42], § 312, [42 Stat. 119][/us/stat/42/119]; [Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 862][/us/act/1956-08-01/ch862], § 1, [70 Stat. 920][/us/stat/70/920]; Aug. 28, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–793][/us/pl/85/793], §§ 1, 2, [72 Stat. 957][/us/stat/72/957], dealt with nomination and appointment of officers by governor and provided for their terms and salaries.
