## § 1356. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 35, [62 Stat. 991][/us/stat/62/991]

    Section, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 390][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch390], § 9, [37 Stat. 565][/us/stat/37/565]; [Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 370][/us/act/1922-09-21/ch370], § 3, [42 Stat. 1006][/us/stat/42/1006]; [Feb. 16, 1933, ch. 91][/us/act/1933-02-16/ch91], § 3, [47 Stat. 817][/us/stat/47/817], related to appeals from district courts. See sections 1291, 1292, and 1294 of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s1], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
