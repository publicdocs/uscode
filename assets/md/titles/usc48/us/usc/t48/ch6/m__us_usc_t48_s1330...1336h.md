## §§ 1330 to 1336h. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1330 to 1336h were omitted as not of general application, and as covered by the Canal Zone Code. The Canal Zone Code was subsequently redesignated the Panama Canal Code by [Pub. L. 96–70][/us/pl/96/70], § 3303(b), and repealed by [Pub. L. 104–201][/us/pl/104/201], § 3549.
