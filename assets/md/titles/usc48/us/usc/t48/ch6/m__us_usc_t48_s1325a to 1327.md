## §§ 1325a to 1327. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1325a to 1327 were omitted as not of general application, and as covered by the Canal Zone Code. The Canal Zone Code was subsequently redesignated the Panama Canal Code by [Pub. L. 96–70, § 3303(b)][/us/pl/96/70/s3303/b], and repealed by [Pub. L. 104–201, § 3549][/us/pl/104/201/s3549].

    Section 1325a, acts [Feb. 16, 1933, ch. 90, § 1][/us/act/1933-02-16/ch90/s1], [47 Stat. 813][/us/stat/47/813]; [Sept. 26, 1950, ch. 1049, § 2(b)][/us/act/1950-09-26/ch1049/s2/b], [64 Stat. 1038][/us/stat/64/1038], related to rules and regulations of the Customs Service.

    Section 1325b, [act Feb. 16, 1933, ch. 90, § 2][/us/act/1933-02-16/ch90/s2], [47 Stat. 813][/us/stat/47/813], related to powers of search, seizure, and arrest of customs officers.

    Section 1325c, acts [Feb. 16, 1933, ch. 90, § 3][/us/act/1933-02-16/ch90/s3], [47 Stat. 813][/us/stat/47/813]; [July 10, 1937, ch. 487, § 1][/us/act/1937-07-10/ch487/s1], [50 Stat. 509][/us/stat/50/509], related to unlawful entry or importation.

    Section 1325d, [act Feb. 16, 1933, ch. 90, § 4][/us/act/1933-02-16/ch90/s4], [47 Stat. 813][/us/stat/47/813], related to unmanifested merchandise.

    Section 1325e, [act Feb. 16, 1933, ch. 90, § 5][/us/act/1933-02-16/ch90/s5], [47 Stat. 814][/us/stat/47/814], related to unlisted sea stores.

    Section 1326, [act Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 371, § 8][/us/act/1916-08-21/ch371/s8], [39 Stat. 528][/us/stat/39/528], related to fees of customs officers.

    Section 1327, [act Aug. 1, 1914, ch. 223, § 4][/us/act/1914-08-01/ch223/s4], [38 Stat. 679][/us/stat/38/679], related to accounting by collection officers.
