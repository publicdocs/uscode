## §§ 1371 to 1371b. Repealed. [July 21, 1949, ch. 356][/us/act/1949-07-21/ch356], § 1(b), [63 Stat. 475][/us/stat/63/475]

    Section 1371, [act Mar. 2, 1931, ch. 375][/us/act/1931-03-02/ch375], § 1, [46 Stat. 1471][/us/stat/46/1471], related to employees entitled to retirement privileges.

    Section 1371a, acts [Mar. 2, 1931, ch. 375][/us/act/1931-03-02/ch375], § 2, [46 Stat. 1471][/us/stat/46/1471]; [June 19, 1934, ch. 667][/us/act/1934-06-19/ch667], § 1, [48 Stat. 1122][/us/stat/48/1122]; [July 29, 1942, ch. 536][/us/act/1942-07-29/ch536], § 1, [56 Stat. 726][/us/stat/56/726], related to automatic separation.

    Section 1371b, acts [Mar. 2, 1931, ch. 375][/us/act/1931-03-02/ch375], § 3, [46 Stat. 1472][/us/stat/46/1472]; [July 2, 1945, ch. 220][/us/act/1945-07-02/ch220], [59 Stat. 212][/us/stat/59/212], related to voluntary retirement.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Apr. 1, 1948, see [Act July 21, 1949, ch. 356][/us/act/1949-07-21/ch356], § 7(a), [63 Stat. 476][/us/stat/63/476].

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Sections were also repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 648][/us/stat/80/648].
