## §§ 1384 to 1387. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 1384, [act July 2, 1940, ch. 516][/us/act/1940-07-02/ch516], § 4, [54 Stat. 724][/us/stat/54/724]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 801, eff.     Section 1387, [act July 2, 1940, ch. 516][/us/act/1940-07-02/ch516], § 7, [54 Stat. 725][/us/stat/54/725], related to authorization of appropriations, and was transferred to [section 79e of Title 20][/us/usc/t20/s79e].
