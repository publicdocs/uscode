## § 1320. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 642][/us/stat/80/642]

    Section, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 390, § 5][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch390/s5], [37 Stat. 562][/us/stat/37/562], related to injuries to employees.

§§ 1321, 1322. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1321 and 1322 were omitted as not of general application, and as covered by the Canal Zone Code. The Canal Zone Code was subsequently redesignated the Panama Canal Code by [Pub. L. 96–70, § 3303(b)][/us/pl/96/70/s3303/b], and repealed by [Pub. L. 104–201, § 3549][/us/pl/104/201/s3549].

    Section 1321, acts [Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 371, § 10][/us/act/1916-08-21/ch371/s10], [39 Stat. 529][/us/stat/39/529]; [June 19, 1934, ch. 667, § 1][/us/act/1934-06-19/ch667/s1], [48 Stat. 1122][/us/stat/48/1122], related to passage of persons through the Canal Zone.

    Section 1322, acts [Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 371][/us/act/1916-08-21/ch371], [39 Stat. 529][/us/stat/39/529]; [June 19, 1934, ch. 667, § 1][/us/act/1934-06-19/ch667/s1], [48 Stat. 1122][/us/stat/48/1122], related to injury to Canal and appurtenances.
