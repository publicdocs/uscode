## § 1490. Repealed. [Mar. 3, 1933, ch. 202][/us/act/1933-03-03/ch202], § 1, [47 Stat. 1428][/us/stat/47/1428]

    Section, R.S. § 1891, related to application of United States Constitution and laws to all organized Territories and in every Territory subsequently organized. Insofar as Territories of Alaska and Hawaii are concerned, it is covered by sections 23 and 495 of this title.

    [Act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369], § 1, [32 Stat. 691][/us/stat/32/691], which was also cited as a credit to this section, and which was not repealed by the act of Mar. 3, 1933, provided that this section should not apply to the Philippine Islands.
