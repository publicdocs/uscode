## §§ 1241 to 1243. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1241 to 1243 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.

    Section 1241, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11], § 11, [47 Stat. 769][/us/stat/47/769]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84], § 11; [48 Stat. 463][/us/stat/48/463], related to neutralization of the Philippines.

    Section 1242, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11], § 12, [47 Stat. 769][/us/stat/47/769]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84], § 12, [48 Stat. 463][/us/stat/48/463], related to notification to foreign governments of Philippine Independence.

    Section 1243, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11], § 13, [47 Stat. 769][/us/stat/47/769]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84], § 13, [48 Stat. 464][/us/stat/48/464]; [Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 502][/us/act/1939-08-07/ch502], § 4, [53 Stat. 1231][/us/stat/53/1231]; [June 29, 1944, ch. 323][/us/act/1944-06-29/ch323], § 1, [58 Stat. 626][/us/stat/58/626]; [Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244], title V, § 511(3), [60 Stat. 158][/us/stat/60/158], related to establishment of Filipino Rehabilitation Commission.
