## §§ 1141 to 1156. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1141 to 1156 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.

    Section 1141, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369], § 76, [32 Stat. 710][/us/stat/32/710], related to establishment of a mint at Manila.

    Section 1142, [act Mar. 2, 1903, ch. 980][/us/act/1903-03-02/ch980], § 1, [32 Stat. 952][/us/stat/32/952], related to establishment of gold peso as unit of value.
