## §§ 1171 to 1173. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1171 to 1173 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.

    Section 1171, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 28][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s28], [39 Stat. 555][/us/stat/39/555], related to granting of franchises and rights and compensation for property taken or damaged.

    Section 1172, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 28][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s28], [39 Stat. 555][/us/stat/39/555], related to involuntary servitude and penalties therefor.

    Section 1173, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369, § 75][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369/s75], [32 Stat. 709][/us/stat/32/709], related to corporation engaged in real estate business.
