## §§ 1041 to 1055. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1041 to 1055 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.

    Section 1041, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 8][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s8], [39 Stat. 547][/us/stat/39/547], related to grant of legislative power to Philippine Legislature.

    Section 1042, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 10][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s10], [39 Stat. 548][/us/stat/39/548], related to Legislature’s authority over trade relations, tariff acts, and immigration.

    Section 1043, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 12][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s12], [39 Stat. 548][/us/stat/39/548], related to composition of Legislature.

    Section 1044, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 13][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s13], [39 Stat. 549][/us/stat/39/549], related to qualifications and election of senators.

    Section 1045, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416], 14, [39 Stat. 549][/us/stat/39/549], related to qualifications and election of representatives.

    Section 1046, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 16][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s16], [39 Stat. 549][/us/stat/39/549], related to senatorial and representative districts.

    Section 1047, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 15][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s15], [39 Stat. 549][/us/stat/39/549], related to qualifications of voters.

    Section 1048, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 17][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s17], [39 Stat. 550][/us/stat/39/550], related to terms of office of senators and representatives.

    Section 1049, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 18][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s18], [39 Stat. 550][/us/stat/39/550], related to legislative sessions.

    Section 1050, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 18][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s18], [39 Stat. 550][/us/stat/39/550], related to compensation and privileges of members.

    Section 1051, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 18][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s18], [39 Stat. 550][/us/stat/39/550], related to ineligibility of senators and representatives to hold certain offices.

    Section 1052, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 19][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s19], [39 Stat. 551][/us/stat/39/551], related to enactment of laws and approval by President of the United States.

    Section 1053, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 19][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s19], [39 Stat. 551][/us/stat/39/551], related to failure to make appropriations.

    Section 1054, acts [Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 19][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s19], [39 Stat. 551][/us/stat/39/551]; [May 29, 1928, ch. 901, § 1][/us/act/1928-05-29/ch901/s1], [45 Stat. 996][/us/stat/45/996]; [Feb. 28, 1929, ch. 364][/us/act/1929-02-28/ch364], §§ 1, 2, [45 Stat. 1348][/us/stat/45/1348], related to reporting of laws to Congress.
