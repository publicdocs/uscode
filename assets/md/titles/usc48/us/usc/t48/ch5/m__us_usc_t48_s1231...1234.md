## §§ 1231 to 1234. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1231 to 1234 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.

    Section 1231, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11], § 1, [47 Stat. 761][/us/stat/47/761]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84], § 1, [48 Stat. 456][/us/stat/48/456], related to a convention to frame a constitution for Philippines. Section 1 of act Jan. 17, 1933, was repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 648][/us/stat/80/648].

    Section 1232, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11], § 10, [47 Stat. 768][/us/stat/47/768]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84], § 2, [48 Stat. 457][/us/stat/48/457]; Proc. No. 2695, eff. July 4, 1946, 11 F.R. 7515, [60 Stat. 1352][/us/stat/60/1352], related to mandatory provisions of constitution.

    Section 1233, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11], § 3, [47 Stat. 763][/us/stat/47/763]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84], § 3, [48 Stat. 458][/us/stat/48/458], related to submission of proposed constitution to President of United States.

    Section 1234, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11], § 4, [47 Stat. 763][/us/stat/47/763]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84], § 4, [48 Stat. 458][/us/stat/48/458], related to submission of constitution to Filipino people.
