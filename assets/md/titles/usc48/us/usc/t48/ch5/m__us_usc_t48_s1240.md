## § 1240. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, acts [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 10][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84/s10], [48 Stat. 463][/us/stat/48/463]; [Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 502, § 3][/us/act/1939-08-07/ch502/s3], [53 Stat. 1230][/us/stat/53/1230], related to recognition of Philippine independence, withdrawal of American sovereignty, and property for diplomatic purposes, and was transferred to [section 1394 of Title 22][/us/usc/t22/s1394], Foreign Relations and Intercourse.

§§ 1241 to 1243. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1241 to 1243 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.

    Section 1241, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 11][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11/s11], [47 Stat. 769][/us/stat/47/769]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 11][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84/s11]; [48 Stat. 463][/us/stat/48/463], related to neutralization of the Philippines.

    Section 1242, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 12][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11/s12], [47 Stat. 769][/us/stat/47/769]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 12][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84/s12], [48 Stat. 463][/us/stat/48/463], related to notification to foreign governments of Philippine Independence.

    Section 1243, acts [Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 13][/us/act/1933-01-17/ch11/s13], [47 Stat. 769][/us/stat/47/769]; [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 13][/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84/s13], [48 Stat. 464][/us/stat/48/464]; [Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 502, § 4][/us/act/1939-08-07/ch502/s4], [53 Stat. 1231][/us/stat/53/1231]; [June 29, 1944, ch. 323, § 1][/us/act/1944-06-29/ch323/s1], [58 Stat. 626][/us/stat/58/626]; [Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244][/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244], title V, § 511(3), [60 Stat. 158][/us/stat/60/158], related to establishment of Filipino Rehabilitation Commission.
