## §§ 2641 to 2643. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III][/us/pl/103/382/tIII], § 367, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3976][/us/stat/108/3976]

    Section 2641, [Pub. L. 100–297, title V][/us/pl/100/297/tV], § 5341, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 411][/us/stat/102/411]; [Pub. L. 100–427][/us/pl/100/427], § 21, Sept. 9, 1988, [102 Stat. 1612][/us/stat/102/1612], related to establishment of Office of Indian Education within Department of Education. See [section 3423c of Title 20][/us/usc/t20/s3423c], Education.

    Section 2642, [Pub. L. 100–297, title V][/us/pl/100/297/tV], § 5342, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 412][/us/stat/102/412]; [Pub. L. 100–427][/us/pl/100/427], § 22, Sept. 9, 1988, [102 Stat. 1613][/us/stat/102/1613], established National Advisory Council on Indian Education.

    Section 2643, [Pub. L. 100–297, title V][/us/pl/100/297/tV], § 5343, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 413][/us/stat/102/413], authorized appropriations for administration of Indian education programs. See [section 7882 of Title 20][/us/usc/t20/s7882], Education.
