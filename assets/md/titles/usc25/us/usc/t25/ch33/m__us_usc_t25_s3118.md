## § 3118. Regulations

    Except as otherwise provided by this chapter, the Secretary is directed to promulgate final regulations for the implementation of the  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be “this”. </sup></sup>  chapter within eighteen months from November 28, 1990. All regulations promulgated pursuant to this chapter shall be developed by the Secretary with the participation of the affected Indian tribes.

([Pub. L. 101–630, title III][/us/pl/101/630/tIII], § 319, Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 4544][/us/stat/104/4544].)
