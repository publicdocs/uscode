## § 4235. Annual allocation

    For each fiscal year, the Secretary shall allocate any amounts made available for assistance under this subchapter for the fiscal year, in accordance with the formula established pursuant to [section 4236 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s4236] to the Department of Hawaiian Home Lands if the Department complies with the requirements under this subchapter for a grant under this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 104–330, title VIII, § 816][/us/pl/104/330/s816], as added [Pub. L. 106–568, title II, § 203][/us/pl/106/568/s203], Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2890][/us/stat/114/2890], and [Pub. L. 106–569, title V, § 513][/us/pl/106/569/s513], Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2984][/us/stat/114/2984].)

 __Codification__
