## § 4227. Regulations

    The Secretary shall issue final regulations necessary to carry out this subchapter not later than October 1, 2001.

([Pub. L. 104–330, title VIII][/us/pl/104/330/tVIII], § 807, as added [Pub. L. 106–568, title II][/us/pl/106/568/tII], § 203, Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2885][/us/stat/114/2885], and [Pub. L. 106–569, title V][/us/pl/106/569/tV], § 513, Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2979][/us/stat/114/2979].)

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 106–568][/us/pl/106/568], § 203, and [Pub. L. 106–569][/us/pl/106/569], § 513, enacted substantially identical sections 807 of [Pub. L. 104–330][/us/pl/104/330]. This section is based on the text of [section 807 of Pub. L. 104–330][/us/pl/104/330/s807], as added by [Pub. L. 106–569][/us/pl/106/569], § 513. [Section 807 of Pub. L. 104–330][/us/pl/104/330/s807], as added by [Pub. L. 106–568][/us/pl/106/568], § 203, required the issuance of final regulations not later than October 1, 2000, instead of October 1, 2001.
