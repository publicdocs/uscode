## § 5632. Definitions

    In this subchapter:

    (1) __BIA__ 

        The term “BIA” means the Bureau of Indian Affairs.

    (2) __Department__ 

        The term “Department” means the Department of the Interior.

    (3) __Secretary__ 

        The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

    (4) __Under Secretary__ 

        The term “Under Secretary” means the Under Secretary for Indian Affairs established under [section 5633(a) of this title][/us/usc/t25/s5633/a].

([Pub. L. 114–178, title III][/us/pl/114/178/tIII], § 302, June 22, 2016, [130 Stat. 439][/us/stat/130/439].)
