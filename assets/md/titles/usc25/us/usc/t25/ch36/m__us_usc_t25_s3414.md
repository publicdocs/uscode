## § 3414. Fiscal accountability

    Nothing in this chapter shall be construed so as to interfere with the ability of the Secretary or the lead agency to fulfill the responsibilities for the safeguarding of Federal funds pursuant to the Single Audit Act of 1984 \[[31 U.S.C. 7501][/us/usc/t31/s7501] et seq.\].

([Pub. L. 102–477, § 15][/us/pl/102/477/s15], Oct. 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 2305][/us/stat/106/2305].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Single Audit Act of 1984, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 98–502][/us/pl/98/502], Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2327][/us/stat/98/2327], which enacted chapter 75 (§ 7501 et seq.) of Title 31, Money and Finance, and provisions set out as notes under [section 7501 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s7501]. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title of 1984 Amendment note set out under [section 7501 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s7501] and Tables.
