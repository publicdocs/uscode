## § 3402. Definitions

    For the purposes of this chapter, the following definitions apply:

    (1) __Federal agency__ 

        The term “federal  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be capitalized. </sup></sup>  agency” has the same meaning given the term “agency” in [section 551(1) of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s551/1].

    (2) __Indian tribe__ 

        The terms “Indian tribe” and “tribe” shall have the meaning given the term “Indian tribe” in [section 450b(e) of this title][/us/usc/t25/s450b/e].

    (3) __Indian__ 

        The term “Indian” shall have the meaning given such term in [section 450b(d) of this title][/us/usc/t25/s450b/d].

    (4) __Secretary__ 

        Except where otherwise provided, the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 102–477, § 3][/us/pl/102/477/s3], Oct. 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 2302][/us/stat/106/2302]; [Pub. L. 106–568, title XI, § 1103(a)][/us/pl/106/568/s1103/a], Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2931][/us/stat/114/2931].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2000—[Pub. L. 106–568][/us/pl/106/568] added par. (1) and redesignated former pars. (1) to (3) as (2) to (4), respectively.
