## § 1300h–8. Compliance with Budget Act

    Notwithstanding any other provision of this subchapter, any spending authority provided under this subchapter shall be effective for any fiscal year only to such extent or in such amounts as are provided in advance in appropriation Acts. For purposes of this subchapter, the term “spending authority” has the meaning provided in section 651(c)(2)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of title 2.

([Pub. L. 100–420, § 10][/us/pl/100/420/s10], Sept. 8, 1988, [102 Stat. 1579][/us/stat/102/1579].)
