## § 1234. Tax exemption

    No part of any funds distributed under this subchapter shall be subject to Federal or State income taxes.

([Pub. L. 91–417, § 4][/us/pl/91/417/s4], Sept. 25, 1970, [84 Stat. 868][/us/stat/84/868].)
