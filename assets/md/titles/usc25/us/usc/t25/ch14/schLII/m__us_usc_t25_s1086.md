## § 1086. Advances or expenditures from tribal funds

    The Secretary is authorized to advance or expend, as provided in [section 1081 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1081], the Hoh tribal funds now on deposit, or hereafter placed on deposit, in the Treasury of the United States under the following symbols and titles:

    14X7235 Proceeds of Labor, Hoh Indians, Washington;

    14X7735 Interest and Accruals on Interest, Proceeds of Labor, Hoh Indians, Washington.

([Pub. L. 89–655, § 6][/us/pl/89/655/s6], Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 905][/us/stat/80/905].)
