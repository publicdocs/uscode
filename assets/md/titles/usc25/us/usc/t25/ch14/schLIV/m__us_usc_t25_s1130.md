## § 1130. Rules and regulations

    The Secretary of the Interior is authorized to prescribe rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of sections 1122 to 1130 of this title, including the establishment of deadlines.

([Pub. L. 92–309, § 9][/us/pl/92/309/s9], June 2, 1972, [86 Stat. 200][/us/stat/86/200].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was not enacted as part of [Pub. L. 89–659][/us/pl/89/659], Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 909][/us/stat/80/909], which comprises this subchapter.
