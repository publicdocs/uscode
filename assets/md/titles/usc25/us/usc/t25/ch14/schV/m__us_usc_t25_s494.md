## § 494. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out sections 488 to 494 of this title $8,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1991 through 1995.

([Pub. L. 91–229, § 6][/us/pl/91/229/s6], as added [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVIII, § 1854(b)][/us/pl/101/624/s1854/b], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3837][/us/stat/104/3837].)

 __Codification__ 

    Another [section 6 of Pub. L. 91–229][/us/pl/91/229/s6] was added by [Pub. L. 101–82, title III, § 303][/us/pl/101/82/s303], Aug. 14, 1989, [103 Stat. 583][/us/stat/103/583], and is classified to [section 493 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s493].
