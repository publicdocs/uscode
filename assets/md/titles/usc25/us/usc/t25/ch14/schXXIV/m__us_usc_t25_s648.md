## § 648. Tax exemption

    Any part of such funds that may be distributed to members of the tribe shall not be subject to Federal or State income tax.

([Pub. L. 91–400, § 2][/us/pl/91/400/s2], Sept. 16, 1970, [84 Stat. 838][/us/stat/84/838].)
