## § 677t. Water rights

    Nothing in this subchapter shall abrogate any water rights of the tribe or its members.

([Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 21][/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s21], [68 Stat. 877][/us/stat/68/877].)
