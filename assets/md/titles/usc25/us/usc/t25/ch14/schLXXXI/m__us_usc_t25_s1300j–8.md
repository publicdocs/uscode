## § 1300j–8. Definitions

    For purposes of this subchapter—

    (1) the term “Band” means the Pokagon Band of Potawatomi Indians;

    (2) the term “member” means those individuals eligible for enrollment in the Band pursuant to [section 1300j–3 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1300j–3]; and

    (3) the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 103–323][/us/pl/103/323], § 10, formerly § 9, Sept. 21, 1994, [108 Stat. 2155][/us/stat/108/2155]; renumbered § 10, [Pub. L. 103–435][/us/pl/103/435], § 20(1), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4574][/us/stat/108/4574].)
