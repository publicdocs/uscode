#### Part F—Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    This part was formerly comprised of title V of [Pub. L. 93–638][/us/pl/93/638], as added by [Pub. L. 106–568, title XIII][/us/pl/106/568/tXIII], § 1302, Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2936][/us/stat/114/2936], which was redesignated title VIII by [Pub. L. 111–211, title II][/us/pl/111/211/tII], § 231(d)(1), July 29, 2010, [124 Stat. 2278][/us/stat/124/2278], and transferred to part H (§ 458ddd et seq.) of this subchapter. Title V of [Pub. L. 93–638][/us/pl/93/638], as added by [Pub. L. 106–260][/us/pl/106/260], § 4, Aug. 18, 2000, [114 Stat. 712][/us/stat/114/712], is classified to part E (§ 458aaa et seq.) of this subchapter.
