## § 967c. Tax exemption

    The funds distributed under the provisions of sections 967a to 967d of this title shall not be subject to Federal or State income taxes.

([Pub. L. 89–717][/us/pl/89/717], § 3, Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1115][/us/stat/80/1115].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was not enacted as part of [Pub. L. 87–235][/us/pl/87/235], Sept. 14, 1961, [75 Stat. 508][/us/stat/75/508], which comprises this subchapter.
