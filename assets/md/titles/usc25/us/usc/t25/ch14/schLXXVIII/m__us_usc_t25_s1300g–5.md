## § 1300g–5. Tiwa Indians Act repealed

    The Tiwa Indians Act is hereby repealed.

([Pub. L. 100–89, title I][/us/pl/100/89/tI], § 106, Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 668][/us/stat/101/668].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Tiwa Indians Act, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 90–287][/us/pl/90/287], Apr. 12, 1968, [82 Stat. 93][/us/stat/82/93], which was not classified to the Code.
