## § 752. Water rights

    Nothing in this subchapter shall abrogate any water rights of a tribe or its members.

([Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207][/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1207], § 12, [68 Stat. 1103][/us/stat/68/1103].)
