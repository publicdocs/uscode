## § 1300h–1. Definitions

For purposes of this subchapter—

    (1) the term “Band” means the Lac Vieux Desert Band of Lake Superior Chippewa Indians;

    (2) the term “member” means those individuals eligible for enrollment under [section 1300h–3 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1300h–3] in the Band; and

    (3) the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 100–420, § 3][/us/pl/100/420/s3], Sept. 8, 1988, [102 Stat. 1577][/us/stat/102/1577].)
