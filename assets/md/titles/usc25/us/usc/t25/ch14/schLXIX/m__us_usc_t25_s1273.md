## § 1273. Tax exemption

    None of the funds distributed per capita under the provisions of this subchapter shall be subject to Federal or State income taxes.

([Pub. L. 92–295, § 3][/us/pl/92/295/s3], May 16, 1972, [86 Stat. 139][/us/stat/86/139].)
