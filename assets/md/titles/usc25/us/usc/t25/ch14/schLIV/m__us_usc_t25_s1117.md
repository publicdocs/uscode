## § 1117. Distribution of funds of Miami Indians of Oklahoma; persons entitled

    The funds on deposit in the Treasury of the United States to the credit of the “Miami Tribe of Oklahoma” that were appropriated by the Act of May 17, 1963 ([77 Stat. 43][/us/stat/77/43]), to pay a judgment in Indian Claims Commission dockets numbered 67 and 124, and the interest thereon, after payment of attorney fees and expenses, shall be distributed to the persons whose names appear on the roll prepared pursuant to [section 1114 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1114], and in accordance with the instructions contained in sections 1118 and 1119 of this title.

([Pub. L. 89–659][/us/pl/89/659], § 7, Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 910][/us/stat/80/910].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Act of May 17, 1963, referred to in text, is act May 17, 1963, [Pub. L. 88–25][/us/pl/88/25], [77 Stat. 43][/us/stat/77/43]. That portion of the act which appropriated the funds referred to was not classified to the Code.

    The Indian Claims Commission, referred to in text, terminated Sept. 30, 1978. See Codification note set out under former section 70 et seq. of this title.
