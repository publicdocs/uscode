## § 1036. Taxes

    No part of any of the funds distributed in accordance with this subchapter shall be subject to Federal or State income tax.

([Pub. L. 88–457, § 6][/us/pl/88/457/s6], Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 556][/us/stat/78/556].)
