## § 1204. Tax exemption

    The funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter shall not be subject to Federal or State income taxes.

([Pub. L. 91–283, § 4][/us/pl/91/283/s4], June 19, 1970, [84 Stat. 313][/us/stat/84/313].)
