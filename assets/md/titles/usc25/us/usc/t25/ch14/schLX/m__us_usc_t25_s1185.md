## § 1185. Tax exemption

    The funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter shall not be subject to Federal or State income tax.

([Pub. L. 90–508, § 5][/us/pl/90/508/s5], Sept. 21, 1968, [82 Stat. 862][/us/stat/82/862].)
