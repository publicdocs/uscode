## § 1300k–1. Definitions

For purposes of this subchapter—

    (1) the term “Bands” means the Little Traverse Bay Bands of Odawa Indians and the Little River Band of Ottawa Indians;

    (2) the term “member” means those individuals enrolled in the Bands pursuant to [section 1300k–5 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1300k–5]; and

    (3) the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 103–324, § 3][/us/pl/103/324/s3], Sept. 21, 1994, [108 Stat. 2157][/us/stat/108/2157].)
