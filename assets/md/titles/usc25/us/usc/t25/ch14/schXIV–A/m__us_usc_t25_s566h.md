## § 566h. Regulations

    The Secretary may make such rules and regulations as are necessary to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 99–398][/us/pl/99/398], § 10, Aug. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 852][/us/stat/100/852].)
