## § 565f. Taxes

    None of the funds distributed pursuant to this subchapter shall be subject to Federal or State income tax.

([Pub. L. 89–224, § 7][/us/pl/89/224/s7], Oct 1, 1965, [79 Stat. 898][/us/stat/79/898].)
