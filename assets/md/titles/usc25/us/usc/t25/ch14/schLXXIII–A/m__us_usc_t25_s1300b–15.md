## § 1300b–15. Jurisdiction

    The State of Texas shall exercise jurisdiction over civil causes of action and criminal offenses arising on the Band’s trust lands in accordance with [section 1360 of title 28][/us/usc/t28/s1360] and [section 1162 of title 18][/us/usc/t18/s1162] as if it had assumed jurisdiction pursuant to sections 1321 and 1322 of this title. The provisions of [section 1323 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1323], shall be applicable and available to the State of Texas.

([Pub. L. 97–429][/us/pl/97/429], § 6, Jan. 8, 1983, [96 Stat. 2270][/us/stat/96/2270].)
