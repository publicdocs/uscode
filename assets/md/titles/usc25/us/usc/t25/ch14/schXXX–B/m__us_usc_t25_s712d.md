## § 712d. Rules

    The Secretary of the Interior may make such rules as are necessary to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 97–391, § 6][/us/pl/97/391/s6], Dec. 29, 1982, [96 Stat. 1961][/us/stat/96/1961].)
