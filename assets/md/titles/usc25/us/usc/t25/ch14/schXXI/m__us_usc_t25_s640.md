## § 640. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–531][/us/pl/93/531], § 26, Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1723][/us/stat/88/1723]

    Section, [act Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92][/us/act/1950-04-19/ch92], § 10, [64 Stat. 47][/us/stat/64/47], established Joint Committee on Navajo-Hopi Indian Administration, with function of making a continuous study of programs for administration and rehabilitation of Navajo and Hopi Indians.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 93–531][/us/pl/93/531], § 26, Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1723][/us/stat/88/1723], provided that the repeal is effective as of the close of business December 31, 1974.
