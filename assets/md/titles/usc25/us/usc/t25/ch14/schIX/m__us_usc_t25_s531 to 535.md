## §§ 531 to 535. Repealed. [Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 12][/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s12], [68 Stat. 721][/us/stat/68/721]

    Sections 531 to 535, [act Aug. 28, 1937, ch. 874][/us/act/1937-08-28/ch874], §§ 2–6, [50 Stat. 872][/us/stat/50/872], 873, related to revolving loan fund. See section 564 et seq. of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 12][/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s12], [68 Stat. 721][/us/stat/68/721], provided that the repeal is effective on the date of the transfer of title to tribal property to a trustee, corporation, or other legal entity pursuant to [section 564e of this title][/us/usc/t25/s564e].
