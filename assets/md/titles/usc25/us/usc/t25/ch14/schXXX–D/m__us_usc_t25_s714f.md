## § 714f. Regulations

    The Secretary may promulgate such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 98–481, § 8][/us/pl/98/481/s8], Oct. 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 2254][/us/stat/98/2254].)
