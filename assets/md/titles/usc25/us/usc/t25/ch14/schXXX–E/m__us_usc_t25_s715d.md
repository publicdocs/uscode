## § 715d. Criminal and civil jurisdiction

    The State shall exercise criminal and civil jurisdiction within the boundaries of the reservation, in accordance with [section 1162 of title 18][/us/usc/t18/s1162], and [section 1360 of title 28][/us/usc/t28/s1360], respectively. Retrocession of such jurisdiction may be obtained pursuant to [section 1323 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1323].

([Pub. L. 101–42][/us/pl/101/42], § 6, June 28, 1989, [103 Stat. 92][/us/stat/103/92].)
