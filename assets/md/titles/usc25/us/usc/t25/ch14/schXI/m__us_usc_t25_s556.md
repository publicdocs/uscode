## § 556. Reversion of interest in property on death without heirs or devisees

    If any enrolled member of the Klamath Tribe dies without lawful heirs or devises, <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be “devisees,”. </sup></sup>  all interest which such member has in any restricted or trust property within the Klamath Reservation shall revert to and become part of the common tribal property.

([June 1, 1938, ch. 310][/us/act/1938-06-01/ch310], § 6, [52 Stat. 606][/us/stat/52/606].)
