## § 458ddd–2. Definitions

    For the purposes of this part—

    (1) the term “Bureau funded school” has the meaning given that term in title XI of the Education Amendments of 1978 \[[25 U.S.C. 2000][/us/usc/t25/s2000] et seq.\];

    (2) the term “Foundation” means the Foundation established by the Secretary pursuant to [section 458ddd of this title][/us/usc/t25/s458ddd]; and

    (3) the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 93–638, title VIII][/us/pl/93/638/tVIII], § 803, formerly title V, § 503, as added [Pub. L. 106–568, title XIII][/us/pl/106/568/tXIII], § 1302, Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2938][/us/stat/114/2938]; renumbered title VIII, § 803, and amended [Pub. L. 111–211, title II][/us/pl/111/211/tII], § 231(d), July 29, 2010, [124 Stat. 2278][/us/stat/124/2278].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Education Amendments of 1978, referred to in par. (1), is [Pub. L. 95–561][/us/pl/95/561], Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2143][/us/stat/92/2143]. Title XI of the Act is classified principally to chapter 22 (§ 2000 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 6301 of Title 20][/us/usc/t20/s6301], Education, and Tables.

 __Amendments__ 

    2010—Par. (2). [Pub. L. 111–211][/us/pl/111/211], § 231(d)(3), substituted “section 458ddd” for “section 458bbb”.
