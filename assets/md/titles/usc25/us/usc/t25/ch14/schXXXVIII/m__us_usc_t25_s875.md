## § 875. Rules and regulations

    The Secretary is authorized to prescribe rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 85–395][/us/pl/85/395], § 5, May 9, 1958, [72 Stat. 106][/us/stat/72/106].)
