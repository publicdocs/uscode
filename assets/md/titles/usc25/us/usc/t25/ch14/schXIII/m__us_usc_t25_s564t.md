## § 564t. Indian claims unaffected

    Nothing contained in this subchapter shall deprive the tribe or its constituent parts of any right, privilege, or benefit granted by the Act of August 13, 1946 ([60 Stat. 1049][/us/stat/60/1049]) \[[25 U.S.C. 70][/us/usc/t25/s70] et seq.\].

([Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 21][/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s21], [68 Stat. 723][/us/stat/68/723].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Act of August 13, 1946, referred to in text, is [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 959][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch959], [60 Stat. 1049][/us/stat/60/1049], as amended, known as the Indian Claims Commission Act of 1946, which was classified generally to chapter 2A (§ 70 et seq.) of this title and was omitted in view of the termination of the Indian Claims Commission on Sept. 30, 1978. See Codification note set out under former section 70 et seq. of this title.
