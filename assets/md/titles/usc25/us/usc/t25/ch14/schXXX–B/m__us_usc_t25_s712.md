## § 712. Definitions

For the purposes of this subchapter—

    (1) the term “tribe” means the Cow Creek Band of Umpqua Tribe of Indians; and

    (2) the term “member”, when used with respect to the tribe, means a person enrolled on the membership roll of the tribe in accordance with [section 712c of this title][/us/usc/t25/s712c].

([Pub. L. 97–391, § 2][/us/pl/97/391/s2], Dec. 29, 1982, [96 Stat. 1960][/us/stat/96/1960]; [Pub. L. 100–139, § 5(b)][/us/pl/100/139/s5/b], Oct. 26, 1987, [101 Stat. 827][/us/stat/101/827].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1987—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 100–139][/us/pl/100/139] substituted “Umpqua Tribe of Indians” for “Umpqua Tribe of Oregon”.

 __Short Title of 1987 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 100–139, § 1][/us/pl/100/139/s1], Oct. 26, 1987, [101 Stat. 822][/us/stat/101/822], provided: 

> “That this Act \[amending this section and sections 712a to 712c of this title\] may be cited as the ‘Cow Creek Band of Umpqua Tribe of Indians Distribution of Judgment Funds Act of 1987’.”

 __Short Title__ 

[Pub. L. 97–391, § 1][/us/pl/97/391/s1], Dec. 29, 1982, [96 Stat. 1960][/us/stat/96/1960], provided that: 

> “This Act \[enacting this subchapter\] may be cited as the ‘Cow Creek Band of Umpqua Tribe of Indians Recognition Act’.”
