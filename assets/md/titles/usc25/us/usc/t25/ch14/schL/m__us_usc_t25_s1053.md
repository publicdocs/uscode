## § 1053. Taxes

    The funds distributed in accordance with this subchapter shall not be subject to the Federal or State income tax.

([Pub. L. 88–506][/us/pl/88/506], § 3, Aug. 30, 1964, [78 Stat. 639][/us/stat/78/639].)
