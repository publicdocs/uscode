## § 728. Protection of Alabama and Coushatta Indians and conservation of resources after issuance of proclamation

    The Act of June 18, 1934 ([48 Stat. 984][/us/stat/48/984]), as amended by the Act of June 15, 1935 ([49 Stat. 387][/us/stat/49/387]) \[[25 U.S.C. 461][/us/usc/t25/s461] et seq.\], shall not apply to the tribe and its members after the date of the proclamation to be issued in accordance with the provisions of [section 722 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s722].

([Aug. 23, 1954, ch. 831, § 8][/us/act/1954-08-23/ch831/s8], [68 Stat. 769][/us/stat/68/769].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Act of June 18, 1934, referred to in text, popularly known as the Indian Reorganization Act, is classified generally to subchapter V (§ 461 et seq.) of this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 461 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s461] and Tables.
