## § 1863. Applicability of other laws

    (a) __In general__ 

        Paragraphs (4) and (8) of subsection (a), and subsection (b), of [section 1801 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1801], sections 1805, 1808, 1811, 1812 and 1813 of this title, subchapters II and III of this chapter, and title II  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  shall not apply to this subchapter.

    (b) __Indian self-determination and education assistance__ 

        Funds made available pursuant to this subchapter shall be subject to the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act ([25 U.S.C. 450][/us/usc/t25/s450] et seq.).

    (c) __Election to receive__ 

        A tribally controlled postsecondary career and technical institution selected for a fiscal year under [section 1862(b) of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1862/b] may elect to receive funds pursuant to [section 1862 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1862] in accordance with an agreement between the tribally controlled postsecondary career and technical institution and the Secretary under the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act ([25 U.S.C. 450][/us/usc/t25/s450] et seq.) if the agreement is in existence on August 14, 2008.

    (d) __Other assistance__ 

    Eligibility for, or receipt of, assistance under this subchapter shall not preclude the eligibility of a tribally controlled postsecondary career and technical institution to receive Federal financial assistance under—

        (1) any program under the Higher Education Act of 1965 ([20 U.S.C. 1001][/us/usc/t20/s1001] et seq.) \[and [42 U.S.C. 2751][/us/usc/t42/s2751] et seq.\];

        (2) any program under the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Act of 2006 \[[20 U.S.C. 2301][/us/usc/t20/s2301] et seq.\]; or

        (3)

         any other applicable program under which a benefit is provided for—

            (A) institutions of higher education;

            (B) community colleges; or

            (C) postsecondary educational institutions.

([Pub. L. 95–471, title V][/us/pl/95/471/tV], § 503, as added [Pub. L. 110–315, title IX][/us/pl/110/315/tIX], § 941(j)(1), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3464][/us/stat/122/3464].)
