## §§ 227, 228. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21], [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862]

    Section 227, R.S. § 2154, related to reparation for injuries to Indian property. See [section 1160 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s1160], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

    Section 228, R.S. § 2155, related to payment of reparation where offender is unable to. See [section 1160 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s1160].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 20][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s20], [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862].
