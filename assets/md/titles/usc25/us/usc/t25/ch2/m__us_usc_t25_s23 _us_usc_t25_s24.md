###### §§ 23, 24. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 23, [act May 17, 1882, ch. 163][/us/act/1882-05-17/ch163], § 1, [22 Stat. 70][/us/stat/22/70], related to the powers and duties of the Board of Indian Commissioners, and was omitted as superseded by Ex. Ord. No. 6145 of May 25, 1933, which abolished the Board and transferred its records, property, and personnel to the supervision of the Secretary of the Interior.

    Section 24, R.S. § 2042, related to the investigations by a member of the Board of Indian Commissioners, and was omitted as superseded by Ex. Ord. No. 6145 of May 25, 1933.
