###### §§ 34, 35. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 632][/us/stat/80/632], 642, 650

    Section 34, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 355][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch355], § 8, [37 Stat. 487][/us/stat/37/487]; [June 6, 1939, ch. 185][/us/act/1939-06-06/ch185], [53 Stat. 810][/us/stat/53/810], authorized the superintendent, acting superintendent, and principal clerks of the different Indian superintendencies or Indian agencies to administer oaths to expense accounts.

    Section 35, R.S. § 2064, authorized Indian Agents to take acknowledgements of deeds and to administer oaths.
