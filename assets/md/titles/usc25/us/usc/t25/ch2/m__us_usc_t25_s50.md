## § 50. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–448, title IV, § 402(a)(2)][/us/pl/88/448/s402/a/2], Aug. 19, 1964, [78 Stat. 492][/us/stat/78/492]

    Section, R.S. § 2074, related to holding of two offices. See [section 5533 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s5533], Government Organization and Employees.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective on first day of first month which begins later than the 90th day following Aug. 19, 1964, see [Pub. L. 88–448, title IV, § 403][/us/pl/88/448/s403], Aug. 19, 1964, [78 Stat. 496][/us/stat/78/496].

§§ 51 to 52a. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 92–310, title II, § 229(a)__  __][/us/pl/92/310/s229/a]__  __, (c)(2), (e),__  __June 6, 1972__  __,__  __[__  __86 Stat. 208__  __][/us/stat/86/208]__ 

    Section 51, R.S. § 2075, empowered President to require additional security from persons charged with disbursement of money or goods.

    Section 52, [act Apr. 30, 1908, ch. 153][/us/act/1908-04-30/ch153], [35 Stat. 71][/us/stat/35/71], empowered Secretary of the Interior to require new bonds from disbursing officers.

    Section 52a, [act Apr. 21, 1904, ch. 1402][/us/act/1904-04-21/ch1402], [33 Stat. 191][/us/stat/33/191], related to special bonds for large per capita payments.
