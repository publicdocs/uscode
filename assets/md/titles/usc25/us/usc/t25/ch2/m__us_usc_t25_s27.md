## § 27. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, R.S. 2062; acts [July 13, 1892, ch. 164, § 1][/us/act/1892-07-13/ch164/s1], [27 Stat. 120][/us/stat/27/120]; [July 1, 1898, ch. 545, § 1][/us/act/1898-07-01/ch545/s1], [30 Stat. 573][/us/stat/30/573], authorized the President to require that military officers perform the duties of Indian agents. The services of Indian agents have been dispensed with since 1908. See [section 64 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s64] and notes thereunder.
