## § 32. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 1, 1898, ch. 545, § 1][/us/act/1898-07-01/ch545/s1], [30 Stat. 595][/us/stat/30/595], required Indian agents to account for funds received and to be responsible for such funds under their official bonds. The services of Indian agents have been dispensed with since 1908. See [section 64 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s64] and note set out thereunder.
