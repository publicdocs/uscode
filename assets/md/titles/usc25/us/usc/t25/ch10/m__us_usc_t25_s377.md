## § 377. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–363, § 2(a)][/us/pl/96/363/s2/a], Sept. 26, 1980, [94 Stat. 1207][/us/stat/94/1207]

    Section, acts [Jan. 24, 1923, ch. 42][/us/act/1923-01-24/ch42], [42 Stat. 1185][/us/stat/42/1185]; [May 29, 1928, ch. 901, § 1(84)][/us/act/1928-05-29/ch901/s1/84], [45 Stat. 992][/us/stat/45/992], related to payment or deduction from trust funds, etc., of cost of determining heirs, and set forth a schedule of fees.

 __Cancellation of Assessed Unpaid Fees__ 

[Pub. L. 96–363, § 2(b)][/us/pl/96/363/s2/b], Sept. 26, 1980, [94 Stat. 1207][/us/stat/94/1207], provided that: 

> “The Secretary of the Interior may cancel any unpaid fees assessed under the provisions repealed by this section \[sections 375b and 377 of this title\].”
