## § 284. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 13, 1892, ch. 164, § 1][/us/act/1892-07-13/ch164/s1], [27 Stat. 143][/us/stat/27/143], which related to issuance and enforcement of regulations by the Commissioner of Indian Affairs to secure attendance of Indian children at school, was omitted as obsolete in view of the enactment of [section 282 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s282], which provides that the Secretary of the Interior now issue and enforce such regulations. See [section 282 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s282].
