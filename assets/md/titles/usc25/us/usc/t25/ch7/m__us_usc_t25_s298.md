## § 298. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 4, 1884, ch. 180, § 9][/us/act/1884-07-04/ch180/s9], [23 Stat. 98][/us/stat/23/98], which required Indian agents to submit a census of the Indians at the agency in their annual report, was omitted as obsolete since there have been no Indian agents since 1908. See note set out under [section 64 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s64].

    Section 299, [act Mar. 2, 1887, ch. 320, § 1][/us/act/1887-03-02/ch320/s1], [24 Stat. 465][/us/stat/24/465], related to report of expenditures of Indian education fund.
