## § 298. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 4, 1884, ch. 180][/us/act/1884-07-04/ch180], § 9, [23 Stat. 98][/us/stat/23/98], which required Indian agents to submit a census of the Indians at the agency in their annual report, was omitted as obsolete since there have been no Indian agents since 1908. See note set out under [section 64 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s64].
