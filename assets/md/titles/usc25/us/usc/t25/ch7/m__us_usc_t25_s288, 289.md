## §§ 288, 289. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–228, § 3(1)][/us/pl/99/228/s3/1], (2), Dec. 28, 1985, [99 Stat. 1748][/us/stat/99/1748]

    Section 289, [act Mar. 3, 1909, ch. 263][/us/act/1909-03-03/ch263], [35 Stat. 783][/us/stat/35/783], provided for admission of white children to Indian boarding schools. See [section 2007 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s2007].
