## § 287. Taking child to school in another State without written consent

    No Indian child shall be taken from any school in any State or Territory to a school in any other State against its will or without the written consent of its parents.

([June 10, 1896, ch. 398, § 1][/us/act/1896-06-10/ch398/s1], [29 Stat. 348][/us/stat/29/348].)

§§ 288, 289. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 99–228, § 3(1)__  __][/us/pl/99/228/s3/1]__  __, (2),__  __Dec. 28, 1985__  __,__  __[__  __99 Stat. 1748__  __][/us/stat/99/1748]__ 

    Section 288, [act Mar. 1, 1907, ch. 2285][/us/act/1907-03-01/ch2285], [34 Stat. 1018][/us/stat/34/1018], provided for admission of white children to Indian day schools.
