## § 1749. Revocation of settlement

In the event the Settlement Agreement between the Miccosukee Tribe and the State of Florida is ever invalidated—

    (1) the transfers, waivers, releases, relinquishments, and other commitments made by the Miccosukee Tribe in paragraph 3 of the Settlement Agreement shall no longer be of any force or effect,

    (2) [section 1744 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1744] shall be inapplicable to the lands, interests in lands, or natural resources of the Miccosukee Tribe and its members as if never enacted, and

    (3) the approvals of prior transfers and the extinguishment of claims and aboriginal title of the Miccosukee Tribe otherwise effected by [section 1744 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1744] shall be void ab initio.

([Pub. L. 97–399, § 10][/us/pl/97/399/s10], Dec. 31, 1982, [96 Stat. 2016][/us/stat/96/2016].)
