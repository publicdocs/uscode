## § 1780b. Judgment and dismissal of litigation

    (a) __Dismissal__ 

        Not later than 90 days after September 27, 2006, the United States and the Pueblo shall execute and file with the United States Court of Federal Claims in the Pending Litigation a motion for entry of final judgment in accordance with section 5 of the Settlement Agreement.

    (b) __Compensation__ 

        Upon entry of the final judgment under subsection (a), $6,900,000 shall be paid into the Settlement Fund as compensation to the Pueblo in accordance with [section 1304 of title 31][/us/usc/t31/s1304].

([Pub. L. 109–286][/us/pl/109/286], § 4, Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1219][/us/stat/120/1219].)
