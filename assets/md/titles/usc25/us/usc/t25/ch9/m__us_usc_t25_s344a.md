## § 344a. Repealed. [Act Nov. 24, 1942, ch. 640, § 4][/us/act/1942-11-24/ch640/s4], [56 Stat. 1022][/us/stat/56/1022]

    Section, [act June 25, 1910, ch. 431, § 12][/us/act/1910-06-25/ch431/s12], [36 Stat. 858][/us/stat/36/858], authorized Secretary of the Interior to investigate the allotment in the name of a deceased Indian and to recommend to Congress the cancellation of such Indian’s patent if he died without heirs.
