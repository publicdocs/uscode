## §§ 2472 to 2478. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–573, title VII][/us/pl/102/573/tVII], § 702(b)(2), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4582][/us/stat/106/4582]

    Section 2474, [Pub. L. 99–570, title IV][/us/pl/99/570/tIV], § 4227, Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–149][/us/stat/100/3207-149]; [Pub.     Section 2475, [Pub. L. 99–570, title IV][/us/pl/99/570/tIV], § 4228, Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–150][/us/stat/100/3207-150]; [Pub.     Section 2476, [Pub. L. 99–570, title IV][/us/pl/99/570/tIV], § 4229, Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–152][/us/stat/100/3207-152]; [Pub. L. 100–690, title II][/us/pl/100/690/tII], § 2214, Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4220][/us/stat/102/4220], provided for establishment of Navajo alcohol rehabilitation demonstration program.

    Section 2477, [Pub. L. 99–570, title IV][/us/pl/99/570/tIV], § 4230, Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–152][/us/stat/100/3207-152], related to compilation of data and preparation of reports on cases of alcohol or substance abuse in which Indian Health Service personnel or services were involved.

    Section 2478, [Pub. L. 99–570, title IV][/us/pl/99/570/tIV], § 4231, as added [Pub. L. 100–690, title II][/us/pl/100/690/tII], § 2215, Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4221][/us/stat/102/4221], authorized grants for alcohol and substance abuse prevention and treatment in urban centers.
