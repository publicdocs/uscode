## § 1660f. Community Health Representatives

    The Secretary, acting through the Service, may enter into contracts with, and make grants to, urban Indian organizations for the employment of Indians trained as health service providers through the Community Health Representative Program under [section 1616 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1616] in the provision of health care, health promotion, and disease prevention services to urban Indians.

([Pub. L. 94–437, title V][/us/pl/94/437/tV], § 516, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title X][/us/pl/111/148/tX], § 10221(a), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 935][/us/stat/124/935].)

 __Codification__ 

    [Section 516 of Pub. L. 94–437][/us/pl/94/437/s516] is based on section 165 of title I of S. 1790, One Hundred Eleventh Congress, as reported by the Committee on Indian Affairs of the Senate in Dec. 2009, which was enacted into law by [section 10221(a) of Pub. L. 111–148][/us/pl/111/148/s10221/a].
