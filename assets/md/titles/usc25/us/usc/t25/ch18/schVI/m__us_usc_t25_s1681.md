## § 1681. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

Section, [Pub. L. 104–134, title I][/us/pl/104/134/tI], § 101(c) \[title II\], Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–156][/us/stat/110/1321-156], 1321–190; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 104–140][/us/pl/104/140], § 1(a), May 2, 1996, [110 Stat. 1327][/us/stat/110/1327], which provided that the Indian Health Service was to neither bill nor charge those Indians who may have economic means to pay unless and until Congress directs Service to implement policy to do so, was from the Department of the Interior and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 1996, and was not repeated in subsequent appropriations acts. Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in the following prior appropriation acts:
