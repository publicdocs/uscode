## § 1621w. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–148, title X][/us/pl/111/148/tX], § 10221(a), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 935][/us/stat/124/935]

    Section, [Pub. L. 94–437, title II][/us/pl/94/437/tII], § 224, as added [Pub. L. 102–573, title II][/us/pl/102/573/tII], § 217(a), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4559][/us/stat/106/4559], authorized appropriations to carry out this subchapter through fiscal year 2000.

    The repeal is based on section 101(b)(5) of title I of S. 1790, One Hundred Eleventh Congress, as reported by the Committee on Indian Affairs of the Senate in Dec. 2009, which was enacted into law by [section 10221(a) of Pub. L. 111–148][/us/pl/111/148/s10221/a].
