### SUBCHAPTER V–A—BEHAVIORAL HEALTH PROGRAMS

    Title VII of the Indian Health Care Improvement Act, comprising this subchapter, was originally enacted by [Pub. L. 94–437, title VII][/us/pl/94/437/tVII], as added [Pub. L. 102–573, title VII][/us/pl/102/573/tVII], § 702(a), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4572][/us/stat/106/4572], and amended by [Pub. L. 104–313][/us/pl/104/313], Oct. 19, 1996, [110 Stat. 3820][/us/stat/110/3820]; [Pub. L. 105–244][/us/pl/105/244], Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1581][/us/stat/112/1581]; [Pub. L. 105–256][/us/pl/105/256], Oct. 14, 1998, [112 Stat. 1896][/us/stat/112/1896]; [Pub. L. 110–315][/us/pl/110/315], Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3078][/us/stat/122/3078]. Such title is shown herein, however, as having been added by [Pub. L. 111–148, title X][/us/pl/111/148/tX], § 10221(a), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 935][/us/stat/124/935], without reference to such intervening amendments because of the extensive revision of the title’s provisions by [Pub. L. 111–148][/us/pl/111/148]. A prior title VII was renumbered VIII by [Pub. L. 102–573][/us/pl/102/573] and is classified to subchapter VI of this chapter.
