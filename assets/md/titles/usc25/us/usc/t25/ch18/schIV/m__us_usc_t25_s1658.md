## § 1658. Limitation on contract authority

    The authority of the Secretary to enter into contracts under this subchapter shall be to the extent, and in an amount, provided for in appropriation Acts.

([Pub. L. 94–437, title V, § 508][/us/pl/94/437/s508], as added [Pub. L. 100–713, title V, § 501][/us/pl/100/713/s501], Nov. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 4824][/us/stat/102/4824].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 1658, [Pub. L. 94–437, title V, § 508][/us/pl/94/437/s508], Sept. 30, 1976, [90 Stat. 1412][/us/stat/90/1412], provided that not to exceed 1 per centum of the amounts authorized by [section 1656 of this title][/us/usc/t25/s1656] be available for not to exceed two pilot projects providing outreach services to eligible Indians residing in rural communities near Indian reservations, prior to the general revision of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 96–537, § 7][/us/pl/96/537/s7], Dec. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 3176][/us/stat/94/3176].
