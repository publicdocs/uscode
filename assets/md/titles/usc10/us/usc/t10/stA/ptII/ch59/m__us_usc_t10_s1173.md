## § 1173. Enlisted members: discharge for hardship

    Under regulations prescribed by the Secretary concerned, a regular enlisted member of an armed force who has dependents may be discharged for hardship.

(Added [Pub. L. 93–64, title I][/us/pl/93/64/tI], § 102, July 9, 1973, [87 Stat. 147][/us/stat/87/147].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective July 1, 1973, see [section 206 of Pub. L. 93–64][/us/pl/93/64/s206], set out as a note under [section 401 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s401], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.
