## § 2302b. Implementation of simplified acquisition procedures

    The simplified acquisition procedures contained in the Federal Acquisition Regulation pursuant to [section 1901 of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s1901] shall apply as provided in such section to the agencies named in [section 2303(a) of this title][/us/usc/t10/s2303/a].

(Added [Pub. L. 103–355, title IV][/us/pl/103/355/tIV], § 4203(a)(1), Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3345][/us/stat/108/3345]; amended [Pub. L. 111–350][/us/pl/111/350], § 5(b)(10), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3843][/us/stat/124/3843].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2011—[Pub. L. 111–350][/us/pl/111/350] substituted “[section 1901 of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s1901]” for “section 31 of the Office of Federal Procurement Policy Act”.

 __Effective Date__ 

    For effective date and applicability of section, see [section 10001 of Pub. L. 103–355][/us/pl/103/355/s10001], set out as an Effective Date of 1994 Amendment note under [section 2302 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s2302].
