## § 949e. Continuances

    The military judge in a military commission under this chapter may, for reasonable cause, grant a continuance to any party for such time, and as often, as may appear to be just.

(Added [Pub. L. 111–84, div. A, title XVIII][/us/pl/111/84/dA/tXVIII], § 1802, Oct. 28, 2009, [123 Stat. 2586][/us/stat/123/2586].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 949e, added [Pub. L. 109–366][/us/pl/109/366], § 3(a)(1), Oct. 17, 2006, [120 Stat. 2613][/us/stat/120/2613], related to continuances, prior to the general amendment of this chapter by [Pub. L. 111–84][/us/pl/111/84].
