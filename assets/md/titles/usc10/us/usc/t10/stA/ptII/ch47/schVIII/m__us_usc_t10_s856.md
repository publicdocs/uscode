## § 856. Art. 56. Maximum limits

    The punishment which a court-martial may direct for an offense may not exceed such limits as the President may prescribe for that offense.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 56][/us/stat/70A/56]; [Pub. L. 113–66, div. A, title XVII, § 1702(a)(1)][/us/pl/113/66/s1702/a/1], (2)(A), Dec. 26, 2013, [127 Stat. 959][/us/stat/127/959].)

 __Amendment of Section__ 

    [Pub. L. 113–66, div. A, title XVII, § 1705(a)(1)][/us/pl/113/66/s1705/a/1], (2)(A), (c), Dec. 26, 2013, [127 Stat. 959][/us/stat/127/959], 960, provided that, effective 180 days after Dec. 26, 2013, and applicable to offenses specified in subsection (b)(2) of this section committed on or after that date, this section is amended:

    (1) by amending the section catchline to read as follows:

    “§ 856. Art. 56. Maximum and minimum limits”;

    (2) by inserting “(a)” before “The punishment”; and

    (3) by adding at the end the following new subsection:

    (b)(1) While a person subject to this chapter who is found guilty of an offense specified in paragraph (2) shall be punished as a general court-martial may direct, such punishment must include, at a minimum, dismissal or dishonorable discharge, except as provided for in [section 860 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s860] (article 60).

    (2) Paragraph (1) applies to the following offenses:

    (A) An offense in violation of subsection (a) or (b) of [section 920 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s920] (article 120(a) or (b)).

    (B) Rape and sexual assault of a child under subsection (a) or (b) of [section 920b of this title][/us/usc/t10/s920b] (article 120b).

    (C) Forcible sodomy under [section 925 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s925] (article 125).

    (D) An attempt to commit an offense specified in subparagraph (A), (B), or (C) that is punishable under [section 880 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s880] (article 80).

    See 2013 Amendment notes below.
