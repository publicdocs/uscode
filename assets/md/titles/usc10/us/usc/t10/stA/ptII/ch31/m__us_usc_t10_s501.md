## § 501. Definition

    In this chapter “enlistment” means original enlistment or reenlistment.

(Added [Pub. L. 90–235, § 2(a)(1)(B)][/us/pl/90/235/s2/a/1/B], Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 753][/us/stat/81/753].)
