## § 335. Guam and Virgin Islands included as “State”

    For purposes of this chapter, the term “State” includes Guam and the Virgin Islands.

(Added [Pub. L. 90–497][/us/pl/90/497], § 11, Sept. 11, 1968, [82 Stat. 847][/us/stat/82/847]; amended [Pub. L. 96–513, title V][/us/pl/96/513/tV], § 511(11)(A), Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2920][/us/stat/94/2920]; [Pub. L. 109–163, div. A, title X][/us/pl/109/163/dA/tX], § 1057(a)(8), Jan. 6, 2006, [119 Stat. 3441][/us/stat/119/3441].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2006—[Pub. L. 109–163][/us/pl/109/163] struck out “the unincorporated territories of” before “Guam”.

    1980—[Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513] inserted “and Virgin Islands” after “Guam” in section catchline and inserted provision respecting applicability to the Virgin Islands.

 __Effective Date of 1980 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513] effective Dec. 12, 1980, see [section 701(b)(3) of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701/b/3], set out as a note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].

 __Effective Date__ 

    [Pub. L. 90–497][/us/pl/90/497], § 11, Sept. 11, 1968, [82 Stat. 847][/us/stat/82/847], provided that this section is effective on date of enactment of [Pub. L. 90–497][/us/pl/90/497], which was approved on Sept. 11, 1968.
