## § 672. Reference to chapter 1209

    Provisions of law relating to service of members of reserve components on active duty are set forth in chapter 1209 of this title (beginning with section 12301).

(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI], § 1662(e)(4), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2992][/us/stat/108/2992].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 672 was renumbered [section 12301 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12301].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see [section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337/s1691], set out as a note under [section 10001 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10001].
