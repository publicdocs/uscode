## § 853. Art. 53. Court to announce action

    A court-martial shall announce its findings and sentence to the parties as soon as determined.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 56][/us/stat/70A/56].)
