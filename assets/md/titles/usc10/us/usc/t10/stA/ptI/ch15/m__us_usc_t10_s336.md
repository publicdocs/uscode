## \[§ 336. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title V][/us/pl/96/513/tV], § 511(11)(B), Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2921][/us/stat/94/2921]\]

    Repeal effective Dec. 12, 1980, see [section 701(b)(3) of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701/b/3], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
