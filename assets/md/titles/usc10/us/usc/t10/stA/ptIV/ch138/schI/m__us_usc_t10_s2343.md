## § 2343. Waiver of applicability of certain laws

    Sections 2207, 2304(a), 2306(a), 2306(b), 2306(e), 2306a, and 2313 of this title and [section 6306 of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s6306] shall not apply to acquisitions made under the authority of [section 2341 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s2341] or to agreements entered into under [section 2342 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s2342].
