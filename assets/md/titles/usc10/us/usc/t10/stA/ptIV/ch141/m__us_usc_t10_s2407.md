L. 101–189, div. A, title IX][/us/pl/101/189/dA/tIX], § 931(d)(1), Nov. 29, 1989, [103 Stat. 1535][/us/stat/103/1535]. See [section 2350a of this title][/us/usc/t10/s2350a].
