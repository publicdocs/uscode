## § 944. Art. 144. Procedure

    The United States Court of Appeals for the Armed Forces may prescribe its rules of procedure and may determine the number of judges required to constitute a quorum.

(Added [Pub. L. 101–189, div. A, title XIII][/us/pl/101/189/dA/tXIII], § 1301(c), Nov. 29, 1989, [103 Stat. 1572][/us/stat/103/1572]; amended [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title IX][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tIX], § 924(c)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2831][/us/stat/108/2831].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337] substituted “Court of Appeals for the Armed Forces” for “Court of Military Appeals”.
