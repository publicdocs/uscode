## § 742. Rank: warrant officers

    (a) Among warrant officer grades, warrant officer grades of a higher numerical designation are senior to warrant officer grades of a lower numerical designation.

    (b) Rank among warrant officers of the same grade, and date of rank of warrant officers, is determined in the same manner as prescribed in [section 741 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s741] for officers in grades above warrant officer grades.

(Added [Pub. L. 102–190, div. A, title XI, § 1114(a)][/us/pl/102/190/s1114/a], Dec. 5, 1991, [105 Stat. 1502][/us/stat/105/1502].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 742, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 34][/us/stat/70A/34], related to rank of regular officers and reserve officers, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36B(4)][/us/pl/85/861/s36B/4], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1570][/us/stat/72/1570].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Feb. 1, 1992, see [section 1132 of Pub. L. 102–190][/us/pl/102/190/s1132], set out as an Effective Date of 1991 Amendment note under [section 521 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s521].
