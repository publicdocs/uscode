## § 948c. Persons subject to military commissions

    Any alien unprivileged enemy belligerent is subject to trial by military commission as set forth in this chapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 111–84, div. A, title XVIII][/us/pl/111/84/dA/tXVIII], § 1802, Oct. 28, 2009, [123 Stat. 2576][/us/stat/123/2576].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 948c, added [Pub. L. 109–366][/us/pl/109/366], § 3(a)(1), Oct. 17, 2006, [120 Stat. 2602][/us/stat/120/2602], related to persons subject to military commissions, prior to the general amendment of this chapter by [Pub. L. 111–84][/us/pl/111/84].
