## § 484. Quarterly cyber operations briefings

    The Secretary of Defense shall provide to the Committees on Armed Services of the House of Representatives and the Senate quarterly briefings on all offensive and significant defensive military operations in cyberspace carried out by the Department of Defense during the immediately preceding quarter.

(Added [Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title IX, § 939(a)][/us/pl/112/239/s939/a], Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 1888][/us/stat/126/1888].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 484, added [Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title III, § 324(a)(1)][/us/pl/105/85/s324/a/1], Nov. 18, 1997, [111 Stat. 1677][/us/stat/111/1677], which related to annual report on aircraft inventory, was repealed by [Pub. L. 112–81, div. A, title X, § 1061(6)(A)][/us/pl/112/81/s1061/6/A], Dec. 31, 2011, [125 Stat. 1583][/us/stat/125/1583].

 __Initial Briefing__ 

[Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title IX, § 939(b)][/us/pl/112/239/s939/b], Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 1888][/us/stat/126/1888], provided that: 

> “The first briefing required under [section 484 of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s484], United States Code, as added by subsection (a), shall be provided not later than March 1, 2013.”
