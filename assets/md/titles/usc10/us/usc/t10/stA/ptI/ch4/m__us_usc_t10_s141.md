## § 141. Inspector General

    (a) There is an Inspector General of the Department of Defense, who is appointed as provided in section 3 of the Inspector General Act of 1978 ([Public Law 95–452][/us/pl/95/452]; 5 U.S.C. App. 3).

    (b) The Inspector General performs the duties, has the responsibilities, and exercises the powers specified in the Inspector General Act of 1978.

(Added [Pub. L. 99–433, title I, § 108][/us/pl/99/433/s108], Oct. 1, 1986, [100 Stat. 998][/us/stat/100/998], § 140; renumbered § 141, [Pub. L. 103–160, div. A, title IX, § 901(a)(1)][/us/pl/103/160/s901/a/1], Nov. 30, 1993, [107 Stat. 1726][/us/stat/107/1726].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Inspector General Act of 1978, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 95–452][/us/pl/95/452], Oct. 12, 1978, [92 Stat. 1101][/us/stat/92/1101], as amended, which is set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 141 was renumbered [section 138d of this title][/us/usc/t10/s138d].

    Another prior [section 141 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s141] was contained in chapter 5 of this title, prior to amendment by [Pub. L. 99–433][/us/pl/99/433]. See note preceding [section 151 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s151].

 __Amendments__
