## § 1404. Applicability of [section 8301 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8301]

    The retirement provisions of this title are subject to [section 8301 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8301].

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 108][/us/stat/70A/108]; [Pub. L. 89–718][/us/pl/89/718], § 3, Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1115][/us/stat/80/1115].)

    The effect of the act of April 23, 1930 ([5 U.S.C. 47a][/us/usc/t5/s47a]), in temporarily deferring retirement dates otherwise specifically fixed by law is reflected in the sections of the proposed text that name those dates. This section is inserted to make clear that under that act such deferments have no effect on the applicability of the specific rates that are to be used in computing retired pay.

 __Amendments__ 

    1966—[Pub. L. 89–718][/us/pl/89/718] substituted “8301” for “47a” in section catchline and text.
