 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Chapter was comprised of subchapter I, sections 401 to 404, and subchapter II, section 410, prior to amendment by [Pub. L. 104–106, div. A, title V][/us/pl/104/106/dA/tV], § 571(c), Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 353][/us/stat/110/353], which struck out headings for subchapters I and II.
