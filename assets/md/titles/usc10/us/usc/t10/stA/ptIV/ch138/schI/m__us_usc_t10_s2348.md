## § 2348. Inventories of supplies not to be increased

    Inventories of supplies for elements of the armed forces may not be increased for the purpose of transferring supplies under the authority of this subchapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 96–323, § 2(a)][/us/pl/96/323/s2/a], Aug. 4, 1980, [94 Stat. 1018][/us/stat/94/1018], § 2328; amended [Pub. L. 97–22, § 11(a)(8)][/us/pl/97/22/s11/a/8], July 10, 1981, [95 Stat. 138][/us/stat/95/138]; renumbered § 2348, [Pub. L. 99–145, title XIII, § 1304(a)(1)][/us/pl/99/145/s1304/a/1], Nov. 8, 1985, [99 Stat. 741][/us/stat/99/741]; [Pub. L. 99–661, div. A, title XI, § 1104(e)][/us/pl/99/661/s1104/e], Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3965][/us/stat/100/3965]; [Pub. L. 101–189, div. A, title IX, § 931(e)(1)][/us/pl/101/189/s931/e/1], Nov. 29, 1989, [103 Stat. 1535][/us/stat/103/1535].)

 __Amendments__
