###### \[§§ 3302, 3303. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II][/us/pl/96/513/tII], § 204, Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2880][/us/stat/94/2880]\]

    Section 3302, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 187][/us/stat/70A/187], related to promotion to captain, major, or lieutenant colonel of commissioned officers of Medical Corps, Dental Corps, and Veterinary Corps upon examination of professional fitness and effect upon failure of promotion. See sections 631 and 632 of this title.

    Section 3303, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 188][/us/stat/70A/188]; July 12, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–616][/us/pl/86/616], § 1(3), [74 Stat. 386][/us/stat/74/386]; June 28, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–509][/us/pl/87/509], § 4(a), [76 Stat. 121][/us/stat/76/121], related to effect of failure of a promotion-list officer considered for promotion to grade of captain, major, or lieutenant colonel under [section 3299 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s3299] to be recommended for promotion, which officer was to be known as a “deferred officer”. See sections 631 and 632 of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
