## \[§§ 3883 to 3886. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II][/us/pl/96/513/tII], § 216, Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2886][/us/stat/94/2886]\]

    Section 3883, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 222][/us/stat/70A/222]; Aug. 6, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–600][/us/pl/85/600], § 1(6), [72 Stat. 522][/us/stat/72/522]; Nov. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–718][/us/pl/89/718], § 3, [80 Stat. 1115][/us/stat/80/1115]; Oct. 30, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–551][/us/pl/95/551], § 2, [92 Stat. 2069][/us/stat/92/2069], provided that, unless retired or separated at an earlier date, each commissioned officer whose regular grade is below major general, other than a professor or the director of admissions of the United States Military Academy, be retired when he becomes 60 years of age, except as provided by [section 8301 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8301]. See [section 1251 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1251].

    Section 3884, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 222][/us/stat/70A/222]; Nov. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–718][/us/pl/89/718], § 3, [80 Stat. 1115][/us/stat/80/1115], provided that, unless retired or separated at an earlier date, each commissioned officer whose regular grade is major general, and whose retirement under [section 3923 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s3923] has been deferred under cl. (1) of that section, be retired when he becomes 60 years of age, except as provided by [section 8301 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8301]. See [section 1251 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1251].

    Section 3885, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 222][/us/stat/70A/222]; Nov. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–718][/us/pl/89/718], § 3, [80 Stat. 1115][/us/stat/80/1115], provided that, unless retired or separated at an earlier date or unless retained under [section 3923(2) of this title][/us/usc/t10/s3923/2], each commissioned officer whose regular grade is major general be retired when he becomes 62 years of age, except as provided by [section 8301 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8301]. See [section 1251 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1251].

    Section 3886, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 222][/us/stat/70A/222]; Aug. 6, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–600][/us/pl/85/600], § 1(7), [72 Stat. 522][/us/stat/72/522]; Nov. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–718][/us/pl/89/718], § 3, [80 Stat. 1115][/us/stat/80/1115]; Oct. 30, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–551][/us/pl/95/551], § 2, [92 Stat. 2069][/us/stat/92/2069], provided that, unless retired or separated at an earlier date, each commissioned officer whose regular grade is major general, and whose retirement under [section 3923 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s3923] has been deferred under cl. (2) of that section, and each permanent professor and the director of admissions of the United States Military Academy, be retired when he becomes 64 years of age, except as provided by [section 8301 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8301]. See [section 1251 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1251].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
