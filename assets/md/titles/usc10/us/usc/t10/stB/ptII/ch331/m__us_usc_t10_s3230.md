## \[§ 3230. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II, § 232][/us/pl/96/513/s232], Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2886][/us/stat/94/2886]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(69)(B)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/69/B], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1464][/us/stat/72/1464], provided that members of Army who are detailed for duty with agencies of United States outside Department of Defense on a reimbursable basis not be counted in computing strengths under any law.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
