###### \[§§ 3492, 3493. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–235][/us/pl/90/235], § 1(a)(2), (b), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 753][/us/stat/81/753]\]

    Section 3492, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 198][/us/stat/70A/198], provided for extension of active service of Army members during war. See [section 671a of this title][/us/usc/t10/s671a].

    Section 3493, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 198][/us/stat/70A/198], empowered the President to order commissioned officers of the Army Reserve to active duty with the Corps of Engineers.
