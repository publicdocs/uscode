## \[§§ 3544, 3545. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–235, § 4(a)(6)][/us/pl/90/235/s4/a/6], (b)(1), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 759][/us/stat/81/759], 760\]

    Section 3544, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 203][/us/stat/70A/203], restricted performance of civil functions by commissioned officers of Regular Army. See [section 973 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s973].

    Section 3545, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 203][/us/stat/70A/203], provided that cooking for enlisted members of Army should be superintended by officers of organizations to which members belonged.
