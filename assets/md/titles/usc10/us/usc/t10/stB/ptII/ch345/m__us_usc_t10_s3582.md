## \[§ 3582. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II][/us/pl/96/513/tII], § 211, Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2885][/us/stat/94/2885]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 206][/us/stat/70A/206], provided that a retired officer has no right to command except when on active duty. See [section 750 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s750].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
