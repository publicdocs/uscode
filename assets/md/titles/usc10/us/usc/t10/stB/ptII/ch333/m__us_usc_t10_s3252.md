## \[§ 3252. Repealed. [Pub. L. 114–92, div. A, title VI, § 618(a)][/us/pl/114/92/s618/a], Nov. 25, 2015, [129 Stat. 840][/us/stat/129/840]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 110–181, div. A, title VI, § 671(a)(1)][/us/pl/110/181/s671/a/1], Jan. 28, 2008, [122 Stat. 181][/us/stat/122/181]; amended [Pub. L. 110–417][/us/pl/110/417], \[div. A\], title VI, § 615(b), Oct. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 4485][/us/stat/122/4485]; [Pub. L. 111–84, div. A, title VI, § 616(2)][/us/pl/111/84/s616/2], Oct. 28, 2009, [123 Stat. 2354][/us/stat/123/2354]; [Pub. L. 111–383, div. A, title VI, § 616(2)][/us/pl/111/383/s616/2], Jan. 7, 2011, [124 Stat. 4238][/us/stat/124/4238], related to bonus to encourage Army personnel to refer persons for enlistment in the Army.

    A prior section 3252, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 177][/us/stat/70A/177], provided that temporary enlistments could be made only in the Army without specification of component, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 90–235, § 2(a)(2)(B)][/us/pl/90/235/s2/a/2/B], Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 756][/us/stat/81/756].
