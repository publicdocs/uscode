## \[§ 3690. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–235, § 7(b)(1)][/us/pl/90/235/s7/b/1], Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 763][/us/stat/81/763]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 213][/us/stat/70A/213], exempted enlisted members of the Army, while on active duty, from arrest for any debt, unless it was contracted before enlistment and amounted to at least $20 when first contracted.
