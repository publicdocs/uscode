## § 3262. Army: percentage of high-school graduates

    Notwithstanding [section 520(b) of this title][/us/usc/t10/s520/b], of the males with no prior military service who are enlisted or inducted into the Army during any fiscal year, the number who are not high-school graduates may not exceed, as of the end of the fiscal year, 35 percent of all such persons.

(Added [Pub. L. 99–661, div. A, title IV, § 402(a)][/us/pl/99/661/s402/a], Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3859][/us/stat/100/3859]; amended [Pub. L. 100–370, § 1(a)(2)][/us/pl/100/370/s1/a/2], July 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 840][/us/stat/102/840].)
