#### \[CHAPTER 351—REPEALED\]

\[§§ 3661 to 3663. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 90–377, § 6(1)__  __][/us/pl/90/377/s6/1]__  __,__  __July 5, 1968__  __,__  __[__  __82 Stat. 288__  __][/us/stat/82/288]__  __\]__ 

    Section 3661, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 208][/us/stat/70A/208], provided for organization and administration of United States Disciplinary Barracks.

    Section 3662, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 209][/us/stat/70A/209], provided for military training, organization, and equipping of prisoners who have been sent to United States Disciplinary Barracks.

    Section 3663, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 209][/us/stat/70A/209], authorized Secretary of the Army to parole or remit sentence and restore to duty offenders who are confined in United States Disciplinary Barracks.
