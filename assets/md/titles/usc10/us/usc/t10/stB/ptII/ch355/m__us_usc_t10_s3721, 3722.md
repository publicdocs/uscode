## \[§§ 3721, 3722. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–661, div. A, title VI, § 604(f)(1)(A)][/us/pl/99/661/s604/f/1/A], Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3877][/us/stat/100/3877]\]

    Section 3721, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 214][/us/stat/70A/214]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(92)(A)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/92/A], [72 Stat. 1482][/us/stat/72/1482], related to hospital benefits for members of Army, other than of Regular Army.

    Section 3722, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 214][/us/stat/70A/214]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(92)(B)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/92/B], (C), [72 Stat. 1482][/us/stat/72/1482], related to hospital and related benefits for members of a Citizens’ Military Training Camp and for members of Army not covered by [section 3721 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s3721].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal applicable with respect to persons who, after Nov. 14, 1986, incur or aggravate an injury, illness, or disease or die, see [section 604(g) of Pub. L. 99–661][/us/pl/99/661/s604/g], set out as an Effective Date of 1986 Amendment note under [section 1074a of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1074a].
