## \[§§ 4310, 4311. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–106, div. A, title XVI, § 1624(a)(1)][/us/pl/104/106/s1624/a/1], Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 522][/us/stat/110/522]\]

    Section 4310, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 236][/us/stat/70A/236], permitted President and Secretary of the Army to detail members of Army as rifle instructors for civilians.

    Section 4311, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 237][/us/stat/70A/237]; Nov. 5, 1990, [Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title III, § 328(f)][/us/pl/101/510/s328/f], [104 Stat. 1534][/us/stat/104/1534], permitted Secretary of the Army to provide for issue of military rifles and sale of ammunition for use in rifle instruction for civilians.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective on the earlier of the date on which the Secretary of the Army submits a certification in accordance with section 5523 of \[former\] Title 36, Patriotic Societies and Observances, or Oct. 1, 1996, see [section 1624(c) of Pub. L. 104–106][/us/pl/104/106/s1624/c], set out as an Effective Date of 1996 Amendment note under [section 4316 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s4316].
