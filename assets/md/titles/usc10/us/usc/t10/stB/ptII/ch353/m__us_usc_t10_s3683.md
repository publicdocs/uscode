## \[§ 3683. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–145, title XIII][/us/pl/99/145/tXIII], § 1301(b)(1)(A), Nov. 8, 1985, [99 Stat. 735][/us/stat/99/735]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 210][/us/stat/70A/210]; Aug. 25, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–197][/us/pl/86/197], § 1(4), [73 Stat. 426][/us/stat/73/426], related to service credit for certain service as a nurse, woman medical specialist, or civilian employee of Army Medical Department.

 __Person Performing Active Service on Day Before Repeal of Section__ 

[Pub. L. 99–145, title XIII][/us/pl/99/145/tXIII], § 1301(b)(1)(C), Nov. 8, 1985, [99 Stat. 735][/us/stat/99/735], provided that: 

> “The repeal made by subparagraph (A) \[repealing this section\] shall not apply in the case of a person who performed active service described in [section 3683 of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s3683], United States Code, as such section was in effect on the day before the date of the enactment of this Act \[Nov. 8, 1985\].”
