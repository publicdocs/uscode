## \[§ 3847. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–130, § 1(12)(A)][/us/pl/90/130/s1/12/A], Nov. 8, 1967, [81 Stat. 376][/us/stat/81/376]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(94)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/94], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1484][/us/stat/72/1484]; [Pub. L. 86–559, § 1(25)][/us/pl/86/559/s1/25], June 30, 1960, [74 Stat. 272][/us/stat/74/272], provided for transfer to Retired Reserve or discharge from his reserve appointment, after July 1, 1960, of each officer in a reserve grade below lieutenant colonel with 25 years service assigned to Army Nurse Corps, Army Medical Specialist Corps, or Women’s Army Corps who had not been recommended for promotion to reserve grade of lieutenant colonel or who has not remained on active duty since such a recommendation.
