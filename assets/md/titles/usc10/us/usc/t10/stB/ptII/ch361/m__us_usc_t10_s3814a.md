## \[§ 3814a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1629(a)(2)][/us/pl/103/337/s1629/a/2], Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2963][/us/stat/108/2963]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 93–558, § 1][/us/pl/93/558/s1], Dec. 30, 1974, [88 Stat. 1793][/us/stat/88/1793], related to discharge, during a reduction in force, of regular commissioned officers, second lieutenants, first lieutenants, and captains, and expired three years after its effective date, Dec. 30, 1974, in accordance with [section 2 of Pub.     Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1996, see [section 1691(b)(1) of Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337/s1691/b/1], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 10001 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10001].
