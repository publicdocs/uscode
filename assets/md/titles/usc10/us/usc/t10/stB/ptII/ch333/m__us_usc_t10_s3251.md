## § 3251. Definition

    In this chapter, the term “enlistment” means original enlistment or reenlistment.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 177][/us/stat/70A/177]; [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title XII][/us/pl/100/180/dA/tXII], § 1231(19)(A), Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1161][/us/stat/101/1161].)
