## \[§ 4775. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–145, title V][/us/pl/92/145/tV], § 509(a), Oct. 27, 1971, [85 Stat. 408][/us/stat/85/408]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 269][/us/stat/70A/269], authorized assignment of quarters belonging to United States at a post or station by post quartermaster to officers, grade lieutenant general down to second lieutenant, 10 to 2 rooms, respectively, and prohibited other assignment where quarters existed.
