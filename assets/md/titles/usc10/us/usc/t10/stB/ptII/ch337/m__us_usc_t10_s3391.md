    Section 3396, added [Pub. L. 96–513, title II, § 206(a)][/us/pl/96/513/s206/a], Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2884][/us/stat/94/2884], provided that chapter, except section 3353, did not apply to reserve officers on active-duty list.
