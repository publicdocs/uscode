## \[§§ 3815, 3816. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–235, § 3(a)(2)][/us/pl/90/235/s3/a/2], (b)(1), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 757][/us/stat/81/757], 758\]

    Section 3815, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 220][/us/stat/70A/220], provided for resignation of regular enlisted members of Army enlisted on a career basis and limitations thereon.

    Section 3816, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 221][/us/stat/70A/221], provided for minority discharges for regular enlisted members of Army. See [section 1170 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1170].
