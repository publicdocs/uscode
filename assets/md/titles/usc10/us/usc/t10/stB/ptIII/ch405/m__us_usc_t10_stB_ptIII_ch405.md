#### \[CHAPTER 405—REPEALED\]

\[§§ 4381 to 4387. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 88–647, title III, § 301(10)__  __][/us/pl/88/647/s301/10]__  __,__  __Oct. 13, 1964__  __,__  __[__  __78 Stat. 1072__  __][/us/stat/78/1072]__  __\]__ 

    Sections, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 246–248][/us/stat/70A/246-248], related to the Reserve Officers’ Training Corps and defined “advanced training”, provided for its establishment and composition, admission and training of medical, dental pharmacy and veterinary students, set out courses of training, authorized the operation and maintenance of training camps, provided for supplies and uniforms and for advanced training and compensation therefor. See chapter 103 of this title.
