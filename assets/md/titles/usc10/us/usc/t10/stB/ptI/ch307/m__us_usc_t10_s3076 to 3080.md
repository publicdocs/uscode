## \[§§ 3076 to 3080. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1661(a)(3)(A)][/us/pl/103/337/s1661/a/3/A], Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2980][/us/stat/108/2980]\]

    Section 3076, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 170][/us/stat/70A/170], related to composition of Army Reserve. See [section 10104 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10104].

    Section 3077, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 170][/us/stat/70A/170], related to composition of Army National Guard of United States. See [section 10105 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10105].

    Section 3078, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 171][/us/stat/70A/171], provided that Army National Guard is a component of Army while in service of United States. See [section 10106 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10106].

    Section 3079, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 171][/us/stat/70A/171], related to status of Army National Guard of United States when not in Federal service. See [section 10107 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10107].

    Section 3080, added [Pub. L. 86–603, § 1(2)(A)][/us/pl/86/603/s1/2/A], July 7, 1960, [74 Stat. 357][/us/stat/74/357], related to authority of officers of Army National Guard of United States with respect to Federal status. See [section 10215 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10215].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see [section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337/s1691], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 10001 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10001].
