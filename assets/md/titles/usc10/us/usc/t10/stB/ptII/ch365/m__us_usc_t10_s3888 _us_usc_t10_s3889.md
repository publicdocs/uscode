###### \[§§ 3888, 3889. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II][/us/pl/96/513/tII], § 216, Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2886][/us/stat/94/2886]\]

    Section 3888, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 223][/us/stat/70A/223]; Aug. 21, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–155, title I][/us/pl/85/155/tI], § 101(18), [71 Stat. 379][/us/stat/71/379]; May 20, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–422][/us/pl/85/422], § 11(a)(3), [72 Stat. 131][/us/stat/72/131]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 1(96), [72 Stat. 1487][/us/stat/72/1487], related to computation of service for determining retired pay of a commissioned officer of Regular Army retired under section 3883, 3884, 3885, or 3886 of this title. See [section 1405 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1405].

    Section 3889, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 224][/us/stat/70A/224], provided that a member of Army retired under this chapter be entitled to retired pay computed under chapter 371 of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
