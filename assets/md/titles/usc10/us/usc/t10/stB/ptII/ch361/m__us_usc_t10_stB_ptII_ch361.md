#### \[CHAPTER 361—REPEALED\]

\[§§ 3811 to 3813. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 90–235, § 3(a)(2)__  __][/us/pl/90/235/s3/a/2]__  __, (b)(1),__  __Jan. 2, 1968__  __,__  __[__  __81 Stat. 757__  __][/us/stat/81/757]__  __, 758\]__ 

    Section 3811, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 220][/us/stat/70A/220], provided for discharge of enlisted members of Army and limitations thereon, and for issuance of discharge certificates. See [section 1169 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1169].

    Section 3812, act Aug. 1956, ch. 1041, [70A Stat. 220][/us/stat/70A/220], provided for the discharge of members of the Army enlisted during war or emergency. See [section 1172 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1172].

    Section 3813, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 220][/us/stat/70A/220], provided for dependency discharges for enlisted members of the Army.
