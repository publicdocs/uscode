## \[§§ 4307, 4308. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–106, div. A, title XVI, § 1624(a)(1)][/us/pl/104/106/s1624/a/1], Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 522][/us/stat/110/522]\]

    Section 4307, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 235][/us/stat/70A/235], permitted President to detail commissioned officer of the Army or of the Marine Corps as director of civilian marksmanship.

    Section 4308, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 236][/us/stat/70A/236]; Nov. 14, 1986, [Pub. L. 99–661, div. A, title III, § 318(a)][/us/pl/99/661/s318/a], [100 Stat. 3855][/us/stat/100/3855]; Nov. 5, 1990, [Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title III, § 328(b)][/us/pl/101/510/s328/b]–(d), (g)(1), [104 Stat. 1533][/us/stat/104/1533], 1534; Oct. 23, 1992, [Pub. L. 102–484, div. A, title III, § 380(a)(1)][/us/pl/102/484/s380/a/1], [106 Stat. 2389][/us/stat/106/2389]; Nov. 30, 1993, [Pub. L. 103–160, div. A, title III, § 372][/us/pl/103/160/s372], [107 Stat. 1635][/us/stat/107/1635], related to authority of Secretary of the Army to promote civilian marksmanship. See section 40701 et seq. of Title 36, Patriotic and National Observances, Ceremonies, and Organizations.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective on the earlier of the date on which the Secretary of the Army submits a certification in accordance with section 5523 of \[former\] Title 36, Patriotic Societies and Observances, or Oct. 1, 1996, see [section 1624(c) of Pub. L. 104–106][/us/pl/104/106/s1624/c], set out as an Effective Date of 1996 Amendment note under [section 4316 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s4316].
