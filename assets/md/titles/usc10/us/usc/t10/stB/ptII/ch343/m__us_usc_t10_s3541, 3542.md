## \[§§ 3541, 3542. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337], §§ 1661(c)(2), 1662(g)(2), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2982][/us/stat/108/2982], 2996\]

    Section 3541, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 202][/us/stat/70A/202], authorized President to assign regular and reserve Army officers to National Guard Bureau. See [section 10507 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10507].

    Section 3542, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 202][/us/stat/70A/202], authorized President to detail certain officers as chief and assistant chief of staff of divisions of Army National Guard in Federal service. See [section 12502(a) of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12502/a].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see [section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337/s1691], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 10001 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10001].
