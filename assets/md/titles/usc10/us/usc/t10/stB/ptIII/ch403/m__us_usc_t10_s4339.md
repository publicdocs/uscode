## \[§ 4339. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–716, § 1][/us/pl/89/716/s1], Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1114][/us/stat/80/1114]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 240][/us/stat/70A/240], authorized public quarters and fuel and light therefor for the organist and choirmaster of the Academy and for civilian instructors in the departments of foreign languages and tactics.

 __Quarters for Organist, Choirmaster and Certain Civilian Instructors Appointed Prior to__  __Jan. 17, 1963__ 

    [Pub. L. 89–716, § 2][/us/pl/89/716/s2], Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1114][/us/stat/80/1114], provided that the organist and choirmaster and the civilian instructors in departments of foreign languages and tactics at United States Military Academy who were serving under appointments made prior to Jan. 17, 1963, were entitled to public quarters without charge, and to fuel and light without charge when they occupy public quarters.
