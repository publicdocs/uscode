#### \[CHAPTER 365—REPEALED\]

\[§§ 3881, 3882. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 85–155, title IV, § 401(1)__  __][/us/pl/85/155/s401/1]__  __,__  __Aug. 21, 1957__  __,__  __[__  __71 Stat. 390__  __][/us/stat/71/390]__  __\]__ 

    Section 3881, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 222][/us/stat/70A/222], authorized Secretary of the Army to retire regular commissioned officers of Army Nurse Corps or Women’s Medical Specialist Corps whose regular grade is below major.

    Section 3882, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 222][/us/stat/70A/222], authorized Secretary of the Army to retire regular commissioned officers of Army Nurse Corps or Women’s Medical Specialist Corps whose regular grade is above captain.
