## § 10112. Air National Guard: when a component of the Air Force

    The Air National Guard while in the service of the United States is a component of the Air Force.

(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI], § 1661(a)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2971][/us/stat/108/2971].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 8078 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s8078], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337], § 1661(a)(3)(A).
