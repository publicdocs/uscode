## § 10210. Dissemination of information

    The Secretary of Defense shall require the complete and current dissemination, to all Reserves and to the public, of information of interest to the reserve components.

(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI], § 1661(a)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2978][/us/stat/108/2978].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 278 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s278], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337], § 1661(a)(2)(A).
