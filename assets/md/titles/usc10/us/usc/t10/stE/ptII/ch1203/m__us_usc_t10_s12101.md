## § 12101. Definition

    In this chapter, the term “enlistment” means original enlistment or reenlistment.

(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI], § 1662(b)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2988][/us/stat/108/2988].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Chapter effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see [section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337/s1691], set out as a note under [section 10001 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10001].
