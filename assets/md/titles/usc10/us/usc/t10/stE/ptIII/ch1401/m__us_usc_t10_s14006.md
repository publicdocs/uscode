## § 14006. Determination of years in grade

    For the purpose of chapters 1403 through 1411 of this title, an officer’s years of service in a grade are computed from the officer’s date of rank in grade as determined under [section 741(d) of this title][/us/usc/t10/s741/d].

(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1611][/us/pl/103/337/s1611], Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2923][/us/stat/108/2923].)
