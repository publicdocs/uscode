## § 14103. Oath of members

    Each member of a selection board convened under [section 14101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s14101] shall take an oath to perform the duties of a member of the board without prejudice or partiality, having in view both the special fitness of officers and the efficiency of the member’s armed force.

(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI], § 1611, Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2925][/us/stat/108/2925].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in sections 3362(d), 5894, and 8362(d) of this title, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337], § 1629(a)(1), (b)(2), (c)(1).
