## § 10107. Army National Guard of the United States: status when not in Federal service

    When not on active duty, members of the Army National Guard of the United States shall be administered, armed, equipped, and trained in their status as members of the Army National Guard.

(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI], § 1661(a)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2971][/us/stat/108/2971].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 3079 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s3079], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337], § 1661(a)(3)(A).
