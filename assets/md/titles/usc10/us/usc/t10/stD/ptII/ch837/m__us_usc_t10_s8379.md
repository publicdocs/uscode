## \[§ 8379. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI], § 1636(c), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2968][/us/stat/108/2968]\]

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Feb. 10, 1996, see [section 1501(f)(1) of Pub. L. 104–106][/us/pl/104/106/s1501/f/1], set out as an Effective Date of 1994 Amendment note under [section 12213 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12213].
