## \[§ 9513. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–291, div. A, title X][/us/pl/113/291/dA/tX], § 1045(a), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3494][/us/stat/128/3494]\]

A, title XVI][/us/pl/101/189/dA/tXVI], § 1636(c)(1), Nov. 29, 1989, [103 Stat. 1610][/us/stat/103/1610], directed that each contract under [section 9512 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s9512] be committed to Civil Reserve Air Fleet, prior to amendment by [Pub. L. 103–355][/us/pl/103/355], § 3032(4), (5), (9), which struck out section catchline and redesignated subsecs. (a) and (b) as subsecs. (b) and (e) of section 9512, respectively.
