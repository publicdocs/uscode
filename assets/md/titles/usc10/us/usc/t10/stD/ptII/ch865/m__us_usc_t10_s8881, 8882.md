## \[§§ 8881, 8882. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–155, title IV, § 401(1)][/us/pl/85/155/s401/1], Aug. 21, 1957, [71 Stat. 390][/us/stat/71/390]\]

    Section 8882, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 546][/us/stat/70A/546], authorized Secretary of Air Force to retire Air Force nurses or woman medical specialists whose regular grade is above captain.
