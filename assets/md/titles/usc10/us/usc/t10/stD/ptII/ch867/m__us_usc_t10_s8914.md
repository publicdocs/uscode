## § 8914. Twenty to thirty years: enlisted members

    Under regulations to be prescribed by the Secretary of the Air Force, an enlisted member of the Air Force who has at least 20, but less than 30, years of service computed under [section 8925 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s8925] may, upon his request, be retired.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 550][/us/stat/70A/550]; [Pub. L. 96–343, § 9(b)(1)][/us/pl/96/343/s9/b/1], Sept. 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 1128][/us/stat/94/1128]; [Pub.  __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337] struck out at end “A regular enlisted member then becomes a member of the Air Force Reserve. A member retired under this section shall perform such active duty as may be prescribed by law until his service computed under [section 8925 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s8925], plus his inactive service as a member of the Air Force Reserve, equals 30 years.”
