## \[§ 8066. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II][/us/pl/96/513/tII], § 201, Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2878][/us/stat/94/2878]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 494][/us/stat/70A/494]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 33(a)(36), [72 Stat. 1566][/us/stat/72/1566], authorized President, by and with consent of the Senate, to make temporary appointments in grades of general and lieutenant general from officers of Air Force on active duty in any grade above brigadier general and specified the number of positions in each such grade. See [section 601 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s601].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
