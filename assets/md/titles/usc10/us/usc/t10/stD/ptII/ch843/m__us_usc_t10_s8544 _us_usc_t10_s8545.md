###### \[§§ 8544, 8545. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–235][/us/pl/90/235], § 4(a)(6), (b)(1), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 759][/us/stat/81/759], 760\]

    Section 8544, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 527][/us/stat/70A/527], restricted performance of civil functions by commissioned officers of Regular Air Force. See [section 973 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s973].

    Section 8545, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 528][/us/stat/70A/528], provided that cooking for enlisted members of Air Force should be superintended by officers of organizations to which members belonged.
