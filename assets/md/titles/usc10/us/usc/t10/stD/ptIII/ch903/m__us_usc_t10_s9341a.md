## § 9341a. Cadets: appointment by the President

    Cadets at the Academy shall be appointed by the President alone. An appointment is conditional until the cadet is admitted.

(Added [Pub. L. 97–60, title II, § 203(c)(2)(A)][/us/pl/97/60/s203/c/2/A], Oct. 14, 1981, [95 Stat. 1006][/us/stat/95/1006].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective with respect to nominations for appointment to the first class admitted to each Academy after Oct. 14, 1981, see [section 203(d) of Pub. L. 97–60][/us/pl/97/60/s203/d], set out as a note under [section 4341a of this title][/us/usc/t10/s4341a].
