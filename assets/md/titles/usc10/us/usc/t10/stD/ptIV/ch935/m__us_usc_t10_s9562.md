## § 9562. Clothing

    The President may prescribe the quantity and kind of clothing to be issued annually to members of the Air Force.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 577][/us/stat/70A/577].)
