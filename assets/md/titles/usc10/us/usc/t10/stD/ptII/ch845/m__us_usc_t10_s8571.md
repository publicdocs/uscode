## \[§ 8571. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II][/us/pl/96/513/tII], § 211, Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2885][/us/stat/94/2885]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 528][/us/stat/70A/528]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], §§ 1(183), 33(a)(41), [72 Stat. 1533][/us/stat/72/1533], 1566; June 30, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–559][/us/pl/86/559], § 1(64), [74 Stat. 278][/us/stat/74/278], provided that commissioned officers of Air Force on active duty in same grade rank themselves according to date of rank and specified procedures for determining date of rank. See [section 741 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s741].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
