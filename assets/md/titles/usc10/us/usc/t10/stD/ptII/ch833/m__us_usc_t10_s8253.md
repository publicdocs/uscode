## \[§ 8253. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–163, div. A, title V, § 542(b)(1)][/us/pl/109/163/s542/b/1], Jan. 6, 2006, [119 Stat. 3253][/us/stat/119/3253]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 503][/us/stat/70A/503]; [Pub. L. 87–143, § 1(2)][/us/pl/87/143/s1/2], Aug. 17, 1961, [75 Stat. 364][/us/stat/75/364]; [Pub. L. 90–235, § 2(a)(4)(A)][/us/pl/90/235/s2/a/4/A], Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 756][/us/stat/81/756]; [Pub. L. 96–513, title V, § 514(2)][/us/pl/96/513/s514/2], Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2935][/us/stat/94/2935], provided that, in peace time, Air Force enlistment was available only to citizens and persons lawfully admitted to the United States for permanent residence.

\[§§ 8254 to 8256. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 90–235, § 2(a)__  __][/us/pl/90/235/s2/a]__  __(4)(B),__  __Jan. 2, 1968__  __,__  __[__  __81 Stat. 756__  __][/us/stat/81/756]__  __\]__ 

    Section 8254, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 503][/us/stat/70A/503], provided for temporary enlistments in Air Force during war or emergency.

    Section 8255, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 504][/us/stat/70A/504], provided for recruiting campaigns to obtain enlistments in Regular Air Force.

    Section 8256, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 504][/us/stat/70A/504], set forth qualifications for and term of enlistments in Regular Air Force and grade in which such enlistments were made.

 __Members of Army and Air Force Serving Under Enlistments for Unspecified Periods on__  __Jan. 2, 1968__  __;__  __Continuance in Status; Discharge__ 

    Members of Air Force serving under enlistments for unspecified periods on Jan. 2, 1968, continued in that status and discharged in accordance with laws applicable on Jan. 1, 1968, see [section 3(c) of Pub. L. 90–235][/us/pl/90/235/s3/c], set out as a note under [section 3256 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s3256].
