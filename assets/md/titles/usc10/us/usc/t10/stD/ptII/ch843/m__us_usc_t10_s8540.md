## \[§ 8540. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–647, title III, § 301(23)][/us/pl/88/647/s301/23], Oct. 13, 1964, [78 Stat. 1073][/us/stat/78/1073]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 527][/us/stat/70A/527], related to detail of members of regular or reserve components as professors and instructors in air science and tactics. See [section 2111 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s2111].

\[§§ 8541, 8542. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI__  __][/us/pl/103/337]__  __, §§ 1661(c)(2), 1662(g)(2),__  __Oct. 5, 1994__  __,__  __[__  __108 Stat. 2982__  __][/us/stat/108/2982]__  __, 2996\]__ 

    Section 8541, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 527][/us/stat/70A/527], authorized President to assign regular and reserve Air Force officers to National Guard Bureau. See [section 10507 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10507].

    Section 8542, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 527][/us/stat/70A/527], authorized President to detail certain officers as chief and assistant chief of staff of wings of Air National Guard in Federal service. See [section 12502(b) of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12502/b].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see [section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337/s1691], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 10001 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10001].
