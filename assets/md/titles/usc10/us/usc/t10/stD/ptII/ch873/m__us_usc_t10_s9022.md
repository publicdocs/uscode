## \[§ 9022. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–94, title IX, § 932(c)(1)][/us/pl/98/94/s932/c/1], Sept. 24, 1983, [97 Stat. 650][/us/stat/97/650]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 558][/us/stat/70A/558], authorized Secretary of the Air Force to employ contract surgeons in an emergency. See [section 1091 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1091].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1983, but with contracts entered into under the authority of this section before Oct. 1, 1983, which are in effect on Oct. 1, 1983, to remain in effect in accordance with the terms of such contracts, see [section 932(f) of Pub. L. 98–94][/us/pl/98/94/s932/f], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 1091 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1091].
