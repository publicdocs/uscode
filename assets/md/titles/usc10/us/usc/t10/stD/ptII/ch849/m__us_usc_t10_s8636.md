## \[§ 8636. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649], § 14c(56), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 502][/us/stat/76/502]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 533][/us/stat/70A/533], provided that pay and allowances do not accrue to an enlisted member of Air Force who is in confinement under sentence of dishonorable discharge, while execution of sentence to discharge is suspended. See [section 858b of this title][/us/usc/t10/s858b].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Nov. 1, 1962, see [section 15 of Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649/s15], set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 101 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s101], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.
