## § 8023. Chief of Legislative Liaison

    (a) There is a Chief of Legislative Liaison in the Department of the Air Force. An officer assigned to that position shall be an officer in the grade of major general.

    (b) The Chief of Legislative Liaison shall perform legislative affairs functions as specified for the Office of the Secretary of the Air Force by [section 8014(c)(1)(F) of this title][/us/usc/t10/s8014/c/1/F].

(Added [Pub. L. 107–314, div. A, title V, § 504(e)(1)(A)][/us/pl/107/314/s504/e/1/A], Dec. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 2532][/us/stat/116/2532].)
