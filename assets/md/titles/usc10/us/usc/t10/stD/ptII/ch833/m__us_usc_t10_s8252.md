## § 8252. Regular Air Force: gender-free basis for acceptance of original enlistments

    In accepting persons for original enlistment in the Regular Air Force, the Secretary of the Air Force may not—

    (1) set a minimum or maximum percentage of persons who may be accepted for such an enlistment according to gender for skill categories or jobs; or

    (2) in any other way base the acceptance of a person for such an enlistment on gender.

(Added [Pub. L. 100–456, div. A, title V][/us/pl/100/456/dA/tV], § 522(a)(1), Sept. 29, 1988, [102 Stat. 1973][/us/stat/102/1973]; amended [Pub. L. 102–484, div. A, title X][/us/pl/102/484/dA/tX], § 1052(40), Oct. 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 2501][/us/stat/106/2501].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 8252, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 503][/us/stat/70A/503], provided that temporary enlistments could be made only in Air Force without specification of component, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 90–235][/us/pl/90/235], § 2(a)(4)(B), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 756][/us/stat/81/756].

 __Amendments__ 

    1992—[Pub. L. 102–484][/us/pl/102/484] substituted “In” for “(a) Except as provided in subsection (b), in” and struck out subsec. (b) which read as follows: “Subsection (a) shall not apply with respect to an enlistment specified as being for training leading to designation in a skill category involving duty assignments to which, under [section 8549 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s8549], female members of the Air Force may not be assigned.”

 __Effective Date__ 

[Pub. L. 100–456, div. A, title V][/us/pl/100/456/dA/tV], § 522(c), Sept. 29, 1988, [102 Stat. 1974][/us/stat/102/1974], provided that: 

> “Such section \[[10 U.S.C. 8252][/us/usc/t10/s8252]\] shall apply with respect to persons accepted for original enlistment in the Regular Air Force after September 30, 1989.”

 __Implementation__ 

[Pub. L. 100–456, div. A, title V][/us/pl/100/456/dA/tV], § 522(b), Sept. 29, 1988, [102 Stat. 1973][/us/stat/102/1973], provided that: 

> “The Secretary of the Air Force shall develop a methodology for implementing [section 8252 of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s8252], United States Code, as added by subsection (a), not later than October 1, 1989.”
