#### \[CHAPTER 851—REPEALED\]

\[§§ 8662, 8663. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 90–377, § 6(3)__  __][/us/pl/90/377/s6/3]__  __,__  __July 5, 1968__  __,__  __[__  __82 Stat. 288__  __][/us/stat/82/288]__  __\]__ 

    Section 8662, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 533][/us/stat/70A/533], provided for military training, organization, and equipping of prisoners who have been sent to United States Disciplinary Barracks.

    Section 8663, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 533][/us/stat/70A/533], authorized Secretary of Air Force to parole or remit sentence and restore to duty offenders who are confined in the United States Disciplinary Barracks.
