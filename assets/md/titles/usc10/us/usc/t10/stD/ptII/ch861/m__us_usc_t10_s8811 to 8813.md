## \[§§ 8811 to 8813. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–235, § 3(a)(2)][/us/pl/90/235/s3/a/2], (b)(1), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 757][/us/stat/81/757], 758\]

    Section 8811, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 544][/us/stat/70A/544], provided for discharge of enlisted members of Air Force and limitations thereon, and for issuance of discharge certificates. See [section 1169 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1169].

    Section 8812, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 544][/us/stat/70A/544], provided for discharge of members of Air Force enlisted during war or emergency. See [section 1172 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1172].

    Section 8813, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 544][/us/stat/70A/544], provided for dependency discharges for enlisted members of Air Force.
