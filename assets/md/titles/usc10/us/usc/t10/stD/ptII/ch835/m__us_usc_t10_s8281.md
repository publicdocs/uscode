## § 8281. Commissioned officer grades

    The commissioned grades in the Regular Air Force are:

    (1) Major general.

    (2) Brigadier general.

    (3) Colonel.

    (4) Lieutenant colonel.

    (5) Major.

    (6) Captain.

    (7) First lieutenant.

    (8) Second lieutenant.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 507][/us/stat/70A/507].)
