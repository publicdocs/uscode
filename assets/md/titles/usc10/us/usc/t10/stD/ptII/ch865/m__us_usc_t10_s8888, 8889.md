## \[§§ 8888, 8889. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II, § 216][/us/pl/96/513/s216], Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2886][/us/stat/94/2886]\]

    Section 8888, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 547][/us/stat/70A/547]; Aug. 21, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–155, title III, § 301(16)][/us/pl/85/155/s301/16], [71 Stat. 388][/us/stat/71/388]; May 20, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–422, § 11(a)(7)][/us/pl/85/422/s11/a/7], [72 Stat. 131][/us/stat/72/131]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(194)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/194], [72 Stat. 1538][/us/stat/72/1538]; Sept. 30, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–609, § 1(30)][/us/pl/89/609/s1/30], [80 Stat. 854][/us/stat/80/854], related to computation of years of service for determining retired pay of a commissioned officer of Regular Air Force retired under section 8883, 8884, 8885, or 8886 of this title. See [section 1405 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1405].

    Section 8889, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 548][/us/stat/70A/548], provided that a member of Air Force retired under this chapter be entitled to retired pay computed under chapter 871 of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
