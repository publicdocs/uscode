## \[§ 8494. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II, § 209(a)][/us/pl/96/513/s209/a], Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2884][/us/stat/94/2884]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(181)(A)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/181/A], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1532][/us/stat/72/1532]; amended [Pub. L. 86–559, § 1(63)][/us/pl/86/559/s1/63], June 30, 1960, [74 Stat. 278][/us/stat/74/278], provided that a reserve commissioned officer who is ordered to active duty be ordered to that duty in his reserve grade unless the Secretary of the Air Force orders him to active duty, other than for training, in a higher temporary grade and authorized a reserve commissioned officer who is selected for participation in a program under which he will be ordered to active duty for at least one academic year at a civilian school or college to be ordered, upon his request, to that duty in a temporary grade that is lower than his reserve grade, without affecting his reserve grade. See [section 12320 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12320].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].

\[§§ 8495 to 8502. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1662(f)(2)__  __][/us/pl/103/337/s1662/f/2]__  __,__  __Oct. 5, 1994__  __,__  __[__  __108 Stat. 2994__  __][/us/stat/108/2994]__  __\]__ 

    Section 8495, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 524][/us/stat/70A/524], provided that members of Air National Guard of United States were not in active Federal service except when ordered thereto under law. See [section 12401 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12401].

    Section 8496, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 524][/us/stat/70A/524], authorized President to order commissioned officers of Air National Guard of United States to active duty in National Guard Bureau. See section 12402(a), (b)(2) of this title.

    Section 8497, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 525][/us/stat/70A/525], provided that members of Air National Guard of United States ordered to active duty were to be ordered to duty as Reserves of Air Force. See [section 12403 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12403].

    Section 8498, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 525][/us/stat/70A/525], related to organization during initial mobilization of units of Air National Guard of United States ordered into active Federal service. See [section 12404 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12404].

    Section 8499, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 525][/us/stat/70A/525], related to application of laws governing Air Force to members of Air National Guard called into Federal service. See [section 12405 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12405].

    Section 8500, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 525][/us/stat/70A/525]; Sept. 29, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–456, div. A, title XII, § 1234(a)(1)][/us/pl/100/456/s1234/a/1], [102 Stat. 2059][/us/stat/102/2059], authorized President to call Air National Guard units and members into Federal service. See [section 12406 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12406].

    Section 8501, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 525][/us/stat/70A/525]; Sept. 29, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–456, div. A, title XII, § 1234(a)(1)][/us/pl/100/456/s1234/a/1], [102 Stat. 2059][/us/stat/102/2059], related to period of service and apportionment of members and units of Air National Guard called into Federal service. See [section 12407 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12407].

    Section 8502, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 526][/us/stat/70A/526], related to physical examinations of members of Air National Guard called into or mustered out of Federal service. See [section 12408 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s12408].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see [section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337/s1691], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 10001 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10001].
