## \[§ 8504. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title II][/us/pl/96/513/tII], § 210, Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2884][/us/stat/94/2884]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 526][/us/stat/70A/526]; Nov. 8, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–130][/us/pl/90/130], § 1(29), [81 Stat. 382][/us/stat/81/382], authorized President to order any retired member of Regular Air Force to active duty and assign him duties considered necessary in interests of national defense.
