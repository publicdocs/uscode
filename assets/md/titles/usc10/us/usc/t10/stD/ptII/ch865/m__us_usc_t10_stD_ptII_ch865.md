#### \[CHAPTER 865—REPEALED\]

\[§§ 8881, 8882. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 85–155, title IV, § 401(1)__  __][/us/pl/85/155/s401/1]__  __,__  __Aug. 21, 1957__  __,__  __[__  __71 Stat. 390__  __][/us/stat/71/390]__  __\]__ 

    Section 8881, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 546][/us/stat/70A/546], authorized Secretary of Air Force to retire Air Force nurses and woman medical specialists whose regular grade is below major.

    Section 8882, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 546][/us/stat/70A/546], authorized Secretary of Air Force to retire Air Force nurses or woman medical specialists whose regular grade is above captain.

    Section 8883, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 546][/us/stat/70A/546]; Aug. 6, 1958, [Pub. See [section 1251 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1251].

See [section 1251 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1251].

L. 89–718, § 3][/us/pl/89/718/s3], [80 Stat. 1115][/us/stat/80/1115], provided that, unless retired or separated at an earlier date, each commissioned officer whose regular grade is major general, and whose retirement under [section 8923 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s8923] has been deferred under cl.
