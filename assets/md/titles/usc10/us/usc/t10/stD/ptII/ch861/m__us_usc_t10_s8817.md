## § 8817. Aviation cadets: discharge

    The Secretary of the Air Force may discharge an aviation cadet at any time.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 545][/us/stat/70A/545].)
