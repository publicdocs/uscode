## \[§§ 8076 to 8080. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI], § 1661(a)(3)(A), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2980][/us/stat/108/2980]\]

    Section 8076, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 496][/us/stat/70A/496], related to composition of Air Force Reserve. See [section 10110 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10110].

    Section 8077, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 496][/us/stat/70A/496], related to composition of Air National Guard of United States. See [section 10111 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10111].

    Section 8078, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 496][/us/stat/70A/496], provided that Air National Guard is component of Air Force when in service of United States. See [section 10112 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10112].

    Section 8079, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 496][/us/stat/70A/496], related to status of Air National Guard of United States when not in Federal service. See [section 10113 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10113].

    Section 8080, added [Pub. L. 86–603][/us/pl/86/603], § 1(3)(A), July 7, 1960, [74 Stat. 357][/us/stat/74/357], related to authority of officers of Air National Guard of United States with respect to Federal status. See [section 10215 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10215].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see [section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337/s1691], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 10001 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10001].
