## § 8251. Definition

    In this chapter, the term “enlistment” means original enlistment or reenlistment.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 503][/us/stat/70A/503]; [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title XII, § 1231(19)(A)][/us/pl/100/180/s1231/19/A], Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1161][/us/stat/101/1161].)
