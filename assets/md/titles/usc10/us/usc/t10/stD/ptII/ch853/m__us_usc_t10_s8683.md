## \[§ 8683. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–145, title XIII][/us/pl/99/145/tXIII], § 1301(d)(1)(A), Nov. 8, 1985, [99 Stat. 736][/us/stat/99/736]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 535][/us/stat/70A/535]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 1(156), [72 Stat. 1513][/us/stat/72/1513]; Aug. 25, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–197][/us/pl/86/197], § 1(7), [73 Stat. 426][/us/stat/73/426], related to service credit for certain service as a nurse, woman medical specialist, or civilian employee of Army Medical Department.

 __Person Performing Active Service on Day Before Repeal of Section__ 

[Pub. L. 99–145, title XIII][/us/pl/99/145/tXIII], § 1301(d)(1)(C), Nov. 8, 1985, [99 Stat. 736][/us/stat/99/736], provided that: 

> “The repeal made by subparagraph (A) \[repealing this section\] shall not apply in the case of a person who performed active service described in [section 8683 of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s8683], United States Code, as such section was in effect on the day before the date of the enactment of this Act \[Nov. 8, 1985\].”
