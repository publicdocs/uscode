#### \[CHAPTER 545—REPEALED\]

\[§§ 5751 to 5758. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 96–513, title III, § 333__  __][/us/pl/96/513/s333]__  __,__  __Dec. 12, 1980__  __,__  __[__  __94 Stat. 2897__  __][/us/stat/94/2897]__  __\]__ 

    Section 5751, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 346][/us/stat/70A/346]; Aug. 3, 1961, [Pub. See [section 619 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s619].

    Section 5753, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 347][/us/stat/70A/347]; Aug. 21, 1957, [Pub. L. 90–130, § 1(19)(D)][/us/pl/90/130/s1/19/D], [81 Stat. 378][/us/stat/81/378]; Dec. 8, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–179, § 12][/us/pl/90/179/s12], [81 Stat. 549][/us/stat/81/549], related to eligibility of Navy staff corps officers for consideration for promotion by a selection board. See [section 619 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s619].

    Section 5754, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 348][/us/stat/70A/348], prescribed general conditions for eligibility for consideration by a selection board for promotion. See [section 619 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s619].

    Section 5755, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 348][/us/stat/70A/348], related to communications between a selection board and an officer eligible for consideration for promotion by such board. See [section 614 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s614].

    Section 5756, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 348][/us/stat/70A/348], directed Secretary of Navy to furnish appropriate selection board with number of male officers in line of Navy or of Marine Corps that could be recommended for promotion to next highest grade and prescribed a formula for arriving at such number. See [section 622 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s622].

    Section 5757, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 348][/us/stat/70A/348], directed Secretary of Navy to furnish appropriate selection board with number of male officers in line of Navy or of Marine Corps designated for limited duty that could be recommended for promotion to next highest grade and prescribed a formula for arriving at such number. See [section 622 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s622].

    Section 5758, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 349][/us/stat/70A/349], directed Secretary of Navy to furnish appropriate selection board with numbers of officers designated for engineering, aeronautical engineering, and special duty that could be recommended for promotion to grade of rear admiral and numbers of male officers designated for such duty that could be recommended for promotion to a grade below rear admiral and prescribed formulas for arriving at such numbers. See [section 622 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s622].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].

 __Selection Boards Convened Between__  __July 10, 1981__  __,__  __and September __  __15, 1981;__  __Service in Grade Requirements; Regulations__ 

    [Pub. L. 97–22, § 9][/us/pl/97/22/s9], July 10, 1981, [95 Stat. 136][/us/stat/95/136], provided that for selection boards convened on or after July 10, 1981, and before Sept. 15, 1981, service in grade requirements shall be established under regulations prescribed by Secretary of the Navy for eligibility for consideration for promotion of female officers in the line of the Navy to grade of lieutenant commander and female officers in the Marine Corps to grade of major.
