## § 7851. Composition

    The Naval Militia consists of the Naval Militia of the States, the District of Columbia, Guam, and the Virgin Islands.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 486][/us/stat/70A/486]; [Pub. L. 109–163, div. A, title X, § 1057(a)(11)][/us/pl/109/163/s1057/a/11], Jan. 6, 2006, [119 Stat. 3441][/us/stat/119/3441].)
