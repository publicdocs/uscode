## \[§ 7208. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–525, title XIV, § 1401(d)(3)(A)][/us/pl/98/525/s1401/d/3/A], Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2616][/us/stat/98/2616]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 443][/us/stat/70A/443], authorized the Secretary of the Navy to pay the travel, subsistence, special compensation, and other expenses of officers and students of Latin American countries that the Secretary considers necessary for Latin American cooperation. See [section 1050 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s1050].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1985, see [section 1404 of Pub. L. 98–525][/us/pl/98/525/s1404], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 520b of this title][/us/usc/t10/s520b].
