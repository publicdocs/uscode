###### \[§§ 5221, 5222. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–82, title VI][/us/pl/95/82/tVI], § 611(a), Aug. 1, 1977, [91 Stat. 378][/us/stat/91/378]\]

    Section 5221, added [Pub. L. 90–110, title X][/us/pl/90/110/tX], § 1001(1), Oct. 21, 1967, [81 Stat. 310][/us/stat/81/310], provided for inclusion of naval districts within organization of Department of the Navy.

    Section 5222, added [Pub. L. 90–110, title X][/us/pl/90/110/tX], § 1001(1), Oct. 21, 1967, [81 Stat. 310][/us/stat/81/310], provided for detailing of officers of the Navy not below the grade of rear admiral as commandants of each of naval districts.
