## \[§ 5412. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title III, § 311(a)][/us/pl/96/513/s311/a], Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2889][/us/stat/94/2889]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 299][/us/stat/70A/299], prescribed authorized strength of Regular Navy in enlisted members in Hospital Corps. See section 521 et seq. of this title.

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].

    Section 5415, added [Pub. See section 521 et seq. of this title.

    Section 5416, added [Pub.
