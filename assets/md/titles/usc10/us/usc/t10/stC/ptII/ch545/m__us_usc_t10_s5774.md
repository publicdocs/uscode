## \[§ 5774. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–130, § 1(19)(T)][/us/pl/90/130/s1/19/T], Nov. 8, 1967, [81 Stat. 379][/us/stat/81/379]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 359][/us/stat/70A/359], made women officers on active list of Navy in staff corps, appointed under [section 5590 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s5590], who were recommended for promotion to a grade above lieutenant (junior grade) in approved report of a selection board convened under chapter 543 of this title eligible for promotion when line officer who was to be her running mate in higher grade became eligible for promotion to that grade.
