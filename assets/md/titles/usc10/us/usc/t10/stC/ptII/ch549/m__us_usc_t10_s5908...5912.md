## \[§§ 5908 to 5912. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI], § 1629(b)(2), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2963][/us/stat/108/2963]\]

    Section 5908, added [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 1(133), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1506][/us/stat/72/1506]; amended [Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649], § 14c(34), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 501][/us/stat/76/501], related to eligibility of ensigns in Naval Reserve and second lieutenants in Marine Corps Reserve for promotion. See section 14001 et seq. of this title.

    Section 5909, added [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 1(133), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1506][/us/stat/72/1506], provided that sea or foreign service not be required for promotion of reserve officers under this chapter.

    Section 5910, added [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 1(133), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1506][/us/stat/72/1506]; amended [Pub. L. 96–513, title V][/us/pl/96/513/tV], § 503(40), Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2914][/us/stat/94/2914], provided that officers in Naval Reserve and Marine Corps Reserve could be promoted under regulations prescribed by Secretary of the Navy. See section 14301 et seq. of this title.

    Section 5911, added [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 1(133), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1507][/us/stat/72/1507]; amended [Pub. L. 86–559][/us/pl/86/559], § 1(45), June 30, 1960, [74 Stat. 274][/us/stat/74/274], related to promotions of reserve officers by temporary and permanent appointments. See section 14301 et seq. of this title.

    Section 5912, added [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 1(133), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1507][/us/stat/72/1507]; amended [Pub. L. 92–129, title VI][/us/pl/92/129/tVI], § 603(d), Sept. 28, 1971, [85 Stat. 362][/us/stat/85/362], related to President’s power to make appointments under this chapter of officers in Naval Reserve and Marine Corps Reserve. See section 14301 et seq. of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1996, see [section 1691(b)(1) of Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337/s1691/b/1], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 10001 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s10001].
