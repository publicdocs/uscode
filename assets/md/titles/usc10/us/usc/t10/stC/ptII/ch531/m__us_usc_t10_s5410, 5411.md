## \[§§ 5410, 5411. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–130, § 1(16)][/us/pl/90/130/s1/16], Nov. 8, 1967, [81 Stat. 376][/us/stat/81/376]\]

    Section 5410, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 928][/us/stat/70A/928], placed upper limits, stated in terms of percentages of the authorized strength of the Regular Navy and Regular Marine Corps in enlisted members, on the authorized strength of enlisted women in each.

    Section 5411, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 299][/us/stat/70A/299], placed upper limits, stated in terms of percentages of the authorized strength in enlisted women of the Regular Navy and Regular Marine Corps, on the authorized strength of the Regular Navy and Regular Marine Corps in women officers.
