## \[§ 5452. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–513, title III, § 373(b)][/us/pl/96/513/s373/b], Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2903][/us/stat/94/2903]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 312][/us/stat/70A/312]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(111)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/111], [72 Stat. 1491][/us/stat/72/1491]; Nov. 8, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–130, § 1(17)(E)][/us/pl/90/130/s1/17/E], [81 Stat. 376][/us/stat/81/376], authorized Secretary of the Navy to prescribe number of women officers in line of Navy eligible to hold appointments in each grade above lieutenant (junior grade) and a similar number in Marine Corps eligible to hold appointments in each grade above first lieutenant.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
