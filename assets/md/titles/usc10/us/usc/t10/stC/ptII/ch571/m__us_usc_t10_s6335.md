## § 6335. Restoration to former grade: warrant officers and enlisted members

    Each retired warrant officer or enlisted member of the naval service who has been advanced on the retired list to a higher commissioned grade under [section 6334 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s6334], and who applies to the Secretary of the Navy within three months after his advancement, shall, if the Secretary approves, be restored on the retired list to his former warrant officer or enlisted status, as the case may be.

(Added [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title V, § 512(b)][/us/pl/100/180/s512/b], Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1090][/us/stat/101/1090].)
