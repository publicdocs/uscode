## \[§ 6224. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649], § 14c(46), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 501][/us/stat/76/501]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 388][/us/stat/70A/388], provided that members of the United States Navy Band and the United States Marine Corps Band shall lose no allowances while on concert tours approved by the President. See [section 425 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s425], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Nov. 1, 1962, see [section 15 of Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649/s15], set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 101 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s101], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.
