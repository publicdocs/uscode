## § 7299. Contracts: applicability of chapter 65 of title 41

    Each contract for the construction, alteration, furnishing, or equipping of a naval vessel is subject to chapter 65 of title 41 unless the President determines that this requirement is not in the interest of national defense.

(Added [Pub. L. 104–106, div. A, title VIII, § 815(a)][/us/pl/104/106/s815/a], Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 396][/us/stat/110/396]; amended [Pub. L. 111–350, § 5(b)(52)][/us/pl/111/350/s5/b/52], Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3846][/us/stat/124/3846].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 7299, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 449][/us/stat/70A/449]; Aug. 25, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–747][/us/pl/85/747], [72 Stat. 839][/us/stat/72/839]; Dec. 12, 1980, [Pub. L. 96–513, title V, § 513(26)][/us/pl/96/513/s513/26], [94 Stat. 2932][/us/stat/94/2932]; Oct. 12, 1982, [Pub. L. 97–295, § 1(47)][/us/pl/97/295/s1/47], [96 Stat. 1298][/us/stat/96/1298], directed that each contract for construction, alteration, furnishing, or equipping of naval vessel was subject to the Walsh-Healey Act, unless President determined that such requirement was not in interest of national defense, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–355, title III, § 3023(a)][/us/pl/103/355/s3023/a], Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3333][/us/stat/108/3333].

 __Amendments__ 

    2011—[Pub. L. 111–350][/us/pl/111/350] substituted “chapter 65 of title 41” for “Walsh-Healey Act” in section catchline and for “the Walsh-Healey Act ([41 U.S.C. 35][/us/usc/t41/s35] et seq.)” in text.
