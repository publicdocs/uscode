## \[§ 5136. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–174, § 2(3)][/us/pl/86/174/s2/3], Aug. 18, 1959, [73 Stat. 396][/us/stat/73/396]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 286][/us/stat/70A/286], related to appointment, qualifications and term of Chief of Bureau of Aeronautics, and authorized detail of an officer as Deputy Chief of Bureau.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective July 1, 1960, or any earlier date on which the Secretary of the Navy makes formal finding that all the functions of the Bureau of Aeronautics and the Bureau of Ordnance have been transferred to the Bureau of Naval Weapons or elsewhere, see note set out under [section 5131 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s5131].
