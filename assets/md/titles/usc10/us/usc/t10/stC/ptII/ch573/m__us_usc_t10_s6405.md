## \[§ 6405. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–235, § 4(a)(12)][/us/pl/90/235/s4/a/12], Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 760][/us/stat/81/760]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 415][/us/stat/70A/415], provided that an officer of Regular Navy, other than a retired officer, who accepted an appointment in the Foreign Service was considered as having resigned from the Navy.
