## \[§ 6030. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–130][/us/pl/90/130], § 1(22), Nov. 8, 1967, [81 Stat. 380][/us/stat/81/380]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1040][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1040], [70A Stat. 378][/us/stat/70A/378], gave officers in the Nurse Corps authority in medical and sanitary matters and other work within the line of their professional duties in activities of the Medical Department after officers in the Medical Corps, Dental Corps, and Medical Service Corps and authorized officers in the Nurse Corps to exercise such military authority, other than command, as the Secretary of the Navy prescribed.
