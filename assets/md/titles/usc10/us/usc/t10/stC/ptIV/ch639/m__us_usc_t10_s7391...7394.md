## \[§§ 7391 to 7394. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–295][/us/pl/97/295], § 1(50)(A), Oct. 12, 1982, [96 Stat. 1299][/us/stat/96/1299]\]

    Section 7391, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 456][/us/stat/70A/456]; July 10, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–533][/us/pl/87/533], § 1(a)(2), [76 Stat. 154][/us/stat/76/154], provided for a United States Naval Oceanographic Office attached to the Office of the Chief of Naval Operations which would provide navigational aids, charts, books, and manuals, and was reenacted as former [section 2791 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s2791].

    Section 7392, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 456][/us/stat/70A/456]; July 10, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–533][/us/pl/87/533], § 1(a)(3), [76 Stat. 154][/us/stat/76/154], provided that the Secretary of the Navy may have the United States Naval Oceanographic Office prepare navigational aids, charts, and books, and that he may publish and distribute such materials and buy copyrights of existing navigational aids, charts, and books, and was reenacted as [section 451 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s451].

    Section 7393, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 456][/us/stat/70A/456]; July 10, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–533][/us/pl/87/533], § 1(a)(4), [76 Stat. 154][/us/stat/76/154], directed that certain identifying information be printed on United States Naval Oceanographic pilot charts, and that the Department of Commerce’s Weather Bureau provide the Naval Oceanographic Office with data necessary for their preparation, and was reenacted as [section 452 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s452].

    Section 7394, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 457][/us/stat/70A/457]; July 10, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–533][/us/pl/87/533], § 1(a)(5), [76 Stat. 154][/us/stat/76/154]; Aug. 14, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–436][/us/pl/88/436], § 1(a), [78 Stat. 443][/us/stat/78/443], directed that publications by the Naval Oceanographic Office be sold under regulations of the Secretary of the Navy, and that proceeds be covered into the Treasury, and was reenacted as [section 453 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s453].
