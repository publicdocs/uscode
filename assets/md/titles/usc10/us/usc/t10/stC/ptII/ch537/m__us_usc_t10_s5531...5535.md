## \[§§ 5531 to 5535. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–235][/us/pl/90/235], § 2(a)(3), (b), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 756][/us/stat/81/756]\]

    Section 5531, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 318][/us/stat/70A/318], provided for recruiting campaigns to obtain enlistments in the Regular Navy and the Regular Marine Corps.

    Section 5532, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 318][/us/stat/70A/318], set forth classes of persons prohibited from enlisting in the naval service.

    Section 5533, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 318][/us/stat/70A/318], provided for enlistment of minors in naval service.

    Section 5534, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 318][/us/stat/70A/318], set forth term of enlistments in Regular Navy or Regular Marine Corps and provided that Secretary of Navy could prescribe grades or ratings in which such enlistments could be made.

    Section 5535, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 319][/us/stat/70A/319], required evidence of age for enlistment of minors in Regular Navy as seamen, seamen apprentices or seamen recruits.
