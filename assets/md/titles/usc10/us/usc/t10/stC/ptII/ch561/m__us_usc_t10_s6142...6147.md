## \[§§ 6142 to 6147. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649], § 14c(38–43), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 501][/us/stat/76/501]\]

    Section 6142, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 382][/us/stat/70A/382], provided for assignments of pay due to enlisted members. See [section 705 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s705].

    Section 6143, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 383][/us/stat/70A/383], related to discouragement of sale of pay. See [section 805 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s805].

    Section 6144, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 383][/us/stat/70A/383], provided for settlement of pay accounts when lost with vessel. See [section 902 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s902].

    Section 6145, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 383][/us/stat/70A/383], related to fixing date of loss of a vessel for purpose of settling accounts of persons aboard other than officers. See [section 902 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s902].

    Section 6146, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 383][/us/stat/70A/383], provided for allotments by officers. See [section 702 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s702].

    Section 6147, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 383][/us/stat/70A/383], related to allowances for prisoners. See [section 426 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s426].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Nov. 1, 1962, see [section 15 of Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649/s15], set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 101 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s101], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.
