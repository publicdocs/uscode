## \[§ 5907. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–649, § 14c(33)][/us/pl/87/649/s14c/33], Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 501][/us/stat/76/501]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(133)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/133], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1506][/us/stat/72/1506]; [Pub. L. 86–559, § 1(44)][/us/pl/86/559/s1/44], June 30, 1960, [74 Stat. 274][/us/stat/74/274], related to pay and allowances of reserve officers promoted to a grade above lieutenant (junior grade) in the Naval Reserve or above first lieutenant in the Marine Corps Reserve, and is covered by [section 905 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s905], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Section repealed effective Nov. 1, 1962, see [section 15 of Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649/s15], set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 101 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s101], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.
