#### \[CHAPTER 547—REPEALED\]

\[§§ 5861, 5862. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 96–513, title III, § 333__  __][/us/pl/96/513/s333]__  __,__  __Dec. 12, 1980__  __,__  __[__  __94 Stat. 2897__  __][/us/stat/94/2897]__  __\]__ 

    Section 5861, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 368][/us/stat/70A/368]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(129)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/129], [72 Stat. 1497][/us/stat/72/1497], required an officer of Regular Navy or of Regular Marine Corps to pass a physical examination as prescribed by Secretary of Navy in order to qualify for promotion to a grade above ensign in Navy or second lieutenant in Marine Corps. See [section 624 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s624].

    Section 5862, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 369][/us/stat/70A/369]; Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–861, § 1(131)][/us/pl/85/861/s1/131], [72 Stat. 1498][/us/stat/72/1498], related to mental, moral, and professional qualifications required to be demonstrated by officers on active list of Navy or Marine Corps in order to be promoted to grades of lieutenant (junior grade) or above in Navy or first lieutenant or above in Marine Corps. See [section 624 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s624].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 15, 1981, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 96–513][/us/pl/96/513/s701], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t10/s101].
