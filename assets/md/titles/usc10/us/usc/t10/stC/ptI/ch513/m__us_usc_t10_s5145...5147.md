## \[§§ 5145 to 5147. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–718][/us/pl/89/718], § 35(5), Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1120][/us/stat/80/1120]\]

    Section 5145, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 289][/us/stat/70A/289]; May 13, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–454][/us/pl/86/454], [74 Stat. 103][/us/stat/74/103]; Sept. 7, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649], § 14(c)(19), [76 Stat. 501][/us/stat/76/501], provided for appointment of Chief of Bureau of Ships, detailing and rank of Deputy Chief, and detailing of heads of major divisions of Bureau of Ships.

    Section 5146, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 289][/us/stat/70A/289], provided for appointment of Chief of Bureau of Supplies and Accounts and detailing of Deputy Chief.

    Section 5147, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 289][/us/stat/70A/289], provided for appointment of Chief of Bureau of Yards and Docks and detailing of Deputy Chief.
