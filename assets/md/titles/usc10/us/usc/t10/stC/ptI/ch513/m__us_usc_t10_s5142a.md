## § 5142a. Deputy Chief of Chaplains

    The Secretary of the Navy may detail as the Deputy Chief of Chaplains an officer of the Chaplain Corps in the grade of commander or above who is on active duty and who has served on active duty in the Chaplain Corps for at least eight years.

(Added [Pub. L. 96–343][/us/pl/96/343], § 11(a), Sept. 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 1130][/us/stat/94/1130]; amended [Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title V][/us/pl/105/85/dA/tV], § 504(c)(2), Nov. 18, 1997, [111 Stat. 1725][/us/stat/111/1725].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1997—[Pub. L. 105–85][/us/pl/105/85] struck out “, who is not on the retired list,” after “who is on active duty”.
