## § 30115. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to the Commission for the purpose of carrying out its functions under this Act, and under chapters 95 and 96 of title 26, not to exceed $5,000,000 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1975. There are authorized to be appropriated to the Commission $6,000,000 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1976, $1,500,000 for the period beginning July 1, 1976, and ending September 30, 1976, $6,000,000 for the fiscal year ending September 30, 1977, $7,811,500 for the fiscal year ending September 30, 1978, and $9,400,000 (of which not more than $400,000 are authorized to be appropriated for the national clearinghouse function described in section 30111(a)(10)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title) for the fiscal year ending September 30, 1981.

 __References in Text__ 

    This Act, referred to in text, means the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971, as defined by [section 30101 of this title][/us/usc/t52/s30101].

    [Section 30111(a)(10) of this title][/us/usc/t52/s30111/a/10], referred to in text, was repealed by [Pub. L. 107–252, title VIII, § 801(b)(3)][/us/pl/107/252/s801/b/3], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1726][/us/stat/116/1726].
