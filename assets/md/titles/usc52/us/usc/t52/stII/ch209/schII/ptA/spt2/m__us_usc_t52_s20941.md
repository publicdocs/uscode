## § 20941. Establishment

    There are hereby established the Election Assistance Commission Standards Board (hereafter in this subchapter referred to as the “Standards Board”) and the Election Assistance Commission Board of Advisors (hereafter in this subchapter referred to as the “Board of Advisors”).

([Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 211][/us/pl/107/252/s211], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1678][/us/stat/116/1678].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 15341 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s15341], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
