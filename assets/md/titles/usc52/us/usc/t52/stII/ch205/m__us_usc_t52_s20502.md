## § 20502. Definitions

    As used in this chapter—

    (1) the term “election” has the meaning stated in [section 30101(1) of this title][/us/usc/t52/s30101/1];

    (2) the term “Federal office” has the meaning stated in [section 30101(3) of this title][/us/usc/t52/s30101/3];

    (3) the term “motor vehicle driver’s license” includes any personal identification document issued by a State motor vehicle authority;

    (4) the term “State” means a State of the United States and the District of Columbia; and

    (5) the term “voter registration agency” means an office designated under [section 20506(a)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t52/s20506/a/1] to perform voter registration activities.

([Pub. L. 103–31, § 3][/us/pl/103/31/s3], May 20, 1993, [107 Stat. 77][/us/stat/107/77].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 1973gg–1 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s1973gg–1], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering in Title 52, Voting and Elections.
