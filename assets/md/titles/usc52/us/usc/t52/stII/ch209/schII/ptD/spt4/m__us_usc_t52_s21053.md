## § 21053. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __In general__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated for grants under this subpart $10,000,000 for fiscal year 2003.

    (b) __Availability of funds__ 

        Amounts appropriated pursuant to the authorization under this section shall remain available, without fiscal year limitation, until expended.

([Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 283][/us/pl/107/252/s283], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1702][/us/stat/116/1702].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 15453 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s15453], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering in Title 52, Voting and Elections.
