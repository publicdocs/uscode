## § 21123. Authorization of appropriations

    In addition to any funds authorized to be appropriated to the Commission under [section 20930 of this title][/us/usc/t52/s20930], there are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter—

    (1) $5,000,000 for fiscal year 2003; and

    (2) such sums as may be necessary for each succeeding fiscal year.

([Pub. L. 107–252, title V, § 503][/us/pl/107/252/s503], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1717][/us/stat/116/1717].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 15523 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s15523], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering in Title 52, Voting and Elections.
