## § 21007. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __In general__ 

    In addition to amounts transferred under [section 20904(c) of this title][/us/usc/t52/s20904/c], there are authorized to be appropriated for requirements payments under this subpart the following amounts:

        (1) For fiscal year 2003, $1,400,000,000.

        (2) For fiscal year 2004, $1,000,000,000.

        (3) For fiscal year 2005, $600,000,000.

        (4) For fiscal year 2010 and subsequent fiscal years, such sums as are necessary for purposes of making requirements payments to States to carry out the activities described in [section 21001(b)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t52/s21001/b/3].

    (b) __Availability__ 

        Any amounts appropriated pursuant to the authority of subsection (a) shall remain available without fiscal year limitation until expended.

([Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 257][/us/pl/107/252/s257], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1697][/us/stat/116/1697]; [Pub. L. 111–84, div. A, title V, § 588(c)][/us/pl/111/84/s588/c], Oct. 28, 2009, [123 Stat. 2334][/us/stat/123/2334].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 15407 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s15407], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

 __Amendments__ 

    2009—Subsec. (a)(4). [Pub. L. 111–84][/us/pl/111/84] added par. (4).
