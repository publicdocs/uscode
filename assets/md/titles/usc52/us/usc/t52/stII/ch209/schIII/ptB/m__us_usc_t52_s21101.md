## § 21101. Adoption of voluntary guidance by Commission

    (a) __In general__ 

        To assist States in meeting the requirements of part A of this subchapter, the Commission shall adopt voluntary guidance consistent with such requirements in accordance with the procedures described in [section 21102 of this title][/us/usc/t52/s21102].

    (b) __Deadlines__ 

    The Commission shall adopt the recommendations under this section not later than—

        (1) in the case of the recommendations with respect to [section 21081 of this title][/us/usc/t52/s21081], January 1, 2004;

        (2) in the case of the recommendations with respect to [section 21082 of this title][/us/usc/t52/s21082], October 1, 2003; and

        (3) in the case of the recommendations with respect to [section 21083 of this title][/us/usc/t52/s21083], October 1, 2003.

    (c) __Quadrennial update__ 

        The Commission shall review and update recommendations adopted with respect to [section 21081 of this title][/us/usc/t52/s21081] no less frequently than once every 4 years.

([Pub. L. 107–252, title III, § 311][/us/pl/107/252/s311], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1715][/us/stat/116/1715].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 15501 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s15501], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering in Title 52, Voting and Elections.
