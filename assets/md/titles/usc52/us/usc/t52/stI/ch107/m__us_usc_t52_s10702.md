## § 10702. “State” defined

    As used in this chapter, the term “State” includes the District of Columbia.

([Pub. L. 89–110, title III, § 302][/us/pl/89/110/s302], as added [Pub. L. 91–285, § 6][/us/pl/91/285/s6], June 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 318][/us/stat/84/318]; amended [Pub. L. 94–73, title IV, § 407][/us/pl/94/73/s407], Aug. 6, 1975, [89 Stat. 405][/us/stat/89/405].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 1973bb–1 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s1973bb–1], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering in Title 52, Voting and Elections. Any notes below for amendments to this section prior to 2013 reflect the former classification of this section.

 __Amendments__ 

    1975—[Pub. L. 94–73][/us/pl/94/73] substituted definition of State for provisions prohibiting denial of right to vote because of age.
