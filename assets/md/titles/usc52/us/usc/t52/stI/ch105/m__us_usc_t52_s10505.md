## § 10505. Penalty

    Whoever shall deprive or attempt to deprive any person of any right secured by section 10501, 10502, or 10503 of this title shall be fined not more than $5,000, or imprisoned not more than five years, or both.

([Pub. L. 89–110, title II, § 205][/us/pl/89/110/s205], formerly § 204, as added [Pub. L. 91–285, § 6][/us/pl/91/285/s6], June 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 317][/us/stat/84/317]; renumbered § 205 and amended [Pub. L. 94–73, title III][/us/pl/94/73], §§ 302, 304, Aug. 6, 1975, [89 Stat. 403][/us/stat/89/403].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 1973aa–3 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s1973aa–3], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering in Title 52, Voting and Elections. Any notes below for amendments to this section prior to 2013 reflect the former classification of this section.

 __Amendments__ 

    1975—[Pub. L. 94–73][/us/pl/94/73] inserted reference to [section 1973aa–1a of this title][/us/usc/t52/s1973aa–1a].
