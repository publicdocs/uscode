## § 110. Regulations

    The President shall prescribe regulations, and issue orders, necessary to organize, discipline, and govern the National Guard.

([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], [70A Stat. 600][/us/stat/70A/600].)
