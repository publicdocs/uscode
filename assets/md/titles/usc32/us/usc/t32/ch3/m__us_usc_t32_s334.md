## \[§ 334. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–124][/us/pl/97/124], § 3, Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1666][/us/stat/95/1666]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 94–464][/us/pl/94/464], § 2(b), Oct. 8, 1976, [90 Stat. 1986][/us/stat/90/1986]; amended [Pub. L. 96–513, title V][/us/pl/96/513/tV], § 515(3), Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2937][/us/stat/94/2937], provided for the payment of malpractice liability of National Guard Medical personnel. See sections 1089(a) of Title 10, Armed Forces, and 2671 of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

 __Amendment After Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], § 3(h)(1), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1065][/us/stat/96/1065], purported to substitute “[section 1304 of title 31][/us/usc/t31/s1304]” for “[section 1302 of the Act of July 27, 1956][/us/act/1956-07-27/s1302], ([31 U.S.C. 724a][/us/usc/t31/s724a])” in subsec. (a) of [section 334 of this title][/us/usc/t32/s334], without reference to the earlier repeal of that section by [Pub. L. 97–124][/us/pl/97/124], § 3, Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1666][/us/stat/95/1666].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective only with respect to claims arising on or after Dec. 29, 1981, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 97–124][/us/pl/97/124/s4], set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under [section 1089 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s1089], Armed Forces.
