## § 6108. Review

    Upon the expiration of 5 years following the date of the first promulgation of rules under [section 6102 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s6102], the Commission shall review the implementation of this chapter and its effect on deceptive telemarketing acts or practices and report the results of the review to the Congress.

([Pub. L. 103–297, § 10][/us/pl/103/297/s10], Aug. 16, 1994, [108 Stat. 1551][/us/stat/108/1551].)
