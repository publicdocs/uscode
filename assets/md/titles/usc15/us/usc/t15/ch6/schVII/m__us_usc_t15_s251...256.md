## §§ 251 to 256. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–628][/us/pl/90/628], § 1(a), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1320][/us/stat/82/1320]

    Section 251, acts [Aug. 31, 1916, ch. 426][/us/act/1916-08-31/ch426], § 1, [39 Stat. 673][/us/stat/39/673]; [June 11, 1934, ch. 447][/us/act/1934-06-11/ch447], § 1, [48 Stat. 930][/us/stat/48/930], set standards for Climax baskets for grapes and other fruits and vegetables and for mushrooms.

    Section 252, [act Aug. 31, 1916, ch. 426][/us/act/1916-08-31/ch426], § 2, [39 Stat. 673][/us/stat/39/673], set standards for standard basket or container for small fruits and vegetables.

    Section 253, acts [Aug. 31, 1916, ch. 426][/us/act/1916-08-31/ch426], § 3, [39 Stat. 674][/us/stat/39/674]; June 11, 1934, c. 447, § 2, [48 Stat. 930][/us/stat/48/930], set penalties for failure to conform to standards.

    Section 254, [act Aug. 31, 1916, ch. 426][/us/act/1916-08-31/ch426], § 4, [39 Stat. 674][/us/stat/39/674], provided for examinations and tests by Department of Agriculture and for promulgation of rules and regulations covering allowable tolerances and variations.

    Section 255, acts [Aug. 31, 1916, ch. 426][/us/act/1916-08-31/ch426], § 5, [39 Stat. 674][/us/stat/39/674]; [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 1, [62 Stat. 909][/us/stat/62/909], made United States attorney responsible for commencing actions to enforce penalties.

    Section 256, [act Aug. 31, 1916, ch. 426][/us/act/1916-08-31/ch426], § 6, [39 Stat. 674][/us/stat/39/674], covered guaranty given by manufacturers or sellers of baskets as to correctness of such containers.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

[Pub. L. 90–628][/us/pl/90/628], § 3, Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1320][/us/stat/82/1320], provided that: 

> “This Act \[repealing sections 251 to 257i of this title and amending [section 1459 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s1459]\] shall become effective 60 days after enactment \[Oct. 22, 1968\].”
