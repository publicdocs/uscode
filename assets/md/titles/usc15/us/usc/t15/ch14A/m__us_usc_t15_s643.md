## § 643. Fair charge for use of Government-owned property

    To the fullest extent the Administration deems practicable, it shall make a fair charge for the use of Government-owned property and make and let contracts on a basis that will result in a recovery of the direct costs incurred by the Administration.

([Pub. L. 85–536, § 2][/us/pl/85/536/s2]\[14\], July 18, 1958, [72 Stat. 395][/us/stat/72/395].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Prior similar provisions were contained in section 220 of [act July 30, 1953, ch. 282][/us/act/1953-07-30/ch282], title II, [67 Stat. 240][/us/stat/67/240], which was previously classified to [section 649 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s649]. The provisions of section 214 of act July 30, 1953, formerly classified to this section, were transferred to section 2\[15\] of [Pub. L. 85–536][/us/pl/85/536], and are classified to [section 644 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s644]. See Codification note set out under [section 631 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s631].
