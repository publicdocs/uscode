## § 657c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/112/239/dA/tXVI], § 1699(a), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2092][/us/stat/126/2092]

    Section, [Pub. L. 85–536][/us/pl/85/536], § 2\[33\], as added [Pub. L. 106–50, title II][/us/pl/106/50/tII], § 202(a), Aug. 17, 1999, [113 Stat. 236][/us/stat/113/236]; amended [Pub. L. 106–554][/us/pl/106/554], § 1(a)(9) \[title VIII, § 808\], Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2763][/us/stat/114/2763], 2763A–706; [Pub. L. 108–447, div. B, title VI][/us/pl/108/447/dB/tVI], § 636, div. K, title I, §§ 143(a), 146, Dec. 8, 2004, [118 Stat. 2922][/us/stat/118/2922], 3455; [Pub. L. 110–186, title I][/us/pl/110/186/tI], § 103(a), Feb. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 625][/us/stat/122/625], established the National Veterans Business Development Corporation.

 __Representation of Authorization__ 

[Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title XVI][/us/pl/112/239/dA/tXVI], § 1699(b), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2092][/us/stat/126/2092], provided that: 

> “On and after the date of enactment of this Act \[Jan. 2, 2013\], the National Veterans Business Development Corporation and any successor thereto may not represent that the corporation is federally chartered or in any other manner authorized by the Federal Government.”
