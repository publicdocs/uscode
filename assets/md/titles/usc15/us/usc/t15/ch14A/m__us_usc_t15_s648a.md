## § 648a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–140, title VI][/us/pl/102/140/tVI], § 609(e), Oct. 28, 1991, [105 Stat. 826][/us/stat/105/826]

    Section, [Pub. L. 85–536][/us/pl/85/536], § 2\[21A\], as added [Pub. L. 101–515, title V][/us/pl/101/515/tV], § 9(a), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 2144][/us/stat/104/2144], related to Small Business Development Center Technical Assistance Program.

 __Effective Date of Repeal; Termination of Funding__ 

[Pub. L. 102–140, title VI][/us/pl/102/140/tVI], § 609(e), Oct. 28, 1991, [105 Stat. 826][/us/stat/105/826], provided that: 

> “Notwithstanding any other law, no funds shall be appropriated to carry out section 21A of the Small Business Act \[[15 U.S.C. 648a][/us/usc/t15/s648a]\] after September 30, 1991, and such section is repealed October 1, 1992.”
