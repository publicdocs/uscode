## CHAPTER 31—DESTRUCTION OF PROPERTY MOVING IN COMMERCE

§§ 1281, 1282. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)__  __][/us/pl/103/272/s7/b]__  __,__  __July 5, 1994__  __,__  __[__  __108 Stat. 1379__  __][/us/stat/108/1379]__ 

    Section 1281, [Pub. L. 87–221, § 1][/us/pl/87/221/s1], Sept. 13, 1961, [75 Stat. 494][/us/stat/75/494], related to willful destruction or injury, or attempted destruction or injury, of property moving in interstate or foreign commerce in possession of common or contract carriers, penalties for such acts, and proof of interstate or foreign nature of property. See [section 80501 of Title 49][/us/usc/t49/s80501], Transportation.

    Section 1282, [Pub. L. 87–221, § 2][/us/pl/87/221/s2], Sept. 13, 1961, [75 Stat. 494][/us/stat/75/494], provided that judgment of conviction or acquittal on merits under laws of any State or possession, District of Columbia, or Puerto Rico, was bar to prosecution under this chapter for same acts. See [section 80501 of Title 49][/us/usc/t49/s80501].
