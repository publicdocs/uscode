## § 3904. Securities laws

    (a) __Ownership interest of members in risk retention groups__ 

    The ownership interests of members in a risk retention group shall be—

        (1) considered to be exempted securities for purposes of section 5 of the Securities Act of 1933 \[[15 U.S.C. 77e][/us/usc/t15/s77e]\] and for purposes of section 12 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 \[[15 U.S.C. 78][/us/usc/t15/s78]l\]; and

        (2) considered to be securities for purposes of the provisions of section 17 of the Securities Act of 1933 \[[15 U.S.C. 77q][/us/usc/t15/s77q]\] and the provisions of section 10 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 \[[15 U.S.C. 78j][/us/usc/t15/s78j]\].

    (b) __Investment companies__ 

        A risk retention group shall not be considered to be an investment company for purposes of the Investment Company Act of 1940 ([15 U.S.C. 80a–1][/us/usc/t15/s80a–1] et seq.).

    (c) __State blue sky laws__ 

        The ownership interests of members in a risk retention group shall not be considered securities for purposes of any State blue sky law.

([Pub. L. 97–45][/us/pl/97/45], § 5, Sept. 25, 1981, [95 Stat. 952][/us/stat/95/952].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Investment Company Act of 1940, referred to in subsec. (b), is title I of [act Aug. 22, 1940, ch. 686][/us/act/1940-08-22/ch686], [54 Stat. 789][/us/stat/54/789], as amended, which is classified generally to subchapter I (§ 80a–1 et seq.) of chapter 2D of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 80a–51 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s80a–51] and Tables.
