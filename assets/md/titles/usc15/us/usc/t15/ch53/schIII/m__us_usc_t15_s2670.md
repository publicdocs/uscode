## § 2670. Regulations

    The Administrator is authorized to issue such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 94–469, title III, § 310][/us/pl/94/469/s310], as added [Pub. L. 100–551, § 1(a)][/us/pl/100/551/s1/a], Oct. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 2764][/us/stat/102/2764].)
