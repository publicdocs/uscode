## § 6205. Exception to certain disclosure restrictions

    [Section 1313 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s1313], and sections 46(f) and 57b–2 of this title, shall not apply to prevent the Attorney General or the Commission from providing to a foreign antitrust authority antitrust evidence in accordance with an antitrust mutual assistance agreement in effect under this chapter and in accordance with the other requirements of this chapter.

([Pub. L. 103–438][/us/pl/103/438], § 6, Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4600][/us/stat/108/4600].)
