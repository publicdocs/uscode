## § 714l. Interest of Members of Congress

    The provisions of [section 6306(a) of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s6306/a] shall apply to all contracts or agreements of the Corporation, except contracts or agreements of a kind which the Corporation may enter into with farmers participating in a program of the Corporation.

([June 29, 1948, ch. 704, § 14][/us/act/1948-06-29/ch704/s14], [62 Stat. 1074][/us/stat/62/1074].)

 __Codification__ 

    In text, “[section 6306(a) of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s6306/a]” substituted for “[section 1 of the Act of February 27, 1877][/us/act/1877-02-27/s1], as amended (41 U.S.C., 1940 edition, 22)” on authority of [Pub. L. 111–350, § 6(c)][/us/pl/111/350/s6/c], Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3854][/us/stat/124/3854], which Act enacted Title 41, Public Contracts.

 __Exceptions From Transfer of Functions__ 

    For exception of functions of corporations of Department of Agriculture from transfer of functions to Secretary of Agriculture by Reorg. Plan No. 2 of 1953, see Exceptions From Transfer of Functions note set out under [section 712a of this title][/us/usc/t15/s712a].
