## § 58. Short title

    This subchapter may be cited as the “Federal Trade Commission Act”.

([Sept. 26, 1914, ch. 311, § 28][/us/act/1914-09-26/ch311/s28], formerly § 18, as added [Mar. 21, 1938, ch. 49, § 4][/us/act/1938-03-21/ch49/s4], [52 Stat. 114][/us/stat/52/114]; renumbered § 21, [Pub.  __Amendment of Section__ 

    For repeal of amendment renumbering this section by [section 13 of Pub. L. 109–455][/us/pl/109/455/s13], see Termination Date of 2006 Amendment note below.

 __Termination Date of 2006 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 109–455][/us/pl/109/455] repealed effective Sept. 30, 2020, and provisions amended by [Pub. L. 109–455][/us/pl/109/455] to be amended to read as if [Pub. L. 112–203][/us/pl/112/203/s1], set out as a note under [section 44 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s44].

 __Short Title of 2006 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 109–455, § 1][/us/pl/109/455/s1], Dec. 22, 2006, [120 Stat. 3372][/us/stat/120/3372], provided that: 

> “This Act \[enacting sections 57b–2a, 57b–2b, 57c–1, and 57c–2 of this title, amending this section, sections 44, 45, 46, 56, and 57b–2 of this title, and [section 3412 of Title 12][/us/usc/t12/s3412], Banks and Banking, and enacting provisions set out as notes under [section 44 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s44]\] may be cited as the ‘Undertaking Spam, Spyware, And Fraud Enforcement With Enforcers beyond Borders Act of 2006’ or the ‘U.S. SAFE WEB Act of 2006’.”

    \[[Section 1 of Pub. L. 109–455][/us/pl/109/455/s1], set out above, repealed effective Sept. 30, 2020, see [section 13 of Pub. L. 109–455][/us/pl/109/455/s13], as amended by [section 1 of Pub. L. 112–203][/us/pl/112/203/s1], set out as a Termination Date of 2006 Amendment note under [section 44 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s44].\]

 __Short Title of 1996 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 104–216, § 1][/us/pl/104/216/s1], Oct. 1, 1996, [110 Stat. 3019][/us/stat/110/3019], provided that: 

> “This Act \[amending [section 57c of this title][/us/usc/t15/s57c]\] may be cited as the ‘Federal Trade Commission Reauthorization Act of 1996’.”

 __Short Title of 1994 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 103–312, § 1(a)][/us/pl/103/312/s1/a], Aug. 26, 1994, [108 Stat. 1691][/us/stat/108/1691], provided that: 

> “This Act \[enacting [section 57b–5 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s57b–5], amending this section and sections 45, 53, 57a, 57b–1, 57b–2, and 57c of this title, and enacting provisions set out as notes under sections 45 and 57c of this title\] may be cited as the ‘Federal Trade Commission Act Amendments of 1994’.”

 __Short Title of 1980 Amendment__ 

    [Pub. L. 96–252, § 1][/us/pl/96/252/s1], May 28, 1980, [94 Stat. 374][/us/stat/94/374], provided that “This Act \[enacting sections 57a–1 and 57b–1 to 57b–4 of this title, amending this section and sections 45, 46, 50, 57a, and 57c of this title, and enacting provisions set out as notes under sections 45, 46, 57a, 57a–1, and 57c of this title\] may be cited as the ‘Federal Trade Commission Improvements Act of 1980’.”
