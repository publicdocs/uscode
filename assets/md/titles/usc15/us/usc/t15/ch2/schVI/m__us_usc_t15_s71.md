## § 71. “Person” defined

    When used in this subchapter the term “person” includes partnerships, corporations, and associations.

([Sept. 8, 1916, ch. 463][/us/act/1916-09-08/ch463], title VIII, § 800, [39 Stat. 798][/us/stat/39/798].)
