## § 74. Rules and regulations

    The Secretary of the Treasury shall make such rules and regulations as are necessary for the carrying out of the provisions of [section 73 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s73].

([Sept. 8, 1916, ch. 463][/us/act/1916-09-08/ch463], title VIII, § 803, [39 Stat. 799][/us/stat/39/799].)
