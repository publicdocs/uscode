## § 72. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–429, title II, § 2006(a)][/us/pl/108/429/s2006/a], Dec. 3, 2004, [118 Stat. 2597][/us/stat/118/2597]

    Section, [act Sept. 8, 1916, ch. 463][/us/act/1916-09-08/ch463], title VIII, § 801, [39 Stat. 798][/us/stat/39/798], related to importation or sale of articles at less than market value or wholesale price.

 __Savings Provision__ 

[Pub. L. 108–429, title II, § 2006(b)][/us/pl/108/429/s2006/b], Dec. 3, 2004, [118 Stat. 2597][/us/stat/118/2597], provided that: 

> “The repeal made by subsection (a) \[repealing this section\] shall not affect any action under section 801 of the Act referred to in subsection (a) \[this section\] that was commenced before the date of the enactment of this Act \[Dec. 3, 2004\] and is pending on such date.”
