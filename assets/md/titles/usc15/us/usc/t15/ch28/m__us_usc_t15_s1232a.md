## § 1232a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–178, title IV][/us/pl/92/178/tIV], § 401(g)(7)(A), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 534][/us/stat/85/534]

    Section, [Pub. L. 91–614, title III][/us/pl/91/614/tIII], § 304, Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1845][/us/stat/84/1845], related to Federal manufacturers excise tax on labels and provided for violations and penalties. It was not a part of the Automobile Information Disclosure Act, which comprises this chapter generally.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

[Pub. L. 92–178, title IV][/us/pl/92/178/tIV], § 401(g)(7)(B), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 534][/us/stat/85/534], provided that: 

> “Subparagraph (A) \[repealing this section\] shall apply to acts (or failures to act) after the date of the enactment of this Act \[Dec. 10, 1971\].”
