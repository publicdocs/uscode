## § 7422. No regulatory authority

    Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to confer any regulatory authority on any Federal, State, tribal, or local department or agency.

([Pub. L. 113–274][/us/pl/113/274], § 3, Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 2972][/us/stat/128/2972].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 113–274][/us/pl/113/274], Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 2971][/us/stat/128/2971], which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 7421 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s7421] and Tables.
