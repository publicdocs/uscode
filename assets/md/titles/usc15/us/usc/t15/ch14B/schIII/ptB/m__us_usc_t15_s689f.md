## § 689f. Fees

    Except as provided in [section 689e(d) of this title][/us/usc/t15/s689e/d], the Administrator may charge such fees as it deems appropriate with respect to any guarantee or grant issued under this part.

([Pub. L. 85–699, title III][/us/pl/85/699/tIII], § 357, as added [Pub. L. 106–554][/us/pl/106/554], § 1(a)(8) \[§ 1(b)(3)\], Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2763][/us/stat/114/2763], 2763A–660.)
