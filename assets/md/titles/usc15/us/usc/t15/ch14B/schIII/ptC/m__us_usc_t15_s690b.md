## § 690b. Establishment

The Administrator shall establish a Renewable Fuel Capital Investment Program, under which the Administrator may—

    (1) enter into participation agreements for the purposes described in [section 690a of this title][/us/usc/t15/s690a]; and

    (2) guarantee the debentures issued by Renewable Fuel Capital Investment companies as provided in [section 690d of this title][/us/usc/t15/s690d].

([Pub. L. 85–699, title III, § 383][/us/pl/85/699/s383], as added [Pub. L. 110–140, title XII, § 1207][/us/pl/110/140/s1207], Dec. 19, 2007, [121 Stat. 1776][/us/stat/121/1776].)
