## § 689b. Establishment

    In accordance with this part, the Administrator shall establish a New Markets Venture Capital Program, under which the Administrator may—

    (1) enter into participation agreements with companies granted final approval under [section 689c(e) of this title][/us/usc/t15/s689c/e] for the purposes set forth in [section 689a of this title][/us/usc/t15/s689a];

    (2) guarantee the debentures issued by New Markets Venture Capital companies as provided in [section 689d of this title][/us/usc/t15/s689d]; and

    (3) make grants to New Markets Venture Capital companies, and to other entities, under [section 689g of this title][/us/usc/t15/s689g].

([Pub. L. 85–699, title III, § 353][/us/pl/85/699/s353], as added [Pub. L. 106–554, § 1(a)(8)][/us/pl/106/554/s1/a/8] \[§ 1(b)(3)\], Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2763][/us/stat/114/2763], 2763A–655.)
