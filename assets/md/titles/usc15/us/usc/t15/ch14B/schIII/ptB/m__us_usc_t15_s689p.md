## § 689p. Regulations

    The Administrator may issue such regulations as it deems necessary to carry out the provisions of this part in accordance with its purposes.

([Pub. L. 85–699, title III][/us/pl/85/699/tIII], § 367, as added [Pub. L. 106–554][/us/pl/106/554], § 1(a)(8) \[§ 1(b)(3)\], Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2763][/us/stat/114/2763], 2763A–664.)
