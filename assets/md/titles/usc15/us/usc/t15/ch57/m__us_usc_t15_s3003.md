## § 3003. Acceptance of interstate off-track wager

    No person may accept an interstate off-track wager except as provided in this chapter.

([Pub. L. 95–515][/us/pl/95/515], § 4, Oct. 25, 1978, [92 Stat. 1813][/us/stat/92/1813].)
