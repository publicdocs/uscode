## §§ 5805 to 5808. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6][/us/pl/111/314/s6], Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444][/us/stat/124/3444]

    Section 5805, [Pub. L. 102–588, title V, § 506][/us/pl/102/588/s506], Nov. 4, 1992, [106 Stat. 5127][/us/stat/106/5127], related to identification of launch support facilities.

    Section 5806, [Pub. L. 102–588, title V, § 507][/us/pl/102/588/s507], Nov. 4, 1992, [106 Stat. 5127][/us/stat/106/5127], related to anchor tenancy and termination liability. See [section 50503 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50503], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Section 5807, [Pub. L. 102–588, title V, § 508][/us/pl/102/588/s508], Nov. 4, 1992, [106 Stat. 5128][/us/stat/106/5128], related to use of Government facilities. See [section 50504 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50504].

    Section 5808, [Pub. L. 102–588, title V, § 510][/us/pl/102/588/s510], Nov. 4, 1992, [106 Stat. 5129][/us/stat/106/5129], related to Commercial Space Achievement Award. See [section 50506 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50506].
