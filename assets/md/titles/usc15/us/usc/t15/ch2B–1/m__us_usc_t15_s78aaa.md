## § 78aaa. Short title

    This chapter may be cited as the “Securities Investor Protection Act of 1970”.

([Pub. L. 91–598][/us/pl/91/598], § 1(a), Dec. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1636][/us/stat/84/1636].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “This Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 91–598][/us/pl/91/598], Dec. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1636][/us/stat/84/1636]. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

 __Short Title of 1978 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 95–283][/us/pl/95/283], § 1, May 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 249][/us/stat/92/249], provided that: 

> “This Act \[enacting sections 78fff–1 to 78fff–4 of this title, amending sections 77c, 78c, 78k, and 78ccc to 78lll of this title and enacting provisions set out as a note under [section 78k of this title][/us/usc/t15/s78k]\] may be cited as the ‘Securities Investor Protection Act Amendments of 1978’.”
