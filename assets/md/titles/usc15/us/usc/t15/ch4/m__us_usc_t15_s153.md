## § 153. Dividends

    Dividends declared by a China Trade Act corporation shall be derived wholly from the surplus profits of its business.

([Sept. 19, 1922, ch. 346][/us/act/1922-09-19/ch346], § 13, [42 Stat. 853][/us/stat/42/853].)
