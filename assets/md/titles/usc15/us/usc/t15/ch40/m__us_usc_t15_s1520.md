## § 1520. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–412, § 3(d)][/us/pl/91/412/s3/d], Sept. 25, 1970, [84 Stat. 864][/us/stat/84/864]

    Section, [act Dec. 19, 1942, ch. 780][/us/act/1942-12-19/ch780], [56 Stat. 1067][/us/stat/56/1067], authorized Secretary of Commerce to establish schedule of fees or charges for services or publications furnished by Department of Commerce, excepting Federal and State governments, provided for covering proceeds thereof into the Treasury as miscellaneous receipts, and specified that its provisions shall not alter, amend, modify, or repeal any existing law for prescription of fees or charges. See sections 1525 to 1527 of this title.
