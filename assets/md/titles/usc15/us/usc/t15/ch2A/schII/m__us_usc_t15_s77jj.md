## § 77jj. Loans to Corporation from Reconstruction Finance Corporation authorized

    The Reconstruction Finance Corporation is authorized to loan out of its funds not to exceed $75,000 for the use of the Corporation.

([May 27, 1933, ch. 38][/us/act/1933-05-27/ch38], title II, § 209, [48 Stat. 95][/us/stat/48/95].)

 __Abolition of Reconstruction Finance Corporation__ 

    Section 6(a) of Reorg. Plan No. 1 of 1957, eff. June 30, 1957, 22 F.R. 4633, [71 Stat. 647][/us/stat/71/647], set out as a note under [section 601 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s601], abolished the Reconstruction Finance Corporation.
