## § 5522. National Aeronautics and Space Administration activities

    (a) __General responsibilities__ 

        As part of the Program described in subchapter I, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration shall conduct basic and applied research in high-performance computing, particularly in the field of computational science, with emphasis on aerospace sciences, earth and space sciences, and remote exploration and experimentation.

    (b) __Authorization of appropriations__ 

        From sums otherwise authorized to be appropriated, there are authorized to be appropriated to the National Aeronautics and Space Administration for the purposes of the Program $72,000,000 for fiscal year 1992; $107,000,000 for fiscal year 1993; $134,000,000 for fiscal year 1994; $151,000,000 for fiscal year 1995; and $145,000,000 for fiscal year 1996.

([Pub. L. 102–194, title II, § 202][/us/pl/102/194/s202], Dec. 9, 1991, [105 Stat. 1600][/us/stat/105/1600].)
