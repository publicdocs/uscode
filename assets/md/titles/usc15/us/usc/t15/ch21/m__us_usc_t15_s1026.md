## § 1026. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–136, title VI, § 601][/us/pl/94/136/s601], Nov. 28, 1975, [89 Stat. 742][/us/stat/89/742]

    Section, [Pub. L. 92–210, § 4][/us/pl/92/210/s4], Dec. 22, 1971, [85 Stat. 753][/us/stat/85/753]; [Pub. L. 93–34][/us/pl/93/34], May 14, 1973, [87 Stat. 72][/us/stat/87/72], created the President’s National Commission on Productivity and authorized appropriations for its operation through June 30, 1973. Thereafter, the Commission’s name was changed to the National Commission on Productivity and Work Quality by [Pub. L. 93–311][/us/pl/93/311], June 8, 1974, [88 Stat. 236][/us/stat/88/236] and appropriations were authorized to continue operations through November 30, 1975, by [Pub. L. 94–42, § 2][/us/pl/94/42/s2], June 28, 1975, [89 Stat. 232][/us/stat/89/232], and [Pub. L. 94–100, § 2][/us/pl/94/100/s2], Oct. 1, 1975, [89 Stat. 483][/us/stat/89/483]. See section 2401 et seq. of this title.
