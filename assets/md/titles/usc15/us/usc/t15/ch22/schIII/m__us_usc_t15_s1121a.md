## § 1121a. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 5, 1946, ch. 540, title VI][/us/act/1946-07-05/ch540/tVI], § 39a, as added Oct. 12, 1982, [Pub. L. 97–296][/us/pl/97/296], [96 Stat. 1316][/us/stat/96/1316], which prohibited State and local requirements that registered trademarks be altered or displayed differently, was transferred to subsec. (b) of section 39 of act July 5, 1946, by [section 131(b)(1) of Pub. L. 100–667][/us/pl/100/667/s131/b/1] and is classified to [section 1121(b) of this title][/us/usc/t15/s1121/b].
