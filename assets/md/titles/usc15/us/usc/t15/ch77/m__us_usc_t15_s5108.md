## § 5108. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out this chapter $12,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2008 through 2012.

([Pub. L. 100–680][/us/pl/100/680], § 9, Nov. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 4076][/us/stat/102/4076]; [Pub. L. 102–486, title XXI][/us/pl/102/486/tXXI], § 2106(a)(3), (4), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 3070][/us/stat/106/3070]; [Pub. L. 110–229, title VI][/us/pl/110/229/tVI], § 602(a), May 8, 2008, [122 Stat. 853][/us/stat/122/853].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2008—[Pub. L. 110–229][/us/pl/110/229] amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section authorized appropriations to the Secretary and to the Director of the National Institute of Standards and Technology to carry out functions under this chapter.

    1992—Subsec. (a)(1). [Pub. L. 102–486][/us/pl/102/486], § 2106(3), substituted “$25,000,000 for fiscal year 1991, $17,968,000 for fiscal year 1992, and $18,091,000 for each of the fiscal years 1993 through 1997, to be derived from sums authorized under [section 13451(e) of title 42][/us/usc/t42/s13451/e]” for “and $25,000,000 for fiscal year 1991”.

    Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 102–486][/us/pl/102/486], § 2106(4), substituted “1991, 1992, 1993, 1994, 1995, 1996, and 1997, to be derived from sums otherwise authorized to be appropriated to the Institute” for “and 1991”.
