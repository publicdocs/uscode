## § 7405. Consultation

    In carrying out sections 7403 and 7404 of this title, the Director shall consult with other Federal agencies.

([Pub. L. 107–305, § 6][/us/pl/107/305/s6], Nov. 27, 2002, [116 Stat. 2374][/us/stat/116/2374].)
