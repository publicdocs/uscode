## § 6734. Other provisions

    (a) __Judicial review__ 

        The appropriate United States district court shall have exclusive jurisdiction over litigation arising under this section  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  involving any redomesticating or redomesticated insurer.

    (b) __Severability__ 

        If any provision of this section,1 or the application thereof to any person or circumstances, is held invalid, the remainder of the section,1 and the application of such provision to other persons or circumstances, shall not be affected thereby.

([Pub. L. 106–102, title III][/us/pl/106/102/tIII], § 314, Nov. 12, 1999, [113 Stat. 1420][/us/stat/113/1420].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This section, referred to in text, probably should be a reference to this subtitle, meaning subtitle B (§§ 311–316) of title III of [Pub. L. 106–102][/us/pl/106/102], which is classified generally to this subchapter.
