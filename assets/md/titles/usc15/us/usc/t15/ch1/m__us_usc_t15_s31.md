## § 31. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title IV, § 14102(a)][/us/pl/107/273/s14102/a], Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1921][/us/stat/116/1921]

    Section, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 390, § 11][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch390/s11], [37 Stat. 567][/us/stat/37/567], related to closure of Panama Canal to violators of antitrust laws.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Nov. 2, 2002, and applicable only with respect to cases commenced on or after Nov. 2, 2002, see [section 14103 of Pub. L. 107–273][/us/pl/107/273/s14103], set out as a note under [section 3 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s3].

§§ 32, 33. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 91–452, title II__  __][/us/pl/91/452]__  __, §§ 209, 210,__  __Oct. 15, 1970__  __,__  __[__  __84 Stat. 929__  __][/us/stat/84/929]__ 

    Section 32, [act Feb. 25, 1903, ch. 755, § 1][/us/act/1903-02-25/ch755/s1], [32 Stat. 904][/us/stat/32/904], granted immunity from prosecution to witnesses testifying or producing evidence, documentary or otherwise, in any proceeding, suit, or prosecution under section 1 to 11 of this title. See section 6001 et seq. of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

    Section 33, [act June 30, 1906, ch. 3920][/us/act/1906-06-30/ch3920], [34 Stat. 798][/us/stat/34/798], provided that, under the immunity provisions of former [section 32 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s32], immunity was to extend only to a natural person who, in obedience to a subpoena, testified or produced evidence.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective on sixtieth day following Oct. 15, 1970, see [section 260 of Pub. L. 91–452][/us/pl/91/452/s260], set out as an Effective Date; Savings Provision note under [section 6001 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s6001], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Repeal of sections by [Pub. L. 91–452][/us/pl/91/452] not to affect any immunity to which any individual was entitled under sections by reason of any testimony given before the sixtieth day following Oct. 15, 1970, see [section 260 of Pub. L. 91–452][/us/pl/91/452/s260], set out as an Effective Date; Savings Provision note under [section 6001 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s6001], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.
