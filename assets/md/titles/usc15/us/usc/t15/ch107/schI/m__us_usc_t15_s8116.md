## § 8116. Authorization of appropriations

    (a)  __ <sup>\[__  __1__  __\]</sup> __  __ <sup><sup> __  __1__  __ So in original. No subsec. (b) has been enacted.__  __ </sup></sup> __  __In general__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 110–403, title III, § 306][/us/pl/110/403/s306], Oct. 13, 2008, [122 Stat. 4270][/us/stat/122/4270].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This subchapter, referred to in subsec. (a), was in the original “this title”, meaning title III of [Pub. L. 110–403][/us/pl/110/403], Oct. 13, 2008, [122 Stat. 4264][/us/stat/122/4264], which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title III to the Code, see Tables.
