###### §§ 192, 192a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–412][/us/pl/91/412], § 3(a), (b), Sept. 25, 1970, [84 Stat. 864][/us/stat/84/864]

    Section 192, acts [Mar. 1, 1919, ch. 86][/us/act/1919-03-01/ch86], § 1, [40 Stat. 1256][/us/stat/40/1256]; [May 27, 1935, ch. 148][/us/act/1935-05-27/ch148], § 2, [49 Stat. 293][/us/stat/49/293], which provided for deposit of moneys received for special statistical studies, compilations, and transcripts in a special account for payment of ordinary expenses incidental to the work and services of nongovernmental individuals and had originally provided for covering into the Treasury as miscellaneous receipts moneys received in payment of photographic and other mechanical reproduction of special statistical compilations from the records, is now covered by [section 1526 of this title][/us/usc/t15/s1526].

    Section 192a, acts [May 27, 1935, ch. 148][/us/act/1935-05-27/ch148], § 3, [49 Stat. 293][/us/stat/49/293]; [Aug. 7, 1946, ch. 770][/us/act/1946-08-07/ch770], § 1(4), [60 Stat. 866][/us/stat/60/866], required Secretary of Commerce to prescribe rules and regulations governing special statistical studies.
