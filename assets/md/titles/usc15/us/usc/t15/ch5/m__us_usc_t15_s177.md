## § 177. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–826][/us/pl/87/826], § 3, Oct. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 953][/us/stat/76/953]

    Section, acts [July 16, 1892, ch. 196][/us/act/1892-07-16/ch196], § 1, [27 Stat. 197][/us/stat/27/197]; [Mar. 3, 1893, ch. 211][/us/act/1893-03-03/ch211], § 1, [27 Stat. 689][/us/stat/27/689]; [Feb. 14, 1903, ch. 552][/us/act/1903-02-14/ch552], § 10, [32 Stat. 829][/us/stat/32/829]; [Aug. 23, 1912, ch. 350][/us/act/1912-08-23/ch350], § 1, [37 Stat. 407][/us/stat/37/407]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736], related to returns of exports to foreign countries leaving the United States by rail.  __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective 180 days after Oct. 15, 1962, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 87–826][/us/pl/87/826/s4], set out as a note under [section 301 of Title 13][/us/usc/t13/s301], Census.

    See [section 3 of Pub. L. 87–826][/us/pl/87/826/s3], set out as a note under sections 173 and 174 of this title.
