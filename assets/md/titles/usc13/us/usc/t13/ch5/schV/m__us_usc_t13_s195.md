## § 195. Use of sampling

    Except for the determination of population for purposes of apportionment of Representatives in Congress among the several States, the Secretary shall, if he considers it feasible, authorize the use of the statistical method known as “sampling” in carrying out the provisions of this title.

(Added [Pub. L. 85–207][/us/pl/85/207], § 14, Aug. 28, 1957, [71 Stat. 484][/us/stat/71/484]; amended [Pub. L. 94–521][/us/pl/94/521], § 10, Oct. 17, 1976, [90 Stat. 2464][/us/stat/90/2464].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–521][/us/pl/94/521] substituted “for purposes of apportionment of Representatives in Congress among the several States, the Secretary shall, if he considers it feasible” for “for apportionment purposes, the Secretary may, where he deems it appropriate”.

 __Effective Date of 1976 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 94–521][/us/pl/94/521] effective Oct. 17, 1976, see [section 17 of Pub. L. 94–521][/us/pl/94/521/s17], set out as a note under [section 1 of this title][/us/usc/t13/s1].
