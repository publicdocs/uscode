## § 184. Definitions

For purposes of this subchapter—

    (1) the term “local unit of general purpose government” means the government of a county, municipality, township, Indian tribe, Alaskan native village, or other unit of government (other than a State) which is a unit of general government, and

    (2) the term “State” includes the District of Columbia.

(Added [Pub. L. 94–521, § 8(a)][/us/pl/94/521/s8/a], Oct. 17, 1976, [90 Stat. 2463][/us/stat/90/2463].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Oct. 17, 1976, see [section 17 of Pub. L. 94–521][/us/pl/94/521/s17], set out as a note under [section 1 of this title][/us/usc/t13/s1].
