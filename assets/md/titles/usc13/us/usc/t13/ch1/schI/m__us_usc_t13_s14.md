## \[§ 14. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–473][/us/pl/89/473], § 2(a), June 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 221][/us/stat/80/221]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 87–489][/us/pl/87/489], § 1(a), June 19, 1962, [76 Stat. 104][/us/stat/76/104], provided for reimbursement between appropriations. See [section 1534 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s1534], Money and Finance.

 __Repeals__ 

    [Pub. L. 89–473][/us/pl/89/473], June 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 221][/us/stat/80/221], which repealed this section and struck out item 14 in the analysis of sections comprising this chapter, was itself repealed by [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], § 5(b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068][/us/stat/96/1068].
