 __Codification__ 

    Analysis of chapters editorially supplied. Part IV added by [Pub. L. 94–131][/us/pl/94/131] without adding analysis for chapters 35, 36, and 37.

[Pub. L. 96–517][/us/pl/96/517] purported to amend the table of chapters of title 35 by adding after the item for chapter 37 the following: 

> “38. Patent Rights in Inventions Made with Federal Assistance”

. Title 35 did not contain a table of chapters, and [section 6(b) of Pub. L. 96–517][/us/pl/96/517/s6/b] and the purported amendment made by it were repealed by [Pub. L. 97–256][/us/pl/97/256]. See chapter 18 (§ 200 et seq.) of this title.
