## § 117. Death or incapacity of inventor

    Legal representatives of deceased inventors and of those under legal incapacity may make application for patent upon compliance with the requirements and on the same terms and conditions applicable to the inventor.

([July 19, 1952, ch. 950][/us/act/1952-07-19/ch950], [66 Stat. 799][/us/stat/66/799].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on Title 35, U.S.C., 1946 ed., § 46 (R.S. 4896, amended (1) [Feb. 28, 1899, ch. 227][/us/act/1899-02-28/ch227], [30 Stat. 915][/us/stat/30/915], (2) [Mar. 3, 1903, ch. 1019][/us/act/1903-03-03/ch1019], § 3, [32 Stat. 1225][/us/stat/32/1225], 1226, (3) [May 23, 1908, ch. 188][/us/act/1908-05-23/ch188], [35 Stat. 245][/us/stat/35/245]).
