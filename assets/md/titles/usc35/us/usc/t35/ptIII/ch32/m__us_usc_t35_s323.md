## § 323. Preliminary response to petition

    If a post-grant review petition is filed under section 321, the patent owner shall have the right to file a preliminary response to the petition, within a time period set by the Director, that sets forth reasons why no post-grant review should be instituted based upon the failure of the petition to meet any requirement of this chapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 112–29, § 6(d)][/us/pl/112/29/s6/d], Sept. 16, 2011, [125 Stat. 306][/us/stat/125/306].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective upon the expiration of the 1-year period beginning Sept. 16, 2011, and applicable only to patents described in [section 3(n)(1) of Pub. L. 112–29][/us/pl/112/29/s3/n/1] ([35 U.S.C. 100][/us/usc/t35/s100] note), with certain exceptions and limitations, see section 6(f)(2), (3) of [Pub. L. 112–29][/us/pl/112/29], set out as a note under [section 321 of this title][/us/usc/t35/s321].
