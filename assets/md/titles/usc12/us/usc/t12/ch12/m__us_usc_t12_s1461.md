## § 1461. Short title

    This chapter may be cited as the “Home Owners’ Loan Act.”

([June 13, 1933, ch. 64][/us/act/1933-06-13/ch64], § 1 (part), [48 Stat. 128][/us/stat/48/128]; [Pub. L. 101–73, title III][/us/pl/101/73/tIII], § 301, Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 277][/us/stat/103/277].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section is comprised of the first sentence of section 1 of act June 13, 1933. The remainder of section 1 of the Act included a table of contents for the Act.

 __Amendments__ 

    1989—[Pub. L. 101–73][/us/pl/101/73] amended section generally, striking out “of 1933” after “Act”.

 __Effective Date of 1989 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 101–73, title III][/us/pl/101/73/tIII], § 305(c), Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 352][/us/stat/103/352], provided that: 

> “The amendments made by section 301 \[amending this chapter\] relating to civil penalties shall apply with respect to violations committed and activities engaged in after the date of the enactment of this Act \[Aug. 9, 1989\], except that the increased maximum civil penalties of $5,000 and $25,000 per violation or per day may apply to such violations or activities committed or engaged in before such date with respect to an institution if such violations or activities—

>     “(1) are not already subject to a notice issued by the appropriate Federal banking agency or the Board (initiating an administrative proceeding); and

>     “(2) occurred after the completion of the last report of examination of the institution by the appropriate Federal banking agency (as defined in section 3 of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act \[[12 U.S.C. 1813][/us/usc/t12/s1813]\]) occurring before the date of the enactment of this Act.”

 __Short Title of 1998 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 105–164][/us/pl/105/164], § 1, Mar. 20, 1998, [112 Stat. 32][/us/stat/112/32], provided that: 

> “This Act \[enacting [section 1786a of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1786a], amending sections 1464 and 1818 of this title, and enacting provisions set out as a note under [section 1811 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1811]\] may be cited as the ‘Examination Parity and Year 2000 Readiness for Financial Institutions Act’.”

 __Short Title of 1991 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 102–242, title IV][/us/pl/102/242/tIV], § 436, Dec. 19, 1991, [105 Stat. 2381][/us/stat/105/2381], provided that: 

> “This subtitle \[subtitle G (§§ 436–441) of title IV of [Pub. L. 102–242][/us/pl/102/242], amending sections 1464 and 1467a of this title\] may be cited as the ‘Qualified Thrift Lender Reform Act of 1991’.”

 __Short Title of 1982 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 97–320, title III][/us/pl/97/320/tIII], § 301, Oct. 15, 1982, [96 Stat. 1496][/us/stat/96/1496], provided that: 

> “This title \[enacting [section 1701j–3 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1701j–3], amending sections 1425a, 1426, 1428a, 1430, 1464, 1725, 1730a, 1841, and 3503 of this title, enacting provisions set out as a note under [section 3503 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s3503], and repealing provisions set out as a note under [section 461 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s461]\] may be cited as the ‘Thrift Institutions Restructuring Act’.”
