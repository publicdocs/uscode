## § 2219. Limitation on separate sale

    If real property is acquired by any institution of the Farm Credit System through foreclosure, no institution of the Farm Credit System shall sell the surface rights to that real property to any person unless the institution also sells all mineral rights to that real property to that person.

([Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4][/us/pl/92/181/s4].35, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title III, § 306][/us/pl/99/205/s306], Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1709][/us/stat/99/1709].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective thirty days after Dec. 23, 1985, see [section 401 of Pub. L. 99–205][/us/pl/99/205/s401], set out as an Effective Date of 1985 Amendment note under [section 2001 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2001].
