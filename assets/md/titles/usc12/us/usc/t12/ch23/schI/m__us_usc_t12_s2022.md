## § 2022. Liens on stock

    The Farm Credit Banks shall have a first lien on the stock or participation certificates it issues for the payment of any liability of the stockholders to the bank.

([Pub. L. 92–181, title I][/us/pl/92/181/tI], § 1.14, as added [Pub. L. 100–233, title IV][/us/pl/100/233/tIV], § 401, Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1629][/us/stat/101/1629].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 1.14 of [Pub. L. 92–181][/us/pl/92/181] was classified to [section 2032 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2032] prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 100–233][/us/pl/100/233], § 401.
