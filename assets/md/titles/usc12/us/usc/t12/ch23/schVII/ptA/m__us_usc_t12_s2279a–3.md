## § 2279a–3. Capitalization

    In accordance with [section 2154a of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2154a], each merged bank shall provide, through bylaws and subject to Farm Credit Administration regulations, for the capitalization of the bank and the manner in which bank stock shall be issued, held, transferred, and retired and bank earnings distributed.

([Pub. L. 92–181, title VII, § 7][/us/pl/92/181/s7].3, as added [Pub. L. 100–399, title IV, § 408(d)][/us/pl/100/399/s408/d], Aug. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 1001][/us/stat/102/1001].)

 __Prior Provisions__

L. 100–233, title IV, § 416][/us/pl/100/233/s416], Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1645][/us/stat/101/1645], related to issuance of shares of capital stock, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 100–399, title IV, § 408(d)][/us/pl/100/399/s408/d], Aug. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 1001][/us/stat/102/1001].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective as if enacted immediately after enactment of [Pub. L. 100–233][/us/pl/100/233], which was approved Jan. 6, 1988, see [section 1001(a) of Pub. L. 100–399][/us/pl/100/399/s1001/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 2002 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2002].
