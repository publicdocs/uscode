## § 2219c. Affirmative action

    The Assistance Board established under [section 2278a of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2278a] and all institutions of the Farm Credit System with more than 20 employees shall establish and maintain an affirmative action program plan that applies the affirmative action standards otherwise applied to contractors of the Federal Government.

([Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4][/us/pl/92/181/s4].38, formerly § 4.37, as added [Pub.
