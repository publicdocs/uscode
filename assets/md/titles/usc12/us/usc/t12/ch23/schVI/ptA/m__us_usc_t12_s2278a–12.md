## § 2278a–12. Termination

    The Assistance Board and the authority provided to the Assistance Board by this part shall terminate on December 31, 1992.

([Pub. L. 92–181, title VI][/us/pl/92/181/tVI], § 6.12, as added [Pub. L. 100–233, title II][/us/pl/100/233/tII], § 201, Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1594][/us/stat/101/1594]; amended [Pub. L. 100–399, title II][/us/pl/100/399/tII], § 201(m), Aug. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 991][/us/stat/102/991].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1988—[Pub. L. 100–399][/us/pl/100/399] inserted “to the Assistance Board” after “provided”.

 __Effective Date of 1988 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 100–399][/us/pl/100/399] effective as if enacted immediately after enactment of [Pub. L. 100–233][/us/pl/100/233], which was approved Jan. 6, 1988, see [section 1001(a) of Pub. L. 100–399][/us/pl/100/399/s1001/a], set out as a note under [section 2002 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2002].
