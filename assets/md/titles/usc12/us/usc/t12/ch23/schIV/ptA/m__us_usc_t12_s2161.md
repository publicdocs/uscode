## § 2161. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–399, title I][/us/pl/100/399/tI], § 101(a), Aug. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 989][/us/stat/102/989]

    Section, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV][/us/pl/92/181/tIV], § 4.9A, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I][/us/pl/99/205/tI], § 105, Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1687][/us/stat/99/1687], authorized a central reserve for Farm Credit System.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective immediately after enactment of [Pub. L. 100–233][/us/pl/100/233], approved Jan. 6, 1988, see [section 1001(a) of Pub. L. 100–399][/us/pl/100/399/s1001/a], set out as a note under [section 2002 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2002].
