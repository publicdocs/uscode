## § 3717. Regulations

    The Secretary is authorized to issue such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

([Pub. L. 97–35, title III][/us/pl/97/35/tIII], § 369I, Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 431][/us/stat/95/431].)
