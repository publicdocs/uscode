## §§ 636 to 636h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5][/us/pl/92/181/s5].26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624][/us/stat/85/624]

    Section 636, acts [May 12, 1933, ch. 25][/us/act/1933-05-12/ch25], title II, § 40, [48 Stat. 51][/us/stat/48/51]; Sept. 6, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], [80 Stat. 648][/us/stat/80/648], provided for organization of Farm Credit Administration. See [section 2247 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2247].

    Section 636a, [act Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 2][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s2], [67 Stat. 390][/us/stat/67/390], stated Congressional declaration of policy concerning agricultural credit. See [section 2001 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2001].

    Section 636a note, [act Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 1][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s1], [67 Stat. 390][/us/stat/67/390], provided that such act Aug. 6, 1953, should be known as “Farm Credit Act of 1953”.

    Section 636b, [act Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 3][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s3], [67 Stat. 390][/us/stat/67/390], covered the creation of Farm Credit Administration as an independent agency in executive branch. See section 2241 et seq. of this title.

    Section 636c, acts [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 4][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s4], [67 Stat. 390][/us/stat/67/390]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title IV, § 402, [69 Stat. 666][/us/stat/69/666]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)][/us/pl/86/168/s104/h], [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387]; Aug. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–525, § 6][/us/pl/89/525/s6], [80 Stat. 335][/us/stat/80/335], provided for creation of Federal Farm Credit Board. See section 2242 et seq. of this title.

    Section 636d, [act Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 5][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s5], [67 Stat. 392][/us/stat/67/392]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(j)][/us/pl/86/168/s104/j], [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387]; Oct. 4, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–367, title III, § 302(a)][/us/pl/87/367/s302/a], [75 Stat. 793][/us/stat/75/793], provided for position of Governor of Farm Credit Administration. See section 2244 et seq. of this title.

    Section 636e, [act Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 6][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s6], [67 Stat. 393][/us/stat/67/393], covered duties of Federal Farm Credit Board. See [section 2227 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2227].

    Section 636f, acts [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 7][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s7], [67 Stat. 393][/us/stat/67/393]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, § 110(c), [69 Stat. 662][/us/stat/69/662]; Oct. 4, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–353, § 3(n)][/us/pl/87/353/s3/n], [75 Stat. 774][/us/stat/75/774], provided for abolishment of certain offices and funds under program as it existed prior to 1953.

    Section 636g, acts [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 8][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s8], [67 Stat. 394][/us/stat/67/394]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 107(a), [70 Stat. 666][/us/stat/70/666]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)][/us/pl/86/168/s104/h], [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387], authorized delegation of powers to institutions in farm credit districts. See [section 2253 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2253].

    Section 636h, [act Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 17(b)][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s17/b], [67 Stat. 399][/us/stat/67/399], covered administrative expenditures of Farm Credit Administration. See [section 2249 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2249].
