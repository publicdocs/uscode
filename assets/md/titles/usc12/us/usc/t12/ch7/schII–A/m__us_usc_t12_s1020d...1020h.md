## §§ 1020d to 1020h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–353][/us/pl/87/353], § 3(a), Oct. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 773][/us/stat/75/773]

    Section 1020d, acts [Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7][/us/act/1934-01-31/ch7], § 4(b), [48 Stat. 346][/us/stat/48/346]; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], §§ 2, 4, [50 Stat. 703][/us/stat/50/703]; [June 30, 1945, ch. 204][/us/act/1945-06-30/ch204], § 15, [59 Stat. 269][/us/stat/59/269], provided for the purchase of consolidated farm loan bonds, loans to Federal and joint-stock land banks, investment in mortgages, extensions, and sale and assignment of notes and mortgages.

    Section 1020e, [act Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7][/us/act/1934-01-31/ch7], § 4(c), [48 Stat. 346][/us/stat/48/346], related to preparation of bonds.

    Section 1020f, acts [Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7][/us/act/1934-01-31/ch7], § 12, [48 Stat. 347][/us/stat/48/347]; [Feb. 26, 1934, ch. 33][/us/act/1934-02-26/ch33], [48 Stat. 360][/us/stat/48/360], provided for exemptions from taxation.

    Sections 1020g, 1020h, [act Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7][/us/act/1934-01-31/ch7], §§ 17, 18, [48 Stat. 348][/us/stat/48/348], 349, related to the severability clause and reservation of right to amend, and short title, respectively.
