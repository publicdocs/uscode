## §§ 1134f to 1134m. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V][/us/pl/92/181/tV], § 5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624][/us/stat/85/624]

    Section 1134f, [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title III][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98/tIII], § 30, [48 Stat. 261][/us/stat/48/261], authorized the creation of a central bank for cooperatives. See [section 2121 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2121].

    Section 1134g, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title III, § 31, [48 Stat. 262][/us/stat/48/262]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, § 104, [69 Stat. 659][/us/stat/69/659]; June 11, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–503][/us/pl/86/503], § 1 (part), [74 Stat. 197][/us/stat/74/197], provided for a board of directors for central bank for cooperatives. See [section 2123 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2123].

    Section 1134h, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title III, § 32, [48 Stat. 262][/us/stat/48/262]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, § 105, [69 Stat. 661][/us/stat/69/661], set out powers of board of directors for central bank for cooperative. See [section 2123 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2123].

    Section 1134i, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title III, § 33, [48 Stat. 262][/us/stat/48/262]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, § 108(a), [69 Stat. 662][/us/stat/69/662], provided for capital stock of central bank for cooperatives. See [section 2124 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2124].

    Section 1134j, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title III, §§ 34, 38, [48 Stat. 262][/us/stat/48/262], 264; [June 3, 1935, ch. 164][/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164], § 13, [49 Stat. 317][/us/stat/49/317]; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 35, [50 Stat. 716][/us/stat/50/716]; [Oct. 31, 1949, ch. 792][/us/act/1949-10-31/ch792], title IV, § 417(b), [63 Stat. 1059][/us/stat/63/1059]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, §§ 106, 108(b), [69 Stat. 661][/us/stat/69/661], 662; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 105(j), [70 Stat. 665][/us/stat/70/665]; Aug. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–525][/us/pl/89/525], § 4(a), [80 Stat. 334][/us/stat/80/334]; Dec. 15, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–204][/us/pl/90/204], § 3(b), [81 Stat. 612][/us/stat/81/612], authorized central bank to make loans and provided for prevention of duplication of effort on part of central bank and banks for cooperatives. See [section 2122 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2122].

    Section 1134k, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title III, § 35, [48 Stat. 263][/us/stat/48/263]; [June 3, 1935, ch. 164][/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164], § 15, [49 Stat. 318][/us/stat/49/318]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, § 102, [69 Stat. 658][/us/stat/69/658], made regional bank stock provisions applicable to ownership of stock by central bank borrowers and provided for payments for such stock. See [section 2130 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2130].

    Section 1134l, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title III, § 36, [48 Stat. 263][/us/stat/48/263]; [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335], § 12, [67 Stat. 395][/us/stat/67/395]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, § 103(a), [69 Stat. 658][/us/stat/69/658]; Oct. 3, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–343][/us/pl/87/343], § 2(3), [75 Stat. 758][/us/stat/75/758]; Aug. 31, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–528][/us/pl/88/528], [78 Stat. 736][/us/stat/78/736], provided for disposition of earnings and maintenance of reserves of central bank. See [section 2132 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2132].

    Section 1134m, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title III, § 37, [48 Stat. 263][/us/stat/48/263]; [Aug. 23, 1954, ch. 834][/us/act/1954-08-23/ch834], § 1, [68 Stat. 770][/us/stat/68/770], covered debentures, amount of such debentures, security, preparation, and issuance of such debentures, and consolidated debentures of central and regional banks for cooperatives. See [section 2153 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2153].
