## § 1138f. Repealed. [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, § 110(b), [69 Stat. 662][/us/stat/69/662]

    Section, acts [June 16, 1938, ch. 98][/us/act/1938-06-16/ch98], title VI, § 66, [48 Stat. 269][/us/stat/48/269]; [Oct. 28, 1949, ch. 782][/us/act/1949-10-28/ch782], title XII, § 1201, [63 Stat. 972][/us/stat/63/972]; [Oct. 24, 1951, ch. 554][/us/act/1951-10-24/ch554], § 3, [65 Stat. 614][/us/stat/65/614]; [June 28, 1955, ch. 189][/us/act/1955-06-28/ch189], § 5, [69 Stat. 178][/us/stat/69/178], limited to $14,620 per annum compensation which could be paid directors, officers, or employees of banks for cooperatives, production credit corporations, and production credit associations.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

[Act Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785/tI], § 112, [69 Stat. 662][/us/stat/69/662], provided that: 

> “This title \[repealing this section and amending [section 1141f of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1141f]\] shall take effect on the first day of the month next following one hundred and twenty days after its enactment \[Aug. 11, 1955\].”
