## §§ 640a to 640l. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V][/us/pl/92/181/tV], § 5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624][/us/stat/85/624]

    Section 640a, acts [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(a), [50 Stat. 704][/us/stat/50/704]; [Oct. 29, 1949, ch. 786][/us/act/1949-10-29/ch786], § 5, [63 Stat. 986][/us/stat/63/986], created farm credit districts. See [section 2221 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2221].

    Section 640c, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(c), [50 Stat. 704][/us/stat/50/704], provided for initial board of directors of each district.

    Section 640d, acts [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(d), [50 Stat. 704][/us/stat/50/704]; [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335], § 15, [67 Stat. 397][/us/stat/67/397]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title IV, § 401(b), [69 Stat. 666][/us/stat/69/666]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 106(a)(1), [70 Stat. 666][/us/stat/70/666]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub.     Section 640e, acts [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(e), [50 Stat. 705][/us/stat/50/705]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub.     Section 640f, acts [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(f), [50 Stat. 705][/us/stat/50/705]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I][/us/pl/86/168/tI], § 104(h), [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387]; Aug. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–525][/us/pl/89/525], § 5, [80 Stat. 334][/us/stat/80/334], provided for election of elected directors. See [section 2223 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2223].

    Section 640g, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(g), [50 Stat. 705][/us/stat/50/705], provided for term of office and filling of vacancies. See section 2221 et seq. of this title.

    Section 640h, acts [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(h), [50 Stat. 705][/us/stat/50/705]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 106(a)(2), [70 Stat. 666][/us/stat/70/666], set out general qualifications of members of each farm credit board. See [section 2222 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2222].

    Section 640i, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(i), [50 Stat. 706][/us/stat/50/706], made felons and defrauders ineligible for membership. See [section 2222 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2222].

    Section 640j, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(j), [50 Stat. 706][/us/stat/50/706], provided for compensation of members of farm credit boards. See [section 2226 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2226].

    Section 640k, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 5(k), [50 Stat. 706][/us/stat/50/706], declared sections 640a to 640j of this title as not affecting laws making agricultural credit laws applicable to territories and possessions.

    Section 640l, acts [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704], § 6, [50 Stat. 706][/us/stat/50/706]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 106(b), [70 Stat. 666][/us/stat/70/666]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub.
