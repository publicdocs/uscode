## §§ 992 to 993. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–353, § 3(a)][/us/pl/87/353/s3/a], (w), Oct. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 773][/us/stat/75/773], 774

    Section 992, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, § 32][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/s32] (par.), as added [May 12, 1933, ch. 25][/us/act/1933-05-12/ch25], title II, § 21, [48 Stat. 41][/us/stat/48/41]; amended [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title VIII, § 80(a), [48 Stat. 273][/us/stat/48/273], related to Government guaranty of interest on qualified Federal land bank bonds issued during limited period, use of proceeds of such bonds, limitation on aggregate amount of such bonds, payment of interest by Government on inability of issuing bank and rights of Government after such payment.

    Section 992a, [act Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7, § 5][/us/act/1934-01-31/ch7/s5], [48 Stat. 346][/us/stat/48/346], prohibited any Federal land bank, ninety days after January 31, 1934, from issuing any bonds under provisions of [section 992 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s992], subject to guarantee of interest on such bonds by United States except for purpose of refinancing any bond which was or had been issued subject to such guarantee of interest.

    Section 993, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, § 32][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/s32] (par.), as added [May 12, 1933, ch. 25][/us/act/1933-05-12/ch25], title II, § 21, [48 Stat. 41][/us/stat/48/41], related to delivery of bonds issued under [section 992 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s992] in payment of certain mortgages.
