## § 683. Omitted

    Section, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245], title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 363][/us/stat/39/363]; [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252][/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252], title III, § 304, [42 Stat. 1474][/us/stat/42/1474]; [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title VII, § 70, title VIII, § 80a, [48 Stat. 269][/us/stat/48/269], 273, related to compensation and allowances of directors, officers, and employees, and was stricken by [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 7(b)][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s7/b], [50 Stat. 707][/us/stat/50/707]. Section 70 of [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], was repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 648][/us/stat/80/648]. See [section 2226 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2226].
