## §§ 1134 to 1134e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5][/us/pl/92/181/s5].26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624][/us/stat/85/624]

    Section 1134, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98] title I, § 2, [48 Stat. 257][/us/stat/48/257]; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 11][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s11], [50 Stat. 708][/us/stat/50/708]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 105(a), [70 Stat. 664][/us/stat/70/664], authorized creation of regional banks for cooperatives and establishment of boards of directors of such banks. See [section 2121 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2121].

    Section 1134a, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title I, § 3, [48 Stat. 257][/us/stat/48/257]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 105(b), [70 Stat. 665][/us/stat/70/665], provided for charters and bylaws for banks for cooperatives. See [section 2121 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2121].

    Section 1134b, [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title IV, § 40, [48 Stat. 264][/us/stat/48/264], provided for capital stock of banks for cooperatives and its amounts, value, and payments. See [section 2124 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2124].

    Section 1134c, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title IV, § 41, [48 Stat. 264][/us/stat/48/264]; [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 14][/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164/s14], [49 Stat. 317][/us/stat/49/317]; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 36][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s36], [50 Stat. 717][/us/stat/50/717]; [Oct. 31, 1949, ch. 792][/us/act/1949-10-31/ch792], title IV, § 417(a), [63 Stat. 1058][/us/stat/63/1058]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, § 107, [69 Stat. 662][/us/stat/69/662]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 105(k), [70 Stat. 665][/us/stat/70/665]; Aug. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–525, § 4(a)][/us/pl/89/525/s4/a], [80 Stat. 334][/us/stat/80/334]; Dec. 15, 1967, [Pub.     Section 1134d, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title IV, § 42, [48 Stat. 264][/us/stat/48/264]; [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 13][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s13], [67 Stat. 396][/us/stat/67/396]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title I, § 101, [69 Stat. 655][/us/stat/69/655], provided for stock of banks for cooperatives. See [section 2124 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2124].

    Section 1134e, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title IV, § 43, [48 Stat. 265][/us/stat/48/265]; Oct. 17, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–582, § 3][/us/pl/90/582/s3], [82 Stat. 1145][/us/stat/82/1145], provided for retirement of stock in banks for cooperatives held by the government. See [section 2126 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2126].
