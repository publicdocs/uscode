## §§ 652 to 655. Repealed. [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 19][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s19], [67 Stat. 400][/us/stat/67/400]

    Sections, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245], title I, § 3 (part), [39 Stat. 360][/us/stat/39/360], related to appointment, compensation, and oath of Land Bank Commissioner, restriction on his right to engage in certain business and filling vacancies.

    Section 652 was amended by [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252][/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252], title III, § 301, [42 Stat. 1473][/us/stat/42/1473]; Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; and [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title VIII, § 80(a), [48 Stat. 273][/us/stat/48/273].

    Sections 653, 654, and 655 were each amended by Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; and [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title VIII, § 80(a), [48 Stat. 273][/us/stat/48/273].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective 120 days after Aug. 6, 1953, see [act Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 18][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s18], [67 Stat. 399][/us/stat/67/399].
