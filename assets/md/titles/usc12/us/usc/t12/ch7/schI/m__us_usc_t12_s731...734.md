## §§ 731 to 734. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V][/us/pl/92/181/tV], § 5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624][/us/stat/85/624]

    Section 731, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245], title I, § 8, [39 Stat. 367][/us/stat/39/367]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I][/us/pl/86/168/tI], § 104(h), [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387], set a $5 par value for shares of land bank association stock. See [section 2031 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2031].

    Section 732, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245], title I, § 8, [39 Stat. 367][/us/stat/39/367]; [Aug. 9, 1937, ch. 704][/us/act/1937-08-09/ch704], § 23, [50 Stat. 710][/us/stat/50/710], set out voting privileges for land bank association stock. See [section 2034 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2034].

    Section 733, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245], title I, § 8, [39 Stat. 367][/us/stat/39/367]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I][/us/pl/86/168/tI], § 104(h), [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387], limited borrowers to members. See [section 2034 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2034].

    Section 734, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245], title I, § 8, [39 Stat. 367][/us/stat/39/367]; Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title III, § 301(c), [69 Stat. 664][/us/stat/69/664]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I][/us/pl/86/168/tI], § 104(h), [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387], provided for increases in stock. See [section 2034 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2034].
