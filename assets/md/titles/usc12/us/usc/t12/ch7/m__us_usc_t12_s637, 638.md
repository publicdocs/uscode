## §§ 637, 638. Repealed. [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 19][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s19], [67 Stat. 400][/us/stat/67/400]

    Section 637, acts [May 12, 1933, ch. 25][/us/act/1933-05-12/ch25], title II, § 39, [48 Stat. 50][/us/stat/48/50]; [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title VIII, § 80(a), [48 Stat. 273][/us/stat/48/273], related to certain functions, powers, authority, and duties of Land Bank Commissioner.

    Section 638, [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title VIII, § 80, [48 Stat. 273][/us/stat/48/273], changed name of office of Farm Loan Commissioner to Land Bank Commissioner, contained provisions relating to his term of office, and contained provisions relating to appointment (within the Farm Credit Administration), compensation, expenses and duties of a Production Credit Commissioner, a Cooperative Bank Commissioner, and an Intermediate Credit Commissioner.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective 120 days after Aug. 6, 1953, see [act Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 18][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s18], [67 Stat. 399][/us/stat/67/399].
