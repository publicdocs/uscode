## CHAPTER 7—FARM CREDIT ADMINISTRATION

 __Codification__ 

    The bulk of this chapter was repealed by [Pub. L. 92–181, § 5][/us/pl/92/181/s5].26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 625][/us/stat/85/625], which completely rewrote the farm credit laws and represented a fundamental reworking of the statutory basis for the farm credit system. The farm credit system is covered in chapter 23 (§ 2001 et seq.) of this title. See notes set out under [section 2001 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2001].
