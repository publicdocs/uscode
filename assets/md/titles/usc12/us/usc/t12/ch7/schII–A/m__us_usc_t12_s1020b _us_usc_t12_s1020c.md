###### §§ 1020b, 1020c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–353][/us/pl/87/353], § 3(a), Oct. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 773][/us/stat/75/773]

    Section 1020b, acts [Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7][/us/act/1934-01-31/ch7], § 3, [48 Stat. 345][/us/stat/48/345]; [June 25, 1940, ch. 427][/us/act/1940-06-25/ch427], § 4, [54 Stat. 573][/us/stat/54/573]; [June 30, 1945, ch. 204][/us/act/1945-06-30/ch204], § 14, [59 Stat. 269][/us/stat/59/269]; [July 12, 1946, ch. 570][/us/act/1946-07-12/ch570], § 2, [60 Stat. 532][/us/stat/60/532], prescribed the capital of the Federal Farm Mortgage Corporation to be in the sum of $200,000,000 and provided for subscription by the United States and repayment by the Corporation.

    Section 1020c, acts [Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7][/us/act/1934-01-31/ch7], § 4(a), [48 Stat. 345][/us/stat/48/345]; [Apr. 27, 1934, ch. 168][/us/act/1934-04-27/ch168], § 14, [48 Stat. 647][/us/stat/48/647], authorized the issuance of bonds not exceeding $2,000,000,000 at any one time and provided for purchase and sale by United States and exchange of such bonds for consolidated farm loan bonds.
