## § 1131g–1. Repealed. [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 105(q), [70 Stat. 666][/us/stat/70/666]

    Section, [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title VIII, § 86a, as added [June 27, 1934, ch. 847][/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847], title V, § 504, [48 Stat. 1263][/us/stat/48/1263], which was formerly designated [section 1131gg of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1131gg], authorized production credit associations to make loans to farmers for home alterations, repairs and improvements without purchase of class B stock, and permitted associations to sell, discount, assign, or otherwise dispose of such loans.

    Section 1131g–2, acts [June 18, 1934, ch. 574][/us/act/1934-06-18/ch574], [48 Stat. 983][/us/stat/48/983]; [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 17(c)][/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164/s17/c], [49 Stat. 318][/us/stat/49/318], provided for loans to oyster planters and purchase and discount of paper by intermediate credit banks.
