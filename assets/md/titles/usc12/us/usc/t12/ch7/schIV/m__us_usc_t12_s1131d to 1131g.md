## §§ 1131d to 1131g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5][/us/pl/92/181/s5].26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624][/us/stat/85/624]

    Section 1131d, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title II, § 20, [48 Stat. 259][/us/stat/48/259]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 105(f), [70 Stat. 665][/us/stat/70/665], authorized organization of production credit associations and provided for charters, bylaws, powers of governor, and other powers of such associations. See [section 2093 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2093].

    Section 1131e, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title II, § 21, [48 Stat. 260][/us/stat/48/260]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title II, § 201, [69 Stat. 663][/us/stat/69/663]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 105(g), [70 Stat. 665][/us/stat/70/665], provided for capital stock of production credit associations and its value, classes, voting rights, limitations on transfer, exchange, and dividends. See [section 2094 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2094].

    Section 1131e–1, acts [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 16][/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s16], [67 Stat. 399][/us/stat/67/399]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 107(b), [70 Stat. 666][/us/stat/70/666]; Oct. 17, 1968, [Pub.     Section 1131f, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title II, § 22, [48 Stat. 261][/us/stat/48/261]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title II, § 202, [69 Stat. 663][/us/stat/69/663]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 105(h), [70 Stat. 665][/us/stat/70/665]; Oct. 3, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–343, § 2(2)][/us/pl/87/343/s2/2], [75 Stat. 758][/us/stat/75/758]; Oct. 4, 1965, [Pub. See [section 2095 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2095].

    Section 1131g, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title II, § 23, [48 Stat. 261][/us/stat/48/261]; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785], title II, § 204, [69 Stat. 663][/us/stat/69/663]; [July 26, 1956, ch. 741][/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741], title I, § 105(i), [70 Stat. 665][/us/stat/70/665]; Oct. 4, 1965, [Pub. See [section 2096 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2096].
