###### §§ 639, 640. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V][/us/pl/92/181/tV], § 5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624][/us/stat/85/624]

    Section 639, [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VIII][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98/tVIII], § 82, [48 Stat. 273][/us/stat/48/273], made a supplementary grant of powers to Governor of Farm Credit Administration.

    Section 640, [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VIII][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98/tVIII], § 85, [48 Stat. 273][/us/stat/48/273], authorized Farm Credit Administration to have a seal. See [section 2248 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s2248].
