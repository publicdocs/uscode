    Section 984, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI], § 31, [39 Stat. 382][/us/stat/39/382], related to fraud and embezzlement. See sections 657 and 1006 of Title 18.

    Section 987, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI], § 31 (par.), as added [June 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98], title VII, § 78, [48 Stat. 272][/us/stat/48/272], related to false statements by mortgagee.
