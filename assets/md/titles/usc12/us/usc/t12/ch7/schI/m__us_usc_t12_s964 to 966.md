## §§ 964 to 966. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5][/us/pl/92/181/s5].26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624][/us/stat/85/624]

    Section 964, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245], title I, § 29, [39 Stat. 381][/us/stat/39/381]; Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [July 16, 1933, ch. 98][/us/act/1933-07-16/ch98], title VIII, § 80(a), [48 Stat. 273][/us/stat/48/273]; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)][/us/pl/86/168/s104/h], [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387], provided for disposition of land bank association’s stock in land bank on declaration of insolvency and appointment of receiver for association.

    Section 965, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245], title I, § 29, [39 Stat. 381][/us/stat/39/381]; Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)][/us/pl/86/168/s104/h], [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387], required written consent from Farm Credit Administration for a land bank, land bank association, or joint stock land bank to go into voluntary liquidation.

    Section 966, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245][/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245], title I, § 29 (par.), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252][/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252], title III, § 311, [42 Stat. 1748][/us/stat/42/1748], and amended Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)][/us/pl/86/168/s104/h], [73 Stat. 387][/us/stat/73/387], provided for disposition of a land bank association’s stock in land bank on voluntary liquidation of an association and for personal liability of members.
