## §§ 33 to 34c. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    [Act Nov. 7, 1918, ch. 209][/us/act/1918-11-07/ch209], [40 Stat. 1043][/us/stat/40/1043], as amended, formerly classified to sections 33 to 34c of this title, which related to consolidation and merger of national banking associations and such associations and State banks, was completely amended by [Pub. L. 86–230][/us/pl/86/230], § 20, Sept. 8 1959 [73 Stat. 460][/us/stat/73/460], and is classified to sections 215 to 215b of this title.

    Section 33, acts [Nov. 7, 1918, ch. 209][/us/act/1918-11-07/ch209], § 1, [40 Stat. 1043][/us/stat/40/1043]; [June 16, 1933, ch. 89][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch89], § 24(a), [48 Stat. 190][/us/stat/48/190]; [Aug. 23, 1935, ch. 614][/us/act/1935-08-23/ch614], § 330, [49 Stat. 718][/us/stat/49/718], related to consolidation of national banks, capital stock, dissenting shareholders, notice and valuation of shares. See [section 215 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s215].

    Section 34, [act Nov. 7, 1918, ch. 209][/us/act/1918-11-07/ch209], § 2, [40 Stat. 1044][/us/stat/40/1044], related to effect of consolidation on rights and liabilities. See [section 215 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s215].

    Section 34a, [act Nov. 7, 1918, ch. 209][/us/act/1918-11-07/ch209], § 3, as added [Feb. 25, 1927, ch. 191][/us/act/1927-02-25/ch191], § 1, [44 Stat. 1225][/us/stat/44/1225], and amended [June 16, 1933, ch. 89][/us/act/1933-06-16/ch89] § 24, [48 Stat. 190][/us/stat/48/190]; [Aug. 23, 1935, ch. 614][/us/act/1935-08-23/ch614], § 331, [49 Stat. 719][/us/stat/49/719]; [July 14, 1952, ch. 722][/us/act/1952-07-14/ch722], § 2, [66 Stat. 601][/us/stat/66/601], related to consolidation of State bank, etc. with national bank, capital stock and dissenting shareholders. See [section 215 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s215].

    Section 34b, [act Nov. 7, 1918, ch. 209][/us/act/1918-11-07/ch209], § 4, as added [July 14, 1952, ch. 722][/us/act/1952-07-14/ch722], § 1, [66 Stat. 599][/us/stat/66/599], related to merger of national banking associations or State banks into national banking associations. See [section 215a of this title][/us/usc/t12/s215a].

    Section 34c, [act Nov. 7, 1918, ch. 209][/us/act/1918-11-07/ch209], § 5, as added [July 14, 1952, ch. 722][/us/act/1952-07-14/ch722], § 1, 66, Stat. 601, related to definitions. See [section 215b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s215b].
