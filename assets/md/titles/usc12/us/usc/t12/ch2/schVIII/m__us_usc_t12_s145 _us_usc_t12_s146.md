###### §§ 145, 146. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], § 5(b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068][/us/stat/96/1068]

    Section 145, [act July 14, 1890, ch. 708][/us/act/1890-07-14/ch708], § 2, [26 Stat. 289][/us/stat/26/289], authorized counting of treasury notes held by national banking associations as part of their lawful reserve.

    Section 146, [act July 12, 1882, ch. 290][/us/act/1882-07-12/ch290], § 12, [22 Stat. 165][/us/stat/22/165], related to holding of gold and silver certificates by national banking associations.
