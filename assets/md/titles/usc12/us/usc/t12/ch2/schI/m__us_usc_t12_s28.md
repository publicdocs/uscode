## § 28. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–325, title VI, § 602(e)(1)][/us/pl/103/325/s602/e/1], Sept. 23, 1994, [108 Stat. 2291][/us/stat/108/2291]

    Section, R.S. § 5170, required publication of certificate of authority to commence banking for 60 days after issuance.

 __Codification__ 

    R.S. § 5170 derived from [act June 3, 1864, ch. 106, § 18][/us/act/1864-06-03/ch106/s18], [13 Stat. 104][/us/stat/13/104], which was the National Bank Act. See [section 38 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s38].
