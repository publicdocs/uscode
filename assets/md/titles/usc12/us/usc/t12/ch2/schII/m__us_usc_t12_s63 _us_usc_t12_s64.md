###### §§ 63, 64. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–230][/us/pl/86/230], § 7, Sept. 8, 1959, [73 Stat. 457][/us/stat/73/457]

    Section 63, R.S. § 5151, related to individual liability of shareholders.

    Section 64, [act Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6][/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6], § 23, [38 Stat. 273][/us/stat/38/273], related to transfer of shares as affecting individual liability of shareholders. Limitation on liability of shareholders, see [section 64a of this title][/us/usc/t12/s64a].

    The status of former [section 63 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s63] had been doubtful. At different times it had been held to have been repealed, superseded, and superseded only in part by former [section 64 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s64] which related to the same subject. See American T. Co. v. Grut, C.C.A. 1935, 80 F.2d 155; Miller v. Hamner, C.C.A. 1920, 269 F. 891; and First Nat. Bank v. First Nat. Bank, D.C. 1926, 14 F.2d 129.
