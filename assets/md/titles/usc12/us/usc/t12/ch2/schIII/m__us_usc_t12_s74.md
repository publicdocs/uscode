## § 74. Vacancies

    Any vacancy in the board shall be filled by appointment by the remaining directors, and any director so appointed shall hold his place until the next election.

(R.S. § 5148.)

 __Codification__ 

    R.S. § 5148 derived from [act June 3, 1864, ch. 106, § 10][/us/act/1864-06-03/ch106/s10], [13 Stat. 102][/us/stat/13/102], which was the National Bank Act. See [section 38 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s38].
