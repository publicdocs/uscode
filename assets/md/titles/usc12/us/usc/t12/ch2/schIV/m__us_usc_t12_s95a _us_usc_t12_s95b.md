###### §§ 95a, 95b. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 95a, [act Oct. 6, 1917, ch. 106][/us/act/1917-10-06/ch106], § 5(b), [40 Stat. 415][/us/stat/40/415]; [Sept. 24, 1918, ch. 176][/us/act/1918-09-24/ch176], § 5, [40 Stat. 966][/us/stat/40/966]; [Mar. 9, 1933, ch. 1][/us/act/1933-03-09/ch1], title I, § 2, [48 Stat. 1][/us/stat/48/1]; [May 7, 1940, ch. 185][/us/act/1940-05-07/ch185], § 1, [54 Stat. 179][/us/stat/54/179]; [Dec. 18, 1941, ch. 593][/us/act/1941-12-18/ch593], title III, § 301, [55 Stat. 839][/us/stat/55/839]; Proc. No. 2695, eff. July 4, 1946, 11 F.R. 7517, [69 Stat. 1352][/us/stat/69/1352]; [Pub. L. 95–223, title I][/us/pl/95/223/tI], §§ 101(a), 102, 103(b), Dec. 28, 1977, [91 Stat. 1625][/us/stat/91/1625], 1626; [Pub. L. 100–418, title II][/us/pl/100/418/tII], § 2502(a)(1), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1371][/us/stat/102/1371]; [Pub. L. 103–236, title V][/us/pl/103/236/tV], § 525(b)(1), Apr. 30, 1994, [108 Stat. 474][/us/stat/108/474], which related to regulation of transactions in foreign exchange of gold and silver, property transfers, vested interests, enforcement and penalties, was omitted because § 5(b) of act Oct. 6, 1917, is also classified to [section 4305(b) of Title 50][/us/usc/t50/s4305/b], War and National Defense.

    Section 95b, [act Mar. 9, 1933, ch. 1, title I][/us/act/1933-03-09/ch1/tI], § 1, [48 Stat. 1][/us/stat/48/1], which related to ratification of acts of President and Secretary of the Treasury, was omitted because § 1 of act Mar. 9, 1933, is also set out as a note under [section 4305 of Title 50][/us/usc/t50/s4305], War and National Defense.
