## § 395. Federal reserve banks as depositaries, custodians and fiscal agents for Commodity Credit Corporation

    The Federal Reserve banks are authorized to act as depositaries, custodians, and fiscal agents for the Commodity Credit Corporation.

([July 16, 1943, ch. 241, § 3][/us/act/1943-07-16/ch241/s3], [57 Stat. 566][/us/stat/57/566].)

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    Administration of program of Commodity Credit Corporation transferred to Secretary of Agriculture by Reorg. Plan No. 3 of 1946, § 501, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7877, [60 Stat. 1100][/us/stat/60/1100]. See Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

 __Exceptions From Transfer of Functions__ 

    Functions of Corporations of Department of Agriculture, boards of directors and officers of such corporations, Advisory Board of Commodity Credit Corporation, and Farm Credit Administration or any agency, officer or entity of, under, or subject to supervision of Administration were excepted from functions of officers, agencies, and employees transferred to Secretary of Agriculture by Reorg. Plan No. 2 of 1953, § 1, eff. June 4, 1953, 18 F.R. 3219, [67 Stat. 633][/us/stat/67/633], set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
