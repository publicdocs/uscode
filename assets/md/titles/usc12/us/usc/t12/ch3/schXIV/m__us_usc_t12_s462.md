## § 462. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, acts [Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 19][/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s19] (par.), [38 Stat. 270][/us/stat/38/270]; [Aug. 15, 1914, ch. 252][/us/act/1914-08-15/ch252], [38 Stat. 691][/us/stat/38/691]; [June 21, 1917, ch. 32, § 10][/us/act/1917-06-21/ch32/s10], [40 Stat. 239][/us/stat/40/239]; [Sept. 26, 1918, ch. 177, § 4][/us/act/1918-09-26/ch177/s4], [40 Stat. 970][/us/stat/40/970]; July 28, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–114][/us/pl/86/114], §§ 1, 2(a), 3(b)(7)–(9), [73 Stat. 863][/us/stat/73/863], which related to balances member banks were required to keep in reserve banks, was omitted from the Code in view of the striking out of second through fifth pars. of section 19 of act Dec. 23, 1913 (formerly comprising this section), and incorporation of provisions of such paragraphs in subsecs. (a) to (c) of section 19 of act Dec. 23, 1913 by [section 2(a) of Pub. L. 89–597][/us/pl/89/597/s2/a], Sept. 21, 1966, [80 Stat. 823][/us/stat/80/823]. See [section 461 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s461].
