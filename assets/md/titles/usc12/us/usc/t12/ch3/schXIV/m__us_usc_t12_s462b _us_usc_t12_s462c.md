###### §§ 462b, 462c. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 462b, [act Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6][/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6], § 19 (par.), as added [May 12, 1933, ch. 25][/us/act/1933-05-12/ch25], title III, § 46, [48 Stat. 54][/us/stat/48/54]; amended [Aug. 23, 1935, ch. 614][/us/act/1935-08-23/ch614], title II, § 207, [49 Stat. 706][/us/stat/49/706]; [July 7, 1942, ch. 488][/us/act/1942-07-07/ch488], § 2, [56 Stat. 648][/us/stat/56/648]; July 28, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–114][/us/pl/86/114], §§ 2(b), 3(b)(10), (11), [73 Stat. 263][/us/stat/73/263], 264, related to change of requirements as to reserves in order to prevent credit expansion or contraction, and was omitted from the Code in view of the striking out of the sixth par. of section 19 of act Dec. 23, 1913 (formerly comprising this section), and incorporation of its provisions in subsecs. (a) to (c) of section 19 of act Dec. 23, 1913 by [section 2(a) of Pub. L. 89–597][/us/pl/89/597/s2/a], Sept. 21, 1966, [80 Stat. 823][/us/stat/80/823]. See [section 461 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s461].

    Section 462c, [act Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6][/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6], § 19 (par.), as added [Aug. 16, 1948, ch. 836][/us/act/1948-08-16/ch836], § 2, [62 Stat. 1291][/us/stat/62/1291], related to change of requirements as to reserves to check credit expansion, and terminated on June 30, 1949.
