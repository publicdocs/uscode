## § 1438. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 22, 1932, ch. 522][/us/act/1932-07-22/ch522], § 18, [47 Stat. 737][/us/stat/47/737]; [Pub. L. 89–754, title X][/us/pl/89/754/tX], § 1016(b), Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1293][/us/stat/80/1293]; [Pub. L. 101–73, title VII][/us/pl/101/73/tVII], §§ 701(b)(2), (b)(3)(B), 711, 712, Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 412][/us/stat/103/412], 419; [Pub. L. 104–66, title II][/us/pl/104/66/tII], § 2191, Dec. 21, 1995, [109 Stat. 732][/us/stat/109/732]; [Pub. L. 106–102, title VI][/us/pl/106/102/tVI], § 606(h), Nov. 12, 1999, [113 Stat. 1455][/us/stat/113/1455]; [Pub. L. 108–271][/us/pl/108/271], § 8(b), July 7, 2004, [118 Stat. 814][/us/stat/118/814]; [Pub. L. 110–289, div. A, title II][/us/pl/110/289/dA/tII], § 1204(2), July 30, 2008, [122 Stat. 2786][/us/stat/122/2786]; [Pub. L. 111–203, title III][/us/pl/111/203/tIII], § 364(a), July 21, 2010, [124 Stat. 1555][/us/stat/124/1555], was omitted in view of the repeal of subsecs. (a) to (c) which comprised this section. Subsec. (a), which related to authorization of appropriations for certain expenses of the Federal Home Loan Bank Board, was repealed by [Pub. L. 101–73, title VII][/us/pl/101/73/tVII], § 712, Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 419][/us/stat/103/419]. Subsec. (b), which related to assessments for administrative expenses of the Federal Housing Finance Board, was repealed by [Pub. L. 110–289, div. A, title II][/us/pl/110/289/dA/tII], § 1204(2), July 30, 2008, [122 Stat. 2786][/us/stat/122/2786]. Subsec. (c), which related to acquisition of property by the Director of the Office of Thrift Supervision, was repealed by [Pub. L. 111–203, title III][/us/pl/111/203/tIII], § 364(a), July 21, 2010, [124 Stat. 1555][/us/stat/124/1555].

 __Effective Date of 2010 Amendment__ 

    [Pub. L. 111–203, title III][/us/pl/111/203/tIII], § 364(a), July 21, 2010, [124 Stat. 1555][/us/stat/124/1555], provided that, effective 90 days after the transfer date, subsection (c) of this section is repealed. For definition of “transfer date”, see [section 5301 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s5301].
