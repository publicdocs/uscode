## § 1445. Succession of Federal Home Loan Banks

    Each Federal Home Loan Bank shall have succession until dissolved by the Director under this chapter or by further act of Congress.

([July 22, 1932, ch. 522][/us/act/1932-07-22/ch522], § 25, [47 Stat. 740][/us/stat/47/740]; [Pub. L. 101–73, title VII][/us/pl/101/73/tVII], § 701(b)(1), (3)(A), Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 412][/us/stat/103/412]; [Pub. L. 110–289, div. A, title II][/us/pl/110/289/dA/tII], § 1204(8), July 30, 2008, [122 Stat. 2786][/us/stat/122/2786].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2008—[Pub. L. 110–289][/us/pl/110/289] substituted “the Director” for “the Board”.

    1989—[Pub. L. 101–73][/us/pl/101/73] substituted “Board” for “board”.
