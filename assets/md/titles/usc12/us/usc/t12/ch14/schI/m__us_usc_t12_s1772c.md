## § 1772c. Trust fund

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, all moneys of the Board shall be treated as trust funds for the purpose of section 906(a)(2)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of title 2. This section is effective for fiscal year 1986 and every fiscal year thereafter.

([June 26, 1934, ch. 750][/us/act/1934-06-26/ch750], title I, § 129, as added [Pub. L. 100–86, title VII][/us/pl/100/86/tVII], § 708, Aug. 10, 1987, [101 Stat. 653][/us/stat/101/653].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 906(a) of title 2][/us/usc/t2/s906/a], referred to in text, was amended generally by [Pub. L. 101–508, title XIII][/us/pl/101/508/tXIII], § 13101(d)(1), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1388–589][/us/stat/104/1388-589], and subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 111–139, title I][/us/pl/111/139/tI], § 10(a), Feb. 12, 2010, [124 Stat. 21][/us/stat/124/21]. Provisions similar to those formerly appearing in section 906(a)(2) are now contained in [section 906(k)(6) of Title 2][/us/usc/t2/s906/k/6], The Congress.
