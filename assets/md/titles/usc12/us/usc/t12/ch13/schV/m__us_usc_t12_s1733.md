## § 1733. Application of other laws

    The provisions of section 1430(a)(1)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  and 1430b of this title; the seventh paragraph of [section 24 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s24]; [section 371 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s371]; subsection (n) of section 77B of the Bankruptcy Act, as amended ([49 Stat. 664][/us/stat/49/664]); [section 606i of title 15][/us/usc/t15/s606i], continuing and extending the functions of the Reconstruction Finance Corporation; and all other provisions of law establishing rights under mortgages insured in accordance with the provisions of this chapter, shall be held to apply to such chapter, as amended.

([June 27, 1934, ch. 847][/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847], title V, § 514, as added [Feb. 3, 1938, ch. 13, § 11][/us/act/1938-02-03/ch13/s11], [52 Stat. 26][/us/stat/52/26].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 1430(a) of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1430/a], referred to in text, was amended by [Pub. L. 106–102, title VI, § 604(a)][/us/pl/106/102/s604/a], Nov. 12, 1999, [113 Stat. 1451][/us/stat/113/1451], and, as so amended, the provisions formerly appearing in section 1430(a)(1) now appear in section 1430(a)(3)(A).

    Section 77B of the Bankruptcy Act, referred to in text, was classified to section 207 of former Title 11, Bankruptcy. The Bankruptcy Act ([act July 1, 1898, ch. 541][/us/act/1898-07-01/ch541], [30 Stat. 544][/us/stat/30/544], as amended), which was classified generally to former Title 11, was repealed effective Oct. 1, 1979, by [Pub. L. 95–598][/us/pl/95/598], §§ 401(a), 402(a), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2682][/us/stat/92/2682], section 101 of which enacted Revised Title 11. See table preceding section 101 of Revised Title 11.

    [Section 606i of title 15][/us/usc/t15/s606i], referred to in text, was omitted from the Code. See note under [section 606i of Title 15][/us/usc/t15/s606i], Commerce and Trade.

 __Abolition of Reconstruction Finance Corporation__ 

    See section 6(a) of Reorg. Plan No. 1 of 1957, effective June 30, 1957, 22 F.R. 4633, [71 Stat. 647][/us/stat/71/647], set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
