## § 1747i. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–117, title XI][/us/pl/89/117/tXI], § 1108(aa), Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 507][/us/stat/79/507]

    Section, [act June 27, 1934, ch. 847, title VII][/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847/tVII], § 710, as added [Aug. 10, 1948, ch. 832][/us/act/1948-08-10/ch832], title IV, § 401, [62 Stat. 1280][/us/stat/62/1280]; amended [Apr. 20, 1950, ch. 94][/us/act/1950-04-20/ch94], title I, § 122, [64 Stat. 59][/us/stat/64/59], created the Housing Investment Insurance Fund, provided for the transfer of funds thereto, and authorized the payment of claims and the issue and cancellation of debentures.

    For establishment of the General Insurance Fund, see [section 1735c of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1735c].
