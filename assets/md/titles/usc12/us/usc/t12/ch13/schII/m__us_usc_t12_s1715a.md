## § 1715a. Repealed. [June 3, 1939, ch. 175][/us/act/1939-06-03/ch175], § 13, [53 Stat. 807][/us/stat/53/807]

    Section, [act June 27, 1934, ch. 847, title II][/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847/tII], § 210, as added by [act Feb. 3, 1938, ch. 13][/us/act/1938-02-03/ch13], § 3, [52 Stat. 22][/us/stat/52/22], related to additional housing insurance.

 __Applications Prior to Repeal__ 

[Act June 3, 1939, ch. 175][/us/act/1939-06-03/ch175], § 13, [53 Stat. 807][/us/stat/53/807], which repealed this section, also provided: 

> “That the Administrator is authorized to insure under said section \[this section\] any mortgage for the insurance of which an application has been filed with him prior to the effective date of this act \[June 3, 1939\].”
