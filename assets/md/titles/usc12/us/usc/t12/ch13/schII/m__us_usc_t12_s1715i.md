## § 1715i. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–372, title I][/us/pl/86/372/tI], § 108, Sept. 23, 1959, [73 Stat. 657][/us/stat/73/657]

    Section, [act June 27, 1934, ch. 847, title II][/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847/tII], § 218, as added [July 14, 1952, ch. 723][/us/act/1952-07-14/ch723], § 8, [66 Stat. 603][/us/stat/66/603], authorized a credit for application fees paid in cases where an application for mortgage insurance under [section 1743 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1743] was received on or before March 1, 1950, and the mortgagee applied for insurance of a mortgage under [section 1713 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1713] with respect to the same property or project.
