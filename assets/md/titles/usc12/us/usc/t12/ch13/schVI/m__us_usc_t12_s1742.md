## § 1742. Rules and regulations

    The Secretary is authorized and directed to make such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

([June 27, 1934, ch. 847][/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847], title VI, § 607, as added [Mar. 28, 1941, ch. 31, § 1][/us/act/1941-03-28/ch31/s1], [55 Stat. 61][/us/stat/55/61]; amended [Apr. 20, 1950, ch. 94][/us/act/1950-04-20/ch94], title I, § 122, [64 Stat. 59][/us/stat/64/59]; [Pub. L. 90–19, § 1(a)(3)][/us/pl/90/19/s1/a/3], May 25, 1967, [81 Stat. 17][/us/stat/81/17].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1967—[Pub. L. 90–19][/us/pl/90/19] substituted “Secretary” for “Commissioner”.

    1950—Act Apr. 20, 1950, substituted “Commissioner” for “Administrator”.
