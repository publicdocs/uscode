## § 605. Repealed. [Aug. 23, 1935, ch. 614][/us/act/1935-08-23/ch614], title III, § 329, [49 Stat. 717][/us/stat/49/717]

    Section, [act Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 25][/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s25] (par.), [38 Stat. 273][/us/stat/38/273], as amended by [act Sept. 7, 1916, ch. 461][/us/act/1916-09-07/ch461], [39 Stat. 755][/us/stat/39/755], related to interlocking directors, officers, and employees. See [section 19 of Title 15][/us/usc/t15/s19], Commerce and Trade.

    Section was comprised of the former seventh undesignated par. of section 25 of act Dec. 23, 1913, which comprises this subchapter. For classification of other pars. of section 25 of this Act, see Codification note under [section 601 of this title][/us/usc/t12/s601].
