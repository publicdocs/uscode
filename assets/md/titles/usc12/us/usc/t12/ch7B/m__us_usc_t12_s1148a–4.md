## § 1148a–4. Security for economic disaster and special livestock loans

    Loans under section 1148a–2(b) and (c)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title shall be secured by the personal obligation and available security of the producer or producers, and in the case of loans to corporations or other business organizations, by the personal obligation and available security of each person holding as much as 10 per centum of the stock or other interest in the corporation or organization.

([July 14, 1953, ch. 192][/us/act/1953-07-14/ch192], § 2, [67 Stat. 150][/us/stat/67/150].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Section 1148a–2(b) and (c) of this title, referred to in text, was repealed by [Pub. L. 87–128, title III][/us/pl/87/128/tIII], § 341(a), Aug. 8, 1961, [75 Stat. 318][/us/stat/75/318].
