## § 1958. Compliance

    The Secretary shall have the responsibility to assure compliance with the requirements of this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b] and may delegate such responsibility to the appropriate bank supervisory agency, or other supervisory agency. The Secretary may rely on examinations conducted by a State supervisory agency of a category of financial institution, if the Secretary determines that the category of financial institution is required to comply with this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b] (and regulations prescribed under this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b]), or the State supervisory agency examines the category of financial institution for compliance with this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b] (and regulations prescribed under this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b]).

([Pub. L. 91–508, title I, § 128][/us/pl/91/508/s128], Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1118][/us/stat/84/1118]; [Pub. L. 113–156, § 2(b)][/us/pl/113/156/s2/b], Aug. 8, 2014, [128 Stat. 1829][/us/stat/128/1829].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2014—[Pub. L. 113–156][/us/pl/113/156] substituted “requirements of this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b]” for “requirements of this chapter and sections 1730d and 1829b of this title” and inserted at end “The Secretary may rely on examinations conducted by a State supervisory agency of a category of financial institution, if the Secretary determines that the category of financial institution is required to comply with this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b] (and regulations prescribed under this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b]), or the State supervisory agency examines the category of financial institution for compliance with this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b] (and regulations prescribed under this chapter and [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b]).”

 __Consultation With State Agencies__ 

[Pub. L. 113–156, § 2(c)][/us/pl/113/156/s2/c], Aug. 8, 2014, [128 Stat. 1830][/us/stat/128/1830], provided that: 

> “In issuing rules to carry out [section 5318(a)(6) of title 31][/us/usc/t31/s5318/a/6], United States Code, and [section 128 of Public Law 91–508][/us/pl/91/508/s128] ([12 U.S.C. 1958][/us/usc/t12/s1958]), the Secretary of the Treasury shall consult with State supervisory agencies.”
