## § 1958. Compliance

    The Secretary shall have the responsibility to assure compliance with the requirements of this chapter and sections 1730d  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  and 1829b of this title and may delegate such responsibility to the appropriate bank supervisory agency, or other supervisory agency.

([Pub. L. 91–508, title I, § 128][/us/pl/91/508/s128], Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1118][/us/stat/84/1118].)
