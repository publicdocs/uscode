## § 1959. Administrative procedure

    The administrative procedure and judicial review provisions of subchapter II of chapter 5 and chapter 7 of title 5 shall apply to all proceedings under this chapter, [section 1829b of this title][/us/usc/t12/s1829b], and section 1730d  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title.

([Pub. L. 91–508, title I][/us/pl/91/508/tI], § 129, Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1118][/us/stat/84/1118].)

 __References in Text__
