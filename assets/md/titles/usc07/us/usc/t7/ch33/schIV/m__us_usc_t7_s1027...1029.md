## §§ 1027 to 1029. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–128, title III][/us/pl/87/128/tIII], § 341(a), Aug. 8, 1961, [75 Stat. 318][/us/stat/75/318]

    Section 1027, acts [July 22, 1937, ch. 517][/us/act/1937-07-22/ch517], title IV, § 53, [50 Stat. 532][/us/stat/50/532]; [Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 964][/us/act/1946-08-14/ch964], § 3, [60 Stat. 1064][/us/stat/60/1064], related to fees and commissions.

    Section 1028, acts [July 22, 1937, ch. 517][/us/act/1937-07-22/ch517], title IV, § 54, [50 Stat. 532][/us/stat/50/532]; [Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 964][/us/act/1946-08-14/ch964], § 3, [60 Stat. 1064][/us/stat/60/1064]; [July 26, 1947, ch. 339][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch339], § 1(b), [61 Stat. 493][/us/stat/61/493], related to application of provisions to territories.

    Section 1029, acts [July 22, 1937, ch. 517][/us/act/1937-07-22/ch517], title IV, § 55, [50 Stat. 533][/us/stat/50/533]; [Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 964][/us/act/1946-08-14/ch964], § 3, [60 Stat. 1064][/us/stat/60/1064], related to separability of provisions.

    For subject matter of sections 1027 to 1029 of this title, see section 1921 et seq. of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective one hundred and twenty days after Aug. 8, 1961, or such earlier date as the provisions of chapter 50 of this title are made effective by regulations of Secretary of Agriculture, see [section 341(a) of Pub. L. 87–128][/us/pl/87/128/s341/a], set out as a note under [section 1921 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1921].

    Sections repealed effective Oct. 15, 1961, by former section 300.1 of Title 6, Code of Federal Regulations, see Effective Date note set out under [section 1921 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1921].
