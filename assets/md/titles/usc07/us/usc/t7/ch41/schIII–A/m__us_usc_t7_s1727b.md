## § 1727b. Grant programs

    To carry out the policies and accomplish the objectives described in [section 1691 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1691], the Administrator may negotiate and execute agreements with least developed countries to provide commodities to such countries on a grant basis either through the Commodity Credit Corporation or through private trade channels.

([July 10, 1954, ch. 469][/us/act/1954-07-10/ch469], title III, § 303, as added [Pub. L. 95–88, title II, § 211(a)(2)][/us/pl/95/88/s211/a/2], Aug. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 550][/us/stat/91/550]; amended [Pub. L. 96–53, title II, § 204(c)][/us/pl/96/53/s204/c], Aug. 14, 1979, [93 Stat. 369][/us/stat/93/369]; [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1512][/us/pl/101/624/s1512], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3642][/us/stat/104/3642].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 303 of [act July 10, 1954, ch. 469][/us/act/1954-07-10/ch469], title III, [68 Stat. 459][/us/stat/68/459], which enacted [section 1692 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1692], was renumbered section 310 by [Pub. L. 95–88, title II, § 211(a)(1)][/us/pl/95/88/s211/a/1], Aug. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 548][/us/stat/91/548].

 __Amendments__
