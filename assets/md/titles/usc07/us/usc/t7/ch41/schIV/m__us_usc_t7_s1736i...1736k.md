## §§ 1736i to 1736k. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV][/us/pl/101/624/tXV], § 1573, Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3702][/us/stat/104/3702]

    Section 1736i, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XII][/us/pl/97/98/tXII], § 1203, Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1275][/us/stat/95/1275], provided for a special standby export subsidy program.

    Section 1736j, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XII][/us/pl/97/98/tXII], § 1204, Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1276][/us/stat/95/1276]; [Pub. L. 99–198, title XI][/us/pl/99/198/tXI], § 1133(b), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1489][/us/stat/99/1489], provided for protection against agricultural embargo. See [section 5672 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s5672].

    Section 1736k, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XII][/us/pl/97/98/tXII], § 1205, Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1277][/us/stat/95/1277], provided for development of plans, recommendations, and programs to alleviate the adverse impact of export embargoes on agricultural commodities. See [section 5672 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s5672].
