###### §§ 1736s, 1736t. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV][/us/pl/101/624/tXV], § 1572(3), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3702][/us/stat/104/3702]

    Section 1736s, [Pub. L. 99–198, title XI][/us/pl/99/198/tXI], § 1124, Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1481][/us/stat/99/1481]; [Pub. L. 99–260][/us/pl/99/260], § 5, Mar. 20, 1986, [100 Stat. 49][/us/stat/100/49]; [Pub. L. 100–418, title IV][/us/pl/100/418/tIV], § 4304, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1397][/us/stat/102/1397]; [Pub. L. 101–239, title I][/us/pl/101/239/tI], § 1005(b), Dec. 19, 1989, [103 Stat. 2109][/us/stat/103/2109], provided for targeted export assistance for fiscal years 1986 through 1990.

    Section 1736t, [Pub. L. 99–198, title XI][/us/pl/99/198/tXI], § 1125, Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1482][/us/stat/99/1482]; [Pub. L. 100–418, title IV][/us/pl/100/418/tIV], § 4402(a), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1400][/us/stat/102/1400], provided for short-term export credits.

 __Export Credit Guarantee Program__ 

    [Pub. L. 100–418, title IV][/us/pl/100/418/tIV], § 4305, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1398][/us/stat/102/1398], which stated the sense of Congress that, to the extent that the Commodity Credit Corporation made a specified allocation of credit guarantees available under the export credit guarantee program referred to in section 1736t for short-term credit extended to finance the export sales of United States agricultural commodities and products, such allocation was to be made on a country-only basis and not on a commodity basis or a commodity and country basis, was repealed by [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV][/us/pl/101/624/tXV], § 1571, Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3702][/us/stat/104/3702].
