###### §§ 1736z, 1736aa. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV][/us/pl/101/624/tXV], § 1572(3), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3702][/us/stat/104/3702]

    Section 1736aa, [Pub. L. 99–198, title XI][/us/pl/99/198/tXI], § 1167(d), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1503][/us/stat/99/1503], provided for barter by exporters of agricultural commodities and products for foreign products needed by exporters.
