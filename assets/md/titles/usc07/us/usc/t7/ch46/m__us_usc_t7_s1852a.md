## § 1852a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–475][/us/pl/90/475], § 8, Aug. 11, 1968, [82 Stat. 703][/us/stat/82/703]

    Section, [Pub. L. 88–638][/us/pl/88/638], § 3, Oct. 8, 1964, [78 Stat. 1038][/us/stat/78/1038], authorized Commodity Credit Corporation to encourage export sales of extra long staple cotton which is in surplus supply at competitive world prices.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 90–475][/us/pl/90/475], § 8, Aug. 11, 1968, [82 Stat. 703][/us/stat/82/703], provided that the repeal of this section is effective Aug. 1, 1968.
