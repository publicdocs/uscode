## § 674. Short title

    This Act may be cited as the “Agricultural Marketing Agreement Act of 1937”.

([June 3, 1937, ch. 296][/us/act/1937-06-03/ch296], § 6, [50 Stat. 249][/us/stat/50/249].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This Act, referred to in text, is [act June 3, 1937, ch. 296][/us/act/1937-06-03/ch296], [50 Stat. 246][/us/stat/50/246], as amended, which among other things enacted this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.
