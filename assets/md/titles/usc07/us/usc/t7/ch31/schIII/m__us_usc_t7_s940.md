## § 940. Refinancing of rural development loans

    At the request of the borrower, the Secretary is authorized and directed to refinance with loans which will be insured under this chapter at the interest rates provided in [section 935 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s935] any loans made for rural electric and telephone facilities under any provision of the Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act \[[7 U.S.C. 1921][/us/usc/t7/s1921] et seq.\].

([May 20, 1936, ch. 432][/us/act/1936-05-20/ch432], title III, § 310, as added [Pub. L. 93–32][/us/pl/93/32], § 2, May 11, 1973, [87 Stat. 70][/us/stat/87/70]; amended [Pub. L. 103–354, title II][/us/pl/103/354/tII], § 235(a)(13), Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3221][/us/stat/108/3221].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act, referred to in text, is title III of [Pub. L. 87–128][/us/pl/87/128], Aug. 8, 1961, [75 Stat. 307][/us/stat/75/307], as amended, which is classified principally to chapter 50 (§ 1921 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of the Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1921 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1921] and Tables.

 __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–354][/us/pl/103/354] substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”.

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective May 11, 1973, see [section 12 of Pub. L. 93–32][/us/pl/93/32/s12], set out as a note under [section 930 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s930].
