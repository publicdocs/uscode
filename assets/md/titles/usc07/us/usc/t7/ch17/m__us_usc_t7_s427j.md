## § 427j. Repealed. [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 790][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch790], § 2, [69 Stat. 674][/us/stat/69/674]

    Section, [act June 29, 1935, ch. 338, title I][/us/act/1935-06-29/ch338/tI], § 11, as added [Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 966][/us/act/1946-08-14/ch966], title I, § 101(2), [60 Stat. 1086][/us/stat/60/1086]; amended [July 31, 1947, ch. 412][/us/act/1947-07-31/ch412], [61 Stat. 694][/us/stat/61/694]; [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1076][/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1076], § 1(7), [68 Stat. 966][/us/stat/68/966], authorized use of funds appropriated for agricultural research program for market research projects and required an annual report to Congress. See sections 361a to 361h of this title.

 __Existing Rights and Liabilities__ 

    Any rights or liabilities existing under this section as unaffected by repeal, see section 2 of act Aug. 11, 1955, set out as a note under [section 361 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s361].
