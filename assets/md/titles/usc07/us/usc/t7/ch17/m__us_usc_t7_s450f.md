## § 450f. Delegation of functions under other laws as unaffected

    The provisions of [section 450d of this title][/us/usc/t7/s450d] shall not be deemed to prohibit the delegation, under authority of any other provision of law, of the whole or any part of any regulatory function or other function to any officer or employee of the Department of Agriculture.

([Apr. 4, 1940, ch. 75, § 4][/us/act/1940-04-04/ch75/s4], [54 Stat. 82][/us/stat/54/82].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 516d of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s516d] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1][/us/pl/89/554/s1], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
