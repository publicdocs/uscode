## § 450g. Authorization of appropriations for cooperative research projects

    There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of sections 450c to 450g of this title.

([Apr. 4, 1940, ch. 75][/us/act/1940-04-04/ch75], § 5, [54 Stat. 82][/us/stat/54/82].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 516e of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s516e] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
