## § 1746. Records and accounts

    The Commodity Credit Corporation shall keep such records and accounts as may be necessary to show, for each commodity set-aside, the initial and current composition, value (in accordance with [section 1742 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1742]), current investment, quantity disposed of, method of disposition, and amounts received on disposition.

([Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041][/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041], title I, § 106, [68 Stat. 898][/us/stat/68/898].)
