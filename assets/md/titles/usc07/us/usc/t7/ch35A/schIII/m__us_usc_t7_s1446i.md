## § 1446i. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–354, title I, § 119(a)(6)][/us/pl/103/354/s119/a/6], Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3208][/us/stat/108/3208]

    Section, [act Oct. 31, 1949, ch. 792][/us/act/1949-10-31/ch792], title II, § 208, as added Nov. 28, 1990, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XI, § 1126][/us/pl/101/624/s1126], [104 Stat. 3507][/us/stat/104/3507], related to disaster payments for 1991 through 1995 crops of peanuts, soybeans, sugar beets, and sugarcane.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 13, 1994, and applicable to provision of crop insurance under Federal Crop Insurance Act ([7 U.S.C. 1501][/us/usc/t7/s1501] et seq.) beginning with 1995 crop year, with such Act, as in effect on the day before Oct. 13, 1994, to continue to apply with respect to 1994 crop year, see [section 120 of Pub. L. 103–354][/us/pl/103/354/s120], set out as an Effective Date of 1994 Amendment note under [section 1502 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1502].
