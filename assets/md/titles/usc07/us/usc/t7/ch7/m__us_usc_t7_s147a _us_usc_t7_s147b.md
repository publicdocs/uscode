###### §§ 147a, 147b. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 147a, acts [Sept. 21, 1944, ch. 412][/us/act/1944-09-21/ch412], title I, § 102, [58 Stat. 735][/us/stat/58/735], as amended, which related to fees for inspection of plants for exporting or transiting, was transferred to [section 7759 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s7759].

    Section 147b, [Pub. L. 97–46][/us/pl/97/46], § 1, Sept. 25, 1981, [95 Stat. 953][/us/stat/95/953], as amended, which related to transfer of funds for emergency arrest of animal or poultry diseases, was transferred to [section 129a of Title 21][/us/usc/t21/s129a], Food and Drugs, and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 107–171, title X][/us/pl/107/171/tX], § 10418(a)(1), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 507][/us/stat/116/507].
