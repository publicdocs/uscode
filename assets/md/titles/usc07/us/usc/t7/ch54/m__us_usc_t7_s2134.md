## § 2134. Valid license for dealers and exhibitors required

    No dealer or exhibitor shall sell or offer to sell or transport or offer for transportation, in commerce, to any research facility or for exhibition or for use as a pet any animal, or buy, sell, offer to buy or sell, transport or offer for transportation, in commerce, to or from another dealer or exhibitor under this chapter any animals, unless and until such dealer or exhibitor shall have obtained a license from the Secretary and such license shall not have been suspended or revoked.

([Pub. L. 89–544, § 4][/us/pl/89/544/s4], Aug. 24, 1966, [80 Stat. 351][/us/stat/80/351]; [Pub. L. 91–579, § 5][/us/pl/91/579/s5], Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1561][/us/stat/84/1561]; [Pub. L. 94–279, § 5][/us/pl/94/279/s5], Apr. 22, 1976, [90 Stat. 418][/us/stat/90/418].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–279][/us/pl/94/279] substituted “in commerce” for “affecting commerce” in two places.

    1970—[Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579] inserted references to exhibitors, offers to sell, and offers to transport, and substituted references to animals for references to dogs and cats.

 __Effective Date of 1970 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579] effective one year after Dec. 24, 1970, see [section 23 of Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579/s23], set out as a note under [section 2131 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2131].
