## § 2150. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–279, § 14][/us/pl/94/279/s14], Apr. 22, 1976, [90 Stat. 421][/us/stat/90/421]

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–544, § 20][/us/pl/89/544/s20], Aug. 24, 1966, [80 Stat. 353][/us/stat/80/353]; [Pub. L. 91–579, § 21][/us/pl/91/579/s21], Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1565][/us/stat/84/1565], provided for issuance of cease and desist orders if Secretary had reason to believe that any research facility had violated any provision of this chapter, provided for a civil penalty, and provided appeal mechanism by which aggrieved person may have judicial review of such final order by Secretary. See [section 2149 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2149].
