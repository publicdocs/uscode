## § 2135. Time period for disposal of dogs or cats by dealers or exhibitors

    No dealer or exhibitor shall sell or otherwise dispose of any dog or cat within a period of five business days after the acquisition of such animal or within such other period as may be specified by the Secretary: Provided, That operators of auction sales subject to [section 2142 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2142] shall not be required to comply with the provisions of this section.

([Pub. L. 89–544][/us/pl/89/544], § 5, Aug. 24, 1966, [80 Stat. 351][/us/stat/80/351]; [Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579], § 6, Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1561][/us/stat/84/1561].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1970—[Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579] inserted references to exhibitors and inserted proviso that operators of auction sales subject to [section 2142 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2142] shall not be required to comply with the provisions of this section.

 __Effective Date of 1970 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579] effective one year after Dec. 24, 1970, see [section 23 of Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579/s23], set out as a note under [section 2131 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2131].
