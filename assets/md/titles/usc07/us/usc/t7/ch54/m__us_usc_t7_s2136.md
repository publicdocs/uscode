## § 2136. Registration of research facilities, handlers, carriers and unlicensed exhibitors

    Every research facility, every intermediate handler, every carrier, and every exhibitor not licensed under [section 2133 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2133] shall register with the Secretary in accordance with such rules and regulations as he may prescribe.

([Pub. L. 89–544][/us/pl/89/544], § 6, Aug. 24, 1966, [80 Stat. 351][/us/stat/80/351]; [Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579], § 7, Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1561][/us/stat/84/1561]; [Pub. L. 94–279][/us/pl/94/279], § 6, Apr. 22, 1976, [90 Stat. 418][/us/stat/90/418].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–279][/us/pl/94/279] inserted “, every intermediate handler, every carrier,” after “research facility”.

    1970—[Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579] inserted reference to exhibitors not licensed under [section 2133 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2133].

 __Effective Date of 1970 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579] effective one year after Dec. 24, 1970, see [section 23 of Pub. L. 91–579][/us/pl/91/579/s23], set out as a note under [section 2131 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2131].
