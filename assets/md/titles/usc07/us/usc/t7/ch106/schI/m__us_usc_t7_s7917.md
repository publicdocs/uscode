## § 7917. Relation to remaining payment authority under production flexibility contracts

    (a) __Termination of superseded payment authority__ 

        Notwithstanding [section 7213(a)(7) of this title][/us/usc/t7/s7213/a/7] or any other provision of law, the Secretary shall not make payments for fiscal year 2002 after May 13, 2002, under a production flexibility contract entered into under [section 7211 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s7211] unless requested by the producer that is a party to the contract.

    (b) __Contract payments made before enactment__ 

        If a producer receives all or any portion of the payment authorized for fiscal year 2002 under a production flexibility contract, the Secretary shall reduce the amount of the direct payment otherwise due the producer for the 2002 crop year under [section 7913 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s7913] by the amount of the fiscal year 2002 payment received by the producer under the production flexibility contract.

([Pub. L. 107–171, title I][/us/pl/107/171/tI], § 1107, May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 154][/us/stat/116/154].)
