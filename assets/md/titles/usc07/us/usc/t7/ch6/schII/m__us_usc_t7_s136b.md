## § 136b. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act June 25, 1947, ch. 125, § 4][/us/act/1947-06-25/ch125/s4], as added Oct. 21, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–516, § 2][/us/pl/92/516/s2], [86 Stat. 983][/us/stat/86/983]; amended Nov. 28, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–140][/us/pl/94/140], §§ 5, 11, [89 Stat. 753][/us/stat/89/753], 754; Sept. 30, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–396, § 9][/us/pl/95/396/s9], [92 Stat. 827][/us/stat/92/827]; Oct. 25, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–532, title VIII, § 801(c)][/us/pl/100/532/s801/c], (q)(1)(A), (B), [102 Stat. 2681][/us/stat/102/2681], 2683, which related to use of restricted use pesticides and certification of applicators, was transferred to subsecs. (a) to (c) of section 11 of act June 25, 1947, by [section 801(q)(1)(A) of Pub. L. 100–532][/us/pl/100/532/s801/q/1/A] and is classified to section 136i(a) to (c) of this title.
