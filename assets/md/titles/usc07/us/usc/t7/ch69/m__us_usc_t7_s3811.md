## § 3811. Issuance of regulations; maintenance of rec­ords

    The Secretary is authorized to issue such regulations and to require the maintenance of such records as he deems necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

([Pub. L. 96–468, § 11][/us/pl/96/468/s11], formerly § 12, Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2233][/us/stat/94/2233]; renumbered § 11, [Pub. L. 104–127, title IX, § 914(b)(2)][/us/pl/104/127/s914/b/2], Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1186][/us/stat/110/1186].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 11 of Pub. L. 96–468][/us/pl/96/468/s11] was classified to [section 3810 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s3810] prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–127][/us/pl/104/127].
