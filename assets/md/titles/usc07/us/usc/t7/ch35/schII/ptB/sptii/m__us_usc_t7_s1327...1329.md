## §§ 1327 to 1329. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections provided for establishment of a commercial corn-producing area and corn acreage allotments, which were discontinued. See sections 1329a, 1444a, and 1444b of this title.

    Section 1327, acts [Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30][/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30], title III, § 327, [52 Stat. 51][/us/stat/52/51]; [Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041][/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041], title III, § 304, [68 Stat. 903][/us/stat/68/903], provided for proclamation of commercial corn-producing area not later than February 1 of each year.

    Section 1328, acts [Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30][/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30], title III, § 328, [52 Stat. 52][/us/stat/52/52]; [Apr. 7, 1938, ch. 107][/us/act/1938-04-07/ch107], § 6, [52 Stat. 202][/us/stat/52/202]; [July 3, 1948, ch. 827][/us/act/1948-07-03/ch827], title II, § 207(a), [62 Stat. 1257][/us/stat/62/1257]; [Oct. 31, 1949, ch. 792][/us/act/1949-10-31/ch792], title IV, § 409(f), [63 Stat. 1057][/us/stat/63/1057]; [Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041][/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041], title III, § 305, [68 Stat. 903][/us/stat/68/903], provided for establishment of acreage allotment of corn for each calendar year and proclamation of such acreage allotment not later than February 1 of each year.

    Section 1329, acts [Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30][/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30], title III, § 329, [52 Stat. 52][/us/stat/52/52]; [Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041][/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041], title III, § 306, [68 Stat. 903][/us/stat/68/903], provided for apportionment of acreage allotment for corn.
