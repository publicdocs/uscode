##### subpart i—publication and review of quotas

 __Inapplicability of Subpart__ 

    Subpart inapplicable to 1996 through 2001 crops of peanuts, see [section 7301(a)(1)(F) of this title][/us/usc/t7/s7301/a/1/F].

    [Pub. L. 101–624, title VIII][/us/pl/101/624/tVIII], § 801(4), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3459][/us/stat/104/3459], provided that subpart I of part C of this subchapter (§ 1361 et seq.) is inapplicable to 1991 through 1995 crops of peanuts.

    [Pub. L. 99–198, title VII][/us/pl/99/198/tVII], § 701(4), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1430][/us/stat/99/1430], provided that subpart I of part C of this subchapter (§ 1361 et seq.) is inapplicable to 1986 through 1990 crops of peanuts.

    [Pub. L. 97–98, title VII][/us/pl/97/98/tVII], § 701(4), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1248][/us/stat/95/1248], provided that subpart I of part C of this subchapter (§ 1361 et seq.) is inapplicable to 1982 through 1985 crops of peanuts.
