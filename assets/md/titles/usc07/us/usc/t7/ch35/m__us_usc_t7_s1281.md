## § 1281. Short title

    This chapter may be cited as the “Agricultural Adjustment Act of 1938”.

([Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30][/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30], § 1, [52 Stat. 31][/us/stat/52/31].)
