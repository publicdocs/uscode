## §§ 1381 to 1382. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 1381, acts [Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30][/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30], title III, § 381, [52 Stat. 66][/us/stat/52/66]; [Apr. 7, 1938, ch. 107][/us/act/1938-04-07/ch107], § 12, [52 Stat. 204][/us/stat/52/204], related to cotton price adjustment payments with respect to 1937 cotton crop, and to transfer of pledged cotton of 1937 crop to Commodity Credit Corporation. Subsec. (c) of section 1381, which authorized sale of pledged cotton by Commodity Credit Corporation, was repealed by [act July 3, 1948, ch. 827, title II][/us/act/1948-07-03/ch827/tII], § 202(b), [62 Stat. 1255][/us/stat/62/1255].

    Section 1381a, [act June 16, 1938, ch. 464, title I][/us/act/1938-06-16/ch464/tI], [52 Stat. 745][/us/stat/52/745], which was not a part of the Agricultural Adjustment Act of 1938, related only to payments for 1937 crops.

    Section 1382, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III][/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII], § 382, [52 Stat. 67][/us/stat/52/67], required the Commodity Credit Corporation to provide for the extension, from July 31, 1938, to July 31, 1939, of 1937 cotton loan.
