## § 1347. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–88, § 2][/us/pl/98/88/s2], Aug. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 494][/us/stat/97/494]

    Section, acts [Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30][/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30], title III, § 347, [52 Stat. 59][/us/stat/52/59]; [Aug. 29, 1949, ch. 518, § 1][/us/act/1949-08-29/ch518/s1], [63 Stat. 675][/us/stat/63/675]; [July 17, 1952, ch. 933, § 4][/us/act/1952-07-17/ch933/s4], [66 Stat. 759][/us/stat/66/759]; Aug. 28, 1958, [Pub. L. 90–475][/us/pl/90/475], §§ 4, 6, [82 Stat. 701][/us/stat/82/701], 702, set out a program for long staple cotton. See [section 1444(h) of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1444/h].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 98–88, § 2][/us/pl/98/88/s2], Aug. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 494][/us/stat/97/494], provided that the repeal of this section is effective beginning with the 1984 crop of extra long staple cotton.
