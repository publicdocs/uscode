## § 1301b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–835, title I, § 108][/us/pl/85/835/s108], Aug. 28, 1958, [72 Stat. 993][/us/stat/72/993]

    Section, [act Aug. 29, 1949, ch. 518, § 3(a)][/us/act/1949-08-29/ch518/s3/a], [63 Stat. 676][/us/stat/63/676], prescribed standard cotton grade for parity and price support purposes.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

[Pub. L. 85–835, title I, § 108][/us/pl/85/835/s108], Aug. 28, 1958, [72 Stat. 993][/us/stat/72/993], provided in part that: 

> “This section \[amending [section 1423 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1423] and repealing this section\] shall become effective with the 1961 crop.”
