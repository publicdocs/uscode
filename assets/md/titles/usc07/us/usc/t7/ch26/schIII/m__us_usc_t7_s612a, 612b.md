## §§ 612a, 612b. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 612a, [act Apr. 7, 1934, ch. 103, § 6][/us/act/1934-04-07/ch103/s6], [48 Stat. 528][/us/stat/48/528]; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. III, § 5, 5 F.R. 2108, [54 Stat. 1232][/us/stat/54/1232], authorized appropriation of $50,000,000 for purpose of dairy and beef products for distribution for relief purposes, and for elimination of diseased cattle.

    Section 612b, [act Aug. 24, 1935, ch. 641, § 37][/us/act/1935-08-24/ch641/s37], [49 Stat. 775][/us/stat/49/775], provided appropriations for elimination of disease in dairy and beef cattle to remain available until June 30, 1936.
