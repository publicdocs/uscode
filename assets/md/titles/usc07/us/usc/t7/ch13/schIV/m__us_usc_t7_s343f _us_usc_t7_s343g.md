###### §§ 343f, 343g. Repealed. [June 26, 1953, ch. 157][/us/act/1953-06-26/ch157], § 2, [67 Stat. 86][/us/stat/67/86]

    Sections, [act Aug. 28, 1937, ch. 878][/us/act/1937-08-28/ch878], §§ 1, 2, [50 Stat. 881][/us/stat/50/881], extended benefits of former [section 343c of this title][/us/usc/t7/s343c] to Puerto Rico, and for such purpose, authorized appropriations, commencing with initial authorization of $88,000 for the fiscal year beginning after August 28, 1937, and on an ascending scale thereafter, until they amounted to $408,000 annually. See sections 341 to 343, 344 to 346, and 347a to 349 of this title.
