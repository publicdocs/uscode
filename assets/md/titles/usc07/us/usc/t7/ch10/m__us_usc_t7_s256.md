## § 256. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

([Aug. 11, 1916, ch. 313][/us/act/1916-08-11/ch313], pt. C, § 17, as added [Pub. L. 106–472, title II, § 201][/us/pl/106/472/s201], Nov. 9, 2000, [114 Stat. 2068][/us/stat/114/2068].)
