## § 1781. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–130][/us/pl/103/130], § 4(a), Nov. 1, 1993, [107 Stat. 1369][/us/stat/107/1369]

    Section, acts [Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041][/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041], title VII, § 702, [68 Stat. 910][/us/stat/68/910]; Sept. 29, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–113, title III][/us/pl/95/113/tIII], § 301, [91 Stat. 921][/us/stat/91/921], declared encouragement of continued domestic production of wool to be policy of Congress.

 __Short Title__ 

    [Act Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041, title VII][/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041/tVII], § 701, [68 Stat. 910][/us/stat/68/910], provided that title VII of act Aug. 28, 1954 (enacting this chapter) could be cited as the “National Wool Act of 1954”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–130][/us/pl/103/130], § 3(a), Nov. 1, 1993, [107 Stat. 1369][/us/stat/107/1369].
