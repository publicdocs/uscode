## § 7601. Definitions

    In this Act:

    (1) __1862 Institution__ 

        The term “1862 Institution” means a college or university eligible to receive funds under the Act of July 2, 1862 ([12 Stat. 503][/us/stat/12/503], chapter 130; [7 U.S.C. 301][/us/usc/t7/s301] et seq.).

    (2) __1890 Institution__ 

        The term “1890 Institution” means a college or university eligible to receive funds under the Act of August 30, 1890 ([26 Stat. 419][/us/stat/26/419], chapter 841; [7 U.S.C. 321][/us/usc/t7/s321] et seq.), including Tuskegee University.

    (3) __1994 Institution__ 

        The term “1994 Institution” means 1 of the 1994 Institutions (as defined in section 532 of the Equity in Educational Land-Grant Status Act of 1994 ([Public Law 103–382][/us/pl/103/382]; [7 U.S.C. 301][/us/usc/t7/s301] note)) (as amended by section 251(a)).

    (4) __Advisory Board__ 

        The term “Advisory Board” means the National Agricultural Research, Extension, Education, and Economics Advisory Board established under [section 3123 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s3123].

    (5) __Department__ 

        The term “Department” means the Department of Agriculture.

    (6) __Hispanic-serving agricultural colleges and universities__ 

        The term “Hispanic-serving agricultural colleges and universities” has the meaning given the term in [section 3103 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s3103].

    (7) __Secretary__ 

        The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture.
