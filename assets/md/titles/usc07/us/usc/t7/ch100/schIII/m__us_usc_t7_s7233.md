## § 7233. Term of loans

    (a) __Term of loan__ 

        In the case of each loan commodity (other than upland cotton or extra long staple cotton), a marketing assistance loan under [section 7231 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s7231] shall have a term of 9 months beginning on the first day of the first month after the month in which the loan is made.

    (b) __Special rule for cotton__ 

        A marketing assistance loan for upland cotton or extra long staple cotton shall have a term of 10 months beginning on the first day of the month in which the loan is made.

    (c) __Extensions prohibited__ 

        The Secretary may not extend the term of a marketing assistance loan for any loan commodity.

([Pub. L. 104–127, title I, § 133][/us/pl/104/127/s133], Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 907][/us/stat/110/907].)
