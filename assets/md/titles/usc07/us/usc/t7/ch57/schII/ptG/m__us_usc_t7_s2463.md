## § 2463. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–349, § 8(c)(2)][/us/pl/103/349/s8/c/2], Oct. 6, 1994, [108 Stat. 3141][/us/stat/108/3141]

    Section, [Pub. L. 91–577, title II, § 73][/us/pl/91/577/s73], Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1550][/us/stat/84/1550]; [Pub. L. 103–349, § 8(c)(1)][/us/pl/103/349/s8/c/1], Oct. 6, 1994, [108 Stat. 3140][/us/stat/108/3140], related to appeal or civil action in contested cases. Prior to repeal, subsec. (b) of this section was transferred to [section 2504 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2504] by [Pub. L. 103–349, § 8(c)(1)][/us/pl/103/349/s8/c/1], Oct. 6, 1994, [108 Stat. 3140][/us/stat/108/3140].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective 180 days after Oct. 6, 1994, see [section 15 of Pub. L. 103–349][/us/pl/103/349/s15], set out as an Effective Date of 1994 Amendment note under [section 2401 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2401].
