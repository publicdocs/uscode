## § 2542. Grandfather clause

    Nothing in this chapter shall abridge the right of any person, or the successor in interest of the person, to reproduce or sell a variety developed and produced by such person more than one year prior to the effective filing date of an adverse application for a certificate of plant variety protection.

([Pub. L. 91–577, title III, § 112][/us/pl/91/577/s112], Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1555][/us/stat/84/1555]; [Pub. L. 103–349, § 13(r)][/us/pl/103/349/s13/r], Oct. 6, 1994, [108 Stat. 3144][/us/stat/108/3144].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–349][/us/pl/103/349] substituted “the successor in interest of the person” for “his successor in interest”.

 __Effective Date of 1994 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 103–349][/us/pl/103/349] effective 180 days after Oct. 6, 1994, see [section 15 of Pub. L. 103–349][/us/pl/103/349/s15], set out as a note under [section 2401 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2401].
