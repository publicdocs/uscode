## §§ 218 to 218d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–173, § 10][/us/pl/100/173/s10], Nov. 23, 1987, [101 Stat. 922][/us/stat/101/922]

    Section 218, [act Aug. 15, 1921, ch. 64][/us/act/1921-08-15/ch64], title V, § 501, as added [Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 532][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch532], [49 Stat. 648][/us/stat/49/648], stated necessity to curb unfair, deceptive, and fraudulent practices relating to live poultry.

    Section 218a, [act Aug. 15, 1921, ch. 64][/us/act/1921-08-15/ch64], title V, § 502, as added [Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 532][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch532], [49 Stat. 648][/us/stat/49/648], authorized Secretary to designate cities and markets where unfair practices exist, to require licensing, and to prescribe information to be contained in application license, and authorized penalty for dealing without license.

    Section 218b, [act Aug. 15, 1921, ch. 64][/us/act/1921-08-15/ch64], title V, § 503, as added [Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 532][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch532], [49 Stat. 649][/us/stat/49/649], defined “live poultry dealer”.

    Section 218c, [act Aug. 15, 1921, ch. 64][/us/act/1921-08-15/ch64], title V, § 504, as added [Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 532][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch532], [49 Stat. 649][/us/stat/49/649], related to application of other provisions of this chapter to this subchapter and posting of rates, charges, and rentals in licensee’s place of business.

    Section 218d, [act Aug. 15, 1921, ch. 64][/us/act/1921-08-15/ch64], title V, § 505, as added [Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 532][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch532], [49 Stat. 649][/us/stat/49/649], related to suspension and revocation of licenses.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective 90 days after Nov. 23, 1987, see [section 12 of Pub. L. 100–173][/us/pl/100/173/s12], set out as an Effective Date of 1987 Amendment note under [section 182 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s182].
