## § 231. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

Section, [act July 22, 1942, ch. 516][/us/act/1942-07-22/ch516], [56 Stat. 689][/us/stat/56/689], was from the Department of Agriculture Appropriation Act, 1943, and provided for fees for inspection of brands appearing upon livestock. See [section 217a of this title][/us/usc/t7/s217a]. Similar provisions were contained in the following prior appropriation acts:

    [July 1, 1941, ch. 267][/us/act/1941-07-01/ch267], [55 Stat. 432][/us/stat/55/432].

    [June 25, 1940, ch. 421][/us/act/1940-06-25/ch421], [54 Stat. 557][/us/stat/54/557].

    [June 29, 1937, ch. 404][/us/act/1937-06-29/ch404], [50 Stat. 406][/us/stat/50/406].

    [June 4, 1936, ch. 489][/us/act/1936-06-04/ch489], [49 Stat. 1432][/us/stat/49/1432].

    [May 17, 1935, ch. 131][/us/act/1935-05-17/ch131], title I, [49 Stat. 257][/us/stat/49/257].

    [Mar. 26, 1934, ch. 89][/us/act/1934-03-26/ch89], [48 Stat. 477][/us/stat/48/477].
