## § 229. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–234, title XI][/us/pl/110/234/tXI], 11004(b), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1356][/us/stat/122/1356], and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246], § 4(a), title XI, § 11004(b), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 2118

    Section, [act Aug. 15, 1921, ch. 64, title IV][/us/act/1921-08-15/ch64/tIV], § 416, as added [Pub. L. 110–234, title XI][/us/pl/110/234/tXI], § 11004(a)(2), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1355][/us/stat/122/1355], and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246], § 4(a), title XI, § 11004(a)(2), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 2117, related to annual reports about investigations and enforcement actions.

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246] repealed this section. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 416 of act Aug. 15, 1921, was renumbered section 417 and is classified to [section 229c of this title][/us/usc/t7/s229c].

    Another prior section 416 of act Aug. 15, 1921, was classified to [section 229a of this title][/us/usc/t7/s229a], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 106–78][/us/pl/106/78].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 110–234, title XI][/us/pl/110/234/tXI], 11004(b), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1356][/us/stat/122/1356], and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246], § 4(a), title XI, § 11004(b), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 2118, provided that the repeal of this section is effective Sept. 30, 2012 \[extended to Sept. 30, 2013, see [section 701(a) of Pub. L. 112–240][/us/pl/112/240/s701/a], set out in a 1-Year Extension of Agricultural Programs note under [section 8701 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s8701]\].

    \[[Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246] enacted identical provisions. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a], set out as a note under [section 8701 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s8701].\]
