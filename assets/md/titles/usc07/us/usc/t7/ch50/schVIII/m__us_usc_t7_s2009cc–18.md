## § 2009cc–18. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $20,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2018.

([Pub. L. 87–128, title III][/us/pl/87/128/tIII], § 384S, as added [Pub. L. 110–234, title VI][/us/pl/110/234/tVI], § 6027(f), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1183][/us/stat/122/1183], and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246], § 4(a), title VI, § 6027(f), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 1944; amended [Pub. L. 113–79, title VI][/us/pl/113/79/tVI], § 6028, Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 850][/us/stat/128/850].)

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246] enacted identical sections. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a].
