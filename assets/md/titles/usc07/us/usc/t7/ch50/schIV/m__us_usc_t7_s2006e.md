## § 2006e. Prohibition on use of loans for certain purposes

    The Secretary shall not approve any loan under this chapter to drain, dredge, fill, level, or otherwise manipulate a wetland (as defined in section 3801(a)(16)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of title 16), or to engage in any activity that results in impairing or reducing the flow, circulation, or reach of water, except in the case of activity related to the maintenance of previously converted wetlands, or in the case of such activity that is already commenced before November 28, 1990. This section shall not apply to a loan made or guaranteed under this chapter for a utility line.

([Pub. L. 87–128, title III, § 363][/us/pl/87/128/s363], as added [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVIII, § 1824][/us/pl/101/624/s1824], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3831][/us/stat/104/3831]; amended [Pub. L. 102–237, title V, § 501(i)][/us/pl/102/237/s501/i], Dec. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 1868][/us/stat/105/1868]; [Pub. L. 104–127, title VII, § 751][/us/pl/104/127/s751], Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1129][/us/stat/110/1129].)

 __References in Text__ 

    For definition of “this chapter”, referred to in text, see note set out under [section 1921 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1921].

    [Section 3801(a) of title 16][/us/usc/t16/s3801/a], referred to in text, was subsequently amended, and section 3801(a)(16) no longer defines the term “wetland”. However, such term is defined elsewhere in that section.

 __Amendments__ 

    1996—[Pub. L. 104–127][/us/pl/104/127] inserted at end “This section shall not apply to a loan made or guaranteed under this chapter for a utility line.”

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–237][/us/pl/102/237] inserted a closing parenthesis after “3801(a)(16) of title 16” and substituted “before November 28, 1990” for “prior to the date of enactment of this section”.

 __Effective Date of 1991 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 102–237][/us/pl/102/237] effective as if included in the provision of the Food, Agriculture, Conservation, and Trade Act of 1990, [Pub. L. 101–624][/us/pl/101/624], to which the amendment relates, see [section 1101(b)(3) of Pub. L. 102–237][/us/pl/102/237/s1101/b/3], set out as a note under [section 1421 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1421].
