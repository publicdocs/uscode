## § 2009aa–9. Consent of States

    Nothing in this subchapter requires any State to engage in or accept any program under this subchapter without the consent of the State.

([Pub. L. 87–128, title III, § 382J][/us/pl/87/128/s382J], as added [Pub. L. 106–554, § 1(a)(4)][/us/pl/106/554/s1/a/4] \[div. B, title V, § 503\], Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2763][/us/stat/114/2763], 2763A–280.)
