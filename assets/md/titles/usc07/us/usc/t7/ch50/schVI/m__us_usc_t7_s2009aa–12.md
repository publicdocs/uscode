## § 2009aa–12. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __In general__ 

        There is authorized to be appropriated to the Authority to carry out this subchapter $30,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2008 through 2012, to remain available until expended.

    (b) __Administrative expenses__ 

        Not more than 5 percent of the amount appropriated under subsection (a) of this section for a fiscal year shall be used for administrative expenses of the Authority.
