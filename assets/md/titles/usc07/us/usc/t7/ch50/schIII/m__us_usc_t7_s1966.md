## § 1966. Emergency Credit Revolving Fund utilization

    The Secretary is authorized to utilize the revolving fund created by section 1148a  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of title 12 (hereinafter in this subchapter referred to as the “Emergency Credit Revolving Fund”) for carrying out the purposes of this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 87–128, title III, § 326][/us/pl/87/128/s326], Aug. 8, 1961, [75 Stat. 312][/us/stat/75/312].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 1148a of title 12][/us/usc/t12/s1148a], referred to in text, was repealed by [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5][/us/pl/92/181/s5].26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624][/us/stat/85/624]. See [section 2252 of Title 12][/us/usc/t12/s2252], Banks and Banking.

    The Emergency Credit Revolving Fund, referred to in text, was abolished and its assets and liabilities transferred to the Agricultural Credit Insurance Fund by [section 1929 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1929].
