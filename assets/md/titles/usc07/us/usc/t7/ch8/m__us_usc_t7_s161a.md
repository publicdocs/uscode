## § 161a. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

Section was from the Department of Agriculture Appropriation Act, 1945, [act June 28, 1944, ch. 296][/us/act/1944-06-28/ch296], [58 Stat. 440][/us/stat/58/440], related to disposition of moneys from inspection and certification of domestic plants and plant products for export, and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts. Similar provisions were contained in prior appropriation acts as follows:

    [July 12, 1943, ch. 215][/us/act/1943-07-12/ch215], [57 Stat. 408][/us/stat/57/408].

    [July 22, 1942, ch. 516][/us/act/1942-07-22/ch516], [56 Stat. 686][/us/stat/56/686].
