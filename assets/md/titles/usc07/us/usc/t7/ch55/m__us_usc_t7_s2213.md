## § 2213. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, acts [June 5, 1952, ch. 369][/us/act/1952-06-05/ch369], ch. XI, § 1101, [66 Stat. 121][/us/stat/66/121]; 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 3, eff. June 4, 1953, 18 F.R. 3219, [67 Stat. 633][/us/stat/67/633]; [June 28, 1955, ch. 189][/us/act/1955-06-28/ch189], § 12(c)(16), [69 Stat. 182][/us/stat/69/182]; Aug. 14, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–426, title III][/us/pl/88/426/tIII], § 307, [78 Stat. 432][/us/stat/78/432], related to the position of Assistant Secretary of Agriculture for Administration established by section 3 of Reorg. Plan No. 2 of 1953. Section 3 of Reorg. Plan No. 2 of 1953 was repealed by [Pub. L. 97–325][/us/pl/97/325], § 8(d), Oct. 15, 1982, [96 Stat. 1606][/us/stat/96/1606]. See [section 2212c of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2212c].

    Section was formerly classified to [section 517b of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s517b] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
