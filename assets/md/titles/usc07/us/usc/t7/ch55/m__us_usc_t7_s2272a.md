## § 2272a. Funds for incidental expenses and promotional items relating to volunteers

    On and after August 6, 1996, funds appropriated to the Department of Agriculture may be used for incidental expenses such as transportation, uniforms, lodging, and subsistence for volunteers serving under the authority of [section 2272 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2272], when such volunteers are engaged in the work of the United States Department of Agriculture; and for promotional items of nominal value relating to the United States Department of Agriculture Volunteer Programs.

([Pub. L. 104–180, title VII, § 729][/us/pl/104/180/s729], Aug. 6, 1996, [110 Stat. 1600][/us/stat/110/1600].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 2272a, [Pub. L. 102–142, title VII, § 739][/us/pl/102/142/s739], Oct. 28, 1991, [105 Stat. 915][/us/stat/105/915], authorized use of funds for incidental expenses and promotional items relating to volunteers on and after Oct. 28, 1991, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 336(f)(2)][/us/pl/104/127/s336/f/2], Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1007][/us/stat/110/1007].
