## § 2203. Seal

    The Secretary of Agriculture is authorized and directed to procure a proper seal, with such suitable inscriptions and devices as he may approve, to be known as the official seal of the Department of Agriculture, and to be kept and used to verify official documents, under such rules and regulations as he may prescribe.

([Aug. 8, 1894, ch. 238][/us/act/1894-08-08/ch238], [28 Stat. 272][/us/stat/28/272].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 513 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s513] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1][/us/pl/89/554/s1], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
