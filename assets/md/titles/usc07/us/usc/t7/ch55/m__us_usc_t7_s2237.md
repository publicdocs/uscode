## § 2237. Use of field work funds for employment of men with equipment, etc.

    Funds available for field work in the Department of Agriculture shall be available for employment by contract or otherwise of men with equipment, boats, work animals, animal-drawn, and motor-propelled vehicles.

([June 4, 1936, ch. 489][/us/act/1936-06-04/ch489], [49 Stat. 1422][/us/stat/49/1422].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 542a of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s542a] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
