## § 2209g. Availability of funds for uniforms or allowances

    On and after November 10, 2005, funds appropriated by this or any other Appropriations Act to the Department of Agriculture (excluding the Forest Service) shall be available for uniforms or allowances as authorized by law ([5 U.S.C. 5901–590][/us/usc/t5/s5901–590]2).

([Pub. L. 109–97, title VII][/us/pl/109/97/tVII], § 702, Nov. 10, 2005, [119 Stat. 2149][/us/stat/119/2149].)
