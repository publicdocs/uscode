## § 2249. Amount and character of cooperation

    Unless otherwise provided by the Department of Agriculture Organic Act of 1944 or by other statute, the measure and character of cooperation authorized by said Act on the part of the Federal Government and on the part of the cooperator shall be such as may be prescribed by the Secretary, unless otherwise provided for in the applicable appropriation.

([Sept. 21, 1944, ch. 412][/us/act/1944-09-21/ch412], title VII, § 711, [58 Stat. 743][/us/stat/58/743].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Department of Agriculture Organic Act of 1944, referred to in text, is [act Sept. 21, 1944, ch. 412][/us/act/1944-09-21/ch412], [58 Stat. 734][/us/stat/58/734], as amended. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

 __Codification__ 

    This section was enacted as part of the Department of Agriculture Organic Act of 1944.

    Section was formerly classified to [section 564a of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s564a] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1][/us/pl/89/554/s1], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    Functions of all officers, agencies, and employees of Department of Agriculture transferred, with certain exceptions, to Secretary of Agriculture by 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 1, eff. June 4, 1953, 18 F.R. 3219, [67 Stat. 633][/us/stat/67/633], set out as a note under [section 2201 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2201].
