## § 2235a. Deposit and retention of credit card refunds or rebates

    On and after November 28, 2001, refunds or rebates received on an on-going basis from a credit card services provider under the Department of Agriculture’s charge card programs may be deposited to and retained without fiscal year limitation in the Department’s Working Capital Fund established under [section 2235 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2235] and used to fund management initiatives of general benefit to the Department of Agriculture bureaus and offices as determined by the Secretary of Agriculture or the Secretary’s designee.

([Pub. L. 107–76, title VII][/us/pl/107/76/tVII], § 729, Nov. 28, 2001, [115 Stat. 736][/us/stat/115/736].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in the following prior appropriation act:

    [Pub. L. 106–387][/us/pl/106/387], § 1(a) \[title I, § 757\], Oct. 28, 2000, [114 Stat. 1549][/us/stat/114/1549], 1549A–43.
