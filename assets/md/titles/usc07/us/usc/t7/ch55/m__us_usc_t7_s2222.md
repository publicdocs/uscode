## § 2222. Details of law clerks

    Law clerks may be detailed by the Secretary of Agriculture for service in or out of Washington.

([Mar. 4, 1911, ch. 238][/us/act/1911-03-04/ch238], [36 Stat. 1236][/us/stat/36/1236].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 531 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s531] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
