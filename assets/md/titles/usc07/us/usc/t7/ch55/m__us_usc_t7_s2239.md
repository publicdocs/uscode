## § 2239. Funds for printing, binding, and scientific and technical article reprint purchases

    Funds available to the Department of Agriculture may be used for printing and binding, including the purchase of reprints of scientific and technical articles.

([Sept. 6, 1950, ch. 896][/us/act/1950-09-06/ch896], Ch. VI, title IV, § 406, [64 Stat. 679][/us/stat/64/679].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 542c of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s542c] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
