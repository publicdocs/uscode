## § 2205. Duties of former Commissioner of Agriculture transferred to Secretary

    The Secretary of Agriculture is authorized and directed to perform all the duties named in all Acts of Congress in force on February 8, 1889, to be performed by the Commissioner of Agriculture.

([Mar. 2, 1889, ch. 373][/us/act/1889-03-02/ch373], [25 Stat. 840][/us/stat/25/840]; [July 14, 1890, ch. 707][/us/act/1890-07-14/ch707], [26 Stat. 288][/us/stat/26/288].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 515 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s515] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    Functions of all officers, agencies, and employees of Department of Agriculture transferred, with certain exceptions, to Secretary of Agriculture by 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 1, eff. June 4, 1953, 18 F.R. 3219, [67 Stat. 633][/us/stat/67/633], set out as a note under [section 2201 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2201].
