## § 2219. Salaries; how paid

    The Secretary of Agriculture is authorized and directed to pay the salary of each employee from the roll of the bureau, independent division, or office in which the employee is working, and no other.

([Mar. 4, 1907, ch. 2907][/us/act/1907-03-04/ch2907], [34 Stat. 1280][/us/stat/34/1280].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 528 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s528] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1][/us/pl/89/554/s1], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
