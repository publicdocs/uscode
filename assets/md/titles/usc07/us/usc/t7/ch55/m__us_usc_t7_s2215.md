## § 2215. Chief clerk

    The Secretary of Agriculture shall appoint a chief clerk.

(R.S. § 523; [Feb. 9, 1889, ch. 122][/us/act/1889-02-09/ch122], §§ 1, 4, [25 Stat. 659][/us/stat/25/659]; [Feb. 10, 1925, ch. 200][/us/act/1925-02-10/ch200], [43 Stat. 822][/us/stat/43/822].)

 __Codification__ 

    R.S. § 523 derived from [act May 15, 1862, ch. 72, § 4][/us/act/1862-05-15/ch72/s4], [12 Stat. 388][/us/stat/12/388].

    Section was formerly classified to [section 519 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s519] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1][/us/pl/89/554/s1], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].

 __Change of Name__ 

    “Secretary of Agriculture” substituted in text for “Commissioner of Agriculture” pursuant to sections 1 and 4 of act Feb. 9, 1889, which are classified to [section 2202 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2202]. See, also, [section 2205 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2205].
