## § 1100. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act Aug. 8, 1947, ch. 519][/us/act/1947-08-08/ch519], § 1, [61 Stat. 922][/us/stat/61/922], provided that this chapter may be cited as the Sugar Act of 1948, and expired on Dec. 31, 1974.

>     “(1) under programs applicable to the crop year 1974 and previous crop years, if the powers vested in the Secretary otherwise terminate on December 31, 1974, or
