## § 2910. Preemption of other Federal and State programs; applicability of provisions to amendments to orders

    (a) Nothing in this chapter may be construed to preempt or supersede any other program relating to beef promotion organized and operated under the laws of the United States or any State.

    (b) The provisions of this chapter applicable to the order shall be applicable to amendments to the order.

([Pub. L. 94–294, § 11][/us/pl/94/294/s11], May 28, 1976, [90 Stat. 535][/us/stat/90/535]; [Pub. L. 99–198, title XVI, § 1601(b)][/us/pl/99/198/s1601/b], Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1606][/us/stat/99/1606].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1985—[Pub. L. 99–198][/us/pl/99/198] amended section generally, substituting administrative provisions for provisions relating to applicability of provisions to amendments to orders.

 __Effective Date of 1985 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 99–198][/us/pl/99/198] effective Jan. 1, 1986, see [section 1601(c) of Pub. L. 99–198][/us/pl/99/198/s1601/c], set out as a note under [section 2901 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s2901].
