## § 1598. Notice of intention to prosecute

    Before any violation of this chapter is reported by the Secretary of Agriculture to any United States attorney for institution of a criminal proceeding, the person against whom such proceeding is contemplated shall be given appropriate notice and an opportunity to prevent  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be “present”. </sup></sup>  his views, either orally or in writing, with regard to such contemplated proceeding.

([Aug. 9, 1939, ch. 615][/us/act/1939-08-09/ch615], title IV, § 408, [53 Stat. 1286][/us/stat/53/1286].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    See [section 1610 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s1610].
