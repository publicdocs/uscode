## § 7471. Suspension or termination

    (a) __In general__ 

        If the Secretary finds that an order issued under [section 7463 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s7463], or a provision of the order, obstructs or does not tend to effectuate the purposes of this subchapter, the Secretary shall suspend or terminate the operation of the order or provision.

    (b) __Limitation__ 

        The suspension or termination of any order, or any provision of an order, shall not be considered an order under this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 104–127, title V, § 562][/us/pl/104/127/s562], Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1074][/us/stat/110/1074].)
