## § 4817. Preemption

    (a) __Promotion and consumer education; funds from pork producers__ 

    This chapter is intended to occupy the field of—

        (1) promotion and consumer education involving pork and pork products; and

        (2) obtaining funds therefor from pork producers.

    (b) __Additional or different State regulation prohibited__ 

        The regulation of such activity (other than a regulation or requirement relating to a matter of public health or the provision of State or local funds for such activity) that is in addition to or different from this chapter may not be imposed by a State.

    (c) __Application of section__ 

        This section shall apply only during a period beginning on the date of the commencement of the collection of assessments under [section 4809 of this title][/us/usc/t7/s4809] and ending on the date of the termination of the collection of assessments under section 4811(a)(3) or 4811(b)(1)(B)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be “section 4811(b)(2)(A) or 4812(b)(1)(B)”. </sup></sup>  of this title.
