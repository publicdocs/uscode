## § 8719. Period of effectiveness

    This subchapter shall be effective beginning with the 2008 crop year of each covered commodity through the 2012 crop year.

([Pub. L. 110–234, title I, § 1109][/us/pl/110/234/s1109], May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 953][/us/stat/122/953]; [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a], title I, § 1109, June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 1681.)

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246] enacted identical sections. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a].
