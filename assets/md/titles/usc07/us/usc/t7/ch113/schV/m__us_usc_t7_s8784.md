## § 8784. Assignment of payments

    (a) __In general__ 

        The provisions of [section 590h(g) of title 16][/us/usc/t16/s590h/g], relating to assignment of payments, shall apply to payments made under this chapter.

    (b) __Notice__ 

        The producer making the assignment, or the assignee, shall provide the Secretary with notice, in such manner as the Secretary may require, of any assignment made under this section.

([Pub. L. 110–234, title I, § 1608][/us/pl/110/234/s1608], May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1018][/us/stat/122/1018]; [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a], title I, § 1608, June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 1746.)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in subsec. (a), was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246], June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Tables.

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246] enacted identical sections. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a].
