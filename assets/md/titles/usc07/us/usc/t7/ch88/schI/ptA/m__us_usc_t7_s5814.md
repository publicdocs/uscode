## § 5814. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part $40,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2013 through 2018. Of amounts appropriated to carry out this part for a fiscal year, not less than $15,000,000, or not less than two thirds of any such appropriation, whichever is greater, shall be used to carry out sections 5811 and 5812 of this title.

([Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1624][/us/pl/101/624/s1624], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3739][/us/stat/104/3739]; [Pub. L. 102–237, title IV, § 408][/us/pl/102/237/s408], Dec. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 1865][/us/stat/105/1865]; [Pub. L. 113–79, title VII, § 7201][/us/pl/113/79/s7201], Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 880][/us/stat/128/880].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2014—[Pub. L. 113–79][/us/pl/113/79], in first sentence, struck out “$40,000,000 for each fiscal year” after “appropriated” and inserted “$40,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2013 through 2018” after “part”.

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–237][/us/pl/102/237] substituted “and 5812” for “and 5813”.
