## §§ 5871 to 5874. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–127, title VIII, § 861][/us/pl/104/127/s861], Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1174][/us/stat/110/1174]

    Section 5871, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1644][/us/pl/101/624/s1644], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3751][/us/stat/104/3751], provided for establishment of research and grant program.

    Section 5873, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1646][/us/pl/101/624/s1646], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3753][/us/stat/104/3753], provided for reports to Congress.

    Section 5874, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1647][/us/pl/101/624/s1647], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3754][/us/stat/104/3754], provided for appropriations for research and grant program.
