## § 704. Modification of project

    The project for the control of floods in the Sacramento River, California, adopted by [section 703 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s703], is modified in accordance with the report of the California Débris Commission submitted in Senate Document Numbered 23, Sixty-ninth Congress, first session: Provided, That the total amounts contributed by the Federal Government, including the amounts heretofore contributed by it, shall in no event exceed in the aggregate $17,600,000.

([May 15, 1928, ch. 569, § 13][/us/act/1928-05-15/ch569/s13], [45 Stat. 539][/us/stat/45/539].)
