## § 702m. Interest of Members of Congress in contracts for acquisition of land

    In every contract or agreement to be made or entered into for the acquisition of land either by private sale or condemnation as in sections 702a, 702b to 702d, 702e to 702g, 702h, 702i, 702j, 702k, 702l, 702m, and 704 of this title provided the provisions contained in [section 6306(a) of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s6306/a] shall be applicable.

([May 15, 1928, ch. 569, § 14][/us/act/1928-05-15/ch569/s14], [45 Stat. 539][/us/stat/45/539].)

 __Codification__ 

    In text, “section 6306(a)” substituted for “section 3741 of the Revised Statutes being [section 22 of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s22] of the United States Code” on authority of [Pub. L. 111–350, § 6(c)][/us/pl/111/350/s6/c], Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3854][/us/stat/124/3854], which Act enacted Title 41, Public Contracts.
