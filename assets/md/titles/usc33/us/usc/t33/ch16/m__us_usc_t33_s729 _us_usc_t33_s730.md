###### §§ 729, 730. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], § 20, [63 Stat. 561][/us/stat/63/561]

    Section 729, acts [June 17, 1910, ch. 301][/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301], § 9, [36 Stat. 538][/us/stat/36/538]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736], related to purchase by Commandant of sites for lighthouses. See sections 92(f) and 653 of Title 14, Coast Guard.

    Section 730, acts [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 168][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch168], [37 Stat. 1018][/us/stat/37/1018]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736]; [Oct. 22, 1913, ch. 32][/us/act/1913-10-22/ch32], [38 Stat. 225][/us/stat/38/225], related to purchase of additional land for lighthouses and depots. See [section 92(f) of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s92/f].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President \[Aug. 4, 1949\], see section 19 of act Aug. 19, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
