## §§ 746 to 746b. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], § 20, [63 Stat. 565][/us/stat/63/565]

    Section 746, [act June 29, 1949, ch. 277][/us/act/1949-06-29/ch277], § 1, [63 Stat. 299][/us/stat/63/299], related to compensation and pay of civilians keepers of lighthouses and civilian employees on Coast Guard vessels.

    Section 746a, [act June 29, 1949, ch. 277][/us/act/1949-06-29/ch277], § 2, [63 Stat. 299][/us/stat/63/299], related to extra pay and hours of duty.

    Section 746b, [act June 29, 1949, ch. 277][/us/act/1949-06-29/ch277], § 3, [63 Stat. 300][/us/stat/63/300], related to additional compensation as affecting retirement compensation.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President \[Aug. 4, 1949\], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
