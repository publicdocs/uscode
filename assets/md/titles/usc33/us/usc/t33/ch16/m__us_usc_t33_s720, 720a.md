## §§ 720, 720a. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20], [63 Stat. 561][/us/stat/63/561]

    Section 720, acts [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 7][/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s7], [36 Stat. 538][/us/stat/36/538]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1], [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736], related to general control by Commandant of Coast Guard. See [section 81 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s81], Coast Guard.

    Section 720a, [act Aug. 16, 1937, ch. 665, § 3][/us/act/1937-08-16/ch665/s3], [50 Stat. 667][/us/stat/50/667], related to establishment and maintenance of aids to navigation in certain waters. See [section 81 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s81], Coast Guard.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President \[Aug. 4, 1949\], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
