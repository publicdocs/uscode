## § 721. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, acts [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 4][/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s4], [36 Stat. 537][/us/stat/36/537]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1], [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736]; [Aug. 16, 1937, ch. 665, § 4][/us/act/1937-08-16/ch665/s4], [50 Stat. 667][/us/stat/50/667], gave Commissioner the power to settle damage claims up to $500. These duties and functions of Commissioner of Lighthouses were taken over by Commandant of Coast Guard under Reorg. Plan II of 1939, § 2(a), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2731, [53 Stat. 1432][/us/stat/53/1432], set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
