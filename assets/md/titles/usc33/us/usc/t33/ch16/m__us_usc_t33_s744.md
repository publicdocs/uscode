## § 744. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act June 20, 1918, ch. 103, § 7][/us/act/1918-06-20/ch103/s7], [40 Stat. 608][/us/stat/40/608], related to superintendents of lighthouses and their salaries.
