## § 736. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], § 20, [63 Stat. 561][/us/stat/63/561]

    Section, R.S. § 4676; acts [June 10, 1910, ch. 301][/us/act/1910-06-10/ch301], § 6, [36 Stat. 538][/us/stat/36/538]; [Aug. 16, 1937, ch. 665][/us/act/1937-08-16/ch665], § 1, [50 Stat. 666][/us/stat/50/666], related to placement of markers over sunken craft and other obstructions. See [section 86 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s86], Coast Guard.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President \[Aug. 4, 1949\], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
