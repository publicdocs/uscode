## §§ 731, 732. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20], [63 Stat. 561][/us/stat/63/561]

    Section 731, acts [Mar. 4, 1909, ch. 299, § 1][/us/act/1909-03-04/ch299/s1], [35 Stat. 972][/us/stat/35/972]; [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 6][/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s6], [36 Stat. 538][/us/stat/36/538], related to lease of sites for temporary lights. See [section 92 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s92], Coast Guard.

    Section 732, acts [Aug. 28, 1916, ch. 414, § 2][/us/act/1916-08-28/ch414/s2], [39 Stat. 538][/us/stat/39/538]; [July 11, 1941, ch. 290, § 1][/us/act/1941-07-11/ch290/s1], [55 Stat. 584][/us/stat/55/584], related to exchange of right-of-way pertaining to Coast Guard. See [section 92 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s92].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President \[Aug. 4, 1949\], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
