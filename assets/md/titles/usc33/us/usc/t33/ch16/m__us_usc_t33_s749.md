## § 749. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], § 20, [63 Stat. 561][/us/stat/63/561]

    Section, acts [July 27, 1912, ch. 255][/us/act/1912-07-27/ch255], § 2, [37 Stat. 239][/us/stat/37/239]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736], related to reimbursement of lighthouse keepers for clothing, etc., furnished shipwrecked persons. See [section 486 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s486], Coast Guard.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President \[Aug. 4, 1949\], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
