## §§ 739 to 740b. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20], [63 Stat. 561][/us/stat/63/561]

    Section 739, R.S. § 4668, related to substitution of lighthouses for lightships.

    Section 740, acts [Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 81, § 5][/us/act/1915-03-03/ch81/s5], [38 Stat. 927][/us/stat/38/927]; [Aug. 28, 1916, ch. 414, § 3][/us/act/1916-08-28/ch414/s3], [39 Stat. 538][/us/stat/39/538]; [June 20, 1918, ch. 103, § 5][/us/act/1918-06-20/ch103/s5], [40 Stat. 608][/us/stat/40/608]; [June 5, 1920, ch. 235, § 1][/us/act/1920-06-05/ch235/s1], [41 Stat. 927][/us/stat/41/927], related to post-lantern lights on particular waters.

    Section 740a, [act May 22, 1926, ch. 371, § 6][/us/act/1926-05-22/ch371/s6], [44 Stat. 626][/us/stat/44/626], related to post-lantern lights on Allegheny and Rock Rivers.

    Section 740b, acts [June 23, 1874, ch. 455, § 1][/us/act/1874-06-23/ch455/s1], [18 Stat. 220][/us/stat/18/220]; [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 6][/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s6], [36 Stat. 538][/us/stat/36/538], related to lights and buoys on Mississippi, Ohio, and Missouri Rivers.

    Sections covered by [section 81 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s81], Coast Guard.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President \[Aug. 4, 1949\], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
