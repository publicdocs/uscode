## §§ 147 to 147d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–131][/us/pl/88/131], § 3, Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 194][/us/stat/77/194]

    Section 147, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495][/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495], § 6, Pt. D, [65 Stat. 419][/us/stat/65/419], related to sound signals of vessels indicating course.

    Section 147a, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495][/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495], § 6, Pt. D, [65 Stat. 419][/us/stat/65/419], related to additional precautions.

    Section 147b, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495][/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495], § 6, Pt. D, [65 Stat. 420][/us/stat/65/420], related to local rules for harbors and inland waters.

    Section 147c, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495][/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495], § 6, Pt. D, [65 Stat. 420][/us/stat/65/420], related to distress signals.

    Section 147d, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495][/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495], § 6, Pt. D, [65 Stat. 420][/us/stat/65/420], related to orders to helmsmen, and has been omitted.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1963, see Proc. No. 3632, Dec. 29, 1964, 29 F.R. 19167, and [section 3 of Pub. L. 88–131][/us/pl/88/131/s3], both set out as notes under [section 1051 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s1051].
