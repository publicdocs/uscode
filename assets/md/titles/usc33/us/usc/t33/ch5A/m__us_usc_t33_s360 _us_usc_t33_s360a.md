###### §§ 360, 360a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591][/us/pl/96/591], § 8(a), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435][/us/stat/94/3435]

    Section 360, acts [Dec. 3, 1945, ch. 511][/us/act/1945-12-03/ch511], § 1, [59 Stat. 590][/us/stat/59/590]; Oct. 15, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–670][/us/pl/89/670], § 6(b)(1), [80 Stat. 938][/us/stat/80/938], provided that vessels of special construction be exempted from requirements with regard to number and position of lights.

    Section 360a, acts [Dec. 3, 1945, ch. 511][/us/act/1945-12-03/ch511], § 2, [59 Stat. 591][/us/stat/59/591]; Oct. 15, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–670][/us/pl/89/670], § 6(b)(1), [80 Stat. 938][/us/stat/80/938], related to publication of notice when the Secretary of the department in which the Coast Guard was operating made findings or certifications described in [section 360 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s360].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Dec. 24, 1981, see [section 7 of Pub. L. 96–591][/us/pl/96/591/s7], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 1604 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s1604].
