## § 3401. Purpose

    The purpose of this subchapter is to establish the national ocean exploration program and the national undersea research program within the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.

([Pub. L. 111–11, title XII, § 12001][/us/pl/111/11/s12001], Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1417][/us/stat/123/1417].)

 __Short Title__ 

[Pub. L. 111–11, title XII, § 12101][/us/pl/111/11/s12101], Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1419][/us/stat/123/1419], provided that: 

> “This part \[part II (§§ 12101–12107) of subtitle A of title XII of [Pub. L. 111–11][/us/pl/111/11], enacting subchapter II of this chapter\] may be cited as the ‘NOAA Undersea Research Program Act of 2009’.”
