## § 540a. Availability of appropriations for attendance by military personnel at meetings and for printing survey reports

    Appropriations in this title  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  or appropriations made in this title  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  in subsequent Energy and Water Development Appropriations Acts shall on and after October 2, 1992, be available for expenses of attendance by military personnel at meetings in the manner authorized by [section 4110 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s4110], uniforms, and allowances therefor, as authorized by law ([5 U.S.C. 5901–590][/us/usc/t5/s5901–590]2), and for printing, either during a recess or session of Congress, of survey reports authorized by law, and such survey reports as may be printed during a recess of Congress shall be printed, with illustrations, as documents of the next succeeding session of Congress.

([Pub. L. 102–377, title I][/us/pl/102/377/tI], Oct. 2, 1992, [106 Stat. 1325][/us/stat/106/1325].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This title, referred to in text, is title I of [Pub. L. 102–377][/us/pl/102/377], Oct. 2, 1992, [106 Stat. 1315][/us/stat/106/1315]. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Tables.
