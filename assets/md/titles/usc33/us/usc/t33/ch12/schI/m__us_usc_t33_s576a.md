## § 576a. Purchase of passenger motor vehicles by Corps of Engineers

    On and after March 4, 1933, the provisions of [section 1343 of title 31][/us/usc/t31/s1343] shall be construed as applying to the Corps of Engineers as to the purchase of motor-propelled passenger-carrying vehicles.

([Mar. 4, 1933, ch. 281][/us/act/1933-03-04/ch281], title II, § 1, [47 Stat. 1599][/us/stat/47/1599].)

 __Codification__ 

    “[Section 1343 of title 31][/us/usc/t31/s1343]” substituted in text for “[section 5 of the Act of July 16, 1914][/us/act/1914-07-16/s5] (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 78)” on authority of [Pub. L. 97–258, § 4(b)][/us/pl/97/258/s4/b], Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1067][/us/stat/96/1067], the first section of which enacted Title 31, Money and Finance.

    Section was formerly classified to [section 638b of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s638b] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 31, Money and Finance, by [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 877][/us/stat/96/877].

    Section was also formerly classified to [section 78a of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s78a] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
