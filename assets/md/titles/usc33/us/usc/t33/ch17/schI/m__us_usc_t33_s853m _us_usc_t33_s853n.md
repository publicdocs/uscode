###### §§ 853m, 853n. Repealed. [Oct. 12, 1949, ch. 681][/us/act/1949-10-12/ch681], title V, § 531(b)(41), [63 Stat. 840][/us/stat/63/840]

    Section 853m, [act June 3, 1948, ch. 390][/us/act/1948-06-03/ch390], § 14, [62 Stat. 299][/us/stat/62/299], related to retirement for disability. See section 1201 et seq. of Title 10, Armed Forces.

    Section 853n, [act June 3, 1948, ch. 390][/us/act/1948-06-03/ch390], § 15, [62 Stat. 299][/us/stat/62/299], related to computation of service for retirement purposes. See sections 1401 et seq., 6321 et seq., and 6371 et seq. of Title 10.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1949, see section 533(a) of act Oct. 12, 1949, set out as an Effective Date of 1949 Amendment note under [section 854a of this title][/us/usc/t33/s854a].
