## §§ 2401 to 2410. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–281, title X][/us/pl/111/281/tX], § 1048, Oct. 15, 2010, [124 Stat. 3032][/us/stat/124/3032]

    Section 2401, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 2, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 605][/us/stat/102/605], provided findings and purposes for chapter.

    Section 2402, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 3, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 605][/us/stat/102/605], provided definitions for chapter.

    Section 2403, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 4, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 606][/us/stat/102/606], prohibited, with exceptions, application of antifouling paint containing organotin to any vessel less than 25 meters in length.

    Section 2404, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 5, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 606][/us/stat/102/606], prohibited certain organotin paints and additives.

    Section 2405, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 6, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 607][/us/stat/102/607], related to certification of antifouling paints containing organotin.

    Section 2406, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 7, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 607][/us/stat/102/607]; [Pub. L. 104–106, div. A, title X][/us/pl/104/106/dA/tX], § 1064(f), Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 445][/us/stat/110/445], related to monitoring and research of ecological effects.

    Section 2407, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 8, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 608][/us/stat/102/608], provided for alternative antifouling research.

    Section 2408, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 9, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 608][/us/stat/102/608], related to issuance of a final water quality criteria document.

    Section 2409, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 10, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 608][/us/stat/102/608], provided for civil and criminal penalties for violations of certain sections of chapter.

    Section 2410, [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 11, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 608][/us/stat/102/608], related to other authorities and State laws.

 __Effective Date; Use of Existing Stocks__ 

    [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 12, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 609][/us/stat/102/609], which provided that this chapter would take effect on June 16, 1988, and provided for a limited amount of time after that date to sell and use existing stocks of organotin paints and additives, was repealed by [Pub. L. 111–281, title X][/us/pl/111/281/tX], § 1048, Oct. 15, 2010, [124 Stat. 3032][/us/stat/124/3032].

 __Short Title__ 

    [Pub. L. 100–333][/us/pl/100/333], § 1, June 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 605][/us/stat/102/605], which provided that this chapter could be cited as the “Organotin Antifouling Paint Control Act of 1988”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 111–281, title X][/us/pl/111/281/tX], § 1048, Oct. 15, 2010, [124 Stat. 3032][/us/stat/124/3032].
