## § 1232b. Requirement to notify Coast Guard of release of objects into the navigable waters of the United States

    (a) __Requirement__ 

        As soon as a person has knowledge of any release from a vessel or facility into the navigable waters of the United States of any object that creates an obstruction prohibited under [section 403 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s403], such person shall notify the Secretary and the Secretary of the Army of such release.

    (b) __Restriction on use of notification__ 

        Any notification provided by an individual in accordance with subsection (a) may not be used against such individual in any criminal case, except a prosecution for perjury or for giving a false statement.

([Pub. L. 92–340, § 15][/us/pl/92/340/s15], as added [Pub. L. 109–241, title VI, § 602][/us/pl/109/241/s602], July 11, 2006, [120 Stat. 553][/us/stat/120/553].)
