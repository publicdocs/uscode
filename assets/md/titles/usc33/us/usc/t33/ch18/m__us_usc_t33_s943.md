## § 943. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–348][/us/pl/89/348], § 1(15), Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1311][/us/stat/79/1311]

    Section, [act Mar. 4, 1927, ch. 509][/us/act/1927-03-04/ch509], § 43, [44 Stat. 1444][/us/stat/44/1444], required Secretary to make a report to Congress at beginning of each regular session of the administration of this chapter for preceding fiscal year, including a detailed statement of receipts of and expenditures from funds established in sections 944 and 945 of this title.
