## §§ 1013 to 1016. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–478][/us/pl/96/478], § 12, Oct. 21, 1980, [94 Stat. 2303][/us/stat/94/2303]

    Section 1013, [Pub. L. 87–167][/us/pl/87/167], § 13, formerly § 14, Aug. 30, 1961, [75 Stat. 407][/us/stat/75/407]; renumbered [Pub. L. 93–119][/us/pl/93/119], § 2(13), Oct. 4, 1973, [87 Stat. 428][/us/stat/87/428], authorized appropriations to carry out this chapter.

    Section 1014, [Pub. L. 87–167][/us/pl/87/167], § 15, formerly § 16, Aug. 30, 1961, [75 Stat. 407][/us/stat/75/407]; renumbered and amended [Pub. L. 93–119][/us/pl/93/119], § 2(14), Oct. 4, 1973, [87 Stat. 428][/us/stat/87/428], related to effect of this chapter on [section 1321 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s1321] and [section 89 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s89], Coast Guard.

    Section 1015, [Pub. L. 87–167][/us/pl/87/167], § 17, Aug. 30, 1961, [75 Stat. 407][/us/stat/75/407]; [Pub. L. 89–551][/us/pl/89/551], § 1(10), Sept. 1, 1966, [80 Stat. 375][/us/stat/80/375], specified the effective date of this chapter and provided savings provision for rights, liabilities, procedures, or regulations existing on the effective date of this chapter. [Pub. L. 93–119][/us/pl/93/119], § 2(15), Oct. 4, 1973, [87 Stat. 428][/us/stat/87/428], which provided for the repeal of this section was repealed by [section 12 of Pub. L. 96–478][/us/pl/96/478/s12].

Section 1016, [Pub. L. 93–119][/us/pl/93/119], § 3, Oct. 4, 1973, [87 Stat. 428][/us/stat/87/428], provided effective date of 1973 amendments to this chapter by [Pub. L. 93–119][/us/pl/93/119], the subsections covering the following subject matter: subsec. (a) general effective date; subsec. (b) savings provision; and subsec. (c) effective date of section 1004(d) and (e) of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 2, 1983, see [section 14(a) of Pub. L. 96–478][/us/pl/96/478/s14/a], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 1901 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s1901].
