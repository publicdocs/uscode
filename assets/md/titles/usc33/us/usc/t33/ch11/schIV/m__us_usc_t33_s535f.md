## § 535f. Applicability of provisions

    This subchapter shall apply to all international bridges constructed under the authority of this subchapter. [Section 535a of this title][/us/usc/t33/s535a] and [section 129(a)(3) of title 23][/us/usc/t23/s129/a/3], shall apply to all international bridges the construction of which has been heretofore approved by Congress, notwithstanding any conflicting provision in any Act authorizing the construction of such a bridge or in any agreement entered into by the Federal Government and a State.

([Pub. L. 92–434][/us/pl/92/434], § 9, Sept. 26, 1972, [86 Stat. 733][/us/stat/86/733].)
