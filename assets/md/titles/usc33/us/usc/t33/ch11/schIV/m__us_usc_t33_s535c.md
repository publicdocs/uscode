## § 535c. Secretary of Transportation’s approval; commencement and completion requirements; extension of time limits

    The approval of the Secretary of Transportation, as required by [section 491 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s491], shall be given only subsequent to the President’s approval, as provided for in [section 535b of this title][/us/usc/t33/s535b], and shall be null and void unless the construction of the bridge is commenced within two years and completed within five years from the date of the Secretary’s approval: Provided, however, That the Secretary, for good cause shown, may extend for a reasonable time either or both of the time limits herein provided.

([Pub. L. 92–434, § 5][/us/pl/92/434/s5], Sept. 26, 1972, [86 Stat. 732][/us/stat/86/732].)
