## § 535i. Reservation of right to alter or repeal

    The right to alter, amend, or repeal this subchapter is expressly reserved.

([Pub. L. 92–434][/us/pl/92/434], § 12, Sept. 26, 1972, [86 Stat. 733][/us/stat/86/733].)
