## § 508. Amount of tolls

    Tolls for passage or transit over any bridge constructed under the authority of the Act of March 23, 1906, commonly known as the “Bridge Act of 1906”, the General Bridge Act of 1946 \[[33 U.S.C. 525][/us/usc/t33/s525] et seq.\], and the International Bridge Act of 1972 \[[33 U.S.C. 535][/us/usc/t33/s535] et seq.\] shall be just and reasonable.

([Pub. L. 100–17, title I, § 135(i)][/us/pl/100/17/s135/i], Apr. 2, 1987, [101 Stat. 174][/us/stat/101/174].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Act of March 23, 1906, commonly known as the “Bridge Act of 1906”, referred to in text, is [act Mar. 23, 1906, ch. 1130][/us/act/1906-03-23/ch1130], [34 Stat. 84][/us/stat/34/84], as amended, which enacted sections 491 to 494 and 495 to 498 of this title. For complete classification of this Act of the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 491 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s491] and Tables.

    The General Bridge Act of 1946, referred to in text, is title V of [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753][/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753], [60 Stat. 847][/us/stat/60/847], as amended, which is classified generally to subchapter III (§ 525 et seq.) of this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 525 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s525] and Tables.

    The International Bridge Act of 1972, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 92–434][/us/pl/92/434], Sept. 26, 1972, [86 Stat. 731][/us/stat/86/731], as amended, which is classified principally to subchapter IV (§ 535 et seq.) of this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 535 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s535] and Tables.
