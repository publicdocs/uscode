§§ 1801, 1802. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 99–662, title XIV, § 1405(b)__  __][/us/pl/99/662/s1405/b]__  __,__  __Nov. 17, 1986__  __,__  __[__  __100 Stat. 4271__  __][/us/stat/100/4271]__ 

    Section 1801, [Pub. L. 95–502, title II, § 203][/us/pl/95/502/s203], Oct. 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 1697][/us/stat/92/1697], established Inland Waterways Trust Fund. See [section 9506 of Title 26][/us/usc/t26/s9506], Internal Revenue Code.

    Section 1802, [Pub. L. 95–502, title II, § 204][/us/pl/95/502/s204], Oct. 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 1698][/us/stat/92/1698], made Inland Waterways Trust Fund available for expenditures for navigation construction and rehabilitation projects on inland waterways. See [section 9506 of Title 26][/us/usc/t26/s9506].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1987, see [section 1405(d)(1) of Pub. L. 99–662][/us/pl/99/662/s1405/d/1], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 9506 of Title 26][/us/usc/t26/s9506], Internal Revenue Code.
