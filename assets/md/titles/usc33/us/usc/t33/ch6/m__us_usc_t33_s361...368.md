## §§ 361 to 368. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–89][/us/pl/98/89], § 4(b), Aug. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 599][/us/stat/97/599]

    Section 361, [act June 20, 1874, ch. 344][/us/act/1874-06-20/ch344], § 10, [18 Stat. 128][/us/stat/18/128]; 1946 Reorg. Plan. No. 3, §§ 101–104, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7875, [60 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/60/1097], required filing of reports to Coast Guard on accidents involving United States vessels and provided penalty for failure to comply. See sections 6101, 6103 of Title 46, Shipping.

    Section 362, [act June 20, 1874, ch. 344][/us/act/1874-06-20/ch344], § 11, [18 Stat. 128][/us/stat/18/128]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, §§ 101–104, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7875, [60 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/60/1097], required reporting of probable loss of vessels and provided penalty for failure to comply. See sections 6101, 6103 of Title 46.

    Section 363, acts [June 20, 1874, ch. 344][/us/act/1874-06-20/ch344], § 12, [18 Stat. 128][/us/stat/18/128]; [Feb. 14, 1903, ch. 552][/us/act/1903-02-14/ch552], § 10, [32 Stat. 829][/us/stat/32/829]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, §§ 101–104, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7875, [60 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/60/1097], related to transmission of reports by Coast Guard officials to Commandant of Coast Guard.

    Section 364, acts [June 20, 1874, ch. 344][/us/act/1874-06-20/ch344], § 13, [18 Stat. 128][/us/stat/18/128]; [Mar. 3, 1897, ch. 389][/us/act/1897-03-03/ch389], § 11, [29 Stat. 689][/us/stat/29/689]; [Feb. 14, 1903, ch. 552][/us/act/1903-02-14/ch552], § 10, [32 Stat. 829][/us/stat/32/829]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, §§ 101–104, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7875, [60 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/60/1097], related to remission and recovery of penalties. See [section 2107 of Title 46][/us/usc/t46/s2107].

    Section 365, [act Mar. 4, 1915, ch. 153][/us/act/1915-03-04/ch153], § 15, [38 Stat. 1184][/us/stat/38/1184]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, §§ 101–104, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7875, [60 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/60/1097], related to reports by owners of barges in tow. See [section 6101 of Title 46][/us/usc/t46/s6101].

    Section 366, [act Mar. 4, 1915, ch. 153][/us/act/1915-03-04/ch153], § 15, [38 Stat. 1184][/us/stat/38/1184]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, §§ 101–104, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7875, [60 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/60/1097], required Commandant of Coast Guard to transmit annually to Congress a summary of reports transmitted to him by Coast Guard officials as required under sections 361 to 365 of this title. See [section 6307 of Title 46][/us/usc/t46/s6307].

    Section 367, [act Sept. 4, 1890, ch. 875][/us/act/1890-09-04/ch875], § 1, [26 Stat. 425][/us/stat/26/425], related to duty of master of a vessel in collision to give aid, and to give name of his vessel together with other information about his vessel. See sections 2303, 2304 of Title 46.

    Section 368, [act Sept. 4, 1890, ch. 875][/us/act/1890-09-04/ch875], § 2, [26 Stat. 425][/us/stat/26/425], set out penalties for failure to give aid as required by [section 367 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s367]. See sections 2303, 2304 of Title 46.

    [Act Sept. 4, 1890, ch. 875][/us/act/1890-09-04/ch875], § 3, [26 Stat. 425][/us/stat/26/425], which provided that sections 367 and 368 of this title were to take effect at a time to be fixed by President by proclamation (effective Dec. 15, 1890, by Presidential Proclamation of Nov. 18, 1890, [26 Stat. 1561][/us/stat/26/1561]), was repealed by [Pub. L. 98–89][/us/pl/98/89], § 4(b), [97 Stat. 599][/us/stat/97/599].
