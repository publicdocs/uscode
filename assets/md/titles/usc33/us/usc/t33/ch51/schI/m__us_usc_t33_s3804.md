## § 3804. Compliance with international law

    Any action taken under this chapter shall be taken in accordance with treaties to which the United States is a party and other international obligations of the United States.

([Pub. L. 111–281, title X][/us/pl/111/281/tX], § 1014, Oct. 15, 2010, [124 Stat. 3025][/us/stat/124/3025].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title X of [Pub. L. 111–281][/us/pl/111/281], Oct. 15, 2010, [124 Stat. 3023][/us/stat/124/3023], which enacted this chapter and repealed chapter 37 (§ 2401 et seq.) of this title.
