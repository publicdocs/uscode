## §§ 201 to 213. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(a)][/us/pl/96/591/s8/a], Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435][/us/stat/94/3435]

    Section 201, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 100][/us/stat/30/100], related to ascertainment of risk of collision.

    Section 202, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 100][/us/stat/30/100], related to sailing vessels approaching one another.

    Section 203, acts [June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 100][/us/stat/30/100]; [Aug. 21, 1935, ch. 595, § 2][/us/act/1935-08-21/ch595/s2], [49 Stat. 669][/us/stat/49/669], related to steam vessels approaching, meeting, or passing one another.

    Section 204, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 101][/us/stat/30/101], related to steam vessels crossing.

    Section 205, acts [June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 101][/us/stat/30/101]; Nov. 5, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–764, § 1][/us/pl/89/764/s1], [80 Stat. 1313][/us/stat/80/1313], related to steam and sailing vessels meeting.

    Section 206, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 101][/us/stat/30/101], provided that vessel having the right of way was to keep course.

    Section 207, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 101][/us/stat/30/101], related to situation when a vessel crosses ahead of a vessel having the right-of-way.

    Section 208, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 101][/us/stat/30/101], related to duty of steam vessels to slacken speed.

    Section 209, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 101][/us/stat/30/101], provided that an overtaking vessel keep out of the way and defined the term “overtaking vessel”.

    Section 210, acts [June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 101][/us/stat/30/101]; Nov. 5, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–764, § 2][/us/pl/89/764/s2], [80 Stat. 1313][/us/stat/80/1313], related to operation of steam vessels in a narrow channel.

    Section 211, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 102][/us/stat/30/102], related to right of way of fishing vessels or boats.

    Section 212, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 102][/us/stat/30/102], provided for departure from the rules in special circumstances.

    Section 213, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1], [30 Stat. 102][/us/stat/30/102], related to signal to be given that a vessel’s engines are going at full speed astern.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Dec. 24, 1981, see [section 7 of Pub. L. 96–591][/us/pl/96/591/s7], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 1604 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s1604].
