###### §§ 221, 222. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591][/us/pl/96/591], § 8(a), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435][/us/stat/94/3435]

    Section 221, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4], § 1, [30 Stat. 102][/us/stat/30/102], related to usual additional precautions generally required.

    Section 222, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4][/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4], § 1, [30 Stat. 102][/us/stat/30/102], related to suspension of rules regarding the exhibition of lights on vessels of war or of the Coast Guard.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Dec. 24, 1981, see [section 7 of Pub. L. 96–591][/us/pl/96/591/s7], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 1604 of this title][/us/usc/t33/s1604].
