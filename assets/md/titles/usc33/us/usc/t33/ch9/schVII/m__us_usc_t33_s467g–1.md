## § 467g–1. Dam safety training

    At the request of any State that has or intends to develop a State dam safety program, the Director shall provide training for State dam safety staff and inspectors.

([Pub. L. 92–367, § 10][/us/pl/92/367/s10], as added [Pub. L. 107–310, § 5(2)][/us/pl/107/310/s5/2], Dec. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 2453][/us/stat/116/2453].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 10 of Pub. L. 92–367][/us/pl/92/367/s10] was renumbered section 11, and is classified to [section 467h of this title][/us/usc/t33/s467h].

    Another prior [section 10 of Pub. L. 92–367][/us/pl/92/367/s10] was classified to [section 467i of this title][/us/usc/t33/s467i] prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–303][/us/pl/104/303].
