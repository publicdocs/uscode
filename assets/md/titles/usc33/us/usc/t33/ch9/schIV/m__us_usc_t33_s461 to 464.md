## §§ 461 to 464. Repealed. [Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 854, § 1636][/us/act/1901-03-03/ch854/s1636], [31 Stat. 1434][/us/stat/31/1434]

    Section 461, [act May 19, 1896, ch. 208, § 1][/us/act/1896-05-19/ch208/s1], [29 Stat. 126][/us/stat/29/126], prohibited deposit of ballast, dirt, ashes or oyster shells into Potomac River except for purpose of making a wharf and then only after approval.

    Section 462, [act May 19, 1896, ch. 208, § 2][/us/act/1896-05-19/ch208/s2], [29 Stat. 127][/us/stat/29/127], prohibited deposit of dead fish, dead animals, fruits, vegetables, ice, snow, filth or trash of any kind into Potomac River.

    Section 463, [act May 19, 1896, ch. 208, § 3][/us/act/1896-05-19/ch208/s3], [29 Stat. 127][/us/stat/29/127], related to penalties.

    Section 464, [act May 19, 1896, ch. 208, § 4][/us/act/1896-05-19/ch208/s4], [29 Stat. 127][/us/stat/29/127], provided that none of these provisions be construed to interfere with any work or improvements in harbor or river area.
