## § 50920. User fees

    The Secretary of Transportation may collect a user fee for a regulatory or other service conducted under this chapter only if specifically authorized by this chapter.

([Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(e)][/us/pl/103/272/s1/e], July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1342][/us/stat/108/1342], [§ 70118 of title 49][/us/usc/t49/s70118]; renumbered § 70118 then [§ 50920 of title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50920], [Pub.
