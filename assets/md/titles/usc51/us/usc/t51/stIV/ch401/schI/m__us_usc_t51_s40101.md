## § 40101. Definition of institution of higher education

    In this chapter, the term “institution of higher education” has the meaning given the term by section 101 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ([20 U.S.C. 1001][/us/usc/t20/s1001]).

([Pub. L. 111–314, § 3][/us/pl/111/314/s3], Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3378][/us/stat/124/3378].)
