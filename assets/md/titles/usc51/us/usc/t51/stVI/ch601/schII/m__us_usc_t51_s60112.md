## § 60112. Transfer of Landsat 6 program responsibilities

    The responsibilities of the Secretary with respect to Landsat 6 shall be transferred to the Landsat Program Management, as agreed to between the Secretary and the Landsat Program Management, pursuant to [section 60111 of this title][/us/usc/t51/s60111].

([Pub. L. 111–314, § 3][/us/pl/111/314/s3], Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3413][/us/stat/124/3413].)
