## § 20101. Short title

    This chapter may be cited as the “National Aeronautics and Space Act”.

([Pub. L. 111–314, § 3][/us/pl/111/314/s3], Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3330][/us/stat/124/3330].)
