## § 1515. Regulations

    The Secretary shall promulgate regulations to carry out this subchapter no later than six months after June 30, 1980.

([Pub. L. 96–294, title VI][/us/pl/96/294/tVI], § 615, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 764][/us/stat/94/764].)
