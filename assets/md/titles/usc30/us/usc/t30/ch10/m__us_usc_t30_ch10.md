## CHAPTER 10—COAL MINE SAFETY

 __Repeal of Chapter__ 

    Chapter repealed by [Pub. L. 91–173, title V][/us/pl/91/173/tV], § 509, Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 803][/us/stat/83/803], on the operative date of sections 811 to 821 and 861 to 878 of this title, which became operative ninety days after the enactment of [Pub.
