## § 541f. Disbursement of moneys

    All moneys received under the provisions of this chapter shall be paid into the Treasury of the United States and distributed in the same manner as provided in [section 191 of this title][/us/usc/t30/s191] and sections 437, 438, and 439  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of title 48.

([Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 795, § 7][/us/act/1955-08-11/ch795/s7], [69 Stat. 681][/us/stat/69/681].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Sections 437, 438, and 439 of title 48, referred to in text, were repealed by [Pub. L. 86–252, § 1][/us/pl/86/252/s1], Sept. 9, 1959, [73 Stat. 490][/us/stat/73/490].
