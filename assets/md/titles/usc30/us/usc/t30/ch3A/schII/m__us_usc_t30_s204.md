## § 204. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–377, § 13(a)][/us/pl/94/377/s13/a], Aug. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1090][/us/stat/90/1090]

    Section, [act Feb. 25, 1920, ch. 85, § 4][/us/act/1920-02-25/ch85/s4], [41 Stat. 439][/us/stat/41/439], provided for the leasing of an additional tract of land or coal deposit, not to exceed 2560 acres, upon a showing by a lessee that all workable deposits of coal would be exhausted, worked out, or removed within three years thereafter.

 __Savings Provision__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–377, § 13(a)][/us/pl/94/377/s13/a], Aug. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1090][/us/stat/90/1090], provided that the repeal of this section is subject to valid existing rights.
