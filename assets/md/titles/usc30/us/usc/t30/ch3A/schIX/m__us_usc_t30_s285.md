## § 285. Laws applicable

    The general provisions of sections 182 to 184, 185 to 188, 189 to 192, 193, and 194  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title, are made applicable to permits and leases under this subchapter.

([Feb. 7, 1927, ch. 66, § 5][/us/act/1927-02-07/ch66/s5], [44 Stat. 1058][/us/stat/44/1058]; [Aug. 8, 1946, ch. 916, § 11][/us/act/1946-08-08/ch916/s11], [60 Stat. 957][/us/stat/60/957].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 194 of this title][/us/usc/t30/s194], referred to in text, was repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 644][/us/stat/80/644].

 __Codification__ 

    Provision of this section that [section 193 of this title][/us/usc/t30/s193] was amended to include deposits of potassium was omitted from this section as executed to [section 193 of this title][/us/usc/t30/s193].

    Section was not enacted as part of [act Feb. 25, 1920, ch. 85][/us/act/1920-02-25/ch85], [41 Stat. 437][/us/stat/41/437], known as the Mineral Leasing Act, which comprises this chapter.

 __Amendments__ 

    1946—Act Aug. 8, 1946, struck out reference to [section 181 of this title][/us/usc/t30/s181].

 __Savings Provision__ 

    See note set out under [section 181 of this title][/us/usc/t30/s181].
