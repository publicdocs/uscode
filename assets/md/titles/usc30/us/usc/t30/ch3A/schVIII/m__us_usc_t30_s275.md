## § 275. Laws applicable

    The general provisions of sections 181 to 184, 185 to 188, 189 to 192, 193, and 194  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title, are made applicable to permits and leases under this subchapter, sections 181 and 193 of this title being amended to include deposits of sulphur, and [section 184 of this title][/us/usc/t30/s184] being amended so as to prohibit any person, association, or corporation from taking or holding more than three sulphur permits or leases in any one State during the life of such permits or leases.

([Apr. 17, 1926, ch. 158, § 5][/us/act/1926-04-17/ch158/s5], [44 Stat. 302][/us/stat/44/302].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 194 of this title][/us/usc/t30/s194], referred to in text, was repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 644][/us/stat/80/644].

 __Codification__ 

    Section was not enacted as part of [act Feb. 25, 1920, ch. 85][/us/act/1920-02-25/ch85], [41 Stat. 437][/us/stat/41/437], known as the Mineral Leasing Act, which comprises this chapter.
