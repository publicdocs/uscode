## § 4e. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act May 15, 1936, ch. 397, § 3][/us/act/1936-05-15/ch397/s3], [49 Stat. 1275][/us/stat/49/1275], appropriated $100,000 for carrying out provisions of sections 4c and 4d of this title to be expended during certain fiscal years, the last ending June 30, 1939.

§§ 4f to 4o. __Transferred__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 4f to 4o were transferred to sections 451 to 460, respectively, of this title, and subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 91–173, title V, § 509][/us/pl/91/173/s509], Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 803][/us/stat/83/803].
