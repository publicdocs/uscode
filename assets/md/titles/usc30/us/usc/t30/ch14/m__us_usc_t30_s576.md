## § 576. Authorization of appropriations

    There is hereby authorized to be appropriated such amounts as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

([July 15, 1955, ch. 369][/us/act/1955-07-15/ch369], § 5, formerly § 6, [69 Stat. 353][/us/stat/69/353]; renumbered § 5, [Pub. L. 105–362, title IX][/us/pl/105/362/tIX], § 901(i)(2), Nov. 10, 1998, [112 Stat. 3290][/us/stat/112/3290].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 5 of [act July 15, 1955, ch. 369][/us/act/1955-07-15/ch369], was classified to [section 575 of this title][/us/usc/t30/s575], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–362][/us/pl/105/362], § 901(i)(1).
