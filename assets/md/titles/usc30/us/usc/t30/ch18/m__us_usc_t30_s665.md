## § 665. Sites for conducting research; availability of personnel and facilities

    Research authorized by this chapter may be conducted wherever suitable personnel and facilities are available.

([Pub. L. 86–599, § 3][/us/pl/86/599/s3], formerly § 5, July 7, 1960, [74 Stat. 337][/us/stat/74/337]; renumbered § 3, [Pub. L. 109–58, title X, § 1009(a)(1)(D)][/us/pl/109/58/s1009/a/1/D], Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 934][/us/stat/119/934].)

 __Prior Provisions__
