## § 41. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], § 21, [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862]

    Section, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VIII][/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVIII], § 4, [40 Stat. 226][/us/stat/40/226], defined “Foreign government”. See [section 11 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s11], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s1], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
