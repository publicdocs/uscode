 __Codification__ 

    [Act Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 426][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch426], [43 Stat. 1110][/us/stat/43/1110], as completely amended, renumbered, and revised by [Pub. L. 86–777][/us/pl/86/777], Sept. 13, 1960, [74 Stat. 918][/us/stat/74/918], is classified to section 167 et seq. of this title.

    Section 161, acts [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 426, § 1][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch426/s1], [43 Stat. 1110][/us/stat/43/1110]; [Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 355][/us/act/1927-03-03/ch355], [44 Stat. 1387][/us/stat/44/1387]; [Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 895][/us/act/1937-09-01/ch895], [50 Stat. 885][/us/stat/50/885], authorized Secretary of the Interior to acquire and reserve helium-gas lands and to produce and store helium gas. See section 3 of act Mar. 3, 1925, as amended by [Pub. L. 86–777][/us/pl/86/777], which is classified to [section 167a of this title][/us/usc/t50/s167a].

    Section 162, acts [Feb. 12, 1925, ch. 225][/us/act/1925-02-12/ch225], title I, [43 Stat. 908][/us/stat/43/908]; [July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], title II, § 205(a), [61 Stat. 501][/us/stat/61/501], authorized Navy Department to acquire helium-gas lands and to produce and experiment with helium gas.

    Section 163, acts [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 426, § 2][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch426/s2], [43 Stat. 1111][/us/stat/43/1111]; [Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 355][/us/act/1927-03-03/ch355], [44 Stat. 1387][/us/stat/44/1387]; [Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 895][/us/act/1937-09-01/ch895], [50 Stat. 886][/us/stat/50/886], authorized Bureau of Mines to produce helium gas. See section 4 of act Mar. 3, 1925, as amended by [Pub. L. 86–777][/us/pl/86/777], which is classified to [section 167b of this title][/us/usc/t50/s167b].

    Section 164, acts [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 426, § 3][/us/act/1925-03-03/ch426/s3], [43 Stat. 1111][/us/stat/43/1111]; [Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 355][/us/act/1927-03-03/ch355], [44 Stat. 1387][/us/stat/44/1387]; [Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 895][/us/act/1937-09-01/ch895], [50 Stat. 886][/us/stat/50/886], related to disposal of helium by sale, upon request of Army or Navy or other Federal Government agencies, or for medicinal, scientific or commercial use, to deposit and use of funds obtained by sale of gas, and to an annual report to Congress by Secretary of the Interior on said funds. See section 6 of act Mar. 3, 1925, as amended by [Pub. L. 86–777][/us/pl/86/777], which is classified to [section 167d of this title][/us/usc/t50/s167d].
