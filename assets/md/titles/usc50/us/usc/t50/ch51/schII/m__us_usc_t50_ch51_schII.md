### SUBCHAPTER II—TITLE II OF WAR CLAIMS ACT OF 1948

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 87–846][/us/pl/87/846], § 103, Oct. 22, 1962, [76 Stat. 1107][/us/stat/76/1107], added “title II” (§§ 201 to 217) to the War Claims Act of 1948 ([act July 3, 1948, ch. 826][/us/act/1948-07-03/ch826]) without supplying a name for such title, which for purposes of codification has been set out as this subchapter.
