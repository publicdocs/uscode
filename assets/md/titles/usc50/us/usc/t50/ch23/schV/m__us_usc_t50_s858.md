## § 858. Applicability to Canal Zone

    This subchapter applies to and within the Canal Zone.

([Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 849][/us/act/1956-08-01/ch849], § 10, as added [Pub. L. 87–845][/us/pl/87/845], § 13, Oct. 18, 1962, [76A Stat. 700][/us/stat/76A/700].)

 __References in Text__ 

    For definition of Canal Zone, referred to in text, see [section 3602(b) of Title 22][/us/usc/t22/s3602/b], Foreign Relations and Intercourse.

 __Codification__ 

    Section was not enacted as part of the Internal Security Act of 1950 which comprises subchapters I to III of this chapter.

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Jan. 2, 1963, see [section 25 of Pub. L. 87–845][/us/pl/87/845/s25], set out as an Effective Date of 1962 Amendment note under [section 414 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s414], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
