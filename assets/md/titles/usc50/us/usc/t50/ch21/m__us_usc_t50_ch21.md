## CHAPTER 21—ABACÁ PRODUCTION

§§ 541 to 546. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 541 to 546, [act Aug. 10, 1950, ch. 673][/us/act/1950-08-10/ch673], §§ 2–7, [64 Stat. 435–437][/us/stat/64/435-437], terminated not later than ten years after Apr. 1, 1950. See Effective and Termination Date note below.

    Section 541 related to Congressional declaration of policy.

    Section 542 related to production program of abacá.

    Section 543 related to administration.

    Section 544 related to financing.

    Section 545 related to disposal of property.

    Section 546 related to reports to Congress.

 __Effective and Termination Dates__ 

    [Act Aug. 10, 1950, ch. 673, § 8][/us/act/1950-08-10/ch673/s8], [64 Stat. 437][/us/stat/64/437], provided that this chapter become effective Apr. 1, 1950, and remain effective for ten years thereafter, unless Congress or President direct earlier termination of operations, and for such further period as necessary to earliest practicable liquidation of operations under this chapter.
