## §§ 1241 to 1243. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A][/us/pl/85/861/s36A], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569][/us/stat/72/1569]

    Section 1241, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257], title III, § 316, [68 Stat. 1159][/us/stat/68/1159], provided for promotion to first lieutenant. See section 14301 et seq. of Title 10, Armed Forces.

    Section 1242, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257], title III, § 317, [68 Stat. 1159][/us/stat/68/1159], provided for promotion to captain, major, lieutenant colonel, and colonel to fill unit vacancies. See section 14301 et seq. of Title 10.

    Section 1243, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257], title III, § 318, [68 Stat. 1159][/us/stat/68/1159], provided for promotion to brigadier general and major general to fill vacancies. See [section 14315 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s14315].
