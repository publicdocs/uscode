###### §§ 1181, 1182. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 36A, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569][/us/stat/72/1569]

    Section 1181, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title I][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tI], § 102, [68 Stat. 1149][/us/stat/68/1149], defined terms used in the Reserve Officer Personnel Act of 1954. See sections 101(d)(4), 12003 to 12005, 12202, 12642, 12646, 12647, and 12772 of Title 10, Armed Forces, and [section 720 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s720], Coast Guard.

    Section 1182, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VII][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVII], § 703, [68 Stat. 1189][/us/stat/68/1189], contained savings provisions. See [section 723 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s723].
