## §§ 1251 to 1255. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 36A, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569][/us/stat/72/1569]

    Section 1251, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII], § 319, [68 Stat. 1160][/us/stat/68/1160], related to examination for Federal recognition upon unit vacancy promotion. See [section 309 of Title 32][/us/usc/t32/s309], National Guard.

    Section 1252, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII], § 320, [68 Stat. 1160][/us/stat/68/1160], related to extension of automatic Federal recognition to higher grade. See [section 310 of Title 32][/us/usc/t32/s310].

    Section 1253, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII], § 321, [68 Stat. 1160][/us/stat/68/1160], provided for promotion to higher grade upon recognition. See [section 14308(f) of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s14308/f], Armed Forces.

    Section 1254, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII], § 322, [68 Stat. 1161][/us/stat/68/1161], provided for promotion upon transfer to Army Reserve. See [section 12213 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s12213].

    Section 1255, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III][/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII], § 323, [68 Stat. 1161][/us/stat/68/1161], provided for appointment of adjutants general and assistant adjutants general as Reserve officers. See [section 12215(a) of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s12215/a].
