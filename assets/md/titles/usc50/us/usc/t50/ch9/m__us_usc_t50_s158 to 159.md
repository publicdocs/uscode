## §§ 158 to 159. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 158, [act Aug. 1, 1947, ch. 433, § 1(b)][/us/act/1947-08-01/ch433/s1/b], (c), as added [July 13, 1949, ch. 332, § 1][/us/act/1949-07-13/ch332/s1], [63 Stat. 410][/us/stat/63/410], related to professional and scientific service on the Committee and was transferred to section 1161 of former Title 5, Executive Departments and Government Officers and Employees, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].

    Section 158a, [act Aug. 1, 1947, ch. 433, § 2][/us/act/1947-08-01/ch433/s2], [61 Stat. 715][/us/stat/61/715], related to classification of positions and appointments and was transferred to section 1162 of former Title 5, Executive Departments and Government Officers and Employees, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378], and reenacted as [section 3104(b) of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s3104/b], Government Organization and Employees.

    Section 159, acts [Aug. 1, 1947, ch. 433, § 3][/us/act/1947-08-01/ch433/s3], [61 Stat. 715][/us/stat/61/715]; [July 13, 1949, ch. 332, § 2][/us/act/1949-07-13/ch332/s2], [63 Stat. 411][/us/stat/63/411], related to reports to Congress and confidential information and was transferred to section 1163 of former Title 5, Executive Departments and Government Officers and Employees, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378], and reenacted as [section 3104(c) of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s3104/c], Government Organization and Employees.
