## § 3367. Requirement for efficient use by intelligence community of open-source intelligence

    The Director of National Intelligence shall ensure that the intelligence community makes efficient and effective use of open-source information and analysis.

([Pub. L. 108–458, title I, § 1052(b)][/us/pl/108/458/s1052/b], Dec. 17, 2004, [118 Stat. 3683][/us/stat/118/3683].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified as a note under section 403–1 of chapter 15 of this title prior to editorial reclassification in chapter 45 of this title.
