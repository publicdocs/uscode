## §§ 1471 to 1476. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 1471, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III][/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII], § 301, [69 Stat. 588][/us/stat/69/588], which related to definitions, was transferred to [section 1973cc–21 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–21], The Public Health and Welfare.

    Section 1472, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III][/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII], § 302, [69 Stat. 588][/us/stat/69/588], which related to free postage, was transferred to [section 1973cc–22 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–22].

    Section 1473, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III][/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII], § 303, [69 Stat. 588][/us/stat/69/588], which related to prevention of fraud and coercion, was transferred to [section 1973cc–23 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–23].

    Section 1474, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III][/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII], § 304, [69 Stat. 589][/us/stat/69/589], which related to acts done in good faith, was transferred to [section 1973cc–24 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–24].

    Section 1475, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III][/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII], § 305, [69 Stat. 589][/us/stat/69/589], which related to undue influence and free discussion, was transferred to [section 1973cc–25 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–25].

    Section 1476, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III][/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII], § 308, [69 Stat. 589][/us/stat/69/589], which authorized appropriations, was transferred to [section 1973cc–26 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–26].

    Sections 1471 to 1476 were formerly classified to sections 2191 to 2196 of Title 5 prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
