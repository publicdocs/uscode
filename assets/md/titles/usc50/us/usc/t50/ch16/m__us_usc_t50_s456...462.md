## §§ 456 to 462. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 456 to 462 were omitted in the general amendment of [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811] by [Pub. L. 93–155, title VIII][/us/pl/93/155/tVIII], § 809, Nov. 16, 1973, [87 Stat. 617][/us/stat/87/617]. See sections 453 to 455 of this title.

    Section 456, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], § 7, [62 Stat. 1227][/us/stat/62/1227], related to powers of Secretary of Defense respecting property in national industrial reserve. See [section 453 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s453].

    Section 457, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], § 8, [62 Stat. 1227][/us/stat/62/1227], related to transportation, maintenance, disposition, etc., of transferred property. See [section 453 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s453].

    Section 458, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], § 9, [62 Stat. 1227][/us/stat/62/1227], related to limitation on acquisition of property.

    Section 459, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], § 10, [62 Stat. 1227][/us/stat/62/1227], provided for an Industrial Reserve Review Committee, its composition, appointment, tenure, and compensation and related to inapplicability of certain laws to Committee solely by reason of appointment to and membership on such Committee.

    Section 460, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], § 11, [62 Stat. 1228][/us/stat/62/1228], related to duties of Industrial Reserve Review Committee, including making of recommendations to Secretary of Defense.

    Section 461, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], § 12, [62 Stat. 1228][/us/stat/62/1228], provided for reports to Congress. See [section 454 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s454].

    Section 462, acts [July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], § 14, [62 Stat. 1228][/us/stat/62/1228]; [June 30, 1949, ch. 288][/us/act/1949-06-30/ch288], title I, § 103, [63 Stat. 380][/us/stat/63/380], authorized appropriations. See [section 455 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s455].
