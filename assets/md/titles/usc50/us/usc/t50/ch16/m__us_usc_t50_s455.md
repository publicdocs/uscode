## § 455. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as the Congress may from time to time determine to be necessary to enable the Secretary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

([July 2, 1948, ch. 811, § 6][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811/s6], [62 Stat. 1226][/us/stat/62/1226]; [Pub. L. 93–155, title VIII, § 809][/us/pl/93/155/s809], Nov. 16, 1973, [87 Stat. 618][/us/stat/87/618].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 14 of [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], [62 Stat. 1228][/us/stat/62/1228] (classified to [section 462 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s462]) prior to the general amendment of act July 2, 1948, by [Pub. L. 93–155][/us/pl/93/155].

 __Amendments__ 

    1973—[Pub. L. 93–155][/us/pl/93/155] substituted provisions respecting authorization of appropriations, for prior provisions respecting acceptance of plants by Administrator of General Services, disposition of plants, and conditions of lease, now covered in [section 453 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s453].

§§ 456 to 462. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 456 to 462 were omitted in the general amendment of [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811] by [Pub. L. 93–155, title VIII, § 809][/us/pl/93/155/s809], Nov. 16, 1973, [87 Stat. 617][/us/stat/87/617]. See sections 453 to 455 of this title.

    Section 456, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811, § 7][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811/s7], [62 Stat. 1227][/us/stat/62/1227], related to powers of Secretary of Defense respecting property in national industrial reserve. See [section 453 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s453].

    Section 457, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811, § 8][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811/s8], [62 Stat. 1227][/us/stat/62/1227], related to transportation, maintenance, disposition, etc., of transferred property. See [section 453 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s453].

    Section 458, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811, § 9][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811/s9], [62 Stat. 1227][/us/stat/62/1227], related to limitation on acquisition of property.

    Section 459, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811, § 10][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811/s10], [62 Stat. 1227][/us/stat/62/1227], provided for an Industrial Reserve Review Committee, its composition, appointment, tenure, and compensation and related to inapplicability of certain laws to Committee solely by reason of appointment to and membership on such Committee.

    Section 460, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811, § 11][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811/s11], [62 Stat. 1228][/us/stat/62/1228], related to duties of Industrial Reserve Review Committee, including making of recommendations to Secretary of Defense.

    Section 461, [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811, § 12][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811/s12], [62 Stat. 1228][/us/stat/62/1228], provided for reports to Congress. See [section 454 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s454].

    Section 462, acts [July 2, 1948, ch. 811, § 14][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811/s14], [62 Stat. 1228][/us/stat/62/1228]; [June 30, 1949, ch. 288][/us/act/1949-06-30/ch288], title I, § 103, [63 Stat. 380][/us/stat/63/380], authorized appropriations. See [section 455 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s455].
