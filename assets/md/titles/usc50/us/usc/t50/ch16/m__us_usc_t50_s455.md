## § 455. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as the Congress may from time to time determine to be necessary to enable the Secretary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

([July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], § 6, [62 Stat. 1226][/us/stat/62/1226]; [Pub. L. 93–155, title VIII][/us/pl/93/155/tVIII], § 809, Nov. 16, 1973, [87 Stat. 618][/us/stat/87/618].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 14 of [act July 2, 1948, ch. 811][/us/act/1948-07-02/ch811], [62 Stat. 1228][/us/stat/62/1228] (classified to [section 462 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s462]) prior to the general amendment of act July 2, 1948, by [Pub. L. 93–155][/us/pl/93/155].

 __Amendments__ 

    1973—[Pub. L. 93–155][/us/pl/93/155] substituted provisions respecting authorization of appropriations, for prior provisions respecting acceptance of plants by Administrator of General Services, disposition of plants, and conditions of lease, now covered in [section 453 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s453].
