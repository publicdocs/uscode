## § 2354. Termination

After September 30, 1999, the President—

    (1) is not required to maintain a National Coordinator for Nonproliferation Matters under [section 2351 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s2351]; and

    (2) may terminate the Committee on Nonproliferation established under [section 2352 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s2352].

([Pub. L. 104–201, div. A, title XIV, § 1444][/us/pl/104/201/s1444], Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2730][/us/stat/110/2730]; [Pub. L. 105–261, div. A, title X, § 1069(c)(3)][/us/pl/105/261/s1069/c/3], Oct. 17, 1998, [112 Stat. 2136][/us/stat/112/2136].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1998—[Pub. L. 105–261][/us/pl/105/261] made technical amendments to references in original act which appear in par. (1) as reference to [section 2351 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s2351] and in par. (2) as reference to [section 2352 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s2352].
