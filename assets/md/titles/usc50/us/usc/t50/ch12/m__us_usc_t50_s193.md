## § 193. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], § 21, [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862]

    Section, acts [June 15, 1917, ch. 30][/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30], title II, § 3, [40 Stat. 220][/us/stat/40/220]; [Mar. 28, 1940, ch. 72][/us/act/1940-03-28/ch72], § 3(b), [54 Stat. 79][/us/stat/54/79], related to destruction of, injury to, or improper use of vessels. See [section 2274 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s2274], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s1], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
