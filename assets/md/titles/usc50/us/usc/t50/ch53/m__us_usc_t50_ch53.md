[Mar. 3, 1921, ch. 136][/us/act/1921-03-03/ch136], [41 Stat. 1359][/us/stat/41/1359], declaring that certain Acts of Congress, joint resolutions, and proclamations should be construed as though the World War had ended and the then present or existing emergency expired.
