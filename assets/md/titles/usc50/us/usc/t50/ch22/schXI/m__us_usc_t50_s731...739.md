## §§ 731 to 739. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], § 53, [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641]

    Section 731, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169], § 1, [64 Stat. 143][/us/stat/64/143], related to courts of inquiry. See [section 935 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s935], Armed Forces.

    Section 732, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169], § 1, [64 Stat. 143][/us/stat/64/143], related to authority to administer oaths and to act as notary. See [section 936 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s936].

    Section 733, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169], § 1, [64 Stat. 144][/us/stat/64/144], required reading and explanation of certain sections of this chapter. See [section 937 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s937].

    Section 734, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169], § 1, [64 Stat. 144][/us/stat/64/144], related to complaints of wrongs. See [section 938 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s938].

    Section 735, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169], § 1, [64 Stat. 144][/us/stat/64/144], related to redress of injuries to property. See [section 939 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s939].

    Section 736, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169], § 1, [64 Stat. 145][/us/stat/64/145], authorized President to delegate his authority. See [section 940 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s940].

    Section 737, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169], § 8, [64 Stat. 146][/us/stat/64/146], provided for oath of enlistment. See [section 502 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s502].

    Section 738, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169], § 9, [64 Stat. 146][/us/stat/64/146], provided for removal of suits. See [section 1442a of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s1442a], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 739, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169], § 10, [64 Stat. 146][/us/stat/64/146], related to dismissal of officers. See sections 1161 and 6408 of Title 10.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1957, see [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], § 53 footnote, [70A Stat. 680][/us/stat/70A/680].
