### SUBCHAPTER IV—COURTS-MARTIAL JURISDICTION

§§ 576 to 581. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53__  __][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53]__  __,__  __[__  __70A Stat. 641__  __][/us/stat/70A/641]__ 

    Section 576, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1], [64 Stat. 113][/us/stat/64/113], classified types of courts-martial. See [section 816 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s816], Armed Forces.

    Section 577, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1], [64 Stat. 114][/us/stat/64/114], related to jurisdiction of courts-martial in general. See [section 817 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s817].

    Section 578, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1], [64 Stat. 114][/us/stat/64/114], related to jurisdiction of general courts-martial. See [section 818 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s818].

    Section 579, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1], [64 Stat. 114][/us/stat/64/114], related to jurisdiction of special courts-martial. See [section 819 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s819].

    Section 580, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1], [64 Stat. 114][/us/stat/64/114], related to jurisdiction of summary courts-martial. See [section 820 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s820].

    Section 581, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1][/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1], [64 Stat. 115][/us/stat/64/115], provided that jurisdiction of courts-martial was not exclusive. See [section 821 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s821].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1957, see [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53] footnote, [70A Stat. 680][/us/stat/70A/680].
