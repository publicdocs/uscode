## §§ 45 to 45d. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], § 21, [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862]

    Section 45, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2][/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2], § 1, [52 Stat. 3][/us/stat/52/3], related to photographing of defensive installations. See sections 795 to 797 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

    Section 45a, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2][/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2], § 2, [52 Stat. 3][/us/stat/52/3], related to photographing, etc., from aircraft. See [section 796 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s796].

    Section 45b, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2][/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2], § 3, [52 Stat. 3][/us/stat/52/3], related to reproducing, publishing, selling uncensored copies. See [section 797 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s797].

    Section 45c, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2][/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2], § 4, [52 Stat. 4][/us/stat/52/4], related to definitions of “aircraft”, “post”, “camp”, and “station”. See sections 795 and 796 of Title 18.

    Section 45d, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2][/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2], § 5, [52 Stat. 4][/us/stat/52/4], related to geographical application of law.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal of sections 45 to 45d effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s1], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
