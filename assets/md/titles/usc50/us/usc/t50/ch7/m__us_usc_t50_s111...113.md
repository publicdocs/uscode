## §§ 111 to 113. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], § 21, [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862]

    Section 111, [act Apr. 19, 1918, ch. 58][/us/act/1918-04-19/ch58], § 1, [40 Stat. 533][/us/stat/40/533], related to prohibited acts affecting homing pigeons owned by United States. See [section 45 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s45], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

    Section 112, [act Apr. 19, 1918, ch. 58][/us/act/1918-04-19/ch58], § 2, [40 Stat. 533][/us/stat/40/533], related to possession of pigeons as evidence of violation of law. See [section 45 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s45].

    Section 113, [act Apr. 19, 1918, ch. 58][/us/act/1918-04-19/ch58], § 3, [40 Stat. 533][/us/stat/40/533], related to punishment. See [section 45 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s45].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal of sections 111 to 113 effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s1], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
