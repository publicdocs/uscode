## § 3046. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–458, title I][/us/pl/108/458/tI], § 1075, Dec. 17, 2004, [118 Stat. 3694][/us/stat/118/3694]

    Section, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, title I][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343/tI], § 111, formerly § 121, as added [Pub. L. 104–201, div. A, title XI][/us/pl/104/201/dA/tXI], § 1114(c), Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2685][/us/stat/110/2685]; renumbered § 111, [Pub. L. 105–107, title III][/us/pl/105/107/tIII], § 303(b), Nov. 20, 1997, [111 Stat. 2252][/us/stat/111/2252], related to collection tasking authority of Director of Central Intelligence.

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 404f of this title][/us/usc/t50/s404f] and repealed prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    For Determination by President that repeal take effect on Apr. 21, 2005, see Memorandum of President of the United States, Apr. 21, 2005, 70 F.R. 23925, set out as a note under [section 3001 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s3001].

    Repeal effective not later than six months after Dec. 17, 2004, except as otherwise expressly provided, see [section 1097(a) of Pub. L. 108–458][/us/pl/108/458/s1097/a], set out in an Effective Date of 2004 Amendment; Transition Provisions note under [section 3001 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s3001].
