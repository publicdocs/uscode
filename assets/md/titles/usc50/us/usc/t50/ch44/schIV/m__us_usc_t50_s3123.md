## § 3123. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–277, title III, § 310(a)(4)(A)][/us/pl/112/277/s310/a/4/A], Jan. 14, 2013, [126 Stat. 2475][/us/stat/126/2475]

    Section, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], title VI, § 603, as added [Pub. L. 97–200, § 2(a)][/us/pl/97/200/s2/a], June 23, 1982, [96 Stat. 123][/us/stat/96/123]; amended [Pub. L. 107–306, title III, § 353(b)(1)(B)][/us/pl/107/306/s353/b/1/B], title VIII, § 811(b)(1)(E), Nov. 27, 2002, [116 Stat. 2402][/us/stat/116/2402], 2422; [Pub. L. 108–458, title I, § 1071(a)(1)(CC)][/us/pl/108/458/s1071/a/1/CC], Dec. 17, 2004, [118 Stat. 3689][/us/stat/118/3689]; [Pub. L. 111–259, title III, § 363(b)][/us/pl/111/259/s363/b], Oct. 7, 2010, [124 Stat. 2702][/us/stat/124/2702], required an annual report by the President to Congress on measures to protect identities of covert agents, with an exemption from disclosure.

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to section 423 of chapter 15 of this title and repealed prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering in chapter 44 of this title.
