## § 3123. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–277, title III][/us/pl/112/277/tIII], § 310(a)(4)(A), Jan. 14, 2013, [126 Stat. 2475][/us/stat/126/2475]

    Section, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, title VI][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343/tVI], § 603, as added [Pub. L. 97–200][/us/pl/97/200], § 2(a), June 23, 1982, [96 Stat. 123][/us/stat/96/123]; amended [Pub. L. 107–306, title III][/us/pl/107/306/tIII], § 353(b)(1)(B), title VIII, § 811(b)(1)(E), Nov. 27, 2002, [116 Stat. 2402][/us/stat/116/2402], 2422; [Pub. L. 108–458, title I][/us/pl/108/458/tI], § 1071(a)(1)(CC), Dec. 17, 2004, [118 Stat. 3689][/us/stat/118/3689]; [Pub. L. 111–259, title III][/us/pl/111/259/tIII], § 363(b), Oct. 7, 2010, [124 Stat. 2702][/us/stat/124/2702], required an annual report by the President to Congress on measures to protect identities of covert agents, with an exemption from disclosure.

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 423 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s423] and repealed prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
