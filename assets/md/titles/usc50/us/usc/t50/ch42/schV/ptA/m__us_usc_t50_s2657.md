## § 2657. Submittal of annual report on status of security functions at nuclear weapons facilities

    Not later than September 1 each year, the Secretary of Energy shall submit to the congressional defense committees the report entitled “Annual Report to the President on the Status of Safeguards and Security of Domestic Nuclear Weapons Facilities”, or any successor report to such report.

([Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4506][/us/pl/107/314/s4506], formerly [Pub. L. 105–85, div. C, title XXXI, § 3162][/us/pl/105/85/s3162], Nov. 18, 1997, [111 Stat. 2049][/us/stat/111/2049]; [Pub. L. 106–65, div. C, title XXXI, § 3142(h)(2)][/us/pl/106/65/s3142/h/2], Oct. 5, 1999, [113 Stat. 934][/us/stat/113/934]; renumbered [Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4506][/us/pl/107/314/s4506], and amended [Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(h)(7)][/us/pl/108/136/s3141/h/7], Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1773][/us/stat/117/1773].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly set out as a note under [section 7274m of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s7274m], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 108–136][/us/pl/108/136].

 __Amendments__ 

    2003—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 108–136, § 3141(h)(7)(D)][/us/pl/108/136/s3141/h/7/D], which directed the amendment of subsec. (b) by inserting “of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1998 ([Public Law 105–85][/us/pl/105/85]; [111 Stat. 2048][/us/stat/111/2048]; [42 U.S.C. 7251][/us/usc/t42/s7251] note)” after “section 3161”, could not be executed because of the repeal of subsec. (b) by [Pub. L. 106–65][/us/pl/106/65]. See 1999 Amendment note below.

    1999—[Pub. L. 106–65][/us/pl/106/65] struck out subsec. (a) designation and heading and struck out heading and text of subsec. (b). Text read as follows: “The Secretary shall include with each report submitted under subsection (a) in fiscal years 1998 through 2000 any comments on such report by the members of the Department of Energy Security Management Board established under section 3161 that such members consider appropriate.”
