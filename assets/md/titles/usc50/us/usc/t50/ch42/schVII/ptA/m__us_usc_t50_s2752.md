## § 2752. Funds available for all national security programs of the Department of Energy

    Subject to the provisions of appropriation Acts and [section 2742 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s2742], amounts appropriated pursuant to a DOE national security authorization for management and support activities and for general plant projects are available for use, when necessary, in connection with all national security programs of the Department of Energy.

([Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVII, § 4712][/us/pl/107/314/s4712], formerly div. C, title XXXVI, § 3631, Dec. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 2762][/us/stat/116/2762]; renumbered div. D, title XLVII, § 4712, and amended [Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(j)(2)(A)][/us/pl/108/136/s3141/j/2/A]–(C), (D)(vi), Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1781][/us/stat/117/1781].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 7386k of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s7386k], The Public Health and Welfare, prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 108–136][/us/pl/108/136].

 __Amendments__ 

    2003—[Pub. L. 108–136, § 3141(j)(2)(D)(vi)][/us/pl/108/136/s3141/j/2/D/vi], made technical amendment to reference in original act which appears in text as reference to [section 2742 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s2742].
