## § 2530. Testing of nuclear weapons

    (a) __Underground testing__ 

        No underground test of nuclear weapons may be conducted by the United States after September 30, 1996, unless a foreign state conducts a nuclear test after this date, at which time the prohibition on United States nuclear testing is lifted.

    (b) __Atmospheric testing__ 

        None of the funds appropriated pursuant to the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1994 ([Public Law 103–160][/us/pl/103/160]; [107 Stat. 1547][/us/stat/107/1547]) or any other Act for any fiscal year may be available to maintain the capability of the United States to conduct atmospheric testing of a nuclear weapon.
