###### §§ 981, 982. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], § 53, [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641]

    Section 981, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. II, § 246, [66 Stat. 495][/us/stat/66/495], related to civil status of reservists. See [section 502 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s502], Government Organization and Employees.

    Section 982, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. II, § 247, [66 Stat. 495][/us/stat/66/495], authorized members of reserve components to accept employment with and compensation from any foreign government or any concern which is controlled in whole or in part by a foreign government. See [section 1032 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s1032], Armed Forces.
