## §§ 1041 to 1053. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53], [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641]

    Section 1041, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 401, [66 Stat. 498][/us/stat/66/498], established Naval Reserve as Reserve component of Navy. See [section 10108 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s10108], Armed Forces.

    Section 1042, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 402, [66 Stat. 498][/us/stat/66/498], established Marine Corps Reserve as reserve component of Marine Corps. See [section 10109 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s10109].

    Section 1043, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 403, [66 Stat. 498][/us/stat/66/498], provided that Coast Guard Reserve is reserve component of Coast Guard. See [section 701 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s701], Coast Guard.

    Section 1044, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 404, [66 Stat. 498][/us/stat/66/498], provided for integration of Naval Reserve. See [section 10108 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s10108], Armed Forces.

    Section 1045, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 405, [66 Stat. 498][/us/stat/66/498], provided for integration of Marine Corps Reserve. See [section 10109 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s10109].

    Section 1046, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 406, [66 Stat. 499][/us/stat/66/499], provided for integration of Coast Guard Reserve. See [section 701 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s701], Coast Guard.

    Section 1047, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 407, [66 Stat. 499][/us/stat/66/499], provided for a Naval Reserve Policy Board, a Marine Corps Reserve Policy Board, a Coast Guard Policy Board, and for membership on such boards. See sections 10303 and 10304 of Title 10, Armed Forces, and [section 703 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s703], Coast Guard.

    Section 1048, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 409, [66 Stat. 499][/us/stat/66/499], authorized Secretary of Navy to prescribe a suitable flag to be known as Naval Reserve flag, and provided class of vessels which may fly such flag. See [section 7225 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s7225], Armed Forces.

    Section 1049, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 410, [66 Stat. 499][/us/stat/66/499], authorized Secretary of Navy to prescribe a Naval Reserve yacht pennant and enumerated class of vessels which may fly such pennant. See [section 7226 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s7226].

    Section 1050, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 411, [66 Stat. 499][/us/stat/66/499], related to appointment and duty of temporary officers in Naval Reserve and Marine Corps Reserve. See [section 603 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s603].

    Section 1051, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 412, [66 Stat. 499][/us/stat/66/499], related to temporary members of Coast Guard Reserve.

    Section 1052, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 413, [66 Stat. 499][/us/stat/66/499], provided for a Retired Reserve, membership, pay, recall to active duty. See sections 6327 and 6483 of Title 10.

    Section 1053, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. IV, § 414, [66 Stat. 500][/us/stat/66/500], related to applicability of laws to women in Naval Reserve, Marine Corps Reserve, and Coast Guard Reserve. See [section 762 of Title 14][/us/usc/t14/s762], Coast Guard.
