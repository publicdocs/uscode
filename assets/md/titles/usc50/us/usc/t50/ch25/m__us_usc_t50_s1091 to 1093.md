## §§ 1091 to 1093. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53], [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641]

    Section 1091, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. VI, § 601, [66 Stat. 501][/us/stat/66/501], provided that Air National Guard and Air Force Reserve are reserve components of Air Force. See [section 8062 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s8062], Armed Forces.

    Section 1092, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. VI, § 602, [66 Stat. 501][/us/stat/66/501], provided for composition of Air Force Reserve. See [section 10110 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s10110].

    Section 1093, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. VI, § 603, [66 Stat. 501][/us/stat/66/501], related to women in Air Force Reserve.
