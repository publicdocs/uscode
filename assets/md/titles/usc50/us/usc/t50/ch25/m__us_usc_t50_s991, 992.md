## §§ 991, 992. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53], [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641]

    Section 991, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. II, § 248, [66 Stat. 495][/us/stat/66/495], provided for discharge of commissioned officers and other members of reserve components. See sections 12681 and 12682 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

    Section 992, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. II, § 249, [66 Stat. 495][/us/stat/66/495], related to limitation on discharges, dropping from rolls, and character of discharge. See sections 12683 to 12686 of Title 10.
