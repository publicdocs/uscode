## §§ 971 to 975. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53], [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641]

    Section 971, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. II, § 240, [66 Stat. 492][/us/stat/66/492], provided for active duty with or without pay. See [section 12315 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s12315], Armed Forces.

    Section 972, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. II, § 241, [66 Stat. 492][/us/stat/66/492], related to pay and allowances of persons retained beyond the term of service. See [section 12315 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s12315].

    Section 973, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. II, § 242, [66 Stat. 492][/us/stat/66/492], related to pay for officer candidates. See [section 12209 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s12209].

    Section 974, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. II, § 243, [66 Stat. 492][/us/stat/66/492], related to uniform allowances. See sections 415 to 419 of Title 37, Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.

    Section 975, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. II, § 245, [66 Stat. 494][/us/stat/66/494], provided for continuation of existing benefits.
