## §§ 1021 to 1024. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53], [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641]

    Section 1021, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. III, § 301, [66 Stat. 498][/us/stat/66/498], established National Guard and Army Reserve as reserve components of the Army. See [section 3062 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s3062], Armed Forces.

    Section 1022, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. III, § 302, [66 Stat. 498][/us/stat/66/498], redesignated Organized Reserve Corps as Army Reserve.

    Section 1023, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. III, § 303, [66 Stat. 498][/us/stat/66/498], related to composition of Army Reserve. See [section 10104 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s10104].

    Section 1024, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608][/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608], pt. III, § 304, [66 Stat. 498][/us/stat/66/498], related to women in Army Reserve.
