## § 1521a. Destruction of existing stockpile of lethal chemical agents and munitions

    (a) __Program management__ 

        The Secretary of Defense shall ensure that the program for destruction of the United States stockpile of lethal chemical agents and munitions is managed as a major defense acquisition program (as defined in [section 2430 of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s2430]) in accordance with the essential elements of such programs as may be determined by the Secretary.

    (b) __Requirement for Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) annual certification__ 

        Beginning with respect to the budget request for fiscal year 2004, the Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) shall submit to the congressional defense committees on an annual basis a certification that the budget request for the chemical agents and munitions destruction program has been submitted in accordance with the requirements of [section 1521 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s1521].

([Pub. L. 107–314, div. A, title I, § 141][/us/pl/107/314/s141], Dec. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 2477][/us/stat/116/2477].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was enacted as part of the Bob Stump National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2003, and not as part of [Pub. L. 91–121, title IV, § 409][/us/pl/91/121/s409], Nov. 19, 1969, [83 Stat. 209][/us/stat/83/209], which comprises this chapter.

 __“Congressional Defense Committees” Defined__ 

    Congressional defense committees means the Committees on Armed Services and Appropriations of the Senate and the House of Representatives, see [section 3 of Pub. L. 107–314][/us/pl/107/314/s3], [116 Stat. 2471][/us/stat/116/2471]. See note under [section 101 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s101], Armed Forces.
