## §§ 441 to 441d. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 441 to 441d, comprising sections 901 to 905, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], were editorially reclassified as sections 3171 to 3175, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.
