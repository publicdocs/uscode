## §§ 435 to 438. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 435, comprising section 801 of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], was editorially reclassified as section 3161 of chapter 44 of this title.

    Section 435a, comprising section 309 of the Intelligence Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2001, [Pub. L. 106–567][/us/pl/106/567], was editorially reclassified as section 3345 of chapter 45 of this title.

    Sections 435b and 435c, comprising sections 3001 and 3002, respectively, of the Intelligence Reform and Terrorism Prevention Act of 2004, [Pub. L. 108–458][/us/pl/108/458], were editorially reclassified as sections 3341 and 3343, respectively, of chapter 45 of this title.

    Section 435d, comprising section 7 of the Reducing Over-Classification Act, [Pub. L. 111–258][/us/pl/111/258], was editorially reclassified as section 3344 of chapter 45 of this title.

    Sections 436 to 438, comprising sections 802 to 804, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], were editorially reclassified as sections 3162 to 3164, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.
