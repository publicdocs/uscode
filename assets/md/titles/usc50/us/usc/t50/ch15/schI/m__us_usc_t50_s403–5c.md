## § 403–5c. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 403–5c, comprising section 105C of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], was renumbered section 702 of act July 26, 1947, by [Pub. L. 108–136, div. A, title IX, § 922(c)][/us/pl/108/136/s922/c], Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1573][/us/stat/117/1573], and transferred to [section 432 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s432], which was editorially reclassified as [section 3142 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s3142].
