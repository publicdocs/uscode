## § 404d–1. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 404d–1, comprising section 110 of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], was renumbered section 112 of act July 26, 1947, by [Pub. L. 105–107, title III][/us/pl/105/107/tIII], § 303(b), Nov. 20, 1997, [111 Stat. 2252][/us/stat/111/2252], and transferred to [section 404g of this title][/us/usc/t50/s404g], which was editorially reclassified as [section 3047 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s3047].
