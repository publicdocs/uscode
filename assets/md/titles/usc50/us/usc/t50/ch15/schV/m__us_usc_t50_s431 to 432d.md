## §§ 431 to 432d. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 431 to 432d, comprising sections 701 to 706, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], were editorially reclassified as sections 3141 to 3146, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.
