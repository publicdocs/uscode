## §§ 421 to 426. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 421 and 422, comprising sections 601 and 602, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], were editorially reclassified as sections 3121 and 3122, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.

    Section 423, comprising former section 603 of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], was editorially reclassified as section 3123 of chapter 44 of this title.

    Sections 424 to 426, comprising sections 603 to 605, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343][/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343], were editorially reclassified as sections 3124 to 3126, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.
