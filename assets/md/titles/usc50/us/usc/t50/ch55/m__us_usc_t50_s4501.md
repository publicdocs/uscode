## § 4501. Short title

    This chapter, divided into subchapters, may be cited as “the Defense Production Act of 1950”.

([Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932, § 1][/us/act/1950-09-08/ch932/s1], [64 Stat. 798][/us/stat/64/798].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [act Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932][/us/act/1950-09-08/ch932], [64 Stat. 798][/us/stat/64/798], known as the Defense Production Act of 1950, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to section 2061 of the former Appendix to this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
