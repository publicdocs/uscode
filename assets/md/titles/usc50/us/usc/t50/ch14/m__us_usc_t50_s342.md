## § 342. Repealed. [May 24, 1949, ch. 139, § 142][/us/act/1949-05-24/ch139/s142], [63 Stat. 109][/us/stat/63/109]

    Section, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561][/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561], title III, § 302, as added [Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142][/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142], [60 Stat. 102][/us/stat/60/102], related to prohibition against taking of polls. See [section 596 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s596], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

    A prior section 342, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561][/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561], title III, § 312, as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150][/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150], [58 Stat. 146][/us/stat/58/146], which provided for safeguards and secrecy of ballots and prevention of fraud and coercion as to voting, was repealed by [act Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142][/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142], [60 Stat. 102][/us/stat/60/102].
