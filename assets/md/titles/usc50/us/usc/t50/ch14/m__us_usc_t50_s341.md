## § 341. Repealed. [Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656][/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656], title III, § 307, [69 Stat. 589][/us/stat/69/589]

    Section, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561][/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561], title III, § 301, as added [Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142][/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142], [60 Stat. 101][/us/stat/60/101], provided for prevention of fraud, coercion, and undue influence; free discussion, and acts done in good faith.

    A prior section 341, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561][/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561], title III, § 311, as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150][/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150], [58 Stat. 146][/us/stat/58/146], related to validity of ballots and was repealed by [act Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142][/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142], [60 Stat. 96][/us/stat/60/96].

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Section was also repealed by [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53], [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641].
