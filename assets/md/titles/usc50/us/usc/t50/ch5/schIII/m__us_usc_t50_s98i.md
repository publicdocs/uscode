## § 98i. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861][/us/pl/85/861], § 36A, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1570][/us/stat/72/1570]

    Section, [act Aug. 3, 1956, ch. 939, title IV][/us/act/1956-08-03/ch939/tIV], § 416, [70 Stat. 1018][/us/stat/70/1018], related to contracts for storage, handling, and distribution of liquid fuels. See [section 2922 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s2922], Armed Forces.
