## §§ 91 to 94. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041], § 53, [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641]

    Section 91, [act June 16, 1938, ch. 458][/us/act/1938-06-16/ch458], § 1, [52 Stat. 707][/us/stat/52/707], authorized Secretary of War to place educational orders for munitions of special or technical design.

    Section 92, [act June 16, 1938, ch. 458][/us/act/1938-06-16/ch458], § 2, [52 Stat. 708][/us/stat/52/708], related to production equipment.

    Section 93, [act June 16, 1938, ch. 458][/us/act/1938-06-16/ch458], § 3, [52 Stat. 708][/us/stat/52/708], placed certain limitations on number of orders.

    Section 94, acts [June 16, 1938, ch. 458][/us/act/1938-06-16/ch458], § 4, [52 Stat. 708][/us/stat/52/708]; [Apr. 3, 1939, ch. 35][/us/act/1939-04-03/ch35], § 13, [53 Stat. 560][/us/stat/53/560], related to availability of appropriations for purposes of sections 91 to 94 of this title.
