## § 176. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act Mar. 28, 1918, ch. 28, § 1][/us/act/1918-03-28/ch28/s1], [40 Stat. 460][/us/stat/40/460], authorized acquisition of property on Hudson River owned by North German Lloyd Dock Company and Hamburg-American Line Terminal & Navigation Company and provided that [section 175 of this title][/us/usc/t50/s175] did not apply to expenditures authorized in connection with such property. The President, by proclamation dated June 28, 1918, took possession of such property.
