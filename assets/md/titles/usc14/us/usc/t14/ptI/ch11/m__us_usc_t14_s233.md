## \[§ 233. Repealed. [Aug. 3, 1950, ch. 536, § 36][/us/act/1950-08-03/ch536/s36], [64 Stat. 408][/us/stat/64/408]\]

    Section, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 515][/us/stat/63/515], related to retirement for disabilities incident to service. See sections 1204 and 1376 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
