## \[§ 309. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–155][/us/pl/86/155], § 10(a)(1), Aug. 11, 1959, [73 Stat. 338][/us/stat/73/338]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 519][/us/stat/63/519]; [Aug. 3, 1950, ch. 536][/us/act/1950-08-03/ch536], § 11, [64 Stat. 407][/us/stat/64/407], authorized retirement of warrant officers with grade of commissioned warrant officer in case of special commendation.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal of section effective November 1, 1959, see [section 10(b) of Pub. L. 86–155][/us/pl/86/155/s10/b], set out as a note under [section 239 of this title][/us/usc/t14/s239].

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Section was also repealed by [Pub. L. 88–130][/us/pl/88/130], § 1(10)(A), Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 177][/us/stat/77/177].
