## \[§ 239. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–155, § 10(a)(1)][/us/pl/86/155/s10/a/1], Aug. 11, 1959, [73 Stat. 338][/us/stat/73/338]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 516][/us/stat/63/516]; [Aug. 3, 1950, ch. 536, § 6][/us/act/1950-08-03/ch536/s6], [64 Stat. 406][/us/stat/64/406], authorized advancement to a higher grade upon retirement in case of special commendation.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 86–155, § 10(b)][/us/pl/86/155/s10/b], Aug. 11, 1959, [73 Stat. 338][/us/stat/73/338], provided that repeal of this section and [section 309 of this title][/us/usc/t14/s309] shall become effective on Nov. 1, 1959.

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Section was also repealed by [Pub. L. 88–130, § 1(10)(A)][/us/pl/88/130/s1/10/A], Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 177][/us/stat/77/177].
