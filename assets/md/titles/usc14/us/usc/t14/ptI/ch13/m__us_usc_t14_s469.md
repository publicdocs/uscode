## § 469. Training

    The Coast Guard may make expenditures for the training of personnel, including books, school supplies, correspondence courses, motion picture equipment, and other equipment for instructional purposes.

([Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 531][/us/stat/63/531].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

Based on the following language contained in the Coast Guard appropriation act for 1949, “Pay and Allowances” and preceding years: 

> “motion picture and other equipment for instructional purposes; . . . training of enlisted personnel, including textbooks, school supplies, and correspondence courses;”

 (June 19, 1948, [62 Stat. 562][/us/stat/62/562]).

    Changes were made in phraseology. 81st Congress, House Report No. 557.
