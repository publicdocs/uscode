## § 192. Assignment of personnel as instructors

    The Commandant may assign any member to appropriate instruction duty at the Academy.

([Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 510][/us/stat/63/510]; [Pub. L. 98–557][/us/pl/98/557], § 15(a)(3)(H), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2865][/us/stat/98/2865].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on title 14, U.S.C., 1946 ed., § 15i ([Apr. 16, 1937, ch. 107][/us/act/1937-04-16/ch107], § 8, [50 Stat. 67][/us/stat/50/67]).

    Changes were made in phraseology. 81st Congress, House Report No. 557.

 __Amendments__ 

    1984—[Pub. L. 98–557][/us/pl/98/557] substituted reference to member for reference to commissioned officer, warrant officer, and enlisted man.
