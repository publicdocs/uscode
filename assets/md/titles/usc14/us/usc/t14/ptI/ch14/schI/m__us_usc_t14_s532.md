## § 532. Surveys of Coast Guard families

    (a)  __Authority__  __.—__ 

    The Commandant, in order to determine the effectiveness of Federal policies, programs, and activities related to the families of Coast Guard members, may survey—

        (1) any Coast Guard member;

        (2) any retired Coast Guard member;

        (3) the immediate family of any Coast Guard member or retired Coast Guard member; and

        (4) any survivor of a deceased Coast Guard member.

    (b)  __Voluntary Participation__  __.—__ 

    Participation in any survey conducted under subsection (a) shall be voluntary.

    (c)  __Federal Recordkeeping__  __.—__ 

    Each person surveyed under subsection (a) shall be considered an employee of the United States for purposes of [section 3502(3)(A)(i) of title 44][/us/usc/t44/s3502/3/A/i].

(Added [Pub. L. 113–281, title II, § 214(a)][/us/pl/113/281/s214/a], Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 3029][/us/stat/128/3029].)
