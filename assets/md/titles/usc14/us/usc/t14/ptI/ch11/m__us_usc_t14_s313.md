## \[§ 313. Repealed. [May 29, 1954, ch. 249, § 20(o)][/us/act/1954-05-29/ch249/s20/o], [68 Stat. 167][/us/stat/68/167]\]

    Section, acts [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 520][/us/stat/63/520]; [Aug. 3, 1950, ch. 536, § 15][/us/act/1950-08-03/ch536/s15], [64 Stat. 407][/us/stat/64/407], provided that any warrant officer who was retired under sections 303 to 305 or 307 of this title should be retired from active service with the highest grade held by him in which his performance of duty was satisfactory, but not lower than his permanent grade, with retired pay of the grade with which retired. It implemented such sections 303 to 305 and 307 which were also repealed by act May 29, 1954. See notes under those former sections.

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Section was also repealed by [Pub. L. 88–130, § 1(10)(A)][/us/pl/88/130/s1/10/A], Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 177][/us/stat/77/177].
