## § 296. Flag officers

    During any period in which the Coast Guard is not operating as a service in the Navy, [section 1216(d) of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s1216/d] does not apply with respect to flag officers of the Coast Guard.

(Added [Pub. L. 113–281, title II, § 212(a)][/us/pl/113/281/s212/a], Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 3029][/us/stat/128/3029].)

\[§§ 301, 302. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 88–130, § 1(10)(A)__  __][/us/pl/88/130/s1/10/A]__  __,__  __Sept. 24, 1963__  __,__  __[__  __77 Stat. 177__  __][/us/stat/77/177]__  __\]__ 

    Section 301, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 518][/us/stat/63/518], related to permanent appointment of warrant officers. See section 571 et seq. of Title 10, Armed Forces.

    Section 302, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 518][/us/stat/63/518], related to temporary appointments of warrant officers.

\[§§ 303 to 305. __Repealed.__  __[__  __May 29, 1954, ch. 249, § 20(o)__  __][/us/act/1954-05-29/ch249/s20/o]__  __,__  __[__  __68 Stat. 167__  __][/us/stat/68/167]__  __\]__ 

    Section 303, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 518][/us/stat/63/518], required compulsory retirement of warrant officers reaching age of sixty-two years, with retired pay of grade with which retired.

    Section 304, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 518][/us/stat/63/518], provided for voluntary retirement of warrant officers after thirty years’ service, with retired pay of grade with which retired.

    Section 305, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 518][/us/stat/63/518], provided for voluntary retirement after twenty years’ service, with retired pay of grade with which retired.

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Sections were also repealed by [Pub. L. 88–130, § 1(10)(A)][/us/pl/88/130/s1/10/A], Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 177][/us/stat/77/177].
