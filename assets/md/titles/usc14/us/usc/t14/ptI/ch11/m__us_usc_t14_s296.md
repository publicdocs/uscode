## § 296. Flag officers

    During any period in which the Coast Guard is not operating as a service in the Navy, [section 1216(d) of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s1216/d] does not apply with respect to flag officers of the Coast Guard.

(Added [Pub. L. 113–281, title II][/us/pl/113/281/tII], § 212(a), Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 3029][/us/stat/128/3029].)
