## \[§§ 464, 465. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–649, § 14d(2)][/us/pl/87/649/s14d/2], (3), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 502][/us/stat/76/502]\]

    Section 464, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 531][/us/stat/63/531], related to allotments of pay. See [section 703 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s703], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.

    Section 465, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 531][/us/stat/63/531], related to advances to officers ordered to and from sea or shore duty beyond the seas. See [section 1006 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s1006].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Nov. 1, 1962, see [section 15 of Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649/s15], set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 101 of Title 37][/us/usc/t37/s101], Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.
