## \[§§ 303 to 305. Repealed. [May 29, 1954, ch. 249][/us/act/1954-05-29/ch249], § 20(o), [68 Stat. 167][/us/stat/68/167]\]

    Section 303, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 518][/us/stat/63/518], required compulsory retirement of warrant officers reaching age of sixty-two years, with retired pay of grade with which retired.

    Section 304, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 518][/us/stat/63/518], provided for voluntary retirement of warrant officers after thirty years’ service, with retired pay of grade with which retired.

    Section 305, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 518][/us/stat/63/518], provided for voluntary retirement after twenty years’ service, with retired pay of grade with which retired.

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Sections were also repealed by [Pub. L. 88–130][/us/pl/88/130], § 1(10)(A), Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 177][/us/stat/77/177].
