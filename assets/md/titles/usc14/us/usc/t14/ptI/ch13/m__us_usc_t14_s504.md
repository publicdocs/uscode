## § 504. Medal of honor: duplicate medal

    A person awarded a medal of honor shall, upon written application of that person, be issued, without charge, one duplicate medal of honor with ribbons and appurtenances. Such duplicate medal of honor shall be marked, in such manner as the Secretary may determine, as a duplicate or for display purposes only.

(Added [Pub. L. 107–107, div. A, title V][/us/pl/107/107/dA/tV], § 553(d)(1)(A), Dec. 28, 2001, [115 Stat. 1116][/us/stat/115/1116].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 504, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 537][/us/stat/63/537], related to disposition of the remains of deceased Coast Guard personnel, prior to repeal by [act July 15, 1954, ch. 507][/us/act/1954-07-15/ch507], § 14(c)(5), [68 Stat. 481][/us/stat/68/481]. For provisions relating to recovery, care, and disposition of the remains of deceased personnel of the uniformed services and deceased civilian personnel, see [section 1481 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s1481], Armed Forces.
