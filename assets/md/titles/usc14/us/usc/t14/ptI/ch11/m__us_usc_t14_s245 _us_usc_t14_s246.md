###### \[§§ 245, 246. Repealed. [Aug. 3, 1950, ch. 536][/us/act/1950-08-03/ch536], § 36, [64 Stat. 408][/us/stat/64/408]\]

    Section 245, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 517][/us/stat/63/517], related to retiring or dropping for disabilities not incident to service. See [section 1207 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s1207], Armed Forces.

    Section 246, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 518][/us/stat/63/518], related to dropping for disabilities due to vicious habits. See [section 1207 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s1207].

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Sections were also repealed by [Pub. L. 88–130][/us/pl/88/130], § 1(10)(A), Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 177][/us/stat/77/177].
