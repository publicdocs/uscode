## \[§ 277. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–324, title II][/us/pl/104/324/tII], § 210(a), Oct. 19, 1996, [110 Stat. 3915][/us/stat/110/3915]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 88–130][/us/pl/88/130], § 1(10)(C), Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 183][/us/stat/77/183], provided that warrant officers could be temporarily promoted to higher warrant officer grades under regulations prescribed by Secretary.

 __Construction of Repeal__ 

[Pub. L. 104–324, title II][/us/pl/104/324/tII], § 210(a), Oct. 19, 1996, [110 Stat. 3915][/us/stat/110/3915], provided that: 

> “[Section 277 of title 14][/us/usc/t14/s277], United States Code, is repealed. The repeal of such section shall not be construed to affect the status of any warrant officer currently serving under a temporary promotion.”
