## § 723. Effect of this subchapter on retirement and retired pay

    Except as provided in sub[section 746(b) of this title][/us/usc/t14/s746/b], nothing in this subchapter authorizes the retirement of a Reserve officer or the payment of retired, retainer, or severance pay to a Reserve officer; or affects in any manner the law relating to the retirement of, or the granting of retired or retainer pay or other benefits to a Reserve officer.

(Added [Pub. L. 96–322][/us/pl/96/322], § 1, Aug. 4, 1980, [94 Stat. 1007][/us/stat/94/1007].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 795 of this title][/us/usc/t14/s795] prior to the complete revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 96–322][/us/pl/96/322].
