## § 829. Radio station deemed government station

    Any radio station, while assigned to authorized Coast Guard duty shall be deemed to be a radio station of the Coast Guard and a “government station” within the meaning of section 305 of the Communications Act of 1934 ([47 U.S.C. 305][/us/usc/t47/s305]).

([Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 556][/us/stat/63/556]; [Pub. L. 94–546][/us/pl/94/546], § 1(38), Oct. 18, 1976, [90 Stat. 2522][/us/stat/90/2522]; [Pub. L. 99–640][/us/pl/99/640], § 10(a)(8), Nov. 10, 1986, [100 Stat. 3549][/us/stat/100/3549].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on title 14, U.S.C., 1946 ed., § 266b ([Feb. 19, 1941, ch. 8][/us/act/1941-02-19/ch8], title I, § 7B, as added [Sept. 30, 1944, ch. 453][/us/act/1944-09-30/ch453], § 4, [58 Stat. 760][/us/stat/58/760]).

    Changes were made in phraseology. 81st Congress, House Report No. 557.

 __Amendments__ 

    1986—[Pub. L. 99–640][/us/pl/99/640] substituted “section” for “Section”.

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–546][/us/pl/94/546] substituted “Section 305 of the Communications Act of 1934 ([47 U.S.C. 305][/us/usc/t47/s305])” for “chapter 5, of Title 47”.
