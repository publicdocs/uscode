## \[§§ 759a to 761. Omitted\]

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 96–322, § 1][/us/pl/96/322/s1], Aug. 4, 1980, [94 Stat. 1002][/us/stat/94/1002].

    Section 759a, added [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 16(a)][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s16/a], [70A Stat. 626][/us/stat/70A/626]; amended Sept. 24, 1963, [Pub. L. 88–130, § 1(12)][/us/pl/88/130/s1/12], [77 Stat. 190][/us/stat/77/190], related to wartime appointments and promotions. See [section 710 of this title][/us/usc/t14/s710].

    Section 760, acts [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 553][/us/stat/63/553]; [Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 655, § 15][/us/act/1951-10-31/ch655/s15], [65 Stat. 715][/us/stat/65/715]; [Aug. 3, 1956, ch. 926, § 2(a)][/us/act/1956-08-03/ch926/s2/a], [70 Stat. 981][/us/stat/70/981]; May 14, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–283, § 1(12)][/us/pl/93/283/s1/12], [88 Stat. 141][/us/stat/88/141], related to disability or death benefits for temporary members. See [section 707 of this title][/us/usc/t14/s707].

    [Act Aug. 3, 1956, ch. 926, § 2(b)][/us/act/1956-08-03/ch926/s2/b], [70 Stat. 982][/us/stat/70/982], provided that the amendments made by that section \[amending subsec. (a) and adding subsec. (e) of section 760\] applied only to benefits for months beginning after the month in which it was enacted \[August, 1956\].

    [Act Aug. 3, 1956, ch. 926, § 2(c)][/us/act/1956-08-03/ch926/s2/c], [70 Stat. 982][/us/stat/70/982], provided that the entitlement of any person to benefits under the Federal Employees’ Compensation Act \[[act Sept. 7, 1916, ch. 458][/us/act/1916-09-07/ch458], [39 Stat. 742][/us/stat/39/742], repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 632][/us/stat/80/632], see section 8101 et seq. of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees\] as it was in effect before the enactment of this section \[Aug. 3, 1956\] was not affected by this section.

    Section 761, [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393][/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393], [63 Stat. 554][/us/stat/63/554], related to members of the Reserve engaging in civil occupations.
