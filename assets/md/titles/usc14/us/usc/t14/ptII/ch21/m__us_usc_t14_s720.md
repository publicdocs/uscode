## § 720. Definitions

As used in this subchapter—

    (1) “Reserve officer” means a commissioned officer in the Reserve, except an officer excluded by [section 721 of this title][/us/usc/t14/s721] or a commissioned warrant officer; and

    (2) “discharged” means released from an appointment as a Reserve officer.

(Added [Pub. L. 96–322, § 1][/us/pl/96/322/s1], Aug. 4, 1980, [94 Stat. 1007][/us/stat/94/1007].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 770 of this title][/us/usc/t14/s770] prior to the complete revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 96–322][/us/pl/96/322].
