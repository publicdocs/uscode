## § 6543. Designation of reporters

    The reporters of debates in the office of the Secretary of the Senate are hereby designated the official reporters of debates of the Senate.

([Pub. L. 89–545][/us/pl/89/545], Aug. 27, 1966, [80 Stat. 354][/us/stat/80/354].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 126–2 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s126–2] prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
