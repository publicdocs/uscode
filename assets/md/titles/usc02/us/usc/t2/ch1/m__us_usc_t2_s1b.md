## § 1b. Countersignature of certificate of election

    The certificate mentioned in [section 1a of this title][/us/usc/t2/s1a] shall be countersigned by the secretary of state of the State.

(R.S. § 19.)

 __Codification__ 

    R.S. § 19 derived from [act July 25, 1866, ch. 245, § 3][/us/act/1866-07-25/ch245/s3], [14 Stat. 244][/us/stat/14/244].
