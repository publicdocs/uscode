## §§ 286 to 286g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–186, title II][/us/pl/104/186/tII], § 208, Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1742][/us/stat/110/1742]

    Section 286, based on H. Res. No. 988, § 203(a), Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554/tI], ch. III, § 101, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777], established Legislative Classification Office in House of Representatives.

    Section 286a, based on H. Res. No. 988, § 203(b), Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554/tI], ch. III, § 101, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777], related to purpose and policy of Legislative Classification Office.

    Section 286b, based on H. Res. No. 988, § 203(c), Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554/tI], ch. III, § 101, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777], related to functions of Legislative Classification Office.

    Section 286c, based on H. Res. No. 988, § 203(d), Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554/tI], ch. III, § 101, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777], related to functions and appointment of Staff Director of Legislative Classification Office.

    Section 286d, based on H. Res. No. 988, § 203(e), Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554/tI], ch. III, § 101, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777], related to appointment of staff, Deputy Staff Director, and delegation of functions of Legislative Classification Office.

    Section 286e, based on H. Res. No. 988, § 203(f), Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554/tI], ch. III, § 101, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777], related to compensation of Staff Director and staff of Legislative Classification Office.

    Section 286f, based on H. Res. No. 988, § 203(g), Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554/tI], ch. III, § 101, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777], related to expenditures of Legislative Classification Office.

    Section 286g, based on H. Res. No. 988, § 203(h), Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554/tI], ch. III, § 101, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777], provided that contingent fund of House was available to carry out this chapter.
