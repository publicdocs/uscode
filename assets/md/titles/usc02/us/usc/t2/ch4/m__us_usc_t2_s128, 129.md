## §§ 128, 129. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 658][/us/stat/80/658], 659

    Section 128, [act Aug. 5, 1955, ch. 568][/us/act/1955-08-05/ch568], [69 Stat. 513][/us/stat/69/513], authorized contributions for group life insurance of House employees from House contingent fund. See [section 8708 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8708], Government Organization and Employees.

    Section 129, [Pub. L. 85–75][/us/pl/85/75], July 1, 1957, [71 Stat. 248][/us/stat/71/248], authorized contributions to retirement and disability fund from House contingent fund. See [section 8334 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8334].
