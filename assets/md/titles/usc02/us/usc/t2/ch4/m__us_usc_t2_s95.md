## § 95. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section was based on provisions of acts [Oct. 2, 1888, ch. 1069][/us/act/1888-10-02/ch1069], [25 Stat. 546][/us/stat/25/546]; [Mar. 4, 1911, ch. 240][/us/act/1911-03-04/ch240], [36 Stat. 1318][/us/stat/36/1318]; [Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753][/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753], § 121, [60 Stat. 822][/us/stat/60/822]; Dec. 27, 1974, [Pub. L. 104–186, title I][/us/pl/104/186/tI], § 105(c), [110 Stat. 1722][/us/stat/110/1722], relating to payments from contingent fund of House of Representatives prior to being struck out by [Pub. L. 104–186][/us/pl/104/186]. See [section 5505 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s5505]. Provisions of act Oct. 2, 1888, relating to payments from contingent fund of the Senate are classified to [section 6503 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s6503].
