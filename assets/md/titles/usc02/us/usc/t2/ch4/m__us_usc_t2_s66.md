## § 66. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–344, title V, § 505(1)][/us/pl/93/344/s505/1], July 12, 1974, [88 Stat. 322][/us/stat/88/322]

    Section, [act June 19, 1934, ch. 648][/us/act/1934-06-19/ch648], title I, § 1, [48 Stat. 1022][/us/stat/48/1022], directed that the fiscal year for adjustment of accounts of Secretary of Senate for compensation and mileage of Senators extend from July 1 to June 30.
