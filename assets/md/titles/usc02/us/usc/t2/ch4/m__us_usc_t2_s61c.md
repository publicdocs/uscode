## § 61c. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 94–59, title I][/us/pl/94/59/tI], July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 270][/us/stat/89/270], which set the compensation for certain positions in office of Secretary of Senate, was omitted for lack of general applicability.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 61c, acts [Aug. 5, 1955, ch. 568][/us/act/1955-08-05/ch568], § 1, [69 Stat. 499][/us/stat/69/499]; [June 27, 1956, ch. 453][/us/act/1956-06-27/ch453], [70 Stat. 356][/us/stat/70/356]; Aug. 21, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–176][/us/pl/86/176], [73 Stat. 398][/us/stat/73/398]; Aug. 10, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–130][/us/pl/87/130], [75 Stat. 320][/us/stat/75/320], set basic annual compensation of certain positions in office of Secretary of Senate.
