    Section 81a, [act July 26, 1949, ch. 366][/us/act/1949-07-26/ch366], [63 Stat. 482][/us/stat/63/482], related to audits and reports of fiscal records of Sergeant at Arms of House.

    Section 81b, based on H. Res. No. 465, Eighty-fourth Congress, Apr. 11, 1956, enacted into permanent law by [act June 27, 1956, ch. 453, title I][/us/act/1956-06-27/ch453/tI], § 103, [70 Stat. 370][/us/stat/70/370], related to payment from House contingent fund for restoration or adjustment of trust fund account of Sergeant at Arms.

L. 85–75, title I][/us/pl/85/75/tI], § 103, July 1, 1957, [71 Stat. 256][/us/stat/71/256], related to purchase of and payment for insurance of office funds of Sergeant at Arms of House.
