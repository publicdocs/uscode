 __Codification__ 

    Section 93, act June 28, 1886, No. 15, [24 Stat. 342][/us/stat/24/342], related to time of beginning of compensation of committee clerks. See [section 72a of this title][/us/usc/t2/s72a] and Rules of House of Representatives.
