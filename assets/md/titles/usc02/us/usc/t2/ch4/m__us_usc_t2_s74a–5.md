## § 74a–5. Limits on uses of funds provided under section 74a–4

    The funds provided under the provisions of [section 74a–4 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s74a–4] shall be limited to use for the compensation of additional personnel and other necessary official expenses.

([Pub. L. 98–51, title I, § 112][/us/pl/98/51/s112], July 14, 1983, [97 Stat. 270][/us/stat/97/270]; [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(16)][/us/pl/104/186/s204/16], Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1732][/us/stat/110/1732].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section is from the Congressional Operations Appropriation Act, 1984, which is title I of the Legislative Branch Appropriation Act, 1984.

    Section, as it applies to funds provided under [section 333 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s333], is classified to [section 333a of this title][/us/usc/t2/s333a].

 __Amendments__ 

    1996—[Pub. L. 104–186][/us/pl/104/186] made technical amendment to reference in original act which appears in text as reference to [section 74a–4 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s74a–4].
