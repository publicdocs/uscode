## § 61–1c. Aggregate gross compensation of employee of Senator of State with population under 5,000,000

    (a) Notwithstanding the provisions of [section 61–1(d)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t2/s61–1/d/1], and except as otherwise provided in subparagraph (C) of such subsection (d)(1), the aggregate of gross compensation paid employees in the office of a Senator shall not exceed during each fiscal year $1,012,083 if the population of his State is less than 5,000,000.

    (b) Subsection (a) of this section shall take effect October 1, 1991.

([Pub. L. 102–90, title I, § 5][/us/pl/102/90/s5], Aug. 14, 1991, [105 Stat. 450][/us/stat/105/450].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section is from the Congressional Operations Appropriations Act, 1992, which is title I of the Legislative Branch Appropriations Act, 1992.
