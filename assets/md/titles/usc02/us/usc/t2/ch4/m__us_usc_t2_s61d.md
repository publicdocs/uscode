## § 61d. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 61d was editorially reclassified as [section 6651 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s6651].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 61d, [Pub. L. 93–145][/us/pl/93/145], Nov. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 528][/us/stat/87/528]; [Pub. L. 95–26, title I][/us/pl/95/26/tI], May 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 80][/us/stat/91/80]; [Pub. L. 86–628][/us/pl/86/628], [74 Stat. 446][/us/stat/74/446]; Aug. 14, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–426, title II][/us/pl/88/426/tII], § 203(h), [78 Stat. 415][/us/stat/78/415]; Dec. 12, 1969, [Pub.
