## § 108. Contracts for separate parts of Senate stationery

    Sections 106 and 107 of this title shall not prevent the Secretary from contracting for separate parts of the supplies of stationery required to be furnished.

(R.S. § 68; [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(56)][/us/pl/104/186/s204/56], Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1738][/us/stat/110/1738].)

 __Codification__ 

    R.S. § 68 derived from Res. Mar. 3, 1815, No. 11, [3 Stat. 249][/us/stat/3/249].

 __Amendments__ 

    1996—[Pub. L. 104–186][/us/pl/104/186] substituted “the Secretary” for “either the Secretary or the Clerk”.
