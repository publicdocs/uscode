## § 126. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 658][/us/stat/80/658]

    Section, [act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1208, title VI][/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1208/tVI], § 603, [68 Stat. 1116][/us/stat/68/1116], provided that official reporters of Senate proceedings and their employees be considered officers or employees of the legislative branch within section 2091(a) of former Title 5. See [section 8701(a)(3) of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8701/a/3], Government Organization and Employees.
