## § 60g–2. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 60g–2 was editorially reclassified as [section 5322 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s5322].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

A prior section 60g–2, based on House Resolution No. 416, Eighty-ninth Congress, June 16, 1965, as enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 89–545, § 103][/us/pl/89/545/s103], Aug. 27, 1966, [80 Stat. 369][/us/stat/80/369], which related to employment of student congressional interns by Members of the House of Representatives and the Resident Commissioner from Puerto Rico, was repealed by section 2 of House Resolution No. 420, Ninety-third Congress, Sept. 18, 1973, as enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–245][/us/pl/93/245], ch. VI, § 600, Jan. 3, 1974, [87 Stat. 1079][/us/stat/87/1079], which provided that: 

> “H. Res. 416, Eighty-ninth Congress, adopted June 16, 1965, and enacted as permanent law by section 103 of the Legislative Branch Appropriation Act, 1967 ([80 Stat. 369][/us/stat/80/369]; [Public Law 89–545][/us/pl/89/545]; [2 U.S.C. 60g–2][/us/usc/t2/s60g–2]), shall not be effective in the Ninety-third Congress on and after the effective date specified in section 3 of this resolution; and, effective on the date of enactment of the provisions of this resolution as permanent law, such H. Res. 416, Eighty-ninth Congress, is repealed.”
