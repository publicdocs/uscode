## § 61b. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 61b was editorially reclassified as [section 6535 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s6535].

§§ 61b–1 to 61b–2. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in view of [section 6539 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s6539] which abolished all statutory positions in Office of Secretary of Senate, with specified exceptions, effective Oct. 1, 1981, and authorized Secretary of Senate to appoint and fix the compensation of such employees as appropriate.

    Section 61b–1, [Pub. L. 87–730][/us/pl/87/730], Oct. 2, 1962, [76 Stat. 680][/us/stat/76/680], provided for the appointment and compensation of a second assistant parliamentarian.

    Section 61b–1a, [Pub. L. 92–342][/us/pl/92/342], July 10, 1972, [86 Stat. 433][/us/stat/86/433]; [Pub. L. 95–94, title I][/us/pl/95/94], Aug. 5, 1977, [91 Stat. 654][/us/stat/91/654], provided for the appointment and compensation of a third assistant parliamentarian.

    Section 61b–2, [Pub. L. 90–608][/us/pl/90/608], ch. VII, § 701, Oct. 21, 1968, [82 Stat. 1195][/us/stat/82/1195], provided for the appointment and compensation of a Curator of Art and Antiquities.
