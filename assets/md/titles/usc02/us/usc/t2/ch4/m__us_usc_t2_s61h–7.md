## § 61h–7. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 61h–7 was editorially reclassified as [section 6133 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s6133].

§§ 61i to 61j–1. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Section 61i, [Pub. L. 86–30, title I][/us/pl/86/30], May 20, 1959, [73 Stat. 48][/us/stat/73/48], which was from the Second Supplemental Appropriation Act, 1959, authorized Senate Majority and Minority Leaders to fix, effective May 1, 1959, basic salaries of research assistants authorized by S. Res. 158, agreed to Dec. 9, 1941, at not to exceed $8,820 per annum. See [section 6131 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s6131].

    Section 61j, [Pub. L. 93–371, § 4][/us/pl/93/371/s4], Aug. 13, 1974, [88 Stat. 429][/us/stat/88/429]; [Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 105][/us/pl/94/59/s105], July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 275][/us/stat/89/275], set a maximum annual rate of compensation of $37,000 for administrative assistants in offices of Senate Majority and Minority Whips. Positions established by Legislative Branch Appropriation Act, 1970, for Offices of Senate Majority and Minority Whips, which Act, [Pub. L. 91–145][/us/pl/91/145], Dec. 12, 1969, [83 Stat. 339][/us/stat/83/339], classified to former [section 61j of this title][/us/usc/t2/s61j], authorized the respective whips to appoint an administrative assistant, were abolished, see title I of [Pub. L. 95–26][/us/pl/95/26], set out in part as a note under former [section 61h–1 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s61h–1]. See, also, [section 6134 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s6134].

    A prior section 61j, [Pub. L. 91–145][/us/pl/91/145], Dec. 12, 1969, [83 Stat. 339][/us/stat/83/339], authorized Senate Majority and Minority Whips to each appoint and fix compensation of an administrative assistant and an executive secretary.

    Section 61j–1, [Pub. L. 94–59, title I][/us/pl/94/59], July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 270][/us/stat/89/270], authorized Senate Majority and Minority Whips, effective July 1, 1975, each to appoint and fix compensation of a legislative assistant. The positions established by [Pub. L. 94–59][/us/pl/94/59] for the Offices of Majority and Minority Whips were abolished effective Apr. 1, 1977, by [Pub. L. 95–26, title I][/us/pl/95/26], May 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 80][/us/stat/91/80], set out as a note under [section 61h–1 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s61h–1]. See, also, [section 6134 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s6134].
