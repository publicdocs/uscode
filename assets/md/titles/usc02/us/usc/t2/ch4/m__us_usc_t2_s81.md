## § 81. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–344, title V, § 505(2)][/us/pl/93/344/s505/2], July 12, 1974, [88 Stat. 322][/us/stat/88/322]

    Section, [act July 2, 1954, ch. 455][/us/act/1954-07-02/ch455], title I, [68 Stat. 400][/us/stat/68/400], directed that the fiscal year for the adjustment of the accounts of Sergeant at Arms of House for compensation and mileage of Members, Delegates, and Resident Commissioner extend from July 1 to June 30.

§§ 81a to 81c. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(27)__  __][/us/pl/104/186/s204/27]__  __–(29),__  __Aug. 20, 1996__  __,__  __[__  __110 Stat. 1734__  __][/us/stat/110/1734]__ 

    Section 81a, [act July 26, 1949, ch. 366][/us/act/1949-07-26/ch366], [63 Stat. 482][/us/stat/63/482], related to audits and reports of fiscal records of Sergeant at Arms of House.

    Section 81b, based on H. Res. No. 465, Eighty-fourth Congress, Apr. 11, 1956, enacted into permanent law by [act June 27, 1956, ch. 453][/us/act/1956-06-27/ch453], title I, § 103, [70 Stat. 370][/us/stat/70/370], related to payment from House contingent fund for restoration or adjustment of trust fund account of Sergeant at Arms.

    Section 81c, based on H. Res. No. 144, Eighty-fifth Congress, Feb. 7, 1957, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 85–75, title I, § 103][/us/pl/85/75/s103], July 1, 1957, [71 Stat. 256][/us/stat/71/256], related to purchase of and payment for insurance of office funds of Sergeant at Arms of House.
