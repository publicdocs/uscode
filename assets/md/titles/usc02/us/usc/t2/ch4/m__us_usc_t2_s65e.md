## § 65e. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 98–63, title I][/us/pl/98/63/tI], July 30, 1983, [97 Stat. 333][/us/stat/97/333], which provided that effective with fiscal year 1983 and each fiscal year thereafter, the expense allowance of Majority and Minority Whips of Senate could not exceed $5,000 each fiscal year for each Whip, was transferred and executed to [section 31a–1 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s31a–1]. Section 31a–1 was editorially reclassified as [section 6102 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s6102].
