## § 62a. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act May 1, 1947, ch. 49, title I][/us/act/1947-05-01/ch49/tI], [61 Stat. 58][/us/stat/61/58], accorded Sergeant at Arms of Senate the same priority as executive agencies under the Surplus Property Act of 1944, [act Oct. 3, 1944, ch. 479][/us/act/1944-10-03/ch479], [58 Stat. 765][/us/stat/58/765]. The Surplus Property Act of 1944, which was formerly classified principally to sections 1611 to 1646 of the former Appendix to Title 50, War and National Defense, was repealed, with certain exceptions, by [act June 30, 1949, ch. 288, title V][/us/act/1949-06-30/ch288/tV], § 503, [63 Stat. 399][/us/stat/63/399], and the priorities thereunder expired Dec. 31, 1949. See References in Text note under [section 352 of Title 30][/us/usc/t30/s352], Mineral Lands and Mining.
