## § 62a. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act May 1, 1947, ch. 49][/us/act/1947-05-01/ch49], title I, [61 Stat. 58][/us/stat/61/58], accorded Sergeant at Arms of Senate the same priority as executive agencies under the Surplus Property Act of 1944 (50 App. U.S.C. 1611–1648). The Surplus Property Act of 1944 was repealed by [act June 30, 1949, ch. 288][/us/act/1949-06-30/ch288], title V, § 503, [63 Stat. 399][/us/stat/63/399], and the priorities thereunder expired Dec. 31, 1949.
