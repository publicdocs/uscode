## § 635. Permissible revisions of concurrent resolutions on the budget

    At any time after the concurrent resolution on the budget for a fiscal year has been agreed to pursuant to [section 632 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s632], and before the end of such fiscal year, the two Houses may adopt a concurrent resolution on the budget which revises or reaffirms the concurrent resolution on the budget for such fiscal year most recently agreed to.

([Pub. L. 93–344, title III][/us/pl/93/344/tIII], § 304, July 12, 1974, [88 Stat. 310][/us/stat/88/310]; [Pub. L. 99–177, title II][/us/pl/99/177/tII], § 201(b), Dec. 12, 1985, [99 Stat. 1047][/us/stat/99/1047]; [Pub. L. 100–119, title II][/us/pl/100/119/tII], § 208(b), Sept. 29, 1987, [101 Stat. 786][/us/stat/101/786]; [Pub. L. 101–508, title XIII][/us/pl/101/508/tXIII], § 13112(a)(8), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1388–608][/us/stat/104/1388-608]; [Pub. L. 105–33, title X][/us/pl/105/33/tX], § 10108, Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 684][/us/stat/111/684].)
