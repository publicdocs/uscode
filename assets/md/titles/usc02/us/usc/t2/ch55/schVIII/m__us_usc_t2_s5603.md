## § 5603. Symbol of office of Sergeant at Arms

    The symbol of his office shall be the mace, which shall be borne by him while enforcing order on the floor.

([Oct. 1, 1890, ch. 1256, § 2][/us/act/1890-10-01/ch1256/s2], [26 Stat. 645][/us/stat/26/645].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 79 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s79] prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
