## § 2281. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

([Pub. L. 110–437, title VI, § 601][/us/pl/110/437/s601], Oct. 20, 2008, [122 Stat. 4999][/us/stat/122/4999].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 110–437][/us/pl/110/437], Oct. 20, 2008, [122 Stat. 4983][/us/stat/122/4983], known as the Capitol Visitor Center Act of 2008, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 2201 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s2201] and Tables.
