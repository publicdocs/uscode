## § 1943. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–145][/us/pl/111/145], § 6(b), Mar. 4, 2010, [124 Stat. 54][/us/stat/124/54]

    Section, R.S. § 1825, required members of the Capitol police to pay for their own uniforms.

 __Codification__ 

    Section was classified to section 211 of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by [Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], § 1, Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1062][/us/stat/116/1062].

    R.S. § 1825 derived from [act July 20, 1868, ch. 176][/us/act/1868-07-20/ch176], § 1, [15 Stat. 94][/us/stat/15/94].
