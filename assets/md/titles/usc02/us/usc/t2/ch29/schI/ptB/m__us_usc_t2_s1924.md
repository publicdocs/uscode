## § 1924. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–145, § 5(a)(2)(A)][/us/pl/111/145/s5/a/2/A], Mar. 4, 2010, [124 Stat. 53][/us/stat/124/53]

    Section, [Pub. L. 92–184][/us/pl/92/184], ch. IV, Dec. 15, 1971, [85 Stat. 636][/us/stat/85/636]; [Pub. L. 93–245][/us/pl/93/245], ch. VI, § 600, Jan. 3, 1974, [87 Stat. 1079][/us/stat/87/1079]; [Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554], ch. III, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777]; [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 221(10)][/us/pl/104/186/s221/10], (11), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1750][/us/stat/110/1750], related to emergency duty overtime pay from funds disbursed by the Chief Administrative Officer.

 __Codification__ 

    Section was classified to section 206b of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by [Pub. L. 107–217, § 1][/us/pl/107/217/s1], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1062][/us/stat/116/1062].

    Subsecs. (a) to (f) of this section were based on section 3 of H. Res. No. 449, Ninety-second Congress, June 2, 1971, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 92–184][/us/pl/92/184], as amended by H. Res. No. 398, Ninety-third Congress, June 4, 1973, eff. Jan. 3, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–245][/us/pl/93/245], and H. Res. No. 1309, Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 10, 1974, eff. Dec. 27, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554][/us/pl/93/554].

    Subsec. (g) of this section was based on section 3 of H. Res. No. 1309, Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 10, 1974, eff. Dec. 27, 1974, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554][/us/pl/93/554], and was repealed by [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 221(11)(B)][/us/pl/104/186/s221/11/B], Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1750][/us/stat/110/1750].

    As originally codified, this section consisted of two undesignated pars. based on sections 3 and 5 of H. Res. No. 449, Ninety-second Congress, June 2, 1971, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 92–184][/us/pl/92/184]. Section 5 of H. Res. No. 449 was superseded by section 3 of H. Res. No. 1309, formerly set out as subsec. (g) of this section, and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 221(11)(B)][/us/pl/104/186/s221/11/B], Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1750][/us/stat/110/1750].
