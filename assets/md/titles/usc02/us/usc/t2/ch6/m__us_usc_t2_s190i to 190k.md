## §§ 190i to 190k. Repealed. S. Res. 274, § 2(b), Nov. 14, 1979

    Section 190i, [Pub. L. 91–510, title II, § 243][/us/pl/91/510/s243], Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1173][/us/stat/84/1173], related to actions and procedures of the Committee on Appropriations of Senate. See Standing Rules of the Senate.

    Section 190j, [Pub. L. 91–510, title II, § 252(a)][/us/pl/91/510/s252/a], Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1173][/us/stat/84/1173]; [Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 302(d)][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s302/d], as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1][/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1], as added Sept. 20, 1977, [Pub.
