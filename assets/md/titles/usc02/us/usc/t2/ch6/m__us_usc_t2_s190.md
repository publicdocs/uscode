## § 190. Repealed. S. Res. 4, § 301(b), Feb. 4, 1977

    Section, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753][/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753], title I, § 137, [60 Stat. 832][/us/stat/60/832], directed that controversies arising as to the jurisdiction of any standing committee of the Senate with respect to any proposed legislation be decided by the presiding officer of the Senate in favor of the committee having jurisdiction over the subject matter which predominated in the proposed legislation.
