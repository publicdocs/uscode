###### §§ 435, 436. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–187, title I][/us/pl/96/187/tI], § 105(1), Jan. 8, 1980, [93 Stat. 1354][/us/stat/93/1354]

    Section 435, [Pub. L. 92–225, title III][/us/pl/92/225/tIII], § 305, Feb. 7, 1972, [86 Stat. 16][/us/stat/86/16]; [Pub. L. 93–443, title II][/us/pl/93/443/tII], § 205(a), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1278][/us/stat/88/1278], related to requirements for campaign advertising.

L. 93–443, title II][/us/pl/93/443/tII], §§ 206, 207, 208(c)(5), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1278][/us/stat/88/1278], 1279, 1286; [Pub.  __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 8, 1980, see [section 301(a) of Pub. L. 96–187][/us/pl/96/187/s301/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 30101 of Title 52][/us/usc/t52/s30101], Voting and Elections.
