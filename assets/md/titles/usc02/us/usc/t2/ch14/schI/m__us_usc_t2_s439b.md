## § 439b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–187, title I, § 105(1)][/us/pl/96/187/s105/1], Jan. 8, 1980, [93 Stat. 1354][/us/stat/93/1354]

    Section, [Pub. L. 92–225, title III, § 318][/us/pl/92/225/s318], formerly § 319, as added [Pub. L. 93–443, title II, § 210][/us/pl/93/443/s210], Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1289][/us/stat/88/1289]; renumbered § 318, [Pub.
