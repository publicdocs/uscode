## § 5326. “Member of the House” defined

    As used in [section 5324 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s5324] the phrase “Member of the House” shall mean a Representative, Representative-elect, Delegate, Delegate-elect, Resident Commissioner, or Resident Commissioner-elect.

([Aug. 21, 1935, ch. 600][/us/act/1935-08-21/ch600], § 3, [49 Stat. 680][/us/stat/49/680].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 92d of this title][/us/usc/t2/s92d] prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Jan. 3, 1935, see section 4 of act Aug. 21, 1935, set out as a note under [section 5324 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s5324].
