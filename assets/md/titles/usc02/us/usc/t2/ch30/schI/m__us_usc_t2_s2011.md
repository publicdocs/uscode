## § 2011. Assignment of unoccupied space

    Unoccupied space in said building shall be assigned by the Architect of the Capitol under the direction of the commission and subject to the control of the House of Representatives.

(May 28, 1908, No. 30, [35 Stat. 579][/us/stat/35/579]; [Mar. 3, 1921, ch. 124][/us/act/1921-03-03/ch124], [41 Stat. 1291][/us/stat/41/1291].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was classified to section 184 of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by [Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], § 1, Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1062][/us/stat/116/1062].

 __Change of Name__ 

    Change of name of Architect of the Capitol, functions abolished, transferred, etc., by prior acts, see Prior Provisions and Change of Name notes set out under [section 1801 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s1801].
