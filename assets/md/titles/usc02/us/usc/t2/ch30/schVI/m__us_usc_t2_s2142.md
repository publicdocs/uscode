## § 2142. Superintendent of Botanic Garden and greenhouses

    There shall be a superintendent and assistants in the Botanical Garden and greenhouses, who shall be under the direction of the Joint Committee on the Library.

(R.S. § 1827.)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was classified to section 216 of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by [Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], § 1, Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1062][/us/stat/116/1062].

    R.S. § 1827 derived from [act Mar. 3, 1873, ch. 226][/us/act/1873-03-03/ch226], § 1, [17 Stat. 491][/us/stat/17/491].
