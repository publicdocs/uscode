## § 2145. Restriction on use of appropriation for Botanic Garden

    On and after July 31, 1958, no part of any appropriation for the Botanic Garden shall be used for the distribution, by congressional allotment, of trees, plants, shrubs, or other nursery stock.

([Pub. L. 85–570][/us/pl/85/570], July 31, 1958, [72 Stat. 450][/us/stat/72/450].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was classified to section 216a of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by [Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], § 1, Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1062][/us/stat/116/1062].
