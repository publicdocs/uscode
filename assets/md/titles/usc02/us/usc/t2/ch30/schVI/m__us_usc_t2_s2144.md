## § 2144. Disbursement of appropriations for Botanic Garden

    On and after November 5, 1990, all appropriations made on account of the Botanic Garden shall be disbursed for that purpose in the same manner as other appropriations under the control of the Architect of the Capitol.

([Pub. L. 101–520, title II][/us/pl/101/520/tII], Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 2270][/us/stat/104/2270].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was classified to section 216d of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by [Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], § 1, Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1062][/us/stat/116/1062].
