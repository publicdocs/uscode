## § 282d. Official mail matter

    The Legislative Counsel may send the official mail matter of the Office as franked mail under [section 3210 of title 39][/us/usc/t39/s3210].

([Pub. L. 91–510, title V][/us/pl/91/510/tV], § 525, Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1203][/us/stat/84/1203]; [Pub. L. 92–51][/us/pl/92/51], § 101, July 9, 1971, [85 Stat. 132][/us/stat/85/132].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1971—[Pub. L. 92–51][/us/pl/92/51] substituted provision for Legislative Counsel to send official mail matter of the Office as franked mail under [section 3210 of title 39][/us/usc/t39/s3210], for former provision granting the Office the same privilege of free transmission of official mail matter as other offices of the United States Government.
