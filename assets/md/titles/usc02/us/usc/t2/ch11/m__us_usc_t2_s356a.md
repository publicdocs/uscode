## § 356a. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 94–440, title II][/us/pl/94/440/tII], \[§ 100,\] Oct. 1, 1976, [90 Stat. 1446][/us/stat/90/1446], the Legislative Branch Appropriation Act, 1977, which provided salary rate limitations for positions or offices referred to in [section 356 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s356], applied to fiscal year 1977 and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts. See decision B–145492 of the Comptroller General of the United States, dated Sept. 21, 1976. [Pub. L. 94–440, title II][/us/pl/94/440/tII], \[§ 100,\] was formerly set out as a note under [section 5318 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s5318], Government Organization and Employees.
