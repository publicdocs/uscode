## § 4913. Regulations of Page Board

    The Page Board shall have authority to prescribe such regulations as may be necessary to carry out sections 4911 to 4913 of this title.

([Pub. L. 97–377, title I][/us/pl/97/377/tI], § 127, Dec. 21, 1982, [96 Stat. 1914][/us/stat/96/1914].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 88b–4 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s88b–4] prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

    Section is based on section 3 of House Resolution No. 611, Ninety-seventh Congress, Nov. 30, 1982, which was enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 97–377][/us/pl/97/377].
