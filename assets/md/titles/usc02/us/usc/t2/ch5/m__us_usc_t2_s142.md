## § 142. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, acts [June 29, 1922, ch. 251][/us/act/1922-06-29/ch251], § 1, [42 Stat. 715][/us/stat/42/715]; Nov. 5, 1990, [Pub. L. 101–520, title III][/us/pl/101/520/tIII], § 307, [104 Stat. 2277][/us/stat/104/2277]; [Pub. L. 108–7, div. H, title I][/us/pl/108/7/dH/tI], § 1208(a), Feb. 20, 2003, [117 Stat. 375][/us/stat/117/375], established office of administrative assistant and disbursing officer of Library of Congress which was abolished by [section 142a of this title][/us/usc/t2/s142a]. As amended generally by [Pub. L. 108–7][/us/pl/108/7], section no longer relates to office of administrative assistant and disbursing officer. See [section 141 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s141].
