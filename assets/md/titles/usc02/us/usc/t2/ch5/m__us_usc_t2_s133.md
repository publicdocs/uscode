## § 133. Joint Committee during recess of Congress

    The portion of the Joint Committee of Congress on the Library on the part of the Senate remaining in office as Senators shall during the recess of Congress exercise the powers and discharge the duties conferred by law upon the Joint Committee of Congress on the Library.

([Mar. 3, 1883, ch. 141, § 2][/us/act/1883-03-03/ch141/s2], [22 Stat. 592][/us/stat/22/592]; [Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753][/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753], title II, § 223, [60 Stat. 838][/us/stat/60/838].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1946—Act Aug. 2, 1946, changed composition of Joint Committee. See [section 132b of this title][/us/usc/t2/s132b].

 __Effective Date of 1946 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by act Aug. 2, 1946, effective Jan. 3, 1947, see section 245 of that act, set out as a note under [section 72a of this title][/us/usc/t2/s72a].
