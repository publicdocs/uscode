###### §§ 136a, 136a–1. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were superseded by [section 136a–2 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s136a–2].

    Section 136a, [Pub. L. 88–426, title II][/us/pl/88/426/tII], § 203(c), Aug. 14, 1964, [78 Stat. 415][/us/stat/78/415]; [Pub. L. 90–206, title II][/us/pl/90/206/tII], §§ 219(2), 225(h), Dec. 16, 1967, [81 Stat. 639][/us/stat/81/639], 644; [Pub. L. 94–82, title II][/us/pl/94/82/tII], § 204(b), Aug. 9, 1975, [89 Stat. 421][/us/stat/89/421], set compensation of Librarian of Congress at an annual rate equal to rate for positions at level IV of Executive Schedule.

    A prior section 136a, acts [Mar. 6, 1928, ch. 134][/us/act/1928-03-06/ch134], [45 Stat. 197][/us/stat/45/197]; [Oct. 15, 1949, ch. 695][/us/act/1949-10-15/ch695], § 5(a), [63 Stat. 890][/us/stat/63/890], which contained similar provisions, was repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 647][/us/stat/80/647], 655.

    Section 136a–1, [Pub. L. 88–426, title II][/us/pl/88/426/tII], § 203(d), Aug. 14, 1964, [78 Stat. 415][/us/stat/78/415]; [Pub. L. 90–206, title II][/us/pl/90/206/tII] §§ 219(3), 225(h), Dec. 16, 1967, [81 Stat. 639][/us/stat/81/639], 644; [Pub. L. 94–82, title II][/us/pl/94/82/tII], § 204(b), Aug. 9, 1975, [89 Stat. 421][/us/stat/89/421], set compensation of Deputy Librarian of Congress at an annual rate equal to rate for positions at level V of Executive Schedule.
