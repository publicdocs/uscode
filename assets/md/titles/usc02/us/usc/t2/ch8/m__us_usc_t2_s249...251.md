## §§ 249 to 251. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], § 21, [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 249, [act Feb. 28, 1925, ch. 368, title III][/us/act/1925-02-28/ch368/tIII], § 310, [43 Stat. 1073][/us/stat/43/1073], related to promises or pledges by candidates. See [section 599 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s599], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.
