## § 46d–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–184][/us/pl/92/184], ch. IV, Dec. 15, 1971, [85 Stat. 635][/us/stat/85/635]

    Section, [Pub. L. 90–21, title I][/us/pl/90/21], May 29, 1967, [81 Stat. 38][/us/stat/81/38], made contingent fund of Senate available for reimbursement of each Senator of strictly official telephone service charges incurred outside District of Columbia up to $300 in each fiscal quarter. See [section 58 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s58].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 92–184][/us/pl/92/184] provided that the repeal is effective Jan. 1, 1972.
