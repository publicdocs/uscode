## § 58b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–137][/us/pl/100/137], § 2, Oct. 21, 1987, [101 Stat. 819][/us/stat/101/819]

    Section, [Pub. L. 97–12, title I][/us/pl/97/12/tI], § 110, June 5, 1981, [95 Stat. 62][/us/stat/95/62]; [Pub. L. 97–51][/us/pl/97/51], § 125, Oct. 1, 1981, [95 Stat. 965][/us/stat/95/965]; [Pub.
