## § 46d–2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–57][/us/pl/90/57], July 28, 1967, [81 Stat. 130][/us/stat/81/130]

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–90, § 101][/us/pl/89/90/s101], July 27, 1965, [79 Stat. 268][/us/stat/79/268], provided for computation of long-distance telephone calls for Senators, wide area telephone service contracts, and effective date of changes. See [section 58 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s58].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 90–57][/us/pl/90/57] provided that the repeal is effective Jan. 1, 1968.
