## § 285f. Expenditures

    In accordance with policies and procedures approved by the Speaker, the Law Revision Counsel is authorized to make such expenditures as may be necessary or appropriate for the functioning of the Office.

([Pub. L. 93–554, title I][/us/pl/93/554/tI], ch. III, § 101, Dec. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1777][/us/stat/88/1777].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section is based on section 205(g) of House Resolution No. 988, Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, which was enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 93–554][/us/pl/93/554].
