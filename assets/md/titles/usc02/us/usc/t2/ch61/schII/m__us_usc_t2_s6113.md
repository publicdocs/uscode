## § 6113. Appointment and compensation of employees by President pro tempore of Senate

    Effective October 1, 1979, the President pro tempore is authorized to appoint and fix the compensation of such employees as he deems appropriate: Provided, That the gross compensation paid to such employees shall not exceed $123,000 each fiscal year.

([Pub. L. 96–38, title I][/us/pl/96/38/tI], § 101, July 25, 1979, [93 Stat. 111][/us/stat/93/111].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 61k of this title][/us/usc/t2/s61k] prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

    Section is from the Supplemental Appropriations Act, 1979.

 __Increases in Compensation__ 

    Increases in compensation for Senate officers and employees under authority of Federal Pay Comparability Act of 1970 ([Pub. L. 91–656][/us/pl/91/656]), see Salary Directives of President pro tempore of the Senate, set out as notes under [section 4571 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s4571].
