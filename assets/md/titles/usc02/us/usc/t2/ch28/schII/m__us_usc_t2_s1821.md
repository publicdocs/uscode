## § 1821. Small purchase contracting authority

    (a) __In general__ 

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law—

        (1) [section 6101 of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s6101] shall apply with respect to purchases and contracts for the Architect of the Capitol as if the reference to “$25,000” in paragraph (1) of such section were a reference to “$100,000”; and

        (2) the Architect may procure services, equipment, and construction for security related projects in the most efficient manner he determines appropriate.

    (b) __Effective date__ 

        This section shall apply to fiscal year 2003 and each fiscal year thereafter.

([Pub. L. 108–7, div. H, title I][/us/pl/108/7/dH/tI], § 1201, Feb. 20, 2003, [117 Stat. 372][/us/stat/117/372].)

 __Codification__ 

    In subsec. (a)(1), “[section 6101 of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s6101]” substituted for “section 3709 of the Revised Statutes of the United States ([41 U.S.C. 5][/us/usc/t41/s5])” on authority of [Pub. L. 111–350][/us/pl/111/350], § 6(c), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3854][/us/stat/124/3854], which Act enacted Title 41, Public Contracts.

    Section is from the Legislative Branch Appropriations Act, 2003, which is div. H of [Pub. L. 108–7][/us/pl/108/7].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Prior similar provisions were contained in [Pub. L. 107–68, title I][/us/pl/107/68/tI], § 131, Nov. 12, 2001, [115 Stat. 581][/us/stat/115/581].
