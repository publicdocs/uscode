## § 1881d. Effective date

    This subchapter shall take effect on May 16, 2016, except that a Capitol-flown flag may not be provided under [section 1881a of this title][/us/usc/t2/s1881a] until the regulations issued under [section 1881b(a) of this title][/us/usc/t2/s1881b/a] take effect in accordance with [section 1881b(b) of this title][/us/usc/t2/s1881b/b].

([Pub. L. 114–156][/us/pl/114/156], § 6, May 16, 2016, [130 Stat. 392][/us/stat/130/392].)
