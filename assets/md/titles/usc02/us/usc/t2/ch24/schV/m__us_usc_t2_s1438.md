## § 1438. Severability

    If any provision of this chapter or the application of such provision to any person or circumstance is held to be invalid, the remainder of this chapter and the application of the provisions of the remainder to any person or circumstance shall not be affected thereby.

([Pub. L. 104–1, title V, § 509][/us/pl/104/1/s509], Jan. 23, 1995, [109 Stat. 44][/us/stat/109/44].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 104–1][/us/pl/104/1], Jan. 23, 1995, [109 Stat. 3][/us/stat/109/3], as amended, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1301 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s1301] and Tables.
