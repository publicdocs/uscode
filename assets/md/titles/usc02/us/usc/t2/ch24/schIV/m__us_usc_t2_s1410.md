## § 1410. Other judicial review prohibited

    Except as expressly authorized by sections 1407, 1408, and 1409 of this title, the compliance or noncompliance with the provisions of this chapter and any action taken pursuant to this chapter shall not be subject to judicial review.

([Pub. L. 104–1, title IV, § 410][/us/pl/104/1/s410], Jan. 23, 1995, [109 Stat. 37][/us/stat/109/37].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 104–1][/us/pl/104/1], Jan. 23, 1995, [109 Stat. 3][/us/stat/109/3], which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1301 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s1301] and Tables.
