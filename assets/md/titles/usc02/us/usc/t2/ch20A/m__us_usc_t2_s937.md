## § 937. Application of BBEDCA

    For purposes of this chapter—

    (1) notwithstanding section 275 of BBEDCA, the provisions of sections 905, 906, 907, and 922 of this title, as amended by this title, <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  shall apply to the provisions of this chapter;

    (2) references in sections 905, 906, 907, and 922 of this title to “this subchapter” or “this title” 1 shall be interpreted as applying to this chapter;

    (3) references in sections 905, 906, 907, and 922 of this title to “[section 904 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s904]” shall be interpreted as referencing [section 934 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s934];

    (4) the reference in [section 906(b) of this title][/us/usc/t2/s906/b] to “section 902 or 903 of this title” shall be interpreted as referencing [section 934 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s934];

    (5) the reference in [section 906(d)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t2/s906/d/1] to “section 902 or 903 of this title” shall be interpreted as referencing [section 935 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s935];

    (6) the reference in [section 906(d)(4) of this title][/us/usc/t2/s906/d/4] to “section 902 or 903 of this title” shall be interpreted as referencing [section 934 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s934];

    (7) [section 906(k) of this title][/us/usc/t2/s906/k] shall apply to a sequestration, if any, under this chapter; and

    (8) references  <sup>\[2\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 2 So in original. Probably should be “the reference”. </sup></sup>  in [section 907(e) of this title][/us/usc/t2/s907/e] to “section 901, 902, or 903 of this title” shall be interpreted as referencing [section 933 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s933].

([Pub. L. 111–139, title I][/us/pl/111/139/tI], § 8, Feb. 12, 2010, [124 Stat. 21][/us/stat/124/21].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 111–139][/us/pl/111/139], Feb. 12, 2010, [124 Stat. 8][/us/stat/124/8], which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 931 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s931] and Tables.

    Section 275 of BBEDCA, referred to in par. (1), is section 275 of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985, [Pub. L. 99–177][/us/pl/99/177], which is set out as a note under [section 900 of this title][/us/usc/t2/s900].

    As amended by this title, referred to in par. (1), means as amended by title I of [Pub. L. 111–139][/us/pl/111/139].

    “This title”, appearing in quotes in par. (2), refers to the references to “this title” in the original in sections 905, 906, 907, and 922 of this title. “This title” appears untranslated in sections 906(h)(1), (j)(1), (3) and 922(a)(2), (3) of this title and translated as “this chapter” following “subchapter I of” in [section 922(d) of this title][/us/usc/t2/s922/d]. Those references to “this title” mean title II of [Pub. L. 99–177][/us/pl/99/177], known as the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985. See References in Text notes set out under section 906 and 922 of this title.
