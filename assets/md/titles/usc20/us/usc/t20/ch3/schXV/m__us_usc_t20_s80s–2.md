## § 80s–2. Private support for civil rights history project

    (a) __Encouraging solicitation and acceptance of donations__ 

        The Librarian of Congress and the Secretary are encouraged to solicit and accept donations of funds and in-kind contributions to support activities under [section 80s–1 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s80s–1].

    (b) __Dedication of funds provided to Library of Congress__ 

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law—

        (1) any funds donated to the Librarian of Congress to support the activities of the Librarian under [section 80s–1 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s80s–1] shall be deposited entirely into an account established for such purpose;

        (2) the funds contained in such account shall be used solely to support such activities; and

        (3) the Librarian of Congress may not deposit into such account any funds donated to the Librarian which are not donated for the exclusive purpose of supporting such activities.

([Pub. L. 111–19, § 4][/us/pl/111/19/s4], May 12, 2009, [123 Stat. 1614][/us/stat/123/1614].)
