## § 80r–1. Definitions

    In this subchapter:

    (1) __Board of Regents__ 

        The term “Board of Regents” means the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution.

    (2) __Council__ 

        The term “Council” means the National Museum of African American History and Culture Council established by [section 80r–3 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s80r–3].

    (3) __Museum__ 

        The term “Museum” means the National Museum of African American History and Culture established by [section 80r–2 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s80r–2].

    (4) __Secretary__ 

        The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution.

([Pub. L. 108–184, § 3][/us/pl/108/184/s3], Dec. 16, 2003, [117 Stat. 2676][/us/stat/117/2676].)
