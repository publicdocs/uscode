###### §§ 3, 4. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV][/us/pl/91/230/tIV], § 401(d)(1), (2), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173][/us/stat/84/173]

    Section 3, [act May 28, 1896, ch. 252][/us/act/1896-05-28/ch252], § 1, [29 Stat. 171][/us/stat/29/171]; Reorg. Plan No. I of 1939, §§ 201, 204, eff.     Section 4, R.S. § 518, directed Commissioner of Education to present an annual report to Congress.
