## §§ 1, 2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–318, title III, § 301(b)(2)(A)][/us/pl/92/318/s301/b/2/A], June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 332][/us/stat/86/332]

    Section 1, R.S. § 516, established Office of Education and provided for purpose and duties of Office.

    Section 2, R.S. § 517, provided for appointment of a Commissioner of Education to manage Office of Education.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 92–318, title III, § 301(b)(2)(A)][/us/pl/92/318/s301/b/2/A], June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 332][/us/stat/86/332], provided that the repeal is effective July 1, 1972.

 __Executive Order No. 11185__ 

    Ex. Ord. No. 11185, Oct. 16, 1964, 29 F.R. 14399, as amended by Ex. Ord. No. 11260, Dec. 11, 1965, 30 F.R. 15395; Ex. Ord. No. 11661, Mar. 24, 1972, 37 FR 6281, which provided for the coordination of federal education programs, was superseded by Ex. Ord. No. 11761, Jan. 17, 1974, 39 F.R. 2345, formerly set out under [section 1221 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1221].
