## § 1221e–4. Educational impact statement

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no regulation affecting any institution of higher education in the United States, promulgated on or after October 3, 1980, shall become effective unless such agency causes to be published in the Federal Register a copy of such proposed regulation together with an educational impact assessment statement which shall determine whether any information required to be transmitted under such regulation is already being gathered by or is available from any other agency or authority of the United States. Notwithstanding the exception provided under [section 553(b) of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s553/b], such statement shall be based upon the record established under the provisions of [section 553 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s553], compiled during the rulemaking proceeding regarding such regulation.

([Pub. L. 90–247, title IV, § 411][/us/pl/90/247/s411], formerly § 409, as added [Pub. L. 96–374, title XIII, § 1306][/us/pl/96/374/s1306], Oct. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 1498][/us/stat/94/1498]; renumbered § 411, [Pub. L. 103–382, title II, § 212(b)(1)][/us/pl/103/382/s212/b/1], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3913][/us/stat/108/3913].)

 __Codification__ 

    October 3, 1980, referred to in text, was in the original “the date of enactment of this Act”, which was translated as meaning the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 96–374][/us/pl/96/374], which enacted this section, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 411 of Pub. L. 90–247][/us/pl/90/247/s411] was renumbered section 420, and is classified to [section 1223 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1223].

    Another prior [section 411 of Pub. L. 90–247][/us/pl/90/247/s411] was classified to [section 1222 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1222] prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 93–380][/us/pl/93/380].

    Another prior [section 411 of Pub. L. 90–247][/us/pl/90/247/s411] was renumbered section 430, and is classified to [section 1231 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1231].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Oct. 1, 1980, see [section 1393(a) of Pub. L. 96–374][/us/pl/96/374/s1393/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 1001 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1001].
