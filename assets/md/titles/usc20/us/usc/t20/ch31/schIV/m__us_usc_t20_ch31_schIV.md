### SUBCHAPTER IV—ENFORCEMENT

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior subchapter IV, consisting of sections 1233 to 1233h, was repealed by [Pub. L. 103–382, title II][/us/pl/103/382/tII], § 212(a)(2), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3913][/us/stat/108/3913]. See note set out under [section 1232j of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1232j].

 __Amendments__ 

    1994—[Pub. L. 103–382, title II][/us/pl/103/382/tII], § 212(b)(2), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3913][/us/stat/108/3913], redesignated subchapter V of this chapter as this subchapter.
