## §§ 2881 to 2883. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I][/us/pl/103/382/tI], § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519][/us/stat/108/3519].

    Section 2881, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I][/us/pl/89/10/tI], § 1461, as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I][/us/pl/100/297/tI], § 1001, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 199][/us/stat/102/199], required report on State and local evaluations.

    Prior sections 2883 to 2890 were repealed by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V][/us/pl/97/35/tV], § 587(a)(1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480][/us/stat/95/480], eff. Oct. 1, 1982.
