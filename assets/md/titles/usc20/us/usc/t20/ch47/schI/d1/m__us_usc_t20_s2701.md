## § 2701. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I][/us/pl/89/10/tI], § 1001, as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I][/us/pl/100/297/tI], § 1001, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 140][/us/stat/102/140], which provided a declaration of policy and statement of purpose, was omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I][/us/pl/103/382/tI], § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519][/us/stat/108/3519]. See [section 6301 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6301].

    A prior section 2701, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I][/us/pl/89/10/tI], § 101, as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title I][/us/pl/95/561/tI], § 101(a), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2153][/us/stat/92/2153], set out Congressional declaration of policy, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 100–297][/us/pl/100/297].

    A prior section 2702, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I][/us/pl/89/10/tI], § 102, as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title I][/us/pl/95/561/tI], § 101(a), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2153][/us/stat/92/2153]; amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title III][/us/pl/96/88/tIII], § 301(a)(1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677][/us/stat/93/677], 692, provided for the duration of assistance on entitlements created under this subchapter, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 100–297][/us/pl/100/297].
