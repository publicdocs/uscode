###### §§ 3271, 3272. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I][/us/pl/103/382/tI], § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519][/us/stat/108/3519].

    Section 3271, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VI][/us/pl/89/10/tVI], § 6201, as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I][/us/pl/100/297/tI], § 1001, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 273][/us/stat/102/273]; amended [Pub. L. 101–250][/us/pl/101/250], § 4, Mar. 6, 1990, [104 Stat. 97][/us/stat/104/97], contained provisions general to this subchapter.

    Section 3272, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VI][/us/pl/89/10/tVI], § 6202, as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I][/us/pl/100/297/tI], § 1001, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 273][/us/stat/102/273], defined terms used in this subchapter.
