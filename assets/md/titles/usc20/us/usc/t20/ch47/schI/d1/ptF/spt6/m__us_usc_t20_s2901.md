## § 2901. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I][/us/pl/89/10/tI], § 1491, as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I][/us/pl/100/297/tI], § 1001, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 202][/us/stat/102/202], which contained transition provisions, was omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I][/us/pl/103/382/tI], § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519][/us/stat/108/3519].

    Prior sections 2901 to 2904 were repealed by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V][/us/pl/97/35/tV], § 587(a)(1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480][/us/stat/95/480], eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

    Section 2901, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II][/us/pl/89/10/tII], § 221, as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II][/us/pl/95/561/tII], § 201, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2204][/us/stat/92/2204], set out statement of purpose for basic skills improvement program.

    Section 2902, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II][/us/pl/89/10/tII], § 222, as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II][/us/pl/95/561/tII], § 201, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2205][/us/stat/92/2205]; amended [Pub. L. 96–46][/us/pl/96/46], § 1(12), Aug. 6, 1979, [93 Stat. 339][/us/stat/93/339], related to agreements with State educational agencies.

    Section 2903, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II][/us/pl/89/10/tII], § 223, as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II][/us/pl/95/561/tII], § 201, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2207][/us/stat/92/2207], related to distribution of grants by the Secretary.

    Section 2904, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II][/us/pl/89/10/tII], § 224, as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II][/us/pl/95/561/tII], § 201, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2207][/us/stat/92/2207], provided for a State leadership program.
