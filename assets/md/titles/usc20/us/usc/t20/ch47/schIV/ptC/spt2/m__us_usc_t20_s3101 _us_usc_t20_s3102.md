###### §§ 3101, 3102. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I][/us/pl/103/382/tI], § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519][/us/stat/108/3519].

    Section 3101, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV][/us/pl/89/10/tIV], § 4321, as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I][/us/pl/100/297/tI], § 1001, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 241][/us/stat/102/241], authorized grants to Close Up Foundation for program for older Americans and recent immigrants.

    A prior section 3101, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV][/us/pl/89/10/tIV], § 421, as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title IV][/us/pl/95/561/tIV], § 401, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2236][/us/stat/92/2236], provided that amounts allotted would be used only for the acquisition of school library resources and instructional equipment and materials, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V][/us/pl/97/35/tV], § 587(a)(1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480][/us/stat/95/480], eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

    Section 3102, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV][/us/pl/89/10/tIV], § 4322, as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I][/us/pl/100/297/tI], § 1001, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 241][/us/stat/102/241], related to applications.

L. 95–561, title IV][/us/pl/95/561/tIV], § 401, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2237][/us/stat/92/2237]; amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title III][/us/pl/96/88/tIII], § 301(a)(1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677][/us/stat/93/677], 692, related to program requirements for the receipt of funds available, prior to repeal by [Pub.
