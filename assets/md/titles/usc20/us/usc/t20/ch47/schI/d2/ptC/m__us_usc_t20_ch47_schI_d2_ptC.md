##### Part C—General Administrative Provisions

§§ 2971 to 2976. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/382/s101], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519][/us/stat/108/3519].

    Section 2971, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1571][/us/pl/89/10/s1571], as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001][/us/pl/100/297/s1001], Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 214][/us/stat/102/214], required that States maintain effort and that Federal funds be supplementary.

    A prior section 2971, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 325][/us/pl/89/10/s325], as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title III, § 301(a)][/us/pl/95/561/s301/a], Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2213][/us/stat/92/2213]; amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 301(a)(1)][/us/pl/96/88/s301/a/1], title V, §§ 507, 509(b), Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677][/us/stat/93/677], 692, 695, set forth provisions establishing and implementing preschool partnership programs, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)][/us/pl/97/35/s587/a/1], Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480][/us/stat/95/480], eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

    Section 2972, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1572][/us/pl/89/10/s1572], as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001][/us/pl/100/297/s1001], Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 215][/us/stat/102/215], related to participation of children enrolled in private schools.

    Section 2973, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1573][/us/pl/89/10/s1573], as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001][/us/pl/100/297/s1001], Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 217][/us/stat/102/217], related to evaluations and reporting.

    Section 2974, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1574][/us/pl/89/10/s1574], as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001][/us/pl/100/297/s1001], Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 218][/us/stat/102/218], related to Federal administration.

    Section 2975, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1575][/us/pl/89/10/s1575], as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001][/us/pl/100/297/s1001], Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 218][/us/stat/102/218], related to application of General Education Provisions Act.

    Section 2976, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1576][/us/pl/89/10/s1576], as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001][/us/pl/100/297/s1001], Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 218][/us/stat/102/218], contained transition provisions.
