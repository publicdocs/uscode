## §§ 4001 to 4003. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–297, title II][/us/pl/100/297/tII], § 2303, Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 324][/us/stat/102/324]

    Section 4001, [Pub. L. 98–377, title IV][/us/pl/98/377/tIV], § 401, Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1287][/us/stat/98/1287], related to Presidential awards.

    Section 4002, [Pub. L. 98–377, title IV][/us/pl/98/377/tIV], § 402, Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1287][/us/stat/98/1287], related to administrative provisions.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective July 1, 1988, see [section 6303 of Pub. L. 100–297][/us/pl/100/297/s6303], set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 1071 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1071].
