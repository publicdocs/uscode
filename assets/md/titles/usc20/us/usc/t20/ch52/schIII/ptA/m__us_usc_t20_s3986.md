## § 3986. Submission of applications

    Each applicant within a State which desires to receive a grant under this part shall submit the application prepared in accordance with [section 3985 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3985] to the State agency on  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be “for”. </sup></sup>  higher education or the State educational agency, as the case may be, for approval and shall submit the approved application to the Secretary under [section 3985 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3985]. Each such application shall be submitted jointly by the local educational agency in the case of activities described in [section 3984(a) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3984/a], or an institution of higher education in the case of activities described in [section 3984(b) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3984/b], and each business concern or other party that is to participate in the program for which assistance is sought.

([Pub. L. 98–377, title III, § 307][/us/pl/98/377/s307], Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1286][/us/stat/98/1286]; [Pub. L. 99–159, title II, § 231][/us/pl/99/159/s231], Nov. 22, 1985, [99 Stat. 900][/us/stat/99/900]; [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6031(b)(2)][/us/pl/100/418/s6031/b/2], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1483][/us/stat/102/1483].)

 __Amendments__

    1985—[Pub. L. 99–159][/us/pl/99/159] substituted “Secretary” for “Foundation”.
