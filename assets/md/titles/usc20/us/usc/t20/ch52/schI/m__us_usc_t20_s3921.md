## § 3921. Prohibition against the Federal control of education

    The provisions of section 1232a  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title, relating to prohibition against Federal control of education, shall apply to each program and award authorized by this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 98–377, title I, § 111][/us/pl/98/377/s111], as added [Pub. L. 99–159, title II, § 201][/us/pl/99/159/s201], Nov. 22, 1985, [99 Stat. 897][/us/stat/99/897].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 1232a of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1232a], referred to in text, was in the original a reference to section 432 of the General Education Provisions Act. Sections 425 and 432 of that Act were renumbered as sections 432 and 438, respectively, by [Pub. L. 103–382, title II, § 212(b)(1)][/us/pl/103/382/s212/b/1], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3913][/us/stat/108/3913], and are classified to sections 1231b–2 and 1232a, respectively, of this title.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 3921, [Pub. L. 98–377, title I, § 121][/us/pl/98/377/s121], Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1269][/us/stat/98/1269], related to programs authorized for mathematics and science education development, prior to the general revision of this subchapter by [section 201 of Pub. L. 99–159][/us/pl/99/159/s201]. See [section 3914 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3914].
