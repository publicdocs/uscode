## § 4358. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–244, title IX, § 920][/us/pl/105/244/s920], Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1831][/us/stat/112/1831]

    Section, [Pub. L. 99–371, title II, § 208][/us/pl/99/371/s208], as added [Pub. L. 102–421, title I, § 138][/us/pl/102/421/s138], Oct. 16, 1992, [106 Stat. 2162][/us/stat/106/2162], related to scholarship program for deaf education or special education careers.

    A prior section 4358, [Pub. L. 99–371, title II, § 208][/us/pl/99/371/s208], formerly title IV, § 408, Aug. 4, 1986, [100 Stat. 792][/us/stat/100/792]; renumbered title II, § 208, [Pub. L. 102–421, title I, § 101(b)(5)][/us/pl/102/421/s101/b/5], (6), Oct. 16, 1992, [106 Stat. 2151][/us/stat/106/2151], provided for a National Technical Institute for the Deaf endowment program, prior to repeal effective Oct. 1, 1992, by [Pub. L. 102–421, title I][/us/pl/102/421], §§ 137(1), 161, Oct. 16, 1992, [106 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/106/2159], 2164. See [section 4357 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4357].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1998, except as otherwise provided in [Pub. L. 105–244][/us/pl/105/244], see [section 3 of Pub. L. 105–244][/us/pl/105/244/s3], set out as an Effective Date of 1998 Amendment note under [section 1001 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1001].
