###### §§ 691h, 691i. Transferred

    Section 691h, R.S. § 441; [Mar. 4, 1911, ch. 285][/us/act/1911-03-04/ch285], [36 Stat. 1422][/us/stat/36/1422]; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. IV, § 11, eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2421, [54 Stat. 1234][/us/stat/54/1234]; 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 1, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631][/us/stat/67/631]; [June 18, 1954, ch. 324][/us/act/1954-06-18/ch324], § 1, [68 Stat. 265][/us/stat/68/265]; Oct. 17, 1979, [Pub. L. 96–88, title III][/us/pl/96/88/tIII], § 301(a)(2)(M), title V, § 507, [93 Stat. 677][/us/stat/93/677], 692, which charged Secretary of Education with supervision of public business of Gallaudet College, was transferred to [section 4361 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4361].

    Section 691i, [Pub. L. 95–355, title I][/us/pl/95/355/tI], § 100, Sept. 8, 1978, [92 Stat. 531][/us/stat/92/531], which authorized Gallaudet College to make purchases through General Services Administration, was transferred to [section 4362 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4362].
