L. 96–88, title III][/us/pl/96/88/tIII], § 301(a)(2)(M), title V, § 507, [93 Stat. 677][/us/stat/93/677], 692, related to property rights of Gallaudet College, assumption of outstanding liabilities and obligations against corporation under any former name, and conveyance or mortgage of property. See [section 4302 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4302].

See [section 4303(a) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4303/a].

    Section 691e, [act June 18, 1954, ch. 324][/us/act/1954-06-18/ch324], § 6, [68 Stat. 266][/us/stat/68/266], related to powers of Board of Directors of Gallaudet College. See [section 4303(b) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4303/b].

    Section 691f, acts [June 18, 1954, ch. 324][/us/act/1954-06-18/ch324], § 7, [68 Stat. 266][/us/stat/68/266]; Oct. 17, 1979, [Pub.
