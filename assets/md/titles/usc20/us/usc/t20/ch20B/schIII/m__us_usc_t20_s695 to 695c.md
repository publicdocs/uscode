## §§ 695 to 695c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–371, title II, § 210(b)][/us/pl/99/371/s210/b], formerly title IV, § 410(b), Aug. 4, 1986, [100 Stat. 794][/us/stat/100/794]; renumbered title II, § 210(b), [Pub. L. 102–421, title I, § 101(b)(5)][/us/pl/102/421/s101/b/5], (6), Oct. 16, 1992, [106 Stat. 2151][/us/stat/106/2151]

    Section 695, [Pub. L. 91–587, § 1][/us/pl/91/587/s1], Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1579][/us/stat/84/1579], authorized Gallaudet College to operate Kendall School as a demonstration elementary school for the deaf. See [section 4311 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4311].

    Section 695a, [Pub. L. 91–587, § 2][/us/pl/91/587/s2], Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1579][/us/stat/84/1579], defined “elementary school” and “construction”. See [section 4351 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4351].

    Section 695b, [Pub. L. 91–587, § 3][/us/pl/91/587/s3], Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1579][/us/stat/84/1579], authorized appropriations for establishment and operation, including construction and equipment, of demonstration elementary school.

    Section 695c, [Pub. L. 91–587, § 4][/us/pl/91/587/s4], Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1579][/us/stat/84/1579], related to design and construction of facilities of demonstration elementary school.
