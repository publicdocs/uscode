## § 2372. Consolidation of funds

    (a) __In general__ 

        An eligible agency receiving an allotment under sections 2321 and 2371 of this title may choose to consolidate all, or a portion of, funds received under [section 2371 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2371] with funds received under [section 2321 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2321] in order to carry out the activities described in the State plan submitted under [section 2342 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2342].

    (b) __Notification requirement__ 

        Each eligible agency that chooses to consolidate funds under this section shall notify the Secretary, in the State plan submitted under [section 2342 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2342], of the eligible agency’s decision to consolidate funds under this section.

    (c) __Treatment of consolidated funds__ 

        Funds consolidated under this section shall be considered as funds allotted under [section 2321 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2321] and shall be distributed in accordance with [section 2322 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2322].

([Pub. L. 88–210, title II][/us/pl/88/210/tII], § 202, as added [Pub. L. 109–270][/us/pl/109/270], § 1(b), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 738][/us/stat/120/738].)
