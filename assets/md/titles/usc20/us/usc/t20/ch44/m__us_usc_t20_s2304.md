## § 2304. Privacy

    (a) __GEPA__ 

        Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to supersede the privacy protections afforded parents and students under section 444 of the General Education Provisions Act ([20 U.S.C. 1232g][/us/usc/t20/s1232g]).

    (b) __Prohibition on development of national database__ 

        Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to permit the development of a national database of personally identifiable information on individuals receiving services under this chapter.

([Pub. L. 88–210, § 5][/us/pl/88/210/s5], as added [Pub. L. 109–270, § 1(b)][/us/pl/109/270/s1/b], Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 690][/us/stat/120/690].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 2304, [Pub. L. 88–210, § 5][/us/pl/88/210/s5], as added [Pub. L. 105–332, § 1(b)][/us/pl/105/332/s1/b], Oct. 31, 1998, [112 Stat. 3082][/us/stat/112/3082], related to privacy, prior to the general amendment of this chapter by [Pub. L. 109–270][/us/pl/109/270].
