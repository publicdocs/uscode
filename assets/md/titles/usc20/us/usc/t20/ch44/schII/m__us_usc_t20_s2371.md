## § 2371. State allotment and application

    (a) __In general__ 

        For any fiscal year, the Secretary shall allot the amount made available under [section 2376 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2376] among the States in the same manner as funds are allotted to States under paragraph (2) of [section 2321(a) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2321/a].

    (b) __Payments to eligible agencies__ 

        The Secretary shall make a payment in the amount of a State’s allotment under subsection (a) to the eligible agency that serves the State and has an application approved under subsection (c).

    (c) __State application__ 

    Each eligible agency desiring an allotment under this subchapter shall submit, as part of its State plan under [section 2342 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2342], an application that—

        (1) describes how activities under this subchapter will be coordinated, to the extent practicable, with activities described in the State plan submitted under [section 2342 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2342]; and

        (2) contains such information as the Secretary may require.

([Pub. L. 88–210, title II][/us/pl/88/210/tII], § 201, as added [Pub. L. 109–270][/us/pl/109/270], § 1(b), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 737][/us/stat/120/737].)
