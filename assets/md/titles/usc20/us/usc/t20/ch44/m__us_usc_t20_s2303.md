## § 2303. Transition provisions

    The Secretary shall take such steps as the Secretary determines to be appropriate to provide for the orderly transition to the authority of this chapter (as amended by the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Improvement Act of 2006) from any authority under the provisions of the Carl D. Perkins Vocational and Technical Education Act of 1998, as in effect on the day before August 12, 2006. The Secretary shall give each eligible agency the opportunity to submit a transition plan for the first fiscal year following August 12, 2006.

([Pub. L. 88–210, § 4][/us/pl/88/210/s4], as added [Pub. L. 109–270, § 1(b)][/us/pl/109/270/s1/b], Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 690][/us/stat/120/690].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter (as amended by the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Improvement Act of 2006), referred to in text, is this chapter as amended by [Pub. L. 109–270][/us/pl/109/270], Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 683][/us/stat/120/683].

    The Carl D. Perkins Vocational and Technical Education Act of 1998, as in effect on the day before August 12, 2006, referred to in text, means [Pub. L. 88–210][/us/pl/88/210], as amended, which was classified generally to this chapter, prior to being amended generally and renamed the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Act of 2006 by [Pub. L. 109–270, § 1(b)][/us/pl/109/270/s1/b], Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 683][/us/stat/120/683].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 2303, [Pub. L. 88–210, § 4][/us/pl/88/210/s4], as added [Pub. L. 105–332, § 1(b)][/us/pl/105/332/s1/b], Oct. 31, 1998, [112 Stat. 3082][/us/stat/112/3082], related to transition provisions, prior to the general amendment of this chapter by [Pub. L. 109–270][/us/pl/109/270].

    Another prior section 2303, [Pub. L. 101–392, § 4][/us/pl/101/392/s4], Sept. 25, 1990, [104 Stat. 758][/us/stat/104/758]; [Pub. L. 104–66, title I, § 1041(f)][/us/pl/104/66/s1041/f], Dec. 21, 1995, [109 Stat. 715][/us/stat/109/715], which related to the Interdepartmental Task Force on Vocational Education and Related Programs, was transferred to [section 2308 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2308].
