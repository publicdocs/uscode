## § 2305. Limitation

    All of the funds made available under this chapter shall be used in accordance with the requirements of this chapter.

([Pub. L. 88–210, § 6][/us/pl/88/210/s6], as added [Pub. L. 109–270, § 1(b)][/us/pl/109/270/s1/b], Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 690][/us/stat/120/690].)
