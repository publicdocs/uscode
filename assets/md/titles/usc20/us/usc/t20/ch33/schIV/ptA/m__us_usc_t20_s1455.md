## § 1455. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2005 through 2010.

([Pub. L. 91–230, title VI][/us/pl/91/230/tVI], § 655, as added [Pub. L. 108–446, title I][/us/pl/108/446/tI], § 101, Dec. 3, 2004, [118 Stat. 2774][/us/stat/118/2774].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 1455, [Pub. L. 91–230, title VI][/us/pl/91/230/tVI], § 655, as added [Pub. L. 105–17, title I][/us/pl/105/17/tI], § 101, June 4, 1997, [111 Stat. 129][/us/stat/111/129], set out minimum State grant amounts, prior to the general amendment of subchapters I to IV of this chapter by [Pub. L. 108–446][/us/pl/108/446].

    A prior section 1456, [Pub. L. 91–230, title VI][/us/pl/91/230/tVI], § 656, as added [Pub. L. 105–17, title I][/us/pl/105/17/tI], § 101, June 4, 1997, [111 Stat. 129][/us/stat/111/129], which authorized appropriations, was omitted in the general amendment of subchapters I to IV of this chapter by [Pub. L. 108–446][/us/pl/108/446].
