## §§ 241c–1 to 241c–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title I][/us/pl/95/561/tI], § 101(c), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2200][/us/stat/92/2200]

    Section 241c–1, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII], § 121, as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 101(a)(2)(E), [88 Stat. 491][/us/stat/88/491], provided for programs for handicapped children.

    Another section 121 of act Sept. 30, 1950, as added by [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 113(b)(6), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 126][/us/stat/84/126], and renumbered section “126” by [Pub. L. 93–380][/us/pl/93/380], was classified to [section 241d of this title][/us/usc/t20/s241d].

    Section 241c–2, act Sept. 30, 1950, title II, § 122, as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 101(a)(2)(E), [88 Stat. 492][/us/stat/88/492], provided for programs for migratory children.

    Another section 122 of act Sept. 30, 1950, as added by [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 113(b)(6), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 126][/us/stat/84/126], and renumbered section “127” by [Pub. L. 93–380][/us/pl/93/380], was classified to [section 241d–1 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s241d–1].

    Section 241c–3, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII], § 123, as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 101(a)(2)(E), [88 Stat. 494][/us/stat/88/494], provided for programs for neglected or delinquent children.

    Another section 123 of act Sept. 30, 1950, as added by [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 113(b)(6), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 127][/us/stat/84/127], and renumbered section “128” by [Pub. L. 93–380][/us/pl/93/380], was classified to [section 241d–2 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s241d–2].

    Section 241c–4, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII], § 124, as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 101(a)(2)(E), [88 Stat. 494][/us/stat/88/494], provided for the reservation of funds for territories.

    Section 241c–5, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII], § 125, as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 101(a)(2)(E), [88 Stat. 495][/us/stat/88/495], and amended Apr. 21, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–273][/us/pl/94/273], § 3(8), [90 Stat. 376][/us/stat/90/376]; Oct. 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–482, title V][/us/pl/94/482/tV], § 501(b)(1)(A), (o), [90 Stat. 2236][/us/stat/90/2236], 2238; Sept. 24, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–112][/us/pl/95/112], § 2(a)(1), [91 Stat. 911][/us/stat/91/911], related to minimum payments for State operated programs.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 1530(a) of Pub. L. 95–561][/us/pl/95/561/s1530/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 1221e–3 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1221e–3].
