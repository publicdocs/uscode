## § 239a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 542(1)][/us/pl/97/35/s542/1], Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 458][/us/stat/95/458]

    Section, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124], title I, § 4A, as added Oct. 3, 1980, [Pub. L. 96–374, title XIII, § 1341][/us/pl/96/374/s1341], [95 Stat. 1500][/us/stat/95/1500], related to payments for special programs for alien children who fled from Cambodia, Vietnam, Laos, Cuba, or Haiti. See provisions set out as notes under [section 1522 of Title 8][/us/usc/t8/s1522], Aliens and Nationality.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 547 of Pub. L. 97–35][/us/pl/97/35/s547], set out as a note under [section 1522 of Title 8][/us/usc/t8/s1522], Aliens and Nationality.
