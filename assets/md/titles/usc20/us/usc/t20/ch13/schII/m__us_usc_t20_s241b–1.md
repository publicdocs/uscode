## § 241b–1. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(10)][/us/pl/93/380/s101/a/10], Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 501][/us/stat/88/501], which authorized appropriations for assistance to local educational agencies whose total basic grants allotment was 90 percent or less than the allotment during the preceding year, was transferred to [section 2713 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2713] and subsequently omitted from the Code.
