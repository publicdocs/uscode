## §§ 241d to 241d–2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title I][/us/pl/95/561/tI], § 101(c), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2200][/us/stat/92/2200]

    Section 241d, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII], § 126, formerly § 121, as added Apr. 13, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 113(b)(6), [84 Stat. 126][/us/stat/84/126], renumbered Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 101(a)(3), [88 Stat. 495][/us/stat/88/495], and amended Oct. 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–482, title V][/us/pl/94/482/tV], § 501(b)(1)(B), [90 Stat. 2236][/us/stat/90/2236], related to maximum entitlement for special incentive grants.

L. 89–10, title I][/us/pl/89/10/tI], § 2, Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 30][/us/stat/79/30], and renumbered by [Pub. L. 89–750, title I][/us/pl/89/750/tI], § 108(a), Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1195][/us/stat/80/1195]. Such repeal, pursuant to [section 191 of Pub. L. 89–750][/us/pl/89/750/s191], was effective with respect to fiscal years beginning after June 30, 1966.

    Provisions (for amount and distribution of special incentive grants) similar to those comprising this section, as added by [Pub. L. 91–230][/us/pl/91/230], were contained in [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title I][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tI], § 121(a), formerly title II, § 221(a), as added and renumbered Jan. 2, 1968, [Pub. L. 91–230][/us/pl/91/230], § 113(b)(6).

    Section 241d–1, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII], § 127, formerly § 122, as added Apr. 13, 1970, [Pub.
