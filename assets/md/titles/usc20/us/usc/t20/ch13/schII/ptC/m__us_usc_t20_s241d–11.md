## § 241d–11. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title I][/us/pl/95/561/tI], § 101(c), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2200][/us/stat/92/2200]

    Section, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII], § 131, as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 101(a)(4)(A), [88 Stat. 495][/us/stat/88/495], related to eligibility for and maximum amount of special grants.

    A prior section 241d–11, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II][/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII], § 131, as added Apr. 13, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 113(b)(6), [84 Stat. 127][/us/stat/84/127], which also related to eligibility and maximum amount of special grants for urban and rural schools serving areas with the highest concentrations of children from low-income families, was repealed by [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 101(a)(4)(B), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 496][/us/stat/88/496], effective July 1, 1975.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 1530(a) of Pub. L. 95–561][/us/pl/95/561/s1530/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 1221e–3 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1221e–3].
