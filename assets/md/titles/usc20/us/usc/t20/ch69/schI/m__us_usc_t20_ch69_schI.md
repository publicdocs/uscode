### SUBCHAPTER I—SCHOOL-TO-WORK OPPORTUNITIES BASIC PROGRAM COMPONENTS

§§ 6111 to 6114. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 6111 to 6114 were omitted pursuant to [section 6251 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6251] which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001.

    Section 6111, [Pub. L. 103–239, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/239/s101], May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 576][/us/stat/108/576], related to general program requirements.

    Section 6112, [Pub. L. 103–239, title I, § 102][/us/pl/103/239/s102], May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 576][/us/stat/108/576], related to school-based learning component.

    Section 6113, [Pub. L. 103–239, title I, § 103][/us/pl/103/239/s103], May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 577][/us/stat/108/577], related to work-based learning component.

    Section 6114, [Pub. L. 103–239, title I, § 104][/us/pl/103/239/s104], May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 577][/us/stat/108/577], related to connecting activities component.
