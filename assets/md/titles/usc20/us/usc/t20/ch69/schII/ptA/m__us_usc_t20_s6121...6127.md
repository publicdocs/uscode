## §§ 6121 to 6127. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 6121 to 6127 were omitted pursuant to [section 6251 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6251] which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001.

    Section 6121, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II][/us/pl/103/239/tII], § 201, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 578][/us/stat/108/578], stated purpose of this part.

    Section 6122, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II][/us/pl/103/239/tII], § 202, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 578][/us/stat/108/578], authorized grants to States and territories.

    Section 6123, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II][/us/pl/103/239/tII], § 203, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 579][/us/stat/108/579], related to application for grant.

    Section 6124, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II][/us/pl/103/239/tII], § 204, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 580][/us/stat/108/580], related to approval of application.

    Section 6125, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II][/us/pl/103/239/tII], § 205, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 580][/us/stat/108/580], related to use of amounts from grant.

    Section 6126, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II][/us/pl/103/239/tII], § 206, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 581][/us/stat/108/581], related to maintenance of effort by State.

    Section 6127, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II][/us/pl/103/239/tII], § 207, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 582][/us/stat/108/582], required reports by State.
