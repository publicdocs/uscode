## §§ 6211 to 6215. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 6211 to 6215 were omitted pursuant to [section 6251 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6251] which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001.

    Section 6211, [Pub. L. 103–239, title V][/us/pl/103/239/tV], § 501, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 597][/us/stat/108/597], related to State and local partnership requests and responsibilities for waivers.

    Section 6212, [Pub. L. 103–239, title V][/us/pl/103/239/tV], § 502, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 598][/us/stat/108/598]; [Pub. L. 103–382, title III][/us/pl/103/382/tIII], § 394(j)(2), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4029][/us/stat/108/4029]; [Pub. L. 107–110, title X][/us/pl/107/110/tX], § 1076(q), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 2092][/us/stat/115/2092], related to waiver authority of Secretary of Education.

    Section 6213, [Pub. L. 103–239, title V][/us/pl/103/239/tV], § 503, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 600][/us/stat/108/600], related to waiver authority of Secretary of Labor.

    Section 6214, [Pub. L. 103–239, title V][/us/pl/103/239/tV], § 504, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 601][/us/stat/108/601], related to combination of Federal funds for high poverty schools.

    Section 6215, [Pub. L. 103–239, title V][/us/pl/103/239/tV], § 505, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 602][/us/stat/108/602], related to combination of Federal funds by States for school-to-work activities.
