## §§ 6191 to 6196. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 6191 to 6196 were omitted pursuant to [section 6251 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6251] which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001.

    Section 6191, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV][/us/pl/103/239/tIV], § 401, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 594][/us/stat/108/594], authorized projects to further the purposes of this chapter.

    Section 6192, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV][/us/pl/103/239/tIV], § 402, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 594][/us/stat/108/594], related to performance outcomes and evaluation.

    Section 6193, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV][/us/pl/103/239/tIV], § 403, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 596][/us/stat/108/596], related to training and technical assistance.

    Section 6194, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV][/us/pl/103/239/tIV], § 404, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 596][/us/stat/108/596]; [Pub. L. 107–279, title IV][/us/pl/107/279/tIV], § 404(e), Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1986][/us/stat/116/1986], related to Capacity Building and Information and Dissemination Network.

    Section 6195, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV][/us/pl/103/239/tIV], § 405, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 597][/us/stat/108/597], required reports to Congress.

    Section 6196, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV][/us/pl/103/239/tIV], § 406, May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 597][/us/stat/108/597], related to funding.
