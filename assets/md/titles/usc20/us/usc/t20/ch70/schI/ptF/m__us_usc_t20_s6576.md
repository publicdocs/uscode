## § 6576. Rule of construction on equalized spending

    Nothing in this subchapter shall be construed to mandate equalized spending per pupil for a State, local educational agency, or school.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title I][/us/pl/89/10/tI], § 1605, formerly § 1906, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title I][/us/pl/107/110/tI], § 101, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1619][/us/stat/115/1619]; renumbered § 1605, [Pub. L. 114–95, title I][/us/pl/114/95/tI], § 1501(a)(5), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1906][/us/stat/129/1906].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 1605 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s1605] was classified to [section 6515 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6515], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 114–95, title I][/us/pl/114/95/tI], § 1501(a)(1), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1905][/us/stat/129/1905].
