    Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III][/us/pl/89/10/tIII], § 3122, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title III][/us/pl/107/110/tIII], § 301, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1702][/us/stat/115/1702], related to achievement objectives and accountability.

L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
