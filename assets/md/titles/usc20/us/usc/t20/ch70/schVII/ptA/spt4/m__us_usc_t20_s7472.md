## § 7472. Peer review

    The Secretary may use a peer review process to review applications submitted to the Secretary under subpart 2 or subpart 3 of this part.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7142][/us/pl/89/10/s7142], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VII, § 701][/us/pl/107/110/s701], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1931][/us/stat/115/1931].)
