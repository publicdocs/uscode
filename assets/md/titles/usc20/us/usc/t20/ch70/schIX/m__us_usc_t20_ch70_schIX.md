### SUBCHAPTER IX—GENERAL PROVISIONS

 __Codification__ 

    Title IX of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, comprising this subchapter, was originally enacted as part of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 27][/us/stat/79/27], amended, and subsequently revised, restated, and amended by other public laws. Title IX is shown, herein, as having been added by [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX, § 901][/us/pl/107/110/s901], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1956][/us/stat/115/1956], without reference to earlier amendments because of the extensive revision of the title’s provisions by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110]. See Codification note preceding [section 6301 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6301].
