## § 6533. Funding distribution rule

    From amounts appropriated under [section 6302(g) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6302/g] for a fiscal year, the Secretary shall give priority to funding activities under [section 6534 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6534] and shall distribute any remaining funds under [section 6535 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6535].

([Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1703][/us/pl/89/10/s1703], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title I, § 101][/us/pl/107/110/s101], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1606][/us/stat/115/1606].)
