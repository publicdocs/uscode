## § 7255. Short title

    This subpart may be cited as the “Star Schools Act”.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5471][/us/pl/89/10/s5471], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1829][/us/stat/115/1829].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions naming former part B (§ 6891 et seq.) of this subchapter as the “Star Schools Act” were contained in [section 6891 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6891], prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
