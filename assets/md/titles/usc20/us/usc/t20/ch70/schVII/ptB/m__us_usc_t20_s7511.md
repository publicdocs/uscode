## § 7511. Short title

    This part may be cited as the “Native Hawaiian Education Act”.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7201][/us/pl/89/10/s7201], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VII, § 701][/us/pl/107/110/s701], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1932][/us/stat/115/1932].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions naming former part B (§ 7901 et seq.) of subchapter IX of this chapter as the “Native Hawaiian Education Act” were contained in [section 7901 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7901], prior to the general amendment of subchapter IX by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].

    A prior section 7511, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7201][/us/pl/89/10/s7201], as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/382/s101], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3737][/us/stat/108/3737], set forth short title of the Foreign Language Assistance Act of 1994, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
