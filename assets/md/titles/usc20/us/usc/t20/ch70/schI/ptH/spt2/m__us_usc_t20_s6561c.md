## § 6561c. State reservation

    A State educational agency that receives a grant under paragraph (2) or (3) of [section 6561a(a) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6561a/a] may reserve not more than 5 percent of the grant funds for administrative costs and State activities related to school dropout prevention and reentry activities, of which not more than 2 percent of the grant funds may be used for administrative costs.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1824][/us/pl/89/10/s1824], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title I, § 101][/us/pl/107/110/s101], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1615][/us/stat/115/1615].)
