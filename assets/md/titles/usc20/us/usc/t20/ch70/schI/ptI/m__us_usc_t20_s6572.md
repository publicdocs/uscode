## § 6572. Agreements and records

    (a) __Agreements__ 

        All published proposed regulations shall conform to agreements that result from negotiated rulemaking described in [section 6571 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6571] unless the Secretary reopens the negotiated rulemaking process or provides a written explanation to the participants involved in the process explaining why the Secretary decided to depart from, and not adhere to, such agreements.

    (b) __Records__ 

        The Secretary shall ensure that an accurate and reliable record of agreements reached during the negotiations process is maintained.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1902][/us/pl/89/10/s1902], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title I, § 101][/us/pl/107/110/s101], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1618][/us/stat/115/1618].)
