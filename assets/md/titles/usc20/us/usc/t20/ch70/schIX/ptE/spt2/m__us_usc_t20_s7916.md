## § 7916. Severability

    If any provision of this chapter is held invalid, the remainder of this chapter shall be unaffected thereby.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 9536][/us/pl/89/10/s9536], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX, § 901][/us/pl/107/110/s901], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1985][/us/stat/115/1985].)
