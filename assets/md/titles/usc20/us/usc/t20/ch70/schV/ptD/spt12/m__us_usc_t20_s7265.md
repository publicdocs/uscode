## § 7265. Short title

    This subpart may be cited as the “Alaska Native and Native Hawaiian Education Through Cultural and Historical Organizations Act”.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5521][/us/pl/89/10/s5521], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1845][/us/stat/115/1845].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 7265, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5305][/us/pl/89/10/s5305], as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/382/s101], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3704][/us/stat/108/3704], related to use of grants for dropout prevention activities, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110]. See [section 6555 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6555].
