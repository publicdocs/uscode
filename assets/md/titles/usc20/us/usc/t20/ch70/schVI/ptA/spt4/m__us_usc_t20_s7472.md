## § 7472. Peer review

    The Secretary may use a peer review process to review applications submitted to the Secretary under subpart 2 or subpart 3 of this part.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VI][/us/pl/89/10/tVI], § 6142, formerly title VII, § 7142, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VII][/us/pl/107/110/tVII], § 701, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1931][/us/stat/115/1931]; renumbered title VI, § 6142, [Pub. L. 114–95, title VI][/us/pl/114/95/tVI], § 6001(a), (b)(1), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2046][/us/stat/129/2046].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 7472, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 7142, as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I][/us/pl/103/382/tI], § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3732][/us/stat/108/3732], related to training for all teachers program, prior to the general amendment of former subchapter VII of this chapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].

    A prior [section 6142 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s6142] was classified to [section 7315a of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7315a], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 114–95][/us/pl/114/95].
