## § 6751. Short title

    This part may be cited as the “Enhancing Education Through Technology Act of 2001”.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2401][/us/pl/89/10/s2401], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title II, § 201][/us/pl/107/110/s201], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1671][/us/stat/115/1671].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 2401 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s2401] was classified to [section 6701 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6701], prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
