## § 7325a. Peer review

    The Secretary shall use a peer review process to review, based on data from the State assessments administered under [section 6311(b)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6311/b/3] and on data from the evaluations conducted under [section 6841 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6841], whether the State has failed to make adequate yearly progress for 2 consecutive years or whether the State has met its annual measurable achievement objectives.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6162][/us/pl/89/10/s6162], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VI, § 601][/us/pl/107/110/s601], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1890][/us/stat/115/1890].)
