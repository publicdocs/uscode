## § 7903. Privacy of assessment results

    Any results from an individual assessment referred to in this chapter of a student that become part of the education records of the student shall have the protections provided in [section 1232g of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1232g].

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII][/us/pl/89/10/tVIII], § 8523, formerly title IX, § 9523, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX][/us/pl/107/110/tIX], § 901, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1980][/us/stat/115/1980]; renumbered title VIII, § 8523, [Pub. L. 114–95, title VIII][/us/pl/114/95/tVIII], § 8001(a)(6), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2088][/us/stat/129/2088], 2089.)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 7903, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], § 9203, as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I][/us/pl/103/382/tI], § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3798][/us/stat/108/3798], set forth purpose of provisions relating to Native Hawaiian educational programs, prior to the general amendment of former subchapter IX of this chapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
