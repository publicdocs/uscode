## § 6663. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part $450,000,000 for fiscal year 2002 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 5 succeeding fiscal years.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2203][/us/pl/89/10/s2203], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title II, § 201][/us/pl/107/110/s201], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1647][/us/stat/115/1647].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 2203 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s2203] was classified to [section 6643 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6643], prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].

    Another prior [section 2203 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s2203] was classified to [section 3013 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3013], prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] by [Pub. L. 103–382][/us/pl/103/382].
