##### subpart 3—gun possession

 __Codification__ 

    Subpart 3 of part A of title IV of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act, comprising this subpart, was redesignated subpart 4 of part F of title VIII of the Act by [Pub. L. 114–95, title IV][/us/pl/114/95/tIV], § 4001(a)(2)(A)–(C), title VIII, § 8001(a), (b)(1), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1966][/us/stat/129/1966], 2088, 2089, and transferred to subpart 4 (§ 7961) of part F of subchapter VIII of this chapter.
