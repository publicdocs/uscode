## § 7911. Prohibition on nationwide database

    Nothing in this chapter (other than [section 6398(b) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6398/b]) shall be construed to authorize the development of a nationwide database of personally identifiable information on individuals involved in studies or other collections of data under this chapter.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 9531][/us/pl/89/10/s9531], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX, § 901][/us/pl/107/110/s901], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1984][/us/stat/115/1984].)

 __Prior Provisions__

L. 103–382, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/382/s101], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3804][/us/stat/108/3804], set out administrative provisions, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110]. See [section 7516 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7516].
