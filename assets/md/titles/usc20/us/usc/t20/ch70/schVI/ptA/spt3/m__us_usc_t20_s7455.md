## § 7455. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 7135, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VII][/us/pl/107/110/tVII], § 701, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1928][/us/stat/115/1928], which related to grants to Indian tribes for education administrative planning and development, was renumbered title VI, § 6132, of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] by [Pub. L. 114–95, title VI][/us/pl/114/95/tVI], §§ 6001(a), (b)(1), 6002(n)(2), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2046][/us/stat/129/2046], 2057, and transferred to [section 7452 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7452].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 7455, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 7135, as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I][/us/pl/103/382/tI], § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3731][/us/stat/108/3731], established National Clearinghouse for Bilingual Education, prior to the general amendment of former subchapter VII of this chapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
