## § 7941. Short title

    This subpart may be cited as the “Paul D. Coverdell Teacher Protection Act of 2001”.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII][/us/pl/89/10/tVIII], § 8551, formerly title II, § 2361, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title II][/us/pl/107/110/tII], § 201, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1667][/us/stat/115/1667]; renumbered title IX, § 9541, renumbered title VIII, § 8551, [Pub. L. 114–95, title II][/us/pl/114/95/tII], § 2001(a)(3)(A), (B), (D), title VIII, § 8001(a)(7), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1913][/us/stat/129/1913], 2088, 2089.)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was classified to [section 6731 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6731] prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 114–95][/us/pl/114/95].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 7941, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], § 9601, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX][/us/pl/107/110/tIX], § 901, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1985][/us/stat/115/1985], which related to evaluations, was renumbered [section 8601 of title VIII of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s8601] by [Pub. L. 114–95, title VIII][/us/pl/114/95/tVIII], § 8001(a)(10), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2088][/us/stat/129/2088], 2089, and transferred to [section 7981 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7981].
