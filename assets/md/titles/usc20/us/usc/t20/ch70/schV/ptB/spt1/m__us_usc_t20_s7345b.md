## § 7345b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 114–95, title V][/us/pl/114/95/tV], § 5003(3), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2043][/us/stat/129/2043]

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V][/us/pl/89/10/tV], § 5213, formerly title VI, § 6213, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VI][/us/pl/107/110/tVI], § 601, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1893][/us/stat/115/1893]; renumbered title V, § 5213, [Pub.  __Effective Date of Repeal__
