#### Part B—State Assessment Grants

 __Codification__ 

    Part B of title I of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, comprising this part, was originally enacted in the general amendment of title I of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 27][/us/stat/79/27], by [Pub. L. 107–110, title I][/us/pl/107/110/tI], § 101, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1535][/us/stat/115/1535], and amended by [Pub. L. 108–7][/us/pl/108/7], Feb. 20, 2003, [117 Stat. 11][/us/stat/117/11]; [Pub. L. 110–154][/us/pl/110/154], Dec. 21, 2007, [121 Stat. 1826][/us/stat/121/1826]; [Pub. L. 113–128][/us/pl/113/128], July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1425][/us/stat/128/1425]. Part B is shown herein, however, as having been added by [Pub. L. 114–95, title I][/us/pl/114/95/tI], § 1201, Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1879][/us/stat/129/1879], without reference to those intervening amendments because of the extensive revision of part B by [Pub. L. 114–95][/us/pl/114/95]. See Codification note preceding [section 6301 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6301].
