## § 8701. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title XIII, § 13401][/us/pl/89/10/s13401], as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/382/s101], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3886][/us/stat/108/3886], which authorized technology-based technical assistance, was renumbered section 1031 of the Educational Research, Development, Dissemination, and Improvement Act of 1994 by [Pub.
