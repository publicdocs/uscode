## § 7429. State educational agency review

    Before submitting an application to the Secretary under [section 7424 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7424], a local educational agency shall submit the application to the State educational agency, which may comment on such application. If the State educational agency comments on the application, the agency shall comment on all applications submitted by local educational agencies in the State and shall provide those comments to the respective local educational agencies, with an opportunity to respond.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VI][/us/pl/89/10/tVI], § 6119, formerly title VII, § 7119, as added [Pub.
