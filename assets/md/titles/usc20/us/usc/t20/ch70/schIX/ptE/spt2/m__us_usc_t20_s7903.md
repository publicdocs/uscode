## § 7903. Privacy of assessment results

    Any results from an individual assessment referred to in this chapter of a student that become part of the education records of the student shall have the protections provided in [section 1232g of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1232g].

([Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 9523][/us/pl/89/10/s9523], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX, § 901][/us/pl/107/110/s901], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1980][/us/stat/115/1980].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 7903, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 9203][/us/pl/89/10/s9203], as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/382/s101], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3798][/us/stat/108/3798], set forth purpose of provisions relating to Native Hawaiian educational programs. See [section 7513 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7513].
