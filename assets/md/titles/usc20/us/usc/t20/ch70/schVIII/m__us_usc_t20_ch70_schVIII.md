### SUBCHAPTER VIII—GENERAL PROVISIONS

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 114–95, title VIII][/us/pl/114/95/tVIII], § 8001(a), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2088][/us/stat/129/2088], redesignated subchapter IX (§ 7801 et seq.) of this chapter as this subchapter.

    Title VIII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, comprising this subchapter, was originally enacted as part of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 27][/us/stat/79/27], and subsequently revised, restated, redesignated, and amended by other public laws. Title VIII is shown, herein, as having been added as title IX of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] by [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX][/us/pl/107/110/tIX], § 901, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1956][/us/stat/115/1956], without reference to earlier amendments because of the extensive revision of the title’s provisions by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110]. Title IX of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] was subsequently redesignated title VIII by [Pub. L. 114–95, title VIII][/us/pl/114/95/tVIII], § 8001(a), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2088][/us/stat/129/2088], and transferred to this subchapter. See Codification note preceding [section 6301 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6301].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior title VIII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, comprising former subchapter VIII (§ 7701 et seq.) of this chapter, was redesignated title VII of the Act by [Pub. L. 114–95, title VII][/us/pl/114/95/tVII], § 7001(c), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2074][/us/stat/129/2074], and transferred to subchapter VII (§ 7701 et seq.) of this chapter.
