## § 7261. Short title

    This subpart may be cited as the “Carol M. White Physical Education Program”.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5501][/us/pl/89/10/s5501], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1841][/us/stat/115/1841].)
