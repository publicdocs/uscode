## § 7117. Programs for Native Hawaiians

    (a) __General authority__ 

        From the funds made available pursuant to [section 7111(a)(1)(C) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7111/a/1/C] to carry out this section, the Secretary shall make grants to or enter into cooperative agreements or contracts with organizations primarily serving and representing Native Hawaiians for the benefit of Native Hawaiians to plan, conduct, and administer programs, or portions thereof, that are authorized by and consistent with the provisions of this subpart.

    (b) __Definition of Native Hawaiian__ 

        For the purposes of this section, the term “Native Hawaiian” means any individual any of whose ancestors were natives, prior to 1778, of the area which now comprises the State of Hawaii.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 4117][/us/pl/89/10/s4117], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IV, § 401][/us/pl/107/110/s401], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1750][/us/stat/115/1750].)
