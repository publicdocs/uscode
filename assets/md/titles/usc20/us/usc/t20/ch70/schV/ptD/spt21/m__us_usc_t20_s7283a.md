## § 7283a. Statement of purpose

    It is the purpose of this subpart—

    (1) to promote gender equity in education in the United States;

    (2) to provide financial assistance to enable educational agencies and institutions to meet the requirements of title IX of the Educational Amendments of 1972 \[[20 U.S.C. 1681][/us/usc/t20/s1681] et seq.\]; and

    (3) to promote equity in education for women and girls who suffer from multiple forms of discrimination based on sex, race, ethnic origin, limited English proficiency, disability, or age.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5612][/us/pl/89/10/s5612], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1868][/us/stat/115/1868].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Educational Amendments of 1972, referred to in par. (2), probably means the Education Amendments of 1972 which is [Pub. L. 92–318][/us/pl/92/318], June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 235][/us/stat/86/235], as amended. Title IX of the Act, known as the Patsy Takemoto Mink Equal Opportunity in Education Act, is classified principally to chapter 38 (§ 1681 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of title IX to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1681 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1681] and Tables.
