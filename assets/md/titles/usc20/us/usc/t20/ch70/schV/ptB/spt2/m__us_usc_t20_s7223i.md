## § 7223i. Definitions

    In this subpart:

    (1) __Charter school__ 

        The term “charter school” has the meaning given such term in [section 7221i of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7221i].

    (2) __Eligible entity__ 

    The term “eligible entity” means—

        (A) a public entity, such as a State or local governmental entity;

        (B) a private nonprofit entity; or

        (C) a consortium of entities described in subparagraphs (A) and (B).

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5230][/us/pl/89/10/s5230], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1803][/us/stat/115/1803].)
