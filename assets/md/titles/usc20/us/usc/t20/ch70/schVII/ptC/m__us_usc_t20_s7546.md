## § 7546. Definitions

    In this part:

    (1) __Alaska Native__ 

        The term “Alaska Native” has the same meaning as the term “Native” has in [section 1602(b) of title 43][/us/usc/t43/s1602/b].

    (2) __Alaska Native organization__ 

    The term “Alaska Native organization” means a federally recognized tribe, consortium of tribes, regional nonprofit Native association, and another organization that—

        (A) has or commits to acquire expertise in the education of Alaska Natives; and

        (B) has Alaska Natives in substantive and policymaking positions within the organization.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7306][/us/pl/89/10/s7306], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VII, § 701][/us/pl/107/110/s701], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1946][/us/stat/115/1946].)
