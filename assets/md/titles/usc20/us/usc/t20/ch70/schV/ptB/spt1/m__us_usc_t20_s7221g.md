## § 7221g. Records transfer

    State educational agencies and local educational agencies, to the extent practicable, shall ensure that a student’s records and, if applicable, a student’s individualized education program as defined in [section 1401 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1401], are transferred to a charter school upon the transfer of the student to the charter school, and to another public school upon the transfer of the student from a charter school to another public school, in accordance with applicable State law.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5208][/us/pl/89/10/s5208], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1798][/us/stat/115/1798]; amended [Pub. L. 108–446, title III, § 305(g)(2)][/us/pl/108/446/s305/g/2], Dec. 3, 2004, [118 Stat. 2805][/us/stat/118/2805].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 5208 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s5208] was classified to [section 7238 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7238], prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].

 __Amendments__ 

    2004—[Pub. L. 108–446][/us/pl/108/446] substituted “section 1401” for “section 1401(11)”.
