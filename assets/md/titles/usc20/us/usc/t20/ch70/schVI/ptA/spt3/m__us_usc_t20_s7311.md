## § 7311. Short title

    This subpart may be cited as the “State and Local Flexibility Demonstration Act”.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6131][/us/pl/89/10/s6131], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VI, § 601][/us/pl/107/110/s601], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1878][/us/stat/115/1878].)
