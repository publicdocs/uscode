## § 6374. Information dissemination

    From the funds the National Institute for Literacy receives under [section 6362(b)(1)(D) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6362/b/1/D], the National Institute for Literacy, in consultation with the Secretary, shall disseminate information regarding projects assisted under this subpart that have proven effective.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1224][/us/pl/89/10/s1224], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title I, § 101][/us/pl/107/110/s101], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1554][/us/stat/115/1554].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 1224 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s1224] was classified to [section 2794 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2794], prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] by [Pub. L. 103–382][/us/pl/103/382].
