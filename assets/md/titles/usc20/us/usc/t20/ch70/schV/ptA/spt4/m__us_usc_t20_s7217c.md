## § 7217c. Supplement, not supplant

    Funds made available under this part shall be used to supplement, and not supplant, any other Federal, State, or local education funds.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5144][/us/pl/89/10/s5144], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1788][/us/stat/115/1788].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 5144 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s5144] was classified to [section 3224 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3224], prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] by [Pub. L. 103–382][/us/pl/103/382].
