## § 7217e. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part—

    (1) $450,000,000 for fiscal year 2002;

    (2) $475,000,000 for fiscal year 2003;

    (3) $500,000,000 for fiscal year 2004;

    (4) $525,000,000 for fiscal year 2005;

    (5) $550,000,000 for fiscal year 2006; and

    (6) $600,000,000 for fiscal year 2007.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5146][/us/pl/89/10/s5146], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1788][/us/stat/115/1788].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 5146 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s5146] was classified to [section 3224b of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3224b], prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] by [Pub. L. 103–382][/us/pl/103/382].
