## § 7281. Reservation

    The Secretary is authorized to provide additional assistance to meet special circumstances relating to the provision of education in local educational agencies eligible to receive assistance under [section 7702 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7702].

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5601][/us/pl/89/10/s5601], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1866][/us/stat/115/1866].)
