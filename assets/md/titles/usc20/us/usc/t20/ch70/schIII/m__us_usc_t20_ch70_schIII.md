### SUBCHAPTER III—LANGUAGE INSTRUCTION FOR ENGLISH LEARNERS AND IMMIGRANT STUDENTS

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 114–95, title III][/us/pl/114/95/tIII], § 3001(1), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1953][/us/stat/129/1953], substituted “ENGLISH LEARNERS” for “LIMITED ENGLISH PROFICIENT” in subchapter heading.

    Title III of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, comprising this subchapter, was originally enacted as part of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 27][/us/stat/79/27], amended, and subsequently revised, restated, and amended by other public laws. Title III is shown, herein, as having been added by [Pub. L. 107–110, title III][/us/pl/107/110/tIII], § 301, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1689][/us/stat/115/1689], without reference to earlier amendments because of the extensive revision of the title’s provisions by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110]. See Codification note preceding [section 6301 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6301].
