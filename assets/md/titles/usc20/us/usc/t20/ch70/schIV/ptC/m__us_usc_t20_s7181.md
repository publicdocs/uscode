## § 7181. Short title

    This part may be cited as the “Pro-Children Act of 2001”.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 4301][/us/pl/89/10/s4301], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IV, § 401][/us/pl/107/110/s401], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1773][/us/stat/115/1773].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 4301 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s4301] was classified to [section 3081 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3081], prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] by [Pub. L. 103–382][/us/pl/103/382].
