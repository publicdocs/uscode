## § 6671. Funding allotment

    From the funds reserved under [section 6621(4) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6621/4], the Secretary—

    (1) shall use not less than 74 percent to carry out activities under [section 6672 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6672];

    (2) shall use not less than 22 percent to carry out activities under [section 6673 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6673];

    (3) shall use not less than 2 percent to carry out activities under [section 6674 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6674]; and

    (4) may reserve not more than 2 percent to carry out activities under [section 6675 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6675].

([Pub. L. 89–10, title II][/us/pl/89/10/tII], § 2241, as added [Pub. L. 114–95, title II][/us/pl/114/95/tII], § 2002, Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1948][/us/stat/129/1948].)
