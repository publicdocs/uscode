## § 6373. Federal administration

    The Secretary shall consult with the Secretary of Health and Human Services to coordinate the activities under this subpart with preschool age programs administered by the Department of Health and Human Services.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1223][/us/pl/89/10/s1223], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title I, § 101][/us/pl/107/110/s101], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1554][/us/stat/115/1554].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 1223 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s1223] was classified to [section 2793 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s2793], prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] by [Pub. L. 103–382][/us/pl/103/382].
