## § 7841. Purposes

The purposes of this part are—

    (1) to improve teaching and learning by encouraging greater cross-program coordination, planning, and service delivery;

    (2) to provide greater flexibility to State and local authorities through consolidated plans, applications, and reporting; and

    (3) to enhance the integration of programs under this chapter with State and local programs.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 9301][/us/pl/89/10/s9301], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX, § 901][/us/pl/107/110/s901], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1968][/us/stat/115/1968].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 9301 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s9301] was classified to [section 7931 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7931], prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
