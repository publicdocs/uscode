## § 6734. Applicability

    This subpart shall only apply to States that receive funds under this chapter, and shall apply to such a State as a condition of receiving such funds.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2364][/us/pl/89/10/s2364], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title II, § 201][/us/pl/107/110/s201], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1668][/us/stat/115/1668].)
