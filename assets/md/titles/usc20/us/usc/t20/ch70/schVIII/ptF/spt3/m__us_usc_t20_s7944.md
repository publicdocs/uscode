## § 7944. Applicability

    This subpart shall only apply to States that receive funds under this chapter, and shall apply to such a State as a condition of receiving such funds.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII][/us/pl/89/10/tVIII], § 8554, formerly title II, § 2364, as added [Pub. L. 114–95, title II][/us/pl/114/95/tII], § 2001(a)(3)(A), (B), (D), title VIII, § 8001(a)(7), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1913][/us/stat/129/1913], 2088, 2089.)
