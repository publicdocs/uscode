## § 7283g. Amount

    From amounts made available to carry out this subpart for a fiscal year, not less than two-thirds of such amount shall be used to carry out the activities described in [section 7283b(b)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7283b/b/1].

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5618][/us/pl/89/10/s5618], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1873][/us/stat/115/1873].)
