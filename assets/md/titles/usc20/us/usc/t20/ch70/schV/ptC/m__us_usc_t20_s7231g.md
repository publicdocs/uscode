## § 7231g. Prohibition

    Grants under this part may not be used for transportation or any activity that does not augment academic improvement.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5308][/us/pl/89/10/s5308], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1810][/us/stat/115/1810].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 5308 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s5308] was classified to [section 7268 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7268], prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
