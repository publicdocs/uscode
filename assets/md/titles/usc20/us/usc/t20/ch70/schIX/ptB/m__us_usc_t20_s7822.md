## § 7822. Single local educational agency States

    A State educational agency that also serves as a local educational agency shall, in its applications or plans under this chapter, describe how the agency will eliminate duplication in conducting administrative functions.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 9202][/us/pl/89/10/s9202], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX, § 901][/us/pl/107/110/s901], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1967][/us/stat/115/1967].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 9202 of Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10/s9202] was classified to [section 7902 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7902], prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
