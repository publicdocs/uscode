## § 7261f. Supplement, not supplant

    Funds made available under this subpart shall be used to supplement, and not supplant, any other Federal, State, or local funds available for physical education activities.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5507][/us/pl/89/10/s5507], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1842][/us/stat/115/1842].)

 __Prior Provisions__

L. 103–382, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/382/s101], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3701][/us/stat/108/3701], which set forth purpose of the School Dropout Assistance Act, was omitted in the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110].
