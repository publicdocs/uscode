### SUBCHAPTER VII—IMPACT AID

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 114–95, title VII][/us/pl/114/95/tVII], § 7001(c), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2074][/us/stat/129/2074], redesignated subchapter VIII (§ 7701 et seq.) of this chapter as this subchapter.

    Title VII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, comprising this subchapter, was originally enacted as part of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 27][/us/stat/79/27], and subsequently revised, restated, redesignated, and amended by other public laws. Title VII is shown, herein, as having been added as title VIII of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I][/us/pl/103/382/tI], § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3749][/us/stat/108/3749], without reference to earlier amendments because of the extensive revision of the title’s provisions by [Pub. L. 103–382][/us/pl/103/382]. Title VIII of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10] was subsequently redesignated title VII by [Pub. L. 114–95, title VII][/us/pl/114/95/tVII], § 7001(c), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2074][/us/stat/129/2074], and transferred to this subchapter. See Codification note preceding [section 6301 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6301].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior title VII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, comprising former subchapter VII (§ 7401 et seq.) of this chapter, was redesignated title VI of the Act by [Pub. L. 114–95, title VI][/us/pl/114/95/tVI], § 6001(a), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2046][/us/stat/129/2046], and transferred to subchapter VI (§ 7401 et seq.) of this chapter.
