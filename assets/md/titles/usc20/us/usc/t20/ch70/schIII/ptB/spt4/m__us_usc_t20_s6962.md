## § 6962. State administrative costs

    For any fiscal year, a State educational agency may reserve not more than 1.5 percent (2 percent if the State educational agency distributes funds received under this subpart to local educational agencies on a competitive basis) of the amount allotted to such agency under [section 6964 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6964] to pay the costs of performing such agency’s administrative functions under this subpart.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 3242][/us/pl/89/10/s3242], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title III, § 301][/us/pl/107/110/s301], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1723][/us/stat/115/1723].)
