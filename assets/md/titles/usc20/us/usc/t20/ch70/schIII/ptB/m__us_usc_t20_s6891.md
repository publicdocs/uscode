## § 6891. Short title

    This part may be cited as the “Improving Language Instruction Educational Programs For Academic Achievement Act”.

([Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 3201][/us/pl/89/10/s3201], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title III, § 301][/us/pl/107/110/s301], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1706][/us/stat/115/1706].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 6891, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 3201][/us/pl/89/10/s3201], as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/382/s101], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3654][/us/stat/108/3654], set out short title of the Star Schools Act, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110][/us/pl/107/110]. See [section 7255 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s7255].
