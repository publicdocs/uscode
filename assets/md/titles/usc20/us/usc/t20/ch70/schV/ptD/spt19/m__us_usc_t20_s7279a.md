## § 7279a. Uses of funds

    A local educational agency that receives a subgrant under this subpart shall use the subgrant funds only to pay for capital expenses incurred in providing equitable services for private school students under [section 6320 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6320].

([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5592][/us/pl/89/10/s5592], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title V, § 501][/us/pl/107/110/s501], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1865][/us/stat/115/1865].)
