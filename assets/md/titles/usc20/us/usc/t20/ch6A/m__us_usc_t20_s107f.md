## § 107f. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for carrying out the provisions of this chapter.

([June 20, 1936, ch. 638][/us/act/1936-06-20/ch638], § 10, formerly § 7, [49 Stat. 1560][/us/stat/49/1560]; renumbered § 8, [Aug. 3, 1954, ch. 655][/us/act/1954-08-03/ch655], § 4(g), [68 Stat. 664][/us/stat/68/664]; renumbered § 10, [Pub. L. 93–516, title II][/us/pl/93/516/tII], § 206, Dec. 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 1626][/us/stat/88/1626]; [Pub. L. 93–651, title II][/us/pl/93/651/tII], § 206, Nov. 21, 1974, [89 Stat. 2–11][/us/stat/89/2-11].)

 __Codification__ 

    The content of [Pub. L. 93–516][/us/pl/93/516], including provisions of section 206 thereof which renumbered this section, were originally contained in H.R. 14225, 93rd Congress, Second Session, which was pocket-vetoed during the 31-day intrasession adjournment of the 93rd Congress for the Congressional elections in November, 1974.

    Pursuant to an order of the United States District Court for the District of Columbia (Kennedy v. Jones, D.C.D.C. 1976, 412 F.Supp. 353) H.R. 14225 was deemed to have become law without the approval of the President on Nov. 21, 1974, and was given the designation [Pub. L. 93–651][/us/pl/93/651]. Therefore, for purposes of codification, this section should be deemed to have been renumbered by [Pub. L. 93–651, title II][/us/pl/93/651/tII], § 206, Nov. 21, 1974, [89 Stat. 2–11][/us/stat/89/2-11], in exactly the same manner as it was amended by [Pub. L. 93–516, title II][/us/pl/93/516/tII], § 206, Nov. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 1626][/us/stat/88/1626].
