## §§ 3801 to 3808. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1003(a)][/us/pl/100/297/s1003/a], Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 293][/us/stat/102/293]

L. 98–211, § 1][/us/pl/98/211/s1], Dec. 8, 1983, [97 Stat. 1412][/us/stat/97/1412]; [Pub.     Section 3805, [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 556][/us/pl/97/35/s556], Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 465][/us/stat/95/465]; [Pub. L. 98–211][/us/pl/98/211], §§ 2–4, Dec. 8, 1983, [97 Stat. 1412–1414][/us/stat/97/1412-1414], related to approval of applications.

L. 98–211][/us/pl/98/211], §§ 6–8, Dec. 8, 1983, [97 Stat. 1415][/us/stat/97/1415], related to general provisions for program of financial assistance to meet the special educational needs of disadvantaged children.

    Section 3808, [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 559][/us/pl/97/35/s559], as added [Pub. L. 98–211, § 22][/us/pl/98/211/s22], Dec. 8, 1983, [97 Stat. 1418][/us/stat/97/1418], related to national assessment of compensatory education assistance.

 __Short Title__ 

    [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 551][/us/pl/97/35/s551], Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 463][/us/stat/95/463], provided that subtitle D \[§§ 551 to 596\] of title V of [Pub. L. 97–35][/us/pl/97/35] was to be cited as the “Education Consolidation and Improvement Act of 1981”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1003(a)][/us/pl/100/297/s1003/a], Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 293][/us/stat/102/293].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective July 1, 1988, see [section 6303 of Pub. L. 100–297][/us/pl/100/297/s6303], set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 1071 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1071].
