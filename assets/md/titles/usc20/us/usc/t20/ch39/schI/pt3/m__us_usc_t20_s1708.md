## § 1708. Jurisdiction of district courts

    The appropriate district court of the United States shall have and exercise jurisdiction of proceedings instituted under [section 1706 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1706].

([Pub. L. 93–380, title II][/us/pl/93/380/tII], § 209, Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 516][/us/stat/88/516].)
