## § 1161n–1. Reservations

    (a) __Reservations__ 

    Of the amount appropriated to carry out this part in each fiscal year—

        (1) not less than 50 percent shall be available for awarding grants to institutions of higher education and consortia of such institutions described in [section 1161n–2(a)(1)(A) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1161n–2/a/1/A] for cooperative education under [section 1161n–2 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1161n–2];

        (2) not less than 25 percent shall be available for awarding grants to institutions of higher education described in [section 1161n–2(a)(1)(B) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1161n–2/a/1/B] for cooperative education under [section 1161n–2 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1161n–2];

        (3) not to exceed 11 percent shall be available for demonstration projects under paragraph (1) of [section 1161n–3(a) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1161n–3/a];

        (4) not to exceed 11 percent shall be available for training and resource centers under paragraph (2) of [section 1161n–3(a) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1161n–3/a]; and

        (5) not to exceed 3 percent shall be available for research under paragraph (3) of [section 1161n–3(a) of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1161n–3/a].

    (b) __Availability of appropriations__ 

        Amounts appropriated under this part shall not be used for the payment of compensation of students for employment by employers participating in a program under this part.

([Pub. L. 89–329, title VIII][/us/pl/89/329/tVIII], § 832, as added [Pub. L. 110–315, title VIII][/us/pl/110/315/tVIII], § 801, Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3415][/us/stat/122/3415].)
