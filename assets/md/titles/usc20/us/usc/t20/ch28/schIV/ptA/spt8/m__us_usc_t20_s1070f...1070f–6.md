## §§ 1070f to 1070f–6. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–315, title IV][/us/pl/110/315/tIV], § 411, Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3226][/us/stat/122/3226]

    Section 1070f, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV][/us/pl/89/329/tIV], § 420D, as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV][/us/pl/105/244/tIV], § 411, Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1671][/us/stat/112/1671], set out congressional findings.

L. 105–244][/us/pl/105/244].

    Section 1070f–1, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV][/us/pl/89/329/tIV], § 420E, as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV][/us/pl/105/244/tIV], § 411, Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1672][/us/stat/112/1672], related to the purpose of this subpart and the program authorized.

    Section 1070f–2, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV][/us/pl/89/329/tIV], § 420F, as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV][/us/pl/105/244/tIV], § 411, Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1672][/us/stat/112/1672], related to grant application.

L. 105–244, title IV][/us/pl/105/244/tIV], § 411, Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1673][/us/stat/112/1673], provided for a matching requirement limiting Federal funds to not more than 50 percent of the cost of a project.

    Section 1070f–5, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV][/us/pl/89/329/tIV], § 420I, as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV][/us/pl/105/244/tIV], § 411, Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1673][/us/stat/112/1673], required that the Secretary use a peer review process to review applications and make recommendations for funding.
