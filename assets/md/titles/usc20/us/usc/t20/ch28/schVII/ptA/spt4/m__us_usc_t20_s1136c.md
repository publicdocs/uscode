## § 1136c. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __Masters degree programs at historically Black colleges and universities__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out [section 1136a of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1136a] such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2009 and each of the five succeeding fiscal years.

    (b) __Masters degree programs at predominantly Black institutions__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out [section 1136b of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1136b] such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2009 and each of the five succeeding fiscal years.

([Pub. L. 89–329, title VII][/us/pl/89/329/tVII], § 725, as added [Pub. L. 110–315, title VII][/us/pl/110/315/tVII], § 706(b), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3356][/us/stat/122/3356].)
