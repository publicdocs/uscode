## § 1135e. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated $35,000,000 for fiscal year 2009 and each of the five succeeding fiscal years to carry out this subpart.

([Pub. L. 89–329, title VII][/us/pl/89/329/tVII], § 716, as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title VII][/us/pl/105/244/tVII], § 701, Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1794][/us/stat/112/1794]; amended [Pub. L. 110–315, title VII][/us/pl/110/315/tVII], § 703(d), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3347][/us/stat/122/3347].)
