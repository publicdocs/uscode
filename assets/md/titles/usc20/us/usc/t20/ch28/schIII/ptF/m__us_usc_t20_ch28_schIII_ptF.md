#### Part F—Strengthening Historically Black Colleges and Universities and Other Minority-Serving Institutions

 __Codification__ 

    Part F of title III of [Pub. L. 89–329][/us/pl/89/329], comprising this part, was originally added as part J of title IV of [Pub. L. 89–329][/us/pl/89/329] by [Pub. L. 110–84, title VIII][/us/pl/110/84/tVIII], § 802, Sept. 27, 2007, [121 Stat. 817][/us/stat/121/817], and was classified to part I (§ 1099e) of subchapter IV of this chapter. Part J of title IV of [Pub. L. 89–329][/us/pl/89/329] was subsequently redesignated part F of title III of [Pub. L. 89–329][/us/pl/89/329] by [Pub. L. 110–315, title III][/us/pl/110/315/tIII], § 316(a)(2), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3185][/us/stat/122/3185], and transferred to this part.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior part F \[§ 1068 et seq.\] of this subchapter was redesignated part G by [Pub. L. 110–315, title III][/us/pl/110/315/tIII], § 316(a)(1), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3185][/us/stat/122/3185].
