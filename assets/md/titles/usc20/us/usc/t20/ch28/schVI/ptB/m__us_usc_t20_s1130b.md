## § 1130b. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __Centers for international business education__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for the fiscal year 2009 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the five succeeding fiscal years to carry out the provisions of [section 1130–1 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1130–1].

    (b) __Education and training programs__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2009, and such sums as may be necessary for the five succeeding fiscal years, to carry out the provisions of [section 1130a of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1130a].
