## § 1140e. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subpart such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2009 and each of the five succeeding fiscal years.

([Pub. L. 89–329, title VII, § 765][/us/pl/89/329/s765], as added [Pub. L. 110–315, title VII, § 709(2)][/us/pl/110/315/s709/2], Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3365][/us/stat/122/3365].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 765 of Pub. L. 89–329][/us/pl/89/329/s765] was classified to [section 1140d of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1140d], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 110–315][/us/pl/110/315].

    Another prior [section 765 of Pub. L. 89–329][/us/pl/89/329/s765] was classified to [section 1132d–4 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1132d–4], prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 96–374][/us/pl/96/374].
