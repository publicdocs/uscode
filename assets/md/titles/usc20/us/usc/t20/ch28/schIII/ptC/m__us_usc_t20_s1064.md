## § 1064. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–325, title III][/us/pl/102/325/tIII], § 304(a)(2), July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 476][/us/stat/106/476]

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–329, title III][/us/pl/89/329/tIII], § 331, as added [Pub. L. 99–498, title III][/us/pl/99/498/tIII], § 301(a), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1298][/us/stat/100/1298], related to establishment of challenge grant program.

    A prior section 1064, [Pub. L. 89–329, title III][/us/pl/89/329/tIII], § 331, as added [Pub. L. 96–374, title III][/us/pl/96/374/tIII], § 301, Oct. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 1395][/us/stat/94/1395]; amended [Pub. L. 97–35, title V][/us/pl/97/35/tV], § 516(c)(2), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 447][/us/stat/95/447], established a challenge grant program, prior to the general revision of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 99–498][/us/pl/99/498].

    Another prior section 1064, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV][/us/pl/89/329/tIV], § 404, Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1233][/us/stat/79/1233], related to time and manner of making application for grant, selection of recipients and conditions precedent to award, prior to the general revision of part A of subchapter IV of this chapter by [Pub. L. 92–318, title I][/us/pl/92/318/tI], § 131(b)(1), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 247][/us/stat/86/247].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1992, see [section 2 of Pub. L. 102–325][/us/pl/102/325/s2], set out as an Effective Date of 1992 Amendment note under [section 1001 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1001].
