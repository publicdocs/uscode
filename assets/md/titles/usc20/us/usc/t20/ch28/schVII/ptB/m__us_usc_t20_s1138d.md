## § 1138d. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2009 and each of the five succeeding fiscal years.

([Pub. L. 89–329, title VII, § 745][/us/pl/89/329/s745], as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title VII, § 701][/us/pl/105/244/s701], Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1798][/us/stat/112/1798]; amended [Pub. L. 110–315, title VII, § 707(g)][/us/pl/110/315/s707/g], Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3360][/us/stat/122/3360].)
