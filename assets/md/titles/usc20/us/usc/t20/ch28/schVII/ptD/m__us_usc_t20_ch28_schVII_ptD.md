#### Part D—Programs To Provide Students With Disabilities With a Quality Higher Education

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior part D consisted of sections 1140 to 1140d and related to demonstration projects to ensure that students with disabilities receive a quality higher education, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 110–315, title VII, § 709(2)][/us/pl/110/315/s709/2], Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3361][/us/stat/122/3361].
