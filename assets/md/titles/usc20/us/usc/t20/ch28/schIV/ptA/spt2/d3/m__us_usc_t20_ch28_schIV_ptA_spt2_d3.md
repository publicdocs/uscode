 __Codification__ 

    Chapter 3 of subpart 2 of part A of title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965, which comprised this division, was originally added to [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV][/us/pl/89/329], by [Pub. L. 102–325, title IV, § 402(a)(4)][/us/pl/102/325/s402/a/4], July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 497][/us/stat/106/497]. L. 105–244, title IV, § 404][/us/pl/105/244/s404], Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1663][/us/stat/112/1663], without reference to [Pub. L. 102–325][/us/pl/102/325] because of the extensive revision of chapter 3 by [Pub. L. 105–244][/us/pl/105/244].
