## § 3422. Office of Inspector General

    There shall be in the Department an Office of Inspector General, established in accordance with the Inspector General Act of 1978.

([Pub. L. 96–88, title II, § 211][/us/pl/96/88/s211], formerly § 212, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 675][/us/stat/93/675]; renumbered § 211, [Pub. L. 103–382, title II, § 271(a)(2)][/us/pl/103/382/s271/a/2], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3929][/us/stat/108/3929].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Inspector General Act of 1978, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 95–452][/us/pl/95/452], Oct. 12, 1978, [92 Stat. 1101][/us/stat/92/1101], as amended, which is set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 211 of Pub. L. 96–88][/us/pl/96/88/s211] was renumbered section 210 and is classified to [section 3421 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s3421].
