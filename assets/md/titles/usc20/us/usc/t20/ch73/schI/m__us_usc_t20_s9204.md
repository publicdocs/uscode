## § 9204. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1999 through 2003.

([Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 205][/us/pl/105/220/s205], Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1062][/us/stat/112/1062]; [Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(f)][/us/pl/105/277/s101/f] \[title VIII, § 404(a)\], Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–337][/us/stat/112/2681-337], 2681–416.)

 __Amendments__ 

    1998—[Pub. L. 105–277][/us/pl/105/277] made technical amendment to reference in original act which appears in text as reference to this subchapter.
