#### Part A—Adult Education and Literacy Programs

 __Codification__ 

    This part was, in the original, designated subtitle A of title II of [Pub. L. 105–220][/us/pl/105/220] and was redesignated part A of this subchapter for purposes of codification, prior to repeal of [Pub. L. 105–220][/us/pl/105/220] by [Pub. L. 113–128, title V, § 511(a)][/us/pl/113/128/s511/a], July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1705][/us/stat/128/1705]. This subchapter did not contain a part B, because subtitle B (§ 251) of title II of [Pub. L. 105–220][/us/pl/105/220] repealed numerous sections of the Code and was executed to those sections, see Tables.
