#### Part A—Adult Education and Literacy Programs

 __Codification__ 

    This part was, in the original, designated subtitle A of title II of [Pub. L. 105–220][/us/pl/105/220] and has been redesignated part A of this subchapter for purposes of codification. This subchapter does not contain a part B, because subtitle B (§ 251) of title II of [Pub.
