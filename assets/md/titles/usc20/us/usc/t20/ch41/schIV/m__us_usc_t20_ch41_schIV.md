### SUBCHAPTER IV—GENERAL PROVISIONS

§§ 1981 to 1983. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 95–561, title XV, § 1529__  __][/us/pl/95/561/s1529]__  __,__  __Nov. 1, 1978__  __,__  __[__  __92 Stat. 2380__  __][/us/stat/92/2380]__ 

    Section 1981, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 731][/us/pl/93/380/s731], Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 596][/us/stat/88/596]; [Pub. L. 94–194, § 7][/us/pl/94/194/s7], Dec. 31, 1975, [89 Stat. 1105][/us/stat/89/1105]; S. Res. 4, Feb. 4, 1977, set forth evaluation requirements for programs.

    Section 1982, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 732][/us/pl/93/380/s732], Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 596][/us/stat/88/596]; [Pub. L. 94–194][/us/pl/94/194], §§ 1(c), 6(c), 9(b), Dec. 31, 1975, [89 Stat. 1104–1106][/us/stat/89/1104-1106], set forth provisions authorizing appropriations for covered programs.

    Section 1983, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 733][/us/pl/93/380/s733], as added [Pub. L. 94–194, § 8][/us/pl/94/194/s8], Dec. 31, 1975, [89 Stat. 1105][/us/stat/89/1105], set forth provisions relating to acceptance of gifts or donations by the Commissioner.
