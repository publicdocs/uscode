## §§ 1941 to 1944. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title XV][/us/pl/95/561/tXV], § 1529, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2380][/us/stat/92/2380]

    Section 1941, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII][/us/pl/93/380/tVII], § 711, Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 591][/us/stat/88/591], set forth provisions respecting statement of purpose for State reading improvement programs.

    Section 1942, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII][/us/pl/93/380/tVII], § 712, Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 591][/us/stat/88/591], set forth applicability and effective date of provisions respecting State reading improvement programs.

    Section 1943, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII][/us/pl/93/380/tVII], § 713, Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 591][/us/stat/88/591], set forth requirements for allotments and reallotments for States.

    Section 1944, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII][/us/pl/93/380/tVII], § 714, Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 592][/us/stat/88/592]; [Pub. L. 94–194][/us/pl/94/194], § 4, Dec. 31, 1975, [89 Stat. 1104][/us/stat/89/1104], set forth provisions respecting requirements for grants for State reading improvement programs.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 1530(a) of Pub. L. 95–561][/us/pl/95/561/s1530/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 1221e–3 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1221e–3].
