#### Part H—National Diffusion Network

 __Codification__ 

    Part L of title IX of [Pub. L. 103–227][/us/pl/103/227], which comprised this part, was designated part H of this subchapter for purposes of codification. Part L of title IX of [Pub. L. 103–227][/us/pl/103/227] was formerly part B of title XIII of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], which was classified to part B (§ 8651 et seq.) of subchapter XIII of chapter 70 of this title, prior to being redesignated as part L of title IX of [Pub. L. 103–227][/us/pl/103/227] by [Pub. L. 107–110, title X, § 1022(a)][/us/pl/107/110/s1022/a], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1987][/us/stat/115/1987], transferred to this part, and subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 107–279, title IV, § 403(2)][/us/pl/107/279/s403/2], Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1985][/us/stat/116/1985].
