## § 6031. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–279, title IV][/us/pl/107/279/tIV], § 403(2), Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1985][/us/stat/116/1985]

L. 103–227, title IX][/us/pl/103/227/tIX], § 931, Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 227][/us/stat/108/227], established the National Institute on Student Achievement, Curriculum, and Assessment, the National Institute on the Education of At-Risk Students, the National Institute on Educational Governance, Finance, Policy-Making, and Management, the National Institute on Early Childhood Development and Education, and the National Institute on Postsecondary Education, Libraries, and Lifelong Education.
