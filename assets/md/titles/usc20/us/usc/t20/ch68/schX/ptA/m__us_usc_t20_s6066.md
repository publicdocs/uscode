## § 6066. Public schools

    Except as provided in section 5890  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title, nothing in this chapter shall be construed to authorize the use of funds under subchapter III  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  of this chapter to directly or indirectly benefit any school other than a public school.

([Pub. L. 103–227, title X][/us/pl/103/227/tX], § 1020, Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 269][/us/stat/108/269].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 5890 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s5890], referred to in text, was repealed by [Pub. L. 106–113, div. B][/us/pl/106/113/dB], § 1000(a)(4) \[title III, § 310(i)\], Nov. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 1535][/us/stat/113/1535], 1501A–265.

    Subchapter III of this chapter, referred to in text, was in the original a reference to title III of [Pub. L. 103–227][/us/pl/103/227], Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 157][/us/stat/108/157], which was classified generally to subchapter III (§ 5881 et seq.) of this chapter and was repealed by [Pub. L. 106–113, div. B][/us/pl/106/113/dB], § 1000(a)(4) \[title III, § 310(i)\], Nov. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 1535][/us/stat/113/1535], 1501A–265.
