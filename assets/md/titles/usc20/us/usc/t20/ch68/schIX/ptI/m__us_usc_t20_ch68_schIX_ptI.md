#### Part I—Eisenhower Regional Mathematics and Science Education Consortia

 __Codification__ 

    Part M of title IX of [Pub. L. 103–227][/us/pl/103/227], which comprised this part, was designated part I of this subchapter for purposes of codification. Part M of title IX of [Pub. L. 103–227][/us/pl/103/227] was formerly part C of title XIII of [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], which was classified to part C (§ 8671 et seq.) of subchapter XIII of chapter 70 of this title, prior to being redesignated as part M of title IX of [Pub. L. 103–227][/us/pl/103/227] by [Pub. L. 107–110, title X][/us/pl/107/110/tX], § 1023(a), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1987][/us/stat/115/1987], transferred to this part, and subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 107–279, title IV][/us/pl/107/279/tIV], § 403(2), Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1985][/us/stat/116/1985].
