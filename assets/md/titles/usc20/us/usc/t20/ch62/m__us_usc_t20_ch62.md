## CHAPTER 62—EDUCATION AND TRAINING FOR AMERICAN COMPETITIVENESS

§§ 5001 to 5004. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)__  __][/us/pl/103/382/s391/i]__  __,__  __Oct. 20, 1994__  __,__  __[__  __108 Stat. 4023__  __][/us/stat/108/4023]__ 

    Section 5001, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6001][/us/pl/100/418/s6001], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1469][/us/stat/102/1469], provided that this chapter could be cited as the “Education and Training for a Competitive America Act of 1988”.

    Section 5002, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6002][/us/pl/100/418/s6002], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1469][/us/stat/102/1469], stated findings of Congress and purpose.

    Section 5003, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6003][/us/pl/100/418/s6003], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1470][/us/stat/102/1470], defined terms used in this chapter.

    Section 5004, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6004][/us/pl/100/418/s6004], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1470][/us/stat/102/1470], contained general restrictions relating to grants and contracts under this chapter.
