## §§ 5091 to 5097. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)][/us/pl/103/382/s391/i], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023][/us/stat/108/4023]

    Section 5091, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6101][/us/pl/100/418/s6101], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1500][/us/stat/102/1500], provided that this part could be cited as the “Training Technology Transfer Act of 1988”.

    Section 5092, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6102][/us/pl/100/418/s6102], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1500][/us/stat/102/1500]; [Pub. L. 101–600, § 6][/us/pl/101/600/s6], Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3046][/us/stat/104/3046], stated findings of Congress and purposes of this part.

    Section 5093, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6103][/us/pl/100/418/s6103], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1500][/us/stat/102/1500]; [Pub. L. 103–227, title II, § 236(a)(2)][/us/pl/103/227/s236/a/2], Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 156][/us/stat/108/156], established an Office of Training Technology Transfer.

    Section 5094, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6104][/us/pl/100/418/s6104], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1500][/us/stat/102/1500], related to functions of Office of Training Technology Transfer.

    Section 5095, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6105][/us/pl/100/418/s6105], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1503][/us/stat/102/1503], contained administrative provisions.

    Section 5096, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6106][/us/pl/100/418/s6106], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1503][/us/stat/102/1503], related to coordination with Federal agencies.

    Section 5097, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6107][/us/pl/100/418/s6107], Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1504][/us/stat/102/1504], defined terms used in this part.
