## §§ 5001 to 5004. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III][/us/pl/103/382/tIII], § 391(i), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023][/us/stat/108/4023]

    Section 5001, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6001, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1469][/us/stat/102/1469], provided that this chapter could be cited as the “Education and Training for a Competitive America Act of 1988”.

    Section 5002, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6002, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1469][/us/stat/102/1469], stated findings of Congress and purpose.

    Section 5003, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6003, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1470][/us/stat/102/1470], defined terms used in this chapter.

    Section 5004, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6004, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1470][/us/stat/102/1470], contained general restrictions relating to grants and contracts under this chapter.
