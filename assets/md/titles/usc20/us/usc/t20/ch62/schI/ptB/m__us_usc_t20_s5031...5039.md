## §§ 5031 to 5039. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III][/us/pl/103/382/tIII], § 391(i), (j), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023][/us/stat/108/4023]

    Section 5031, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6041, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1483][/us/stat/102/1483], provided that this part could be cited as the “Educational Partnerships Act of 1988”.

    Section 5032, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6042, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1483][/us/stat/102/1483], stated purpose of this part.

    Section 5033, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6043, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1483][/us/stat/102/1483], authorized grants to eligible partnerships to use public and private resources for various educational purposes.

    Section 5034, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6044, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1483][/us/stat/102/1483], related to authorized activities by eligible partnerships receiving payments.

    Section 5035, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6045, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1484][/us/stat/102/1484], related to applications for grants.

    Section 5036, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6046, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1485][/us/stat/102/1485], related to approval of applications.

    Section 5037, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6047, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1485][/us/stat/102/1485], related to computation of grant amounts.

    Section 5038, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6048, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1485][/us/stat/102/1485], related to evaluation by Secretary of grants and dissemination of information relating to assisted activities.

    Section 5039, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI][/us/pl/100/418/tVI], § 6049, Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1486][/us/stat/102/1486], defined terms used in this part.
