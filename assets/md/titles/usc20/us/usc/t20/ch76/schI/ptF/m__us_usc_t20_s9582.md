## § 9582. Copyright

    Nothing in this Act shall be construed to affect the rights, remedies, limitations, or defense under title 17.

([Pub. L. 107–279, title I][/us/pl/107/279/tI], § 192, Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1974][/us/stat/116/1974].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This Act, referred to in text, means [Pub.
