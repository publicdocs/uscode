## §§ 681 to 685. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–371, title II, § 210(d)][/us/pl/99/371/s210/d], formerly title IV, § 410(d), Aug. 4, 1986, [100 Stat. 794][/us/stat/100/794]; renumbered title II, § 210(d), [Pub. L. 102–421, title I, § 101(b)(5)][/us/pl/102/421/s101/b/5], (6), Oct. 16, 1992, [106 Stat. 2151][/us/stat/106/2151]

    Section 681, [Pub. L. 89–36, § 2][/us/pl/89/36/s2], June 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 125][/us/stat/79/125], stated purpose of and authorized appropriations for National Technical Institute for the Deaf. See [section 4331 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4331].

    Section 682, [Pub. L. 89–36, § 3][/us/pl/89/36/s3], June 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 125][/us/stat/79/125]; [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 301(a)(1)][/us/pl/96/88/s301/a/1], (2)(M), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677][/us/stat/93/677], 678, 692, defined “Secretary”, “institution of higher education”, and “construction”. See [section 4351 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4351].

    Section 683, [Pub. L. 89–36, § 4][/us/pl/89/36/s4], June 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 125][/us/stat/79/125], related to proposals for establishing and operating a National Technical Institute for the Deaf.

    Section 684, [Pub. L. 89–36, § 5][/us/pl/89/36/s5], June 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 126][/us/stat/79/126], related to entering into an agreement for establishment and operation of a National Technical Institute for the Deaf. See [section 4332 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s4332].

    Section 685, [Pub. L. 89–36, § 6][/us/pl/89/36/s6], June 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 127][/us/stat/79/127], related to a National Advisory Board for the establishment of a national technical institute for the deaf.

L. 99–371, title II, § 210(d)][/us/pl/99/371/s210/d], formerly title IV, § 410(d), Aug. 4, 1986, [100 Stat. 794][/us/stat/100/794]; renumbered title II, § 210(d), [Pub. L. 102–421, title I, § 101(b)(5)][/us/pl/102/421/s101/b/5], (6), Oct. 16, 1992, [106 Stat. 2151][/us/stat/106/2151].
