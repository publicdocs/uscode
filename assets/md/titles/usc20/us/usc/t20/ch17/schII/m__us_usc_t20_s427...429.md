## §§ 427 to 429. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    This subchapter has not been funded since fiscal year 1975.

    Section 427, [Pub. L. 85–864, title II][/us/pl/85/864/tII], § 207, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1587][/us/stat/72/1587]; [Pub. L. 96–88, title III][/us/pl/96/88/tIII], § 301(a)(1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677][/us/stat/93/677], 692, related to loans to help finance an institution’s capital contribution to a student loan fund.

    Section 428, [Pub. L. 85–864, title II][/us/pl/85/864/tII], § 208, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1587][/us/stat/72/1587]; [Pub. L. 96–88, title III][/us/pl/96/88/tIII], § 301(a)(1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677][/us/stat/93/677], 692, related to payments to cover reductions in amounts of student loans.

    Section 429, [Pub. L. 85–864, title II][/us/pl/85/864/tII], § 209, Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1587][/us/stat/72/1587]; [Pub. L. 96–88, title III][/us/pl/96/88/tIII], § 301(a)(1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677][/us/stat/93/677], 692, contained administrative provisions relating to modification of agreements and loans, compromise, waiver, or release of rights, and finality and conclusiveness of financial transactions and vouchers of the Secretary.
