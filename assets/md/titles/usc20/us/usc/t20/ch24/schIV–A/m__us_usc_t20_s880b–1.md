## § 880b–1. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 880b–1, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 703, as added and amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 105(a)(1), (b), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 504][/us/stat/88/504], 512, which made general provisions for the bilingual education program, was omitted in the general revision of title VII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 105(a)(1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 511][/us/stat/88/511], by [Pub. L. 95–561, title VII][/us/pl/95/561/tVII], § 701, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2268][/us/stat/92/2268].

    A prior section 880b–1, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 703, as added [Pub. L. 90–247, title VII][/us/pl/90/247/tVII], § 702, Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 816][/us/stat/81/816]; amended [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 151, Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 151][/us/stat/84/151], authorized appropriation of 15, 30, 40, 80, 100, and 135 million dollars for fiscal years ending June 30, 1968 through 1973, respectively, and for distribution of funds.
