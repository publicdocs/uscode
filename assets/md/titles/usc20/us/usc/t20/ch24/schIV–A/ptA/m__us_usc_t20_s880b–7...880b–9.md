## §§ 880b–7 to 880b–9. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general revision of title VII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 105(a)(1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 511][/us/stat/88/511], by [Pub. L. 95–561, title VII][/us/pl/95/561/tVII], § 701, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2268][/us/stat/92/2268].

    Section 880b–7, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 721, as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 105(a)(1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 506][/us/stat/88/506], provided for bilingual education programs.

    Section 880b–8, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 722, as added [Pub. L. 93–380, Title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 105(a)(1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 507][/us/stat/88/507], related to Indian children in schools.

    Section 880b–9, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 723, as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 105(a)(1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 508][/us/stat/88/508]; amended [Pub. L. 94–273][/us/pl/94/273], § 3(7), Apr. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 376][/us/stat/90/376]; S. Res. 4, Feb. 4, 1977, related to grants and contract for training.
