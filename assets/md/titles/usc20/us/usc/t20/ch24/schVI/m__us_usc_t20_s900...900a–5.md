## §§ 900 to 900a–5. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general revision of title IX of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V][/us/pl/92/318/tV], § 504(a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347][/us/stat/86/347], by [Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII][/us/pl/95/561/tVIII], § 802, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2292][/us/stat/92/2292].

    Section 900, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], § 901, as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V][/us/pl/92/318/tV], § 504(a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 346][/us/stat/86/346], contained the Congressional statement of policy with regard to the ethnic heritage program.

    Section 900a, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], § 902, as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V][/us/pl/92/318/tV], § 504(a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347][/us/stat/86/347], authorized grants for the ethnic heritage studies program.

    Section 900a–1, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], § 903, as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V][/us/pl/92/318/tV], § 504(a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347][/us/stat/86/347]; amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 111(b), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 513][/us/stat/88/513], described the authorized activities for the ethnic heritage program.

    Section 900a–2, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], § 904, as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V][/us/pl/92/318/tV], § 504(a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347][/us/stat/86/347], related to the form and content of applications for assistance.

    Section 900a–3, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], § 905, as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V][/us/pl/92/318/tV], § 504(a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347][/us/stat/86/347], related to the administration of the program.

    Section 900a–4, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], § 906, as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V][/us/pl/92/318/tV], § 504(a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 348][/us/stat/86/348], established the National Advisory Council on Ethnic Heritage Studies.

    Section 900a–5, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX][/us/pl/89/10/tIX], § 907, as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V][/us/pl/92/318/tV], § 504(a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 348][/us/stat/86/348]; amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 111(a)(1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 513][/us/stat/88/513], authorized appropriations for fiscal years through fiscal year 1978.
