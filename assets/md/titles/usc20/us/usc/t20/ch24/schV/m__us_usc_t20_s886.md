## § 886. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV][/us/pl/91/230/tIV], § 401(d)(4), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173][/us/stat/84/173]

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII][/us/pl/89/10/tVIII], § 806, formerly title VII, § 706, as added [Pub. L. 89–750, title I][/us/pl/89/750/tI], § 171, Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1209][/us/stat/80/1209]; renumbered § 806 and amended [Pub. L. 90–247, title I][/us/pl/90/247/tI], § 171, title VII, § 702, Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 805][/us/stat/81/805], 816, provided for dissemination of information and authorization of appropriations of $1,500,000; $3,500,000; $3,700,000; and $4,000,000 for fiscal years ending June 30, 1967, 1968, 1969, and 1970.

 __Termination of Section__ 

    [Pub. L. 90–576, title III][/us/pl/90/576/tIII], § 303(e), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1096][/us/stat/82/1096], which provided that this section shall become ineffective the first fiscal year for which funds are appropriated to carry out the provisions of [section 6 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s6], was repealed by [section 401(d)(3) of Pub. L. 91–230][/us/pl/91/230/s401/d/3].
