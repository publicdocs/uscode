## §§ 880b–10, 880b–11. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general revision of title VII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10], as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 105(a)(1)][/us/pl/93/380/s105/a/1], Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 511][/us/stat/88/511], by [Pub. L. 95–561, title VII, § 701][/us/pl/95/561/s701], Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2268][/us/stat/92/2268].

    Section 880b–10, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 731][/us/pl/89/10/s731], as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 105(a)(1)][/us/pl/93/380/s105/a/1], Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 509][/us/stat/88/509]; amended [Pub. L. 94–273, § 3(7)][/us/pl/94/273/s3/7], Apr. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 376][/us/stat/90/376]; [Pub. L. 94–482, title V, § 501(d)(1)][/us/pl/94/482/s501/d/1], Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2237][/us/stat/90/2237], established the Office of Bilingual Education.

    Section 880b–11, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 732][/us/pl/89/10/s732], as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 105(a)(1)][/us/pl/93/380/s105/a/1], Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 510][/us/stat/88/510]; amended [Pub. L. 94–482, title V, § 501(a)(21)][/us/pl/94/482/s501/a/21], (d)(2), Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2236][/us/stat/90/2236], 2237, provided for the establishment of the National Advisory Council on Bilingual Education.

 __Appointment of National Advisory Council on Bilingual Education__ 

    [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 105(a)(2)(B)][/us/pl/93/380/s105/a/2/B], Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 512][/us/stat/88/512], provided for the appointment of the National Advisory Council on Bilingual Education, which Council was provided for by section 880b–11, within 90 days after Aug. 21, 1974. See Codification note above.
