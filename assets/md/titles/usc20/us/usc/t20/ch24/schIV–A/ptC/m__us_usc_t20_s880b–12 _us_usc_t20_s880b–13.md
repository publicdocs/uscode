###### §§ 880b–12, 880b–13. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general revision of title VII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 105(a)(1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 511][/us/stat/88/511], by [Pub. L. 95–561, title VII][/us/pl/95/561/tVII], § 701, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2268][/us/stat/92/2268].

    Section 880b–12, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 741, as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 105(a)(1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 511][/us/stat/88/511], provided for the administration of the research and demonstration projects program.

    Section 880b–13, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII][/us/pl/89/10/tVII], § 742, as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 105(a)(1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 511][/us/stat/88/511]; amended [Pub. L. 94–482, title V][/us/pl/94/482/tV], § 501(d)(3), Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2237][/us/stat/90/2237], related to research and demonstration projects.
