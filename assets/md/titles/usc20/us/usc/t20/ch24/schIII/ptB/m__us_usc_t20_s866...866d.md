## §§ 866 to 866d. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general revision of title V of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V][/us/pl/89/10/tV], Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 47][/us/stat/79/47], by [Pub. L. 95–561, title V][/us/pl/95/561/tV], § 501, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2240][/us/stat/92/2240].

    Section 866, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V][/us/pl/89/10/tV], § 521, as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 143(a)(4)(D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 142][/us/stat/84/142]; amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I][/us/pl/93/380/tI], § 104(b), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 503][/us/stat/88/503]; [Pub. L. 95–112][/us/pl/95/112], § 2(e)(2), Sept. 24, 1977, [91 Stat. 911][/us/stat/91/911], authorized appropriations for grants to local educational agencies for fiscal years ending prior to Oct. 1, 1979.

A prior section 866, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V][/us/pl/89/10/tV], § 506, Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 51][/us/stat/79/51], provided for payments to States: installments; advances or reimbursement; and adjustments, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 143(a)(4)(A), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 142][/us/stat/84/142].

    Section 866a, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V][/us/pl/89/10/tV], § 522, as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 143(a)(4)(D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 143][/us/stat/84/143]; amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title VIII][/us/pl/93/380/tVIII], § 843(a)(2), (3), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 611][/us/stat/88/611], related to the apportionment of grant funds among States.

    Section 866b, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V][/us/pl/89/10/tV], § 523, as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 143(a)(4)(D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 143][/us/stat/84/143], related to grants from apportioned funds and to the programs and activities covered.

    Section 866c, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V][/us/pl/89/10/tV], § 524, as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 143(a)(4)(D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 144][/us/stat/84/144], related to the approval of applications by the Commissioner.

    Section 866d, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V][/us/pl/89/10/tV], § 525, as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I][/us/pl/91/230/tI], § 143(a)(4)(D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 144][/us/stat/84/144], related to the approval of applications by State educational agencies.
