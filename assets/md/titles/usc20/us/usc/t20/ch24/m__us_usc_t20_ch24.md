## CHAPTER 24—GRANTS FOR EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS, FACILITIES AND SERVICES, AND STRENGTHENING OF EDUCATIONAL AGENCIES

 __Codification__ 

    Titles I to IX of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10][/us/pl/89/10], Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 27][/us/stat/79/27], as amended, which were classified to this chapter (§ 821 et seq.), subchapter II (§ 241a et seq.) of chapter 13, and subchapter I (§ 1801 et seq.) of chapter 40 of this title, were amended generally by [Pub. L. 95–561][/us/pl/95/561], Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2152][/us/stat/92/2152], known as the Education Amendments of 1978, and the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 was transferred to chapter 47 (§ 2701 et seq.) of this title. Subsequently, that act was amended generally by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101][/us/pl/103/382/s101], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519][/us/stat/108/3519], and transferred to chapter 70 (§ 6301 et seq.) of this title.
