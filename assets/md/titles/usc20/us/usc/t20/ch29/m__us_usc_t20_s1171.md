## § 1171. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–698, § 2][/us/pl/89/698/s2], Oct. 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 1066][/us/stat/80/1066], which set out the Congressional findings and declaration of purpose in providing for a program of international studies and research, was omitted in view of the repeal of the remaining sections of this chapter by [Pub. L. 96–374, title VI, § 601(c)(2)][/us/pl/96/374/s601/c/2], Oct. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 1472][/us/stat/94/1472].

§§ 1172 to 1174. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 96–374, title VI, § 601(c)(2)__  __][/us/pl/96/374/s601/c/2]__  __,__  __Oct. 3, 1980__  __,__  __[__  __94 Stat. 1472__  __][/us/stat/94/1472]__ 

    Section 1172, [Pub. L. 89–698, title I, § 101][/us/pl/89/698/s101], Oct. 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 1066][/us/stat/80/1066], made provision for a program of international studies and research involving operation of centers for advanced international studies. See [section 1123 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1123].

    Section 1173, [Pub. L. 89–698, title I, § 102][/us/pl/89/698/s102], Oct. 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 1067][/us/stat/80/1067], provided for undergraduate programs in international studies. See [section 1124 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1124].

    Section 1174, [Pub. L. 89–698, title I, § 103][/us/pl/89/698/s103], Oct. 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 1068][/us/stat/80/1068]; [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV, § 401(c)(7)][/us/pl/91/230/s401/c/7], Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173][/us/stat/84/173], authorized payment in installments and in advance or by way of reimbursement.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Sections repealed effective Oct. 1, 1980, see [section 1393(a) of Pub. L. 96–374][/us/pl/96/374/s1393/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 1001 of this title][/us/usc/t20/s1001].
