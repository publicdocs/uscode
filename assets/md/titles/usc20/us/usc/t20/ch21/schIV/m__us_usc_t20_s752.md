## § 752. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV][/us/pl/91/230/tIV], § 401(c)(6), (h)(2), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173][/us/stat/84/173], 174

    Section, [Pub. L. 88–204, title IV][/us/pl/88/204/tIV], § 402, Dec. 16, 1963, [77 Stat. 377][/us/stat/77/377], related to Federal administration as to higher education facilities, providing in subsecs.
