## CHAPTER 21—HIGHER EDUCATION FACILITIES

 __Continuation of Programs__ 

[Pub. L. 92–318, § 161(b)(1)][/us/pl/92/318/s161/b/1], June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 303][/us/stat/86/303], provided that: 

> “The programs authorized by title VII of the Higher Education Act of 1965 \[title VII of [Pub. L. 89–329][/us/pl/89/329], which was classified to section 1132a et seq. of this title, prior to being amended generally by [Pub. L. 105–244][/us/pl/105/244]\] shall be deemed to be a continuation of the comparable programs authorized by the Higher Education Facilities Act of 1963 \[this chapter\].”
