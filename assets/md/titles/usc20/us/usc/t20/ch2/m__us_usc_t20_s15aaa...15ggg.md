## §§ 15aaa to 15ggg. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–576, title I][/us/pl/90/576/tI], § 103, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091][/us/stat/82/1091]

    Section 15aaa, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII], § 301, as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII][/us/pl/85/864/tVIII], § 802, [72 Stat. 1597][/us/stat/72/1597]; amended Oct. 3, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–344, title II][/us/pl/87/344/tII], § 207, [75 Stat. 760][/us/stat/75/760]; Dec. 18, 1963, [Pub. L. 88–210][/us/pl/88/210], § 11(b), [77 Stat. 411][/us/stat/77/411], authorized an appropriation of $15,000,000 annually for area vocational education programs.

    Section 15bbb, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII], § 302, as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII][/us/pl/85/864/tVIII], § 802, [72 Stat. 1598][/us/stat/72/1598], covered allotment of funds appropriated under [section 15aaa of this title][/us/usc/t20/s15aaa].

    Section 15ccc, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII], § 303, as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII][/us/pl/85/864/tVIII], § 802, [72 Stat. 1598][/us/stat/72/1598], set out conditions which States had to fulfill to qualify for payments.

    Section 15ddd, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII], § 304, as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII][/us/pl/85/864/tVIII], § 802, [72 Stat. 1599][/us/stat/72/1599], set out requirements of programs under sections 15aaa to 15ggg of this title.

    Section 15eee, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII], § 305, as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII][/us/pl/85/864/tVIII], § 802, [72 Stat. 1599][/us/stat/72/1599], covered additional State plan requirements for eligibility under sections 15aaa to 15ggg of this title.

    Section 15fff, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII], § 306, as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII][/us/pl/85/864/tVIII], § 802, [72 Stat. 1600][/us/stat/72/1600], authorized appropriations to administer sections 15aaa to 15ggg of this title.

    Section 15ggg, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII], § 307, as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII][/us/pl/85/864/tVIII], § 802, [72 Stat. 1600][/us/stat/72/1600]; amended June 25, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–70][/us/pl/86/70], § 18(b)(3), [73 Stat. 144][/us/stat/73/144]; July 12, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–624][/us/pl/86/624], § 14(b)(4), [74 Stat. 414][/us/stat/74/414], defined terms as used in sections 15aaa to 15ggg of this title.

    For general subject matter of sections 15aaa to 15ggg, see section 1241 et seq. of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 90–576, title I][/us/pl/90/576/tI], § 103, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091][/us/stat/82/1091], provided that the repeal is effective July 1, 1969.
