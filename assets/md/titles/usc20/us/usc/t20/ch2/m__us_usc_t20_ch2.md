 __Short Title__ 

    [Act June 8, 1936, ch. 541, § 2][/us/act/1936-06-08/ch541/s2], [49 Stat. 1488][/us/stat/49/1488], as amended by [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725], title I, § 101, [60 Stat. 775][/us/stat/60/775], provided that the [act of Feb. 23, 1917, ch. 114][/us/act/1917-02-23/ch114], [39 Stat. 929][/us/stat/39/929] (enacting sections 11 to 15 and 16 to 28 of this title), was to be known as the Smith-Hughes Vocational Education Act.  __Codification__ 

    Section 15 was comprised of provisions of [act Feb. 23, 1917, ch. 114, § 7][/us/act/1917-02-23/ch114/s7], [39 Stat. 933][/us/stat/39/933]; Ex. Ord. No. 6166, § 15, June 10, 1933; [June 26, 1934, ch. 756, § 2][/us/act/1934-06-26/ch756/s2], [48 Stat. 1225][/us/stat/48/1225]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 204, eff.     Sections 15a to 15c, [act Feb. 5, 1929, ch. 153][/us/act/1929-02-05/ch153], §§ 1–3, [45 Stat. 1151][/us/stat/45/1151], appropriated money to be used for further development of vocational education in States and Territories but appropriations were authorized for only four years after the fiscal year ending June 30, 1930.

    Sections 15d to 15g, [act May 21, 1934, ch. 324][/us/act/1934-05-21/ch324], §§ 1–4, [48 Stat. 792][/us/stat/48/792], provided for further development of vocational education in several States and Territories by authorizing additional appropriations for the fiscal years 1935–37.

    [Act June 8, 1936, ch. 541, § 7][/us/act/1936-06-08/ch541/s7], [49 Stat. 1490][/us/stat/49/1490], incorporated in section 15o of this title, provided that appropriations authorized by act June 8, 1936, incorporated in sections 15h to 15p of this title, “shall be in lieu thereof and not in addition to the appropriations authorized in” sections 1 and 2 of act May 21, 1934.
