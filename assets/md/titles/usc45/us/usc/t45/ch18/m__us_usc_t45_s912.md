## § 912. Election

    Any employee who receives any assistance under section 909 or [section 911 of this title][/us/usc/t45/s911] or under an employee protection agreement entered into under [section 908 of this title][/us/usc/t45/s908] shall be deemed to waive any employee protection benefits otherwise available to such employee under the Bankruptcy Act, subtitle IV of title 49, or any applicable contract or agreement.

([Pub. L. 96–101, § 13][/us/pl/96/101/s13], Nov. 4, 1979, [93 Stat. 743][/us/stat/93/743].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 909 of this title][/us/usc/t45/s909], referred to in text, was in the original “section 10”, meaning [section 10 of Pub. L. 96–101][/us/pl/96/101/s10], Nov. 4, 1979, [93 Stat. 741][/us/stat/93/741]. Subsecs. (a) to (g) of section 10 are classified to [section 909 of this title][/us/usc/t45/s909]. Subsec. (h) of section 10 amended [section 231f(b)(7) of this title][/us/usc/t45/s231f/b/7].

    The Bankruptcy Act, referred to in text, is [act July 1, 1898, ch. 541][/us/act/1898-07-01/ch541], [30 Stat. 544][/us/stat/30/544], as amended, which was classified generally to former Title 11, Bankruptcy. The Act was repealed effective Oct. 1, 1979, by [Pub. L. 95–598][/us/pl/95/598], §§ 401(a), 402(a), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2682][/us/stat/92/2682], section 101 of which enacted revised Title 11. For current provisions relating to railroad reorganization, see subchapter IV (§ 1161 et seq.) of chapter 11 of Title 11.
