## § 1116. Applicability of other laws

    (a) The provisions of chapters 5 and 7 of title 5 (popularly known as the Administrative Procedure Act and including provisions popularly known as the Government in the Sunshine Act), the Federal Advisory Committee Act, section 102(2)(C) of the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 \[[42 U.S.C. 4332(2)(C)][/us/usc/t42/s4332/2/C]\], the National Historic Preservation Act of 1966 \[[16 U.S.C. 470][/us/usc/t16/s470] et seq.\], and [section 303 of title 49][/us/usc/t49/s303] are inapplicable to actions taken in negotiating, approving, or implementing service transfers under title IV of the Regional Rail Reorganization Act of 1973 \[[45 U.S.C. 761][/us/usc/t45/s761] et seq.\]  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  and to the implementation of the sale of the interest of the United States in Conrail under the Conrail Privatization Act \[[45 U.S.C. 1301][/us/usc/t45/s1301] et seq.\].

    (b) The operation of trains by Conrail shall not be subject to the requirement of any State or local law which specifies the minimum number of crew members who must be employed in connection with the operation of such trains.

([Pub. L. 97–35, title XI, § 1168][/us/pl/97/35/s1168], Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 687][/us/stat/95/687]; [Pub. L. 99–509, title IV, § 4033(c)(1)(B)][/us/pl/99/509/s4033/c/1/B], Oct. 21, 1986, [100 Stat. 1908][/us/stat/100/1908].)
