## § 1344. Status of Conrail after sale

    The Corporation shall be a rail carrier as defined in [section 10102 of title 49][/us/usc/t49/s10102], notwithstanding this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 99–509, title IV, § 4036][/us/pl/99/509/s4036], Oct. 21, 1986, [100 Stat. 1909][/us/stat/100/1909]; [Pub. L. 104–88, title III, § 332][/us/pl/104/88/s332], Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 953][/us/stat/109/953].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1995—[Pub. L. 104–88][/us/pl/104/88] substituted “section 10102” for “section 10102(19)”.

 __Effective Date of 1995 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 104–88][/us/pl/104/88] effective Jan. 1, 1996, see [section 2 of Pub. L. 104–88][/us/pl/104/88/s2], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 1301 of Title 49][/us/usc/t49/s1301], Transportation.
