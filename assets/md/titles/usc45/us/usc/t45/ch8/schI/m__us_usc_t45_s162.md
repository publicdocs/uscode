## § 162. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for expenditure by the Mediation Board in carrying out the provisions of this chapter.

([May 20, 1926, ch. 347, § 12][/us/act/1926-05-20/ch347/s12], [44 Stat. 587][/us/stat/44/587]; [June 21, 1934, ch. 691, § 7][/us/act/1934-06-21/ch691/s7], [48 Stat. 1197][/us/stat/48/1197].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1934—Act June 21, 1934, substituted “Mediation Board” for “Board of Mediation”.
