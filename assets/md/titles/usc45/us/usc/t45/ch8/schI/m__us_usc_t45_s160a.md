## § 160a. Rules and regulations

    (a) __In general__ 

        The Mediation Board shall have the authority from time to time to make, amend, and rescind, in the manner prescribed by [section 553 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s553], and after opportunity for a public hearing, such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

    (b) __Application__ 

        The requirements of subsection (a) shall not apply to any rule or proposed rule to which the third sentence of [section 553(b) of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s553/b] applies.

([May 20, 1926, ch. 347, § 10A][/us/act/1926-05-20/ch347/s10A], as added [Pub. L. 112–95, title X, § 1001][/us/pl/112/95/s1001], Feb. 14, 2012, [126 Stat. 146][/us/stat/126/146].)
