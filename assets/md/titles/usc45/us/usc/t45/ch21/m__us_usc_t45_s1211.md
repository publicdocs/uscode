## § 1211. Denali National Park and Preserve lands

    On the date of transfer to the State (pursuant to [section 1203 of this title][/us/usc/t45/s1203]) or other disposition (pursuant to [section 1210 of this title][/us/usc/t45/s1210]), that portion of rail properties of the Alaska Railroad within the Denali National Park and Preserve shall, subject to the exclusive-use easement granted pursuant to [section 1203(b)(1)(D) of this title][/us/usc/t45/s1203/b/1/D], be transferred to the Secretary of the Interior for administration as part of the Denali National Park and Preserve, except that a transferee under [section 1210 of this title][/us/usc/t45/s1210] shall receive the same interest as the State under [section 1203(b)(1)(D) of this title][/us/usc/t45/s1203/b/1/D].

([Pub. L. 97–468, title VI, § 612][/us/pl/97/468/s612], Jan. 14, 1983, [96 Stat. 2576][/us/stat/96/2576].)
