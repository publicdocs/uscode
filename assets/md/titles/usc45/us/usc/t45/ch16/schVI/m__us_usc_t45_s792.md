## § 792. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–375, title I, § 111(b)][/us/pl/97/375/s111/b], Dec. 21, 1982, [96 Stat. 1821][/us/stat/96/1821]

    Section, [Pub. L. 93–236, title VI, § 602][/us/pl/93/236/s602], Jan. 2, 1974, [87 Stat. 1022][/us/stat/87/1022], provided that as part of his annual report, the Secretary transmit to Congress a comprehensive report on the effectiveness of the Association and the Corporation in implementing the purposes of this chapter, together with any recommendations for additional legislative or other action.

Subsequent to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–375][/us/pl/97/375], section was repealed and the provisions thereof were reenacted as the last sentence of [section 308(a) of Title 49][/us/usc/t49/s308/a], Transportation, by [Pub. L. 97–449][/us/pl/97/449], §§ 1(b), 7(b), Jan. 12, 1983, [96 Stat. 2413][/us/stat/96/2413], 2443. [Section 6(a) of Pub. L. 97–449][/us/pl/97/449/s6/a] provided that: 

> “Sections 1–5 of this act restate, without substantive change, laws enacted before November 15, 1982, that were replaced by those sections. \* \* \* Laws enacted after November 14, 1982, that are inconsistent with this Act supersede this Act to the extent of the inconsistency.”

 Accordingly, the last sentence of [section 308(a) of Title 49][/us/usc/t49/s308/a] was superseded by the repeal made by [section 111(b) of Pub. L. 97–375][/us/pl/97/375/s111/b], and was specifically repealed by [Pub. L. 98–216, § 2(1)(A)(ii)][/us/pl/98/216/s2/1/A/ii], Feb. 14, 1984, [98 Stat. 4][/us/stat/98/4].

    [Pub. L. 97–375, title I, § 111(b)][/us/pl/97/375/s111/b], Dec. 21, 1982, [96 Stat. 1821][/us/stat/96/1821], which repealed this section, was itself repealed by [Pub. L. 98–216, § 6(b)][/us/pl/98/216/s6/b], Feb. 14, 1984, [98 Stat. 8][/us/stat/98/8].
