## § 793. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–473, § 4(b)][/us/pl/95/473/s4/b], Oct. 17, 1978, [92 Stat. 1466][/us/stat/92/1466]; [Pub. L. 97–449, § 4(b)(2)][/us/pl/97/449/s4/b/2], Jan. 12, 1983, [96 Stat. 2441][/us/stat/96/2441], eff. Oct. 17, 1978

    Section, [Pub. L. 93–236, title VI, § 603][/us/pl/93/236/s603], Jan. 2, 1974, [87 Stat. 1023][/us/stat/87/1023], related to freight rates for recyclables.

 __Investigation of Discriminatory Freight Rates for Transportation of Recyclable or Recycled Materials__ 

    [Pub. L. 94–210, title II, § 204][/us/pl/94/210/s204], Feb. 5, 1976, [90 Stat. 40][/us/stat/90/40], which provided for an investigation by the Interstate Commerce Commission of discriminatory freight rates for transportation of recyclable or recycled materials, was repealed by [Pub. L. 95–473, § 4(b)][/us/pl/95/473/s4/b], Oct. 17, 1978, [92 Stat. 1466][/us/stat/92/1466].
