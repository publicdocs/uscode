## § 470. Transferred or Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–665, § 1][/us/pl/89/665/s1], Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 915][/us/stat/80/915]; [Pub. L. 96–515, title I, § 101(a)][/us/pl/96/515/s101/a], Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2987][/us/stat/94/2987], related to short title, findings, and policy of National Historic Preservation Act. Subsec. (a), which provided short title for subchapter, was transferred and is set out as a note under [section 100101 of Title 54][/us/usc/t54/s100101], National Park Service and Related Programs. Subsec. (b), which related to findings, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 54.
