## § 469k. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–333, div. I, title VI][/us/pl/104/333/dI/tVI], § 604(e)(1), Nov. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 4173][/us/stat/110/4173]; [Pub. L. 107–359][/us/pl/107/359], § 3(4)(A), Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 3016][/us/stat/116/3016]; [Pub. L. 113–287][/us/pl/113/287], § 7, Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272][/us/stat/128/3272]

    Section, [Pub. L. 104–333, div. I, title VI][/us/pl/104/333/dI/tVI], § 604, Nov. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 4173][/us/stat/110/4173]; [Pub. L. 107–359][/us/pl/107/359], § 3, Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 3016][/us/stat/116/3016], known as the American Battlefield Protection Act of 1996, established the American Battlefield Protection Program.

 __Short Title of 2002 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 107–359][/us/pl/107/359], § 1, Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 3016][/us/stat/116/3016], provided that: 

> “This Act \[amending this section and enacting provisions set out as a note under this section\], may be cited as the ‘Civil War Battlefield Preservation Act of 2002’.”
