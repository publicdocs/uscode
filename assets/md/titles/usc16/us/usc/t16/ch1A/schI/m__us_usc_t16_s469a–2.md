## § 469a–2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287][/us/pl/113/287], § 7, Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272][/us/stat/128/3272]

L. 93–291][/us/pl/93/291], § 1(3), May 24, 1974, [88 Stat. 174][/us/stat/88/174], related to survey by Secretary of the Interior for preservation of data.
