## § 469m. Repealed or Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 111–11, title VII][/us/pl/111/11/tVII], § 7111, Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1199][/us/stat/123/1199], which related to Women’s Rights National Historical Park, was repealed in part and omitted in part. Subsec. (a), which enacted section 410ll–1 of this title, was omitted from the Code. Subsecs. (b) and (c) were repealed and restated in sections 308501 and 308502 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs, by [Pub. L. 113–287][/us/pl/113/287], §§ 3, 7, Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3094][/us/stat/128/3094], 3272, which act enacted Title 54.
