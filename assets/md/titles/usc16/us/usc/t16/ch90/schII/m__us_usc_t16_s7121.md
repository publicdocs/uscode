## § 7121. Definitions

    In this subchapter:

    (1) __Participating county__ 

        The term “participating county” means an eligible county that elects under [section 7112(d) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s7112/d] to expend a portion of the Federal funds received under [section 7112 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s7112] in accordance with this subchapter.

    (2) __Project funds__ 

        The term “project funds” means all funds an eligible county elects under [section 7112(d) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s7112/d] to reserve for expenditure in accordance with this subchapter.

    (3) __Resource advisory committee__ 

    The term “resource advisory committee” means—

        (A) an advisory committee established by the Secretary concerned under [section 7125 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s7125]; or

        (B) an advisory committee determined by the Secretary concerned to meet the requirements of [section 7125 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s7125].

    (4) __Resource management plan__ 

    The term “resource management plan” means—

        (A) a land use plan prepared by the Bureau of Land Management for units of the Federal land described in [section 7102(7)(B) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s7102/7/B] pursuant to [section 1712 of title 43][/us/usc/t43/s1712]; or

        (B) a land and resource management plan prepared by the Forest Service for units of the National Forest System pursuant to [section 1604 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1604].

([Pub. L. 106–393, title II][/us/pl/106/393/tII], § 201, as added [Pub. L. 110–343, div. C, title VI][/us/pl/110/343/dC/tVI], § 601(a), Oct. 3, 2008, [122 Stat. 3900][/us/stat/122/3900].)
