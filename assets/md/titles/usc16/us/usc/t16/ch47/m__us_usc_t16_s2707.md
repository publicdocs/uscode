## § 2707. Authorizations

    There are hereby authorized to be appropriated for each of the fiscal years ending September 30, 1978, September 30, 1979, and September 30, 1980, not to exceed $10,000,000 for loans to be made pursuant to [section 2702 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s2702], such funds to remain available until expended. There are hereby authorized to be appropriated for each of the fiscal years ending September 30, 1978, September 30, 1979, September 30, 1980, not to exceed $100,000,000 for loans to be made pursuant to [section 2703 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s2703], such funds to remain available until expended.

([Pub. L. 95–617, title IV][/us/pl/95/617/tIV], § 407, Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3156][/us/stat/92/3156].)
