## § 482q. Perfection of mining claims within forest

    Valid mining claims within the said lands, existing on July 12, 1951, and thereafter maintained in compliance with the law under which they were initiated and the laws of the State of Arizona, may be perfected under sections 482o to 482q of this title, or under the laws under which they were initiated, as the claimant may desire.

([July 12, 1951, ch. 222, § 3][/us/act/1951-07-12/ch222/s3], [65 Stat. 119][/us/stat/65/119].)

    Sections, [act Sept. 22, 1922, ch. 404][/us/act/1922-09-22/ch404], §§ 1, 2, [42 Stat. 1017][/us/stat/42/1017], related to exchange of lands in national forests with persons who have relinquished lands as basis for lieu selection, procedure, relinquishment of original lands to such persons, and selection of other lands in lieu of lands relinquished.
