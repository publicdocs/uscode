## § 513. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–588][/us/pl/94/588], § 17(a)(1), (2), Oct. 22, 1976, [90 Stat. 2961][/us/stat/90/2961]

    Section, acts [Mar. 1, 1911, ch. 186][/us/act/1911-03-01/ch186], §§ 4, 5, [36 Stat. 962][/us/stat/36/962]; [Sept. 21, 1950, ch. 966][/us/act/1950-09-21/ch966], [64 Stat. 872][/us/stat/64/872], provided for creation and membership of National Forest Reservation Commission.

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    [Section 17(a)(1) of Pub. L. 94–588][/us/pl/94/588/s17/a/1] provided in part that all functions of National Forest Reservation Commission are transferred to the Secretary of Agriculture.
