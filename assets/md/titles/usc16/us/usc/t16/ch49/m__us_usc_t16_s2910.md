## § 2910. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated for purposes of making reimbursements under [section 2905 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s2905] to States for the development and implementation of conservation plans and for administration of this chapter under [section 2907 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s2907] not to exceed $5,000,000 for each of fiscal years 1992, 1993, 1994, 1995, 1996, and 1997.

([Pub. L. 96–366, § 11][/us/pl/96/366/s11], Sept. 29, 1980, [94 Stat. 1329][/us/stat/94/1329]; [Pub. L. 99–375][/us/pl/99/375], Aug. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 803][/us/stat/100/803]; [Pub. L. 100–653, title VIII, § 801][/us/pl/100/653/s801], Nov. 14, 1988, [102 Stat. 3833][/us/stat/102/3833]; [Pub. L. 101–593, title I, § 106][/us/pl/101/593/s106], Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2955][/us/stat/104/2955]; [Pub. L. 102–440, title III, § 301][/us/pl/102/440/s301], Oct. 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 2234][/us/stat/106/2234].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1992—[Pub. L. 102–440][/us/pl/102/440] substituted “fiscal years 1992, 1993, 1994, 1995, 1996, and 1997” for “fiscal years 1982, 1983, 1984, 1985, 1986, 1987, 1988, 1989, 1990, 1991, and 1992”.

    1990—[Pub. L. 101–593][/us/pl/101/593] substituted “1990, 1991, and 1992” for “and 1990”.

    1988—[Pub. L. 100–653][/us/pl/100/653] substituted “1988, 1989, and 1990” for “and 1988”.

    1986—[Pub. L. 99–375][/us/pl/99/375] substituted “1985, 1986, 1987, and 1988” for “and 1985”.
