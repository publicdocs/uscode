## § 47f. Inapplicability of certain laws to lands acquired under section 47e

    The provisions of the Federal Power Act, as amended \[[16 U.S.C. 791a][/us/usc/t16/s791a] et seq.\], shall not apply to any of the lands added to the Yosemite National Park pursuant to the provisions of [section 47e of this title][/us/usc/t16/s47e].

([July 9, 1937, ch. 469][/us/act/1937-07-09/ch469], § 3, [50 Stat. 486][/us/stat/50/486].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Federal Power Act, referred to in text, was in the original the “Act approved June 10, 1920, as amended, known as the Federal Water Power Act,” and was redesignated as the Federal Power Act by [section 791a of this title][/us/usc/t16/s791a]. The Federal Power Act is [act June 10, 1920, ch. 285][/us/act/1920-06-10/ch285], [41 Stat. 1063][/us/stat/41/1063], as amended, and is classified generally to chapter 12 (§ 791a et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 791a of this title][/us/usc/t16/s791a] and Tables.
