## § 460l–10. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7][/us/pl/113/287/s7], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272][/us/stat/128/3272]

    Section, [Pub. L. 88–578, title I, § 8][/us/pl/88/578/s8], formerly § 7, Sept. 3, 1964, [78 Stat. 903][/us/stat/78/903]; renumbered § 8, [Pub. L. 92–347, § 2][/us/pl/92/347/s2], July 11, 1972, [86 Stat. 459][/us/stat/86/459]; amended [Pub. L. 94–422, title I, § 101(5)][/us/pl/94/422/s101/5], Sept. 28, 1976, [90 Stat. 1318][/us/stat/90/1318], related to availability of land and water conservation fund for publicity purposes. See [section 200307 of Title 54][/us/usc/t54/s200307], National Park Service and Related Programs.

    A prior [section 8 of Pub. L. 88–578][/us/pl/88/578/s8] was renumbered section 9 and was classified to section 460l–10a of this title prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 113–287][/us/pl/113/287].
