## § 410eee–1. Definitions

As used in this subchapter:

    (1) __Historical park__ 

        The term “historical park” means the Adams National Historical Park established in [section 410eee–2 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s410eee–2].

    (2) __Secretary__ 

        The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 105–342, § 3][/us/pl/105/342/s3], Nov. 2, 1998, [112 Stat. 3201][/us/stat/112/3201].)
