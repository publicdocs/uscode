## § 410y–1a. Boundary revision

    The boundaries of the park are revised to include approximately 600 additional acres.

([Pub. L. 95–625, title III, § 320][/us/pl/95/625/s320], Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3488][/us/stat/92/3488]; [Pub. L. 96–199, title I, § 101(c)][/us/pl/96/199/s101/c], Mar. 5, 1980, [94 Stat. 68][/us/stat/94/68].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was enacted as part of the National Parks and Recreation Act of 1978, and not as part of the Chesapeake and Ohio Canal Development Act which comprises this subchapter.

 __Amendments__ 

    1980—[Pub. L. 96–199][/us/pl/96/199] struck out proviso that additions to the park as authorized by this section shall not include any properties located between 30th Street and Thomas Jefferson Street in the northwest section of the District of Columbia.
