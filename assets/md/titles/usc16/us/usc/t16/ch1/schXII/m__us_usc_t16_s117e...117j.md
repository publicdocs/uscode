## §§ 117e to 117j. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 39, [62 Stat. 992][/us/stat/62/992], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 117e, acts [Apr. 25, 1928, ch. 434][/us/act/1928-04-25/ch434], § 6, [45 Stat. 460][/us/stat/45/460]; [June 28, 1938, ch. 778][/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778], § 1, [52 Stat. 1213][/us/stat/52/1213], related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 117f, [act Apr. 25, 1928, ch. 434][/us/act/1928-04-25/ch434], § 7, [45 Stat. 460][/us/stat/45/460], related to criminal offenses. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

    Section 117g, [act Apr. 25, 1928, ch. 434][/us/act/1928-04-25/ch434], § 8, [45 Stat. 460][/us/stat/45/460], related to issuance of process.

    Section 117h, acts [Apr. 25, 1928, ch. 434][/us/act/1928-04-25/ch434], § 9, [45 Stat. 461][/us/stat/45/461]; [June 28, 1938, ch. 778][/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778], § 1, [52 Stat. 1213][/us/stat/52/1213], related to salary of commissioner \[now magistrate judge\].

    Section 117i, [act Apr. 25, 1928, ch. 434][/us/act/1928-04-25/ch434], § 10, [45 Stat. 461][/us/stat/45/461], related to fees, costs, and expenses against the United States.

    Section 117j, [act Apr. 25, 1928, ch. 434][/us/act/1928-04-25/ch434], § 11, [45 Stat. 461][/us/stat/45/461], related to disposition of fines and costs.
