## § 430f–12. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out sections 430f–6 to 430f–12 of this title, including $3,000,000 for the construction of an interpretive center under [section 430f–5(d) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s430f–5/d].

([Pub. L. 106–271][/us/pl/106/271], § 7, formerly § 8, Sept. 22, 2000, [114 Stat. 796][/us/stat/114/796]; renumbered § 7, [Pub. L. 110–161, div. F, title I][/us/pl/110/161/dF/tI], § 127(5), Dec. 26, 2007, [121 Stat. 2122][/us/stat/121/2122].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 7 of Pub. L. 106–271][/us/pl/106/271/s7] was classified to [section 430f–11 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s430f–11], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 110–161][/us/pl/110/161].
