## § 410yy–1. Definitions

As used in this subchapter, the term—

    (1) “Commission” means the Keweenaw Historic Preservation Advisory Commission established by [section 410yy–8 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s410yy–8].

    (2) “park” means the Keweenaw National Historical Park established by [section 410yy–2(a)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s410yy–2/a/1].

    (3) “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 102–543, § 2][/us/pl/102/543/s2], Oct. 27, 1992, [106 Stat. 3570][/us/stat/106/3570].)
