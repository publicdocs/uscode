## § 460yyy. Definitions

    In this subchapter:

    (1) __Conservation Area__ 

        The term “Conservation Area” means the Fort Stanton-Snowy River Cave National Conservation Area established by [section 460yyy–1(a) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s460yyy–1/a].

    (2) __Management plan__ 

        The term “management plan” means the management plan developed for the Conservation Area under [section 460yyy–2(c) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s460yyy–2/c].

    (3) __Secretary__ 

        The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior, acting through the Director of the Bureau of Land Management.

([Pub. L. 111–11, title II, § 2201][/us/pl/111/11/s2201], Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1099][/us/stat/123/1099].)
