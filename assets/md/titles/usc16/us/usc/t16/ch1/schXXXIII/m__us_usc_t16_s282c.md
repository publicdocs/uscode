## § 282c. Authorization of appropriations

    There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums, but not more than $13,575,000 for the acquisition of lands and interests therein and for the development of the San Juan National Historical Park. <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be “San Juan Island National Historical Park.” </sup></sup> 

([Pub. L. 89–565, § 4][/us/pl/89/565/s4], Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 737][/us/stat/80/737]; [Pub. L. 95–625, title I, § 101(22)][/us/pl/95/625/s101/22], Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3472][/us/stat/92/3472]; [Pub. L. 111–88, div. A, title I, § 118][/us/pl/111/88/s118], Oct. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 2929][/us/stat/123/2929].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2009—[Pub. L. 111–88][/us/pl/111/88] substituted “$13,575,000” for “$5,575,000”.

    1978—[Pub. L. 95–625][/us/pl/95/625] substituted “$5,575,000” for “$3,542,000”.
