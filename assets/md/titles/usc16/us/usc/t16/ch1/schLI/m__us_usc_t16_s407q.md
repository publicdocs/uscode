## § 407q. Administration, protection, and development

    The administration, protection, and development of the park shall be exercised under the direction of the Secretary of the Interior by the National Park Service, subject to the provisions of sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, as amended and supplemented, and sections 461 to 467 of this title.

([June 28, 1948, ch. 687, § 5][/us/act/1948-06-28/ch687/s5], [62 Stat. 1062][/us/stat/62/1062].)

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    For transfer of functions of other officers, employees, and agencies of Department of the Interior, with certain exceptions, to Secretary of the Interior, with power to delegate, see Reorg. Plan No. 3 of 1950, §§ 1, 2, eff. May 24, 1950, 15 F.R. 3174, [64 Stat. 1262][/us/stat/64/1262], set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

 __Development of Park; Appropriation__ 

    [Pub. L. 85–764][/us/pl/85/764], §§ 1, 2, Aug. 27, 1958, [72 Stat. 861][/us/stat/72/861], authorized the Secretary of the Interior to proceed with the development of Independence National Historical Park, the establishment of which was authorized by the Act of June 28, 1948 ([62 Stat. 1061][/us/stat/62/1061]) \[this subchapter\], in accordance with plans to be approved by the Secretary, authorized to be appropriated for the development of said park pursuant to this Act the sum of $7,250,000, and provided that all funds authorized to be appropriated under this Act be expended by June 30, 1963.
