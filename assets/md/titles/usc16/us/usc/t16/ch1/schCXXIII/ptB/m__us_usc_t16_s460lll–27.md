## § 460lll–27. Designation of national recreation trail

    Effective on the date of transfer pursuant to section 460lll–41 of this title, the North-South Trail is designated as a national recreation trail under [section 1243 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1243].

([Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(e)][/us/pl/105/277/s101/e] \[title V, § 527\], Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–231][/us/stat/112/2681-231], 2681–317.)
