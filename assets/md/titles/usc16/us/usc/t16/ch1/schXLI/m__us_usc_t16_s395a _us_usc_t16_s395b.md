###### §§ 395a, 395b. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 39, [62 Stat. 992][/us/stat/62/992], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 395a, [act Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 200][/us/act/1930-04-19/ch200], § 2, [46 Stat. 227][/us/stat/46/227], related to courts having jurisdiction of offenses. See [section 3231 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s3231], Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rule 18 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

    Section 395b, [act Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 200][/us/act/1930-04-19/ch200], § 3, [46 Stat. 227][/us/stat/46/227], related to applicability of Hawaiian laws to offenses. See [section 13 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s13].
