## § 459i–9. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated not to exceed $28,500,000 for the acquisition of lands and interests in lands and not to exceed $27,840,000 for development of the seashore.

([Pub. L. 92–536, § 10][/us/pl/92/536/s10], Oct. 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 1068][/us/stat/86/1068]; [Pub. L. 95–625, title II, § 201(3)][/us/pl/95/625/s201/3], Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3473][/us/stat/92/3473].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1978—[Pub. L. 95–625][/us/pl/95/625] substituted “$28,500,000” for “$10,500,000”.
