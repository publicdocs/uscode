## §§ 204e to 204j. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 39, [62 Stat. 992][/us/stat/62/992], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 204e, acts [Apr. 26, 1928, ch. 438][/us/act/1928-04-26/ch438], § 6, [45 Stat. 464][/us/stat/45/464]; [June 28, 1938, ch. 778][/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778], § 1, [52 Stat. 1213][/us/stat/52/1213], related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 204f, [act Apr. 26, 1928, ch. 438][/us/act/1928-04-26/ch438], § 7, [45 Stat. 465][/us/stat/45/465], related to issuance of process. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

    Section 204g, [act Apr. 26, 1928, ch. 438][/us/act/1928-04-26/ch438], § 8, [45 Stat. 465][/us/stat/45/465], related to issuance of process and arrest without process. See [section 3053 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s3053], Crimes and Criminal Procedure, Rule 4 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix, and rule 4 of Federal Rules of Civil Procedure, Title 28, Appendix, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 204h, acts [Apr. 26, 1928, ch. 438][/us/act/1928-04-26/ch438], § 9, [45 Stat. 465][/us/stat/45/465]; [June 28, 1938, ch. 778][/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778], § 1, [52 Stat. 1213][/us/stat/52/1213], related to commissioner’s \[now magistrate judge’s\] salary.

    Section 204i, [act Apr. 26, 1928, ch. 438][/us/act/1928-04-26/ch438], § 10, [45 Stat. 465][/us/stat/45/465], related to fees, costs and expenses against the United States.

    Section 204j, [act Apr. 26, 1928, ch. 438][/us/act/1928-04-26/ch438], § 11, [45 Stat. 465][/us/stat/45/465], related to disposition of fines and costs.
