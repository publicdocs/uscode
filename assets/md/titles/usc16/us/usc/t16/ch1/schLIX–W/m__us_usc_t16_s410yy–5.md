## § 410yy–5. General management plan

    Not later than 3 fiscal years after October 27, 1992, the Secretary shall prepare, in consultation with the Commission, and submit to Congress a general management plan for the park containing the information described in [section 100502 of title 54][/us/usc/t54/s100502]. Such plan shall interpret the technological and social history of the area, and the industrial complexes of the Calumet and Hecla, and Quincy Mining Companies, with equal emphasis.

([Pub. L. 102–543, § 6][/us/pl/102/543/s6], Oct. 27, 1992, [106 Stat. 3571][/us/stat/106/3571].)

 __Codification__ 

    In text, “[section 100502 of title 54][/us/usc/t54/s100502]” substituted for “[section 12(b) of the Act of August 18, 1970][/us/act/1970-08-18/s12/b] ([16 U.S.C. 1a–7(b)][/us/usc/t16/s1a–7/b])” on authority of [Pub. L. 113–287, § 6(e)][/us/pl/113/287/s6/e], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272][/us/stat/128/3272], which Act enacted Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs.
