## § 459d–7. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of sections 459d to 459d–7 of this title; except that no more than $5,350,000 shall be appropriated for the acquisition of land and waters and improvements thereon, and interests therein, and incidental costs relating thereto, in accordance with the provisions of such sections.

([Pub. L. 87–712, § 8][/us/pl/87/712/s8], Sept. 28, 1962, [76 Stat. 652][/us/stat/76/652]; [Pub. L. 94–578, title I, § 101(13)][/us/pl/94/578/s101/13], Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2733][/us/stat/90/2733].)

 __Codification__ 

    [Section 101(13) of Pub. L. 94–578][/us/pl/94/578/s101/13], cited as a credit to this section, as amended by [Pub. L. 96–199, title I, § 111][/us/pl/96/199/s111], Mar. 5, 1980, [94 Stat. 70][/us/stat/94/70], is also set out in part as a note under [section 459d–1 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s459d–1].

 __Amendments__ 

    1976—[Pub. L. 94–578][/us/pl/94/578] substituted “$5,350,000” for “$5,000,000”.
