## §§ 256d to 256h. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 39, [62 Stat. 992][/us/stat/62/992], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 256d, acts [Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151], § 5, [56 Stat. 137][/us/stat/56/137]; [Apr. 21, 1948, ch. 223][/us/act/1948-04-21/ch223], § 4, [62 Stat. 197][/us/stat/62/197], related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 256e, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151], § 6, [56 Stat. 137][/us/stat/56/137], related to issuance of process. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

    Section 256f, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151], § 7, [56 Stat. 137][/us/stat/56/137], related to commissioner’s \[now magistrate judge’s\] salary.

    Section 256g, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151], § 8, [56 Stat. 137][/us/stat/56/137], related to certification and payment of fees, costs, and expenses.

    Section 256h, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151], § 9, [56 Stat. 137][/us/stat/56/137], related to disposition of fines and costs.
