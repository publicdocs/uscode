## § 17j–1. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act June 18, 1940, ch. 395, § 1][/us/act/1940-06-18/ch395/s1], [54 Stat. 445][/us/stat/54/445], which related to travel expenses for attendance of National Park Service field employees at authorized meetings, was enacted as part of the Interior Department Appropriations Act, 1941, and was not repeated in the Interior Department Appropriations Act, 1942 (approved [June 28, 1941, ch. 259][/us/act/1941-06-28/ch259], [55 Stat. 303][/us/stat/55/303]), or subsequent appropriation acts. Similar provisions were contained in prior appropriation acts.
