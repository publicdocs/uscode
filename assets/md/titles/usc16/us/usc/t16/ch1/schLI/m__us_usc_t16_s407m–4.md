## § 407m–4. Erection of replica of Graff House; maintenance

    The Secretary is further authorized to erect on the site aforesaid, with donated funds, a replica of the Graff House and to furnish and maintain the same.

([Pub. L. 88–477, § 2][/us/pl/88/477/s2], Aug. 21, 1964, [78 Stat. 587][/us/stat/78/587].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was not enacted as a part of [act June 28, 1948, ch. 687][/us/act/1948-06-28/ch687], [62 Stat. 1061][/us/stat/62/1061], as amended, which comprises this subchapter.
