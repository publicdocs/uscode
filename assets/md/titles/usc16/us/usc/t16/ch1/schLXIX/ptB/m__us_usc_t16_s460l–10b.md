## § 460l–10b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287][/us/pl/113/287], § 7, Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272][/us/stat/128/3272]

    Section, [Pub. L. 88–578, title I][/us/pl/88/578/tI], § 10, formerly § 9, as added [Pub. L. 90–401][/us/pl/90/401], § 4, July 15, 1968, [82 Stat. 355][/us/stat/82/355]; renumbered § 10, [Pub. L. 92–347][/us/pl/92/347], § 2, July 11, 1972, [86 Stat. 459][/us/stat/86/459], related to contracts for options to acquire lands and waters in national park system. See [section 200309 of Title 54][/us/usc/t54/s200309], National Park Service and Related Programs.

    A prior [section 10 of Pub. L. 88–578][/us/pl/88/578/s10] was renumbered section 11 and was classified to section 460l–10c of this title prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 113–287][/us/pl/113/287].
