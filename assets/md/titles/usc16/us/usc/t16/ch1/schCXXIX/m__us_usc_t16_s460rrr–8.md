## § 460rrr–8. Termination of Commission

    The Commission shall terminate on the date that is 10 years after October 12, 2006.

([Pub. L. 109–337, § 10][/us/pl/109/337/s10], Oct. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 1782][/us/stat/120/1782].)
