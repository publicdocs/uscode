## § 241g. Change in name of Theodore Roosevelt National Memorial Park

    The area formerly known as the “Theodore Roosevelt National Memorial Park”, established by this subchapter shall henceforth be known as the “Theodore Roosevelt National Park.”

([Pub. L. 95–625, title VI][/us/pl/95/625/tVI], § 610, Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3521][/us/stat/92/3521].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was not enacted as part of [act Apr. 25, 1947, ch. 41][/us/act/1947-04-25/ch41], [61 Stat. 52][/us/stat/61/52], which comprises this subchapter.
