## § 410y–5. Administration

    The Chesapeake and Ohio Canal National Historical Park shall be administered by the Secretary of the Interior in accordance with sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, as amended and supplemented.

([Pub. L. 91–664, § 7][/us/pl/91/664/s7], Jan. 8, 1971, [84 Stat. 1980][/us/stat/84/1980].)
