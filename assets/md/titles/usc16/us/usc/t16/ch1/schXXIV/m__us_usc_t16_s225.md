## § 225. Laws applicable; easements and rights-of-way

    Whenever consistent with the primary purposes of Grand Canyon National Park, [section 79 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s79] and subsequent Acts shall be applicable to the lands included within the park. The Secretary of the Interior may, in his discretion and upon such conditions as he may deem proper, grant easements or rights of way for railroads upon or across the park.

([Feb. 26, 1919, ch. 44, § 5][/us/act/1919-02-26/ch44/s5], [40 Stat. 1178][/us/stat/40/1178].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 79 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s79], referred to in text, was in the original a reference to [act Feb. 15, 1901, ch. 372][/us/act/1901-02-15/ch372], [31 Stat. 790][/us/stat/31/790]. For further details, see Codification note set out under [section 79 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s79].
