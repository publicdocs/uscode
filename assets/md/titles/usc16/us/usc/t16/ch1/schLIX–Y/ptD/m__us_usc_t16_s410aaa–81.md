## § 410aaa–81. Definition

    Any reference to the term “this Act” in titles I through IX shall be deemed to be solely a reference to sections 1 and 2, and titles I through IX.

([Pub. L. 103–433, title VII, § 711][/us/pl/103/433/s711], Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4501][/us/stat/108/4501].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Sections 1 and 2 and titles I to IX, referred to in text, are sections 1 and 2 and titles I to IX of [Pub. L. 103–433][/us/pl/103/433], Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4471][/us/stat/108/4471], known as the California Desert Protection Act of 1994. Sections 1 and 2 of the Act are set out as notes under [section 410aaa of this title][/us/usc/t16/s410aaa]. Titles I to IX of the Act are classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 410aaa of this title][/us/usc/t16/s410aaa] and Tables.

 __“Secretary” Defined__ 

    [Section 103 of Pub. L. 103–433][/us/pl/103/433/s103] provided in part that in this subchapter “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.
