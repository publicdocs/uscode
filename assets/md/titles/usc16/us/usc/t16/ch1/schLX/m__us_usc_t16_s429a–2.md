## § 429a–2. Change in name to Tupelo National Battlefield; administration

    The Tupelo National Battlefield site is redesignated the Tupelo National Battlefield which shall continue to be administered pursuant to sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, as amended and supplemented.

([Pub. L. 87–133, § 2][/us/pl/87/133/s2], Aug. 10, 1961, [75 Stat. 336][/us/stat/75/336].)
