## § 1a–4. Uniform allowance

    Notwithstanding [section 5901(a) of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s5901/a], the uniform allowance for uniformed employees of the National Park Service may be up to $400 annually.

([Pub. L. 91–383, § 7][/us/pl/91/383/s7], as added [Pub. L. 94–458, § 2][/us/pl/94/458/s2], Oct. 7, 1976, [90 Stat. 1940][/us/stat/90/1940].)
