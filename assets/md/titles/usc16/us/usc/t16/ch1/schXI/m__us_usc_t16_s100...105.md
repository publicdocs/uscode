    Section 100, acts [June 30, 1916, ch. 197][/us/act/1916-06-30/ch197], § 6, [39 Stat. 245][/us/stat/39/245]; [June 28, 1938, ch. 778][/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778], § 1, [52 Stat. 1213][/us/stat/52/1213], related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner.     Section 101, [act June 30, 1916, ch. 197][/us/act/1916-06-30/ch197], § 7, [39 Stat. 245][/us/stat/39/245], related to arrest and bail by commissioner \[now magistrate judge\].
