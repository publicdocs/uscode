## § 410kkk. Definitions

As used in this subchapter:

    (1) __Park__ 

        The term “park” means the Lewis and Clark National Historical Park designated in [section 410kkk–1 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s410kkk–1].

    (2) __Secretary__ 

        The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 108–387, title I, § 102][/us/pl/108/387/s102], Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2234][/us/stat/118/2234].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This subchapter, referred to in introductory provisions, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 108–387][/us/pl/108/387], Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2234][/us/stat/118/2234], which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Short Title note below and Tables.

 __Short Title__ 

[Pub. L. 108–387, title I, § 101][/us/pl/108/387/s101], Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2234][/us/stat/118/2234], provided that: 

> “This title \[enacting this subchapter and repealing sections 450mm to 450mm–3 of this title\] may be cited as the ‘Lewis and Clark National Historical Park Designation Act’.”
