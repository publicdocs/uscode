## § 460l–10a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287][/us/pl/113/287], § 7, Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272][/us/stat/128/3272]

    Section, [Pub. L. 88–578, title I][/us/pl/88/578/tI], § 9, formerly § 8, as added [Pub. L. 90–401][/us/pl/90/401], § 4, July 15, 1968, [82 Stat. 355][/us/stat/82/355]; amended [Pub. L. 91–308][/us/pl/91/308], § 3, July 7, 1970, [84 Stat. 410][/us/stat/84/410]; renumbered § 9, [Pub. L. 92–347][/us/pl/92/347], § 2, July 11, 1972, [86 Stat. 459][/us/stat/86/459], and amended [Pub. L. 93–303][/us/pl/93/303], § 3, June 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 194][/us/stat/88/194], related to contracts for acquisition of lands and waters. See [section 200308 of Title 54][/us/usc/t54/s200308], National Park Service and Related Programs.
