## § 430mm. Authorization of appropriations

    For development of the Wilson’s Creek National Battlefield, there are authorized to be appropriated not more than $5,640,000. There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out [section 430kk(b) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s430kk/b].

([Pub. L. 86–434][/us/pl/86/434], § 3, Apr. 22, 1960, [74 Stat. 76][/us/stat/74/76]; [Pub. L. 91–554][/us/pl/91/554], § 1(b), Dec. 16, 1970, [84 Stat. 1441][/us/stat/84/1441]; [Pub. L. 95–625, title I][/us/pl/95/625/tI], § 101(29), Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3472][/us/stat/92/3472]; [Pub. L. 108–394][/us/pl/108/394], § 2(b), Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2248][/us/stat/118/2248].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2004—[Pub. L. 108–394][/us/pl/108/394] inserted last sentence.

    1978—[Pub. L. 95–625][/us/pl/95/625] substituted “$5,640,000.” for “$2,285,000 (March 1969 prices), plus or minus such amounts, if any, as may be justified by reason of ordinary fluctuations in construction cost indices applicable to the types of construction involved herein.”

    1970—[Pub. L. 91–554][/us/pl/91/554] increased authorization of appropriations from not more than $120,000 to not more than $2,285,000 (March 1969 prices), plus or minus such amounts, if any, as may be justified by reason of ordinary fluctuations in construction costs as indicated by engineering cost indices.
