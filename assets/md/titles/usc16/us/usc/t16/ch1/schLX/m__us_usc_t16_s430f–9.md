## § 430f–9. Land acquisition

    (a) __In general__ 

    The Secretary may acquire land and interests in land within the boundary of the Park described in [section 430f–8(b) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s430f–8/b], by—

        (1) donation;

        (2) purchase with donated or appropriated funds; or

        (3) exchange.

    (b) __Exception__ 

    Land may be acquired only by donation from—

        (1) the State of Mississippi (including a political subdivision of the State);

        (2) the State of Tennessee (including a political subdivision of the State); or

        (3) the organization known as “Friends of the Siege and Battle of Corinth”.

([Pub. L. 106–271, § 5][/us/pl/106/271/s5], Sept. 22, 2000, [114 Stat. 793][/us/stat/114/793]; [Pub. L. 110–161, div. F, title I, § 127(3)][/us/pl/110/161/s127/3], Dec. 26, 2007, [121 Stat. 2122][/us/stat/121/2122].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2007—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 110–161][/us/pl/110/161] substituted “described in [section 430f–8(b) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s430f–8/b]” for “as depicted on the Map” in introductory provisions.
