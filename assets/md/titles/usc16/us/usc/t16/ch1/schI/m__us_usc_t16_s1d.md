## § 1d. Appropriations

    On and after August 8, 1953, applicable appropriations of the National Park Service shall be available for the objects and purposes specified in [section 17j–2 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s17j–2].

([Aug. 8, 1953, ch. 384, § 3][/us/act/1953-08-08/ch384/s3], [67 Stat. 496][/us/stat/67/496].)
