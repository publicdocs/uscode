## § 459a–6. Acquisition of non-Federal land within boundaries of recreational area

    [Section 452a of this title][/us/usc/t16/s452a] is amended to extend the authority of the Secretary of the Interior, contained therein, to the Cape Hatteras National Seashore Recreational Area.

([Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 988, § 1][/us/act/1956-08-06/ch988/s1], [70 Stat. 1066][/us/stat/70/1066].)
