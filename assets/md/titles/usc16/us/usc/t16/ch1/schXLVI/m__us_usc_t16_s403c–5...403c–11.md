## §§ 403c–5 to 403c–11. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 39, [62 Stat. 992][/us/stat/62/992], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 403c–5, acts [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 703][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch703], § 5, [50 Stat. 702][/us/stat/50/702]; [May 15, 1947, ch. 57][/us/act/1947-05-15/ch57], [61 Stat. 92][/us/stat/61/92], related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 403c–6, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 703][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch703], § 6, [50 Stat. 702][/us/stat/50/702], related to jurisdiction of other commissioners. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28.

    Section 403c–7, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 703][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch703], § 7, [50 Stat. 702][/us/stat/50/702], related to issuance of process. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

    Section 403c–8, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 703][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch703], § 8, [50 Stat. 702][/us/stat/50/702], related to whom process is directed. See [section 3053 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s3053], rule 4 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix, and rule 4 of Federal Rules of Civil Procedure, Title 28, Appendix, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 403c–9, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 703][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch703], § 9, [50 Stat. 702][/us/stat/50/702], related to commissioner’s \[now magistrate judge’s\] salary.

    Section 403c–10, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 703][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch703], § 10, [50 Stat. 703][/us/stat/50/703], related to fees, costs, and expenses against United States. See [section 604 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s604], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 403c–11, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 703][/us/act/1937-08-19/ch703], § 11, [50 Stat. 703][/us/stat/50/703], related to disposition of fines and costs. See [section 634 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s634].
