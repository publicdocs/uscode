## §§ 158a to 158d. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 39, [62 Stat. 992][/us/stat/62/992], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 158a, [act May 15, 1947, ch. 55][/us/act/1947-05-15/ch55], § 1, [61 Stat. 91][/us/stat/61/91], related to appointment and compensation of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 158b, [act May 15, 1947, ch. 55][/us/act/1947-05-15/ch55], § 2, [61 Stat. 91][/us/stat/61/91], related to jurisdiction of commissioner \[now magistrate judge\] over petty offenses.

    Section 158c, [act May 15, 1947, ch. 55][/us/act/1947-05-15/ch55], § 3, [61 Stat. 91][/us/stat/61/91], related to jurisdiction of commissioner \[now magistrate judge\] over criminal offenses.

    Section 158d, [act May 15, 1947, ch. 55][/us/act/1947-05-15/ch55], § 4, [61 Stat. 91][/us/stat/61/91], related to disposition of fees, costs, and expenses.
