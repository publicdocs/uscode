## § 450y–7. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated the sum of not to exceed $3,000 for the purpose of acquiring lands, interests in lands, and improvements thereon as may be necessary for carrying out this Act.

([Pub. L. 86–689][/us/pl/86/689], § 4, Sept. 2, 1960, [74 Stat. 737][/us/stat/74/737].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This Act, referred to in text, means [Pub. L. 86–689][/us/pl/86/689], which enacted sections 450y–5 to 450y–7 of this title, and amended [section 17j–2 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s17j–2]. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.
