## § 410bbb–3. Acquisition of property

    (a) __General authority__ 

        The Secretary may acquire lands and interests therein within the sites designated pursuant to section 410bbb–1(b)(1) and (3) of this title by donation or purchase with donated or appropriated funds or long term lease: Provided, That sites designated pursuant to [section 410bbb–1(b)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s410bbb–1/b/3] shall only be acquired with the consent of the owner thereof.

    (b) __State and local properties__ 

        Lands and interests in lands which are owned by the State of Louisiana, or any political subdivision thereof, may be acquired only by donation.

([Pub. L. 103–433, title XII][/us/pl/103/433/tXII], § 1205, Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4522][/us/stat/108/4522].)
