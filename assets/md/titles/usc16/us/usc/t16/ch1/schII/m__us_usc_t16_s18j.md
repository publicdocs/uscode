## § 18j. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this subchapter, but not more than $3,500,000 shall be appropriated in any one year.

([Pub. L. 91–357, § 4][/us/pl/91/357/s4], July 29, 1970, [84 Stat. 472][/us/stat/84/472]; [Pub. L. 94–128][/us/pl/94/128], Nov. 13, 1975, [89 Stat. 682][/us/stat/89/682]; [Pub. L. 98–540, § 1(a)][/us/pl/98/540/s1/a], Oct. 24, 1984, [98 Stat. 2718][/us/stat/98/2718]; [Pub. L. 104–333, div. I, title VIII, § 805][/us/pl/104/333/s805], Nov. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 4188][/us/stat/110/4188].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1996—[Pub. L. 104–333][/us/pl/104/333] substituted “$3,500,000” for “$1,000,000”.

    1984—[Pub. L. 98–540][/us/pl/98/540] substituted “$1,000,000” for “$250,000”.

    1975—[Pub. L. 94–128][/us/pl/94/128] substituted “$250,000” for “$100,000”.

 __Effective Date of 1984 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 98–540, § 1(a)][/us/pl/98/540/s1/a], Oct. 24, 1984, [98 Stat. 2718][/us/stat/98/2718], provided in part that: 

> “The amendment made by this subsection \[amending this section\] shall apply with respect to fiscal years beginning after September 30, 1984.”
