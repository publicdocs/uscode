## § 450pp–1. Establishment; notice of establishment; administration

    The property acquired pursuant to [section 450pp of this title][/us/usc/t16/s450pp] shall be established as the Roger Williams National Memorial and the Secretary of the Interior shall publish notice of such establishment in the Federal Register. Such national Memorial shall be administered by the Secretary subject to the provisions of sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, as amended and supplemented, and sections 461 to 467 of this title.

([Pub. L. 89–293, § 2][/us/pl/89/293/s2], Oct. 22, 1965, [79 Stat. 1069][/us/stat/79/1069].)
