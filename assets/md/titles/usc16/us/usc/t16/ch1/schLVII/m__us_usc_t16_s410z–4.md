## § 410z–4. Administration

    When established as provided in [section 410z of this title][/us/usc/t16/s410z], the Boston National Historical Park shall be administered by the Secretary in accordance with the provisions of this subchapter, sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, as amended and supplemented, and sections 461 to 467 of this title.

([Pub. L. 93–431, § 6][/us/pl/93/431/s6], Oct. 1, 1974, [88 Stat. 1186][/us/stat/88/1186].)
