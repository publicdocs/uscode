## §§ 408m to 408q. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], § 39, [62 Stat. 992][/us/stat/62/992], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 408m, acts [Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 150][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch150], § 5, [56 Stat. 134][/us/stat/56/134]; [Apr. 21, 1948, ch. 223][/us/act/1948-04-21/ch223], § 1, [62 Stat. 196][/us/stat/62/196], related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 408n, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 150][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch150], § 6, [56 Stat. 135][/us/stat/56/135], related to issuance of process. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

    Section 408o, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 150][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch150], § 7, [56 Stat. 135][/us/stat/56/135], related to commissioner’s \[now magistrate judge’s\] salary. See [section 634 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s634], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 408p, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 150][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch150], § 8, [56 Stat. 135][/us/stat/56/135], related to fees, costs, and expenses against United States. See [section 604 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s604].

    Section 408q, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 150][/us/act/1942-03-06/ch150], § 9, [56 Stat. 135][/us/stat/56/135], related to disposition of fines and costs. See [section 634 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s634].
