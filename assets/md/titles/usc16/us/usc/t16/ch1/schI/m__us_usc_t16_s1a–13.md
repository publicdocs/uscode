## § 1a–13. Proposals for boundary changes

    In proposing any boundary change after November 28, 1990, the Secretary shall—

    (a) consult with affected agencies of State and local governments  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be followed by a comma. </sup></sup>  surrounding communities, affected landowners and private national, regional, and local organizations;

    (b) apply the criteria developed pursuant to [section 1a–12 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1a–12] and accompany this proposal with a statement reflecting the results of the application of such criteria;

    (c) include with such proposal an estimate of the cost for acquisition of any parcels proposed for acquisition together with the basis for the estimate and a statement on the relative priority for the acquisition of each parcel within the priorities for acquisition of other lands for such unit and for the National Park System.

([Pub. L. 101–628, title XII, § 1217][/us/pl/101/628/s1217], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 4508][/us/stat/104/4508].)
