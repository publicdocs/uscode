## §§ 204a, 204b. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39], [62 Stat. 992][/us/stat/62/992], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 204a, [act Apr. 26, 1928, ch. 438, § 2][/us/act/1928-04-26/ch438/s2], [45 Stat. 463][/us/stat/45/463], related to inclusion of park in a judicial district. See [section 84 of Title 28][/us/usc/t28/s84], Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

    Section 204b, [act Apr. 26, 1928, ch. 438, § 3][/us/act/1928-04-26/ch438/s3], [45 Stat. 463][/us/stat/45/463], related to application of California laws to offenses. See [section 13 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s13], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.
