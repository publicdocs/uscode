## § 460l–6. Appropriations for expenditure of land and water conservation fund moneys; transfers to miscellaneous receipts of Treasury

    Moneys covered into the fund shall be available for expenditure for the purposes of this part only when appropriated therefor. Such appropriations may be made without fiscal-year limitation. Moneys made available for obligation or expenditure from the fund or from the special account established under section 460l–6a(i)(1)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title may be obligated or expended only as provided in this part.

([Pub. L. 88–578][/us/pl/88/578]. title I, § 3, Sept. 3, 1964, [78 Stat. 899][/us/stat/78/899]; [Pub. L. 100–203, title V, § 5201(f)(2)][/us/pl/100/203/s5201/f/2], Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–267][/us/stat/101/1330-267].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This part, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 88–578][/us/pl/88/578], Sept. 3, 1964, [78 Stat. 897][/us/stat/78/897], which is classified principally to this part. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under section 460l–4 of this title and Tables.

    Section 460l–6a(i)(1) of this title, referred to in text, was repealed, with the exception of subpar. (C) of subsec. (i)(1), by [Pub. L. 108–447, div. J, title VIII, § 813(a)][/us/pl/108/447/s813/a], Dec. 8, 2004, [118 Stat. 3390][/us/stat/118/3390], as amended by [Pub. L. 109–54, title I, § 132(a)][/us/pl/109/54/s132/a], Aug. 2, 2005, [119 Stat. 526][/us/stat/119/526].

 __Amendments__ 

    1987—[Pub. L. 100–203][/us/pl/100/203] amended last sentence generally. Prior to amendment, last sentence read as follows: “Moneys covered into this fund not subsequently authorized by the Congress for expenditures within two fiscal years following the fiscal year in which such moneys had been credited to the fund, shall be transferred to miscellaneous receipts of the Treasury.”
