## § 430f–7. Definitions

In sections 430f–6 to 430f–12 of this title:

    (1) __Map__ 

        The term “Map” means the map entitled “Park Boundary-Corinth Unit”, numbered 304A/80009, and dated April 2007.

    (2) __Park__ 

        The term “Park” means the Shiloh National Military Park.

    (3) __Secretary__ 

        The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

    (4) __Unit__ 

        The term “Unit” means the Corinth Unit of Shiloh National Military Park established under [section 430f–8 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s430f–8].

([Pub. L. 106–271, § 3][/us/pl/106/271/s3], Sept. 22, 2000, [114 Stat. 793][/us/stat/114/793]; [Pub. L. 110–161, div. F, title I, § 127(1)][/us/pl/110/161/s127/1], Dec. 26, 2007, [121 Stat. 2122][/us/stat/121/2122].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2007—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 110–161][/us/pl/110/161] substituted “304A/80009, and dated April 2007” for “304/80,007, and dated October 1998”.
