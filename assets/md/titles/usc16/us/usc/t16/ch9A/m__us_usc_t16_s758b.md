## § 758b. Cooperation with agencies, organizations, and others

    In carrying out the purposes and objectives of sections 758 and 758a of this title, the Secretary of Commerce may cooperate with appropriate agencies of the State and island governments, and with such educational, industrial, or other organizations, enterprises, and individuals as may be expedient.

([Aug. 4, 1947, ch. 451][/us/act/1947-08-04/ch451], § 3, [61 Stat. 726][/us/stat/61/726]; [Pub. L. 86–624][/us/pl/86/624], § 11(c), July 12, 1960, [74 Stat. 412][/us/stat/74/412]; 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 4, eff. Oct. 3, 1970, 35 F.R. 15627, [84 Stat. 2090][/us/stat/84/2090].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1960—[Pub. L. 86–624][/us/pl/86/624] substituted “State” for “Territorial”.

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    Transfer of functions to Secretary of Commerce from Secretary of the Interior by Reorg. Plan No. 4 of 1970, see note set out under [section 758a of this title][/us/usc/t16/s758a].
