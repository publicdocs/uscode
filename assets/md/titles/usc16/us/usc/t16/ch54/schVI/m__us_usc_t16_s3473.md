## § 3473. Regulations

    The Secretary of Agriculture shall prescribe such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

([Pub. L. 97–98, title XV][/us/pl/97/98/tXV], § 1554, Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1345][/us/stat/95/1345].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title XV of [Pub. L. 97–98][/us/pl/97/98], Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1328][/us/stat/95/1328], which enacted this chapter and sections 2272 and 2273 and chapter 73 (§ 4201 et seq.) of Title 7, Agriculture, amended sections 1002 to 1005 of this title, [section 1010 of Title 7][/us/usc/t7/s1010], [section 714b of Title 15][/us/usc/t15/s714b], Commerce and Trade, and [section 1236 of Title 30][/us/usc/t30/s1236], Mineral Lands and Mining, and enacted a provision set out as a note under [section 2272 of Title 7][/us/usc/t7/s2272].
