### SUBCHAPTER V—RESOURCE CONSERVATION AND DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

 __Codification__ 

    Subtitle H of title XV of the Agriculture and Food Act of 1981, comprising this subchapter, was originally enacted by [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV][/us/pl/97/98], Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1337][/us/stat/95/1337], and amended by [Pub. L. 101–624][/us/pl/101/624], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3359][/us/stat/104/3359]; [Pub. L. 104–127][/us/pl/104/127], Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 888][/us/stat/110/888]. Subtitle H is shown herein, however, as having been added by [Pub. L. 107–171, title II, § 2504][/us/pl/107/171/s2504], May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 269][/us/stat/116/269], without reference to the intervening amendments because of the extensive revision of the subtitle’s provisions by [Pub. L. 107–171][/us/pl/107/171].
