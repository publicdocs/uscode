## § 3460. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __In general__ 

        There are authorized to be  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be followed by “appropriated”. </sup></sup>  such sums as are necessary to carry out this subchapter.

    (b) __Loans__ 

        The Secretary shall not use more than $15,000,000 of any funds made available for a fiscal year to make loans under this subchapter.

    (c) __Availability__ 

        Funds appropriated to carry out this subchapter shall remain available until expended.

([Pub. L. 97–98, title XV][/us/pl/97/98/tXV], § 1537, as added [Pub. L. 107–171, title II][/us/pl/107/171/tII], § 2504, May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 274][/us/stat/116/274].)
