## § 691d. Applicability of certain statutes

    Sections 715f to 715i and 715l to 715n  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title are made applicable for the purposes of sections 691, 691a, and 691c of this title in the same manner and to the same extent as though they were enacted as a part of those sections.

([June 12, 1930, ch. 469][/us/act/1930-06-12/ch469], § 5, [46 Stat. 579][/us/stat/46/579].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Sections 715l and 715m of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by [Pub. L. 89–669][/us/pl/89/669], § 7(d), Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 930][/us/stat/80/930]. See section 668dd(f) and (e) of this title.
