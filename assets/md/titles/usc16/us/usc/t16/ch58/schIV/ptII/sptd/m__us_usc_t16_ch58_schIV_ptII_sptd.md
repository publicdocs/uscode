##### subpart d—grassland reserve program

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 110–234, title II][/us/pl/110/234], §§ 2301(a)(1), 2403, May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1040][/us/stat/122/1040], 1051, and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a], title II, §§ 2301(a)(1), 2403, June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 1768, 1779, both redesignated subpart C of this part as subpart D. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a] effective May 22, 2008, the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234].

    [Section 2401 of Pub. L. 107–171][/us/pl/107/171/s2401], which directed that subchapter C (this subpart) be added at the end of chapter 2 of the Food Security Act of 1985, was executed by adding subchapter C at the end of chapter 2 of subtitle D of title XII of the Food Security Act of 1985 (this part) to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
