#### Part IV—Environmental Quality Incentives Program

 __Codification__ 

    Chapter 4 of subtitle D of title XII of the Food Security Act of 1985, comprising this part, was originally added to [Pub. L. 99–198][/us/pl/99/198] by [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 334][/us/pl/104/127/s334], Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 996][/us/stat/110/996]. Chapter 4 is shown herein, however, as having been added by [Pub. L. 107–171, title II, § 2301][/us/pl/107/171/s2301], May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 253][/us/stat/116/253], because of the extensive revision of the chapter’s provisions by [Pub. L. 107–171][/us/pl/107/171]. Such revision did not contain a chapter heading, which was subsequently added by [Pub. L. 110–234, title II, § 2501(b)][/us/pl/110/234/s2501/b], May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1058][/us/stat/122/1058], and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a], title II, § 2501(b), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 1786. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a] effective May 22, 2008, the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234].
