## § 3823. Affiliated persons

    If a person is affected by a reduction in benefits under [section 3821 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s3821] and the affected person is affiliated with other persons for the purpose of receiving the benefits, the benefits of each affiliated person shall be reduced under [section 3821 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s3821] in proportion to the interest held by the affiliated person.

([Pub. L. 99–198, title XII, § 1223][/us/pl/99/198/s1223], as added [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 324][/us/pl/104/127/s324], Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 992][/us/stat/110/992].)
