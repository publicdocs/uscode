## § 6573. Restoration plans

    (a) __In general__ 

        Land enrolled in the healthy forests reserve program shall be subject to a restoration plan, to be developed jointly by the landowner and the Secretary of Agriculture, in coordination with the Secretary of  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be “of the”. </sup></sup>  Interior.

    (b) __Practices__ 

    The restoration plan shall require such restoration practices as are necessary to restore and enhance habitat for—

        (1) species listed as endangered or threatened under [section 1533 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1533]; and

        (2) animal or plant species before the species reach threatened or endangered status, such as candidate, State-listed species, and special concern species.

([Pub. L. 108–148, title V][/us/pl/108/148/tV], § 503, Dec. 3, 2003, [117 Stat. 1912][/us/stat/117/1912].)
