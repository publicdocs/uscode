## § 715b. Annual report

    The commission created by [section 715a of this title][/us/usc/t16/s715a] shall, through its chairman, annually report in detail to Congress, not later than the first Monday in December, the operations of the commission during the preceding fiscal year.

([Feb. 18, 1929, ch. 257, § 3][/us/act/1929-02-18/ch257/s3], [45 Stat. 1223][/us/stat/45/1223].)

 __Termination of Reporting Requirements__ 

    For termination, effective May 15, 2000, of provisions of law requiring submittal to Congress of any annual, semiannual, or other regular periodic report listed in House Document No. 103–7 (in which a report required under this section is listed on page 177), see [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66][/us/pl/104/66/s3003], as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s1113], Money and Finance.
