## § 702. Importation of eggs of game birds for propagation

    The Secretary of the Interior shall have the power to authorize the importation of eggs of game birds for purposes of propagation, and he shall prescribe all necessary rules and regulations governing the importation of eggs of said birds for such purposes.

([June 3, 1902, ch. 983][/us/act/1902-06-03/ch983], [32 Stat. 285][/us/stat/32/285]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. II, § 4(f), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2731, [53 Stat. 1433][/us/stat/53/1433].)

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    Transfer of functions of Secretary of Agriculture to Secretary of the Interior by Reorg. Plan No. II of 1939, see Transfer of Functions note set out under [section 701 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s701].
