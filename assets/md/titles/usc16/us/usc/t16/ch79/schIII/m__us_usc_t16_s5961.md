## § 5961. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7][/us/pl/113/287/s7], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272][/us/stat/128/3272]

    Section, [Pub. L. 105–391, title IV, § 412][/us/pl/105/391/s412], Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3515][/us/stat/112/3515]; [Pub. L. 106–113, div. B, § 1000(a)(3)][/us/pl/106/113/s1000/a/3] \[title I, § 143\], Nov. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 1535][/us/stat/113/1535], 1501A–171; [Pub. (a), limiting the length of a service contract, was repealed and reenacted as [section 101524 of Title 54][/us/usc/t54/s101524], National Park Service and Related Programs. Subsec. (b), relating to obligation of funds received in fiscal year 2002, was repealed as obsolete.
