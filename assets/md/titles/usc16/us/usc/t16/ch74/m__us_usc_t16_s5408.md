## § 5408. Savings provision

    The authorities contained in this chapter shall be in addition to, and shall not be construed to supercede or modify those contained in the National Historic Preservation Act ([16 U.S.C. 470][/us/usc/t16/s470]—470x–6).

([Pub. L. 103–451, § 9][/us/pl/103/451/s9], Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4778][/us/stat/108/4778].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The National Historic Preservation Act, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 89–665][/us/pl/89/665], Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 915][/us/stat/80/915], as amended, which is classified generally to subchapter II (§ 470 et seq.) of chapter 1A of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 470 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s470] and Tables.
