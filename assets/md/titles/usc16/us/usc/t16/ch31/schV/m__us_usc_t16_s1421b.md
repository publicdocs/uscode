## § 1421b. Stranding response agreements

    (a) __In general__ 

        The Secretary may enter into an agreement under [section 1382(c) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1382/c] with any person to take marine mammals under [section 1379(h)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1379/h/1] in response to a stranding.

    (b) __Required provision__ 

    An agreement authorized by subsection (a) of this section shall—

        (1) specify each person who is authorized to perform activities under the agreement; and

        (2) specify any terms and conditions under which a person so specified may delegate that authority to another person.

    (c) __Review__ 

        The Secretary shall periodically review agreements under [section 1382(c) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1382/c] that are entered into pursuant to this subchapter, for performance adequacy and effectiveness.
