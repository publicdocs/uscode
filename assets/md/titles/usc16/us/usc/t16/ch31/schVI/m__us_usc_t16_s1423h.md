## § 1423h. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __In general__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out the functions and responsibilities of the Secretary under this subchapter and the Agreement $1,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2006 through 2010.

    (b) __Commission__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out functions and responsibilities of the United States Section  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should not be capitalized. </sup></sup>  $150,000 for each of fiscal years 2006 through 2010.

    (c) __Alaskan cooperative management program__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out this subchapter and the Agreement in Alaska $150,000 for each of fiscal years 2006 through 2010.

([Pub. L. 92–522, title V, § 509][/us/pl/92/522/s509], as added [Pub. L. 109–479, title IX, § 902(a)][/us/pl/109/479/s902/a], Jan. 12, 2007, [120 Stat. 3665][/us/stat/120/3665].)
