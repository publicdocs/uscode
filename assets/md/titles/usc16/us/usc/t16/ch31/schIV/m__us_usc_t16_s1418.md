## § 1418. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–42][/us/pl/105/42], § 6(e), Aug. 15, 1997, [111 Stat. 1137][/us/stat/111/1137]

    Section, [Pub. L. 92–522, title III][/us/pl/92/522/tIII], § 308, as added [Pub. L. 102–523][/us/pl/102/523], § 2(a), Oct. 26, 1992, [106 Stat. 3432][/us/stat/106/3432], related to authorization of appropriations.
