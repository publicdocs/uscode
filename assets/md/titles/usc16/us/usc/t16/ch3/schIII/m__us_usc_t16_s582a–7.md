## § 582a–7. “State” defined

    The term “State” as used in this subchapter shall include Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands, and Guam.

([Pub. L. 87–788, § 8][/us/pl/87/788/s8], Oct. 10, 1962, [76 Stat. 807][/us/stat/76/807]; [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 506(j)][/us/pl/92/318/s506/j], June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 351][/us/stat/86/351].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1972—[Pub. L. 92–318][/us/pl/92/318] inserted reference to Virgin Islands and Guam.

 __Effective Date of 1972 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 92–318][/us/pl/92/318] effective after June 30, 1970, see [section 506(n) of Pub. L. 92–318][/us/pl/92/318/s506/n], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 326a of Title 7][/us/usc/t7/s326a], Agriculture.
