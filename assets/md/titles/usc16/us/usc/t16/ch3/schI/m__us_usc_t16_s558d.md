## § 558d. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of sections 558a to 558d of this title.

([Pub. L. 92–300, § 4][/us/pl/92/300/s4], May 18, 1972, [86 Stat. 147][/us/stat/86/147]; [Pub. L. 95–289, § 1][/us/pl/95/289/s1], June 5, 1978, [92 Stat. 289][/us/stat/92/289].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1978—[Pub. L. 95–289][/us/pl/95/289] struck out limitation requiring not more than $100,000 to be appropriated in any one year.

 __Effective Date of 1978 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 95–289, § 2][/us/pl/95/289/s2], June 5, 1978, [92 Stat. 289][/us/stat/92/289], provided that: 

> “The amendment made by this Act to the Volunteers in the National Forests Act of 1972 \[amending this section\] shall become effective October 1, 1978.”
