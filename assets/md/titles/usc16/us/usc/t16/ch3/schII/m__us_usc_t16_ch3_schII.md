    Section 581, acts [May 22, 1928, ch. 678, § 1][/us/act/1928-05-22/ch678/s1], [45 Stat. 699][/us/stat/45/699]; [Apr. 24, 1950, ch. 97, § 17(a)][/us/act/1950-04-24/ch97/s17/a], [64 Stat. 87][/us/stat/64/87], authorized investigations, experiments, and tests affecting reforestation and forest products through cooperation with State and other agencies.
