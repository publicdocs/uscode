## § 559f. Approval of Secretary of Agriculture and Attorney General

    The authorities conferred herein shall be exercised pursuant to an agreement approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and the Attorney General.

([Pub. L. 99–570, title XV][/us/pl/99/570/tXV], § 15007, Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–192][/us/stat/100/3207-192].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Herein, referred to in text, probably means title XV of [Pub. L. 99–570][/us/pl/99/570], Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–191][/us/stat/100/3207-191], known as the National Forest System Drug Control Act of 1986, which enacted sections 559b to 559f of this title, amended [section 841 of Title 21][/us/usc/t21/s841], Food and Drugs, and enacted provisions set out as a note under [section 559b of this title][/us/usc/t16/s559b]. For complete classification of title XV to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 559b of this title][/us/usc/t16/s559b] and Tables.
