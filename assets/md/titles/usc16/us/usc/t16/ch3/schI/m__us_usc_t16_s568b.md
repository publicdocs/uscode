## § 568b. Repealed. [Aug. 25, 1950, ch. 781, § 3][/us/act/1950-08-25/ch781/s3], [64 Stat. 473][/us/stat/64/473], eff. June 30, 1951

    Section, [act May 18, 1937, ch. 226][/us/act/1937-05-18/ch226], [50 Stat. 188][/us/stat/50/188], related to cooperation by Secretary of Agriculture in development of farm forestry in States and Territories. This section was known as the “Cooperative Farm Forestry Act”.

§§ 568c to 568e. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 95–313, § 16(a)(4)__  __][/us/pl/95/313/s16/a/4]__  __, (5), formerly § 13(a)(4), (5),__  __July 1, 1978__  __,__  __[__  __92 Stat. 374__  __][/us/stat/92/374]__  __; renumbered § 16(a)(4), (5),__  __[__  __Pub. L. 101–624, title XII, § 1215(1)__  __][/us/pl/101/624/s1215/1]__  __,__  __Nov. 28, 1990__  __,__  __[__  __104 Stat. 3525__  __][/us/stat/104/3525]__ 

    Section 568c, acts [Aug. 25, 1950, ch. 781, § 1][/us/act/1950-08-25/ch781/s1], [64 Stat. 473][/us/stat/64/473]; May 5, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–288, § 1][/us/pl/92/288/s1], [86 Stat. 134][/us/stat/86/134], required cooperation between Secretary of Agriculture and State officials in providing technical services to private landowners, etc.

    Section 568d, acts [Aug. 25, 1950, ch. 781, § 2][/us/act/1950-08-25/ch781/s2], [64 Stat. 473][/us/stat/64/473]; Sept. 25, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–680][/us/pl/87/680], [76 Stat. 569][/us/stat/76/569]; May 5, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–288, § 2][/us/pl/92/288/s2], [86 Stat. 134][/us/stat/86/134], set forth funding requirements for cooperative forest management programs involving private landowners, etc.

    Section 568e, [act May 28, 1956, ch. 327][/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327], title IV, § 401, [70 Stat. 207][/us/stat/70/207], set forth provisions relating to assistance to States for tree planting and reforestation through the Secretary of Agriculture.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 17 of Pub. L. 95–313][/us/pl/95/313/s17], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 2101 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s2101].

 __Short Title__ 

    Section 4 of act Aug. 25, 1950, setting forth the popular name of the act of Aug. 25, 1950, as the “Cooperative Forest Management Act,” was repealed by [Pub. L. 95–313, § 16(a)(4)][/us/pl/95/313/s16/a/4], formerly § 13(a)(4), July 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 374][/us/stat/92/374], renumbered § 16(a)(4), [Pub. L. 101–624, title XII, § 1215(1)][/us/pl/101/624/s1215/1], Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3525][/us/stat/104/3525].

§§ 568f, 568g. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Section 568f, [act May 28, 1956, ch. 327][/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327], title IV, § 402, [70 Stat. 208][/us/stat/70/208], required the Secretary of Agriculture to make a study of price trends and relationships for basic forest products and submit a report to the Congress within one year from May 28, 1956.

    Section 568g, [act May 28, 1956, ch. 327][/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327], title IV, § 403, as added Aug. 28, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–829][/us/pl/85/829], [72 Stat. 986][/us/stat/72/986], which provided that as used in [section 568e of this title][/us/usc/t16/s568e], the term “State” included the Territory of Hawaii, is obsolete in view of the repeal of [section 568e of this title][/us/usc/t16/s568e] by [Pub. L. 95–313, § 13(a)(5)][/us/pl/95/313/s13/a/5], July 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 374][/us/stat/92/374].
