## §§ 1181 to 1187. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 98–129, § 2][/us/pl/98/129/s2], Oct. 14, 1983, [97 Stat. 835][/us/stat/97/835].

    Section 1181, [Pub. L. 89–702, title IV, § 401][/us/pl/89/702/s401], Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/80/1097], related to seizure and forfeiture of vessels. See [section 1171 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1171].

    Section 1182, [Pub. L. 89–702, title IV, § 402][/us/pl/89/702/s402], Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/80/1097]; [Pub. L. 90–578, title IV, § 402(b)(2)][/us/pl/90/578/s402/b/2], Oct. 17, 1968, [82 Stat. 1118][/us/stat/82/1118]; 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 4, eff. Oct. 3, 1970, 35 F.R. 15627, [84 Stat. 2090][/us/stat/84/2090]; 1978 Reorg. Plan No. 2 of 1978, § 102, eff. Jan. 1, 1979, 43 F.R. 36037, [92 Stat. 3784][/us/stat/92/3784], set out additional enforcement provisions. See [section 1172 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1172].

    Section 1183, [Pub. L. 89–702, title IV, § 403][/us/pl/89/702/s403], Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1098][/us/stat/80/1098]; 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 4, eff. Oct. 3, 1970, 35 F.R. 15627, [84 Stat. 2090][/us/stat/84/2090], authorized the Secretary of Commerce to issue regulations. See [section 1173 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1173].

    Section 1184, [Pub. L. 89–702, title IV, § 404][/us/pl/89/702/s404], Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1098][/us/stat/80/1098], set out penalties for violations. See [section 1174 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1174].

    Section 1185, [Pub. L. 89–702, title IV, § 405][/us/pl/89/702/s405], Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1098][/us/stat/80/1098]; 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 4, eff. Oct. 3, 1970, 35 F.R. 15627, [84 Stat. 2090][/us/stat/84/2090], related to contracts or agreements for research. See [section 1155(b) of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1155/b].

    Section 1186, [Pub. L. 89–702, title IV, § 406][/us/pl/89/702/s406], Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1098][/us/stat/80/1098], defined terms as used in this chapter. See [section 1151 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1151].

    Section 1187, [Pub. L. 89–702, title IV, § 407][/us/pl/89/702/s407], Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1098][/us/stat/80/1098], related to creation of a Pribilof Islands fund. See [section 1166 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1166].
