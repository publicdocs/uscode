## § 1169a. Annuities and survivor annuities; recomputation

    (a)

        (1)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. No subsec. (b) has been enacted. </sup></sup>  An annuity or survivor annuity based on the service of an employee or Member who performed service described in the second paragraph (13)  <sup>\[2\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 2 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of subsection (b) or subsection (l)(1)(C) of [section 8332 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8332], as added by subsections (b) and (e), respectively, of section 209 of this Act \[[Pub. L. 89–702][/us/pl/89/702]\], shall, upon application to the Office of Personnel Management, be recomputed in accordance with the second paragraph (13) of subsection (b) and subsection (l), respectively, of such section 8332, regardless of whether the employee or Member retires before, on, or after October 14, 1983.

        (2) Any recomputation of annuity under paragraph (1) of this subsection shall apply with respect to months beginning more than 30 days after the date on which application for such recomputation is received by the Office.

([Pub. L. 89–702, title II, § 212][/us/pl/89/702/s212], as added [Pub. L. 98–369, div. B, title II, § 2208(b)][/us/pl/98/369/s2208/b], July 18, 1984, [98 Stat. 1061][/us/stat/98/1061].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The second paragraph (13) of subsection (b) of [section 8332 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8332], referred to in subsec. (a)(1), relates to service performed by a Native of the Pribilof Islands. That par. (13) was added by subsec. (b) of [section 209 of Pub. L. 89–702][/us/pl/89/702/s209] (as added by [section 2 of Pub. L. 98–129][/us/pl/98/129/s2]) and is set out in the Code as the first par. (13) of subsec. (b) of [section 8332 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8332], Government Organization and Employees.

 __Codification__ 

    Another [section 212 of Pub. L. 89–702][/us/pl/89/702/s212] is classified to [section 1169b of this title][/us/usc/t16/s1169b].

 __Effective Date__ 

[Pub. L. 98–369, div. B, title II, § 2208(c)][/us/pl/98/369/s2208/c], July 18, 1984, [98 Stat. 1061][/us/stat/98/1061], provided that: 

> “The amendments made by this section \[enacting this section and amending [section 8332 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s8332], Government Organization and Employees\] shall take effect as of October 14, 1983.”
