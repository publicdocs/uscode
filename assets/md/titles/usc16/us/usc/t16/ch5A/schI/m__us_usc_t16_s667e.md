## § 667e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–79][/us/pl/97/79], § 9(b)(2), Nov. 16, 1981, [95 Stat. 1079][/us/stat/95/1079]

    Section, [act May 25, 1900, ch. 553][/us/act/1900-05-25/ch553], § 5, [31 Stat. 188][/us/stat/31/188], provided that the dead bodies of game animals or game or song birds be subject to the laws of the State into which they are transported.
