## § 4503d. Definitions

    As used in this chapter (unless the context otherwise requires):

    (1) __Institutes of Tropical Forestry__ 

        The term “Institutes of Tropical Forestry” means the Institute of Tropical Forestry in Puerto Rico and the Institute of Pacific Islands Forestry established under [section 6706 of title 7][/us/usc/t7/s6706].

    (2) __Secretary__ 

        The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture.

    (3) __State__ 

        The term “State” means each of the 50 States, Guam, American Samoa, the Republic of Palau (until the Compact of Free Association enters into effect), Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.

([Pub. L. 101–513, title VI, § 608][/us/pl/101/513/s608], as added [Pub. L. 102–574, § 2(a)(2)][/us/pl/102/574/s2/a/2], Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4595][/us/stat/106/4595].)

 __References in Text__ 

    For Oct. 1, 1994, as the date the Compact of Free Association with the Republic of Palau enters into effect, referred to in par. (3), see Proc. No. 6726, Sept. 27, 1994, 59 F.R. 49777, set out as a note under [section 1931 of Title 48][/us/usc/t48/s1931], Territories and Insular Possessions.
