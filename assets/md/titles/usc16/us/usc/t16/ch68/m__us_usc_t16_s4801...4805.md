## §§ 4801 to 4805. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 4801 to 4805 were omitted as expired pursuant to [section 4807 of this title][/us/usc/t16/s4807].

    Section 4801, [Pub. L. 102–335][/us/pl/102/335], § 2, Aug. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 859][/us/stat/106/859], related to findings, purposes, and definitions.

    Section 4802, [Pub. L. 102–335][/us/pl/102/335], § 3, Aug. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 860][/us/stat/106/860], related to Pacific yew conservation and management.

    Section 4803, [Pub. L. 102–335][/us/pl/102/335], § 4, Aug. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 861][/us/stat/106/861], related to research regarding ecology of Pacific yew, development of alternative methods of procuring taxol, and propagation of species.

    Section 4804, [Pub. L. 102–335][/us/pl/102/335], § 5, Aug. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 861][/us/stat/106/861], related to collection and sale of Pacific yew resources.

    Section 4805, [Pub. L. 102–335][/us/pl/102/335], § 6, Aug. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 862][/us/stat/106/862], related to construction of chapter with other laws.

 __Short Title__ 

    [Pub. L. 102–335][/us/pl/102/335], § 1(a), Aug. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 859][/us/stat/106/859], provided that [Pub. L. 102–335][/us/pl/102/335] (this chapter) could be cited as the “Pacific Yew Act”.
