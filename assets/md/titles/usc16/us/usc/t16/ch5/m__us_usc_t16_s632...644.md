## §§ 632 to 644. Repealed. [Feb. 26, 1944, ch. 65][/us/act/1944-02-26/ch65], § 18, [58 Stat. 104][/us/stat/58/104]

    Section 632, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 1, [37 Stat. 499][/us/stat/37/499], prohibited killing seal or sea otter in certain waters of North Pacific.

    Section 633, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 2, [37 Stat. 500][/us/stat/37/500], forbade equipping vessels for pelagic sealing or sea-otter hunting and use of ports of United States by such vessels.

    Section 634, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 3, [37 Stat. 500][/us/stat/37/500], permitted natives to carry on pelagic fishing.

    Section 635, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 4, [37 Stat. 500][/us/stat/37/500], prohibited importing illegally taken skins, and forfeiture thereof.

    Section 636, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 5, [37 Stat. 500][/us/stat/37/500]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. II, § 4(e) eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2731, [53 Stat. 1433][/us/stat/53/1433], related to regulations made by President, and enforcement of law and regulations.

    Section 637, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 6, [37 Stat. 501][/us/stat/37/501], related to punishment for violation of law, and forfeitures.

    Section 638, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 7, [37 Stat. 501][/us/stat/37/501], related to presumption as to violations.

    Section 639, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 8, [37 Stat. 501][/us/stat/37/501], related to venue of prosecutions.

    Section 640, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 9, [37 Stat. 501][/us/stat/37/501], related to guard or patrol of waters, and seizure and search of certain vessels.

    Section 641, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 10, [37 Stat. 501][/us/stat/37/501], related to seizure of vessels outside of jurisdiction of signatory powers, and procedure.

    Section 642, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 11, [37 Stat. 502][/us/stat/37/502]; [June 22, 1916, ch. 171][/us/act/1916-06-22/ch171], [39 Stat. 236][/us/stat/39/236]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. II, § 4(e), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2731, [53 Stat. 1433][/us/stat/53/1433], which related to delivery by, receipt and disposal of skins by the United States under convention of July 7, 1911, and performance of articles thereunder.

    Section 643, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 12, [37 Stat. 502][/us/stat/37/502], related to definition of “Pelagic sealing”, and construction of “person”.

    Section 643a, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373], § 13, [37 Stat. 502][/us/stat/37/502], related to continuance in force of sections 632–643.

    Section 644, R.S. § 1956; acts [Apr. 21, 1910, ch. 183][/us/act/1910-04-21/ch183], § 4, [36 Stat. 327][/us/stat/36/327]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 1, [37 Stat. 736][/us/stat/37/736]; [Jan. 13, 1925, ch. 75][/us/act/1925-01-13/ch75], §§ 2, 14, [43 Stat. 739][/us/stat/43/739], 747; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. II, § 4(e), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2731, [53 Stat. 1433][/us/stat/53/1433], forbade killing of certain fur-bearing animals in Alaska, punishment and forfeiture, power of Secretary of the Interior to authorize killing, and duty to execute law.

 __Termination of Sections__ 

    Sections 632 to 643a were terminated on the authority of former [section 643a of this title][/us/usc/t16/s643a] when Japan abrogated the treaty on Oct. 23, 1940, eff. Oct. 23, 1941. Sections 632 to 644 were later specifically repealed by [act Feb. 26, 1944, ch. 65][/us/act/1944-02-26/ch65], § 18, [58 Stat. 104][/us/stat/58/104].
