## § 2513. Limitation of use of funds

    No funds available under this chapter shall be used for the acquisition of land or interests in land.

([Pub. L. 95–625, title X, § 1014][/us/pl/95/625/s1014], Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3544][/us/stat/92/3544].)
