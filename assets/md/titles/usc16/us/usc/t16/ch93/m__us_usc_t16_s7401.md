## § 7401. Purpose

    The purpose of this chapter is to implement the Agreement on Port State Measures to Prevent, Deter and Eliminate Illegal, Unreported and Unregulated Fishing.

([Pub. L. 114–81, title III, § 302][/us/pl/114/81/s302], Nov. 5, 2015, [129 Stat. 664][/us/stat/129/664].)

 __Short Title__ 

[Pub. L. 114–81, title III, § 301][/us/pl/114/81/s301], Nov. 5, 2015, [129 Stat. 664][/us/stat/129/664], provided that: 

> “This title \[enacting this chapter\] may be cited as the ‘Port State Measures Agreement Act of 2015’.”
