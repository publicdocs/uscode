## §§ 972 to 972h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 114–81, title II][/us/pl/114/81/tII], § 210, Nov. 5, 2015, [129 Stat. 664][/us/stat/129/664]

    Section 972, [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 2, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1715][/us/stat/98/1715], provided definitions for this chapter.

    Section 972a, [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 3, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1716][/us/stat/98/1716], related to United States representation on the Council.

    Section 972b, [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 4, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1716][/us/stat/98/1716], authorized the Secretary of State to act for United States.

    Section 972c, [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 5, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1716][/us/stat/98/1716]; [Pub. L. 104–208, div. A, title I][/us/pl/104/208/dA/tI], § 101(a) \[title II, § 211(b)\], Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3009][/us/stat/110/3009], 3009–41, related to application to other laws.

    Section 972d, [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 6, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1716][/us/stat/98/1716], related to disposition of fees.

    Section 972e, [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 7, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1716][/us/stat/98/1716], related to regulations.

    Section 972f, [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 8, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1717][/us/stat/98/1717], related to prohibited acts.

    Section 972g, [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 9, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1717][/us/stat/98/1717]; [Pub. L. 101–650, title III][/us/pl/101/650/tIII], § 321, Dec. 1, 1990, [104 Stat. 5117][/us/stat/104/5117], related to enforcement.

    Section 972h, [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 10, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1719][/us/stat/98/1719], authorized appropriations.

 __Short Title__ 

    [Pub. L. 98–445][/us/pl/98/445], § 1, Oct. 4, 1984, [98 Stat. 1715][/us/stat/98/1715], which provided that this Act \[enacting this chapter\] could be cited as the “Eastern Pacific Tuna Licensing Act of 1984”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 114–81, title II][/us/pl/114/81/tII], § 210, Nov. 5, 2015, [129 Stat. 664][/us/stat/129/664].
