## § 971k. Savings clause

    Nothing in this chapter shall have the effect of diminishing the rights and obligations of any Nation under Article VIII(3) of the Convention.

([Pub. L. 94–70, § 12][/us/pl/94/70/s12], as added [Pub. L. 104–43, title III, § 308][/us/pl/104/43/s308], Nov. 3, 1995, [109 Stat. 387][/us/stat/109/387]; amended [Pub. L. 105–384, title II, § 202(b)(1)(E)][/us/pl/105/384/s202/b/1/E], Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3453][/us/stat/112/3453].)
