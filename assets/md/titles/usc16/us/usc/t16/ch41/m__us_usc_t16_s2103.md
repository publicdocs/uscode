## § 2103. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–79, title VIII][/us/pl/113/79/tVIII], § 8001(a), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 913][/us/stat/128/913]

    Section, [Pub. L. 95–313][/us/pl/95/313], § 4, as added [Pub. L. 107–171, title VIII][/us/pl/107/171/tVIII], § 8002(b), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 468][/us/stat/116/468], related to the establishment and implementation of a forest land enhancement program.

    A prior section 2103, [Pub. L. 95–313][/us/pl/95/313], § 4, July 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 367][/us/stat/92/367]; [Pub. L. 101–624, title XII][/us/pl/101/624/tXII], §§ 1214, 1224(1), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3525][/us/stat/104/3525], 3542; [Pub. L. 104–127, title III][/us/pl/104/127/tIII], §§ 336(a)(2)(B), 373, Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1005][/us/stat/110/1005], 1015, related to forestry incentives program, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 107–171, title VIII][/us/pl/107/171/tVIII], § 8001(a), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 468][/us/stat/116/468].

 __Purposes of Forest Land Enhancement Program__ 

    [Pub. L. 107–171, title VIII][/us/pl/107/171/tVIII], § 8002(a), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 468][/us/stat/116/468], which listed purposes for the establishment of a forest land enhancement program, was repealed by [Pub. L. 113–79, title VIII][/us/pl/113/79/tVIII], § 8001(b), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 913][/us/stat/128/913].
