## CHAPTER 2—THE TARIFF COMMISSION

 __Codification__ 

    Sections related to the United States Tariff Commission as it existed prior to [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497], [46 Stat. 696][/us/stat/46/696].

 __Change of Name__ 

    United States Tariff Commission redesignated United States International Trade Commission by [Pub. L. 93–618, title I, § 171][/us/pl/93/618/s171], Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2009][/us/stat/88/2009]. See [section 2231(a) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2231/a].

§§ 91, 92. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)__  __][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a]__  __,__  __Sept. 6, 1966__  __,__  __[__  __80 Stat. 643__  __][/us/stat/80/643]__ 

    Section 91, [act Sept. 8, 1916, ch. 463, § 700][/us/act/1916-09-08/ch463/s700], [39 Stat. 795][/us/stat/39/795], related to organization of the Commission. See [section 1330 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1330].

    Section 92, act Sept. 8, 1916, § 701, [39 Stat. 795][/us/stat/39/795], related to general power and personnel of the Commission. See [section 1331 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1331].
