## § 2494. Progress reports

    The President shall include as a part of the annual report required under [section 2291h of title 22][/us/usc/t22/s2291h] an evaluation of progress that each major drug producing country and each major drug-transit country has made during the reporting period in achieving the objectives set forth in [section 2492(b) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2492/b].

([Pub. L. 93–618, title VIII][/us/pl/93/618/tVIII], § 804, as added [Pub. L. 99–570, title IX][/us/pl/99/570/tIX], § 9001, Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–166][/us/stat/100/3207-166]; amended [Pub. L. 106–36, title I][/us/pl/106/36/tI], § 1001(a)(9), June 25, 1999, [113 Stat. 131][/us/stat/113/131].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1999—[Pub. L. 106–36][/us/pl/106/36] substituted “[section 2291h of title 22][/us/usc/t22/s2291h]” for “[section 2291(e)(1) of title 22][/us/usc/t22/s2291/e/1]”.
