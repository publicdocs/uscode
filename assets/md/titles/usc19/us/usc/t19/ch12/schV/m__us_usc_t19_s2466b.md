## § 2466b. Termination of benefits for sub-Saharan African countries

    In the case of a beneficiary sub-Saharan African country, as defined in section 2466a(c)  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title, duty-free treatment provided under this subchapter shall remain in effect through September 30, 2025.

([Pub. L. 93–618, title V][/us/pl/93/618/tV], § 506B, as added [Pub. L. 106–200, title I][/us/pl/106/200/tI], § 114, May 18, 2000, [114 Stat. 266][/us/stat/114/266]; amended [Pub. L. 108–274][/us/pl/108/274], § 7(a)(1), July 13, 2004, [118 Stat. 823][/us/stat/118/823]; [Pub. L. 114–27, title I][/us/pl/114/27/tI], § 103(a), June 29, 2015, [129 Stat. 364][/us/stat/129/364].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 2466a(c) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2466a/c], referred to in text, was redesignated [section 2466a(e) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2466a/e] by [Pub. L. 114–27, title I][/us/pl/114/27/tI], § 105(b), (c), June 29, 2015, [129 Stat. 366][/us/stat/129/366].

 __Amendments__ 

    2015—[Pub. L. 114–27][/us/pl/114/27] substituted “September 30, 2025” for “September 30, 2015”.

    2004—[Pub. L. 108–274][/us/pl/108/274] substituted “2015” for “2008”.
