## § 420. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–456, title III, § 303(c)][/us/pl/87/456/s303/c], May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 78][/us/stat/76/78]

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    For effective date of repeal, see [section 501(a) of Pub. L. 87–456][/us/pl/87/456/s501/a], set out as an Effective Date of Tariff Classification Act of 1962 note preceding [section 1202 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1202].
