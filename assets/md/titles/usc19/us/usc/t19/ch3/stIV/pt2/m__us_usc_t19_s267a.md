## § 267a. Foreign language proficiency awards

    Cash awards for foreign language proficiency may, under regulations prescribed by the Secretary of the Treasury, be paid to customs officers (as referred to in [section 267(e)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s267/e/1]) to the same extent and in the same manner as would be allowable under subchapter III of chapter 45 of title 5 with respect to law enforcement officers (as defined by section 4521 of such title).

([Pub. L. 103–66, title XIII, § 13812(b)][/us/pl/103/66/s13812/b], Aug. 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 671][/us/stat/107/671].)

> “Subsection (b) \[enacting this section\] takes effect on January 1, 1994.”
