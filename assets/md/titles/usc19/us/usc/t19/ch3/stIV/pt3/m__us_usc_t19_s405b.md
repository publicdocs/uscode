## § 405b. Omitted

    Section, [act May 28, 1926, ch. 411, § 2][/us/act/1926-05-28/ch411/s2], [44 Stat. 669][/us/stat/44/669], provided that the jurisdiction, powers, and duties of the Board referred to in [section 405a of this title][/us/usc/t19/s405a], its subdivisions and its officers, and their appointment, including the designation of its presiding officers, and the immunities, tenure of office, powers, duties, rights, and privileges of the members of the Board, shall remain the same as provided by existing law.

§§ 406 to 409. __Repealed.__  __[__  __June 17, 1930, ch. 497__  __][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497]__  __, title IV, § 651(a)(1), 46 Stat 762, eff.__  __June 18, 1930__ 

    Sections, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356][/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356], title IV, §§ 519, 520(a), (b), 521, [42 Stat. 973][/us/stat/42/973], related to copies of decisions of United States Customs Court for Collectors and Secretary of the Treasury, refund of duties and liquidation of duties.

    Provisions of Tariff Act of 1930 corresponding to section 406, see [section 1519 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1519] \[repealed\]; section 407, see [section 1520(a) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1520/a]; section 408, see [section 1520(b) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1520/b]; section 409, see [section 1521 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1521].

§§ 413 to 419. __Repealed.__  __[__  __June 17, 1930, ch. 497__  __][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497]__  __, title IV, §§ 641(e), 651(a)(1),__  __[__  __46 Stat. 760__  __][/us/stat/46/760]__  __, 762, eff.__  __June 18, 1930__ 

    Sections 413 and 414, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356][/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356], title IV §§ 524, 525, [42 Stat. 975][/us/stat/42/975], related to disposal of receipts and detail from field service for Washington duty.

Sections 415 to 419, [act June 10, 1910, ch. 283][/us/act/1910-06-10/ch283], §§ 1–5, [36 Stat. 464][/us/stat/36/464], 465, related to issuance and revocation of customshouse broker’s licenses. Repeal was subject to an exception as follows: 

> “Except that any license issued \* \* \* shall continue in force and effect, subject to suspension and revocation in the same manner and upon the same conditions as licenses issued pursuant to subdivision (a) of this section.”

 Subdivision (a) is set forth in [section 1641 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1641], and such section now regulates the licensing of customhouse brokers. Subsection (e) of section 641, act of 1930, the repealing act, was repealed by [act August 26, 1935, ch. 689, § 5][/us/act/1935-08-26/ch689/s5], [49 Stat. 865][/us/stat/49/865], but sections 415 to 419 of this title were not thereby revived.

    Provisions of Tariff Act of 1930 corresponding to section 413, see [section 1524 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1524]; section 414, see section 1525 \[repealed\] of this title; sections 415 to 419, see [section 1641 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1641].
