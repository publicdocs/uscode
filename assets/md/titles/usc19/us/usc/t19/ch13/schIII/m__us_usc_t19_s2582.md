## § 2582. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–67, title II, § 214(d)][/us/pl/98/67/s214/d], Aug. 5, 1983, [97 Stat. 393][/us/stat/97/393]

    Section, [Pub. L. 96–39, title XI, § 1112][/us/pl/96/39/s1112], July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 316][/us/stat/93/316], authorized payment of compensation to losses in taxes resulting from concessions granted by United States in Tokyo Round of Multilateral Trade Negotiations on articles produced by United States possessions on which excise taxes are levied by United States.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Aug. 5, 1983, see [section 218(a) of Pub. L. 98–67][/us/pl/98/67/s218/a], which is classified to [section 2706 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2706].
