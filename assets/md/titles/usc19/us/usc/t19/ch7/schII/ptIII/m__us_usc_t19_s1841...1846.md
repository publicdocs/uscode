## §§ 1841 to 1846. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI][/us/pl/93/618/tVI], § 602(d), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072][/us/stat/88/2072]

    Section 1841, [Pub. L. 87–794, title II][/us/pl/87/794/tII], § 221, Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 874][/us/stat/76/874], made provision for the giving of advice by the Tariff Commission \[now the United States International Trade Commission\] concerning trade agreements. See [section 2151 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2151].

    Section 1842, [Pub. L. 87–794, title II][/us/pl/87/794/tII], § 222, Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 875][/us/stat/76/875], made provision for the giving of advice by other sources concerning trade agreements. See [section 2152 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2152].

    Section 1843, [Pub. L. 87–794, title II][/us/pl/87/794/tII], § 223, Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 875][/us/stat/76/875], provided for public hearings in connection with proposed trade agreements. See [section 2153 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2153].

    Section 1844, [Pub. L. 87–794, title II][/us/pl/87/794/tII], § 224, Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 875][/us/stat/76/875], set out prerequisites for offers for modification or continuance of duties or other import restrictions, or continuance of duty-free or excise treatment. See [section 2154 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2154].

    Section 1845, [Pub. L. 87–794, title II][/us/pl/87/794/tII], § 225, Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 876][/us/stat/76/876], provided for the reservation of articles from trade negotiations. See [section 2137 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2137].

    Section 1846, [Pub. L. 87–794, title II][/us/pl/87/794/tII], § 226, Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 876][/us/stat/76/876], provided for the transmission of agreements to Congress. See [section 2212 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2212].
