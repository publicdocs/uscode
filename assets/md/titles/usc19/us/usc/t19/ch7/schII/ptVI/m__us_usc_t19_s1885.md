## § 1885. Termination of proclamations

    (a) Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(d)][/us/pl/93/618/s602/d], Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072][/us/stat/88/2072].

    (b) The President may at any time terminate, in whole or in part, any proclamation made under this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 87–794, title II, § 255][/us/pl/87/794/s255], Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 880][/us/stat/76/880]; [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(d)][/us/pl/93/618/s602/d], Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072][/us/stat/88/2072].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1975—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 93–618][/us/pl/93/618] struck out subsec. (a) which provided for termination of or withdrawal from trade agreements. See [section 2135 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s2135].
