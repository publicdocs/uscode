## § 1887. Limitation on imports under [section 624 of title 7][/us/usc/t7/s624]

    Nothing contained in this chapter shall be construed to affect in any way the provisions of [section 624 of title 7][/us/usc/t7/s624], or to apply to any import restriction heretofore or hereafter imposed under such section.

([Pub. L. 87–794, title II][/us/pl/87/794/tII], § 257(h), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 883][/us/stat/76/883].)
