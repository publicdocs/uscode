## § 3334. Prohibition on drawback for television picture tubes

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no customs duties may be refunded, waived, or reduced on color cathode-ray television picture tubes, including video monitor cathode-ray tubes (provided for in subheading 8540.11.00 of the HTS), that are nonoriginating goods under [section 3332(p)(19) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s3332/p/19] and are—

    (A) exported to a NAFTA country;

    (B) used as a material in the production of other goods that are exported to a NAFTA country; or

    (C) substituted for by goods of the same kind and quality used as a material in the production of other goods that are exported to a NAFTA country.

([Pub. L. 103–182, title II, § 210][/us/pl/103/182/s210], Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2099][/us/stat/107/2099].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective on the date the North American Free Trade Agreement enters into force with respect to the United States \[Jan. 1, 1994\], see [section 213(b) of Pub. L. 103–182][/us/pl/103/182/s213/b], set out as a note under [section 3331 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s3331].
