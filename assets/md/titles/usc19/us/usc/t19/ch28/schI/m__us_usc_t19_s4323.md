## § 4323. Appropriate congressional committees defined

    In this subchapter, the term “appropriate congressional committees” means—

    (1) the Committee on Finance and the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs of the Senate; and

    (2) the Committee on Ways and Means and the Committee on Homeland Security of the House of Representatives.

([Pub. L. 114–125, title I][/us/pl/114/125/tI], § 118, Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 145][/us/stat/130/145].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 114–125][/us/pl/114/125], Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 127][/us/stat/130/127], which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Tables.
