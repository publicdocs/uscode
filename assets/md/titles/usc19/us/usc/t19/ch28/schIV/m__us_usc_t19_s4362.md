## § 4362. Application to Canada and Mexico

    Pursuant to article 1902 of the North American Free Trade Agreement and [section 3438 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s3438], this subchapter and the amendments made by this subchapter shall apply with respect to goods from Canada and Mexico.

([Pub. L. 114–125, title IV][/us/pl/114/125/tIV], § 403, Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 156][/us/stat/130/156].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title IV of [Pub. L. 114–125][/us/pl/114/125], Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 155][/us/stat/130/155], which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title IV to the Code, see [section 401 of Pub. L. 114–125][/us/pl/114/125/s401], set out as a Short Title of 2016 Amendment note under [section 4301 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s4301], and Tables.
