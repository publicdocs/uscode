## § 49. Repealed. [Aug. 26, 1935, ch. 689, § 1][/us/act/1935-08-26/ch689/s1], [49 Stat. 864][/us/stat/49/864]

    Section, R.S. § 1790, related to restriction on payment for services of officers or other persons in customs service.

§§ 50, 51. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)__  __][/us/pl/89/554/s8/a]__  __,__  __Sept. 6, 1966__  __,__  __[__  __80 Stat. 642__  __][/us/stat/80/642]__  __, 645, 650__ 

    Section 50, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 355, § 8][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch355/s8], [37 Stat. 487][/us/stat/37/487]; [June 6, 1939, ch. 185][/us/act/1939-06-06/ch185], [53 Stat. 810][/us/stat/53/810], authorized collectors of customs to administer oaths to expense accounts.

    Section 51, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 251, § 7][/us/act/1923-03-04/ch251/s7], [42 Stat. 1454][/us/stat/42/1454], provided limitations on compensation.

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Section 51 was additionally repealed by [Pub. L. 91–271, title III, § 321(j)][/us/pl/91/271/s321/j], June 2, 1970, [84 Stat. 293][/us/stat/84/293].
