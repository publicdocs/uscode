###### §§ 50, 51. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 642][/us/stat/80/642], 645, 650

    Section 50, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 355][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch355], § 8, [37 Stat. 487][/us/stat/37/487]; [June 6, 1939, ch. 185][/us/act/1939-06-06/ch185], [53 Stat. 810][/us/stat/53/810], authorized collectors of customs to administer oaths to expense accounts.

    Section 51, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 251][/us/act/1923-03-04/ch251], § 7, [42 Stat. 1454][/us/stat/42/1454], provided limitations on compensation.

 __Additional Repeal__ 

    Section 51 was additionally repealed by [Pub. L. 91–271, title III][/us/pl/91/271/tIII], § 321(j), June 2, 1970, [84 Stat. 293][/us/stat/84/293].
