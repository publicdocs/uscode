## § 63. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 636][/us/stat/80/636]

    Section, [act Aug. 28, 1890, ch. 812][/us/act/1890-08-28/ch812], §§ 1, 2, [26 Stat. 362][/us/stat/26/362], provided for leaves of absence of officers and employees in customs service who receive per diem compensation.

    Section was additionally repealed by [Pub. L. 91–271, title III][/us/pl/91/271/tIII], § 321(k), June 2, 1970, [84 Stat. 293][/us/stat/84/293].
