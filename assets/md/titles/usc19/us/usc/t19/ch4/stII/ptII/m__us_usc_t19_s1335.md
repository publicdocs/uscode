## § 1335. Rules and regulations

    The commission is authorized to adopt such reasonable procedures and rules and regulations as it deems necessary to carry out its functions and duties.

([June 17, 1930, ch. 497][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497], title III, § 335, as added Aug. 20, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–686][/us/pl/85/686], § 9(c)(2), [72 Stat. 680][/us/stat/72/680].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 335 of act June 17, 1930, related to disclosure of trade secrets and prescribed penalty therefor, prior to repeal by [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645], § 21, [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862], eff. Sept. 1, 1948. See [section 1905 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s1905], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.
