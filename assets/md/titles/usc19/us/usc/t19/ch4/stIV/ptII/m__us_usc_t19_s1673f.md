## § 1673f. Treatment of difference between deposit of estimated antidumping duty and final assessed duty under antidumping duty order

    (a) __Deposit of estimated antidumping duty under__  __[__  __section 1673b(d)(1)(B) of this title__  __][/us/usc/t19/s1673b/d/1/B]__ 

    If the amount of a cash deposit, or the amount of any bond or other security, required as security for an estimated antidumping duty under [section 1673b(d)(1)(B) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1673b/d/1/B] is different from the amount of the antidumping duty determined under an antidumping duty order published under [section 1673e of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1673e], then the difference for entries of merchandise entered, or withdrawn from warehouse, for consumption before notice of the affirmative determination of the Commission under [section 1673d(b) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1673d/b] is published shall be—

        (1) disregarded, to the extent that the cash deposit, bond, or other security is lower than the duty under the order, or

        (2) refunded or released, to the extent that the cash deposit, bond, or other security is higher than the duty under the order.

    (b) __Deposit of estimated antidumping duty under__  __[__  __section 1673e(a)(3) of this title__  __][/us/usc/t19/s1673e/a/3]__ 

    If the amount of an estimated antidumping duty deposited under [section 1673e(a)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1673e/a/3] is different from the amount of the antidumping duty determined under an antidumping duty order published under [section 1673e of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1673e], then the difference for entries of merchandise entered, or withdrawn from warehouse, for consumption after notice of the affirmative determination of the Commission under [section 1673d(b) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1673d/b] is published shall be—

        (1) collected, to the extent that the deposit under [section 1673e(a)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1673e/a/3] is lower than the duty determined under the order, or

        (2) refunded, to the extent that the deposit under [section 1673e(a)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1673e/a/3] is higher than the duty determined under the order,

        together with interest as provided by [section 1677g of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1677g].

([June 17, 1930, ch. 497][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497], title VII, § 737, as added [Pub. L. 96–39, title I][/us/pl/96/39/tI], § 101, July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 173][/us/stat/93/173]; amended [Pub.     Subsec. (a)(1). [Pub. L. 104–295][/us/pl/104/295], § 40(2), substituted “that the cash deposit, bond, or other security” for “the cash deposit collected”.

    Subsec. (a)(2). [Pub. L. 104–295][/us/pl/104/295], § 40(3), substituted “refunded or released, to the extent that the cash deposit, bond, or other security” for “refunded, to the extent the cash deposit”.
