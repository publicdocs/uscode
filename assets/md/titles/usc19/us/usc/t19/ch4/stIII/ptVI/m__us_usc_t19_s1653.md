## § 1653. Effective date of chapter

    Except as otherwise provided, this chapter shall take effect on June 18, 1930.

([June 17, 1930, ch. 497][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497], title IV, § 653, [46 Stat. 763][/us/stat/46/763].)
