## § 1673i. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–573, title VI, § 622(a)(1)][/us/pl/98/573/s622/a/1], Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 3039][/us/stat/98/3039]

    Section, [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497], title VII, § 740, as added July 26, 1979, [Pub. L. 96–39, title I, § 101][/us/pl/96/39/s101], [93 Stat. 175][/us/stat/93/175], provided that the antidumping duty imposed by [section 1673 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1673] was to be treated as a normal customs duty for drawback purposes. See [section 1677h of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1677h].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Section repealed effective Oct. 30, 1984, see [section 626(a) of Pub. L. 98–573][/us/pl/98/573/s626/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1984 Amendment note under [section 1671 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1671].
