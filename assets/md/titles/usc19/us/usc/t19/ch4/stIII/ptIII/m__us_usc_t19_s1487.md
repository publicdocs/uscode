## § 1487. Value in entry; amendment

    The importer of record or his agent may, under such regulations as the Secretary of the Treasury may prescribe, at the time entry is made, make in the entry such additions to or deductions from the cost or value given in the invoice as, in his opinion, may raise or lower the same to the value of such merchandise.

([June 17, 1930, ch. 497][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497], title IV, § 487, [46 Stat. 725][/us/stat/46/725]; [Aug. 8, 1953, ch. 397, § 18(a)][/us/act/1953-08-08/ch397/s18/a], [67 Stat. 517][/us/stat/67/517]; [Pub. L. 97–446, title II, § 201(e)][/us/pl/97/446/s201/e], Jan. 12, 1983, [96 Stat. 2350][/us/stat/96/2350].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions somewhat similar to those in this section were contained in [act Oct. 3, 1913, ch. 16][/us/act/1913-10-03/ch16], § III, I, [38 Stat. 184][/us/stat/38/184], which were substituted for provisions made by the Customs Administrative [Act of June 10, 1890, ch. 407, § 7][/us/act/1890-06-10/ch407/s7], [26 Stat. 134][/us/stat/26/134], as amended by [act July 24, 1897, ch. 11, § 32][/us/act/1897-07-24/ch11/s32], [30 Stat. 211][/us/stat/30/211], and as further amended by the Payne-Aldrich Tariff [Act of Aug. 5, 1909, ch. 6, § 28][/us/act/1909-08-05/ch6/s28], [36 Stat. 95][/us/stat/36/95]. Section III of the act of 1913 was superseded by [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356][/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356], title IV, § 487, [42 Stat. 962][/us/stat/42/962], and was repealed by section 643 thereof. Section 487 of the 1922 act was superseded by section 487 of act June 17, 1930, comprising this section, and repealed by section 651(a)(1) of the 1930 act.

    Provisions for addition to the invoice values made by R.S. § 2900, were repealed by section 29 of the Customs Administrative Act.

 __Amendments__ 

    1983—[Pub. L. 97–446][/us/pl/97/446] substituted “importer of record” for “consignee” before “or his agent”.

    1953—Act Aug. 8, 1953, struck out “or at any time before the invoice or the merchandise has come under the observation of the appraiser for the purpose of appraisement,” after “at the time entry is made,”.

 __Effective Date of 1983 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 97–446][/us/pl/97/446] applicable with respect to merchandise entered on and after 30th day after Jan. 12, 1983, see [section 201(g) of Pub. L. 97–446][/us/pl/97/446/s201/g], set out as a note under [section 1484 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1484].

 __Effective Date of 1953 Amendment; Savings Provision__ 

    Amendment by act Aug. 8, 1953, effective on and after thirtieth day following Aug. 8, 1953, and savings provision, see notes set out under [section 1304 of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1304].
