## § 1613a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–514, title XVIII][/us/pl/99/514/tXVIII], § 1888(7), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2925][/us/stat/100/2925]

    Section, [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497, title IV][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497/tIV], § 613a, as added and amended Oct. 12, 1984, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II][/us/pl/98/473/tII], §§ 317, 2304, [98 Stat. 2054][/us/stat/98/2054], 2193; Oct. 27, 1986, [Pub. L. 100–71, title I][/us/pl/100/71/tI], § 101, [101 Stat. 438][/us/stat/101/438], related to establishment, purpose, etc. of the Customs Forfeiture Fund. See [section 1613b of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1613b].

    [Section 1152(b)(1) of Pub. L. 99–570][/us/pl/99/570/s1152/b/1], which amended this section subsequently to repeal by [Pub. L. 99–514][/us/pl/99/514], was repealed by [section 101 of Pub. L. 100–71][/us/pl/100/71/s101], which also provided in part that [section 1152(b) of Pub. L. 99–570][/us/pl/99/570/s1152/b] be treated as though it had never been enacted.
