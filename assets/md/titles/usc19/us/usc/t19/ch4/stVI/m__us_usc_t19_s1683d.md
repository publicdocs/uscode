## § 1683d. Reconciliation

    The Secretary of the Treasury shall conduct reconciliations to ensure the proper implementation and operation of international agreements entered into between a country of export of softwood lumber or softwood lumber products described in [section 1683b(a) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1683b/a] and the United States. The Secretary of Treasury shall reconcile the following:

    (1) The export price declared by a United States importer pursuant to [section 1683a(b)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1683a/b/1] with the export price reported to the United States by the country of export, if any.

    (2) The export price declared by a United States importer pursuant to [section 1683a(b)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1683a/b/1] with the revised export price reported to the United States by the country of export, if any.

([June 17, 1930, ch. 497][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497], title VIII, § 806, as added [Pub. L. 110–246, title III][/us/pl/110/246/tIII], § 3301(a), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1851][/us/stat/122/1851].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 1683a of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1683a], referred to in pars. (1) and (2), was in the original section “803”, and was translated as meaning the section 803 of act June 17, 1930, as added by [section 3301(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s3301/a], to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
