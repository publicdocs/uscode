## § 1616. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–570, title I, § 1863(b)][/us/pl/99/570/s1863/b], Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–54][/us/stat/100/3207-54]

    Section, [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497], title IV, § 616, as added Oct. 12, 1984, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 318][/us/pl/98/473/s318], [98 Stat. 2055][/us/stat/98/2055], related to disposition of forfeited property.

    Another section 616 of act June 17, 1930, as added by [Pub. L. 98–573, title II, § 213(a)(4)][/us/pl/98/573/s213/a/4], Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2987][/us/stat/98/2987], is classified to [section 1616a of this title][/us/usc/t19/s1616a].

    A prior section 616 of [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497][/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497], title IV, [46 Stat. 757][/us/stat/46/757], related to prohibition against compromising Government claims and was classified to this section, prior to repeal by [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 24][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s24], [62 Stat. 682][/us/stat/62/682], eff. Sept. 1, 1948. See [section 1915 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s1915], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.
