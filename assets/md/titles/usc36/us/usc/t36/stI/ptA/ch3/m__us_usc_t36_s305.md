## § 305. National tree

    The tree genus Quercus, commonly known as the oak tree, is the national tree.

(Added [Pub. L. 108–447, div. J, title I][/us/pl/108/447/dJ/tI], § 109(a), Dec. 8, 2004, [118 Stat. 3344][/us/stat/118/3344].)
