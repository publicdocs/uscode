## § 80306. Restrictions

    (a)  __Profit__  __.—__ 

    The corporation may not operate for profit.

    (b)  __Political Activities__  __.—__ 

    The corporation shall be nonpolitical and nonsectarian.

([Pub. L. 105–225][/us/pl/105/225], Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1362][/us/stat/112/1362].)
