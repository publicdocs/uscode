## § 170301. Definition

    For purposes of this chapter, “State” includes the District of Columbia and the territories and possessions of the United States.

([Pub. L. 105–225][/us/pl/105/225], Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1442][/us/stat/112/1442].)
