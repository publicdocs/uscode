## § 90108. Annual audit

    The foundation shall enter into a contract with an independent auditor to conduct an annual audit of the foundation.

(Added [Pub. L. 107–252, title VI][/us/pl/107/252/tVI], § 601(a), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1721][/us/stat/116/1721], § 152608; renumbered § 90108, [Pub. L. 113–237][/us/pl/113/237], § 3(c)(6)(B), Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 2840][/us/stat/128/2840].)

 __Amendments__
