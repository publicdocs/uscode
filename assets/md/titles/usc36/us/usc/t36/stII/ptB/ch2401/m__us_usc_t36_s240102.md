## § 240102. Organization

    (a)  __Federal Charter__  __.—__ 

    Women’s Army Corps Veterans’ Association (in this chapter, the “corporation”), a nonprofit corporation incorporated in the District of Columbia, is a federally chartered corporation.

    (b)  __Expiration of Charter__  __.—__ 

    If the corporation does not comply with any provision of this chapter, the charter granted by this chapter expires.

([Pub. L. 105–225][/us/pl/105/225], Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1488][/us/stat/112/1488].)
