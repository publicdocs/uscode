## § 50108. Duty to maintain tax-exempt status

    The corporation shall maintain its status as an organization exempt from taxation under the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 ([26 U.S.C. 1][/us/usc/t26/s1] et seq.). If the corporation does not maintain that status, the charter granted by this chapter expires.

([Pub. L. 105–225][/us/pl/105/225], Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1343][/us/stat/112/1343].)
