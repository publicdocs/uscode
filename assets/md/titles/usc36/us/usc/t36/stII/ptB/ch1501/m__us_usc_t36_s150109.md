## § 150109. Duty to maintain corporate and tax-exempt status

    (a)  __Corporate Status__  __.—__ 

    The corporation shall maintain its status as a corporation incorporated under the laws of the District of Columbia.

    (b)  __Tax-Exempt Status__  __.—__ 

    The corporation shall maintain its status as an organization exempt from taxation under the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 ([26 U.S.C. 1][/us/usc/t26/s1] et seq.).

([Pub. L. 105–225][/us/pl/105/225], Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1387][/us/stat/112/1387].)
