## § 153901. Organization

    The National Yeomen (F) (in this chapter, the “corporation”) is a body corporate and politic in the District of Columbia.

([Pub. L. 105–225][/us/pl/105/225], Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1431][/us/stat/112/1431]; [Pub. L. 113–237, § 3(c)(8)(B)][/us/pl/113/237/s3/c/8/B], Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 2840][/us/stat/128/2840].)
