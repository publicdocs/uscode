## § 152608. Annual audit

    The foundation shall enter into a contract with an independent auditor to conduct an annual audit of the foundation.

(Added [Pub. L. 107–252, title VI, § 601(a)][/us/pl/107/252/s601/a], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1721][/us/stat/116/1721].)
