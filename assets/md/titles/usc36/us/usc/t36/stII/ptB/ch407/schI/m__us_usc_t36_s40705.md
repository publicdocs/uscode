## § 40705. Duty to maintain tax-exempt status

    The corporation shall be operated in a manner and for purposes that qualify the corporation for exemption from taxation under section 501(a) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 ([26 U.S.C. 501(a)][/us/usc/t26/s501/a]) as an organization described in section 501(c)(3) of that Code ([26 U.S.C. 501(c)(3)][/us/usc/t26/s501/c/3]).

([Pub. L. 105–225][/us/pl/105/225], Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1336][/us/stat/112/1336].)
