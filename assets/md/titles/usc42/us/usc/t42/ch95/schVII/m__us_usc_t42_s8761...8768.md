## §§ 8761 to 8768. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 8761 to 8768 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under [section 8791 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s8791].

    Section 8761, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 161, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 670][/us/stat/94/670], related to penalty for false statements.

    Section 8762, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 162, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 670][/us/stat/94/670], related to penalty for forgery.

    Section 8763, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 163, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 671][/us/stat/94/671], related to penalties and injunctive relief for misappropriation of funds and for unauthorized activities.

    Section 8764, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 164, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 671][/us/stat/94/671], related to penalty for conspiracy.

    Section 8765, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 165, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 672][/us/stat/94/672], related to deceptive use of corporate name.

    Section 8766, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 166, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 672][/us/stat/94/672], authorized an action for damages by Corporation in addition to penalties prescribed for violations.

    Section 8767, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 167, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 672][/us/stat/94/672], related to actions by Attorney General.

    Section 8768, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 168, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 672][/us/stat/94/672]; [Pub. L. 98–473, title I][/us/pl/98/473/tI], § 101(c) \[title II, § 201\], Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 1837][/us/stat/98/1837], 1860, related to civil actions against Corporation.
