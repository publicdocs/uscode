## §§ 8731 to 8740. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 8731 to 8740 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under [section 8791 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s8791].

    Section 8731, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 131, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 654][/us/stat/94/654], authorized financial assistance.

    Section 8732, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 132, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 658][/us/stat/94/658], related to loans made by Corporation.

    Section 8733, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 133, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 660][/us/stat/94/660], related to loan guarantees made by Corporation.

    Section 8734, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 134, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 661][/us/stat/94/661], related to price guarantees by Corporation.

    Section 8735, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 135, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 661][/us/stat/94/661], related to purchase agreements made by Corporation.

    Section 8736, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 136, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 662][/us/stat/94/662], related to joint ventures by Corporation.

    Section 8737, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 137, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 663][/us/stat/94/663], related to control of assets.

    Section 8738, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 138, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 665][/us/stat/94/665], related to unlawful contracts.

    Section 8739, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 139, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 665][/us/stat/94/665], related to fees and application of receipts.

    Section 8740, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 140, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 665][/us/stat/94/665], related to disposition of securities.
