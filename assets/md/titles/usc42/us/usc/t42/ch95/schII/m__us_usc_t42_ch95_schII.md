### SUBCHAPTER II—ESTABLISHMENT OF CORPORATION

§§ 8711 to 8719. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 8711 to 8719 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under [section 8791 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s8791].

    Section 8711, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 115][/us/pl/96/294/s115], June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 636][/us/stat/94/636], created the Corporation, provided for its offices and residence status, and referred to its general powers.

    Section 8712, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 116][/us/pl/96/294/s116], June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 636][/us/stat/94/636], provided for board of directors of Corporation.

    Section 8713, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 117][/us/pl/96/294/s117], June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 638][/us/stat/94/638]; [Pub. L. 98–473, title I, § 101(c)][/us/pl/98/473/s101/c] \[title II, § 201\], Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 1837][/us/stat/98/1837], 1860, related to officers and employees of Corporation.

    Section 8714, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 118][/us/pl/96/294/s118], June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 638][/us/stat/94/638], related to conflicts of interest and financial disclosure.

    Section 8715, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 119][/us/pl/96/294/s119], June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 639][/us/stat/94/639], related to delegation of authority and transfer of functions.

    Section 8716, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 120][/us/pl/96/294/s120], June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 640][/us/stat/94/640], authorized administrative expenses.

    Section 8717, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 121][/us/pl/96/294/s121], June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 641][/us/stat/94/641], related to public access to information.

    Section 8718, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 122][/us/pl/96/294/s122], June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 641][/us/stat/94/641], related to Inspector General of Corporation.

    Section 8719, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 123][/us/pl/96/294/s123], June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 644][/us/stat/94/644], established an Advisory Committee to the Board of Directors. For continuation of Advisory Committee, see [section 7404(c) of Pub. L. 99–272][/us/pl/99/272/s7404/c], set out as a note under [section 8791 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s8791].
