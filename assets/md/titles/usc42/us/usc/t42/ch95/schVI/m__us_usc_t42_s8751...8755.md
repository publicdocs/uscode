## §§ 8751 to 8755. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 8751 to 8755 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under [section 8791 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s8791].

    Section 8751, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 151, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 667][/us/stat/94/667], related to issue by Corporation of obligations purchasable by United States only.

    Section 8752, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 152, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 668][/us/stat/94/668], related to limitations on total amount of obligational authority.

    Section 8753, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 153, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 669][/us/stat/94/669], related to budgetary treatment.

    Section 8754, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 154, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 669][/us/stat/94/669], related to receipts of Corporation.

    Section 8755, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 155, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 669][/us/stat/94/669], related to tax status of Corporation.
