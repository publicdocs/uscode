## §§ 8771 to 8780. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 8771 to 8780 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under [section 8791 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s8791].

    Section 8771, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 171, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 673][/us/stat/94/673], related to general powers of Corporation.

    Section 8772, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 172, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 674][/us/stat/94/674], related to coordination by Corporation with Federal entities.

    Section 8773, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 173, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 675][/us/stat/94/675], related to patents.

    Section 8774, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 174, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 676][/us/stat/94/676], related to utilization of small and disadvantaged businesses.

    Section 8775, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 175, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 676][/us/stat/94/676]; [Pub. L. 98–426][/us/pl/98/426], § 27(d)(2), Sept. 28, 1984, [98 Stat. 1654][/us/stat/98/1654], provided relationship to other Federal laws.

    Section 8776, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 176, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 678][/us/stat/94/678], provided for severability of provisions.

    Section 8777, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 177, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 678][/us/stat/94/678], specified Corporation’s fiscal year and provided for audits and reports.

    Section 8778, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 178, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 679][/us/stat/94/679], related to water rights.

    Section 8779, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 179, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 679][/us/stat/94/679], related to Western Hemisphere projects.

    Section 8780, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I][/us/pl/96/294/tI], § 180, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 680][/us/stat/94/680], related to a lender financial protection and completion guarantee study.
