## § 13853. Prohibition

    None of the funds authorized under this part shall be used to finance the construction of housing.

([Pub. L. 103–322, title III][/us/pl/103/322/tIII], § 31133, Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1888][/us/stat/108/1888].)
