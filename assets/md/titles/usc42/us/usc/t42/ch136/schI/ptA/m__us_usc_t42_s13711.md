## § 13711. Support of Federal prisoners in non-Federal institutions

    (a) __In general__ 

        The Attorney General may make payments to States and units of local government for the purposes authorized in [section 4013 of title 18][/us/usc/t18/s4013].

    (b) __Authorization of appropriations__ 

        Notwithstanding any other provision of this part other than [section 13708(a)(2) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13708/a/2], there are authorized to be appropriated from amounts authorized under [section 13708 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13708] for each of fiscal years 1996 through 2000 such sums as may be necessary to carry out this section.
