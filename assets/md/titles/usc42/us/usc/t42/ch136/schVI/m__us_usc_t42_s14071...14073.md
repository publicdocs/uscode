## §§ 14071 to 14073. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–248, title I][/us/pl/109/248/tI], § 129(a), July 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 600][/us/stat/120/600]

    Section 14071, [Pub. L. 103–322, title XVII][/us/pl/103/322/tXVII], § 170101, Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2038][/us/stat/108/2038]; [Pub. L. 104–145][/us/pl/104/145], § 2, May 17, 1996, [110 Stat. 1345][/us/stat/110/1345]; [Pub. L. 104–236][/us/pl/104/236], §§ 3–7, Oct. 3, 1996, [110 Stat. 3096][/us/stat/110/3096], 3097; [Pub. L. 105–119, title I][/us/pl/105/119/tI], § 115(a)(1)–(5), Nov. 26, 1997, [111 Stat. 2461–2463][/us/stat/111/2461-2463]; [Pub. L. 105–314, title VI][/us/pl/105/314/tVI], § 607(a), Oct. 30, 1998, [112 Stat. 2985][/us/stat/112/2985]; [Pub. L. 106–386, div. B, title VI][/us/pl/106/386/dB/tVI], § 1601(b)(1), Oct. 28, 2000, [114 Stat. 1537][/us/stat/114/1537]; [Pub. L. 108–21, title VI][/us/pl/108/21/tVI], §§ 604(a), 605(a), 606, Apr. 30, 2003, [117 Stat. 688][/us/stat/117/688]; [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI][/us/pl/109/162/tXI], § 1153(b), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3113][/us/stat/119/3113], required the Attorney General to establish guidelines for State programs that required registration by persons convicted of a criminal offense against a minor or a sexually violent offense and by sexually violent predators.

    Section 14072, [Pub. L. 103–322, title XVII][/us/pl/103/322/tXVII], § 170102, as added [Pub. L. 104–236][/us/pl/104/236], § 2(a), Oct. 3, 1996, [110 Stat. 3093][/us/stat/110/3093]; amended [Pub. L. 105–119, title I][/us/pl/105/119/tI], § 115(a)(6), Nov. 26, 1997, [111 Stat. 2463][/us/stat/111/2463]; [Pub. L. 105–277, div. A][/us/pl/105/277/dA], § 101(b) \[title I, § 123\], Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–50][/us/stat/112/2681-50], 2681–72, required the Attorney General to establish a national database at the FBI to track sexual offenders.

    Section 14073, [Pub. L. 104–236][/us/pl/104/236], § 8, Oct. 3, 1996, [110 Stat. 3097][/us/stat/110/3097], immunized certain agencies and officials from liability for good faith conduct.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

[Pub. L. 109–248, title I][/us/pl/109/248/tI], § 129(b), July 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 601][/us/stat/120/601], provided that: 

> “Notwithstanding any other provision of this Act \[see Tables for classification\], this section \[repealing sections 14071 to 14073 of this title\] shall take effect on the date of the deadline determined in accordance with section 124(a) \[[42 U.S.C. 16924(a)][/us/usc/t42/s16924/a]\] \[3 years after July 27, 2006\].”

 __Short Title__ 

    Subtitle A of title XVII of [Pub. L. 103–322][/us/pl/103/322], which was classified generally to this subchapter prior to repeal, was popularly known as the Jacob Wetterling Crimes Against Children and Sexually Violent Offender Registration Act.
