## § 14101. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part $50,000,000 for fiscal year 1999, $70,000,000 for fiscal year 2000, $90,000,000 for fiscal year 2001, and $90,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2002 through 2005.

([Pub. L. 103–322, title XX][/us/pl/103/322/tXX], § 200112, Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2057][/us/stat/108/2057]; [Pub. L. 105–277, div. C, title I][/us/pl/105/277/dC/tI], § 138(b), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–597][/us/stat/112/2681-597]; [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title I][/us/pl/107/273/dC/tI], § 11006(5), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1817][/us/stat/116/1817].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2002—[Pub. L. 107–273][/us/pl/107/273] substituted “each of fiscal years 2002 through 2005” for “fiscal year 2002”.

    1998—[Pub. L. 105–277][/us/pl/105/277] substituted “$50,000,000 for fiscal year 1999, $70,000,000 for fiscal year 2000, $90,000,000 for fiscal year 2001, and $90,000,000 for fiscal year 2002” for “$20,000 for each of the fiscal years 1996 through 2000”.
