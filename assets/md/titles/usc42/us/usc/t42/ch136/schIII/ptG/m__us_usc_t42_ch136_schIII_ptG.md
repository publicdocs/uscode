#### Part G—Elder Abuse, Neglect, and Exploitation, Including Domestic Violence and Sexual Assault Against Older or Disabled Individuals

 __General Amendment of Part__ 

[Pub. L. 113–4, § 4][/us/pl/113/4/s4], title II, § 204(a), Mar. 7, 2013, [127 Stat. 64][/us/stat/127/64], 82, provided that, effective at the beginning of the fiscal year following Mar. 7, 2013, this part (subtitle H of title IV of [Pub. L. 103–322][/us/pl/103/322]) is generally amended by (1) substituting “Enhanced Training and Services To End Abuse Later in Life” as the heading, (2) generally amending [section 14041 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s14041], and (3) omitting sections 14041a and 14041b of this title.

 __Codification__ 

    This part was, in the original, subtitle H of title IV of [Pub. L. 103–322][/us/pl/103/322], as added by [Pub. L. 106–386][/us/pl/106/386], and has been redesignated as part G of this subchapter for purposes of codification.
