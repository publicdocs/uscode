#### Part G—Enhanced Training and Services To End Abuse Later in Life

 __Codification__ 

    This part was, in the original, subtitle H of title IV of [Pub. L. 103–322][/us/pl/103/322], as added by [Pub. L. 106–386][/us/pl/106/386], and has been redesignated as part G of this subchapter for purposes of codification.

 __Amendments__ 

    [Pub. L. 113–4, title II][/us/pl/113/4/tII], § 204(a), Mar. 7, 2013, [127 Stat. 82][/us/stat/127/82], substituted “ENHANCED TRAINING AND SERVICES TO END ABUSE LATER IN LIFE” for “ELDER ABUSE, NEGLECT, AND EXPLOITATION, INCLUDING DOMESTIC VIOLENCE AND SEXUAL ASSAULT AGAINST OLDER OR DISABLED INDIVIDUALS” in heading.
