## § 13109. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated to the Administrator $8,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1991, 1992, and 1993 for functions carried out under this chapter (other than State Grants), <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should not be capitalized. </sup></sup>  and $8,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1991, 1992, and 1993, for grant programs to States issued pursuant to [section 13104 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13104].

([Pub. L. 101–508, title VI][/us/pl/101/508/tVI], § 6610, Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1388–327][/us/stat/104/1388-327].)
