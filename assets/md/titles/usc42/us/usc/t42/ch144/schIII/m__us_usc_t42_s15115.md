## § 15115. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __Scholarships__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out [section 15113 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s15113] $800,000 for fiscal year 2001 and such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 2002 through 2007.

    (b) __Staff development curriculum__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out [section 15114 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s15114] $800,000 for fiscal year 2001 and such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 2002 and 2003.

([Pub. L. 106–402, title III, § 305][/us/pl/106/402/s305], Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1737][/us/stat/114/1737].)
