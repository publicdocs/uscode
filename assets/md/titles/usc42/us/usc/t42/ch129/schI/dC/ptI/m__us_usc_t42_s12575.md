## § 12575. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title I][/us/pl/111/13/tI], § 1304, Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1500][/us/stat/123/1500]

    Section, [Pub. L. 101–610, title I][/us/pl/101/610/tI], § 125, as added [Pub. L. 103–82, title I][/us/pl/103/82/tI], § 101(b), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 795][/us/stat/107/795], related to training and technical assistance.

L. 101–610][/us/pl/101/610] (former part D of this subchapter) by [Pub. L. 103–82][/us/pl/103/82].
