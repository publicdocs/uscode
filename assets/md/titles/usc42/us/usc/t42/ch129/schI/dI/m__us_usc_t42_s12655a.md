## § 12655a. Limitation on purchase of capital equipment

    Not to exceed 10 percent of the amount of assistance made available to a program agency under this division shall be used for the purchase of major capital equipment.

([Pub. L. 101–610, title I][/us/pl/101/610/tI], § 199B, formerly § 122, Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3140][/us/stat/104/3140]; [Pub. L. 102–10][/us/pl/102/10], § 5(2), Mar. 12, 1991, [105 Stat. 30][/us/stat/105/30]; renumbered § 199B and amended [Pub. L. 103–82, title I][/us/pl/103/82/tI], § 101(a), (e)(3), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 788][/us/stat/107/788], 815.)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 12542 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s12542] prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 103–82][/us/pl/103/82], § 101(a).

 __Amendments__ 

    1993—[Pub. L. 103–82][/us/pl/103/82], § 101(e)(3), amended section generally, substituting provisions relating to limitation on purchase of capital equipment for provisions relating to allocation of funds.

    1991—Subsec. (e). [Pub. L. 102–10][/us/pl/102/10] inserted “service” after “youth”.

 __Effective Date of 1993 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 103–82][/us/pl/103/82] effective Oct. 1, 1993, see [section 123 of Pub. L. 103–82][/us/pl/103/82/s123], set out as a note under [section 1701 of Title 16][/us/usc/t16/s1701], Conservation.
