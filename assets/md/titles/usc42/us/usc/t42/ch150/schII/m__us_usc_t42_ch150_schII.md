    Section 16631, [Pub. L. 109–155, title II, § 202][/us/pl/109/155/s202], Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2915][/us/stat/119/2915], related to appropriations for fiscal year 2007.

    Section 16634, [Pub. L. 109–155, title II, § 205][/us/pl/109/155/s205], Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2916][/us/stat/119/2916], related to charges and funding account for test facilities. See [section 50505 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50505].

§§ 16635, 16636. __Omitted__ 

    Section 16635, [Pub.
