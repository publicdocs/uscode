###### §§ 16652, 16653. Omitted

    Section 16652, [Pub. L. 109–155, title III][/us/pl/109/155/tIII], § 302, Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2917][/us/stat/119/2917], which related to status of Hubble Space Telescope servicing mission and required Administrator’s report to Congress not later than 60 days after second Space Shuttle mission, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 51, National and Commercial Space Programs, by [Pub. L. 111–314][/us/pl/111/314].

    Section 16653, [Pub. L. 109–155, title III][/us/pl/109/155/tIII], § 303, Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2917][/us/stat/119/2917], which required independent assessment of Landsat-NPOESS integrated mission to be transmitted to Congress not later than 180 days after Dec. 30, 2005, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 51.
