## §§ 16821 to 16823. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314][/us/pl/111/314], § 6, Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444][/us/stat/124/3444]

    Section 16821, [Pub. L. 109–155, title VII][/us/pl/109/155/tVII], § 707, Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2937][/us/stat/119/2937], related to small business contracting. See [section 30306 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s30306], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Section 16822, [Pub. L. 109–155, title VII][/us/pl/109/155/tVII], § 708, Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2938][/us/stat/119/2938], related to NASA healthcare program. See [section 31301 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s31301].

    Section 16823, [Pub. L. 109–155, title VII][/us/pl/109/155/tVII], § 709, Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2938][/us/stat/119/2938], related to offshore performance of contracts for the procurement of goods and services. See [section 30704 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s30704].
