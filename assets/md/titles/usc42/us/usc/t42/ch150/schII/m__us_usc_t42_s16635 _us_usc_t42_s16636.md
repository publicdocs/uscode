###### §§ 16635, 16636. Omitted

    Section 16635, [Pub. L. 109–155, title II][/us/pl/109/155/tII], § 206, Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2916][/us/stat/119/2916], which related to limitation on use of appropriated funds for official representation, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 51, National and Commercial Space Programs, by [Pub. L. 111–314][/us/pl/111/314].

    Section 16636, [Pub. L. 109–155, title II][/us/pl/109/155/tII], § 207, Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2916][/us/stat/119/2916], which required Administrator to report ISS development costs and related matters to Congress and provided for repeal of [section 202 of Pub. L. 106–391][/us/pl/106/391/s202] thirty days after transmission of report, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 51.
