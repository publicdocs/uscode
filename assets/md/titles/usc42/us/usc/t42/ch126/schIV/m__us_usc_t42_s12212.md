## § 12212. Alternative means of dispute resolution

    Where appropriate and to the extent authorized by law, the use of alternative means of dispute resolution, including settlement negotiations, conciliation, facilitation, mediation, factfinding, minitrials, and arbitration, is encouraged to resolve disputes arising under this chapter.

([Pub. L. 101–336, title V][/us/pl/101/336/tV], § 514, formerly § 513, July 26, 1990, [104 Stat. 377][/us/stat/104/377]; renumbered § 514, [Pub. L. 110–325][/us/pl/110/325], § 6(a)(2), Sept. 25, 2008, [122 Stat. 3558][/us/stat/122/3558].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 101–336][/us/pl/101/336], July 26, 1990, [104 Stat. 327][/us/stat/104/327], which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 12101 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s12101] and Tables.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 514 of Pub. L. 101–336][/us/pl/101/336/s514] was renumbered section 515 and is classified to [section 12213 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s12213].
