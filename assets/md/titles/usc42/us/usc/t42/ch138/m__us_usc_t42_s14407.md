## § 14407. Application to District of Columbia

    For purposes of this chapter, the term “funds appropriated by Congress” includes funds appropriated to the District of Columbia pursuant to an authorization of appropriations under title V of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act and the term “Federal government” includes the government of the District of Columbia.

([Pub. L. 105–12][/us/pl/105/12], § 8, Apr. 30, 1997, [111 Stat. 26][/us/stat/111/26]; [Pub. L. 105–33, title XI][/us/pl/105/33/tXI], § 11717(b), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 786][/us/stat/111/786].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 105–12][/us/pl/105/12], Apr. 30, 1997, [111 Stat. 23][/us/stat/111/23], known as the Assisted Suicide Funding Restriction Act of 1997, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 14401 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s14401] and Tables.

    The District of Columbia Home Rule Act, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 93–198][/us/pl/93/198], Dec. 24, 1973, [87 Stat. 774][/us/stat/87/774], as amended. Title V of the Act was classified to the District of Columbia Code prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–33, title XI][/us/pl/105/33/tXI], § 11601, Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 777][/us/stat/111/777].

 __Amendments__ 

    1997—[Pub. L. 105–33][/us/pl/105/33] substituted “District of Columbia Home Rule Act” for “District of Columbia Self-Government and Governmental Reorganization Act”.

 __Effective Date of 1997 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 105–33][/us/pl/105/33] effective Oct. 1, 1997, except as otherwise provided in title XI of [Pub. L. 105–33][/us/pl/105/33], see [section 11721 of Pub. L. 105–33][/us/pl/105/33/s11721], set out as a note under [section 4246 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s4246], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.
