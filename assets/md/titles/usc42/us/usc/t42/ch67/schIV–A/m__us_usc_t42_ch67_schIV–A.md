### SUBCHAPTER IV–A—ABANDONED INFANTS ASSISTANCE

 __Codification__ 

    This subchapter is comprised generally of [Pub. L. 100–505][/us/pl/100/505], Oct. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 2533][/us/stat/102/2533], as amended. [Pub. L. 100–505][/us/pl/100/505] was formerly set out as a note under [section 670 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s670]. [Section 105 of Pub.
