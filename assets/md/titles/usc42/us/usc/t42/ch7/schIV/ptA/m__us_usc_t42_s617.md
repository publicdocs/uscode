## § 617. Limitation on Federal authority

    No officer or employee of the Federal Government may regulate the conduct of States under this part or enforce any provision of this part, except to the extent expressly provided in this part.

([Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531], title IV, § 417, as added [Pub. L. 104–193, title I][/us/pl/104/193/tI], § 103(a)(1), Aug. 22, 1996, [110 Stat. 2159][/us/stat/110/2159]; amended [Pub. L. 105–33, title V][/us/pl/105/33/tV], § 5514(c), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 620][/us/stat/111/620].)
