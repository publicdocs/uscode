## § 1320b–18. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–203, title II][/us/pl/108/203/tII], § 210(b)(3), Mar. 2, 2004, [118 Stat. 517][/us/stat/118/517]

    Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXI], § 1147A, as added [Pub. L. 106–169, title II][/us/pl/106/169/tII], § 251(b)(8), Dec. 14, 1999, [113 Stat. 1856][/us/stat/113/1856], related to recovery of social security benefit overpayments from subchapter VIII benefits. See [section 1320b–17 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1320b–17].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Mar. 2, 2004, and effective with respect to overpayments under subchapters II, VIII, and XVI of this chapter that are outstanding on or after such date, see [section 210(c) of Pub. L. 108–203][/us/pl/108/203/s210/c], set out as an Effective Date of 2004 Amendment note under [section 404 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s404].
