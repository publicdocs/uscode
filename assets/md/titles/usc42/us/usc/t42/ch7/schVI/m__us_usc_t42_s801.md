## § 801. Repealed

    Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVI], § 601, as added [Pub. L. 108–27, title IV][/us/pl/108/27/tIV], § 401(b), May 28, 2003, [117 Stat. 766][/us/stat/117/766], related to temporary State fiscal relief, prior to repeal by [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVI], § 601(g), as added [Pub. L. 108–27, title IV][/us/pl/108/27/tIV], § 401(b), May 28, 2003, [117 Stat. 768][/us/stat/117/768].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Prior sections 801 to 805 were repealed by [Pub. L. 93–647][/us/pl/93/647], §§ 3(b), 7(b), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2349][/us/stat/88/2349], 2351, effective with respect to payments under section 803 for quarters commencing after Sept. 30, 1975.

    Section 801, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVI], § 601, as added Oct. 30, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–603, title III][/us/pl/92/603/tIII], § 302, [86 Stat. 1478][/us/stat/86/1478], authorized appropriations for encouraging States to furnish rehabilitation to needy individuals 65 years of age or older, and the blind or disabled.

    Another prior section 801, acts [Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531], title VI, § 601, [49 Stat. 634][/us/stat/49/634]; [Aug. 10, 1939, ch. 666][/us/act/1939-08-10/ch666], title V, § 509, [53 Stat. 1381][/us/stat/53/1381], which provided appropriations for the purpose of assisting States and subdivisions in maintaining adequate public health services, was repealed by [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XIII][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXIII], § 1313, formerly title VI, § 611, [58 Stat. 719][/us/stat/58/719]. See [section 246 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s246].

    Section 802, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVI], § 602, as added Oct. 30, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–603, title III][/us/pl/92/603/tIII], § 302, [86 Stat. 1479][/us/stat/86/1479], set out the necessary provisions for State plans for services to the aged, blind, or disabled.

    Another prior section 802, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVI], § 602, [49 Stat. 634][/us/stat/49/634], which provided for allotments to States by Surgeon General, was repealed by [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XIII][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXIII], § 1313, formerly title VI, § 611, [58 Stat. 719][/us/stat/58/719]. See [section 246 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s246].

    Section 803, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVI], § 603, as added Oct. 30, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–603, title III][/us/pl/92/603/tIII], § 302, [86 Stat. 1481][/us/stat/86/1481], provided for payments to States under approved plans for services to the aged, blind, or disabled.

    Another prior section 803, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVI], § 603, [49 Stat. 635][/us/stat/49/635], which provided for allotments to States by appropriations for investigation of diseases by Public Health Service, was repealed by [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XIII][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXIII], § 1313, formerly title VI, § 611, [58 Stat. 719][/us/stat/58/719]. See [section 246 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s246].

    Section 804, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVI], § 604, as added Oct. 30, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–603, title III][/us/pl/92/603/tIII], § 302, [86 Stat. 1484][/us/stat/86/1484], provided for notification to States and termination of payments in case of noncompliance with laws or State plan.

    Section 805, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VI][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVI], § 605, as added Oct. 30, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–603, title III][/us/pl/92/603/tIII], § 302, [86 Stat. 1484][/us/stat/86/1484], defined “services to the aged, blind or disabled”.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 2004, see section 601(g) of act Aug. 14, 1935, as added by [Pub. L. 108–27, title IV][/us/pl/108/27/tIV], § 401(b), May 28, 2003, [117 Stat. 768][/us/stat/117/768], which was formerly classified to subsec. (g) of this section.

 __Renumbering of Repealing Act__ 

    [Act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title VI][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tVI], § 611, [58 Stat. 719][/us/stat/58/719], which repealed prior sections 801 to 803, was renumbered title VII, § 711, by [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 958][/us/act/1946-08-13/ch958], § 5, [60 Stat. 1049][/us/stat/60/1049]; renumbered § 713 by [act Feb. 28, 1948, ch. 83][/us/act/1948-02-28/ch83], § 9(b), [62 Stat. 47][/us/stat/62/47]; renumbered title VIII, § 813, by [act July 30, 1956, ch. 779][/us/act/1956-07-30/ch779], § 3(b), [70 Stat. 721][/us/stat/70/721]; renumbered title IX, § 913, by [Pub.
