## § 907. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–296, title I][/us/pl/103/296/tI], § 108(a)(2), Aug. 15, 1994, [108 Stat. 1481][/us/stat/108/1481]

    Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VII][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVII], § 706, as added July 30, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–97, title I][/us/pl/89/97/tI], § 109(a), [79 Stat. 339][/us/stat/79/339]; amended Jan. 2, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–248, title I][/us/pl/90/248/tI], § 165, title IV, § 403(d), [81 Stat. 874][/us/stat/81/874], 932; July 18, 1984, [Pub. L. 98–369, div. B, title VI][/us/pl/98/369/dB/tVI], § 2663(j)(2)(C)(ii), [98 Stat. 1170][/us/stat/98/1170]; Apr. 7, 1986, [Pub. L. 99–272, title XII][/us/pl/99/272/tXII], § 12102(g)(1), [100 Stat. 285][/us/stat/100/285]; Oct. 22, 1986, [Pub. L. 99–514][/us/pl/99/514], § 2, [100 Stat. 2095][/us/stat/100/2095], provided for appointment by Secretary every four years of an Advisory Council on Social Security and functions of Council.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Mar. 31, 1995, see [section 110(a) of Pub. L. 103–296][/us/pl/103/296/s110/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1994 Amendment note under [section 401 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s401].

 __Applicability of Repeal to 1994 Council__ 

[Pub. L. 103–296, title I][/us/pl/103/296/tI], § 108(a)(2), Aug. 15, 1994, [108 Stat. 1481][/us/stat/108/1481], provided in part that: 

> “This paragraph \[repealing this section\] shall not apply with respect to the Advisory Council for Social Security appointed in 1994.”
