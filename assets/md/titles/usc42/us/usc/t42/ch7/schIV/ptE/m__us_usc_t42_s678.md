## § 678. Rule of construction

    Nothing in this part shall be construed as precluding State courts from exercising their discretion to protect the health and safety of children in individual cases, including cases other than those described in [section 671(a)(15)(D) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s671/a/15/D].

([Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531], title IV, § 478, as added [Pub. L. 105–89, title I][/us/pl/105/89/tI], § 101(d), Nov. 19, 1997, [111 Stat. 2117][/us/stat/111/2117].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 678, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title IV][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tIV], § 478, as added Oct. 22, 1986, [Pub. L. 99–514, title XVIII][/us/pl/99/514/tXVIII], § 1883(b)(10)(A), [100 Stat. 2917][/us/stat/100/2917], excluded from AFDC unit child for whom foster care maintenance payments are made, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 101–508, title V][/us/pl/101/508/tV], § 5052(b), (c), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1388–228][/us/stat/104/1388-228], applicable with respect to benefits for months beginning on or after the first day of the sixth calendar month following November 1990.

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective Nov. 19, 1997, except as otherwise provided, with delay permitted if State legislation is required, see [section 501 of Pub. L. 105–89][/us/pl/105/89/s501], set out as an Effective Date of 1997 Amendments note under [section 622 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s622].
