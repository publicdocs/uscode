## § 1395i–1a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–234, title I][/us/pl/101/234/tI], § 102(a), Dec. 13, 1989, [103 Stat. 1980][/us/stat/103/1980]

    Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XVIII][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXVIII], § 1817A, as added July 1, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–360, title I][/us/pl/100/360/tI], § 112(a), [102 Stat. 698][/us/stat/102/698], provided for establishment and operation of Federal Hospital Insurance Catastrophic Coverage Reserve Fund.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1990, see [section 102(d)(1) of Pub. L. 101–234][/us/pl/101/234/s102/d/1], set out as a note under [section 59B of Title 26][/us/usc/t26/s59B], Internal Revenue Code.

 __Adjustments for Interest Lost Due to Delay of Transfers to Reserve Fund During 1989__ 

    [Pub. L. 100–360, title I][/us/pl/100/360/tI], § 112(b), July 1, 1988, [102 Stat. 699][/us/stat/102/699], which directed Secretary of the Treasury, in July of 1990, to calculate interest lost to Federal Hospital Insurance Catastrophic Coverage Reserve Fund due to lag between outlays (attributable to amendments made by [Pub. L. 100–360][/us/pl/100/360]) from Federal Hospital Insurance Trust Fund during 1989 and transfers made to such Reserve Fund to cover such outlays, and provided that appropriations under subsection (a)(2) of this section include amount so calculated, was repealed by [Pub. L. 101–234, title I][/us/pl/101/234/tI], § 102(a), Dec. 13, 1989, [103 Stat. 1980][/us/stat/103/1980].
