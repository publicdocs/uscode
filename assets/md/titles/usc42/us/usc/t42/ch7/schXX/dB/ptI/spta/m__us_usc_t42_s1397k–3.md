## § 1397k–3. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subpart—

    (1) for fiscal year 2011, $6,500,000; and

    (2) for each of fiscal years 2012 through 2014, $7,000,000.

([Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531], title XX, § 2024, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VI, § 6703(a)(1)(C)][/us/pl/111/148/s6703/a/1/C], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 790][/us/stat/124/790].)
