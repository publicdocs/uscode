### SUBCHAPTER IX—EMPLOYMENT SECURITY ADMINISTRATIVE FINANCING

 __Amendments__ 

    1954—[Act Aug. 5, 1954, ch. 657, § 2][/us/act/1954-08-05/ch657/s2], [68 Stat. 668][/us/stat/68/668], in amending subchapter generally substituted subchapter heading “EMPLOYMENT SECURITY ADMINISTRATIVE FINANCING” for “TAX ON EMPLOYMENT OF EIGHT OR MORE”.

 __Prior Law; Tax on Employers of Eight or More__ 

Former subchapter IX, sections 1101–1103, 1105–1110, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531][/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531], title IX, §§ 901–903, 905–910, [49 Stat. 639–644][/us/stat/49/639-644], related to taxes on employers of eight or more. Section 4 of [act Feb. 10, 1939, ch. 2][/us/act/1939-02-10/ch2], [53 Stat. 1][/us/stat/53/1], which act enacted Title 26, Internal Revenue Code of 1939, provided that all laws and parts of laws codified into the I.R.C. 1939, to the extent that they related exclusively to internal revenue laws, were repealed. Provisions of I.R.C. 1939 were generally repealed by [section 7851 of Title 26][/us/usc/t26/s7851], Internal Revenue Code of 1954 ([act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736][/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736], [68A Stat. 3][/us/stat/68A/3]). See, also, section 7807 of said Title 26, I.R.C. 1954, respecting rules in effect upon enactment of I.R.C. 1954. The I.R.C. 1954 was redesignated I.R.C. 1986 by [Pub. L. 99–514, § 2][/us/pl/99/514/s2], Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2095][/us/stat/100/2095]. Said prior law sections were formerly and are now covered by certain sections in Title 26, I.R.C. 1939 and I.R.C. 1986, respectively, as follows:

 __Repair of 1938 Hurricane Damage__ 

    [Act Aug. 11, 1939, ch. 719, § 1][/us/act/1939-08-11/ch719/s1], [53 Stat. 1420][/us/stat/53/1420], provided that no special security taxes should be collected for work done prior to Jan. 1, 1940, in cleaning up debris and damage caused by the 1938 hurricane.

 __Credits Against Social Security Tax__ 

    [Act Aug. 10, 1939, ch. 666][/us/act/1939-08-10/ch666], title IX, § 902(a)–(d), (h), [53 Stat. 1399][/us/stat/53/1399], provided for a credit against the social security tax of certain contributions made with respect to employment during calendar years 1936, 1937, or 1938. Said act Aug. 10, 1939, was affected by [act Sept. 20, 1941, ch. 412][/us/act/1941-09-20/ch412], title VII, § 701(c), [55 Stat. 728][/us/stat/55/728].

    [Act May 28, 1938, ch. 289, § 810][/us/act/1938-05-28/ch289/s810], [52 Stat. 576][/us/stat/52/576], related to credits against Social Security Tax for 1936. It was affected by [act Sept. 20, 1941, ch. 412][/us/act/1941-09-20/ch412], title VII, § 701(c), [55 Stat. 728][/us/stat/55/728], relating to credit against Federal unemployment taxes.
