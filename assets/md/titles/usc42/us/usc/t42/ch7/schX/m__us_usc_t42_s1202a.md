## § 1202a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–543, title I, § 136(b)][/us/pl/87/543/s136/b], July 25, 1962, [76 Stat. 197][/us/stat/76/197]

    Section, [act Aug. 28, 1950, ch. 809][/us/act/1950-08-28/ch809], title III, pt. 4, § 344(a), [64 Stat. 554][/us/stat/64/554], provided, in the case of any State without a plan for aid to the blind approved on Jan. 1, 1949, for approval of the plan of such a State conforming to all requirements except those relating to determination of need and consideration of resources but conditioned payments to the State meeting the excepted requirement.

 __Effective and Termination Dates__ 

    [Section 136(b) of Pub. L. 87–543][/us/pl/87/543/s136/b] also repealed section 344(b) of act Aug. 28, 1950, as amended [Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1206][/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1206], title III, § 302, [68 Stat. 1097][/us/stat/68/1097]; Apr. 25, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–26][/us/pl/85/26], [71 Stat. 27][/us/stat/71/27]; Aug. 28, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–840, title V, § 509][/us/pl/85/840/s509], [72 Stat. 1051][/us/stat/72/1051]; Sept. 13, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–778, title VII, § 706][/us/pl/86/778/s706], [74 Stat. 995][/us/stat/74/995], which provided that this section should become effective Oct. 1, 1950 and terminate June 30, 1964.
