## § 9858d. Limitations on State allotments

    (a) __No entitlement to contract or grant__ 

    Nothing in this subchapter shall be construed—

        (1) to entitle any child care provider or recipient of a child care certificate to any contract, grant or benefit; or

        (2) to limit the right of any State to impose additional limitations or conditions on contracts or grants funded under this subchapter.

    (b) __Construction of facilities__ 

        (1) __In general__ 

            Except as provided for in [section 9858m(c)(6) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s9858m/c/6], no funds made available under this subchapter shall be expended for the purchase or improvement of land, or for the purchase, construction, or permanent improvement (other than minor remodeling) of any building or facility.

        (2) __Sectarian agency or organization__ 

            In the case of a sectarian agency or organization, no funds made available under this subchapter may be used for the purposes described in paragraph (1) except to the extent that renovation or repair is necessary to bring the facility of such agency or organization into compliance with health and safety requirements referred to in [section 9858c(c)(2)(I) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s9858c/c/2/I].
