## § 11421. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–220, title I][/us/pl/105/220/tI], § 199(b)(1), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1059][/us/stat/112/1059]

    Section, [Pub. L. 100–77, title VII][/us/pl/100/77/tVII], § 701, as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title III][/us/pl/103/382/tIII], § 322, Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3956][/us/stat/108/3956], related to State literacy initiatives.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__

L. 105–220, title I][/us/pl/105/220/tI], § 199(c)(2)(A), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1059][/us/stat/112/1059], which provided that the repeal made by subsection (b)(1) (repealing sections 11421, 11461 to 11466, 11471, and 11472 of this title) would take effect on July 1, 1999, was repealed by [Pub.
