## § 11435. Authorization of appropriations

    For the purpose of carrying out this part, there are authorized to be appropriated $100,000,000 for fiscal year 2009 and such sums as may be necessary for each subsequent fiscal year.

([Pub. L. 100–77, title VII, § 726][/us/pl/100/77/s726], as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title X, § 1032][/us/pl/107/110/s1032], Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 2006][/us/stat/115/2006]; amended [Pub. L. 110–289, div. B, title IX, § 2901(a)][/us/pl/110/289/s2901/a], July 30, 2008, [122 Stat. 2875][/us/stat/122/2875].)
