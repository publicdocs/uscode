## § 11435. Authorization of appropriations

    For the purpose of carrying out this part, there are authorized to be appropriated $100,000,000 for fiscal year 2009 and such sums as may be necessary for each subsequent fiscal year.

([Pub. L. 100–77, title VII][/us/pl/100/77/tVII], § 726, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title X][/us/pl/107/110/tX], § 1032, Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 2006][/us/stat/115/2006]; amended [Pub. L. 110–289, div. B, title IX][/us/pl/110/289/dB/tIX], § 2901(a), July 30, 2008, [122 Stat. 2875][/us/stat/122/2875]; [Pub. L. 114–95, title IX][/us/pl/114/95/tIX], § 9106, Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2137][/us/stat/129/2137].)

 __Amendment of Section__ 

    [Pub. L. 114–95, title IX][/us/pl/114/95/tIX], §§ 9106, 9107, Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2137][/us/stat/129/2137], provided that, effective Oct. 1, 2016, this section is amended to read as follows:

    § 11435. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part $85,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2017 through 2020.

    See 2015 Amendment note below.

 __Amendments__ 

    2015—[Pub. L. 114–95][/us/pl/114/95], § 9106, amended section generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “For the purpose of carrying out this part, there are authorized to be appropriated $100,000,000 for fiscal year 2009 and such sums as may be necessary for each subsequent fiscal year.”

    2008—[Pub. L. 110–289][/us/pl/110/289] substituted “$100,000,000 for fiscal year 2009 and such sums as may be necessary for each subsequent fiscal year” for “$70,000,000 for fiscal year 2002 and such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 2003 through 2007”.
