## § 11317. Definitions

    For purposes of this subchapter:

    (1) The term “Council” means the United States Interagency Council on Homelessness established in [section 11311 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s11311].

    (2) The term “Federal agency” has the meaning given the term “agency” in [section 551(1) of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s551/1].

([Pub. L. 100–77, title II, § 207][/us/pl/100/77/s207], July 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 489][/us/stat/101/489]; [Pub. L. 108–199, div. G, title II, § 216(4)][/us/pl/108/199/s216/4], Jan. 23, 2004, [118 Stat. 394][/us/stat/118/394].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2004—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 108–199][/us/pl/108/199] substituted “United States Interagency Council on Homelessness” for “Interagency Council on the Homeless”.
