## § 16929. Immunity for good faith conduct

    The Federal Government, jurisdictions, political subdivisions of jurisdictions, and their agencies, officers, employees, and agents shall be immune from liability for good faith conduct under this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 109–248, title I][/us/pl/109/248/tI], § 131, July 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 601][/us/stat/120/601].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 109–248][/us/pl/109/248], July 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 590][/us/stat/120/590], known as the Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 16901 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s16901] and Tables.
