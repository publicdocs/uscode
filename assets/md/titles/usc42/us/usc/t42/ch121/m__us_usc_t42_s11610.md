## § 11610. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of the Convention and this chapter.

([Pub. L. 100–300, § 12][/us/pl/100/300/s12], Apr. 29, 1988, [102 Stat. 442][/us/stat/102/442].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act” meaning [Pub. L. 100–300][/us/pl/100/300], Apr. 29, 1988, [102 Stat. 437][/us/stat/102/437], which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note under [section 11601 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s11601] and Tables.
