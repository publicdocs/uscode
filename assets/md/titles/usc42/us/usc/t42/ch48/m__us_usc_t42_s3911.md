## § 3911. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–181, title I][/us/pl/98/181] \[title IV, § 474(e)\], Nov. 30, 1983, [97 Stat. 1239][/us/stat/97/1239]

    Section, [Pub. L. 90–448, title IV, § 412][/us/pl/90/448/s412], 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 516][/us/stat/82/516]; [Pub. L. 91–152, title III, § 304][/us/pl/91/152/s304], Dec. 24, 1969, [83 Stat. 391][/us/stat/83/391]; [Pub. L. 91–609, title III, § 303(b)][/us/pl/91/609/s303/b], Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1780][/us/stat/84/1780], provided for supplementary grants under this chapter.

§§ 3912, 3913. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted pursuant to [section 4528 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s4528], which terminated authority to guarantee bonds, debentures, notes, or other obligations under this chapter after Dec. 31, 1970, with exceptions now inapplicable.

    Section 3912, [Pub. L. 90–448, title IV, § 413][/us/pl/90/448/s413], Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 517][/us/stat/82/517], set out functions, powers and duties of the Secretary under this chapter.

    Section 3913, [Pub. L. 90–448, title IV, § 414][/us/pl/90/448/s414], Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 517][/us/stat/82/517], related to audit of financial transactions of those whose obligations are guaranteed under this chapter.
