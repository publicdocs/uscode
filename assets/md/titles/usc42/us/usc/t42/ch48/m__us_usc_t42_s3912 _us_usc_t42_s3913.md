###### §§ 3912, 3913. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted pursuant to [section 4528 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s4528], which terminated authority to guarantee bonds, debentures, notes, or other obligations under this chapter after Dec. 31, 1970, with exceptions now inapplicable.

    Section 3912, [Pub. L. 90–448, title IV][/us/pl/90/448/tIV], § 413, Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 517][/us/stat/82/517], set out functions, powers and duties of the Secretary under this chapter.

    Section 3913, [Pub. L. 90–448, title IV][/us/pl/90/448/tIV], § 414, Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 517][/us/stat/82/517], related to audit of financial transactions of those whose obligations are guaranteed under this chapter.
