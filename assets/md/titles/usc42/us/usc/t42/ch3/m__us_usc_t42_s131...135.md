## §§ 131 to 135. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section 131, [act Feb. 3, 1917, ch. 26][/us/act/1917-02-03/ch26], § 1, [39 Stat. 872][/us/stat/39/872], provided for establishment of a leprosy home in the United States.

    Section 132, [act Feb. 3, 1917, ch. 26][/us/act/1917-02-03/ch26], § 4, [39 Stat. 873][/us/stat/39/873], provided for erection of buildings for the home.

    Section 133, [act Feb. 3, 1917, ch. 26][/us/act/1917-02-03/ch26], § 2, [39 Stat. 873][/us/stat/39/873], provided for receipt of lepers into the home. See [section 255 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s255].

    Section 134, [act Feb. 3, 1917, ch. 26][/us/act/1917-02-03/ch26], § 3, [39 Stat. 873][/us/stat/39/873], provided for regulations governing the home. See [section 255 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s255].

    Section 135, [act Feb. 3, 1917, ch. 26][/us/act/1917-02-03/ch26], § 5, [39 Stat. 873][/us/stat/39/873], provided for additional pay of officers detailed to duty at said home. See [section 210 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s210].
