## §§ 121 to 125. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section 121, acts [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443][/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443], § 1, [33 Stat. 1009][/us/stat/33/1009]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288], § 1, [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], provided for establishment in perpetuity of a hospital station and laboratory at Molokai, Hawaii.

    Section 122, [act Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443][/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443], § 3, [33 Stat. 1009][/us/stat/33/1009], provided for admission and treatment of leper patients. See [section 255 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s255].

    Section 123, acts [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443][/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443], § 4, [33 Stat. 1009][/us/stat/33/1009]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288], § 1, [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], provided for detail of officers and employees for leprosy work. See [section 215 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s215].

    Section 124, acts [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443][/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443], § 6, [33 Stat. 1010][/us/stat/33/1010]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288], § 1, [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], provided regulations for administration of hospital station and laboratory.

    Section 125, acts [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443][/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443], § 7, [33 Stat. 1010][/us/stat/33/1010]; [Mar. 4, 1911, ch. 285][/us/act/1911-03-04/ch285], § 1, [36 Stat. 1394][/us/stat/36/1394]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288], § 1, [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], provided for additional pay and allowances to officers detailed on leprosy duty. See [section 210 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s210].
