## § 7471. Plan requirements

    In accordance with the policy of [section 7401(b)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s7401/b/1], each applicable implementation plan shall contain emission limitations and such other measures as may be necessary, as determined under regulations promulgated under this part, to prevent significant deterioration of air quality in each region (or portion thereof) designated pursuant to [section 7407 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s7407] as attainment or unclassifiable.

([July 14, 1955, ch. 360][/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360], title I, § 161, as added [Pub. L. 95–95, title I][/us/pl/95/95/tI], § 127(a), Aug. 7, 1977, [91 Stat. 731][/us/stat/91/731]; amended [Pub. L. 101–549, title I][/us/pl/101/549/tI], § 110(1), Nov. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 2470][/us/stat/104/2470].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1990—[Pub. L. 101–549][/us/pl/101/549] substituted “designated pursuant to [section 7407 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s7407] as attainment or unclassifiable” for “identified pursuant to section 7407(d)(1)(D) or (E) of this title”.
