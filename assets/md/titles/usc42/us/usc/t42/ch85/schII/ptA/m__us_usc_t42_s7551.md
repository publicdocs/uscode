## § 7551. Omitted

    Section, [Pub. L. 95–95, title II, § 203][/us/pl/95/95/s203], Aug. 7, 1977, [91 Stat. 754][/us/stat/91/754]; [Pub. L. 97–375, title I, § 106(a)][/us/pl/97/375/s106/a], Dec. 21, 1982, [96 Stat. 1820][/us/stat/96/1820], which required the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency to report to Congress respecting the motor vehicle fuel consumption associated with the standards applicable for the immediately preceding model year, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66][/us/pl/104/66/s3003], as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s1113], Money and Finance. See, also, the 5th item on page 165 of House Document No. 103–7. Section was enacted as part of the Clean Air Act Amendments of 1977, and not as part of the Clean Air Act which comprises this chapter.
