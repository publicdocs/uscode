## § 7574. Definitions

    Terms used in this part (other than Administrator) shall have the same meaning as such terms have under [section 40102(a) of title 49][/us/usc/t49/s40102/a].

([July 14, 1955, ch. 360][/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360], title II, § 234, as added [Pub. L. 91–604][/us/pl/91/604], § 11(a)(1), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1705][/us/stat/84/1705].)

 __Codification__ 

    In text, “[section 40102(a) of title 49][/us/usc/t49/s40102/a]” substituted for “section 101 of the Federal Aviation Act of 1958” on authority of [Pub. L. 103–272][/us/pl/103/272], § 6(b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1378][/us/stat/108/1378], the first section of which enacted subtitles II, III, and V to X of Title 49, Transportation.

    Section was formerly classified to [section 1857f–12 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1857f–12].
