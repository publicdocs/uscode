## § 4585. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 91–616, title V, § 501][/us/pl/91/616/s501], as added [Pub. L. 94–371, § 7][/us/pl/94/371/s7], July 26, 1976, [90 Stat. 1038][/us/stat/90/1038]; amended [Pub. L. 95–622, title II, § 268(c)][/us/pl/95/622/s268/c], (d), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3437][/us/stat/92/3437]; [Pub. L. 96–180, § 14][/us/pl/96/180/s14], Jan. 2, 1980, [93 Stat. 1305][/us/stat/93/1305], which directed Secretary to take certain steps to encourage research, was redesignated section 510 of the Public Health Service Act by [Pub. L. 98–24, § 2(b)(9)][/us/pl/98/24/s2/b/9], Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 179][/us/stat/97/179], and is classified to [section 290bb of this title][/us/usc/t42/s290bb].
