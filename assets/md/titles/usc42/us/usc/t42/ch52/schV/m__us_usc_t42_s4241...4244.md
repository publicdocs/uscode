## §§ 4241 to 4244. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], § 5(b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068][/us/stat/96/1068]

    Section 4241, [Pub. L. 90–577, title VI][/us/pl/90/577/tVI], § 601, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1106][/us/stat/82/1106], related to Congressional review of grant-in-aid programs. See [section 6507 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s6507], Money and Finance.

    Section 4242, [Pub. L. 90–577, title VI][/us/pl/90/577/tVI], § 602, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1107][/us/stat/82/1107], related to studies by the Comptroller General of Federal grant-in-aid programs and reports to Congress. See [section 6508(a) of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s6508/a].

    Section 4243, [Pub. L. 90–577, title VI][/us/pl/90/577/tVI], § 603, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1107][/us/stat/82/1107], related to studies by the Advisory Commission on Intergovernmental Relations and a report of its findings to Congress. See [section 6508(b) of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s6508/b].

    Section 4244, [Pub. L. 90–577, title VI][/us/pl/90/577/tVI], § 604, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1107][/us/stat/82/1107], provided for preservation of House and Senate committee jurisdiction.
