### SUBCHAPTER II—GRANTS-IN-AID TO THE STATES; IMPROVED ADMINISTRATION

§§ 4211 to 4214. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)__  __][/us/pl/97/258/s5/b]__  __,__  __Sept. 13, 1982__  __,__  __[__  __96 Stat. 1068__  __][/us/stat/96/1068]__ 

    Section 4211, [Pub. L. 90–577, title II, § 201][/us/pl/90/577/s201], Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1101][/us/stat/82/1101], required the Federal Government to provide to States full information on grant-in-aid funds. See [section 6502 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s6502], Money and Finance.

    Section 4212, [Pub. L. 90–577, title II, § 202][/us/pl/90/577/s202], Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1101][/us/stat/82/1101], related to deposit of grants-in-aid. See [section 6503(b) of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s6503/b].

    Section 4213, [Pub. L. 90–577, title II, § 203][/us/pl/90/577/s203], Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1101][/us/stat/82/1101], related to scheduling of Federal grant-in-aid transfers to States. See [section 6503(a) of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s6503/a].

    Section 4214, [Pub. L. 90–577, title II, § 204][/us/pl/90/577/s204], Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1101][/us/stat/82/1101], related to eligibility of State agencies to administer a grant-in-aid program. See [section 6504 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s6504].
