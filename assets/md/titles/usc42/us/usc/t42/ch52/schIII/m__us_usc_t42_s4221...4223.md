## §§ 4221 to 4223. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], § 5(b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068][/us/stat/96/1068]

    Section 4221, [Pub. L. 90–577, title III][/us/pl/90/577/tIII], § 301, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1102][/us/stat/82/1102], set out the statement of purpose for the provision of special or technical services to State and local units of government by Federal departments and agencies.

    Section 4222, [Pub. L. 90–577, title III][/us/pl/90/577/tIII], § 302, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1102][/us/stat/82/1102]; 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 102, eff. July 1, 1970, 35 F.R. 7959, [84 Stat. 2085][/us/stat/84/2085], authorized Federal departments and agencies to provide specialized or technical services to States or their political subdivisions. See section 6505(a), (b) of Title 31, Money and Finance.

    Section 4223, [Pub. L. 90–577, title III][/us/pl/90/577/tIII], § 303, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1102][/us/stat/82/1102], related to reimbursement of appropriations to Federal departments and agencies. See [section 6505(c) of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s6505/c].
