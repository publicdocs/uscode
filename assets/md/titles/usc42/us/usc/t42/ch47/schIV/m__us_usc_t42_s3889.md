## § 3889. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–415, title IV, § 402(b)][/us/pl/93/415/s402/b], Sept. 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 1133][/us/stat/88/1133]

    Section, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV, § 409][/us/pl/90/445/s409], as added [Pub. L. 92–381, § 1][/us/pl/92/381/s1], Aug. 14, 1972, [86 Stat. 537][/us/stat/86/537], related to preparation and submission of a report to the President by the Interdepartmental Council concerning all Federal activities in the field of juvenile delinquency.

    A prior section 3889, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV, § 409][/us/pl/90/445/s409], July 31, 1968, [82 Stat. 473][/us/stat/82/473], related to appointment of advisory committees by the Secretary in respect to activities under prior chapter 47, prior to the general amendment of [Pub.  __Repeals__ 

    Title IV of [Pub. L. 93–415][/us/pl/93/415], which repealed this section, was repealed by [Pub. L. 95–115, § 10][/us/pl/95/115/s10], Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1061][/us/stat/91/1061], and [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title II, § 12221(a)(4)][/us/pl/107/273/s12221/a/4], Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1894][/us/stat/116/1894].

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 95–115][/us/pl/95/115] effective Oct. 1, 1977, see [section 263(c) of Pub. L. 93–415][/us/pl/93/415/s263/c], as added by [Pub. L. 95–115][/us/pl/95/115], set out as an Effective Date of 1977 Amendment note under [section 5601 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s5601].

§§ 3890, 3891. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Appropriations for this subchapter have not been authorized for fiscal years after 1975.

    Section 3890, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV, § 410][/us/pl/90/445/s410], as added [Pub. L. 92–381, § 1][/us/pl/92/381/s1], Aug. 14, 1972, [86 Stat. 538][/us/stat/86/538], prohibited application of this chapter in such a way as to be detrimental to parental and individual rights.

    A prior section 3890, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV, § 410][/us/pl/90/445/s410], July 31, 1968, [82 Stat. 473][/us/stat/82/473], related to definitions, prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 90–445][/us/pl/90/445] by [section 1 of Pub. L. 92–381][/us/pl/92/381/s1].

L. 93–644, § 9(a)][/us/pl/93/644/s9/a], Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 805][/us/stat/88/805], provided definitions for purposes of this chapter.
