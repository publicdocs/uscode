§§ 3861, 3862. __Omitted__ 

 __Codification__

L. 92–381, § 1][/us/pl/92/381/s1], Aug. 14, 1972, [86 Stat. 534][/us/stat/86/534], related to project grants and contracts for the training of personnel in fields related to diagnosis and treatment of delinquent youths and parental counseling or instruction.
