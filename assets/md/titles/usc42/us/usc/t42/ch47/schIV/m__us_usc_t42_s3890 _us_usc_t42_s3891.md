###### §§ 3890, 3891. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Appropriations for this subchapter have not been authorized for fiscal years after 1975.

    Section 3890, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV][/us/pl/90/445/tIV], § 410, as added [Pub. L. 92–381][/us/pl/92/381], § 1, Aug. 14, 1972, [86 Stat. 538][/us/stat/86/538], prohibited application of this chapter in such a way as to be detrimental to parental and individual rights.

    A prior section 3890, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV][/us/pl/90/445/tIV], § 410, July 31, 1968, [82 Stat. 473][/us/stat/82/473], related to definitions, prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 90–445][/us/pl/90/445] by [section 1 of Pub. L. 92–381][/us/pl/92/381/s1].

    Section 3891, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV][/us/pl/90/445/tIV], § 411, as added [Pub. L. 92–381][/us/pl/92/381], § 1, Aug. 14, 1972, [86 Stat. 538][/us/stat/86/538]; amended [Pub. L. 93–644][/us/pl/93/644], § 9(a), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 805][/us/stat/88/805], provided definitions for purposes of this chapter.

    Provisions similar to those comprising this section were contained in prior section 3890, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV][/us/pl/90/445/tIV], § 410, July 31, 1968, [82 Stat. 473][/us/stat/82/473], prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 90–445][/us/pl/90/445] by [section 1 of Pub. L. 92–381][/us/pl/92/381/s1].
