###### §§ 3861, 3862. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Appropriations for this subchapter have not been authorized for fiscal years after 1975.

    Section 3861, [Pub. L. 90–445, title II][/us/pl/90/445/tII], § 201, as added [Pub. L. 92–381][/us/pl/92/381], § 1, Aug. 14, 1972, [86 Stat. 534][/us/stat/86/534], related to project grants and contracts for the training of personnel in fields related to diagnosis and treatment of delinquent youths and parental counseling or instruction.

    A prior section 3861, [Pub. L. 90–445, title II][/us/pl/90/445/tII], § 201, July 31, 1968, [82 Stat. 470][/us/stat/82/470], related to similar subject matter, prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 90–445][/us/pl/90/445] by [section 1 of Pub. L. 92–381][/us/pl/92/381/s1].
