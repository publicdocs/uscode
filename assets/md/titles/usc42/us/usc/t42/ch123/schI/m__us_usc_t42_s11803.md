## § 11803. Approval of applications

In selecting among applications submitted under [section 11802(a) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s11802/a], the Secretary shall give priority to applicants who propose to carry out projects and activities—

    (1) for the purposes specified in [section 11801 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s11801] in geographical areas in which frequent and severe drug-related crimes are committed by gangs whose membership is composed primarily of youth, and

    (2) that the applicant demonstrates have the broad support of community based organizations in such geographical areas.

([Pub. L. 100–690, title III, § 3503][/us/pl/100/690/s3503], Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4255][/us/stat/102/4255]; [Pub. L. 101–204, title X, § 1001(a)][/us/pl/101/204/s1001/a], Dec. 7, 1989, [103 Stat. 1826][/us/stat/103/1826].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1989—Par. (2). [Pub. L. 101–204][/us/pl/101/204] substituted “have” for “that it has”.
