## § 1857c–10. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–95, title I][/us/pl/95/95/tI], § 112(b)(1), Aug. 7, 1977, [91 Stat. 709][/us/stat/91/709]

    Section, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I][/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI], § 119, as added June 22, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–319][/us/pl/93/319], § 3, [88 Stat. 248][/us/stat/88/248], related to the authority of the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency to deal with energy shortages. See [section 7413 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s7413].

    References to section 1857c–10 appearing in [section 792 of Title 15][/us/usc/t15/s792], Commerce and Trade, shall be construed to refer to [section 7413(d) of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s7413/d], The Public Health and Welfare, see Compliance Orders note set out under [section 792 of Title 15][/us/usc/t15/s792].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Aug. 7, 1977, see [section 406 of Pub. L. 95–95][/us/pl/95/95/s406], set out as an Effective Date of 1977 Amendment note under [section 7401 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s7401].
