## § 290cc–13. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title V, § 520, formerly § 520A, as added Nov. 18, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–690, title II, § 2057(3)][/us/pl/100/690/s2057/3], [102 Stat. 4212][/us/stat/102/4212], and amended, which related to establishment of grant programs for demonstration projects for drug abuse research, was renumbered section 520A of act July 1, 1944 by [Pub. L. 102–321, title I, § 116(a)][/us/pl/102/321/s116/a], July 10, 1992, [106 Stat. 348][/us/stat/106/348], and transferred to [section 290bb–32 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s290bb–32].
