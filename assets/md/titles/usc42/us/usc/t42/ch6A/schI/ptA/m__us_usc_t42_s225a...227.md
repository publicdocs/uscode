## §§ 225a to 227. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 225a, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV], § 507, as added June 24, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–31][/us/pl/90/31], § 5, [81 Stat. 79][/us/stat/81/79]; amended Oct. 27, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–513, title I][/us/pl/91/513/tI], § 3(c), [84 Stat. 1241][/us/stat/84/1241]; Apr. 22, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–278, title XI][/us/pl/94/278/tXI], § 1102(b), [90 Stat. 415][/us/stat/90/415]; Oct. 7, 1980, [Pub. L. 96–398, title VIII][/us/pl/96/398/tVIII], § 804(b), [94 Stat. 1603][/us/stat/94/1603]; Aug. 13, 1981, [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX][/us/pl/97/35/tIX], § 902(g)(2), [95 Stat. 560][/us/stat/95/560], which related to availability of appropriations for grants to Federal institutions, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238d of this title][/us/usc/t42/s238d].

    A prior section 507 of [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV], providing for settlement of accounts of deceased officers, was classified to [section 225 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s225] and subsequently repealed.

    Section 226, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV], § 508, [58 Stat. 711][/us/stat/58/711]; 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 1, §§ 5, 8, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631][/us/stat/67/631]; 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 102, eff. July 1, 1970, 35 F.R. 7959, [84 Stat. 2085][/us/stat/84/2085], which related to transfer of funds between appropriations, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238e of this title][/us/usc/t42/s238e].

    Section 227, acts [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title V, § 509 [58 Stat. 711][/us/stat/58/711]; [June 16, 1948, ch. 481][/us/act/1948-06-16/ch481], § 6(b), [62 Stat. 469][/us/stat/62/469]; [June 25, 1948, ch. 654][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch654], § 7, [62 Stat. 1018][/us/stat/62/1018]; Reorg. Plan No. 1 of 1953 §§ 5, 8, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631][/us/stat/67/631], which related to availability of appropriations for carrying out purposes of this chapter, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238f of this title][/us/usc/t42/s238f].
