## §§ 300ll to 300ll–9. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–240, title VI, § 642(a)][/us/pl/112/240/s642/a], Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2358][/us/stat/126/2358]

    Section 300ll, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3201, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 828][/us/stat/124/828], established the purpose of this subchapter.

    Section 300ll–1, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3202, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 828][/us/stat/124/828], set out definitions.

    Section 300ll–2, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3203, as added and amended [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], title X, § 10801(a)(1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 830][/us/stat/124/830], 1015, required the Secretary to develop at least 3 actuarially sound benefit plans as alternatives for consideration for designation by the Secretary as the CLASS Independence Benefit Plan.

    Section 300ll–3, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3204, as added and amended [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], title X, § 10801(a)(2), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 834][/us/stat/124/834], 1015, related to enrollment and disenrollment requirements.

    Section 300ll–4, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3205, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 836][/us/stat/124/836], related to benefits and determination of eligibility.

    Section 300ll–5, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3206, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 842][/us/stat/124/842], related to the CLASS Independence Fund.

    Section 300ll–6, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3207, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 844][/us/stat/124/844], created the CLASS Independence Advisory Council.

    Section 300ll–7, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3208, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 845][/us/stat/124/845], related to solvency and fiscal independence of the CLASS program and required regulations and annual reports.

    Section 300ll–8, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3209, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 845][/us/stat/124/845], required the Inspector General of the Department of Health and Human Services to submit an annual report on CLASS program progress and waste, fraud, and abuse.

    Section 300ll–9, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXXII, § 3210, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s8002/a/1], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 846][/us/stat/124/846], provided that the CLASS program would be treated for tax purposes as a qualified long-term care insurance contract for qualified long-term care services.

L. 111–148][/us/pl/111/148] (enacting this subchapter, amending [section 1396a of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1396a], enacting provisions set out as notes under this section and [section 201 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s201], and amending provisions set out as a note under [section 1396p of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1396p]) was intended to replace or displace public or private disability insurance benefits, including such benefits for income replacement, was repealed by [Pub. L. 112–240, title VI, § 642(b)(1)][/us/pl/112/240/s642/b/1], Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2358][/us/stat/126/2358].
