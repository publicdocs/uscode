## § 291n. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–174, § 3(b)(1)][/us/pl/90/174/s3/b/1], Dec. 5, 1967, [81 Stat. 535][/us/stat/81/535]

    Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title VI, § 644, formerly § 624, as added Aug. 18, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–443, § 3(a)][/us/pl/88/443/s3/a], [78 Stat. 459][/us/stat/78/459], and renumbered § 644, June 30, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–296, title II, § 201][/us/pl/91/296/s201], [84 Stat. 344][/us/stat/84/344], provided for research, experiments and demonstrations in utilization of medical facilities, authorization, grants-in-aid, amounts, payment, conditions, authorization of appropriations, and right of recovery of United States Government. See [section 242b of this title][/us/usc/t42/s242b].

    Provisions similar to those comprising this section were contained in a prior section 291n, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title VI, § 636, as added [Oct. 25, 1949, ch. 722, § 5][/us/act/1949-10-25/ch722/s5], [63 Stat. 900][/us/stat/63/900]; amended Oct. 6, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–395, § 4][/us/pl/87/395/s4], [75 Stat. 825][/us/stat/75/825], prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 88–443][/us/pl/88/443].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective with respect to appropriations for fiscal year ending after June 30, 1967, see [section 3(b) of Pub. L. 90–174][/us/pl/90/174/s3/b], set out as an Effective Date of 1967 Amendment note under [section 246 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s246].
