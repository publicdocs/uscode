##### subpart xi—support of graduate medical education in qualified teaching health centers

 __Codification__ 

    Subpart is comprised of subpart XI of part D of title III of act July 1, 1944, as added by [Pub. L. 111–148, title V, § 5508(c)][/us/pl/111/148/s5508/c], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 670][/us/stat/124/670]. Another subpart XI of part D of title III of the Act was added by [Pub.
