## § 300dd–41. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXIV, § 2441, as added Nov. 4, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–607, title II, § 211][/us/pl/100/607/s211], [102 Stat. 3092][/us/stat/102/3092], which related to demonstration projects for individuals with positive AIDS test results, was renumbered section 520B of act July 1, 1944, by [Pub. L. 102–321, title I, § 118(a)][/us/pl/102/321/s118/a], July 10, 1992, [106 Stat. 348][/us/stat/106/348], and was transferred to [section 290bb–33 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s290bb–33], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 106–310, div. B, title XXXII, § 3201(b)(2)][/us/pl/106/310/s3201/b/2], Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1190][/us/stat/114/1190].
