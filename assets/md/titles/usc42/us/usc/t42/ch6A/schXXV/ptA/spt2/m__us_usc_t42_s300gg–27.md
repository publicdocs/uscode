## § 300gg–27. Required coverage for reconstructive surgery following mastectomies

    The provisions of [section 1185b of title 29][/us/usc/t29/s1185b] shall apply to group health plans, and and  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. </sup></sup>  health insurance issuers offering group or individual health insurance coverage, as if included in this subpart.

([July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XXVII, § 2727, formerly § 2706, as added [Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(f)][/us/pl/105/277/s101/f] \[title IX, § 903(a)\], Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–337][/us/stat/112/2681-337], 2681–438; renumbered § 2727 and amended [Pub. L. 111–148, title I][/us/pl/111/148], §§ 1001(2), 1563(c)(5), formerly § 1562(c)(5), title X, § 10107(b)(1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 130][/us/stat/124/130], 266, 911.)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 300gg–6 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s300gg–6] prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 111–148][/us/pl/111/148].

 __Amendments__ 

    2010—[Pub. L. 111–148, § 1563(c)(5)][/us/pl/111/148/s1563/c/5], formerly § 1562(c)(5), as renumbered by [Pub. L. 111–148, § 10107(b)(1)][/us/pl/111/148/s10107/b/1], substituted “and health insurance issuers offering group or individual health insurance coverage” for “health insurance issuers providing health insurance coverage in connection with group health plans”.

 __Effective Date__ 

[Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(f)][/us/pl/105/277/s101/f] \[title IX, § 903(c)(1)\], Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–337][/us/stat/112/2681-337], 2681–438, provided that:

>     “(A)  __In general__  __.—__ 

>     The amendment made by subsection (a) \[enacting this section\] shall apply to group health plans for plan years beginning on or after the date of enactment of this Act \[Oct. 21, 1998\].

>     “(B)  __Special rule for collective bargaining agreements__  __.—__ 

>     In the case of a group health plan maintained pursuant to 1 or more collective bargaining agreements between employee representatives and 1 or more employers, any plan amendment made pursuant to a collective bargaining agreement relating to the plan which amends the plan solely to conform to any requirement added by the amendment made by subsection (a) shall not be treated as a termination of such collective bargaining agreement.”
