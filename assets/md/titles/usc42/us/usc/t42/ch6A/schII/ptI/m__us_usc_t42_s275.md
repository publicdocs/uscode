## § 275. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–43, title I][/us/pl/103/43/tI], § 121(a), June 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 133][/us/stat/107/133]

    Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIII], § 381, as added Nov. 20, 1985, [Pub. L. 99–158][/us/pl/99/158], § 11, [99 Stat. 883][/us/stat/99/883]; amended Nov. 4, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–607, title I][/us/pl/100/607/tI], § 157(a), [102 Stat. 3059][/us/stat/102/3059], established the Biomedical Ethics Board and provided for its membership, functions, reports to Congress, etc., and provided for appointment of a Biomedical Ethics Advisory Committee to assist the Biomedical Ethics Board.

    A prior section 275, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIII], § 381, formerly § 371, as added [Aug. 3, 1956, ch. 907][/us/act/1956-08-03/ch907], § 1, [70 Stat. 960][/us/stat/70/960]; renumbered § 381, Mar. 13, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–212][/us/pl/91/212], § 10(a)(3), [84 Stat. 66][/us/stat/84/66], established a National Library of Medicine in the Public Health Service and stated the congressional purposes for such establishment, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 99–158][/us/pl/99/158], § 3(b), Nov. 20, 1985, [99 Stat. 879][/us/stat/99/879].
