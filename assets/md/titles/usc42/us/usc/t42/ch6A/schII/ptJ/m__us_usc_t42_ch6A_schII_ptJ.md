#### Part J—Prevention and Control of Injuries

 __Amendments__ 

    1993—[Pub. L. 103–183, title II][/us/pl/103/183/tII], § 203(a)(1), Dec. 14, 1993, [107 Stat. 2232][/us/stat/107/2232], substituted “Prevention and Control of Injuries” for “Injury Control” in part heading.

    [Pub. L. 103–43, title XX][/us/pl/103/43/tXX], § 2008(i)(2)(B)(i), June 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 213][/us/stat/107/213], redesignated part K “Injury Control” as J. Former part J “Biomedical Ethics”, consisting of section 275, was repealed by [Pub. L. 103–43, title I][/us/pl/103/43/tI], § 121(a), June 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 133][/us/stat/107/133].

    1990—[Pub. L. 101–616, title I][/us/pl/101/616/tI], § 101(a)(1), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3279][/us/stat/104/3279], redesignated part I “Biomedical Ethics” as J. Former part J “Injury Control” redesignated K.
