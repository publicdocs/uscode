## § 211b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–412, title V, § 501(f)][/us/pl/94/412/s501/f], Sept. 14, 1976, [90 Stat. 1258][/us/stat/90/1258]

    Repeal not to affect any action taken or proceeding pending at the time of repeal, see [section 501(h) of Pub. L. 94–412][/us/pl/94/412/s501/h], set out as a note under [section 1601 of Title 50][/us/usc/t50/s1601], War and National Defense.
