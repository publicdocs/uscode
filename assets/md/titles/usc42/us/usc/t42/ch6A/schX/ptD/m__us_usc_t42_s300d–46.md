## § 300d–46. Definition

    In this part, the term “uncompensated care costs” means unreimbursed costs from serving self-pay, charity, or Medicaid patients, without regard to payment under [section 1396r–4 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1396r–4], all of which are attributable to emergency care and trauma care, including costs related to subsequent inpatient admissions to the hospital.

([July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XII, § 1246, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title III, § 3505(a)(6)][/us/pl/111/148/s3505/a/6], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 525][/us/stat/124/525].)
