### SUBCHAPTER XXV—REQUIREMENTS RELATING TO HEALTH INSURANCE COVERAGE

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior subchapter XXV (§ 300aaa et seq.), comprised of title XXVII of the Public Health Service Act, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], §§ 2701 to 2714, was renumbered title II, part B, §§ 231 to 244, of the Public Health Service Act, and transferred to part B (§ 238 et seq.) of subchapter I of this chapter.

 __Amendments__ 

    1996—[Pub. L. 104–204, title VI][/us/pl/104/204/tVI], § 604(a)(1), Sept. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 2938][/us/stat/110/2938], substituted “REQUIREMENTS RELATING TO HEALTH INSURANCE COVERAGE” for “ASSURING PORTABILITY, AVAILABILITY, AND RENEWABILITY OF HEALTH INSURANCE COVERAGE” as subchapter heading.
