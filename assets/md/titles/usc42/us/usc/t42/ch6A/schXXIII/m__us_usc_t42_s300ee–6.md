## § 300ee–6. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 100–607, title IX][/us/pl/100/607/tIX], § 902, Nov. 4, 1988, [102 Stat. 3171][/us/stat/102/3171]; [Pub. L. 100–690, title II][/us/pl/100/690/tII], § 2605(a), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4234][/us/stat/102/4234], which provided for testing of State prisoners, was renumbered section 2648 of the Public Health Service Act by [Pub. L. 101–381, title III][/us/pl/101/381/tIII], § 301(b)(1), Aug. 18, 1990, [104 Stat. 615][/us/stat/104/615], and transferred to [section 300ff–48 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s300ff–48], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 106–345, title III][/us/pl/106/345/tIII], § 301(a), Oct. 20, 2000, [114 Stat. 1345][/us/stat/114/1345].
