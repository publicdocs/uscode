## § 242d. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title III, § 306, as added [Aug. 2, 1956, ch. 871][/us/act/1956-08-02/ch871], title I, § 101, [70 Stat. 923][/us/stat/70/923]; amended July 23, 1959, [Pub.
