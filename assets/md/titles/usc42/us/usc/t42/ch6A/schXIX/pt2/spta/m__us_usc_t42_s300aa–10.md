## § 300aa–10. Establishment of program

    (a) __Program established__ 

        There is established the National Vaccine Injury Compensation Program to be administered by the Secretary under which compensation may be paid for a vaccine-related injury or death.

    (b) __Attorney’s obligation__ 

        It shall be the ethical obligation of any attorney who is consulted by an individual with respect to a vaccine-related injury or death to advise such individual that compensation may be available under the program  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 So in original. Probably should be capitalized. </sup></sup>  for such injury or death.

    (c) __Publicity__ 

        The Secretary shall undertake reasonable efforts to inform the public of the availability of the Program.
