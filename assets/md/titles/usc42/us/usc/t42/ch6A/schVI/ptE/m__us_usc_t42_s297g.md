## § 297g. Modification of agreements; compromise, waiver or release

    The Secretary may agree to modifications of agreements made under this part, and may compromise, waive, or release any right, title, claim, or demand of the United States arising or acquired under this part.

([July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title VIII, § 840, formerly § 828, as added [Pub. L. 88–581, § 2][/us/pl/88/581/s2], Sept. 4, 1964, [78 Stat. 917][/us/stat/78/917]; renumbered § 840 and amended [Pub. L. 94–63, title IX, § 941(h)(1)][/us/pl/94/63/s941/h/1], (4)(C), (i)(1), July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 365][/us/stat/89/365]; [Pub. L. 111–148, title V, § 5310(b)(2)][/us/pl/111/148/s5310/b/2], Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 631][/us/stat/124/631].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2010—[Pub. L. 111–148][/us/pl/111/148] substituted “this part” for “this subpart” in two places.

    1975—[Pub. L. 94–63, § 941(h)(1)][/us/pl/94/63/s941/h/1], (4)(C), substituted “subpart” for “part” in two places and struck out “or loans” after “agreements”.

 __Effective Date of 1975 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 94–63][/us/pl/94/63] effective July 1, 1975, see [section 942 of Pub. L. 94–63][/us/pl/94/63/s942], set out as a note under [section 297a of this title][/us/usc/t42/s297a].
