## § 300d–2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–23, § 3(1)][/us/pl/110/23/s3/1], May 3, 2007, [121 Stat. 90][/us/stat/121/90]

    Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XII, § 1202, formerly § 1203, as added [Pub. L. 101–590, § 3][/us/pl/101/590/s3], Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2917][/us/stat/104/2917]; renumbered § 1202, [Pub. L. 103–183, title VI, § 601(b)(2)][/us/pl/103/183/s601/b/2], Dec. 14, 1993, [107 Stat. 2238][/us/stat/107/2238]; amended [Pub. L. 105–392, title IV, § 401(a)(1)(A)][/us/pl/105/392/s401/a/1/A], Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3587][/us/stat/112/3587], required the Secretary to provide for the establishment and operation of a National Clearinghouse on Trauma Care and Emergency Medical Services.

    A prior section 300d–2, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XII, § 1203, as added Nov. 16, 1973, [Pub. L. 93–154, § 2(a)][/us/pl/93/154/s2/a], [87 Stat. 596][/us/stat/87/596]; amended Oct. 21, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–573, § 4][/us/pl/94/573/s4], [90 Stat. 2710][/us/stat/90/2710]; Nov. 10, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–626, title II, § 210(a)][/us/pl/95/626/s210/a], [92 Stat. 3588][/us/stat/92/3588]; July 10, 1979, [Pub. L. 96–32, § 7][/us/pl/96/32/s7](l), [93 Stat. 84][/us/stat/93/84], set forth provisions relating to grants and contracts for establishing and initial operation of emergency medical services systems, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX, § 902(d)(1)][/us/pl/97/35/s902/d/1], (h), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 560][/us/stat/95/560], 561, effective Oct. 1, 1981.
