## § 300b–16. Authorization of appropriations for newborn screening programs and activities

    There are authorized to be appropriated—

    (1) to carry out sections 300b–8, 300b–9, 300b–10, and 300b–11, $11,900,000 for each of fiscal years 2015 through 2019; and

    (2) to carry out section 300b–12, $8,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2015 through 2019.

([July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XI, § 1117, as added [Pub. L. 113–240, § 10][/us/pl/113/240/s10], Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 2856][/us/stat/128/2856].)
