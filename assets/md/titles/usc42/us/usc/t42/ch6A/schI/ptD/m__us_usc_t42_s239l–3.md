## § 239l–3. Funding

    Beginning with fiscal year 2010, the Secretary shall transfer from the Public Health and Social Services Emergency Fund such sums as may be necessary to carry out this part.

([July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title II, § 274, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title V][/us/pl/111/148/tV], § 5315, Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 642][/us/stat/124/642].)

 __Transfer of Appropriated Funds__ 

[Pub. L. 112–10, div. B, title VIII][/us/pl/112/10/dB/tVIII], § 1828, Apr. 15, 2011, [125 Stat. 162][/us/stat/125/162], provided that: 

> “Hereafter, no funds appropriated by this division or by any previous or subsequent Act shall be available for transfer under section 274 \[[42 U.S.C. 239][/us/usc/t42/s239]l–3\] of the PHS Act \[Public Health Service Act\].”
