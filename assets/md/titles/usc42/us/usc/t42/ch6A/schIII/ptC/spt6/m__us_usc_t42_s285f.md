## § 285f. Purpose of Institute

    The general purpose of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases is the conduct and support of research, training, health information dissemination, and other programs with respect to allergic and immunologic diseases and disorders and infectious diseases, including tropical diseases.

([July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title IV, § 446, as added [Pub. L. 99–158][/us/pl/99/158], § 2, Nov. 20, 1985, [99 Stat. 855][/us/stat/99/855]; amended [Pub. L. 103–43, title IX][/us/pl/103/43/tIX], § 901, June 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 164][/us/stat/107/164].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1993—[Pub. L. 103–43][/us/pl/103/43] inserted before period at end “, including tropical diseases”.
