## § 290cc–21. Formula grants to States

    For the purpose of carrying out [section 290cc–22 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s290cc–22], the Secretary, acting through the Director of the Center for Mental Health Services, shall for each of the fiscal years 1991 through 1994 make an allotment for each State in an amount determined in accordance with [section 290cc–24 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s290cc–24]. The Secretary shall make payments, as grants, each such fiscal year to each State from the allotment for the State if the Secretary approves for the fiscal year involved an application submitted by the State pursuant to [section 290cc–29 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s290cc–29].

 __Amendments__
