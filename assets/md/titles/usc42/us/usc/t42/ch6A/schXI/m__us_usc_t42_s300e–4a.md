    Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XIII][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXIII], § 1305A, as added Nov. 1, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–559][/us/pl/95/559], § 5(a), [92 Stat. 2133][/us/stat/92/2133]; amended July 10, 1979, [Pub. L. 96–32][/us/pl/96/32], § 2(e), [93 Stat. 82][/us/stat/93/82]; Aug. 13, 1981, [Pub.
