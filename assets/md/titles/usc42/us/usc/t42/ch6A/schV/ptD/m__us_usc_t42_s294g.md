## § 294g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–148, title V][/us/pl/111/148/tV], § 5306(a)(1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 626][/us/stat/124/626]

    Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title VII][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tVII], § 757, as added [Pub. L. 105–392, title I][/us/pl/105/392/tI], § 103, Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3551][/us/stat/112/3551], authorized appropriations to carry out this part.

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 111–148, title V][/us/pl/111/148/tV], § 5306(a)(1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 626][/us/stat/124/626], which directed the amendment of part D of title VII by striking section 757, without specifying the act to be amended, was executed by repealing this section, which was section 757 of act July 1, 1944, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
