## § 292p. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __In general__ 

        For fiscal year 1993 and subsequent fiscal years, there are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for the adequacy of the student loan insurance account under this subpart and for the purpose of administering this subpart.

    (b) __Availability of sums__ 

        Sums appropriated under subsection (a) of this section shall remain available until expended.

([July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title VII, § 720, as added [Pub. L. 102–408, title I][/us/pl/102/408/tI], § 102, Oct. 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 2011][/us/stat/106/2011].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 720 of act July 1, 1944, was classified to [section 293 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s293] prior to the general revision of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 102–408][/us/pl/102/408].
