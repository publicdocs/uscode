## § 10250. Termination of Office

    The Office shall cease to exist not later than 30 days after the date 7 years after December 22, 1987.

([Pub. L. 97–425, title IV][/us/pl/97/425/tIV], § 410, as added [Pub. L. 100–202][/us/pl/100/202], § 101(d) \[title III\], Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1329–104][/us/stat/101/1329-104], 1329–121; [Pub. L. 100–203, title V][/us/pl/100/203/tV], § 5041, Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–247][/us/stat/101/1330-247]; amended [Pub. L. 102–486, title VIII][/us/pl/102/486/tVIII], § 802(a), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2923][/us/stat/106/2923].)

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 100–202][/us/pl/100/202] and [Pub. L. 100–203][/us/pl/100/203] added identical sections.

 __Amendments__ 

    1992—[Pub. L. 102–486][/us/pl/102/486] substituted “7 years” for “5 years”.
