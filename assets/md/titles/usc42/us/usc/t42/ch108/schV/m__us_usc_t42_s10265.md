## § 10265. Compensation of members

    (a) __In general__ 

        Each member of the Board shall be paid at the rate of pay payable for level III of the Executive Schedule for each day (including travel time) such member is engaged in the work of the Board.

    (b) __Travel expenses__ 

        Each member of the Board may receive travel expenses, including per diem in lieu of subsistence, in the same manner as is permitted under sections 5702 and 5703 of title 5.

([Pub. L. 97–425, title V][/us/pl/97/425/tV], § 505, as added [Pub. L. 100–202][/us/pl/100/202], § 101(d) \[title III\], Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1329–104][/us/stat/101/1329-104], 1329–121; [Pub. L. 100–203, title V][/us/pl/100/203/tV], § 5051, Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–249][/us/stat/101/1330-249].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Level III of the Executive Schedule, referred to in subsec. (a), is set out in [section 5314 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s5314], Government Organization and Employees.

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 100–202][/us/pl/100/202] and [Pub. L. 100–203][/us/pl/100/203] added identical sections.
