## § 10174. Consideration in siting facilities

    The Secretary, in siting Federal research projects, shall give special consideration to proposals from States where a repository is located.

([Pub. L. 97–425, title I][/us/pl/97/425/tI], § 174, as added [Pub. L. 100–202][/us/pl/100/202], § 101(d) \[title III, § 300\], Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1329–104][/us/stat/101/1329-104], 1329–121; [Pub. L. 100–203, title V][/us/pl/100/203/tV], § 5031, Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–240][/us/stat/101/1330-240].)

 __Codification__
