## § 10261. Definitions

    As used in this subchapter:

    (1) The term “Chairman” means the Chairman of the Nuclear Waste Technical Review Board.

    (2) The term “Board” means the Nuclear Waste Technical Review Board established under [section 10262 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s10262].

([Pub. L. 97–425, title V][/us/pl/97/425/tV], § 501, as added [Pub. L. 100–202][/us/pl/100/202], § 101(d) \[title III\], Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1329–104][/us/stat/101/1329-104], 1329–121; [Pub. L. 100–203, title V][/us/pl/100/203/tV], § 5051, Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–248][/us/stat/101/1330-248].)
