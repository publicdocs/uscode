## § 2473c. Repealed or Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 102–195, § 21][/us/pl/102/195/s21], Dec. 9, 1991, [105 Stat. 1616][/us/stat/105/1616], related to drug and alcohol testing for certain employees of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.

    Subsec. (a) was repealed by [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6][/us/pl/111/314/s6], Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444][/us/stat/124/3444].

    Subsec. (b) was transferred and is set out as a note under [section 31102 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s31102], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Subsecs. (c) to (h) were repealed by [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6][/us/pl/111/314/s6], Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444][/us/stat/124/3444]. See [section 31102 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s31102].
