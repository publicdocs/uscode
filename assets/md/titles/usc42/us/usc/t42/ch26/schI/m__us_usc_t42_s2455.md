## § 2455. Repealed or Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 85–568, title III, § 304(a)][/us/pl/85/568/s304/a], (b), July 29, 1958, [72 Stat. 433][/us/stat/72/433], 434; 1978 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 102, eff. Jan. 1, 1979, 43 F.R. 36037, [92 Stat. 3783][/us/stat/92/3783], related to security requirements.

    Subsec. (a) was repealed by [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6][/us/pl/111/314/s6], Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444][/us/stat/124/3444]. See [section 20132 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s20132], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Subsec. (b) was transferred and is set out as a note under [section 20132 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s20132].
