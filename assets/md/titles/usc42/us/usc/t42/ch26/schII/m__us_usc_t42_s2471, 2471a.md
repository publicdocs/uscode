## §§ 2471, 2471a. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 2471, [Pub. L. 100–685, title V, § 501][/us/pl/100/685/s501], Nov. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 4102][/us/stat/102/4102], which related to the National Space Council, was transferred and is set out as a note under [section 20111 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s20111], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    A prior section 2471, [Pub. L. 85–568, title II, § 201][/us/pl/85/568/s201], July 29, 1958, [72 Stat. 427][/us/stat/72/427]; [Pub. L. 87–26, § 1][/us/pl/87/26/s1], Apr. 25, 1961, [75 Stat. 46][/us/stat/75/46]; [Pub. L. 87–367, title II, § 207][/us/pl/87/367/s207], Oct. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 792][/us/stat/75/792]; [Pub. L. 87–584, § 7][/us/pl/87/584/s7], Aug. 14, 1962, [76 Stat. 385][/us/stat/76/385]; [Pub. L. 88–426, title III][/us/pl/88/426], §§ 305(13)(A), 306(c), Aug. 14, 1964, [78 Stat. 423][/us/stat/78/423], 428; [Pub. L. 91–406][/us/pl/91/406], Sept. 23, 1970, [84 Stat. 855][/us/stat/84/855], which related to the establishment, membership, duties, and personnel of the National Aeronautics and Space Council, was omitted from the Code, because the Council, including the office of Executive Secretary of the Council, together with the functions of the Council, was abolished by section 3(a)(4) of Reorg. Plan No. 1 of 1973, 38 F.R. 9579, [87 Stat. 1089][/us/stat/87/1089], effective July 1, 1973, set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees. [Section 201 of Pub. L. 85–568][/us/pl/85/568/s201] was repealed by [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6][/us/pl/111/314/s6], Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444][/us/stat/124/3444], which Act enacted Title 51.

    Section 2471a, [Pub. L. 101–611, title I, § 121][/us/pl/101/611/s121], Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3204][/us/stat/104/3204], which related to Users’ Advisory Group, was transferred and is set out as a note under [section 20111 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s20111].
