## §§ 141 to 148. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section 141, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1378][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1378], § 1, [32 Stat. 728][/us/stat/32/728], provided for regulation of sale of and interstate traffic of viruses, serums, toxins, antitoxins, etc. See [section 262 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s262].

    Section 142, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1378][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1378], § 2, [32 Stat. 729][/us/stat/32/729], related to falsely labeling or marking container or package. See [section 262 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s262].

    Section 143, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1378][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1378], § 3, [32 Stat. 729][/us/stat/32/729], provided for inspection of manufacturing establishments. See [section 262 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s262].

    Section 144, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1378][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1378], § 4, [32 Stat. 729][/us/stat/32/729], provided for inspection of foreign manufacturing establishments. See [section 262 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s262].

    Section 145, acts [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370], § 1, [32 Stat. 712][/us/stat/32/712]; [July 1, 1902, ch. 1378][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1378], § 4, [32 Stat. 729][/us/stat/32/729]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288], [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], provided for issuing of licenses to manufacturing establishments. See [section 262 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s262].

    Section 146, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1378][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1378], § 5, [32 Stat. 729][/us/stat/32/729], provided for enforcement of regulations. See [section 262 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s262].

    Section 147, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1378][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1378], § 6, [32 Stat. 729][/us/stat/32/729], provided against interfering with officers. See [section 262 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s262].

    Section 148, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1378][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1378], § 7, [32 Stat. 729][/us/stat/32/729], related to penalties for offenses. See [section 262 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s262].
