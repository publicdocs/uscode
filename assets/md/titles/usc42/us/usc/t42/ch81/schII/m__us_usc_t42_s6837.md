## § 6837. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 94–385, title III][/us/pl/94/385/tIII], § 308, as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title I][/us/pl/102/486/tI], § 101(a)(2), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2786][/us/stat/106/2786], which required the Secretary to report annually to Congress on activities conducted pursuant to this subchapter, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66][/us/pl/104/66/s3003], as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s1113], Money and Finance. See, also, the 4th item on page 88 of House Document No. 103–7.

L. 97–35, title X][/us/pl/97/35/tX], § 1041(e), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 621][/us/stat/95/621], related to technical assistance to States, etc., prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–486][/us/pl/102/486], § 101(a)(2).
