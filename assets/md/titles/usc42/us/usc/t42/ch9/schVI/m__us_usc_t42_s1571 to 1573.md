## §§ 1571 to 1573. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 1571, acts [Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 862][/us/act/1940-10-14/ch862], title V, § 501, as added [June 23, 1945, ch. 192][/us/act/1945-06-23/ch192], [59 Stat. 260][/us/stat/59/260]; amended [Apr. 20, 1950, ch. 94][/us/act/1950-04-20/ch94], title II, § 204, [64 Stat. 73][/us/stat/64/73], related to the construction of temporary housing facilities, and was omitted pursuant to [act July 3, 1952, ch. 570, § 1(a)(12)][/us/act/1952-07-03/ch570/s1/a/12], [66 Stat. 332][/us/stat/66/332], as amended by [act Mar. 31, 1953, ch. 13, § 1][/us/act/1953-03-31/ch13/s1], [67 Stat. 18][/us/stat/67/18], which provided that this section and sections 1572, 1573, 1575, and 1576 of this title continue in force until six months after the termination of the National emergency proclaimed by the President on Dec. 16, 1950 by Proc. No. 2914, 15 F.R. 9029, 64 Stat. A 454, set out as a note preceding section 1 of the Appendix to Title 50, War and National Defense, or on such earlier date or dates as provided by Congress, but in no event beyond July 1, 1953.

    Section 1572, acts [Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 862][/us/act/1940-10-14/ch862], title V, § 502, as added [June 23, 1945, ch. 192][/us/act/1945-06-23/ch192], [59 Stat. 260][/us/stat/59/260]; amended [Dec. 31, 1945, ch. 657][/us/act/1945-12-31/ch657], [59 Stat. 674][/us/stat/59/674]; [Mar. 28, 1946, ch. 118][/us/act/1946-03-28/ch118], §§ 1, 2, [60 Stat. 85][/us/stat/60/85]; [Aug. 8, 1946, ch. 917, § 1][/us/act/1946-08-08/ch917/s1], [60 Stat. 958][/us/stat/60/958]; [May 31, 1947, ch. 91, § 1][/us/act/1947-05-31/ch91/s1], [61 Stat. 128][/us/stat/61/128]; [Apr. 20, 1950, ch. 94][/us/act/1950-04-20/ch94], title II, § 204, [64 Stat. 73][/us/stat/64/73], related to the availability of funds for purposes of this subchapter, and was omitted in view of the termination of sections 1571 and 1573 of this title.

    Section 1573, acts [Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 862][/us/act/1940-10-14/ch862], title V, § 503, as added [June 23, 1945, ch. 192][/us/act/1945-06-23/ch192], [59 Stat. 260][/us/stat/59/260]; amended [June 30, 1953, ch. 174, § 1][/us/act/1953-06-30/ch174/s1], [67 Stat. 132][/us/stat/67/132], related to definitions for purposes of this subchapter, and was omitted pursuant to the time limitation set out in [act July 3, 1952, ch. 570, § 1(a)(21)][/us/act/1952-07-03/ch570/s1/a/21], [66 Stat. 332][/us/stat/66/332], as amended by [act Mar. 31, 1953, ch. 13, § 1][/us/act/1953-03-31/ch13/s1], [67 Stat. 18][/us/stat/67/18]. See [section 1571 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1571].

 __Availability of Funds__ 

    [Act May 31, 1947, ch. 91, § 2][/us/act/1947-05-31/ch91/s2], [61 Stat. 128][/us/stat/61/128], provided that there were to be additional funds available under sections 1571, 1572, and 1573 of this title for necessary expenses incurred in completing the provision of temporary housing pursuant to a contract in writing executed prior to May 31, 1947, for reimbursement of certain eligible organizations for particular expenditures, and for payments to meet certain actual expenses prior to Apr. 1, 1947.
