## §§ 92 to 96. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section 92, acts [Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114], § 3, [27 Stat. 450][/us/stat/27/450]; [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370], § 1, [32 Stat. 712][/us/stat/32/712]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288], § 1, [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], provided for enforcement of quarantine regulations.     Section 92a, acts [Apr. 29, 1878, ch. 66][/us/act/1878-04-29/ch66], § 5, [20 Stat. 38][/us/stat/20/38]; [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370], § 1, [32 Stat. 712][/us/stat/32/712]; Aug. 14, 1912, § 1, [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], provided for a national quarantine system. See sections 264 to 270 of this title.

    Section 94, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114], § 5, [27 Stat. 451][/us/stat/27/451], provided for regulations to secure sanitary conditions of vessels. See [section 269 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s269].

    Section 94a, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114], § 14, as added [Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409][/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409], § 1, [46 Stat. 1492][/us/stat/46/1492], provided for hours of inspection at quarantine stations. See [section 267 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s267].

    Section 94b, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114], § 16, as added [Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409][/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409], § 1, [46 Stat. 1492][/us/stat/46/1492], provided for schedule of charges and payment of them. See [section 269 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s269].

    Section 94c, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114], § 15, as added [Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409][/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409], § 1, [46 Stat. 1492][/us/stat/46/1492], provided for procurement of health certificates. See [section 269 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s269].

    Section 94d, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114], § 17, as added [Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409][/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409], § 1, [46 Stat. 1492][/us/stat/46/1492], provided medical and hospital benefits to officers and employees of national quarantine service. See [section 253 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s253].

    Section 94e, [act Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409][/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409], § 3, [46 Stat. 1492][/us/stat/46/1492], provided for extended quarantine service. See [section 267 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s267].

    Section 95, acts [Mar. 27, 1890, ch. 51][/us/act/1890-03-27/ch51], § 1, [26 Stat. 31][/us/stat/26/31]; [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370], § 1, [32 Stat. 712][/us/stat/32/712]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288], § 1, [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], related to regulations to prevent spread of communicable diseases. See [section 264 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s264].

    Section 96, [act June 19, 1906, ch. 3433][/us/act/1906-06-19/ch3433], § 6, [34 Stat. 301][/us/stat/34/301], provided that jurisdiction over established station acquired by the United States be ceded before payment of compensation.
