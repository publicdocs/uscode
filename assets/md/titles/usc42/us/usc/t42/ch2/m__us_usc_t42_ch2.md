§§ 81 to 87. __Repealed.__  __[__  __July 1, 1944, ch. 373__  __][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373]__  __, title XIII, § 1313,__  __[__  __58 Stat. 714__  __][/us/stat/58/714]__ 

    Section 81, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 1][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s1], [27 Stat. 449][/us/stat/27/449], provided penalties for entry of vessels in violation of quarantine laws. See [section 271 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s271].

    Section 82, acts [Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 2][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s2], [27 Stat. 450][/us/stat/27/450]; [Aug. 18, 1894, ch. 300][/us/act/1894-08-18/ch300], [28 Stat. 372][/us/stat/28/372]; [Feb. 27, 1921, ch. 80][/us/act/1921-02-27/ch80], [41 Stat. 1149][/us/stat/41/1149]; [Feb. 7, 1925, ch. 146][/us/act/1925-02-07/ch146], [43 Stat. 809][/us/stat/43/809]; [July 10, 1940, ch. 566][/us/act/1940-07-10/ch566], [54 Stat. 747][/us/stat/54/747], provided for bills of health. See [section 269 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s269].

    Section 82a, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 13][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s13], as added [Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409, § 1][/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409/s1], [46 Stat. 1491][/us/stat/46/1491], provided for duplicate bills of health. See [section 269 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s269].

    Section 83, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 11][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s11], as added [Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 836][/us/act/1901-03-03/ch836], [31 Stat. 1087][/us/stat/31/1087], provided that vessels from foreign ports without bill of health not entering the United States were subject to quarantine regulations.

    Section 84, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 6][/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s6], [27 Stat. 452][/us/stat/27/452], provided for disposition of infected vessels.

    Section 85, [act June 19, 1906, ch. 3433, § 4][/us/act/1906-06-19/ch3433/s4], [34 Stat. 300][/us/stat/34/300], provided penalties for infractions of quarantine. See [section 271 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s271].

    Section 86, [act Apr. 29, 1878, ch. 66, § 1][/us/act/1878-04-29/ch66/s1], [20 Stat. 37][/us/stat/20/37], prohibited entry of vessels and vehicles contrary to State quarantine laws. See sections 264 to 272 of this title. Compliance with State laws, see [section 97 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s97].

    Section 87, [act Apr. 17, 1917, ch. 3][/us/act/1917-04-17/ch3], [40 Stat. 6][/us/stat/40/6], provided for payment of cost of fumigation and disinfection of foreign vessels.
