## §§ 66 to 69. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section 66, [act Apr. 9, 1930, ch. 125, § 8][/us/act/1930-04-09/ch125/s8], [46 Stat. 151][/us/stat/46/151], provided for disability pay for commissioned officers. See [section 212 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s212].

    Section 67, [act May 14, 1937, ch. 180][/us/act/1937-05-14/ch180], title I, [50 Stat. 148][/us/stat/50/148], provided for compensation of field employees rendering part-time and call services. See [section 210 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s210].

    Section 68, acts [June 26, 1940, ch. 428][/us/act/1940-06-26/ch428], title II, [54 Stat. 584][/us/stat/54/584]; [July 1, 1941, ch. 269][/us/act/1941-07-01/ch269], title II, [55 Stat. 480][/us/stat/55/480], provided transportation funds for shipment of deceased officers. See [section 224 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s224].

    Section 69, acts [May 27, 1908, ch. 200][/us/act/1908-05-27/ch200], [35 Stat. 373][/us/stat/35/373]; [May 13, 1938, ch. 213][/us/act/1938-05-13/ch213], [52 Stat. 352][/us/stat/52/352]; [Oct. 28, 1943, ch. 289][/us/act/1943-10-28/ch289], [57 Stat. 583][/us/stat/57/583], provided for settlement of accounts of deceased officers and men. See [section 2771 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s2771], Armed Forces.
