## § 16. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, which was from the Interior Department Appropriation Act, 1950, [act Oct. 12, 1949, ch. 680][/us/act/1949-10-12/ch680], title I, [63 Stat. 791][/us/stat/63/791], was not repeated in the General Appropriation Act, 1951, [act Sept. 6, 1950, ch. 896][/us/act/1950-09-06/ch896], [64 Stat. 595][/us/stat/64/595].
