## § 70. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 655][/us/stat/80/655]

    Section, [act June 30, 1949, ch. 286, title I][/us/act/1949-06-30/ch286/tI], [63 Stat. 365][/us/stat/63/365], provided for a per diem allowance of officers detailed to the Coast Guard.

    Acts [June 26, 1943, ch. 147][/us/act/1943-06-26/ch147], title I, [57 Stat. 210][/us/stat/57/210]; [Mar. 31, 1943, ch. 30][/us/act/1943-03-31/ch30], § 1, [57 Stat. 55][/us/stat/57/55]; [June 22, 1944, ch. 269][/us/act/1944-06-22/ch269], title I, [58 Stat. 315][/us/stat/58/315]; [May 29, 1945, ch. 130][/us/act/1945-05-29/ch130], [59 Stat. 216][/us/stat/59/216]; [July 12, 1946, ch. 569][/us/act/1946-07-12/ch569], § 1, [60 Stat. 530][/us/stat/60/530]; [July 1, 1947, ch. 186][/us/act/1947-07-01/ch186], title I, [61 Stat. 225][/us/stat/61/225]; [June 19, 1948, ch. 558][/us/act/1948-06-19/ch558], title I, [62 Stat. 562][/us/stat/62/562], which contained provisions similar to [section 70 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s70], were repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 651–654][/us/stat/80/651-654].
