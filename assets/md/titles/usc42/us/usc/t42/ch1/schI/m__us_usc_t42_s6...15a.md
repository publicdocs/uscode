## §§ 6 to 15a. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section 6, acts [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370], § 1, [32 Stat. 712][/us/stat/32/712]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288], § 1, [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], provided for care of sick and disabled seamen. See [section 249 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s249].
