## §§ 3256 to 3259. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 3256 to 3259 were omitted in the general amendment of the Solid Waste Disposal Act by [Pub. L. 94–580][/us/pl/94/580], § 2, Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2795][/us/stat/90/2795].

    Section 3256, [Pub. L. 89–272, title II][/us/pl/89/272/tII], § 213, formerly § 207, Oct. 20, 1965, [79 Stat. 1000][/us/stat/79/1000], renumbered [Pub. L. 91–512, title I][/us/pl/91/512/tI], § 104(b), Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1229][/us/stat/84/1229], set the labor standards for construction projects funded by grants under this chapter. See [section 6979 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s6979].

    Section 3257, [Pub. L. 89–272, title II][/us/pl/89/272/tII], § 214, formerly § 208, Oct. 20, 1965, [79 Stat. 1000][/us/stat/79/1000], renumbered [Pub. L. 91–512, title I][/us/pl/91/512/tI], § 104(b), Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1229][/us/stat/84/1229], prohibited a construction of this chapter which might supersede or limit the authorities and responsibilities of Federal officers under other provisions of law. See [section 6905 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s6905].

    Section 3258, [Pub. L. 89–272, title II][/us/pl/89/272/tII], § 215, formerly § 209, Oct. 20, 1965, [79 Stat. 1001][/us/stat/79/1001], renumbered and amended [Pub. L. 91–512, title I][/us/pl/91/512/tI], § 104(b), (c), Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1229][/us/stat/84/1229], 1233, 1234, provided for mode of payments and prohibited making of grants to profitmaking organizations. See [section 6978 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s6978].

    Section 3259, [Pub. L. 89–272, title II][/us/pl/89/272/tII], § 216, formerly § 210, Oct. 20, 1965, [79 Stat. 1001][/us/stat/79/1001], amended [Pub. L. 90–574, title V][/us/pl/90/574/tV], § 506, Oct. 15, 1968, [82 Stat. 1013][/us/stat/82/1013], and renumbered and amended [Pub. L. 91–512, title I][/us/pl/91/512/tI], §§ 104(b), 105, Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1229][/us/stat/84/1229], 1234; [Pub. L. 93–14][/us/pl/93/14], § 1, Apr. 9, 1973, [87 Stat. 11][/us/stat/87/11]; [Pub. L. 93–611][/us/pl/93/611], Jan. 2, 1975, [88 Stat. 1974][/us/stat/88/1974], authorized appropriations. See [section 6987 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s6987].
