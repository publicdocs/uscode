## § 7271. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 95–509, title II, § 208][/us/pl/95/509/s208], Oct. 24, 1978, [92 Stat. 1779][/us/stat/92/1779], which related to single annual requests by the Secretary for authorizations of appropriations for programs involving the common defense and security of the United States, was renumbered [section 4731 of Pub. L. 107–314][/us/pl/107/314/s4731], the Bob Stump National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2003, by [Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(j)(7)][/us/pl/108/136/s3141/j/7], Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1782][/us/stat/117/1782], and was classified to [section 2771 of Title 50][/us/usc/t50/s2771], War and National Defense, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(u)(1)][/us/pl/112/239/s3131/u/1], Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2184][/us/stat/126/2184].
