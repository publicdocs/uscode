## § 7274r. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 108–7, div. D, title III, § 308][/us/pl/108/7/s308], Feb. 20, 2003, [117 Stat. 154][/us/stat/117/154], which related to research, development, and demonstration activities with respect to engineering and manufacturing capabilities at covered nuclear weapons production plants, was transferred and is listed in a similar provisions note under the heading Activities at Covered Nuclear Weapons Facilities under [section 2812 of Title 50][/us/usc/t50/s2812], War and National Defense.

 __Engineering and Manufacturing Research, Development, and Demonstration by Plant Managers of Certain Nuclear Weapons Production Plants__ 

    [Pub. L. 106–398, § 1][/us/pl/106/398/s1] \[div. C, title XXXI, § 3156\], Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1654][/us/stat/114/1654], 1654A–467, which was formerly set out as a note under this section, was renumbered [section 4832 of Pub. L. 107–314][/us/pl/107/314/s4832], the Bob Stump National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2003, by [Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(k)(12)][/us/pl/108/136/s3141/k/12], Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1785][/us/stat/117/1785], and is classified to [section 2812 of Title 50][/us/usc/t50/s2812], War and National Defense.
