## § 7385s. Definitions

In this part:

    (1) The term “covered DOE contractor employee” means any Department of Energy contractor employee determined under [section 7385s–4 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s7385s–4] to have contracted a covered illness through exposure at a Department of Energy facility.

    (2) The term “covered illness” means an illness or death resulting from exposure to a toxic substance.

    (3) The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Labor.

([Pub. L. 106–398, § 1][/us/pl/106/398/s1] \[div. C, title XXXVI, § 3671\], as added [Pub. L. 108–375, div. C, title XXXI, § 3161][/us/pl/108/375/s3161], Oct. 28, 2004, [118 Stat. 2178][/us/stat/118/2178].)
