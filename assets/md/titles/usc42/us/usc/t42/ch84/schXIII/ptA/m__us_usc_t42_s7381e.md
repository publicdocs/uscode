## § 7381e. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary for carrying out university research support and other science, mathematics, and engineering education programs authorized by this part and administered by the Office of Science of the Department of Energy, $40,000,000 for fiscal year 1991.

([Pub. L. 101–510, div. C, title XXXI][/us/pl/101/510/dC/tXXXI], § 3169, formerly § 3168, Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1843][/us/stat/104/1843]; [Pub. L. 105–245, title III][/us/pl/105/245/tIII], § 309(b)(2)(D), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1853][/us/stat/112/1853]; renumbered § 3169, [Pub. L. 109–58, title XI][/us/pl/109/58/tXI], § 1105(b)(1), Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 939][/us/stat/119/939]; [Pub. L. 110–69, title V][/us/pl/110/69/tV], § 5003(d)(2), Aug. 9, 2007, [121 Stat. 603][/us/stat/121/603].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2007—[Pub. L. 110–69][/us/pl/110/69] substituted “this part” for “this subchapter”.

    1998—[Pub. L. 105–245][/us/pl/105/245] substituted “Office of Science” for “Office of Energy Research”.
