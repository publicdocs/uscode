###### §§ 2297, 2297a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–134, title III][/us/pl/104/134/tIII], § 3116(a)(1), Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–349][/us/stat/110/1321-349]

    Section 2297, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII], § 1201, as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX][/us/pl/102/486/tIX], § 901, Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2923][/us/stat/106/2923], defined terms for purposes of this division.

    Section 2297a, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII], § 1202, as added Oct. 24, 1992, [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX][/us/pl/102/486/tIX], § 901, [106 Stat. 2924][/us/stat/106/2924], related to purposes of United States Enrichment Corporation.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

[Pub. L. 104–134, title III][/us/pl/104/134/tIII], § 3116(a)(1), Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–349][/us/stat/110/1321-349], provided that: 

> “Chapters 22 through 26 of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 ([42 U.S.C. 2297–229][/us/usc/t42/s2297–229]7e–7) are repealed as of the privatization date \[July 28, 1998, see [42 U.S.C. 2297h(9)][/us/usc/t42/s2297h/9] for definition of privatization date as date on which 100 percent of ownership of United States Enrichment Corporation has been transferred to private investors\].”

 __Severability__ 

[Pub. L. 102–486, title IX][/us/pl/102/486/tIX], § 904, Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2946][/us/stat/106/2946], provided that: 

> “If any provision of this title \[see Tables for classification\], or the amendments made by this title, or the application of any provision to any entity, person, or circumstance, is for any reason adjudged by a court of competent jurisdiction to be invalid, the remainder of this title, and the amendments made by this title, or its application shall not be affected.”

 __References to United States Enrichment Corporation__ 

[Pub. L. 104–134, title III][/us/pl/104/134/tIII], § 3116(e), Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–350][/us/stat/110/1321-350], provided that: 

> “Following the privatization date \[July 28, 1998, see Effective Date of Repeal note above\], all references in the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 \[[42 U.S.C. 2011][/us/usc/t42/s2011] et seq.\] to the United States Enrichment Corporation shall be deemed to be references to the private corporation.”
