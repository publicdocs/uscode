## § 2291. Definitions

As used in this subchapter—

    (a) “The Community” means the European Atomic Energy Community (EURATOM).

    (b) The “Commission” means the Atomic Energy Commission, as established by the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended \[[42 U.S.C. 2011][/us/usc/t42/s2011] et seq.\].

    (c) “Joint program” means the cooperative program established by the Community and the United States and carried out in accordance with the provisions of an agreement for cooperation entered into pursuant to the provisions of [section 2153 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2153], to bring into operation in the territory of the members of the Community powerplants using nuclear reactors of types selected by the Commission and the Community, having as a goal a total installed capacity of approximately one million kilowatts of electricity by December 31, 1963, except that two reactors may be selected to be in operation by December 31, 1965.

    (d) All other terms used in this subchapter shall have the same meaning as terms described in [section 2014 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2014].

([Pub. L. 85–846, § 2][/us/pl/85/846/s2], Aug. 28, 1958, [72 Stat. 1084][/us/stat/72/1084].)

(b), is [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724], as added by [act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1][/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1], [68 Stat. 921][/us/stat/68/921], and amended, which is classified generally to this chapter (§ 2011 et seq.).  __Codification__ 

    Section was enacted as part of the EURATOM Cooperation Act of 1958 which comprises this subchapter, and not as part of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 which comprises this chapter.
