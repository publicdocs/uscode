## § 2202. Contracts

    The President may, in advance, exempt any specific action of the Commission in a particular matter from the provisions of law relating to contracts whenever he determines that such action is essential in the interest of the common defense and security.

([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724], title I, § 162, as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1][/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1], [68 Stat. 951][/us/stat/68/951]; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 902(a)(8)][/us/pl/102/486/s902/a/8], Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2944][/us/stat/106/2944].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to this section were contained in [section 1812(b) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1812/b], prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946, by act Aug. 30, 1954.
