## § 2296a–2. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __In general__ 

        There is authorized to be appropriated $715,000,000 to carry out this part. The aggregate amount authorized in the preceding sentence shall be increased annually as provided in [section 2296a of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2296a], based upon an inflation index to be determined by the Secretary.

    (b) __Source__ 

        Funds described in subsection (a) of this section shall be provided from the Fund established under [section 2297g of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2297g].

([Pub. L. 102–486, title X][/us/pl/102/486/tX], § 1003, Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2947][/us/stat/106/2947]; [Pub. L. 104–259][/us/pl/104/259], § 3(b), Oct. 9, 1996, [110 Stat. 3174][/us/stat/110/3174]; [Pub. L. 105–388][/us/pl/105/388], § 11(b), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3485][/us/stat/112/3485]; [Pub. L. 107–222][/us/pl/107/222], § 1(b), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1336][/us/stat/116/1336].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was enacted as part of the Energy Policy Act of 1992, and not as part of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 which comprises this chapter.

 __Amendments__ 

    2002—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 107–222][/us/pl/107/222] substituted “$715,000,000” for “$490,000,000”.

    1998—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 105–388][/us/pl/105/388] substituted “$490,000,000” for “$415,000,000”.

    1996—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 104–259][/us/pl/104/259] substituted “$415,000,000” for “$310,000,000”.
