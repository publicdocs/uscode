## § 2279. Applicability of other laws

    Sections 2274 to 2278b of this title shall not exclude the applicable provisions of any other laws.

([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724], title I, § 231, formerly § 229, as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1][/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1], [68 Stat. 959][/us/stat/68/959]; renumbered § 231 and amended [Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 1015][/us/act/1956-08-06/ch1015], §§ 6, 7, [70 Stat. 1070][/us/stat/70/1070]; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 902(a)(8)][/us/pl/102/486/s902/a/8], Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2944][/us/stat/106/2944].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1956—Act Aug. 6, 1956, § 7, substituted “2274 to 2278b” for “2274 to 2278”.
