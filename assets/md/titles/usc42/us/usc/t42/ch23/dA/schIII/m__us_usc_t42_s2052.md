## § 2052. Research by Commission

    The Commission is authorized and directed to conduct, through its own facilities, activities and studies of the types specified in [section 2051 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2051].

([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724], title I, § 32, as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073][/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073], § 1, [68 Stat. 928][/us/stat/68/928]; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX][/us/pl/102/486/tIX], § 902(a)(8), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2944][/us/stat/106/2944].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to this section were contained in [section 1803(b) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1803/b], prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946, by act Aug. 30, 1954.
