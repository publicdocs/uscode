## § 2036. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–91, title VII][/us/pl/95/91/tVII], § 709(c)(1), Aug. 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 608][/us/stat/91/608]

    Section, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724], § 26, as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073][/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073], § 1, [68 Stat. 926][/us/stat/68/926], established a General Advisory Committee to advise the Atomic Energy Commission on scientific and technical matters relating to materials, production, and research and development.

    Provisions similar to this section were contained in [section 1802(b) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1802/b] prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946, by act Aug. 30, 1954.
