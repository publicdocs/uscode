## § 2154. International atomic pool

    The President is authorized to enter into an international arrangement with a group of nations providing for international cooperation in the nonmilitary applications of atomic energy and he may thereafter cooperate with that group of nations pursuant to sections 2074(a), 2077, 2094, 2112, 2133, 2134, or 2164(a) of this title: Provided, however, That the cooperation is undertaken pursuant to an agreement for cooperation entered into in accordance with [section 2153 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2153].

([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724], title I, § 124, as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073][/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073], § 1, [68 Stat. 940][/us/stat/68/940]; amended [Pub. L. 93–377][/us/pl/93/377], § 5, Aug. 17, 1974, [88 Stat. 475][/us/stat/88/475]; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX][/us/pl/102/486/tIX], § 902(a)(8), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2944][/us/stat/106/2944].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1974—[Pub. L. 93–377][/us/pl/93/377] substituted reference to [section 2074(a) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2074/a] for reference to [section 2074 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2074].

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    Atomic Energy Commission abolished and functions transferred by sections 5814 and 5841 of this title. See also Transfer of Functions notes set out under those sections.
