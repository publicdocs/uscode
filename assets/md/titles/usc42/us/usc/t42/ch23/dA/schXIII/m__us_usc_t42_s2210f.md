## § 2210f. Recruitment tools

    The Commission may purchase promotional items of nominal value for use in the recruitment of individuals for employment.

([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724], title I, § 170F, as added [Pub. L. 109–58, title VI, § 651(c)(2)][/us/pl/109/58/s651/c/2], Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 801][/us/stat/119/801].)
