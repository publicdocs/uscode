## § 2015a. Cold standby

    The Secretary is authorized to expend such funds as may be necessary for the purposes of maintaining enrichment capability at the Portsmouth, Ohio, facility.

([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724][/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724], title I, § 242, as added [Pub. L. 107–222, § 1(d)(1)][/us/pl/107/222/s1/d/1], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1336][/us/stat/116/1336].)
