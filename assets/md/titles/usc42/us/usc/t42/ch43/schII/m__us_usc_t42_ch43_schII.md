### SUBCHAPTER II—OFFICE OF INSPECTOR GENERAL

§§ 3521 to 3527. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 100–504, title I, § 102(e)(2)__  __][/us/pl/100/504/s102/e/2]__  __,__  __Oct. 18, 1988__  __,__  __[__  __102 Stat. 2517__  __][/us/stat/102/2517]__ 

    Section 3521, [Pub. L. 94–505, title II, § 201][/us/pl/94/505/s201], Oct. 15, 1976, [90 Stat. 2429][/us/stat/90/2429]; [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)][/us/pl/96/88/s509/b], Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695][/us/stat/93/695], established Office of Inspector General.

    Section 3522, [Pub. L. 94–505, title II, § 202][/us/pl/94/505/s202], Oct. 15, 1976, [90 Stat. 2430][/us/stat/90/2430]; [Pub. L. 95–452, § 10(c)][/us/pl/95/452/s10/c], Oct. 12, 1978, [92 Stat. 1109][/us/stat/92/1109], provided for Inspector General and Deputy Inspector General, removal of such officers, political activities restriction, and appointment of Assistant Inspector General for Auditing and Assistant Inspector General for Investigations.

    Section 3523, [Pub. L. 94–505, title II, § 203][/us/pl/94/505/s203], Oct. 15, 1976, [90 Stat. 2430][/us/stat/90/2430]; [Pub. L. 96–226, title II, § 201][/us/pl/96/226/s201], Apr. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 315][/us/stat/94/315], prescribed duties and responsibilities of Inspector General.

    Section 3524, [Pub. L. 94–505, title II, § 204][/us/pl/94/505/s204], Oct. 15, 1976, [90 Stat. 2431][/us/stat/90/2431]; [Pub. L. 95–142, § 4(c)][/us/pl/95/142/s4/c], Oct. 25, 1977, [91 Stat. 1183][/us/stat/91/1183]; [Pub. L. 97–375, title II, § 206(c)][/us/pl/97/375/s206/c], Dec. 21, 1982, [96 Stat. 1824][/us/stat/96/1824], required preparation and submission of reports.

    Section 3525, [Pub. L. 94–505, title II, § 205][/us/pl/94/505/s205], Oct. 15, 1976, [90 Stat. 2432][/us/stat/90/2432], related to administrative provisions.

    Section 3526, [Pub. L. 94–505, title II, § 206][/us/pl/94/505/s206], Oct. 15, 1976, [90 Stat. 2433][/us/stat/90/2433], provided for transfer of functions.

    Section 3527, [Pub. L. 94–505, title II, § 207][/us/pl/94/505/s207], Oct. 15, 1976, [90 Stat. 2434][/us/stat/90/2434]; [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)][/us/pl/96/88/s509/b], Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695][/us/stat/93/695], defined “Secretary”, “Department”, “Inspector General”, “Deputy”, and “Federal agency”.

    For transfer of functions under sections 3521 to 3527 to and establishment of the Office of Inspector General of the Department of Health and Human Services, see the Inspector General Act of 1978, [Pub. L. 95–452][/us/pl/95/452], as amended, set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective 180 days after Oct. 18, 1988, see [section 113 of Pub. L. 100–504][/us/pl/100/504/s113], set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 5 of Pub. L. 95–452][/us/pl/95/452/s5] \[Inspector General Act of 1978\] in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
