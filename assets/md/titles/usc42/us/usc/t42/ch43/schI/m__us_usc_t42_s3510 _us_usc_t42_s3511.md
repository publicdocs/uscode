###### §§ 3510, 3511. Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section 3510, [Pub. L. 91–667, title II][/us/pl/91/667/tII], Jan. 11, 1971, [84 Stat. 2015][/us/stat/84/2015], which related to the Working Capital Fund, was transferred to [section 3513a of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3513a].

    Section 3511, [Pub. L. 93–282, title II][/us/pl/93/282/tII], § 201, May 14, 1974, [88 Stat. 134][/us/stat/88/134]; [Pub. L. 94–371][/us/pl/94/371], § 8, July 26, 1976, [90 Stat. 1040][/us/stat/90/1040]; [Pub. L. 96–88, title V][/us/pl/96/88/tV], § 509(b), Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695][/us/stat/93/695], which established the Alcohol, Drug Abuse, and Mental Health Administration and National Panel on Alcohol, Drug Abuse, and Mental Health, was redesignated section 501 of the Public Health Service Act by [Pub. L. 98–24][/us/pl/98/24], § 2(b)(2), Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 176][/us/stat/97/176], and is classified to [section 290aa of this title][/us/usc/t42/s290aa].
