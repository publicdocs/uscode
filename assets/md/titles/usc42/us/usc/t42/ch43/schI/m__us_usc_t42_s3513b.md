## § 3513b. Working capital fund; availability for common personnel support services

    The Working Capital Fund of the Department of Health and Human Services shall on and after August 10, 1971, be available for expenses necessary for common personnel support services in the Washington area.

([Pub. L. 92–80, title II][/us/pl/92/80], Aug. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 297][/us/stat/85/297]; [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)][/us/pl/96/88/s509/b], Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695][/us/stat/93/695].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was enacted as part of title II of [Pub. L. 92–80][/us/pl/92/80], popularly known as the Department of Health, Education, and Welfare Appropriation Act, 1972.

    Section was formerly classified to [section 905a of this title][/us/usc/t42/s905a].

 __Change of Name__ 

    “Department of Health and Human Services” substituted in text for “Department of Health, Education, and Welfare” pursuant to [section 509(b) of Pub. L. 96–88][/us/pl/96/88/s509/b], which is classified to [section 3508(b) of Title 20][/us/usc/t20/s3508/b], Education.
