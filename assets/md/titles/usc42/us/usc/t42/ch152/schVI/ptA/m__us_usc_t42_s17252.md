## § 17252. Review of large-scale programs

    The Secretary shall enter into an arrangement with the National Academy of Sciences for an independent review and oversight, beginning in 2011, of the programs under [section 16293(c)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s16293/c/3] and under [section 17251 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s17251], to ensure that the benefits of such programs are maximized. Not later than January 1, 2012, the Secretary shall transmit to the Congress a report on the results of such review and oversight.

([Pub. L. 110–140, title VII][/us/pl/110/140/tVII], § 704, Dec. 19, 2007, [121 Stat. 1709][/us/stat/121/1709].)
