## § 17215. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out this part $50,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2008 through 2012, except that no funds shall be appropriated under this section for activities that are receiving funds under [section 16231(a)(2)(E)(i) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s16231/a/2/E/i].

([Pub. L. 110–140, title VI, § 636][/us/pl/110/140/s636], Dec. 19, 2007, [121 Stat. 1688][/us/stat/121/1688].)
