## § 17096. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out sections 434 through 439 and 482  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  $4,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2008 through 2012, to remain available until expended.

([Pub. L. 110–140, title IV][/us/pl/110/140/tIV], § 440, Dec. 19, 2007, [121 Stat. 1623][/us/stat/121/1623].)

 __References in Text__ 

    Sections 434 through 439, referred to in text, are sections 434 to 439 of [Pub. L. 110–140][/us/pl/110/140], which enacted sections 17091 to 17095 of this title and amended [section 8253 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s8253]. Section 482 is unidentifiable because [Pub. L. 110–140][/us/pl/110/140] does not contain a section 482.
