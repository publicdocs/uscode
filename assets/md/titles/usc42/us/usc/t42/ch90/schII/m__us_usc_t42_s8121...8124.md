## §§ 8121 to 8124. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title III][/us/pl/97/35/tIII], § 313(a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 398][/us/stat/95/398]

    Section 8121, [Pub. L. 95–557, title VII][/us/pl/95/557/tVII], § 702, Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2119][/us/stat/92/2119], set forth congressional findings and statement of purposes for neighborhood self-help development programs.

    Section 8122, [Pub. L. 95–557, title VII][/us/pl/95/557/tVII], § 703, Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2120][/us/stat/92/2120], defined terms applicable to subchapter.

    Section 8123, [Pub. L. 95–557, title VII][/us/pl/95/557/tVII], § 704, Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2120][/us/stat/92/2120]; [Pub. L. 96–153, title I][/us/pl/96/153/tI], § 107(b), Dec. 21, 1979, [93 Stat. 1104][/us/stat/93/1104], set forth provisions respecting grants and other forms of assistance.

    Section 8124, [Pub. L. 95–557, title VII][/us/pl/95/557/tVII], § 705, Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2121][/us/stat/92/2121]; [Pub. L. 96–153, title I][/us/pl/96/153/tI], § 107(a), Dec. 21, 1979, [93 Stat. 1104][/us/stat/93/1104]; [Pub. L. 96–399, title I][/us/pl/96/399/tI], § 115, Oct. 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 1623][/us/stat/94/1623], related to authorization of appropriations for grants.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Sections 8121 to 8124 repealed effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 371 of Pub. L. 97–35][/us/pl/97/35/s371], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3701 of Title 12][/us/usc/t12/s3701], Banks and Banking.
