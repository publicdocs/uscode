## § 1437bbb–8. Definitions

    For purposes of this subchapter, the following definitions shall apply:

    (1) __Jurisdiction__ 

    The term “jurisdiction” means—

        (A)

         a unit of general local government (as such term is defined in [section 12704 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s12704]) that has boundaries, for purposes of carrying out this subchapter, that—

            (i) wholly contain the area within which a public housing agency is authorized to operate; and

            (ii) do not contain any areas contained within the boundaries of any other participating jurisdiction; and

        (B) a consortia of such units of general local government, organized for purposes of this subchapter.

    (2) __Participating jurisdiction__ 

        The term “participating jurisdiction” means, with respect to a period for which such an agreement is made, a jurisdiction that has entered into an agreement under [section 1437bbb–5(b)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1437bbb–5/b/3] to receive assistance pursuant to this subchapter for such fiscal year.

([Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 896][/us/act/1937-09-01/ch896], title IV, § 409, as added [Pub. L. 105–276, title V][/us/pl/105/276/tV], § 561, Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2624][/us/stat/112/2624].)
