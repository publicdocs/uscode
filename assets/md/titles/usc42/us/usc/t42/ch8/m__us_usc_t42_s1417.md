## § 1417. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–448, title XVII][/us/pl/90/448/tXVII], § 1719(b), Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 610][/us/stat/82/610]

    Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 896][/us/act/1937-09-01/ch896], § 17, [50 Stat. 897][/us/stat/50/897], related to capital stock of the Authority.

 __Retirement of Capital Stock__ 

    [Pub. L. 90–448, title XVII][/us/pl/90/448/tXVII], § 1719(b), Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 610][/us/stat/82/610], provided in part that the capital stock referred to in this section be retired and the sum of $1,000,000 represented by such stock returned to the Treasury of the United States.
