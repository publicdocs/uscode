## § 1438. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–276, title V][/us/pl/105/276/tV], § 582(a)(15), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2644][/us/stat/112/2644]

    Section, [Pub. L. 93–383, title II][/us/pl/93/383/tII], § 209, Aug. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 669][/us/stat/88/669]; [Pub. L. 98–479, title II][/us/pl/98/479/tII], § 201(g), Oct. 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 2228][/us/stat/98/2228]; [Pub.  __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective and applicable beginning upon Oct. 1, 1999, except as otherwise provided, with provision that Secretary may implement repeal before such date, and with savings provision, see [section 503 of Pub. L. 105–276][/us/pl/105/276/s503], set out as an Effective Date of 1998 Amendment note under [section 1437 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1437].
