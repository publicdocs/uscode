## § 13370. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary for carrying out this part, other than section 13362  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  of this title, such sums as may be necessary for fiscal years 1993 through 1998.

([Pub. L. 102–486, title XIII, § 1341][/us/pl/102/486/s1341], Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2993][/us/stat/106/2993].)

 __References in Text__ 

    [Section 13362 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13362], referred to in text, was in the original “section 1322” and was translated as reading “section 1332” meaning [section 1332 of Pub. L. 102–486][/us/pl/102/486/s1332], to reflect the probable intent of Congress, because [Pub. L. 102–486][/us/pl/102/486] does not contain a section 1322.
