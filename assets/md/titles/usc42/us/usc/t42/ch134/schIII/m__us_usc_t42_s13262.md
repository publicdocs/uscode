## § 13262. Enforcement

    (a) __Violation__ 

        Whoever violates [section 13261 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13261] shall be subject to a civil penalty of not more than $5,000 for each violation.

    (b) __Willful violation__ 

        Whoever willfully violates [section 13261 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13261] shall be fined not more than $10,000 for each violation.

    (c) __Knowing and willful violation following prior violation and penalty__ 

        Any person who knowingly and willfully violates [section 13261 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13261] after having been subjected to a civil penalty for a prior violation of [section 13261 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13261] shall be fined not more than $50,000.

([Pub. L. 102–486, title V][/us/pl/102/486/tV], § 512, Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2899][/us/stat/106/2899].)
