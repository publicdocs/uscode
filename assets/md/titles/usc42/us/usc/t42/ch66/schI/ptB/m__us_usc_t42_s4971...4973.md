## § 4971 to 4973. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title II][/us/pl/111/13/tII], § 2121, Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1584][/us/stat/123/1584]

    Section 4971, [Pub. L. 93–113, title I][/us/pl/93/113/tI], § 111, Oct. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 399][/us/stat/87/399]; [Pub. L. 98–288][/us/pl/98/288], § 9, May 21, 1984, [98 Stat. 191][/us/stat/98/191]; [Pub. L. 99–551][/us/pl/99/551], § 5, Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3073][/us/stat/100/3073]; [Pub. L. 101–204, title II][/us/pl/101/204/tII], § 201(2), Dec. 7, 1989, [103 Stat. 1813][/us/stat/103/1813]; [Pub. L. 103–82, title III][/us/pl/103/82/tIII], § 330(a)(2), (3), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 902][/us/stat/107/902], set forth the congressional statement of purpose for the University Year for VISTA program of full-time volunteer service by students enrolled in institutions of higher education.

    Section 4972, [Pub. L. 93–113, title I][/us/pl/93/113/tI], § 112, Oct. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 399][/us/stat/87/399]; [Pub. L. 98–288][/us/pl/98/288], § 30(b)(1), May 21, 1984, [98 Stat. 197][/us/stat/98/197]; [Pub. L. 99–551][/us/pl/99/551], § 10(i)(2), Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3078][/us/stat/100/3078]; [Pub. L. 103–82, title III][/us/pl/103/82/tIII], § 330(a)(4), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 902][/us/stat/107/902], provided the Director with authority to operate University Year for VISTA program.

    Section 4973, [Pub. L. 93–113, title I][/us/pl/93/113/tI], § 113, Oct. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 399][/us/stat/87/399]; [Pub. L. 96–143][/us/pl/96/143], § 5, Dec. 13, 1979, [93 Stat. 1075][/us/stat/93/1075]; [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI][/us/pl/97/35/tVI], § 608(f)(1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 488][/us/stat/95/488]; [Pub. L. 103–82, title III][/us/pl/103/82/tIII], § 330(a)(2), (3), (b), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 902][/us/stat/107/902], set out special conditions related to the University Year for VISTA program.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 2009, see [section 6101(a) of Pub. L. 111–13][/us/pl/111/13/s6101/a], set out as an Effective Date of 2009 Amendment note under [section 4950 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s4950].
