## § 5164. Rules and regulations

    The President may prescribe such rules and regulations as may be necessary and proper to carry out the provisions of this chapter, and may exercise, either directly or through such Federal agency as the President may designate, any power or authority conferred to the President by this chapter.

([Pub. L. 93–288, title III][/us/pl/93/288/tIII], § 321, as added [Pub. L. 100–707, title I][/us/pl/100/707/tI], § 105(m)(1), Nov. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 4696][/us/stat/102/4696].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 93–288][/us/pl/93/288], May 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 143][/us/stat/88/143]. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 5121 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s5121] and Tables.
