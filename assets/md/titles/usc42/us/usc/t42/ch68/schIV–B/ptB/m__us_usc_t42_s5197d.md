## § 5197d. Applicability of subchapter

    The provisions of this subchapter shall be applicable to the United States, its States, Territories and possessions, and the District of Columbia, and their political subdivisions.

([Pub. L. 93–288, title VI, § 625][/us/pl/93/288/s625], as added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. C, title XXXIV, § 3411(a)(3)][/us/pl/103/337/s3411/a/3], Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 3110][/us/stat/108/3110].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 2259 of Title 50][/us/usc/t50/s2259], Appendix, War and National Defense, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–337, § 3412(a)][/us/pl/103/337/s3412/a].
