## § 5197d. Applicability of subchapter

    The provisions of this subchapter shall be applicable to the United States, its States, Territories and possessions, and the District of Columbia, and their political subdivisions.

([Pub. L. 93–288, title VI][/us/pl/93/288/tVI], § 625, as added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. C, title XXXIV][/us/pl/103/337/dC/tXXXIV], § 3411(a)(3), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 3110][/us/stat/108/3110].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 2259 of the former Appendix to Title 50, War and National Defense, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–337][/us/pl/103/337], § 3412(a).
