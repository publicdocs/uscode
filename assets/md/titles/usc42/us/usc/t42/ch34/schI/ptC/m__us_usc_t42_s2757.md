## § 2757. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–575, title I][/us/pl/90/575/tI], § 131(a), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1028][/us/stat/82/1028]

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV][/us/pl/89/329/tIV], § 442, Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1251][/us/stat/79/1251], authorized appropriations for the fiscal years ending June 30, 1966, 1967, and 1968, to carry out the provisions of this part. For authorization of appropriations to carry out the provisions of this part for fiscal years ending after June 30, 1968, see [section 2751(b) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2751/b].

    Another [section 442 of Pub. L. 89–329][/us/pl/89/329/s442] was added by [section 131 of Pub. L. 90–575][/us/pl/90/575/s131] and is set out as [section 2752 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2752].
