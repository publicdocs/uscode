## §§ 2981 to 2981c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI][/us/pl/97/35/tVI], § 683(a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 519][/us/stat/95/519]

    Section 2981, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII][/us/pl/88/452/tVII], § 701, as added [Pub. L. 92–424][/us/pl/92/424], § 25(a), Sept. 19, 1972, [86 Stat. 697][/us/stat/86/697]; amended [Pub. L. 93–644][/us/pl/93/644], § 10(a), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2315][/us/stat/88/2315], set forth Congressional statement of purpose with respect to community economic development programs.

    A prior section 2981, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII][/us/pl/88/452/tVII], § 701, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 534][/us/stat/78/534]; [Pub. L. 89–794, title VII][/us/pl/89/794/tVII], § 701(a), Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1472][/us/stat/80/1472]; [Pub. L. 90–222, title I][/us/pl/90/222/tI], § 109, Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 722][/us/stat/81/722], respecting treatment of income for certain public assistance purposes, related to provisions of State plans and withholding of payments.

    Section 2981a, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII][/us/pl/88/452/tVII], § 702, as added [Pub. L. 93–644][/us/pl/93/644], § 10(a), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2315][/us/stat/88/2315], defined “community development corporation”.

    Section 2981b, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII][/us/pl/88/452/tVII], § 703, as added [Pub. L. 93–644][/us/pl/93/644], § 10(a), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2316][/us/stat/88/2316]; amended [Pub. L. 95–568][/us/pl/95/568], § 14(a), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2436][/us/stat/92/2436], related to authorization of appropriations.

    Section 2981c, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII][/us/pl/88/452/tVII], § 704, as added [Pub. L. 95–568][/us/pl/95/568], § 14(b), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2436][/us/stat/92/2436], related to establishment, etc., of advisory community investment boards, and the National Board.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 9912(a) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s9912/a], prior to the general amendment of chapter 106 (§ 9901 et seq.) of this title by [Pub. L. 105–285][/us/pl/105/285].
