## §§ 2801 to 2807. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–750, title III][/us/pl/89/750/tIII], § 315, Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1222][/us/stat/80/1222]

    Section 2801, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 212, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 520][/us/stat/78/520], set out the declaration of purpose for adult basic education programs.

    Section 2802, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 213, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 520][/us/stat/78/520]; [Pub. L. 89–253][/us/pl/89/253], § 19(1), Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 976][/us/stat/79/976]; [Pub. L. 89–794, title IX][/us/pl/89/794/tIX], § 901(c), Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1475][/us/stat/80/1475], provided for the making of grants to States having approved plans for adult basic education programs.

    Section 2803, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 214, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 520][/us/stat/78/520], set out the requirements for State plans and made provision for a hearing.

    Section 2804, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 215, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 521][/us/stat/78/521], set out the manner of allotting grant funds to the States and outlying areas.

    Section 2805, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 216, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 522][/us/stat/78/522]; [Pub. L. 89–253][/us/pl/89/253], § 18, Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 976][/us/stat/79/976], provided for the methods of payment of the Federal share of expenditures.

    Section 2806, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 217, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 522][/us/stat/78/522], provided for termination of payments for noncompliance with the State plan and for hearings and judicial review.

    Section 2806a, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 218, as added [Pub. L. 89–253][/us/pl/89/253], § 19(2), Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 976][/us/stat/79/976], made provision for teacher training projects.

    Section 2807, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 219, formerly § 218, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 523][/us/stat/78/523], renumbered [Pub. L. 89–253][/us/pl/89/253], § 19(2), Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 976][/us/stat/79/976], set out the definitions of terms.
