## §§ 2769 to 2769f. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VI][/us/pl/93/203/tVI], § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883][/us/stat/87/883]

    Section 2769, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I][/us/pl/88/452/tI], § 161, as added [Pub. L. 91–177, title II][/us/pl/91/177/tII], § 201, Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 833][/us/stat/83/833], set out Congressional statement of purpose in providing for special work and career development programs.

    Section 2769a, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I][/us/pl/88/452/tI], § 162, as added [Pub. L. 91–177, title II][/us/pl/91/177/tII], § 201, Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 834][/us/stat/83/834], provided for special programs for unemployed.

    Section 2769b, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I][/us/pl/88/452/tI], § 163, as added [Pub. L. 91–177, title II][/us/pl/91/177/tII], § 201, Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 834][/us/stat/83/834], provided for establishment of internal administrative procedures by Director covering special work and career development programs.

    Section 2769c, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I][/us/pl/88/452/tI], § 164, as added [Pub. L. 91–177, title II][/us/pl/91/177/tII], § 201, Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 835][/us/stat/83/835], set out certain special conditions covering special work and career development programs.

    Section 2769d, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I][/us/pl/88/452/tI], § 165, as added [Pub. L. 91–177, title II][/us/pl/91/177/tII], § 201, Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 835][/us/stat/83/835], set out criteria for program participants.

    Section 2769e, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I][/us/pl/88/452/tI], § 166, as added [Pub. L. 91–177, title II][/us/pl/91/177/tII], § 201, Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 835][/us/stat/83/835], set out criteria for equitable distribution of assistance among States.

    Section 2769f, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I][/us/pl/88/452/tI], § 167, as added [Pub. L. 91–177, title II][/us/pl/91/177/tII], § 201, Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 836][/us/stat/83/836], set limitations on Federal assistance.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 93–203, title VI][/us/pl/93/203/tVI], § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883][/us/stat/87/883], provided that the repeal by that section is effective with respect to fiscal years after June 30, 1974.
