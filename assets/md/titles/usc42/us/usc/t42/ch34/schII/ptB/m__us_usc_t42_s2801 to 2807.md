## §§ 2801 to 2807. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–750, title III, § 315][/us/pl/89/750/s315], Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1222][/us/stat/80/1222]

    Section 2801, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 212][/us/pl/88/452/s212], Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 520][/us/stat/78/520], set out the declaration of purpose for adult basic education programs.

    Section 2802, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 213][/us/pl/88/452/s213], Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 520][/us/stat/78/520]; [Pub. L. 89–253, § 19(1)][/us/pl/89/253/s19/1], Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 976][/us/stat/79/976]; [Pub. L. 89–794, title IX, § 901(c)][/us/pl/89/794/s901/c], Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1475][/us/stat/80/1475], provided for the making of grants to States having approved plans for adult basic education programs.

    Section 2803, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 214][/us/pl/88/452/s214], Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 520][/us/stat/78/520], set out the requirements for State plans and made provision for a hearing.

    Section 2804, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 215][/us/pl/88/452/s215], Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 521][/us/stat/78/521], set out the manner of allotting grant funds to the States and outlying areas.

    Section 2805, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 216][/us/pl/88/452/s216], Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 522][/us/stat/78/522]; [Pub. L. 89–253, § 18][/us/pl/89/253/s18], Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 976][/us/stat/79/976], provided for the methods of payment of the Federal share of expenditures.

    Section 2806, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 217][/us/pl/88/452/s217], Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 522][/us/stat/78/522], provided for termination of payments for noncompliance with the State plan and for hearings and judicial review.

    Section 2806a, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 218][/us/pl/88/452/s218], as added [Pub. L. 89–253, § 19(2)][/us/pl/89/253/s19/2], Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 976][/us/stat/79/976], made provision for teacher training projects.

    Section 2807, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 219][/us/pl/88/452/s219], formerly § 218, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 523][/us/stat/78/523], renumbered [Pub. L. 89–253, § 19(2)][/us/pl/89/253/s19/2], Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 976][/us/stat/79/976], set out the definitions of terms.
