## §§ 2761, 2762. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general amendment of Part D of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 103][/us/pl/90/222/s103], Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 688][/us/stat/81/688].

    Section 2761, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 131][/us/pl/88/452/s131], Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 516][/us/stat/78/516]; [Pub. L. 89–253, § 30(a)][/us/pl/89/253/s30/a], Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 978][/us/stat/79/978]; [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 441(7)][/us/pl/89/329/s441/7], Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1250][/us/stat/79/1250]; [Pub. L. 89–794, title I, § 113(2)][/us/pl/89/794/s113/2], Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1455][/us/stat/80/1455], provided for establishment of special impact programs and the grants, criteria, and procedures to be used in carrying them out.

    Section 2762, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 132][/us/pl/88/452/s132], as added [Pub. L. 89–794, title I, § 113(2)][/us/pl/89/794/s113/2], Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1456][/us/stat/80/1456], placed a limitation on Federal share of cost of special impact programs.
