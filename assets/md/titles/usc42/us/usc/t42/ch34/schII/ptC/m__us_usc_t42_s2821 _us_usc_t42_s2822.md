###### §§ 2821, 2822. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections were omitted in the general amendment of part C of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 90–222, title I][/us/pl/90/222/tI], § 104, Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 703][/us/stat/81/703].

    Section 2821, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 219–1, formerly § 219, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 523][/us/stat/78/523]; renumbered § 219–1, [Pub. L. 89–794, title IX][/us/pl/89/794/tIX], § 901(b), Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1475][/us/stat/80/1475], set out the statement of purpose for the voluntary assistance program for needy children.

    Section 2822, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II][/us/pl/88/452/tII], § 220, Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 523][/us/stat/78/523]; [Pub. L. 89–253][/us/pl/89/253], § 20, Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 976][/us/stat/79/976], provided for the establishment within the Office of Economic Opportunity of an information and coordinating center but prohibiting the interference with jurisdiction of State and local welfare agencies with respect to programs for needy children.
