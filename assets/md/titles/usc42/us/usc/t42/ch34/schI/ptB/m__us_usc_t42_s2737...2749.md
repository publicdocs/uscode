    Section 2743, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I][/us/pl/88/452/tI], § 126, as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I][/us/pl/90/222/tI], § 102, Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 686][/us/stat/81/686], provided for programs dealing with long-term unemployment of persons fifty-five years and older.
