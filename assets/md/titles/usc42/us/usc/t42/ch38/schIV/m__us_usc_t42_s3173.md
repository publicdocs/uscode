## § 3173. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–373, title IV][/us/pl/108/373/tIV], § 401(a), Oct. 27, 2004, [118 Stat. 1767][/us/stat/118/1767]

    Section, [Pub. L. 89–136, title IV][/us/pl/89/136/tIV], § 403, as added [Pub. L. 105–393, title I][/us/pl/105/393/tI], § 102(a), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3609][/us/stat/112/3609], related to incentives for projects in economic development districts.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 3173, [Pub. L. 89–136, title IV][/us/pl/89/136/tIV], § 405, as added [Pub. L. 94–487, title I][/us/pl/94/487/tI], § 117, Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2335][/us/stat/90/2335], directed furnishing of unemployment rate information by either Federal Government or State, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–393][/us/pl/105/393], § 102(a).

    A prior [section 403 of Pub. L. 89–136][/us/pl/89/136/s403] was classified to [section 3171 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3171] prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–393][/us/pl/105/393], § 102(a).
