## § 1466. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [act July 15, 1949, ch. 338, title I][/us/act/1949-07-15/ch338/tI], § 115, as added Aug. 10, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–117, title I][/us/pl/89/117/tI], § 106(a), [79 Stat. 457][/us/stat/79/457]; amended May 25, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–19][/us/pl/90/19], § 6(b), [81 Stat. 21][/us/stat/81/21]; Aug. 1, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–448, title V][/us/pl/90/448/tV], § 503, [82 Stat. 521][/us/stat/82/521]; Dec. 24, 1969, [Pub. L. 91–152, title II][/us/pl/91/152/tII], § 205, [83 Stat. 387][/us/stat/83/387], which related to rehabilitation grants, was omitted pursuant to [section 5316 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s5316] which terminated authority to make grants or loans under this subchapter after Jan. 1, 1975.

 __Amendment of Contracts Executed Prior to Enactment of Section__ 

    [Pub. L. 89–117, title I][/us/pl/89/117/tI], § 106(b), Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 458][/us/stat/79/458], provided that any contract with a local public agency executed under this subchapter before Aug. 10, 1965, could be amended to provide for grants authorized by this section.
