## § 1465. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–646, title II, § 220(a)(5)][/us/pl/91/646/s220/a/5], Jan. 2, 1971, [84 Stat. 1903][/us/stat/84/1903]

Section, [act July 15, 1949, ch. 338][/us/act/1949-07-15/ch338], title I, § 114, as added Sept. 2, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–560, title III, § 310(a)][/us/pl/88/560/s310/a], [78 Stat. 788][/us/stat/78/788]; amended Aug. 10, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–117, title I, § 101(i)][/us/pl/89/117/s101/i], title IV, § 404(b), (c)(1), [79 Stat. 453][/us/stat/79/453], 486; May 25, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–19, § 6(b)][/us/pl/90/19/s6/b], [81 Stat. 21][/us/stat/81/21]; Aug. 1, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–448, title V, § 516][/us/pl/90/448/s516], [82 Stat. 526][/us/stat/82/526]; Dec. 31, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–609, title II, § 212][/us/pl/91/609/s212], [84 Stat. 1779][/us/stat/84/1779], related to relocation assistance, providing as follows: subsec. (a), financial assistance to displaced individuals, families, businesses, and nonprofit organizations; subsec. (b), payments to business concerns or nonprofit organizations, considerations, and maximum amounts; subsec. (c), payments to individuals and families, considerations, computation of amount, maximum amounts, and restrictions; subsec. (d), payments to individuals, families, business concerns, and nonprofit organizations for recording fees, transfer taxes, incidental expenses, penalty costs, and pro rata taxes; and subsec. (e), rules and regulations, finality of administrative decisions, and promptness of payments. See chapter 61 (section 4601 et seq.) of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal not applicable to any State so long as sections 4630 and 4655 of this title are not applicable in such State; but such sections completely applicable to all States after July 1, 1972, but until such date applicable to a State to extent the State is able under its laws to comply with such sections, see [section 221 of Pub. L. 91–646][/us/pl/91/646/s221], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 4601 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s4601].

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Any rights or liabilities existing under provisions repealed by [section 220(a) of Pub. L. 91–646][/us/pl/91/646/s220/a] as not affected by such repeal, see [section 220(b) of Pub. L. 91–646][/us/pl/91/646/s220/b], set out as a note under [section 4621 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s4621].
