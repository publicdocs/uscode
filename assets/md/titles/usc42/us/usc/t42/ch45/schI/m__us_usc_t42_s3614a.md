## § 3614a. Rules to implement subchapter

    The Secretary may make rules (including rules for the collection, maintenance, and analysis of appropriate data) to carry out this subchapter. The Secretary shall give public notice and opportunity for comment with respect to all rules made under this section.

([Pub. L. 90–284, title VIII, § 815][/us/pl/90/284/s815], as added [Pub. L. 100–430, § 8(2)][/us/pl/100/430/s8/2], Sept. 13, 1988, [102 Stat. 1635][/us/stat/102/1635].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 815 of Pub. L. 90–284][/us/pl/90/284/s815] was renumbered section 816 and is classified to [section 3615 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3615].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective on 180th day beginning after Sept. 13, 1988, see [section 13(a) of Pub. L. 100–430][/us/pl/100/430/s13/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 3601 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3601].

 __Initial Rulemaking__ 

    Secretary to issue rules to implement this subchapter as amended by [Pub. L. 100–430][/us/pl/100/430] not later than the 180th day after Sept. 13, 1988, see [section 13(b) of Pub. L. 100–430][/us/pl/100/430/s13/b], set out as a note under [section 3601 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3601].
