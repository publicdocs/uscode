## § 3057l. Administration

    In establishing regulations for the purpose of part A of this subchapter the Assistant Secretary shall consult with the Secretary of the Interior.

([Pub. L. 89–73, title VI, § 641][/us/pl/89/73/s641], formerly § 631, as added [Pub. L. 100–175, title I, § 171][/us/pl/100/175/s171], Nov. 29, 1987, [101 Stat. 963][/us/stat/101/963]; amended [Pub. L. 103–171, § 3(a)(13)][/us/pl/103/171/s3/a/13], Dec. 2, 1993, [107 Stat. 1990][/us/stat/107/1990]; renumbered § 641, [Pub. L. 106–501, title VI, § 604(2)][/us/pl/106/501/s604/2], Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2288][/us/stat/114/2288].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1993—[Pub. L. 103–171][/us/pl/103/171] substituted “Assistant Secretary” for “Commissioner”.
