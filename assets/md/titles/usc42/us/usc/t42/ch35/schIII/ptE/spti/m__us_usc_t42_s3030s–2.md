## § 3030s–2. Maintenance of effort

    Funds made available under this subpart shall supplement, and not supplant, any Federal, State, or local funds expended by a State or unit of general purpose local government (including an area agency on aging) to provide services described in [section 3030s–1 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3030s–1].

([Pub. L. 89–73, title III, § 374][/us/pl/89/73/s374], as added [Pub. L. 106–501, title III, § 316(2)][/us/pl/106/501/s316/2], Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2256][/us/stat/114/2256].)
