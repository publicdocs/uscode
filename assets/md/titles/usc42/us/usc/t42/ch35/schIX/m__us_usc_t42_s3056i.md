## § 3056i. Coordination with the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 Catchline updated editorially based on amendment by [Pub. L. 113–128][/us/pl/113/128]. </sup></sup> 

    (a) __Partners__ 

        Grantees under this subchapter shall be one-stop partners as described in subparagraphs (A) and (B)(v) of section 121(b)(1) of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act \[[29 U.S.C. 3151(b)(1)][/us/usc/t29/s3151/b/1]\] in the one-stop delivery system established under section 121(e) of such Act \[[29 U.S.C. 3151(e)][/us/usc/t29/s3151/e]\] for the appropriate local workforce development areas, and shall carry out the responsibilities relating to such partners.

    (b) __Coordination__ 

    In local workforce investment areas where more than 1 grantee under this subchapter provides services, the grantees shall—

        (1) coordinate their activities related to the one-stop delivery systems; and

        (2) be signatories of the memorandum of understanding established under section 121(c) of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act \[[29 U.S.C. 3151(c)][/us/usc/t29/s3151/c]\].

([Pub. L. 89–73, title V, § 511][/us/pl/89/73/s511], as added [Pub. L. 109–365, title V, § 501][/us/pl/109/365/s501], Oct. 17, 2006, [120 Stat. 2580][/us/stat/120/2580]; amended [Pub. L. 113–128, title V, § 512(w)(7)][/us/pl/113/128/s512/w/7], July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1716][/us/stat/128/1716].)
