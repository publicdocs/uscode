## § 3030s–2. Maintenance of effort

    Funds made available under this part shall supplement, and not supplant, any Federal, State, or local funds expended by a State or unit of general purpose local government (including an area agency on aging) to provide services described in [section 3030s–1 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3030s–1].

([Pub. L. 89–73, title III][/us/pl/89/73/tIII], § 374, as added [Pub. L. 106–501, title III][/us/pl/106/501/tIII], § 316(2), Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2256][/us/stat/114/2256]; amended [Pub. L. 114–144][/us/pl/114/144], § 4(m), Apr. 19, 2016, [130 Stat. 341][/us/stat/130/341].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Prior sections 3030s–11 and 3030s–12, which comprised subpart II of this part, were repealed by [Pub. L. 109–365, title III][/us/pl/109/365/tIII], § 322, Oct. 17, 2006, [120 Stat. 2552][/us/stat/120/2552].

    Section 3030s–11, [Pub. L. 89–73, title III][/us/pl/89/73/tIII], § 375, as added [Pub. L. 106–501, title III][/us/pl/106/501/tIII], § 316(2), Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2256][/us/stat/114/2256], provided for an innovation grant program.

    Section 3030s–12, [Pub. L. 89–73, title III][/us/pl/89/73/tIII], § 376, as added [Pub. L. 106–501, title III][/us/pl/106/501/tIII], § 316(2), Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2257][/us/stat/114/2257], directed Assistant Secretary to carry out activities of national significance to promote quality and continuous improvement in the support provided to family and other informal caregivers of older individuals.

 __Amendments__ 

    2016—[Pub. L. 114–144][/us/pl/114/144] substituted “this part” for “this subpart”.
