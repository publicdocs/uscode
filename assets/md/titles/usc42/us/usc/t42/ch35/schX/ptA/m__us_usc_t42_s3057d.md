## § 3057d. Grants authorized

    The Assistant Secretary may make grants to eligible tribal organizations to pay all of the costs for delivery of supportive services and nutrition services for older individuals who are Indians.

([Pub. L. 89–73, title VI, § 613][/us/pl/89/73/s613], as added [Pub. L. 100–175, title I, § 171][/us/pl/100/175/s171], Nov. 29, 1987, [101 Stat. 960][/us/stat/101/960]; amended [Pub. L. 103–171, § 3(a)(13)][/us/pl/103/171/s3/a/13], Dec. 2, 1993, [107 Stat. 1990][/us/stat/107/1990].)
