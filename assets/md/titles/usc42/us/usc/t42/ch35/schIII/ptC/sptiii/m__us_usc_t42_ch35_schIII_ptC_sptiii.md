##### subpart iii—general provisions

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 106–501, title III, § 312(b)][/us/pl/106/501/s312/b], Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2252][/us/stat/114/2252], redesignated subpart IV of this part as subpart III.

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior subpart III, consisting of sections 3030g–11 to 3030g–13 of this title, related to school-based meals for volunteer older individuals and multigenerational programs, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 106–501, title III, § 312(a)][/us/pl/106/501/s312/a], Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2252][/us/stat/114/2252].

    Section 3030g–11, [Pub. L. 89–73, title III, § 338][/us/pl/89/73/s338], as added [Pub. L. 102–375, title III, § 316(a)][/us/pl/102/375/s316/a], Sept. 30, 1992, [106 Stat. 1239][/us/stat/106/1239]; amended [Pub. L. 103–171, § 3(a)(13)][/us/pl/103/171/s3/a/13], Dec. 2, 1993, [107 Stat. 1990][/us/stat/107/1990], provided for establishment and implementation of the programs.

    Section 3030g–12, [Pub. L. 89–73, title III, § 338A][/us/pl/89/73/s338A], as added [Pub. L. 102–375, title III, § 316(a)][/us/pl/102/375/s316/a], Sept. 30, 1992, [106 Stat. 1240][/us/stat/106/1240]; amended [Pub. L. 103–171, § 3(a)(13)][/us/pl/103/171/s3/a/13], Dec. 2, 1993, [107 Stat. 1990][/us/stat/107/1990]; [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(r)(1)][/us/pl/103/382/s391/r/1], Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4024][/us/stat/108/4024], described required contents of application and criteria for selection among applicants.

    Section 3030g–13, [Pub. L. 89–73, title III, § 338B][/us/pl/89/73/s338B], as added [Pub. L. 102–375, title III, § 316(a)][/us/pl/102/375/s316/a], Sept. 30, 1992, [106 Stat. 1240][/us/stat/106/1240]; amended [Pub. L. 103–171, § 3(a)(11)][/us/pl/103/171/s3/a/11], (13), Dec. 2, 1993, [107 Stat. 1990][/us/stat/107/1990], required States receiving grants to submit reports evaluating projects.
