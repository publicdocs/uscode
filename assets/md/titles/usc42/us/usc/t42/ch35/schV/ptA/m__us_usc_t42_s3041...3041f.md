## §§ 3041 to 3041f. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–478, title V][/us/pl/95/478/tV], § 501(a), Oct. 18, 1978, [92 Stat. 1558][/us/stat/92/1558]

    Section 3041, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V][/us/pl/89/73/tV], § 501, as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V][/us/pl/93/29/tV], § 501, May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 50][/us/stat/87/50], related to provisions for grants or contracts. See sections 3028 and 3030d(b)(1) of this title.

    A prior section 3041, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V][/us/pl/89/73/tV], § 501, July 14, 1965, [79 Stat. 224][/us/stat/79/224]; [Pub. L. 91–69][/us/pl/91/69], § 8, Sept. 17, 1969, [83 Stat. 111][/us/stat/83/111], which related to training project grants and contracts, training programs, studies, preparation and dissemination of materials, and conferences, was repealed by [Pub. L. 93–29, title IV][/us/pl/93/29/tIV], § 401, May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 45][/us/stat/87/45].

    Section 3041a, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V][/us/pl/89/73/tV], § 502, as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V][/us/pl/93/29/tV], § 501, May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 50][/us/stat/87/50], related to requirements for approval of applications, preferred areas, and agency cooperation.

    Section 3041b, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V][/us/pl/89/73/tV] § 503, as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V][/us/pl/93/29/tV], § 501, May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 51][/us/stat/87/51], provided for payments, reservation from appropriations amount of grant or contract, amendments, advances, reimbursement, and installments.

    Section 3041c, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V][/us/pl/89/73/tV], § 504, as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V][/us/pl/93/29/tV], § 501, May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 51][/us/stat/87/51], provided for recapture of payments. See [section 3030b of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3030b].

    Section 3041d, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V][/us/pl/89/73/tV], § 505, as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V][/us/pl/93/29/tV], § 501, May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 51][/us/stat/87/51]; amended [Pub. L. 94–135, title I][/us/pl/94/135/tI], § 112(e), Nov. 28, 1975, [89 Stat. 720][/us/stat/89/720], provided for authorization of appropriations. See [section 3023 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3023].

    Section 3041e, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V][/us/pl/89/73/tV], § 506, as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V][/us/pl/93/29/tV], § 501, May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 51][/us/stat/87/51], related to mortgage insurance for Multipurpose Senior Centers.

    Section 3041f, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V][/us/pl/89/73/tV], § 507, as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V][/us/pl/93/29/tV], § 501, May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 54][/us/stat/87/54]; amended [Pub. L. 94–135, title I][/us/pl/94/135/tI], § 114(g), Nov. 28, 1975, [89 Stat. 725][/us/stat/89/725], related to annual interest grants.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 95–478, title V][/us/pl/95/478/tV], § 501(a), Oct. 18, 1978, [92 Stat. 1558][/us/stat/92/1558], provided that the repeal by that section is effective at the close of Sept. 30, 1978.
