## § 15545. No effect on other laws

    (a) __In general__ 

    Except as specifically provided in [section 15483(b) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s15483/b] with regard to the National Voter Registration Act of 1993 ([42 U.S.C. 1973gg][/us/usc/t42/s1973gg] et seq.), nothing in this chapter may be construed to authorize or require conduct prohibited under any of the following laws, or to supersede, restrict, or limit the application of such laws:

        (1) The Voting Rights Act of 1965 ([42 U.S.C. 1973][/us/usc/t42/s1973] et seq.).

        (2) The Voting Accessibility for the Elderly and Handicapped Act ([42 U.S.C. 1973ee][/us/usc/t42/s1973ee] et seq.).

        (3) The Uniformed and Overseas Citizens Absentee Voting Act ([42 U.S.C. 1973ff][/us/usc/t42/s1973ff] et seq.).

        (4) The National Voter Registration Act of 1993 ([42 U.S.C. 1973gg][/us/usc/t42/s1973gg] et seq.).

        (5) The Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 ([42 U.S.C. 12101][/us/usc/t42/s12101] et seq.).

        (6) The Rehabilitation Act of 1973 ([29 U.S.C. 701][/us/usc/t29/s701] et seq.).

    (b) __No effect on preclearance or other requirements under Voting Rights Act__ 

        The approval by the Administrator or the Commission of a payment or grant application under subchapter I or subchapter II of this chapter, or any other action taken by the Commission or a State under such subchapter, shall not be considered to have any effect on requirements for preclearance under section 5 of the Voting Rights Act of 1965 ([42 U.S.C. 1973c][/us/usc/t42/s1973c]) or any other requirements of such Act \[[42 U.S.C. 1973][/us/usc/t42/s1973] et seq.\].

([Pub. L. 107–252, title IX, § 906][/us/pl/107/252/s906], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1729][/us/stat/116/1729].)
