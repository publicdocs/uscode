## § 15532. Transfer of functions

    There are transferred to the Election Assistance Commission established under [section 15321 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s15321] all functions which the Federal Election Commission exercised under [section 1973gg–7(a) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s1973gg–7/a] before October 29, 2002.

([Pub. L. 107–252, title VIII, § 802(a)][/us/pl/107/252/s802/a], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1726][/us/stat/116/1726].)
