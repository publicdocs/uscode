## § 15330. Authorization of appropriations

    In addition to the amounts authorized for payments and grants under this subchapter and the amounts authorized to be appropriated for the program under [section 15523 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s15523], there are authorized to be appropriated for each of the fiscal years 2003 through 2005 such sums as may be necessary (but not to exceed $10,000,000 for each such year) for the Commission to carry out this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 210][/us/pl/107/252/s210], Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1678][/us/stat/116/1678].)
