## § 6956. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary of Commerce $5,000,000 for each of fiscal years 1980, 1981, and 1982 and $1,500,000 for each of the fiscal years 1985 through 1988 to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 89–272, title II, § 5006][/us/pl/89/272/s5006], as added [Pub. L. 96–482, § 31(f)(1)][/us/pl/96/482/s31/f/1], Oct. 21, 1980, [94 Stat. 2353][/us/stat/94/2353]; amended [Pub. L. 98–616, § 2(h)][/us/pl/98/616/s2/h], Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3223][/us/stat/98/3223].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1984—[Pub. L. 98–616][/us/pl/98/616] authorized appropriation of $1,500,000 for each of fiscal years 1985 through 1988.
