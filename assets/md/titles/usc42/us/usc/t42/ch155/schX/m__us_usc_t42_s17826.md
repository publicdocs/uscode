## § 17826. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1111][/us/pl/110/422/s1111], Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4811][/us/stat/122/4811], which required a plan and inventory of natural methane stocks and fluxes in the polar region of the United States within 12 months after Oct. 15, 2008, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 51, National and Commercial Space Programs, by [Pub. L. 111–314][/us/pl/111/314].

§§ 17827 to 17829. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 111–314, § 6__  __][/us/pl/111/314/s6]__  __,__  __Dec. 18, 2010__  __,__  __[__  __124 Stat. 3444__  __][/us/stat/124/3444]__ 

    Section 17827, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1112][/us/pl/110/422/s1112], Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4811][/us/stat/122/4811], provided an exception to alternative fuel procurement requirement. See [section 30310 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s30310], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Section 17828, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1116][/us/pl/110/422/s1116], Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4813][/us/stat/122/4813], related to cooperative unmanned aerial vehicle activities. See [section 31504 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s31504].

    Section 17829, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1117][/us/pl/110/422/s1117], Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4813][/us/stat/122/4813], related to development of enhanced-use lease policy. See [section 31505 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s31505].
