## §§ 17827 to 17829. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314][/us/pl/111/314], § 6, Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444][/us/stat/124/3444]

    Section 17827, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI][/us/pl/110/422/tXI], § 1112, Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4811][/us/stat/122/4811], provided an exception to alternative fuel procurement requirement. See [section 30310 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s30310], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Section 17828, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI][/us/pl/110/422/tXI], § 1116, Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4813][/us/stat/122/4813], related to cooperative unmanned aerial vehicle activities. See [section 31504 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s31504].

    Section 17829, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI][/us/pl/110/422/tXI], § 1117, Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4813][/us/stat/122/4813], related to development of enhanced-use lease policy. See [section 31505 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s31505].
