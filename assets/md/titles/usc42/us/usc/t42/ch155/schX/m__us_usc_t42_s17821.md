## § 17821. Repealed or Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1102][/us/pl/110/422/s1102], Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4808][/us/stat/122/4808], which related to initiation of discussions on development of framework for space traffic management, was repealed in part and transferred in part. Subsec. (b) was repealed and reenacted as [section 71302 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s71302], National and Commercial Space Programs, by [Pub. L. 111–314][/us/pl/111/314], §§ 3, 6, Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3328][/us/stat/124/3328], 3444, which Act enacted Title 51. Subsec. (a), which provided congressional finding of need for space traffic management, was transferred and is set out as a note under [section 71302 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s71302].

§§ 17822 to 17824. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 111–314, § 6__  __][/us/pl/111/314/s6]__  __,__  __Dec. 18, 2010__  __,__  __[__  __124 Stat. 3444__  __][/us/stat/124/3444]__ 

    Section 17822, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1103][/us/pl/110/422/s1103], Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4808][/us/stat/122/4808], related to astronaut health care. See [section 31302 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s31302], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Section 17823, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1104][/us/pl/110/422/s1104], Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4809][/us/stat/122/4809], related to National Academies decadal surveys. See [section 20305 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s20305].

    Section 17824, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1107][/us/pl/110/422/s1107], Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4810][/us/stat/122/4810], related to NASA outreach program to promote business competitiveness through space and aeronautics technologies. See [section 30305 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s30305].
