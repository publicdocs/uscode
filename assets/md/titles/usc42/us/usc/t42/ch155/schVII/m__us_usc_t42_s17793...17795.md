## §§ 17793 to 17795. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314][/us/pl/111/314], § 6, Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444][/us/stat/124/3444]

    Section 17793, [Pub. L. 110–422, title VIII][/us/pl/110/422/tVIII], § 803, Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4803][/us/stat/122/4803], related to requests for specific mission information. See [section 71102 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s71102], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Section 17794, [Pub. L. 110–422, title VIII][/us/pl/110/422/tVIII], § 804, Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4804][/us/stat/122/4804], established policy with respect to threats posed by near-Earth objects. See [section 71103 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s71103].

    Section 17795, [Pub. L. 110–422, title VIII][/us/pl/110/422/tVIII], § 805, Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4804][/us/stat/122/4804], related to required planetary radar capability. See [section 71104 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s71104].
