## § 4278. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

([Pub. L. 86–380][/us/pl/86/380], § 8, Sept. 24, 1959, [73 Stat. 706][/us/stat/73/706].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 2378 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s2378] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].
