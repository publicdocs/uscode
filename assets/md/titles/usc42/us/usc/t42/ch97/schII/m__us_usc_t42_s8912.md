## § 8912. Authorization of appropriations

    For the expenses of carrying out the carbon dioxide study authorized by [section 8911 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s8911] (as determined by the Office of Science and Technology Policy) there are authorized to be appropriated such sums, not exceeding $3,000,000 in the aggregate, as may be necessary. At least 80 percent of any amounts appropriated pursuant to the preceding sentence shall be provided to the National Academy of Sciences.

([Pub. L. 96–294, title VII][/us/pl/96/294/tVII], § 712, June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 775][/us/stat/94/775].)
