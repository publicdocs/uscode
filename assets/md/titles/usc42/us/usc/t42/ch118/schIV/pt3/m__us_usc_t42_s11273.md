## § 11273. Authorization of appropriations

    To carry out this part, there are authorized to be appropriated $2,000,000 for each of fiscal years 1988 through 1991.

([Pub. L. 99–660, title IX][/us/pl/99/660/tIX], § 939, formerly § 949C, Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3813][/us/stat/100/3813]; renumbered § 939, [Pub. L. 100–607, title I][/us/pl/100/607/tI], § 142(c)(2)(B), Nov. 4, 1988, [102 Stat. 3057][/us/stat/102/3057].)
