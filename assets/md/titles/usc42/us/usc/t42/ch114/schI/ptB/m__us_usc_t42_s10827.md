## § 10827. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated for allotments under this subchapter, $19,500,000 for fiscal year 1992, and such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1993 through 2003.

([Pub. L. 99–319, title I, § 117][/us/pl/99/319/s117], May 23, 1986, [100 Stat. 485][/us/stat/100/485]; [Pub. L. 100–509, § 7(f)][/us/pl/100/509/s7/f], Oct. 20, 1988, [102 Stat. 2546][/us/stat/102/2546]; [Pub. L. 102–173, § 8][/us/pl/102/173/s8], Nov. 27, 1991, [105 Stat. 1219][/us/stat/105/1219]; [Pub. L. 106–310, div. B, title XXXII, § 3206(f)][/us/pl/106/310/s3206/f], Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1195][/us/stat/114/1195].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2000—[Pub. L. 106–310][/us/pl/106/310] substituted “2003” for “1995”.

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–173][/us/pl/102/173] amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section read as follows: “For allotments under this subchapter, there are authorized to be appropriated $14,300,000 for fiscal year 1989, and such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 1990 and fiscal year 1991.”

    1988—[Pub. L. 100–509][/us/pl/100/509] amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section read as follows: “For allotments under this subchapter, there are authorized to be appropriated $10,000,000 for fiscal year 1986, $10,500,000 for fiscal year 1987, and $11,025,000 for fiscal year 1988.”

 __Effective Date of 1988 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 100–509, § 8][/us/pl/100/509/s8], Oct. 20, 1988, [102 Stat. 2546][/us/stat/102/2546], provided that:

>     “(a) __In General.—__ 

>     The amendments made by this Act \[amending sections 10802, 10804 to 10806, 10821, 10822, and 10825 of this title\], other than the amendment made by section 7(f) \[amending this section\], shall become effective on the date of the enactment of this Act \[Oct. 20, 1988\].

>     “(b) __Authorization of Appropriations.—__ 

>     The amendment made by section 7(f) \[amending this section\] shall become effective on October 1, 1988.”
