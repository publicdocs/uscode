## § 9675. Applicability of securities laws

    (a) __Ownership interests__ 

    The ownership interests of members of a risk retention group shall be considered to be—

        (1) exempted securities for purposes of [section 77e of title 15][/us/usc/t15/s77e] and for purposes of section 78l of title 15; and

        (2) securities for purposes of the provisions of [section 77q of title 15][/us/usc/t15/s77q] and the provisions of [section 78j of title 15][/us/usc/t15/s78j].

    (b) __Investment Company Act__ 

        A risk retention group shall not be considered to be an investment company for purposes of the Investment Company Act of 1940 ([15 U.S.C. 80a–1][/us/usc/t15/s80a–1] et seq.).

    (c) __Blue sky law__ 

        The ownership interests of members in a risk retention group shall not be considered securities for purposes of any State blue sky law.

([Pub. L. 96–510, title IV, § 405][/us/pl/96/510/s405], as added [Pub. L. 99–563, § 11(c)(1)][/us/pl/99/563/s11/c/1], Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3177][/us/stat/100/3177].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Investment Company Act of 1940, referred to in subsec. (b), is title I of [act Aug. 22, 1940, ch. 686][/us/act/1940-08-22/ch686], [54 Stat. 789][/us/stat/54/789], as amended, which is classified generally to subchapter I (§ 80a–1 et seq.) of chapter 2D of Title 15, Commerce and Trade.
