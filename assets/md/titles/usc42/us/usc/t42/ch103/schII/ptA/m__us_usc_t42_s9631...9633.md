## §§ 9631 to 9633. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–499, title V][/us/pl/99/499/tV], § 517(c)(1), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1774][/us/stat/100/1774]

    Section 9631, [Pub. L. 96–510, title II][/us/pl/96/510/tII], § 221, Dec. 11, 1980, [94 Stat. 2801][/us/stat/94/2801]; [Pub. L. 99–499, title II][/us/pl/99/499/tII], § 204, Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1696][/us/stat/100/1696], provided for establishment of a Hazardous Substances Superfund, so redesignated by [section 204 of Pub. L. 99–499][/us/pl/99/499/s204]. See [section 9507 of Title 26][/us/usc/t26/s9507], Internal Revenue Code.

    Section 9632, [Pub. L. 96–510, title II][/us/pl/96/510/tII], § 222, Dec. 11, 1980, [94 Stat. 2802][/us/stat/94/2802], limited liability of United States to amount in Trust Fund.

    Section 9633, [Pub. L. 96–510, title II][/us/pl/96/510/tII], § 223, Dec. 11, 1980, [94 Stat. 2802][/us/stat/94/2802], contained administrative provisions.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal by [Pub. L. 99–499][/us/pl/99/499] effective Jan. 1, 1987, see [section 517(e) of Pub. L. 99–499][/us/pl/99/499/s517/e], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 9507 of Title 26][/us/usc/t26/s9507], Internal Revenue Code.
