## §§ 14713 to 14715. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314][/us/pl/111/314], § 6, Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444][/us/stat/124/3444]

    Section 14713, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I][/us/pl/105/303/tI], § 105, Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2852][/us/stat/112/2852], related to acquisition of space science data. See [section 50113 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50113], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Section 14714, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I][/us/pl/105/303/tI], § 106, Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2853][/us/stat/112/2853], related to administration of commercial space centers. See [section 50114 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50114].

    Section 14715, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I][/us/pl/105/303/tI], § 107, Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2853][/us/stat/112/2853], related to sources of Earth Science data. Subsecs. (a), (b), (d), and (e) were repealed and reenacted as subsecs. (a), (b), (c) and (d) of [section 50115 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50115]. Subsec (c), which required the Administrator to submit certain study results to Congress within six months after Oct. 28, 1998, was repealed as obsolete.
