## § 14712. Repealed or Transferred

 __Codification__ 

    Section, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I, § 104][/us/pl/105/303/s104], Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2852][/us/stat/112/2852], which related to promotion of United States Global Positioning System standards, was repealed in part and transferred in part. Subsec. (b) was repealed and reenacted as [section 50112 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50112], National and Commercial Space Programs, by [Pub. L. 111–314][/us/pl/111/314], §§ 3, 6, Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3328][/us/stat/124/3328], 3444, which Act enacted Title 51. Subsec. (a) was transferred and is set out as a note under [section 50112 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50112].

§§ 14713 to 14715. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 111–314, § 6__  __][/us/pl/111/314/s6]__  __,__  __Dec. 18, 2010__  __,__  __[__  __124 Stat. 3444__  __][/us/stat/124/3444]__ 

    Section 14713, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I, § 105][/us/pl/105/303/s105], Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2852][/us/stat/112/2852], related to acquisition of space science data. See [section 50113 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50113], National and Commercial Space Programs.

    Section 14714, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I, § 106][/us/pl/105/303/s106], Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2853][/us/stat/112/2853], related to administration of commercial space centers. See [section 50114 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50114].

    Section 14715, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I, § 107][/us/pl/105/303/s107], Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2853][/us/stat/112/2853], related to sources of Earth Science data. Subsecs. (a), (b), (d), and (e) were repealed and reenacted as subsecs. (a), (b), (c) and (d) of [section 50115 of Title 51][/us/usc/t51/s50115]. Subsec (c), which required the Administrator to submit certain study results to Congress within six months after Oct. 28, 1998, was repealed as obsolete.
