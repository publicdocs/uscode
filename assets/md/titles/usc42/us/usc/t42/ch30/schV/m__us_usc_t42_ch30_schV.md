    Section 2624, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 501][/us/pl/87/415/s501], as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13][/us/pl/90/636/s13], Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1355][/us/stat/82/1355], set out Congressional declaration of purpose in providing for supplementary State programs.

    Section 2625, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 502][/us/pl/87/415/s502], as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13][/us/pl/90/636/s13], Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1356][/us/stat/82/1356], provided authorization for grants for supplementary State programs.

    Section 2626, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 503][/us/pl/87/415/s503], as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13][/us/pl/90/636/s13], Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1356][/us/stat/82/1356], provided for applications for supplementary State programs and conditions for such programs.

    Section 2627, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 504][/us/pl/87/415/s504], as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13][/us/pl/90/636/s13], Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1356][/us/stat/82/1356], provided for promulgation of rules and regulations.

    Section 2628, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 505][/us/pl/87/415/s505], as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13][/us/pl/90/636/s13], Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1356][/us/stat/82/1356], authorized appropriations for supplementary State programs.
