## §§ 2611 to 2620. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VII][/us/pl/93/203/tVII], § 714, formerly title VI, § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883][/us/stat/87/883]; renumbered title VII, § 714, [Pub. L. 93–567, title I][/us/pl/93/567/tI], § 101, Dec. 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 1845][/us/stat/88/1845]

    Section 2611, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 301, Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 30][/us/stat/76/30]; [Pub. L. 89–15][/us/pl/89/15], § 10, Apr. 26, 1965, [79 Stat. 79][/us/stat/79/79]; [Pub. L. 89–792][/us/pl/89/792], § 7, Nov. 7, 1966, [80 Stat. 1437][/us/stat/80/1437]; [Pub. L. 90–636][/us/pl/90/636], §§ 8–10, Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1354][/us/stat/82/1354]; [Pub. L. 91–4][/us/pl/91/4], § 1, Mar. 19, 1969, [83 Stat. 6][/us/stat/83/6], provided for apportionment of benefits and State administration of funds.

    Section 2612, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 302, Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 31][/us/stat/76/31]; [Pub. L. 89–15][/us/pl/89/15], § 11, Apr. 26, 1965, [79 Stat. 79][/us/stat/79/79], required maintenance of a State effort as a prerequisite to approval of Federal financing.

    Section 2613, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 303, Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 31][/us/stat/76/31], called for the utilization of available services and facilities of other Federal agencies and instrumentalities and of resources for skill development.

    Section 2614, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 304, Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 31][/us/stat/76/31]; [Pub. L. 88–214][/us/pl/88/214], § 7, Dec. 19, 1963, [77 Stat. 424][/us/stat/77/424]; [Pub. L. 89–15][/us/pl/89/15], § 12, Apr. 26, 1965, [79 Stat. 80][/us/stat/79/80]; [Pub. L. 89–792][/us/pl/89/792], § 6(b), Nov. 7, 1966, [80 Stat. 1437][/us/stat/80/1437]; [Pub. L. 90–636][/us/pl/90/636], § 1(4), Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1352][/us/stat/82/1352], authorized appropriations.

    Section 2615, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 305, Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 32][/us/stat/76/32]; [Pub. L. 88–214][/us/pl/88/214], § 8, Dec. 19, 1963, [77 Stat. 424][/us/stat/77/424]; [Pub. L. 89–15][/us/pl/89/15], § 13, Apr. 26, 1965, [79 Stat. 80][/us/stat/79/80]; 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 2, eff. July 1, 1970, 35 F.R. 7959, [84 Stat. 2085][/us/stat/84/2085], placed certain limitations on uses of appropriated funds.

    Section 2616, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 306, Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 32][/us/stat/76/32]; [Pub. L. 89–15][/us/pl/89/15], § 14, Apr. 26, 1965, [79 Stat. 80][/us/stat/79/80], set out authority of Secretaries of Labor and of Health, Education, and Welfare to contract, establish procedures, and make payments.

    Section 2617, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 307, Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 32][/us/stat/76/32], provided that selection of persons for training shall not be contingent upon their membership or non-membership in a labor organization.

    Section 2618, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 308, Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 32][/us/stat/76/32]; [Pub. L. 90–636][/us/pl/90/636], § 5(c), Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1353][/us/stat/82/1353], defined “State”.

    Section 2619, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 309, as added [Pub. L. 90–636][/us/pl/90/636], § 11, Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1354][/us/stat/82/1354], provided for training and technical assistance.

    Section 2620, [Pub. L. 87–415, title III][/us/pl/87/415/tIII], § 310, Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 33][/us/stat/76/33]; [Pub. L. 88–214][/us/pl/88/214], § 10, Dec. 19, 1963, [77 Stat. 424][/us/stat/77/424]; [Pub. L. 89–15][/us/pl/89/15], § 16, Apr. 26, 1965, [79 Stat. 80][/us/stat/79/80]; [Pub. L. 90–636][/us/pl/90/636], § 1(5), Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1352][/us/stat/82/1352]; [Pub. L. 92–277][/us/pl/92/277], § 1, Apr. 24, 1972, [86 Stat. 124][/us/stat/86/124], called for termination of authority to operate training and skill development programs under title II of [Pub. L. 87–415][/us/pl/87/415] at the close of June 30, 1973.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 93–203, title VII][/us/pl/93/203/tVII], § 714, formerly title VI, § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883][/us/stat/87/883]; renumbered title VII, § 714, [Pub.
