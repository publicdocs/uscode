## § 11924. Definitions

    For purposes of this subchapter:

    (1) __Controlled substance__ 

        The term “controlled substance” has the meaning given such term in [section 802 of title 21][/us/usc/t21/s802].

    (2) __Secretary__ 

        The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development.

([Pub. L. 100–690, title V, § 5145][/us/pl/100/690/s5145], Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4304][/us/stat/102/4304].)
