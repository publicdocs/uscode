    Section, [Pub. L. 101–625, title V][/us/pl/101/625/tV], § 520, Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 4202][/us/stat/104/4202]; [Pub.  __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective and applicable beginning upon Oct. 1, 1999, except as otherwise provided, with provision that Secretary may implement repeal before such date, and with savings provision, see [section 503 of Pub.
