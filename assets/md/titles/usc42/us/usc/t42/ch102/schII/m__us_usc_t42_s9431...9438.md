## §§ 9431 to 9438. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX][/us/pl/97/35/tIX], § 902(e)(1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 560][/us/stat/95/560]

    Section 9431, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II][/us/pl/96/398/tII], § 201, Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1571][/us/stat/94/1571], set forth authority, etc., for grants for community mental health centers.

    Section 9432, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II][/us/pl/96/398/tII], § 202, Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1573][/us/stat/94/1573], set forth authority, etc., for grants for services for chronically, mentally ill individuals.

    Section 9433, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II][/us/pl/96/398/tII], § 203, Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1575][/us/stat/94/1575], set forth authority, etc., for grants for services for severely, mentally disturbed children and adults.

    Section 9434, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II][/us/pl/96/398/tII], § 204, Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1577][/us/stat/94/1577], set forth authority, etc., for grants for mental health services for elderly individuals and other priority populations.

    Section 9435, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II][/us/pl/96/398/tII], § 205, Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1581][/us/stat/94/1581], set forth authority, etc., for grants for non-revenue producing services.

    Section 9436, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II][/us/pl/96/398/tII], § 206, Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1582][/us/stat/94/1582], set forth authority, etc., for grants for mental health services in health care centers.

    Section 9437, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II][/us/pl/96/398/tII], § 207, Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1583][/us/stat/94/1583], set forth authority, etc., for grants and contracts for innovative projects.

    Section 9438, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II][/us/pl/96/398/tII], § 208, Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1584][/us/stat/94/1584], set forth authority, etc., for grants for prevention of mental illness and promotion of mental health.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 902(h) of Pub. L. 97–35][/us/pl/97/35/s902/h], set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under section 238l of this title.
