## § 2692. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–211, title I][/us/pl/91/211/tI], § 104, Mar. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 55][/us/stat/84/55]

    Section, [Pub. L. 88–164, title IV][/us/pl/88/164/tIV], § 402, Oct. 31, 1963, [77 Stat. 297][/us/stat/77/297]; [Pub. L. 89–105][/us/pl/89/105], § 2(a), Aug. 4, 1965, [79 Stat. 427][/us/stat/79/427], provided for State standards for variable Federal share of cost.
