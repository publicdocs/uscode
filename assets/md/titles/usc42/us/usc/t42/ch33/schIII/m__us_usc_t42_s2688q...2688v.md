## §§ 2688q to 2688v. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 2688q to 2688v were superseded in the general amendment of title II of [Pub. L. 88–164][/us/pl/88/164], by [Pub. L. 94–63, title III][/us/pl/94/63/tIII], § 303, July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 309][/us/stat/89/309].

    Section 2688q, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 263, as added [Pub. L. 90–574, title III][/us/pl/90/574/tIII], § 303(a), Oct. 15, 1968, [82 Stat. 1011][/us/stat/82/1011], required consent for alcoholics, narcotic addicts, and persons with drug abuse and drug dependence problems to be subjects of research.

    Section 2688r, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 264, as added [Pub. L. 91–211, title V][/us/pl/91/211/tV], § 501, Mar. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 61][/us/stat/84/61]; amended [Pub. L. 91–513, title I][/us/pl/91/513/tI], § 1(b)(3), Oct. 27, 1970, [84 Stat. 1238][/us/stat/84/1238], authorized grants for consultation services for community mental health centers, alcoholism prevention and treatment facility, etc., and set forth conditions for such grants.

    Section 2688s, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 265, as added [Pub. L. 91–211, title V][/us/pl/91/211/tV], § 502, Mar. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 62][/us/stat/84/62], defined “technical personnel”.

    Section 2688t, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 266, as added [Pub. L. 91–211, title V][/us/pl/91/211/tV], § 503(a), Mar. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 62][/us/stat/84/62]; amended [Pub. L. 91–616, title IV][/us/pl/91/616/tIV], § 402, Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1854][/us/stat/84/1854]; [Pub. L. 92–255, title V][/us/pl/92/255/tV], § 502(b), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 85][/us/stat/86/85], required approval of advisory council concerned with area of interest for grants for construction and staffing of centers and facilities.

    Section 2688u, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 271, as added [Pub. L. 91–211, title IV][/us/pl/91/211/tIV], § 401, Mar. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 60][/us/stat/84/60]; amended [Pub. L. 93–45, title II][/us/pl/93/45/tII], § 207, June 18, 1973, [87 Stat. 94][/us/stat/87/94]; [Pub. L. 94–63, title VII][/us/pl/94/63/tVII], § 701(e)(8), July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 353][/us/stat/89/353], authorized grants for construction and staffing of treatment facilities concerned with the mental health of children and set forth conditions for such grants.

    Section 2688v, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 272, as added [Pub. L. 91–211, title IV][/us/pl/91/211/tIV], § 401, Mar. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 61][/us/stat/84/61], authorized grants for developing specialized training programs, training personnel, and conducting surveys and field trials in services concerned with the mental health of children.
