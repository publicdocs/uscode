## §§ 2689i to 2689l. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX][/us/pl/97/35/tIX], § 902(e)(2)(B), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 560][/us/stat/95/560]

    Section 2689i, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 221, as added [Pub. L. 94–63, title III][/us/pl/94/63/tIII], § 303, July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 322][/us/stat/89/322]; amended [Pub. L. 94–273][/us/pl/94/273], § 44, Apr. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 381][/us/stat/90/381], set forth provisions respecting Federal share, etc., for facilities assistance.

    A prior [section 221 of Pub. L. 88–164][/us/pl/88/164/s221], title II, was classified to [section 2688a of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2688a].

    Section 2689j, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 222, as added [Pub. L. 94–63, title III][/us/pl/94/63/tIII], § 303, July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 323][/us/stat/89/323], related to approval of projects, and procedures applicable.

    A prior [section 222 of Pub. L. 88–164][/us/pl/88/164/s222], title II, was classified to [section 2688b of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2688b].

    Section 2689k, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 223, as added [Pub. L. 94–63, title III][/us/pl/94/63/tIII], § 303, July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 325][/us/stat/89/325], related to payments for projects.

    A prior [section 223 of Pub. L. 88–164][/us/pl/88/164/s223], title II, was classified to [section 2688c of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2688c].

    Section 2689l, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II][/us/pl/88/164/tII], § 224, as added [Pub. L. 94–63, title III][/us/pl/94/63/tIII], § 303, July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 326][/us/stat/89/326], related to judicial review of projects.

    A prior [section 224 of Pub. L. 88–164][/us/pl/88/164/s224], title II, was classified to [section 2688d of this title][/us/usc/t42/s2688d].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 902(h) of Pub. L. 97–35][/us/pl/97/35/s902/h], set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under section 238l of this title.
