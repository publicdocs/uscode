## § 8275. Administration

    The Secretary shall administer the program established under [section 8272 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s8272] and shall—

    (1) consult with the Secretary of Defense to insure that the installation and purchase of photovoltaic solar electric systems pursuant to this part shall not interfere with defense-related activities;

    (2) prescribe such requirements as may be appropriate to monitor and assess the performance and operation of photovoltaic electric systems installed pursuant to this part; and

    (3) report annually to the Congress on the status of the program.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary shall not be subject to the requirements of [section 553 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s553], in the performance of his functions under this part.

([Pub. L. 95–619, title V][/us/pl/95/619/tV], § 566, Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3281][/us/stat/92/3281]; [Pub. L. 96–294, title IV][/us/pl/96/294/tIV], § 407(3), (4), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 717][/us/stat/94/717], 718.)

 __Amendments__ 

    1980—[Pub. L. 96–294][/us/pl/96/294] inserted provisions relating to inapplicability of [section 553 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s553] and substituted “requirements” for “rules and regulations” in par. (2).

 __Termination of Reporting Requirements__ 

    For termination, effective May 15, 2000, of the reporting provision in par. (3) of this section, see [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66][/us/pl/104/66/s3003], as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31][/us/usc/t31/s1113], Money and Finance, and the 3rd item on page 87 of House Document No. 103–7.
