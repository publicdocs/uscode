## § 5741. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 656][/us/pl/98/473/s656], Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2124][/us/stat/98/2124]

    Section, [Pub. L. 93–415, title III, § 331][/us/pl/93/415/s331], as added [Pub. L. 95–115, § 7(c)][/us/pl/95/115/s7/c], Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1059][/us/stat/91/1059]; amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)][/us/pl/96/88/s509/b], Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695][/us/stat/93/695], authorized President to submit to Congress after April 30, 1978, a reorganization plan for establishment of an Office of Youth Assistance, subject to Congressional resolution of disapproval.

    Prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 98–473][/us/pl/98/473], [section 5741 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s5741] comprised part C of this subchapter. [Section 657(e) of Pub. L. 98–473][/us/pl/98/473/s657/e] redesignated former part D, consisting of [section 5751 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s5751], as part C. Previously, part C was redesignated part D by [Pub. L. 95–115, § 7(c)][/us/pl/95/115/s7/c], Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1059][/us/stat/91/1059].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 12, 1984, see [section 670(a) of Pub. L. 98–473][/us/pl/98/473/s670/a], set out as an Effective Date of 1984 Amendment note under [section 5601 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s5601].
