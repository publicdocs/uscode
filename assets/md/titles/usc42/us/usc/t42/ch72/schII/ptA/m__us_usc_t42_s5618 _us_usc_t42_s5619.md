###### §§ 5618, 5619. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–509][/us/pl/96/509], § 9, Dec. 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 2753][/us/stat/94/2753]

    Section 5618, [Pub. L. 93–415, title II][/us/pl/93/415/tII], § 208, Sept. 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 1117][/us/stat/88/1117], [Pub. L. 95–115][/us/pl/95/115], § 3(a)(3)(B), (f), Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1048][/us/stat/91/1048], 1050, set out the duties and provided for the staffing of the National Advisory Committee and numerous subcommittees.

    Section 5619, [Pub. L. 93–415, title II][/us/pl/93/415/tII], § 209, Sept. 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 1118][/us/stat/88/1118], set out provisions for compensation and reimbursement for travel and other expenses of full and part time Federal employees serving on the Advisory Committee.
