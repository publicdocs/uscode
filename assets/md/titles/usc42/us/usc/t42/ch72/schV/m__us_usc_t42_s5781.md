## § 5781. Definition

    In this subchapter, the term “State advisory group” means the advisory group appointed by the chief executive officer of a State under a plan described in [section 5633(a) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s5633/a].

([Pub. L. 93–415, title V, § 502][/us/pl/93/415/s502], as added [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title II, § 12222(a)][/us/pl/107/273/s12222/a], Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1894][/us/stat/116/1894].)
