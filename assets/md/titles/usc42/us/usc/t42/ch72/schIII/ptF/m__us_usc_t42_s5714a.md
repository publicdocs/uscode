## § 5714a. Assistance to potential grantees

    The Secretary shall provide informational assistance to potential grantees interested in establishing runaway and homeless youth centers and transitional living youth projects.

([Pub. L. 93–415, title III][/us/pl/93/415/tIII], § 380, formerly § 315, as added [Pub. L. 98–473, title II][/us/pl/98/473/tII], § 655(2), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2124][/us/stat/98/2124]; renumbered § 341 and amended [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII][/us/pl/100/690/tVII], § 7273(a), (e)(2), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4454][/us/stat/102/4454], 4455; renumbered § 371, [Pub. L. 102–586][/us/pl/102/586], § 3(g)(1)(B)(ii), Nov. 4, 1992, [106 Stat. 5022][/us/stat/106/5022]; renumbered § 380 and amended [Pub. L. 106–71][/us/pl/106/71], § 3(j), (q), Oct. 12, 1999, [113 Stat. 1038][/us/stat/113/1038], 1042.)

 __Amendments__ 

    1999—[Pub. L. 106–71][/us/pl/106/71], § 3(j), struck out at end: “Such assistance shall consist of information on—

    “(1) steps necessary to establish a runaway and homeless youth center or transitional living youth project, including information on securing space for such center or such project, obtaining insurance, staffing, and establishing operating procedures;

    “(2) securing local private or public financial support for the operation of such center or such project, including information on procedures utilized by grantees under this subchapter; and

    “(3) the need for the establishment of additional runaway and homeless youth centers in the geographical area identified by the potential grantee involved.”

    1988—[Pub. L. 100–690][/us/pl/100/690], § 7273(a)(1), inserted “and transitional living youth projects” after “homeless youth centers” in introductory provisions.

    Par. (1). [Pub. L. 100–690][/us/pl/100/690], § 7273(a)(2), (3), inserted “or transitional living youth project” after “homeless youth center” and “or such project” after “such center”.

    Par. (2). [Pub. L. 100–690][/us/pl/100/690], § 7273(a)(3), inserted “such project” after “such center”.

    Par. (3). [Pub. L. 100–690][/us/pl/100/690], § 7273(a)(4), inserted “and homeless” after “runaway”.
