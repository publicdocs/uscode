## § 10506. Prohibition of land acquisition

    No funds provided under this chapter shall be used for land acquisition.

([Pub. L. 98–473, title II][/us/pl/98/473/tII], § 609R, Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2106][/us/stat/98/2106].)
