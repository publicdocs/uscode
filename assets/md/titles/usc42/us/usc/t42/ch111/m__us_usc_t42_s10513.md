## § 10513. Authorization of appropriations

    (a) __Assistance in form of funds__ 

        There is authorized to be appropriated $20,000,000 for each fiscal year ending after September 30, 2021, to provide under this chapter Federal law enforcement assistance in the form of funds.

    (b) __Assistance other than funds__ 

        There are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year ending after September 30, 1984, such sums as may be necessary to provide under this chapter Federal law enforcement assistance other than funds.

([Pub. L. 98–473, title II][/us/pl/98/473/tII], § 609Y, Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2107][/us/stat/98/2107]; [Pub. L. 114–198, title II][/us/pl/114/198/tII], § 201(b), July 22, 2016, [130 Stat. 714][/us/stat/130/714].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2016—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 114–198][/us/pl/114/198] substituted “September 30, 2021” for “September 30, 1984”.
