## §§ 3071 to 3074. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–646, title II][/us/pl/91/646/tII], § 220(a)(8), title III, § 306, Jan. 2, 1971, [84 Stat. 1903][/us/stat/84/1903], 1907

    Section 3071, [Pub. L. 89–117, title IV][/us/pl/89/117/tIV], § 401, Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 485][/us/stat/79/485]; [Pub. L. 90–19][/us/pl/90/19], § 22(f), May 25, 1967, [81 Stat. 27][/us/stat/81/27], related to definitions.

    Section 3072, [Pub. L. 89–117, title IV][/us/pl/89/117/tIV], § 402, Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 485][/us/stat/79/485]; [Pub. L. 90–19][/us/pl/90/19], § 22(a), May 25, 1967, [81 Stat. 26][/us/stat/81/26], related to acquisition of realty by eminent domain.

    Section 3073, [Pub. L. 89–117, title IV][/us/pl/89/117/tIV], § 403, Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 485][/us/stat/79/485], related to financing of payments in eminent domain where amount is in dispute.

    Section 3074, [Pub. L. 89–117, title IV][/us/pl/89/117/tIV], § 404(a), Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 485][/us/stat/79/485]; [Pub. L. 90–19][/us/pl/90/19], § 22(a), May 25, 1967, [81 Stat. 26][/us/stat/81/26], related to relocation payments under federally assisted development programs.

    Subject matter is covered by section 4601 et seq. of this title.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal not applicable to any State so long as sections 4630 and 4655 of this title are not applicable in such State; but such sections completely applicable to all States after July 1, 1972, but until such date applicable to a State to extent the State is able under its laws to comply with such sections, see [section 221 of Pub. L. 91–646][/us/pl/91/646/s221], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 4601 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s4601].

 __Savings Provision__ 

    Any rights or liabilities existing under sections 3071 to 3074 as not affected by such repeal, see sections 220(b) and 306 of [Pub. L. 91–646][/us/pl/91/646], set out as Savings Provision notes under sections 4621 and 4651 of this title.
