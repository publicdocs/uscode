## § 11047. Exemption

    Except as provided in [section 11004 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s11004], this chapter does not apply to the transportation, including the storage incident to such transportation, of any substance or chemical subject to the requirements of this chapter, including the transportation and distribution of natural gas.

([Pub. L. 99–499, title III][/us/pl/99/499/tIII], § 327, Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1757][/us/stat/100/1757].)
