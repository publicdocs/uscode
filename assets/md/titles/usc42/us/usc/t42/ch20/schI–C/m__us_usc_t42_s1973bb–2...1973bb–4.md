## §§ 1973bb–2 to 1973bb–4. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–73, title IV][/us/pl/94/73/tIV], § 407, Aug. 6, 1975, [89 Stat. 405][/us/stat/89/405]

    Section 1973bb–2, [Pub. L. 89–110, title III][/us/pl/89/110/tIII], § 303, as added [Pub. L. 91–285][/us/pl/91/285], § 6, June 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 318][/us/stat/84/318], authorized the Attorney General to institute actions to enforce this subchapter, and provided for jurisdiction of district courts, appeals, and penalties for denial of rights secured by this subchapter. See [section 10701 of Title 52][/us/usc/t52/s10701], Voting and Elections.

    Section 1973bb–3, [Pub. L. 89–110, title III][/us/pl/89/110/tIII], § 304, as added [Pub. L. 91–285][/us/pl/91/285], § 6, June 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 319][/us/stat/84/319], defined “State”. See [section 10702 of Title 52][/us/usc/t52/s10702], Voting and Elections.

    Section 1973bb–4, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III][/us/pl/89/10/tIII], § 305, as added [Pub. L. 91–285][/us/pl/91/285], § 6, June 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 319][/us/stat/84/319], provided for effective date of this subchapter.
