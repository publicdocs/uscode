    Section, [Pub. L. 89–174][/us/pl/89/174], § 13, as added [Pub. L. 101–235, title I][/us/pl/101/235/tI], § 112, Dec. 15, 1989, [103 Stat. 2016][/us/stat/103/2016]; amended [Pub.
