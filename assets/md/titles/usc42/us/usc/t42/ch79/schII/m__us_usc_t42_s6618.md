## § 6618. Major science and technology proposals

    The Director shall identify and provide an annual report to Congress on each major multinational science and technology project, in which the United States is not a participant, which has a total estimated cost greater than $1,000,000,000.

([Pub. L. 94–282, title II, § 209][/us/pl/94/282/s209], as added [Pub. L. 102–245, title V, § 502][/us/pl/102/245/s502], Feb. 14, 1992, [106 Stat. 24][/us/stat/106/24].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 6618, [Pub. L. 94–282, title II, § 209][/us/pl/94/282/s209], May 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 468][/us/stat/90/468], directed President to transmit annually to Congress a report on science and technology to be prepared by Office of Science and Technology Policy, and directed Director of Office to make the report available as a public document, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–375, title II, § 215(1)][/us/pl/97/375/s215/1], Dec. 21, 1982, [96 Stat. 1826][/us/stat/96/1826]. See [section 6615 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s6615].
