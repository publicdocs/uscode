## § 14614. Effect on other laws

    (a) __Privacy Act of 1974__ 

        Nothing in the Compact shall affect the obligations and responsibilities of the FBI under [section 552a of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s552a] (commonly known as the “Privacy Act of 1974”).

    (b) __Access to certain records not affected__ 

    Nothing in the Compact shall interfere in any manner with—

        (1)

         access, direct or otherwise, to records pursuant to—

            (A) [section 9101 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s9101];

            (B) the National Child Protection Act \[[42 U.S.C. 5119][/us/usc/t42/s5119] et seq.\];

            (C) the Brady Handgun Violence Prevention Act ([Public Law 103–159][/us/pl/103/159]; [107 Stat. 1536][/us/stat/107/1536]);

            (D) the Violent Crime Control and Law Enforcement Act of 1994 ([Public Law 103–322][/us/pl/103/322]; [108 Stat. 2074][/us/stat/108/2074]) or any amendment made by that Act;

            (E) the United States Housing Act of 1937 ([42 U.S.C. 1437][/us/usc/t42/s1437] et seq.); or

            (F) the Native American Housing Assistance and Self-Determination Act of 1996 ([25 U.S.C. 4101][/us/usc/t25/s4101] et seq.); or

        (2) any direct access to Federal criminal history records authorized by law.

    (c) __Authority of FBI under Departments of State, Justice, and Commerce, the Judiciary, and Related Agencies Appropriation Act, 1973__ 

        Nothing in the Compact shall be construed to affect the authority of the FBI under the Departments of State, Justice, and Commerce, the Judiciary, and Related Agencies Appropriation Act, 1973 ([Public Law 92–544][/us/pl/92/544] ([86 Stat. 1115][/us/stat/86/1115])).

    (d) __Federal Advisory Committee Act__ 

        The Council shall not be considered to be a Federal advisory committee for purposes of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.).

    (e) __Members of Council not Federal officers or employees__ 

    Members of the Council (other than a member from the FBI or any at-large member who may be a Federal official or employee) shall not, by virtue of such membership, be deemed—

        (1) to be, for any purpose other than to effect the Compact, officers or employees of the United States (as defined in sections 2104 and 2105 of title 5); or

        (2) to become entitled by reason of Council membership to any compensation or benefit payable or made available by the Federal Government to its officers or employees.

([Pub. L. 105–251, title II][/us/pl/105/251/tII], § 215, Oct. 9, 1998, [112 Stat. 1875][/us/stat/112/1875].)
