## § 15205. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to the Attorney General such sums as may be necessary to carry out this chapter.

([Pub. L. 107–12, § 6][/us/pl/107/12/s6], May 30, 2001, [115 Stat. 22][/us/stat/115/22].)

 __References in Text__ 

    This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 107–12][/us/pl/107/12], May. 30, 2001, [115 Stat. 20][/us/stat/115/20], known as the Public Safety Officer Medal of Valor Act of 2001, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 15201 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s15201] and Tables.
