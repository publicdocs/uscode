## § 3750. Name of program

    (a) __In general__ 

        The grant program established under this part shall be known as the “Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant Program”.

    (b) __References to former programs__ 

        (1) Any reference in a law, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Edward Byrne Memorial State and Local Law Enforcement Assistance Programs, or to the Local Government Law Enforcement Block Grants program, shall be deemed to be a reference to the grant program referred to in subsection (a) of this section.

        (2) Any reference in a law, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to [section 3756 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3756] as such section was in effect on the date of the enactment of the Department of Justice Appropriations Authorization Act, Fiscal Years 2006 through 2009, <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  shall be deemed to be a reference to [section 3755(a) of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3755/a] as amended by the Department of Justice Appropriations Authorization Act, Fiscal Years 2006 through 2009. <sup>\[1\]</sup> 

([Pub. L. 90–351, title I][/us/pl/90/351/tI], § 500, as added [Pub. L. 100–690, title VI][/us/pl/100/690/tVI], § 6091(a), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4329][/us/stat/102/4329]; amended [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI][/us/pl/109/162/tXI], § 1111(a)(2)(B), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3094][/us/stat/119/3094].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The Department of Justice Appropriations Authorization Act, Fiscal Years 2006 through 2009, referred to in subsec. (b)(2), probably means the Violence Against Women and Department of Justice Reauthorization Act of 2005, [Pub. L. 109–162][/us/pl/109/162], Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 2960][/us/stat/119/2960], which repealed [section 3756 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3756] and enacted [section 3755 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3755].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Prior sections 3750a to 3750d were repealed by [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI][/us/pl/109/162/tXI], § 1111(b)(2), (d), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3101][/us/stat/119/3101], 3102, applicable with respect to the first fiscal year beginning after Jan. 5, 2006, and each fiscal year thereafter.

    Section 3750a, [Pub. L. 102–519, title I][/us/pl/102/519/tI], § 130, Oct. 25, 1992, [106 Stat. 3386][/us/stat/106/3386], related to the purpose of former sections 3750a to 3750d, to supplement the provisions of the Edward Byrne Memorial State and Local Law Enforcement Assistance Program to help States to curb motor vehicle thefts and related violence, and authorization of grants to Anti Car Theft Committees.

    Section 3750b, [Pub. L. 102–519, title I][/us/pl/102/519/tI], § 131, Oct. 25, 1992, [106 Stat. 3386][/us/stat/106/3386], related to application for grants.

    Section 3750c, [Pub. L. 102–519, title I][/us/pl/102/519/tI], § 132, Oct. 25, 1992, [106 Stat. 3387][/us/stat/106/3387], related to award of grants.

    Section 3750d, [Pub. L. 102–519, title I][/us/pl/102/519/tI], § 133, Oct. 25, 1992, [106 Stat. 3387][/us/stat/106/3387], related to authorization of appropriations.

    For other prior sections 3750 to 3750d of this title, see note set out preceding [section 3741 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3741].

 __Amendments__ 

    2006—[Pub. L. 109–162][/us/pl/109/162] substituted “Name of program” for “Name of programs” in section catchline and amended text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “The grant programs established under this subchapter shall be known as the ‘Edward Byrne Memorial State and Local Law Enforcement Assistance Programs’.”

 __Effective Date of 2006 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 109–162, title XI][/us/pl/109/162/tXI], § 1111(d), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3102][/us/stat/119/3102], provided that: 

> “The amendments made by this section \[enacting sections 3751 to 3758 of this title, amending this section and sections 3763, 3766, 3766b, 3782, 3789, 3791, 3796bb–1, 3796cc–1, 3796dd–1, 3796ff–1, and 14601 of this title, and repealing sections 3750a to 3750d, former sections 3751 to 3759, and sections 3760 to 3762 of this title\] shall apply with respect to the first fiscal year beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act \[Jan. 5, 2006\] and each fiscal year thereafter.”
