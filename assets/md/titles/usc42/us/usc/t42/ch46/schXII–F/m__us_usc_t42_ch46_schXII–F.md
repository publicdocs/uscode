 __Codification__

L. 103–322, title II][/us/pl/103/322/tII], § 20201(a)(3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1819][/us/stat/108/1819], and amended by [Pub. C, title II][/us/pl/107/273/dC/tII], § 12102(a), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1859][/us/stat/116/1859], without reference to those intervening amendments because of the extensive revision of the Part R by [Pub. L. 107–273][/us/pl/107/273].
