## § 3796gg–2. Definitions and grant conditions

    In this subchapter the definitions and grant conditions in [section 13925 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13925] shall apply.

([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2008][/us/pl/90/351/s2008], as added [Pub. L. 109–162, § 3(c)(1)][/us/pl/109/162/s3/c/1], Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 2971][/us/stat/119/2971].)

 __Codification__ 

    Another [section 2008 of Pub. L. 90–351][/us/pl/90/351/s2008] was renumbered section 2016 and is classified to [section 3796gg–11 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3796gg–11].
