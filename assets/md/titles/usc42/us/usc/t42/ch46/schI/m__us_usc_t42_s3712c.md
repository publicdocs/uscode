## § 3712c. Inclusion of Indian tribes

    For purposes of sections 3712a and 3712b of this title, the term “State” includes an Indian tribal government.

([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 105][/us/pl/90/351/s105], as added [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI, § 1121(a)][/us/pl/109/162/s1121/a], Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3107][/us/stat/119/3107].)

 __Codification__ 

    Another [section 105 of Pub. L. 90–351][/us/pl/90/351/s105] was renumbered section 109 and is classified to [section 3712h of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3712h].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective with respect to appropriations for fiscal year 2007 and for each fiscal year thereafter, see [section 1121(c) of Pub. L. 109–162][/us/pl/109/162/s1121/c], set out as a note under [section 3712a of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3712a].
