## § 3796dd–5. Revocation or suspension of funding

    If the Attorney General determines, as a result of the reviews required by [section 3796dd–4 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3796dd–4], or otherwise, that a grant recipient under this subchapter is not in substantial compliance with the terms and requirements of an approved grant application submitted under [section 3796dd–1 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3796dd–1], the Attorney General may revoke or suspend funding of that grant, in whole or in part.

([Pub. L. 90–351, title I][/us/pl/90/351/tI], § 1706, as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title I][/us/pl/103/322/tI], § 10003(a)(3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1813][/us/stat/108/1813].)
