## § 3789k. Prohibition on use of Central Intelligence Agency services

    Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, no use will be made of services, facilities, or personnel of the Central Intelligence Agency.

([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 815][/us/pl/90/351/s815], formerly § 822, as added [Pub. L. 96–157, § 2][/us/pl/96/157/s2], Dec. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 1214][/us/stat/93/1214]; renumbered § 815, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609B(f)][/us/pl/98/473/s609B/f], Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2093][/us/stat/98/2093].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 815 of Pub. L. 90–351][/us/pl/90/351/s815] was renumbered section 809 and is classified to [section 3789d of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3789d].
