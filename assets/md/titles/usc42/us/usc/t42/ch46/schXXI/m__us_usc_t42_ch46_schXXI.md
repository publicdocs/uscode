### SUBCHAPTER XXI—SEX OFFENDER APPREHENSION GRANTS; JUVENILE SEX OFFENDER TREATMENT GRANTS

 __Codification__ 

    This subchapter is comprised of part KK, formerly part X, of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968, [Pub. L. 90–351][/us/pl/90/351], as added by [Pub. L. 109–248, title VI, § 623][/us/pl/109/248/s623], July 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 635][/us/stat/120/635], and redesignated part KK by [Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 114(1)][/us/pl/110/199/s114/1], Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 677][/us/stat/122/677]. Another part X of title I of [Pub. L. 90–351][/us/pl/90/351], as added by [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXI, § 210302(c)(1)(C)][/us/pl/103/322/s210302/c/1/C], Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2066][/us/stat/108/2066], is classified to subchapter XII–L (§ 3796kk et seq.) of this chapter.

    [Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 114(1)][/us/pl/110/199/s114/1], Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 677][/us/stat/122/677], which directed amendment of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 by redesignating part X as part KK, was executed by redesignating part X of title I of [Pub. L. 90–351][/us/pl/90/351], as added by [Pub. L. 109–248][/us/pl/109/248], as part KK to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
