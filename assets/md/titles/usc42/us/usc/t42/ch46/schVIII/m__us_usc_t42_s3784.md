## § 3784. Finality of determinations

    In carrying out the functions vested by this chapter in the Bureau of Justice Assistance, the Bureau of Justice Statistics, or the National Institute of Justice, their determinations, findings, and conclusions shall be final and conclusive upon all applications.

([Pub. L. 90–351, title I][/us/pl/90/351/tI], § 803, formerly § 804, as added [Pub. L. 96–157][/us/pl/96/157], § 2, Dec. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 1203][/us/stat/93/1203]; renumbered § 803 and amended [Pub. L. 98–473, title II][/us/pl/98/473/tII], § 609B(c), (f), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2093][/us/stat/98/2093]; [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI][/us/pl/109/162/tXI], § 1155(2), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3113][/us/stat/119/3113].)

 __Codification__

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 803 of Pub. L. 90–351][/us/pl/90/351/s803] was renumbered section 802 and is classified to [section 3783 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3783].

 __Amendments__ 

    2006—[Pub. L. 109–162][/us/pl/109/162] struck out “, after reasonable notice and opportunity for a hearing,” after “conclusions shall” and “, except as otherwise provided herein” before period at end.
