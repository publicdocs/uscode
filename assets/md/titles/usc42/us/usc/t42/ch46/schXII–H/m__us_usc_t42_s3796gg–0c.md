## § 3796gg–0c. Staff of Violence Against Women Office

    The Attorney General shall ensure that the Director has adequate staff to support the Director in carrying out the Director’s responsibilities under this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 90–351, title I][/us/pl/90/351/tI], § 2005, as added [Pub. L. 107–273, div. A, title IV][/us/pl/107/273/dA/tIV], § 402(3), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1791][/us/stat/116/1791].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior [section 2005 of Pub. L. 90–351][/us/pl/90/351/s2005] was renumbered section 2010 and is classified to [section 3796gg–4 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3796gg–4].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective 90 days after Nov. 2, 2002, see [section 403 of Pub. L. 107–273][/us/pl/107/273/s403], set out as a note under [section 3796gg–0 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s3796gg–0].
