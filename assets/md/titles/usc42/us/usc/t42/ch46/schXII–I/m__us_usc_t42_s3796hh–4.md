## § 3796hh–4. Definitions and grant conditions

    In this subchapter the definitions and grant conditions in [section 13925 of this title][/us/usc/t42/s13925] shall apply.

([Pub. L. 90–351, title I][/us/pl/90/351/tI], § 2105, as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title IV][/us/pl/103/322/tIV], § 40231(a)(3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1933][/us/stat/108/1933]; amended [Pub. L. 106–386, div. B, title I][/us/pl/106/386/dB/tI], § 1109(a)(2), Oct. 28, 2000, [114 Stat. 1503][/us/stat/114/1503]; [Pub. L. 109–162][/us/pl/109/162], § 3(c)(2), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 2972][/us/stat/119/2972].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2006—[Pub. L. 109–162][/us/pl/109/162] amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section consisted of pars. (1) to (3) defining for purposes of this subchapter “domestic violence”, “protection order”, and “dating violence”.

    2000—Par. (3). [Pub. L. 106–386][/us/pl/106/386] added par. (3).
