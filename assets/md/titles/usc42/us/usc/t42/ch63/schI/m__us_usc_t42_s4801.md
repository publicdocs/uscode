## § 4801. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–626, title II][/us/pl/95/626/tII], § 208(b), Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3588][/us/stat/92/3588]

    Section, [Pub. L. 91–695, title I][/us/pl/91/695/tI], § 101, Jan. 13, 1971, [84 Stat. 2078][/us/stat/84/2078]; [Pub. L. 93–151][/us/pl/93/151], § 1, Nov. 9, 1973, [87 Stat. 565][/us/stat/87/565]; [Pub. L. 94–317, title II][/us/pl/94/317/tII], 204(a), June 23, 1976, [90 Stat. 705][/us/stat/90/705], related to the development of local programs with respect to detection and treatment of lead-based paint poisoning.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 95–626, title II][/us/pl/95/626/tII], § 208(b), Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3588][/us/stat/92/3588], provided that the repeal of this section is effective Oct. 1, 1979.

 __Short Title__ 

[Pub. L. 91–695][/us/pl/91/695], § 1, Jan. 13, 1971, [84 Stat. 2078][/us/stat/84/2078], provided: 

> “That this Act \[enacting this chapter\] may be cited as the ‘Lead-Based Paint Poisoning Prevention Act’.”
