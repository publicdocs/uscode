## \[§ 1963A. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–702, title X][/us/pl/100/702/tX], § 1002(b)(2), Nov. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 4664][/us/stat/102/4664]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 96–417, title V][/us/pl/96/417/tV], § 511(a), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1743][/us/stat/94/1743], provided for registration of judgments of the Court of International Trade. See [section 1963 of this title][/us/usc/t28/s1963].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective 90 days after Nov. 19, 1988, see [section 1002(c) of Pub. L. 100–702][/us/pl/100/702/s1002/c], set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 1963 of this title][/us/usc/t28/s1963].
