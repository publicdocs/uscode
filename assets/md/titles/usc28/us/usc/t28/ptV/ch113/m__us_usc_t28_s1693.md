## § 1693. Place of arrest in civil action

    Except as otherwise provided by Act of Congress, no person shall be arrested in one district for trial in another in any civil action in a district court.

([June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], [62 Stat. 945][/us/stat/62/945].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 112 ([Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231][/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231], § 51, [36 Stat. 1101][/us/stat/36/1101]; [Sept. 19, 1922, ch. 345][/us/act/1922-09-19/ch345], [42 Stat. 849][/us/stat/42/849]; [Mar. 4, 1925, ch. 526][/us/act/1925-03-04/ch526], § 1, [43 Stat. 1264][/us/stat/43/1264]; [Apr. 16, 1936, ch. 230][/us/act/1936-04-16/ch230], [49 Stat. 1213][/us/stat/49/1213]).

    The exception at the beginning of the section was substituted for “Except as provided in sections 113–117 of this title.”
