## § 1931. Disposition of filing fees

    (a) Of the amounts paid to the clerk of court as a fee under section 1914(a) or as part of a judgment for costs under [section 2412(a)(2) of this title][/us/usc/t28/s2412/a/2], $190 shall be deposited into a special fund of the Treasury to be available to offset funds appropriated for the operation and maintenance of the courts of the United States.

    (b) If the court authorizes a fee under section 1914(a) or an amount included in a judgment for costs under [section 2412(a)(2) of this title][/us/usc/t28/s2412/a/2] of less than $250, the entire fee or amount, up to $190, shall be deposited into the special fund provided in this section.
