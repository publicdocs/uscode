## § 108. Nevada

    Nevada constitutes one judicial district.

    Court shall be held at Carson City, Elko, Las Vegas, Reno, Ely, and Lovelock.

([June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], [62 Stat. 885][/us/stat/62/885]; [Pub. L. 101–650, title III, § 324(a)(1)][/us/pl/101/650/s324/a/1], Dec. 1, 1990, [104 Stat. 5120][/us/stat/104/5120].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 174 ([Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231, § 94][/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231/s94], [36 Stat. 1118][/us/stat/36/1118]; [June 24, 1930, ch. 595][/us/act/1930-06-24/ch595], [46 Stat. 806][/us/stat/46/806]; [Nov. 15, 1945, ch. 482][/us/act/1945-11-15/ch482], [59 Stat. 582][/us/stat/59/582]).

    Changes in arrangement and phraseology were made.

 __Amendments__ 

    1990—[Pub. L. 101–650][/us/pl/101/650] substituted “, Reno, Ely, and Lovelock” for “and Reno”.
