## § 86. Connecticut

    Connecticut constitutes one judicial district.

    Court shall be held at Bridgeport, Hartford, New Haven, New London, and Waterbury.

([June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], [62 Stat. 875][/us/stat/62/875]; [Pub. L. 87–36][/us/pl/87/36], § 3(b), May 19, 1961, [75 Stat. 83][/us/stat/75/83]; [Pub. L. 89–558][/us/pl/89/558], Sept. 7, 1966, [80 Stat. 705][/us/stat/80/705].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 147 ([Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231][/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231], § 74, [36 Stat. 1108][/us/stat/36/1108]; [Feb. 27, 1921, ch. 74][/us/act/1921-02-27/ch74], [41 Stat. 1146][/us/stat/41/1146]; [June 15, 1933, ch. 80][/us/act/1933-06-15/ch80], [48 Stat. 148][/us/stat/48/148]; [Dec. 28, 1945, ch. 599][/us/act/1945-12-28/ch599], [59 Stat. 663][/us/stat/59/663]).

    Changes in arrangement and phraseology were made.

 __Amendments__ 

    1966—[Pub. L. 89–558][/us/pl/89/558] provided for holding court at New London.

    1961—[Pub. L. 87–36][/us/pl/87/36] provided for holding court at Bridgeport and Waterbury.
