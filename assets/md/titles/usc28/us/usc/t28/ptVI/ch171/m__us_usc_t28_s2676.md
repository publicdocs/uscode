## § 2676. Judgment as bar

    The judgment in an action under [section 1346(b) of this title][/us/usc/t28/s1346/b] shall constitute a complete bar to any action by the claimant, by reason of the same subject matter, against the employee of the government whose act or omission gave rise to the claim.

([June 25, 1948, ch. 646][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646], [62 Stat. 984][/us/stat/62/984].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 931(b) ([Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, § 410(b)][/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/s410/b], [60 Stat. 844][/us/stat/60/844]).

    Section constitutes the first sentence of [section 931(b) of title 28][/us/usc/t28/s931/b], U.S.C., 1940 ed. Other provisions of such section 931(b) are incorporated in [section 2675 of this title][/us/usc/t28/s2675].

    Changes were made in phraseology.

 __Senate Revision Amendment__ 

    This section was eliminated by Senate amendment. See 80th Congress Senate Report No. 1559.
