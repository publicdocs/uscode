 __Senate Revision Amendment__ 

    As printed in this report, this chapter should have read “173” and not “171”. It was properly numbered “173” in the bill. However, the chapter was renumbered “171”, without change in its section numbers, by Senate amendment. See 80th Congress Senate Report No. 1559.

 __Amendments__ 

    1966—[Pub. L. 89–506][/us/pl/89/506], § 9(b), July 18, 1966, [80 Stat. 308][/us/stat/80/308], substituted “claims” for “claims of $2,500 or less” in item 2672.

    1959—[Pub. L. 86–238][/us/pl/86/238], § 1(2), Sept. 8, 1959, [73 Stat. 472][/us/stat/73/472], substituted “$2,500” for “$1,000” in item 2672.
