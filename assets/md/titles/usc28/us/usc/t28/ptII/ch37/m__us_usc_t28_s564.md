## § 564. Powers as sheriff

    United States marshals, deputy marshals and such other officials of the Service as may be designated by the Director, in executing the laws of the United States within a State, may exercise the same powers which a sheriff of the State may exercise in executing the laws thereof.

(Added [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII][/us/pl/100/690/tVII], § 7608(a)(1), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4513][/us/stat/102/4513].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 564, added [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 4(c), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 619][/us/stat/80/619], related to bonds of United States marshals, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 92–310, title II][/us/pl/92/310/tII], § 206(a)(1), June 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 203][/us/stat/86/203].
