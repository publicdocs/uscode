## \[§§ 570, 571. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII, § 7608(a)(1)][/us/pl/100/690/s7608/a/1], Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4512][/us/stat/102/4512]\]

    Section 570, added [Pub. L. 89–554, § 4(c)][/us/pl/89/554/s4/c], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 620][/us/stat/80/620], granted United States marshals the power of a sheriff in executing laws of the United States in a State. See [section 564 of this title][/us/usc/t28/s564].

    Section 571, added [Pub. L. 89–554, § 4(c)][/us/pl/89/554/s4/c], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 621][/us/stat/80/621]; amended [Pub. L. 95–598, title II][/us/pl/95/598], §§ 222, 223, Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2662][/us/stat/92/2662]; [Pub. L. 97–258, § 2(g)(2)][/us/pl/97/258/s2/g/2], Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1060][/us/stat/96/1060], related to disbursement of salaries and moneys.
