### \[CHAPTER 90—OMITTED\]

 __Codification__ 

    Chapter 90, consisting of sections 1471 to 1482, which was added by [Pub. L. 95–598, title II][/us/pl/95/598/tII], § 241(a), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2668][/us/stat/92/2668], and which related to district courts and bankruptcy courts, did not become effective pursuant to [section 402(b) of Pub. L. 95–598][/us/pl/95/598/s402/b], as amended, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 101 of Title 11][/us/usc/t11/s101], Bankruptcy.

 __Transition to New Court System__ 

    [Pub. L. 95–598, title IV][/us/pl/95/598/tIV], § 409, Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2687][/us/stat/92/2687], as amended by [Pub. L. 98–249][/us/pl/98/249], § 1(d), Mar. 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 116][/us/stat/98/116]; [Pub. L. 98–271][/us/pl/98/271], § 1(d), Apr. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 163][/us/stat/98/163]; [Pub. L. 98–299][/us/pl/98/299], § 1(d), May 25, 1984, [98 Stat. 214][/us/stat/98/214]; [Pub. L. 98–325][/us/pl/98/325], § 1(d), June 20, 1984, [98 Stat. 268][/us/stat/98/268]; [Pub. L. 98–353, title I][/us/pl/98/353/tI], § 121(d), July 10, 1984, [98 Stat. 346][/us/stat/98/346], which provided for transfer to the new court system of cases, and matters and proceedings in cases, under the Bankruptcy Act \[former Title 11\] pending at the end of Sept. 30, 1983, in the courts of bankruptcy continued under [section 404(a) of Pub. L. 95–598][/us/pl/95/598/s404/a], with certain exceptions, and cases and proceedings arising under or related to cases under Title 11 pending at the end of July 9, 1984, and directed that civil actions pending on July 9, 1984, over which a bankruptcy court had jurisdiction on July 9, 1984, not abate, but continuation of such actions not finally determined before Apr. 1, 1985, be removed to a bankruptcy court under this chapter, and that all law books, publications, etc., furnished bankruptcy judges as of July 9, 1984, be transferred to the United States bankruptcy courts under the supervision of the Director of the Administrative Office of the United States Courts, was repealed by [Pub. L. 98–353, title I][/us/pl/98/353/tI], § 122(a), July 10, 1984, [98 Stat. 343][/us/stat/98/343], 346, eff. July 10, 1984.
