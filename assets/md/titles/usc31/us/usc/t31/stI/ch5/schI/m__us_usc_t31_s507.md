## § 507. Office of Electronic Government

    The Office of Electronic Government, established under [section 3602 of title 44][/us/usc/t44/s3602], is an office in the Office of Management and Budget.

(Added [Pub. L. 107–347, title I][/us/pl/107/347/tI], § 102(c)(1), Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 2910][/us/stat/116/2910].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective 120 days after Dec. 17, 2002, see [section 402(a)(1) of Pub. L. 107–347][/us/pl/107/347/s402/a/1], set out as a note under [section 3601 of Title 44][/us/usc/t44/s3601], Public Printing and Documents.
