## § 754. Action by the Comptroller General

    When the Comptroller General has authority, the Comptroller General promptly shall carry out action the General Accounting Office Personnel Appeals Board  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See Change of Name note below. </sup></sup>  orders under [section 753 of this title][/us/usc/t31/s753].

([Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 902][/us/stat/96/902].)
