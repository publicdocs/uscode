## § 6105. Oversight responsibility of Director

    The Director shall have oversight responsibility for the exercise of all authorities and responsibilities in this chapter not specifically assigned to the Director.

(Added [Pub. L. 98–169][/us/pl/98/169], § 5, Nov. 29, 1983, [97 Stat. 1115][/us/stat/97/1115].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 6105, [Pub. L. 97–258][/us/pl/97/258], Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1002][/us/stat/96/1002], related to authorization of appropriations to carry out this chapter, prior to repeal by [section 5 of Pub. L. 98–169][/us/pl/98/169/s5]. See [section 6106 of this title][/us/usc/t31/s6106].
