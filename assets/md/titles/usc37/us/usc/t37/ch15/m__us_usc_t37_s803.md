## § 803. Commissioned officers of Army or Air Force: forfeiture of pay when dropped from rolls

    A commissioned officer of the Army or the Air Force who is dropped from the rolls under [section 1161(b) of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s1161/b] for absence without authority for three months forfeits all pay due or to become due.

([Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649], Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 486][/us/stat/76/486].)
