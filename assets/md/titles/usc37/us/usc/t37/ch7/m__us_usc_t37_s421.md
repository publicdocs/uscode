## § 421. Allowances: no increase while dependent is entitled to basic pay

    A member of a uniformed service may not be paid an increased allowance under this chapter, on account of a dependent, for any period during which that dependent is entitled to basic pay under [section 204 of this title][/us/usc/t37/s204].

([Pub. L. 87–649][/us/pl/87/649], Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 479][/us/stat/76/479], § 420; renumbered § 421, [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title VI, § 611(a)(1)(A)][/us/pl/100/180/s611/a/1/A], Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1093][/us/stat/101/1093].)
