## § 2231. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–394, title IV][/us/pl/105/394/tIV], § 401, Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3661][/us/stat/112/3661]

    Section, [Pub. L. 100–407, title II][/us/pl/100/407/tII], § 201, as added [Pub. L. 103–218, title II][/us/pl/103/218/tII], § 201, Mar. 9, 1994, [108 Stat. 85][/us/stat/108/85], related to classification system for assistive technology devices and assistive technology services.

    A prior section 2231, [Pub. L. 100–407, title II][/us/pl/100/407/tII], § 201, Aug. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 1059][/us/stat/102/1059], directed National Council on the Handicapped to study implementation, acquisition or financing assistive technology devices and services for individuals with disabilities, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–218][/us/pl/103/218], § 201.
