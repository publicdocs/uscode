## §§ 3011 to 3015. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections, comprising subchapter I of this chapter “State Grant Programs”, were omitted in the general amendment of this chapter by [Pub. L. 108–364][/us/pl/108/364], § 2, Oct. 25, 2004, [118 Stat. 1707][/us/stat/118/1707].

    Section 3011, [Pub. L. 105–394, title I][/us/pl/105/394/tI], § 101, Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3635][/us/stat/112/3635], provided for continuity grants for States that received funding for a limited period for technology-related assistance.

    Section 3012, [Pub. L. 105–394, title I][/us/pl/105/394/tI], § 102, Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3644][/us/stat/112/3644]; [Pub. L. 106–402, title IV][/us/pl/106/402/tIV], § 401(b)(4)(B), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1738][/us/stat/114/1738], provided for State grants for protection and advocacy related to assistive technology.

    Section 3013, [Pub. L. 105–394, title I][/us/pl/105/394/tI], § 103, Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3646][/us/stat/112/3646], related to administrative provisions.

    Section 3014, [Pub. L. 105–394, title I][/us/pl/105/394/tI], § 104, Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3648][/us/stat/112/3648], related to a technical assistance program.

    Section 3015, [Pub. L. 105–394, title I][/us/pl/105/394/tI], § 105, Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3651][/us/stat/112/3651], authorized appropriations.
