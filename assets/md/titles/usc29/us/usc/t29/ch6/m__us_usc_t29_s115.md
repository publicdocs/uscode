## § 115. Repeal of conflicting acts

    All acts and parts of acts in conflict with the provisions of this chapter are repealed.

([Mar. 23, 1932, ch. 90][/us/act/1932-03-23/ch90], § 15, [47 Stat. 73][/us/stat/47/73].)
