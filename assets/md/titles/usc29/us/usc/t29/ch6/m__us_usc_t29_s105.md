## § 105. Doing in concert of certain acts as constituting unlawful combination or conspiracy subjecting person to injunctive remedies

    No court of the United States shall have jurisdiction to issue a restraining order or temporary or permanent injunction upon the ground that any of the persons participating or interested in a labor dispute constitute or are engaged in an unlawful combination or conspiracy because of the doing in concert of the acts enumerated in [section 104 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s104].

([Mar. 23, 1932, ch. 90][/us/act/1932-03-23/ch90], § 5, [47 Stat. 71][/us/stat/47/71].)
