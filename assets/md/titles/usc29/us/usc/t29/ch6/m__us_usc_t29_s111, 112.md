## §§ 111, 112. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21], [62 Stat. 862][/us/stat/62/862], eff. Sept. 1, 1948

    Section 111, [act Mar. 23, 1932, ch. 90, § 11][/us/act/1932-03-23/ch90/s11], [47 Stat. 72][/us/stat/47/72], related to contempts, speedy and public trial, and jury. See [section 3692 of Title 18][/us/usc/t18/s3692], Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

    Section 112, [act Mar. 23, 1932, ch. 90, § 12][/us/act/1932-03-23/ch90/s12], [47 Stat. 73][/us/stat/47/73], related to contempts and demand for retirement of sitting judge. See rule 42 of the Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, set out in the Appendix to Title 18.
