## § 2883. Establishment

    There shall be within the Department of Labor a “Job Corps”.

([Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 143][/us/pl/105/220/s143], Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1007][/us/stat/112/1007].)
