#### Part E—Adult and Dislocated Worker Employment and Training Activities

§§ 2861 to 2864. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 113–128, title V, § 511(a)__  __][/us/pl/113/128/s511/a]__  __,__  __July 22, 2014__  __,__  __[__  __128 Stat. 1705__  __][/us/stat/128/1705]__

    Section 2862, [Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 132][/us/pl/105/220/s132], Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 983][/us/stat/112/983]; [Pub. L. 107–210, div.     Section 2864, [Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 134][/us/pl/105/220/s134], Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 990][/us/stat/112/990]; [Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(f)][/us/pl/105/277/s101/f] \[title VIII, § 401(5)\], Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–337][/us/stat/112/2681-337], 2681–411; [Pub. L. 109–270, § 2(h)(6)][/us/pl/109/270/s2/h/6], Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 748][/us/stat/120/748], related to Use of funds for employment and training activities. See [section 3174 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s3174].
