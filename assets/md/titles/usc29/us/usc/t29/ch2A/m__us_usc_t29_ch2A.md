## CHAPTER 2A—CHILDREN’S BUREAU

§§ 18 to 18c. __Transferred__ 

 __Codification__ 

    Section 18, acts [Apr. 9, 1912, ch. 73, § 1][/us/act/1912-04-09/ch73/s1], [37 Stat. 79][/us/stat/37/79]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 3][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s3], [37 Stat. 737][/us/stat/37/737], which established a Children’s Bureau in Department of Labor, was transferred to [section 191 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s191], The Public Health and Welfare.

    Section 18a, acts [Apr. 9, 1912, ch. 73, § 2][/us/act/1912-04-09/ch73/s2], [37 Stat. 79][/us/stat/37/79]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], §§ 3, 6, [37 Stat. 737][/us/stat/37/737], 738; [Feb. 27, 1925, ch. 364][/us/act/1925-02-27/ch364], title IV, [43 Stat. 1050][/us/stat/43/1050], which created office of chief of Children’s Bureau, and enumerated powers and duties of said Bureau, was transferred to [section 192 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s192].

    Section 18b, acts [Apr. 9, 1912, ch. 73, § 3][/us/act/1912-04-09/ch73/s3], [37 Stat. 80][/us/stat/37/80]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], §§ 3, 6, [37 Stat. 737][/us/stat/37/737], 738, which created office of Assistant Chief of Children’s Bureau, was transferred to [section 193 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s193].

    Section 18c, acts [Apr. 9, 1912, ch. 73, § 4][/us/act/1912-04-09/ch73/s4], [37 Stat. 80][/us/stat/37/80]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 3][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s3], [37 Stat. 737][/us/stat/37/737], which related to quarters for Children’s Bureau, was transferred to [section 194 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s194].
