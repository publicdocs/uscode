## CHAPTER 17—COMPREHENSIVE EMPLOYMENT AND TRAINING PROGRAMS

 __Codification__ 

    The Comprehensive Employment and Training Act of 1973, [Pub. L. 93–203][/us/pl/93/203], Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 839][/us/stat/87/839], as amended by [Pub. L. 93–567][/us/pl/93/567], Dec. 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 1845][/us/stat/88/1845]; [Pub. L. 94–444][/us/pl/94/444], Oct. 1, 1976, [90 Stat. 1476][/us/stat/90/1476]; [Pub. L. 94–482][/us/pl/94/482], Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2081][/us/stat/90/2081]; [Pub. L. 95–40][/us/pl/95/40], June 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 203][/us/stat/91/203]; [Pub. L. 95–44][/us/pl/95/44], June 15, 1977, [91 Stat. 220][/us/stat/91/220]; [Pub. L. 95–93][/us/pl/95/93], Aug. 5, 1977, [91 Stat. 627][/us/stat/91/627], comprised this chapter prior to its complete revision by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524], Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1909][/us/stat/92/1909]. The Act, [Pub. L. 93–203][/us/pl/93/203], as amended generally by [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2][/us/pl/95/524/s2], Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1909][/us/stat/92/1909], was known as the Comprehensive Employment and Training Act, and was set out as having been added by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524] without reference to the intervening amendments in view of the extensive revision of the Act’s provisions by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524].

§§ 801, 802. __Repealed.__  __[__  __Pub. L. 97–300, title I, § 184(a)(1)__  __][/us/pl/97/300/s184/a/1]__  __,__  __Oct. 13, 1982__  __,__  __[__  __96 Stat. 1357__  __][/us/stat/96/1357]__ 

    Section 801, [Pub. L. 93–203, § 2][/us/pl/93/203/s2], as added [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2][/us/pl/95/524/s2], Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1912][/us/stat/92/1912], set out Congressional statement of purpose in enacting this chapter.

    A prior section 801, [Pub. L. 93–203, § 2][/us/pl/93/203/s2], Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 839][/us/stat/87/839], provided for a Congressional statement of purpose for this chapter, prior to the general revision of [Pub. L. 93–203][/us/pl/93/203] by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524].

    Section 802, [Pub. L. 93–203, § 3][/us/pl/93/203/s3], as added [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2][/us/pl/95/524/s2], Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1912][/us/stat/92/1912], provided definitions for this chapter.

    A prior section 802, [Pub. L. 93–203, § 4][/us/pl/93/203/s4], Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 839][/us/stat/87/839]; [Pub. L. 95–44, § 2(a)][/us/pl/95/44/s2/a], June 15, 1977, [91 Stat. 220][/us/stat/91/220]; [Pub. L. 95–93, title III, § 302][/us/pl/95/93/s302], Aug. 5, 1977, [91 Stat. 650][/us/stat/91/650], authorized appropriations for this chapter, prior to the general revision of [Pub. L. 93–203][/us/pl/93/203] by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524].

    A prior [section 3 of Pub. L. 93–203][/us/pl/93/203/s3], Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 839][/us/stat/87/839], provided for transitional provisions and was set out as a note under [section 801 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s801], prior to the general revision of [Pub. L. 93–203][/us/pl/93/203] by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524].

    Provisions similar to those comprising this section were contained in former [section 981 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s981] prior to the general revision of [Pub. L. 93–203][/us/pl/93/203] by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524].

    A prior section 803, [Pub. L. 95–93, title III, § 305][/us/pl/95/93/s305], Aug. 5, 1977, [91 Stat. 651][/us/stat/91/651], providing for increased participation of veterans in public service employment programs and job training opportunities, was omitted because it was limited to fiscal years 1977 and 1978.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    [Pub. L. 97–300, title I, § 184(a)][/us/pl/97/300/s184/a], Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1357][/us/stat/96/1357], which provided for the repeal of the Comprehensive Employment and Training Act, this chapter, effective Oct. 13, 1982, was itself repealed by [Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 199(b)(2)][/us/pl/105/220/s199/b/2], (c)(2)(B), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1059][/us/stat/112/1059], eff. July 1, 2000.
