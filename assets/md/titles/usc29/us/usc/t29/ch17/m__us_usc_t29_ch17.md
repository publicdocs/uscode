## CHAPTER 17—COMPREHENSIVE EMPLOYMENT AND TRAINING PROGRAMS

 __Codification__ 

    The Comprehensive Employment and Training Act of 1973, [Pub. L. 93–203][/us/pl/93/203], Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 839][/us/stat/87/839], as amended by [Pub. L. 93–567][/us/pl/93/567], Dec. 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 1845][/us/stat/88/1845]; [Pub. L. 94–444][/us/pl/94/444], Oct. 1, 1976, [90 Stat. 1476][/us/stat/90/1476]; [Pub. L. 94–482][/us/pl/94/482], Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2081][/us/stat/90/2081]; [Pub. L. 95–40][/us/pl/95/40], June 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 203][/us/stat/91/203]; [Pub. L. 95–44][/us/pl/95/44], June 15, 1977, [91 Stat. 220][/us/stat/91/220]; [Pub. L. 95–93][/us/pl/95/93], Aug. 5, 1977, [91 Stat. 627][/us/stat/91/627], comprised this chapter prior to its complete revision by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524], Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1909][/us/stat/92/1909]. The Act, [Pub. L. 93–203][/us/pl/93/203], as amended generally by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524], § 2, Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1909][/us/stat/92/1909], was known as the Comprehensive Employment and Training Act, and was set out as having been added by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524] without reference to the intervening amendments in view of the extensive revision of the Act’s provisions by [Pub. L. 95–524][/us/pl/95/524].
