## §§ 1791i, 1791j. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 1791i and 1791j of this title were omitted in the general revision of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 102–367, title V, § 501][/us/pl/102/367/s501], title VII, § 701(a), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1099][/us/stat/106/1099], 1103, effective July 1, 1993.

    Section 1791i, [Pub. L. 97–300, title V, § 510][/us/pl/97/300/s510], as added [Pub. L. 100–628, title VII, § 712(a)(3)][/us/pl/100/628/s712/a/3], Nov. 7, 1988, [102 Stat. 3253][/us/stat/102/3253], related to awards to States for start-up costs for participation in the incentive bonus program.

    Section 1791j, [Pub. L. 97–300, title V, § 511][/us/pl/97/300/s511], as added [Pub. L. 100–628, title VII, § 712(a)(3)][/us/pl/100/628/s712/a/3], Nov. 7, 1988, [102 Stat. 3254][/us/stat/102/3254], related to evaluation and performance standards for the incentive bonus program.
