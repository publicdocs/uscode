## § 1737. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–220, title I][/us/pl/105/220/tI], § 199(b)(2), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1059][/us/stat/112/1059]

    Section, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV][/us/pl/97/300/tIV], § 456, formerly § 457, as added [Pub. L. 102–235][/us/pl/102/235], § 9, Dec. 12, 1991, [105 Stat. 1809][/us/stat/105/1809]; renumbered § 456 and amended [Pub. L. 102–367, title IV][/us/pl/102/367/tIV], § 403(a)(5), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1084][/us/stat/106/1084], related to nontraditional employment demonstration program.

    A prior [section 456 of Pub. L. 97–300][/us/pl/97/300/s456] was classified to [section 1736 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s1736], prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–367, title IV][/us/pl/102/367/tIV], § 403(a)(4), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1084][/us/stat/106/1084].

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective July 1, 2000, see [section 199(c)(2)(B) of Pub. L. 105–220][/us/pl/105/220/s199/c/2/B], set out as a note under [section 1501 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s1501].
