## § 554. Assistants to Secretary

    There shall be in the Department of Labor not more than two assistants to the Secretary, who shall be appointed by the President and shall perform such duties as may be prescribed by the Secretary of Labor or required by law.

([Mar. 4, 1927, ch. 498][/us/act/1927-03-04/ch498], [44 Stat. 1415][/us/stat/44/1415].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section was formerly classified to [section 613a of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s613a] prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], § 1, Sept. 1, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    For transfer of functions of other officers, employees, and agencies of Department of Labor, with certain exceptions, to Secretary of Labor, with power to delegate, see Reorg. Plan No. 6, of 1950, §§ 1, 2, 15 F.R. 3174, [64 Stat. 1263][/us/stat/64/1263], set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
