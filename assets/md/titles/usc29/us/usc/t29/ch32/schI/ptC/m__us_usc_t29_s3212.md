## § 3212. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part—

    (1) $1,688,155,000 for fiscal year 2015;

    (2) $1,818,548,000 for fiscal year 2016;

    (3) $1,856,283,000 for fiscal year 2017;

    (4) $1,897,455,000 for fiscal year 2018;

    (5) $1,942,064,000 for fiscal year 2019; and

    (6) $1,983,236,000 for fiscal year 2020.

([Pub. L. 113–128, title I, § 162][/us/pl/113/128/s162], July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1559][/us/stat/128/1559].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective on the first day of the first full program year after July 22, 2014 (July 1, 2015), see [section 506 of Pub. L. 113–128][/us/pl/113/128/s506], set out as a note under [section 3101 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s3101].
