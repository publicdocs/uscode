## § 795m. Savings provision

    (a) __Supported employment services__ 

        Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to prohibit a State from providing supported employment services in accordance with the State plan submitted under [section 721 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s721] by using funds made available through a State allotment under [section 730 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s730].

    (b) __Postemployment services__ 

        Nothing in this part shall be construed to prohibit a State from providing discrete postemployment services in accordance with the State plan submitted under [section 721 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s721] by using funds made available through a State allotment under [section 730 of this title][/us/usc/t29/s730] to an individual who is eligible under this part.

([Pub. L. 93–112, title VI, § 627][/us/pl/93/112/s627], as added [Pub. L. 105–220, title IV, § 409][/us/pl/105/220/s409], Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1216][/us/stat/112/1216]; amended [Pub.
