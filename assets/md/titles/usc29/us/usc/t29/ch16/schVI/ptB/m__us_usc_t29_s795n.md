## § 795n. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 1999 through 2003.

([Pub. L. 93–112, title VI, § 628][/us/pl/93/112/s628], as added [Pub. L. 105–220, title IV, § 409][/us/pl/105/220/s409], Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1217][/us/stat/112/1217]; amended [Pub.
