 __Codification__ 

    Title VII of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, comprising this subchapter, was originally added to [Pub. L. 93–112][/us/pl/93/112] by [Pub. L. 102–569, title VII][/us/pl/102/569/tVII], § 701(2), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4443][/us/stat/106/4443], and amended by [Pub. L. 103–73][/us/pl/103/73], Aug. 11, 1993, [107 Stat. 718][/us/stat/107/718]. Title VII is shown herein, however, as having been added by [Pub. L. 105–220, title IV][/us/pl/105/220/tIV], § 410, Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1217][/us/stat/112/1217], without reference to those intervening amendments because of the extensive revision of title VII by [Pub. L. 105–220][/us/pl/105/220].
