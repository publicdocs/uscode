## § 796l. Authorization of appropriations

    There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1999 through 2003.

([Pub. L. 93–112, title VII, § 753][/us/pl/93/112/s753], as added [Pub. L. 105–220, title IV, § 410][/us/pl/105/220/s410], Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1241][/us/stat/112/1241].)
