## § 766. Definition of covered school

    In this subchapter, the term “covered school” means an elementary school or secondary school (as such terms are defined in [section 7801 of title 20][/us/usc/t20/s7801]) or an institution of higher education.

([Pub. L. 93–112, title II][/us/pl/93/112/tII], § 206, as added [Pub. L. 113–128, title IV][/us/pl/113/128/tIV], § 437, July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1671][/us/stat/128/1671]; amended [Pub. L. 114–95, title IX][/us/pl/114/95/tIX], § 9215(mmm)(2), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2188][/us/stat/129/2188].)
