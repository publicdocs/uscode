## § 100102. Definitions

    In this title:

    (1)  __Director__  __.—__ 

    The term “Director” means the Director of the National Park Service.

    (2)  __National park system__  __.—__ 

    The term “National Park System” means the areas of land and water described in [section 100501 of this title][/us/usc/t54/s100501].

    (3)  __Secretary__  __.—__ 

    The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

    (4)  __Service__  __.—__ 

    The term “Service” means the National Park Service.

    (5)  __System__  __.—__ 

    The term “System” means the National Park System.

    (6)  __System unit__  __.—__ 

    The term “System unit” means one of the areas described in [section 100501 of this title][/us/usc/t54/s100501].

([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3][/us/pl/113/287/s3], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3096][/us/stat/128/3096].)
