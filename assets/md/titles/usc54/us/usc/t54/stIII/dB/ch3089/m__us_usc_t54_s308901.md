## § 308901. Definitions

    In this chapter:

    (1)  __Collection__  __.—__ 

    The term “collection” means a collection of intellectual and cultural artifacts, including documents, sculpture, and works of art.

    (2)  __Eligible entity__  __.—__ 

    The term “eligible entity” means a Federal entity, State, local, or tribal government, educational institution, or nonprofit organization.

    (3)  __Historic property__  __.—__ 

    The term “historic property” has the meaning given the term in [section 300308 of this title][/us/usc/t54/s300308].

    (4)  __Nationally significant__  __.—__ 

    The term “nationally significant”, in reference to a collection or historic property, means a collection or historic property that meets the applicable criteria for national significance, in accordance with regulations promulgated by the Secretary pursuant to [section 302103 of this title][/us/usc/t54/s302103].

    (5)  __Program__  __.—__ 

    The term “program” means the Save America’s Treasures Program established under [section 308902(a) of this title][/us/usc/t54/s308902/a].

    (6)  __Secretary__  __.—__ 

    The term “Secretary” means the Secretary, acting through the Director.

([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3][/us/pl/113/287/s3], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3243][/us/stat/128/3243].)
