## § 300306. Historic Preservation Fund

    In this division, the term “Historic Preservation Fund” means the Historic Preservation Fund established under [section 303101 of this title][/us/usc/t54/s303101].

([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3][/us/pl/113/287/s3], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3189][/us/stat/128/3189].)
