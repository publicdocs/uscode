## § 300301. Agency

    In this division, the term “agency” has the meaning given the term in [section 551 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s551].

([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3][/us/pl/113/287/s3], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3188][/us/stat/128/3188].)
