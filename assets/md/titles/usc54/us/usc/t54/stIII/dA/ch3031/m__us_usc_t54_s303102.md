## § 303102. Contents

    For each of fiscal years 2012 to 2015, $150,000,000 shall be deposited in the Historic Preservation Fund from revenues due and payable to the United States under section 9 of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act ([43 U.S.C. 1338][/us/usc/t43/s1338]), [section 7433(b) of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s7433/b], or both, notwithstanding any provision of law that those proceeds shall be credited to miscellaneous receipts of the Treasury.

([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3][/us/pl/113/287/s3], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3206][/us/stat/128/3206].)
