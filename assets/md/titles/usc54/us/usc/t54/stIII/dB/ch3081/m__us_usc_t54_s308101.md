## § 308101. Definition

    In this chapter, the term “Secretary” means the Secretary, acting through the American Battlefield Protection Program.

([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3][/us/pl/113/287/s3], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3233][/us/stat/128/3233].)
