## § 320105. Criminal penalties

    Criminal penalties for a violation of a regulation authorized by this chapter are provided by [section 1866 of title 18][/us/usc/t18/s1866].

([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3][/us/pl/113/287/s3], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3259][/us/stat/128/3259].)
