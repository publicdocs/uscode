## § 312301. Definition

    In this chapter, the term “Commission” means the Commission for the Preservation of America’s Heritage Abroad established under [section 312303 of this title][/us/usc/t54/s312303].

([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3][/us/pl/113/287/s3], Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3252][/us/stat/128/3252].)
