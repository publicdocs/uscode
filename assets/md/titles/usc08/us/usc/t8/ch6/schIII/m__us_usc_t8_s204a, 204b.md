## §§ 204a, 204b. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections related to natives of Virgin Islands residing in foreign countries on June 22, 1932, and were based on [act June 28, 1932, ch. 283][/us/act/1932-06-28/ch283], §§ 1, 2, [47 Stat. 336][/us/stat/47/336]. Former [section 204b of this title][/us/usc/t8/s204b] provided that section 204a should not apply after June 28, 1934.
