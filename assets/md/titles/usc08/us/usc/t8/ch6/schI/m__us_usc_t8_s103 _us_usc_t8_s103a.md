###### §§ 103, 103a. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section 103, acts [Mar. 2, 1895, ch. 177][/us/act/1895-03-02/ch177], § 1, [28 Stat. 780][/us/stat/28/780]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141], § 3, [37 Stat. 737][/us/stat/37/737]; Ex. Ord. No. 6166, § 14, June 10, 1933; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. V, eff. June 14, 1940, 5 F.R. 2423, [54 Stat. 1238][/us/stat/54/1238], which related to administration of alien contract laws, was transferred to section 342h of former Title 5, Executive Departments and Government Officers and Employees, and subsequently eliminated from the Code on enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378]. See [section 1103 of this title][/us/usc/t8/s1103].

Section 103a, [act July 9, 1947, ch. 211, title II][/us/act/1947-07-09/ch211/tII], [61 Stat. 292][/us/stat/61/292], which related to reimbursement by Attorney General of certain expenses incurred by other agencies in connection with administration and enforcement of laws relating to immigration, etc., was from the Department of Justice Appropriation Act, 1948, and was not repeated in the Department of Justice Appropriation Act, 1949, [act June 3, 1948, ch. 400, title II][/us/act/1948-06-03/ch400/tII], [62 Stat. 316][/us/stat/62/316]. Similar provisions were contained in the following prior appropriation acts:

    [July 5, 1946, ch. 541][/us/act/1946-07-05/ch541], title II, [60 Stat. 462][/us/stat/60/462].

    [May 21, 1945, ch. 129][/us/act/1945-05-21/ch129], title II, [59 Stat. 185][/us/stat/59/185].

    [June 28, 1944, ch. 294][/us/act/1944-06-28/ch294], title II, [58 Stat. 412][/us/stat/58/412].

    [July 1, 1943, ch. 182][/us/act/1943-07-01/ch182], title II, [57 Stat. 288][/us/stat/57/288].

    [July 2, 1942, ch. 472][/us/act/1942-07-02/ch472], title II, [56 Stat. 483][/us/stat/56/483].

    [June 28, 1941, ch. 258][/us/act/1941-06-28/ch258], title III, [55 Stat. 292][/us/stat/55/292].
