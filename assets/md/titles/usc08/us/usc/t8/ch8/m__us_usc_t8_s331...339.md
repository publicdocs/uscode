## §§ 331 to 339. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–461][/us/pl/93/461], Oct. 20, 1974, [88 Stat. 1387][/us/stat/88/1387]

    Section 331, R.S. § 2158, prohibited cooly trade.

    Section 332, R.S. § 2159; [Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231][/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231], § 291, [36 Stat. 1167][/us/stat/36/1167], related to forfeiture of vessels engaged in cooly trade.

    Section 333, R.S. § 2160, related to penalty for building vessels to engage in cooly trade.

    Section 334, R.S. § 2161, related to punishment for engaging in cooly trade.

    Section 335, R.S. § 2162, excepted voluntary emigration of coolies from prohibition.

    Section 336, [act Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 141][/us/act/1875-03-03/ch141], § 1, [18 Stat. 477][/us/stat/18/477], related to inquiry and certification by consular officers.

    Section 337, R.S. § 2163, related to examination of vessels.

    Section 338, [act Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 141][/us/act/1875-03-03/ch141], § 2, [18 Stat. 477][/us/stat/18/477], related to penalties for involuntary transportation of Chinese, Japanese, and others for purpose of holding to service.

    Section 339, [act Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 141][/us/act/1875-03-03/ch141], § 4, [18 Stat. 477][/us/stat/18/477], related to punishment for contracting to supply cooly labor.
