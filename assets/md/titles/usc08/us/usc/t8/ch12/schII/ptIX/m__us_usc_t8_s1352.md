## § 1352. Printing of reentry permits and blank forms of manifest and crew lists; sale to public

    (a) Reentry permits issued under [section 1203 of this title][/us/usc/t8/s1203] shall be printed on distinctive safety paper and shall be prepared and issued under regulations prescribed by the Attorney General.

    (b) The Public Printer is authorized to print for sale to the public by the Superintendent of Documents, upon prepayment, copies of blank forms of manifests and crew lists and such other forms as may be prescribed and authorized by the Attorney General to be sold pursuant to the provisions of this subchapter.

([June 27, 1952, ch. 477][/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477], title II, ch. 9, § 282, [66 Stat. 231][/us/stat/66/231].)

 __Abolition of Immigration and Naturalization Service and Transfer of Functions__ 

    For abolition of Immigration and Naturalization Service, transfer of functions, and treatment of related references, see note set out under [section 1551 of this title][/us/usc/t8/s1551].
