## § 1363b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–277, div. A][/us/pl/105/277/dA], § 101(b) \[title I, § 109(b)\], Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–50][/us/stat/112/2681-50], 2681–67

    Section, [act June 27, 1952, ch. 477, title II][/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477/tII], ch. 9, § 295, as added [Pub. L. 104–208, div. C, title VI][/us/pl/104/208/dC/tVI], § 626(a), Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3009–700][/us/stat/110/3009-700], related to transportation of remains of immigration officers and border patrol agents killed in the line of duty. [Pub. L. 105–277][/us/pl/105/277], which directed the repeal of section 626 of the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant Responsibility Act of 1996, which is [section 626 of Pub. L. 104–208][/us/pl/104/208/s626], div. C, title VI, Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3009–700][/us/stat/110/3009-700], was executed by repealing this section, which was section 295 of the Immigration and Nationality Act and was enacted by [section 626(a) of Pub. L. 104–208][/us/pl/104/208/s626/a], to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
