## § 1405. Persons born in Hawaii

    A person born in Hawaii on or after August 12, 1898, and before April 30, 1900, is declared to be a citizen of the United States as of April 30, 1900. A person born in Hawaii on or after April 30, 1900, is a citizen of the United States at birth. A person who was a citizen of the Republic of Hawaii on August 12, 1898, is declared to be a citizen of the United States as of April 30, 1900.

([June 27, 1952, ch. 477][/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477], title III, ch. 1, § 305, [66 Stat. 237][/us/stat/66/237].)

 __Admission of Hawaii as State__ 

    Hawaii Statehood provisions as not repealing, amending, or modifying the provisions of this section, see [section 20 of Pub. L. 86–3][/us/pl/86/3/s20], Mar. 18, 1959, [73 Stat. 13][/us/stat/73/13], set out as a note at the beginning of chapter 3 of Title 48, Territories and Insular Possessions.
