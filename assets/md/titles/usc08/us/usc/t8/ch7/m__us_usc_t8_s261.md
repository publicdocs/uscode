## § 261. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Section, acts [Feb. 14, 1903, ch. 552, § 7][/us/act/1903-02-14/ch552/s7], [32 Stat. 828][/us/stat/32/828]; [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 3][/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s3], [37 Stat. 737][/us/stat/37/737]; Ex. Ord. No. 6166, § 14, June 10, 1933; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. V, eff. June 14, 1940, 5 F.R. 2423, [54 Stat. 1238][/us/stat/54/1238], conferred upon the Attorney General the authority, power, and jurisdiction by virtue of any law relating to the exclusion from and residence within the United States, its Territories and the District of Columbia, of Chinese and persons of Chinese descent, and vested in the collectors of customs and collectors of internal revenue, under control of the Commissioner of Immigration and Naturalization, as the Attorney General might designate therefor, the authority, power, and jurisdiction in relation to such exclusion previously vested in such officers. It was omitted as obsolete in view of the repeal, by [act Dec. 17, 1943, ch. 344, § 1][/us/act/1943-12-17/ch344/s1], [57 Stat. 600][/us/stat/57/600], of sections 262 to 297 and 299 of this title.
