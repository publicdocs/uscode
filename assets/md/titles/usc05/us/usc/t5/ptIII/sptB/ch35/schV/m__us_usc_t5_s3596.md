## § 3596. Regulations

    The Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe regulations to carry out the purpose of this subchapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 95–454, title IV][/us/pl/95/454/tIV], § 404(b), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1167][/us/stat/92/1167], § 3595; renumbered § 3596, [Pub. L. 97–35, title XVII][/us/pl/97/35/tXVII], § 1704(a)(1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 756][/us/stat/95/756].)

 __Amendments__
