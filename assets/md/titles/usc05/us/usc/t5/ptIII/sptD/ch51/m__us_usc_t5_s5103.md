## § 5103. Determination of applicability

    The Office of Personnel Management shall determine finally the applicability of [section 5102 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s5102] to specific positions and employees, except for positions and employees in the Office of the Architect of the Capitol.

([Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 446][/us/stat/80/446]; [Pub. L. 95–454, title IX][/us/pl/95/454/tIX], § 906(a)(2), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1224][/us/stat/92/1224].)
