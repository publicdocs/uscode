## § 4315. Regulations

    The Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe regulations to carry out the purpose of this subchapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 95–454, title IV][/us/pl/95/454/tIV], § 405(a), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1170][/us/stat/92/1170].)
