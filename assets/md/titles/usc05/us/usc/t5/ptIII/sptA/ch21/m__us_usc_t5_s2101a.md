## § 2101a. The Senior Executive Service

    The “Senior Executive Service” consists of Senior Executive Service positions (as defined in [section 3132(a)(2) of this title][/us/usc/t5/s3132/a/2]).

(Added [Pub. L. 95–454, title IV][/us/pl/95/454/tIV], § 401(a), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1154][/us/stat/92/1154].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective 9 months after Oct. 13, 1978, and congressional review of provisions of sections 401 through 412 of [Pub. L. 95–454][/us/pl/95/454], see [section 415 of Pub. L. 95–454][/us/pl/95/454/s415], set out as a note under [section 3131 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s3131].
