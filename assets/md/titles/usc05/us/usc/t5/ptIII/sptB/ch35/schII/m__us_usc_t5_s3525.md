## § 3525. Regulations

    The Office of Personnel Management may prescribe regulations to carry out this subchapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 107–296, title XIII, § 1313(a)(1)(A)][/us/pl/107/296/s1313/a/1/A], Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2294][/us/stat/116/2294].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 3551, [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 429][/us/stat/80/429]; [Pub. L. 90–491, § 2][/us/pl/90/491/s2], Aug. 17, 1968, [82 Stat. 791][/us/stat/82/791], related to restoration of positions of Federal and District of Columbia employees upon release from duty in Reserves or National Guard, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–353][/us/pl/103/353], §§ 2(b)(2)(B), 8, Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3169][/us/stat/108/3169], 3175, effective with respect to reemployments initiated on or after first day after 60-day period beginning Oct. 13, 1994, with transition rules.
