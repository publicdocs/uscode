## § 5507. Officer affidavit; condition to pay

    An officer required by [section 3332 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s3332] to file an affidavit may not be paid until the affidavit has been filed.

([Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 477][/us/stat/80/477].)
