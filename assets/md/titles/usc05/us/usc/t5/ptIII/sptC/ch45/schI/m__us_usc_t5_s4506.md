## § 4506. Regulations

    The Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe regulations and instructions under which the awards programs set forth by this subchapter shall be carried out.

([Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 443][/us/stat/80/443]; [Pub. L. 95–454, title V, § 503(d)][/us/pl/95/454/s503/d], Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1184][/us/stat/92/1184]; [Pub.
