## § 4801. Nonapplicability of chapter 47

    Chapter 47 shall not apply to this chapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 107–123, § 8(a)][/us/pl/107/123/s8/a], Jan. 16, 2002, [115 Stat. 2398][/us/stat/115/2398].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Chapter effective Oct. 1, 2001, see [section 11 of Pub. L. 107–123][/us/pl/107/123/s11], set out as an Effective Date of 2002 Amendment note under [section 78ee of Title 15][/us/usc/t15/s78ee], Commerce and Trade.
