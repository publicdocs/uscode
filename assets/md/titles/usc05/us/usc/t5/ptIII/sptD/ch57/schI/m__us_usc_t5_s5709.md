## § 5709. Air evacuation patients: furnished subsistence

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, and under regulations prescribed under [section 5707 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s5707], an employee and his dependents may be furnished subsistence without charge while being evacuated as a patient by military aircraft of the United States.

(Added [Pub. L. 91–481][/us/pl/91/481], § 1(1), Oct. 21, 1970, [84 Stat. 1081][/us/stat/84/1081].)
