## \[§ 5380. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–378, § 8(a)][/us/pl/102/378/s8/a], Oct. 2, 1992, [106 Stat. 1359][/us/stat/106/1359]\]

    Section, added [Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title XII, § 1206(i)(1)][/us/pl/101/510/s1206/i/1], Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1662][/us/stat/104/1662], related to pay authority for critical positions. See [section 5377 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s5377]. [Pub. L. 102–378, § 8(a)][/us/pl/102/378/s8/a], repealed [Pub. L. 101–510, § 1206(i)(1)][/us/pl/101/510/s1206/i/1], and provided that this title shall read as if section 1206(i)(1) had not been enacted.

    [Pub. L. 101–510, § 1206(i)(3)][/us/pl/101/510/s1206/i/3], provided that (A) unless [section 5380 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s5380] did not take effect as provided in subpar. (B), such section would cease to be in effect on the earlier of Oct. 1, 1992, or the date of the enactment of the Federal Employees Pay Comparability Act of 1990 \[Nov. 5, 1990\], and (B) [section 5380 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s5380] would not take effect if the Federal Employees Pay Comparability Act of 1990 \[[Pub. L. 101–509][/us/pl/101/509]\] was enacted before the date of the enactment of this Act \[Nov. 5, 1990\]. [Pub. L. 102–378, § 8(a)][/us/pl/102/378/s8/a], repealed [Pub. L. 101–510, § 1206(i)(3)][/us/pl/101/510/s1206/i/3], and provided that this title shall read as if section 1206(i)(3) had not been enacted.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Nov. 5, 1990, see [section 9(b)(6) of Pub. L. 102–378][/us/pl/102/378/s9/b/6], set out as an Effective Date of 1992 Amendment note under [section 6303 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s6303].
