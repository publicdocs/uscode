## § 4311. Definitions

    For the purpose of this subchapter, “agency”, “senior executive”, and “career appointee” have the meanings set forth in [section 3132(a) of this title][/us/usc/t5/s3132/a].

(Added [Pub. L. 95–454, title IV][/us/pl/95/454/tIV], § 405(a), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1167][/us/stat/92/1167].)

 __Effective Date__ 

    Subchapter effective 9 months after Oct. 13, 1978, and congressional review of provisions of sections 401 through 412 of [Pub. L. 95–454][/us/pl/95/454], see section 415(a)(1), (b), of [Pub. L. 95–454][/us/pl/95/454], set out as a note under [section 3131 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s3131].
