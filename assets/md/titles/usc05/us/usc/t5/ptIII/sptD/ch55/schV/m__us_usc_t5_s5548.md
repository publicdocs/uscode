## § 5548. Regulations

    (a) The Office of Personnel Management may prescribe regulations, subject to the approval of the President, necessary for the administration of this subchapter, except section 5545(d), insofar as this subchapter affects employees in or under an Executive agency.

    (b) The Office shall prescribe regulations necessary for the administration of section 5545(d).

    This section consolidates into [5 U.S.C. 5548(b)][/us/usc/t5/s5548/b] general regulatory authority granted to the Civil Service Commission by section 1101 of the Classification Act of 1949 (as applicable to sec. 804 of that act, added by [Public Law 89–512][/us/pl/89/512]) and the specific requirement in section 804(4) of that act that the Commission prescribe regulations.
