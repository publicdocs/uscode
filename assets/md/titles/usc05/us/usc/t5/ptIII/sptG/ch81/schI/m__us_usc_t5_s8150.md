## § 8150. Effect on other statutes

    (a) This subchapter does not affect the maritime rights and remedies of a master or member of the crew of a vessel.

    (b) [Section 8141 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s8141] and [section 9441 of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s9441] do not confer military or veteran status on any individual.

([Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 555][/us/stat/80/555].)
