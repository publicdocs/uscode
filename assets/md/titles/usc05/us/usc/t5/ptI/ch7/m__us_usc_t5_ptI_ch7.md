 __Short Title__ 

    The provisions of sections 551 to 559 of this title and this chapter were originally enacted by [act June 11, 1946, ch. 423][/us/act/1946-06-11/ch423], [60 Stat. 237][/us/stat/60/237], popularly known as the “Administrative Procedure Act”.
