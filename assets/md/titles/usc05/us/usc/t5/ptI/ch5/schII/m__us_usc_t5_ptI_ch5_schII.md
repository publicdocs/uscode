#### SUBCHAPTER II—ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURE

 __Short Title__ 

    The provisions of this subchapter and chapter 7 of this title were originally enacted by [act June 11, 1946, ch. 324][/us/act/1946-06-11/ch324], [60 Stat. 237][/us/stat/60/237], popularly known as the “Administrative Procedure Act”. That Act was repealed as part of the general revision of this title by [Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554] and its provisions incorporated into this subchapter and chapter 7 hereof.
