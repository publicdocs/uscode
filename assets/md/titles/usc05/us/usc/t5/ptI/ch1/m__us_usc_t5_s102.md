## § 102. Military departments

    The military departments are:

    The Department of the Army.

    The Department of the Navy.

    The Department of the Air Force.

([Pub. L. 89–554][/us/pl/89/554], Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378][/us/stat/80/378].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    The section is supplied to avoid the necessity for defining “military departments” each time it is used in this title. See [section 101(7) of title 10][/us/usc/t10/s101/7].
