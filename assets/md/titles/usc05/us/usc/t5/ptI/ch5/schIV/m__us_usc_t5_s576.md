## § 576. Enforcement of arbitration agreements

    An agreement to arbitrate a matter to which this subchapter applies is enforceable pursuant to [section 4 of title 9][/us/usc/t9/s4], and no action brought to enforce such an agreement shall be dismissed nor shall relief therein be denied on the grounds that it is against the United States or that the United States is an indispensable party.

(Added [Pub. L. 101–552][/us/pl/101/552], § 4(b), Nov. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 2742][/us/stat/104/2742], § 586; renumbered § 576, [Pub. L. 102–354][/us/pl/102/354], § 3(b)(2), Aug. 26, 1992, [106 Stat. 944][/us/stat/106/944].)

 __Codification__ 

    Section 576 of former Title 5, Executive Departments and Government Officers and Employees, was transferred to [section 2260 of Title 7][/us/usc/t7/s2260], Agriculture, and subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 107–171, title X][/us/pl/107/171/tX], § 10418(a)(3), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 507][/us/stat/116/507].

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 576 was renumbered [section 596 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s596].

 __Amendments__ 

    1992—[Pub. L. 102–354][/us/pl/102/354] renumbered [section 586 of this title][/us/usc/t5/s586] as this section.
