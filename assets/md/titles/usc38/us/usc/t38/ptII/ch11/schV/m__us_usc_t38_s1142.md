## § 1142. Rates of peacetime death compensation

    For the purposes of [section 1141 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1141], the monthly rates of death compensation payable shall be those specified in [section 1122 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1122].

([Pub. L. 85–857][/us/pl/85/857], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1124][/us/stat/72/1124], § 342; [Pub. L. 93–295, title II][/us/pl/93/295/tII], § 206(a), May 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 183][/us/stat/88/183]; renumbered § 1142 and amended [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), (c)(1), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406][/us/stat/105/406].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), renumbered [section 342 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s342] as this section.

    [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(c)(1), substituted “1141” for “341” and “1122” for “322”.

    1974—[Pub. L. 93–295][/us/pl/93/295] substituted “those specified in [section 322 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s322]” for “equal to 80 per centum of the rates prescribed by [section 322 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s322], adjusted upward or downward to the nearest dollar, counting fifty cents and over as a whole dollar”.

 __Effective Date of 1974 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 93–295][/us/pl/93/295] effective May 1, 1974, see [section 401 of Pub. L. 93–295][/us/pl/93/295/s401], set out as a note under [section 1114 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1114].
