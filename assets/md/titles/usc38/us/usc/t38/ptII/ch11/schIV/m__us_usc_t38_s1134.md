## § 1134. Rates of peacetime disability compensation

    For the purposes of [section 1131 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1131], the compensation payable for the disability shall be that specified in [section 1114 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1114].

([Pub. L. 85–857][/us/pl/85/857], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1123][/us/stat/72/1123], § 334; [Pub. L. 92–328, title I][/us/pl/92/328/tI], § 108(a), June 30, 1972, [86 Stat. 396][/us/stat/86/396]; renumbered § 1134 and amended [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), (c)(1), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406][/us/stat/105/406].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), renumbered [section 334 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s334] as this section.

    [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(c)(1), substituted “1131” for “331” and “1114” for “314”.

    1972—[Pub. L. 92–328][/us/pl/92/328] substituted provisions that compensation payable for the disability be that specified in [section 314 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s314], for provisions that compensation payable for the disability be equal to 80% of the compensation payable for such disability under [section 314 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s314], adjusted upward or downward to the nearest dollar.

 __Effective Date of 1972 Amendment__ 

[Pub. L. 92–328, title III][/us/pl/92/328/tIII], § 301(b), June 30, 1972, [86 Stat. 398][/us/stat/86/398], provided that: 

> “Section 108 \[repealing [section 336 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s336] and amending this section and section 335 \[now 1135\] of this title\] shall take effect on July 1, 1973.”
