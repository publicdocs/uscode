## § 1751. Screening, counseling, and medical treatment

    The Secretary is authorized to carry out a comprehensive program of providing sickle cell anemia screening, counseling, treatment, and information under the provisions of this chapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 93–82, title I][/us/pl/93/82/tI], § 109(a), Aug. 2, 1973, [87 Stat. 186][/us/stat/87/186], § 651; renumbered § 1751 and amended [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], §§ 4(b)(1), (2)(E), 5(a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 404–406][/us/stat/105/404-406].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83] renumbered [section 651 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s651] as this section and substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”.

 __Effective Date__ 

    Subchapter effective Sept. 1, 1973, see [section 501 of Pub. L. 93–82][/us/pl/93/82/s501], set out as an Effective Date of 1973 Amendment note under [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1701].
