## § 1978. Reinstatement

    Reinstatement of insurance coverage granted under this subchapter but lapsed for nonpayment of premiums shall be under terms and conditions prescribed by the Secretary.

(Added [Pub. L. 93–289][/us/pl/93/289], § 9(a), May 24, 1974, [88 Stat. 172][/us/stat/88/172], § 778; renumbered § 1978 and amended [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], §§ 4(b)(1), (2)(E), 5(a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 404–406][/us/stat/105/404-406].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83] renumbered [section 778 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s778] as this section and substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”.

 __Effective Date__ 

    Section effective first day of third calendar month following May 1974, see [section 12(4) of Pub. L. 93–289][/us/pl/93/289/s12/4], set out as an Effective Date of 1974 Amendment note under [section 1968 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1968].
