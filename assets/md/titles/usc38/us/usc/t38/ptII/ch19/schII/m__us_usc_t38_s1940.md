## § 1940. Definition

    For the purposes of this subchapter, the term “insurance” means United States Government life insurance.

([Pub. L. 85–857][/us/pl/85/857], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1158][/us/stat/72/1158], § 740; renumbered § 1940, [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406][/us/stat/105/406].)
