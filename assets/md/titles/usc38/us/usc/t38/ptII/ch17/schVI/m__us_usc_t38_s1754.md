## § 1754. Reports

    The Secretary shall include in the annual report to the Congress required by [section 529 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s529] a comprehensive report on the administration of this subchapter, including such recommendations for additional legislation as the Secretary deems necessary.

(Added [Pub. L. 93–82, title I][/us/pl/93/82/tI], § 109(a), Aug. 2, 1973, [87 Stat. 187][/us/stat/87/187], § 654; renumbered § 1754 and amended [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], §§ 2(c)(3), 4(b)(1), (2)(E), 5(a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 402][/us/stat/105/402], 404–406.)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), renumbered [section 654 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s654] as this section.

    [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 4(b)(1), (2)(E), substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator” in two places.

    [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 2(c)(3), substituted “section 529” for “section 214”.
