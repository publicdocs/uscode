## § 1816. Regulations

    The Secretary shall prescribe regulations for purposes of the administration of this subchapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 106–419, title IV][/us/pl/106/419/tIV], § 401(a), Nov. 1, 2000, [114 Stat. 1859][/us/stat/114/1859].)
