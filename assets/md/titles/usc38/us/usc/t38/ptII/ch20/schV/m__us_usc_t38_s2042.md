## § 2042. Supported housing for veterans participating in compensated work therapies

    The Secretary may authorize homeless veterans in the compensated work therapy program to be provided housing through the therapeutic residence program under [section 2032 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s2032] or through grant and per diem providers under subchapter II of this chapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 107–95][/us/pl/107/95], § 5(a)(1), Dec. 21, 2001, [115 Stat. 913][/us/stat/115/913].)
