## § 1535. Children of Indian War veterans

    Whenever there is no surviving spouse entitled to pension under [section 1534 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1534], the Secretary shall pay to the children of each Indian War veteran who met the service requirements of [section 1511 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1511] a pension at the monthly rate of $73.13 for one child, plus $8.13 for each additional child, with the total amount equally divided.

([Pub. L. 85–857][/us/pl/85/857], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1137][/us/stat/72/1137], § 535; [Pub. L. 94–169, title I][/us/pl/94/169/tI], § 106(31), Dec. 23, 1975, [89 Stat. 1018][/us/stat/89/1018]; renumbered § 1535 and amended [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], §§ 4(b)(1), (2)(E), 5(a), (c)(1), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 404–406][/us/stat/105/404-406].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), renumbered [section 535 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s535] as this section.

    [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(c)(1), substituted “1534” for “534” and “1511” for “511”.

    [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 4(b)(1), (2)(E), substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”.

    1975—[Pub. L. 94–169][/us/pl/94/169] substituted “surviving spouse” for “widow”.

 __Effective Date of 1975 Amendment__
