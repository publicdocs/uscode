## § 1802. Spina bifida conditions covered

    This subchapter applies with respect to all forms and manifestations of spina bifida except spina bifida occulta.

(Added [Pub. L. 104–204, title IV][/us/pl/104/204/tIV], § 421(b)(1), Sept. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 2923][/us/stat/110/2923]; amended [Pub. L. 106–419, title IV][/us/pl/106/419/tIV], § 401(e)(1), Nov. 1, 2000, [114 Stat. 1860][/us/stat/114/1860]; [Pub. L. 107–14][/us/pl/107/14], § 8(b)(3), June 5, 2001, [115 Stat. 36][/us/stat/115/36].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    A prior section 1802 was renumbered [section 3702 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s3702].

 __Amendments__
