## \[§ 1510. Vacant\]

 __Codification__ 

    Prior to renumbering of sections 501 to 543 of this chapter as sections 1501 to 1543 by [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406][/us/stat/105/406], section 510 of this chapter, [Pub. L. 85–857][/us/pl/85/857], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1135][/us/stat/72/1135], which provided monthly pension for persons who served in military or naval forces of Confederate States of America, was repealed by [Pub. L. 94–169, title I][/us/pl/94/169/tI], § 101(2)(F), Dec. 23, 1975, [89 Stat. 1014][/us/stat/89/1014], effective Jan. 1, 1976.
