## § 309. Chief Financial Officer

    The Secretary shall designate the Assistant Secretary whose functions include budgetary and financial functions as the Chief Financial Officer of the Department. The Chief Financial Officer shall advise the Secretary on financial management of the Department and shall exercise the authority and carry out the functions specified in [section 902 of title 31][/us/usc/t31/s902].

(Added [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 2(a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 382][/us/stat/105/382].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 4(c) of Pub. L. 100–527][/us/pl/100/527/s4/c], known as the Department of Veterans Affairs Act, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 3(3).
