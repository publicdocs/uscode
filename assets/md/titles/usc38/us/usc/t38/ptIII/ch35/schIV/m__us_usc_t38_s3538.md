## \[§ 3538. Vacant\]

 __Codification__ 

    Prior to renumbering of sections 1700 to 1766 of this chapter as sections 3500 to 3566 by [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406][/us/stat/105/406], section 1738 of this chapter, added [Pub. L. 95–202, title II][/us/pl/95/202/tII], § 201(b), Nov. 23, 1977, [91 Stat. 1437][/us/stat/91/1437], which related to accelerated payment of educational assistance allowances, was repealed by [Pub. L. 100–689, title I][/us/pl/100/689/tI], § 124(a), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4174][/us/stat/102/4174].
