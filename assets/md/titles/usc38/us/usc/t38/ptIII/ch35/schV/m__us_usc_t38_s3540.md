## § 3540. Purpose

    The purpose of special restorative training is to overcome, or lessen, the effects of a manifest physical or mental disability which would handicap an eligible person (other than a person made eligible under subparagraph (C) of such section  <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See References in Text note below. </sup></sup>  by reason of a spouse being listed in one of the categories referred to in that subparagraph) in the pursuit of a program of education.
