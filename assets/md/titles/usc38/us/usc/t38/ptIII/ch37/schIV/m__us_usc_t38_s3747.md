## § 3747. Eligible financial institutions

    The Secretary may not guarantee under this subchapter a loan made by an entity not subject to examination and supervision by an agency of the United States or of a State.

(Added [Pub. L. 97–72, title III][/us/pl/97/72/tIII], § 302(a), Nov. 3, 1981, [95 Stat. 1058][/us/stat/95/1058], § 1847; amended [Pub. L. 101–237, title III][/us/pl/101/237/tIII], § 313(b)(1), Dec. 18, 1989, [103 Stat. 2077][/us/stat/103/2077]; renumbered § 3747, [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 5(a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406][/us/stat/105/406].)

 __Amendments__
