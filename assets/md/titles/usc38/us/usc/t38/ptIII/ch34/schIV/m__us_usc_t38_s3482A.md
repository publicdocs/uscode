## \[§ 3482A. Vacant\]

 __Codification__

L. 102–83, § 5(a)][/us/pl/102/83/s5/a], Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406][/us/stat/105/406], section 1682A of this chapter, added [Pub. L. 95–202, title II, § 201(a)][/us/pl/95/202/s201/a], Nov. 23, 1977, [91 Stat. 1436][/us/stat/91/1436]; amended [Pub. L. 100–689, title I, § 124(a)][/us/pl/100/689/s124/a], Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4174][/us/stat/102/4174].
