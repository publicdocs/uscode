## \[§§ 3477, 3478. Vacant\]

 __Codification__ 

    Prior to renumbering of sections 1651 to 1693 of this chapter as sections 3451 to 3493 by [Pub. L. 102–83, § 5(a)][/us/pl/102/83/s5/a], Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406][/us/stat/105/406], sections 1677 and 1678 of this chapter were repealed.

    Section 1677, added [Pub. L. 90–77, title III, § 302(b)][/us/pl/90/77/s302/b], Aug. 31, 1967, [81 Stat. 185][/us/stat/81/185]; [Pub. L. 90–631, § 5][/us/pl/90/631/s5], Oct. 23, 1968, [82 Stat. 1335][/us/stat/82/1335]; [Pub. L. 91–219, title I, § 102][/us/pl/91/219/s102], title II, § 203, Mar. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 76][/us/stat/84/76], 78; [Pub. L. 92–540, title I, § 102(1)][/us/pl/92/540/s102/1], title IV, § 401(3), Oct. 24, 1972, [86 Stat. 1075][/us/stat/86/1075], 1090; [Pub. L. 93–508, title I, § 102(1)][/us/pl/93/508/s102/1], Dec. 3, 1974, [88 Stat. 1579][/us/stat/88/1579]; [Pub. L. 93–602, title II, § 203(a)][/us/pl/93/602/s203/a], Jan. 2, 1975, [88 Stat. 1958][/us/stat/88/1958]; [Pub. L. 95–202, title I, § 102(1)][/us/pl/95/202/s102/1], Nov. 23, 1977, [91 Stat. 1433][/us/stat/91/1433]; [Pub. L. 96–466, title II][/us/pl/96/466], §§ 201(1), 211(1), title VI, § 603(a), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2187][/us/stat/94/2187], 2189, 2209, which set forth provisions respecting eligibility, approval, etc., for flight training, was repealed by [Pub. L. 97–35, title XX][/us/pl/97/35], §§ 2003(b)(3)(A), 2006, Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 782][/us/stat/95/782], 783, effective Oct. 1, 1981, except as otherwise provided for persons receiving educational assistance.

    Section 1678, added [Pub.
