## § 8527. Notice of provisions of subchapter

    The Secretary shall prescribe a form of application for hospital treatment and domiciliary care which shall include notice of the provisions of this subchapter.

([Pub. L. 85–857][/us/pl/85/857], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1262][/us/stat/72/1262], § 5227; renumbered § 8527, [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV][/us/pl/102/40/tIV], § 402(b)(1), May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 238][/us/stat/105/238]; [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 4(b)(1), (2)(E), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 404][/us/stat/105/404], 405.)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–40][/us/pl/102/40] renumbered [section 5227 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s5227] as this section.

    [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83] substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”.
