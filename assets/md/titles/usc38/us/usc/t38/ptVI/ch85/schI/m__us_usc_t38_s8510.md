## § 8510. Finality of decisions

    Decisions by the Secretary under this subchapter shall not be reviewable administratively by any other officer of the United States.

([Pub. L. 85–857][/us/pl/85/857], Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1259][/us/stat/72/1259], § 5210; renumbered § 8510, [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV, § 402(b)(1)][/us/pl/102/40/s402/b/1], May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 238][/us/stat/105/238]; [Pub. L. 102–83, § 4(b)(1)][/us/pl/102/83/s4/b/1], (2)(E), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 404][/us/stat/105/404], 405.)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–40][/us/pl/102/40] renumbered [section 5210 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s5210] as this section.

    [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83] substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”.
