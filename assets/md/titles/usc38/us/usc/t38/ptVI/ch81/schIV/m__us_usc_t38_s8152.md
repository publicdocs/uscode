## § 8152. Definitions

For the purposes of this subchapter—

    (1) The term “health-care resource” includes hospital care and medical services (as those terms are defined in [section 1701 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s1701]), services under sections 1782 and 1783 of this title, any other health-care service, and any health-care support or administrative resource.

    (2) The term “health-care providers” includes health-care plans and insurers and any organizations, institutions, or other entities or individuals who furnish health-care resources.

    (3) The term “hospital”, unless otherwise specified, includes any Federal, State, local, or other public or private hospital.
