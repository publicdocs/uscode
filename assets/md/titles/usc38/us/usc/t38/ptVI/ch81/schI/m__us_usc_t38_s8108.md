## § 8108. Contributions to local authorities

    The Secretary may make contributions to local authorities toward, or for, the construction of traffic controls, road improvements, or other devices adjacent to a medical facility if considered necessary for safe ingress or egress.

(Added [Pub. L. 96–22, title III, § 301(a)][/us/pl/96/22/s301/a], June 13, 1979, [93 Stat. 58][/us/stat/93/58], § 5008; renumbered § 8108, [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV, § 402(b)(1)][/us/pl/102/40/s402/b/1], May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 238][/us/stat/105/238]; amended [Pub. L. 102–83, § 4(b)(1)][/us/pl/102/83/s4/b/1], (2)(E), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 404][/us/stat/105/404], 405.)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–40][/us/pl/102/40] renumbered [section 5008 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s5008] as this section.
