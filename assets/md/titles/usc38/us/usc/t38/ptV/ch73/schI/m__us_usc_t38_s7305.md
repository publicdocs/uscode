## § 7305. Divisions of Veterans Health Administration

    The Veterans Health Administration shall include the following:

    (1) The Office of the Under Secretary for Health.

    (2) A Medical Service.

    (3) A Dental Service.

    (4) A Podiatric Service.

    (5) An Optometric Service.

    (6) A Nursing Service.

    (7) A Readjustment Counseling Service.

    (8) Such other professional and auxiliary services as the Secretary may find to be necessary to carry out the functions of the Administration.

(Added [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV][/us/pl/102/40/tIV], § 401(a)(3), May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 212][/us/stat/105/212]; amended [Pub. L. 102–405, title III][/us/pl/102/405/tIII], § 302(c)(1), Oct. 9, 1992, [106 Stat. 1984][/us/stat/106/1984]; [Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title VII][/us/pl/112/239/dA/tVII], § 728(c), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 1813][/us/stat/126/1813].)

 __Amendments__ 

    2013—Pars. (7), (8). [Pub. L. 112–239][/us/pl/112/239] added par. (7) and redesignated former par. (7) as (8).

    1992—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 102–405][/us/pl/102/405] substituted “Under Secretary for Health” for “Chief Medical Director”.
