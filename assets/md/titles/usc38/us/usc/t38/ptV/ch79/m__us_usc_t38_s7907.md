## § 7907. Termination

    The authority of the Secretary to make a payment under this chapter shall terminate on July 31, 2017.

(Added [Pub. L. 109–461, title IX, § 903(a)(1)][/us/pl/109/461/s903/a/1], Dec. 22, 2006, [120 Stat. 3464][/us/stat/120/3464].)
