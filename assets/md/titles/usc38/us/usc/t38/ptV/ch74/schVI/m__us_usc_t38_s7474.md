## § 7474. Consultation

    The Under Secretary for Health shall carry out this subchapter after consultation with the special medical advisory group established pursuant to [section 7312(a) of this title][/us/usc/t38/s7312/a].

(Added [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV, § 401(b)(5)][/us/pl/102/40/s401/b/5], May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 238][/us/stat/105/238]; amended [Pub. L. 102–405, title III, § 302(c)(1)][/us/pl/102/405/s302/c/1], Oct. 9, 1992, [106 Stat. 1984][/us/stat/106/1984].)

 __Prior Provisions__ 

    Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 4124 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s4124] prior to the repeal of that section as part of the complete revision of chapter 73 of this title by [Pub. L. 102–40][/us/pl/102/40].

 __Amendments__ 

    1992—[Pub. L. 102–405][/us/pl/102/405] substituted “Under Secretary for Health” for “Chief Medical Director”.
