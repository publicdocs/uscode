## § 7633. Regulations

    The Secretary shall prescribe regulations to carry out the Educational Assistance Program.

(Added [Pub. L. 100–322, title II][/us/pl/100/322/tII], § 216(b), May 20, 1988, [102 Stat. 529][/us/stat/102/529], § 4333; renumbered § 7633, [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV][/us/pl/102/40/tIV], § 402(b)(1), May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 238][/us/stat/105/238]; amended [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83], § 4(b)(1), (2)(E), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 404][/us/stat/105/404], 405.)

 __Amendments__ 

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–40][/us/pl/102/40] renumbered [section 4333 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s4333] as this section.

    [Pub. L. 102–83][/us/pl/102/83] substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”.
