## § 7256. Times and places of sessions

    The times and places of sessions of the Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims shall be prescribed by the chief judge.

(Added [Pub. L. 100–687, div. A, title III, § 301(a)][/us/pl/100/687/s301/a], Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4115][/us/stat/102/4115], § 4056; renumbered § 7256, [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV, § 402(b)(1)][/us/pl/102/40/s402/b/1], May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 238][/us/stat/105/238]; amended [Pub. L. 105–368, title V, § 512(a)(1)][/us/pl/105/368/s512/a/1], Nov. 11, 1998, [112 Stat. 3341][/us/stat/112/3341].)

 __Amendments__ 

    1998—[Pub. L. 105–368][/us/pl/105/368] substituted “Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims” for “Court of Veterans Appeals”.

    1991—[Pub. L. 102–40][/us/pl/102/40] renumbered [section 4056 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s4056] as this section.

 __Effective Date of 1998 Amendment__ 

    Amendment by [Pub. L. 105–368][/us/pl/105/368] effective on first day of first month beginning more than 90 days after Nov. 11, 1998, see [section 513 of Pub. L. 105–368][/us/pl/105/368/s513], set out as a note under [section 7251 of this title][/us/usc/t38/s7251].
