## § 193. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section, acts [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 156, § 5][/us/act/1875-03-03/ch156/s5], [18 Stat. 486][/us/stat/18/486]; [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1][/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1], [32 Stat. 712][/us/stat/32/712]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1], [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309]; [July 1, 1916, ch. 209][/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209], [39 Stat. 309][/us/stat/39/309]; [July 1, 1918, ch. 113][/us/act/1918-07-01/ch113], [40 Stat. 644][/us/stat/40/644]; 1939 Reorg.
