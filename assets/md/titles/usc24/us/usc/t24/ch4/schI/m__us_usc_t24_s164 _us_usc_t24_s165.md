###### §§ 164, 165. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–621][/us/pl/98/621], § 10(a), Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3379][/us/stat/98/3379]

    Section 164, R.S. § 4842; [July 1, 1916, ch. 209][/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209], [39 Stat. 309][/us/stat/39/309]; Reorg. Plan No. IV of 1940, § 11(a), eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2422, [54 Stat. 1236][/us/stat/54/1236], along with sections 162 and 163 of this title, provided for the appointment, powers and duties of Board of Visitors of Saint Elizabeths Hospital.

    Section 165, R.S. § 4839; [Feb. 2, 1909, ch. 58][/us/act/1909-02-02/ch58], § 1, [35 Stat. 592][/us/stat/35/592]; [Mar. 4, 1911, ch. 285][/us/act/1911-03-04/ch285], [36 Stat. 1422][/us/stat/36/1422]; [Aug. 17, 1912, ch. 301][/us/act/1912-08-17/ch301], [37 Stat. 312][/us/stat/37/312]; [July 1, 1916, ch. 209][/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209], [39 Stat. 309][/us/stat/39/309]; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. IV, § 11(a), eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2422, [54 Stat. 1236][/us/stat/54/1236]; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 9, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7875, [60 Stat. 1096][/us/stat/60/1096]; 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 1, § 5, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631][/us/stat/67/631]; June 6, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–310, title II][/us/pl/92/310/tII], § 228(b), [86 Stat. 207][/us/stat/86/207]; Dec. 19, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–215][/us/pl/95/215], § 9, [91 Stat. 1508][/us/stat/91/1508]; Oct. 17, 1979, [Pub. L. 96–88, title V][/us/pl/96/88/tV], § 509(b), [93 Stat. 695][/us/stat/93/695], related to superintendent, disbursing agent, and pension money of inmates of Saint Elizabeths Hospital.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1987, see [section 12(b) of Pub. L. 98–621][/us/pl/98/621/s12/b], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 225 of this title][/us/usc/t24/s225].
