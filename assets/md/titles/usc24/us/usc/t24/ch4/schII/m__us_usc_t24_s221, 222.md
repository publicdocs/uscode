## §§ 221, 222. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–621, § 10(a)][/us/pl/98/621/s10/a], Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3379][/us/stat/98/3379]

    Section 221, R.S. § 4856; [June 25, 1936, ch. 804][/us/act/1936-06-25/ch804], [49 Stat. 1921][/us/stat/49/1921]; [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 32(b)][/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s32/b], [62 Stat. 991][/us/stat/62/991]; [May 24, 1949, ch. 139, § 127][/us/act/1949-05-24/ch139/s127], [63 Stat. 107][/us/stat/63/107], related to discharge of insane patients on bond.

    Section 222, R.S. § 4857, provided that no insane person not charged with any breach of the peace should ever be confined in the United States jail in the District of Columbia.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1987, see [section 12(b) of Pub. L. 98–621][/us/pl/98/621/s12/b], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 225 of this title][/us/usc/t24/s225].
