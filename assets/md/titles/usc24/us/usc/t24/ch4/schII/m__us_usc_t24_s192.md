## § 192. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–621][/us/pl/98/621], § 10(k), Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3380][/us/stat/98/3380]

    Section, acts [July 1, 1916, ch. 209][/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209], [39 Stat. 309][/us/stat/39/309]; [Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 417][/us/act/1916-08-29/ch417], [39 Stat. 558][/us/stat/39/558]; [Oct. 6, 1917, ch. 79][/us/act/1917-10-06/ch79], [40 Stat. 373][/us/stat/40/373], related to admission to Saint Elizabeths Hospital of insane prisoners of war and interned persons.
