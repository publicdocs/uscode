## §§ 211 to 211b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–621, § 10(a)][/us/pl/98/621/s10/a], Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3379][/us/stat/98/3379]

    Section 211, R.S. § 4851; [July 1, 1916, ch. 209][/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209], [39 Stat. 309][/us/stat/39/309]; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. IV, § 11(a), eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2422, [54 Stat. 1236][/us/stat/54/1236]; 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 1, § 5, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631][/us/stat/67/631]; Oct. 17, 1979, [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)][/us/pl/96/88/s509/b], [93 Stat. 695][/us/stat/93/695], related to admission to Saint Elizabeths Hospital of insane persons accused of crimes.

    Section 211a, R.S. § 4852; [July 1, 1916, ch. 209][/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209], [39 Stat. 309][/us/stat/39/309], related to admission to Saint Elizabeths Hospital of insane convicts.

    Section 211b, R.S. § 4855; [July 1, 1916, ch. 209][/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209], [39 Stat. 309][/us/stat/39/309], related to insane persons in Saint Elizabeths Hospital who are accused of crimes and their delivery to court upon restoration of sanity.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1987, see [section 12(b) of Pub. L. 98–621][/us/pl/98/621/s12/b], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 225 of this title][/us/usc/t24/s225].
