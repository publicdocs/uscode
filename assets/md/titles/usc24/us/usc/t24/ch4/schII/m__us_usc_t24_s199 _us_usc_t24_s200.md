###### §§ 199, 200. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–621][/us/pl/98/621], § 10(k)(2), Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3380][/us/stat/98/3380]

    Section 199, [act Oct. 6, 1917, ch. 79][/us/act/1917-10-06/ch79], [40 Stat. 373][/us/stat/40/373], related to transfer of insane patients from military hospitals to nearest public hospitals.

    Section 200, acts [Oct. 6, 1917, ch. 79][/us/act/1917-10-06/ch79], [40 Stat. 373][/us/stat/40/373]; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. IV, § 11(a), eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2422, [54 Stat. 1236][/us/stat/54/1236]; 1953, Reorg. Plan No. 1, § 5, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631][/us/stat/67/631]; Oct. 17, 1979, [Pub. L. 96–88, title V][/us/pl/96/88/tV], § 509(b), [93 Stat. 695][/us/stat/93/695], related to transfer of part of appropriations for support of patients at Saint Elizabeths Hospital to public hospitals for patient support.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1987, see [section 12(b) of Pub. L. 98–621][/us/pl/98/621/s12/b], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 225 of this title][/us/usc/t24/s225].
