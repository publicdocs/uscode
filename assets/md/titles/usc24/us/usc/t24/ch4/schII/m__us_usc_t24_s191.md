    Section, R.S. § 4843; [Feb. 9, 1900, ch. 13][/us/act/1900-02-09/ch13], [31 Stat. 7][/us/stat/31/7]; [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 391, § 3][/us/act/1912-08-24/ch391/s3], [37 Stat. 591][/us/stat/37/591]; Oct. 15, 1966, [Pub.
