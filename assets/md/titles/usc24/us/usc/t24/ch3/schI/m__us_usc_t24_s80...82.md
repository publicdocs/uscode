## §§ 80 to 82. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–857][/us/pl/85/857], § 14(3), (18), (19), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1268][/us/stat/72/1268], 1270

    Section 80, [act Mar. 3, 1879, ch. 182][/us/act/1879-03-03/ch182], § 1, [20 Stat. 390][/us/stat/20/390], related to purchase of supplies and expenditures for new buildings.

    Section 81, [act July 1, 1898, ch. 546][/us/act/1898-07-01/ch546], § 1, [30 Stat. 640][/us/stat/30/640], related to purchase, shipment, and distribution of supplies for National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers.

    Section 82, [act June 11, 1896, ch. 420][/us/act/1896-06-11/ch420], § 1, [29 Stat. 445][/us/stat/29/445], authorized sale of medical and hospital supplies by Medical Department of Army to National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1959, see [section 2 of Pub. L. 85–857][/us/pl/85/857/s2], set out as a note preceding Part I of Title 38, Veterans’ Benefits.
