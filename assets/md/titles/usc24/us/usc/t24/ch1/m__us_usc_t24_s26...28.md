## §§ 26 to 28. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section 26, acts [Mar. 3, 1919, ch. 98][/us/act/1919-03-03/ch98], § 1, [40 Stat. 1302][/us/stat/40/1302]; [Aug. 9, 1921, ch. 57][/us/act/1921-08-09/ch57], § 7, [42 Stat. 149][/us/stat/42/149]; [July 3, 1930, ch. 863][/us/act/1930-07-03/ch863], § 1, [46 Stat. 1016][/us/stat/46/1016], provided for additional hospital and sanatorium facilities. See sections 249, 251, and 253 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, and [section 763c of Title 33][/us/usc/t33/s763c], Navigation and Navigable Waters.

    Section 26a, R.S. § 4803; acts [June 26, 1884, ch. 121][/us/act/1884-06-26/ch121], § 15, [23 Stat. 57][/us/stat/23/57]; [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1484][/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1484], § 1, [33 Stat. 1217][/us/stat/33/1217]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288], § 1, [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309]; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205(b), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, 2729, [53 Stat. 1424][/us/stat/53/1424], 1425, provided for use of certain funds for the relief of sick and disabled seamen. See [section 249 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s249], The Public Health and Welfare.

    Section 27, [act Mar. 3, 1919, ch. 98][/us/act/1919-03-03/ch98], § 2, [40 Stat. 1302][/us/stat/40/1302], provided for transfer of hospitals to Treasury Department for the Public Health Service. See [section 248 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s248].

    Section 28, acts [Mar. 3, 1919, ch. 98][/us/act/1919-03-03/ch98], § 3, [40 Stat. 1303][/us/stat/40/1303]; [Mar. 4, 1921, ch. 156][/us/act/1921-03-04/ch156], [41 Stat. 1365][/us/stat/41/1365], provided for transfer of property and equipment by Secretary of War to the Treasury Department for use of the Public Health Service.

 __Renumbering of Repealing Act__ 

    For renumbering of act July 1, 1944, which repealed these sections, see note set out under sections 1, 2 of this title.
