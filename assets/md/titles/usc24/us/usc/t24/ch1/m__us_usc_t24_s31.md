## § 31. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53][/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53], [70A Stat. 641][/us/stat/70A/641]

    Section, [act Jan. 19, 1929, ch. 85][/us/act/1929-01-19/ch85], [45 Stat. 1090][/us/stat/45/1090], related to care of naval patients in other Government hospitals where naval hospital facilities are not available. See [section 6201 of Title 10][/us/usc/t10/s6201], Armed Forces.

§§ 32, 33. __Repealed.__  __[__  __June 7, 1956, ch. 374, § 306(2)__  __][/us/act/1956-06-07/ch374/s306/2]__  __,__  __[__  __70 Stat. 254__  __][/us/stat/70/254]__ 

    Section 32, [act May 10, 1943, ch. 95, § 2][/us/act/1943-05-10/ch95/s2], [57 Stat. 80][/us/stat/57/80], authorized hospitalization of dependents of naval and Marine Corps personnel. See section 1071 et seq. of Title 10, Armed Forces.

    Section 33, [act May 10, 1943, ch. 95, § 3][/us/act/1943-05-10/ch95/s3], [57 Stat. 81][/us/stat/57/81], defined “dependents” and “children”. See section 1071 et seq. of Title 10.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective six months after June 7, 1956, see section 307 of [act June 7, 1956, ch. 374][/us/act/1956-06-07/ch374], [70 Stat. 254][/us/stat/70/254].

 __Repeal of Appropriations__ 

    [Act May 10, 1943, ch. 95, § 1][/us/act/1943-05-10/ch95/s1], [57 Stat. 80][/us/stat/57/80], formerly set out as a note under [section 32 of this title][/us/usc/t24/s32], which appropriated funds for the purpose of expanding facilities for the hospitalization of dependents of the Navy and Marine Corps, was also repealed by act June 7, 1956.
