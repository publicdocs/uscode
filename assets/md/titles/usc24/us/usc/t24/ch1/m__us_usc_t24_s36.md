## § 36. Repealed. [June 7, 1956, ch. 374, § 306(2)][/us/act/1956-06-07/ch374/s306/2], [70 Stat. 254][/us/stat/70/254]

    Section, [act May 10, 1943, ch. 95, § 6][/us/act/1943-05-10/ch95/s6], [57 Stat. 41][/us/stat/57/41], made sections 32 to 36 of this title applicable to dependents of personnel of the Coast Guard.

 __Effective Date of Repeal__ 

    Repeal effective six months after June 7, 1956, see section 307 of [act June 7, 1956, ch. 374][/us/act/1956-06-07/ch374], [70 Stat. 254][/us/stat/70/254].
