## §§ 1, 2. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373][/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373], title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714][/us/stat/58/714]

    Section 1, acts [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 156, § 3][/us/act/1875-03-03/ch156/s3], [18 Stat. 485][/us/stat/18/485]; [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1][/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1], [37 Stat. 309][/us/stat/37/309], defined seamen. See [section 201 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s201], The Public Health and Welfare.

    Section 2, R.S. § 4801, provided for acceptance of gifts in aid of marine hospitals. See [section 219 of Title 42][/us/usc/t42/s219].
