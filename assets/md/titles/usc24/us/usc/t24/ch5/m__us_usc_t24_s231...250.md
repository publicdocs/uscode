## §§ 231 to 250. Omitted

 __Codification__ 

    Sections 231 to 250, relating to Columbia Institution for the Deaf, were transferred to sections 31–1001 to 31–1020 of the District of Columbia Code and subsequently repealed by acts [Aug. 6, 1946, ch. 770][/us/act/1946-08-06/ch770], § 1(61), [60 Stat. 871][/us/stat/60/871]; [June 18, 1954, ch. 324][/us/act/1954-06-18/ch324], § 9, [68 Stat. 267][/us/stat/68/267]; Dec. 24, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–587][/us/pl/91/587], § 5, [84 Stat. 1579][/us/stat/84/1579]. The Columbia Institution for the Deaf was redesignated Gallaudet College by act June 18, 1954, and thereafter redesignated Gallaudet University by [Pub. L. 99–371, title I][/us/pl/99/371/tI], § 101(a), Aug. 4, 1986, [100 Stat. 781][/us/stat/100/781], which is classified to subchapter I (§ 4301 et seq.) of chapter 55 of Title 20, Education.
