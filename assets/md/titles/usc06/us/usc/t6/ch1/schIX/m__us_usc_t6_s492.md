## § 492. Function

    The function of the Council shall be to advise the President on homeland security matters.

([Pub. L. 107–296, title IX, § 902][/us/pl/107/296/s902], Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2258][/us/stat/116/2258].)
