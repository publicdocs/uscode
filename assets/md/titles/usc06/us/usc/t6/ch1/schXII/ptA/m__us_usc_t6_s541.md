## § 541. Definitions

    For purposes of this subchapter:

    (1) The term “agency” includes any entity, organizational unit, program, or function.

    (2) The term “transition period” means the 12-month period beginning on the effective date of this chapter.

([Pub. L. 107–296, title XV, § 1501][/us/pl/107/296/s1501], Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2307][/us/stat/116/2307].)

 __References in Text__ 

    The effective date of this chapter, referred to in par. (2), is 60 days after Nov. 25, 2002, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 107–296][/us/pl/107/296/s4], set out as an Effective Date note under [section 101 of this title][/us/usc/t6/s101].
