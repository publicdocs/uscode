## § 1306. Civil Service Commission: report

    In addition to the usual number of the report of the Civil Service Commission twenty-three thousand copies shall be printed: one thousand for the Senate, two thousand for the House of Representatives, and twenty thousand for distribution by the Civil Service Commission. <sup>\[1\]</sup>  <sup><sup> 1 See Transfer of Functions note below. </sup></sup> 

([Pub. L. 90–620][/us/pl/90/620], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1266][/us/stat/82/1266].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 248 ([Jan. 12, 1895, ch. 23, § 73][/us/act/1895-01-12/ch23/s73], [28 Stat. 614][/us/stat/28/614]).

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    All functions vested by statute in the United States Civil Service Commission were transferred to the Director of the Office of Personnel Management (except as otherwise specified) by Reorg. Plan No. 2 of 1978, § 102, 43 F.R. 36037, [92 Stat. 3783][/us/stat/92/3783], set out under [section 1101 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s1101], Government Organization and Employees, effective Jan. 1, 1979, as provided by section 1–102 of Ex. Ord. No. 12107, Dec. 28, 1978, 44 F.R. 1055, set out under [section 1101 of Title 5][/us/usc/t5/s1101].
