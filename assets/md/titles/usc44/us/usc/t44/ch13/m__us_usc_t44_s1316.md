## § 1316. Fish and Wildlife Service: report of the Director

    In addition to the usual number of the report of the Director of the Fish and Wildlife Service, eight thousand copies shall be printed: two thousand for the Senate, four thousand for the House of Representatives, and two thousand for distribution by the Service.

([Pub. L. 90–620][/us/pl/90/620], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1268][/us/stat/82/1268].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 259 ([Jan. 12, 1895, ch. 23, § 73][/us/act/1895-01-12/ch23/s73], [28 Stat. 614][/us/stat/28/614]; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. III, § 3, eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2108, [54 Stat. 1232][/us/stat/54/1232]).

 __Transfer of Functions__ 

    Fish and Wildlife Service, created by Reorg. Plan No. III of 1940, § 3, eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2107, [54 Stat. 1231][/us/stat/54/1231], succeeded by United States Fish and Wildlife Service established by [act Aug. 8, 1956, ch. 1036, § 3][/us/act/1956-08-08/ch1036/s3], [70 Stat. 1119][/us/stat/70/1119]. See [section 742b of Title 16][/us/usc/t16/s742b], Conservation.
