## § 1704. Superintendent of Documents: pay of employees for night, Sunday, holiday, and overtime work

    Employees in the office of the Superintendent of Documents may be paid for night, Sunday, holiday, and overtime work at rates not in excess of the rates of additional pay for this work allowed other employees of the Government Printing Office under [section 305 of this title][/us/usc/t44/s305].

([Pub. L. 90–620][/us/pl/90/620], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1279][/us/stat/82/1279].)
