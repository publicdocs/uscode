## § 4104. Definition

    As used in this chapter, the term “Federal electronic information” means Federal public information stored electronically.

(Added [Pub. L. 103–40][/us/pl/103/40], § 2(a), June 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 113][/us/stat/107/113].)
