## § 514. Determination of quality of paper

    The Joint Committee on Printing shall determine differences of opinion between the Director of the Government Publishing Office and a contractor for paper respecting the paper’s quality; and the decision of the Committee is final as to the United States.

([Pub. L. 90–620][/us/pl/90/620], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1245][/us/stat/82/1245]; [Pub. L. 113–235, div. H, title I, § 1301(c)(1)][/us/pl/113/235/s1301/c/1], Dec. 16, 2014, [128 Stat. 2537][/us/stat/128/2537].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 10 ([Jan. 12, 1895, ch. 23, § 8][/us/act/1895-01-12/ch23/s8], [28 Stat. 602][/us/stat/28/602]; [Mar. 3, 1917, ch. 163, § 6][/us/act/1917-03-03/ch163/s6], [39 Stat. 1121][/us/stat/39/1121]).

 __Amendments__ 

    2014—[Pub. L. 113–235][/us/pl/113/235] substituted “Director of the Government Publishing Office” for “Public Printer”.
