## § 517. Purchase of paper in open market

    The Joint Committee on Printing may authorize the Director of the Government Publishing Office to purchase paper in open market when they consider the quantity required so small or the want so immediate as not to justify advertisement for proposals.

([Pub. L. 90–620][/us/pl/90/620], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1245][/us/stat/82/1245]; [Pub. L. 113–235, div. H, title I][/us/pl/113/235/dH/tI], § 1301(c)(1), Dec. 16, 2014, [128 Stat. 2537][/us/stat/128/2537].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 13 ([Jan. 12, 1895, ch. 23][/us/act/1895-01-12/ch23], § 11, [28 Stat. 602][/us/stat/28/602]; [Mar. 3, 1917, ch. 163][/us/act/1917-03-03/ch163], § 6, [39 Stat. 1121][/us/stat/39/1121]).

 __Amendments__ 

    2014—[Pub. L. 113–235][/us/pl/113/235] substituted “Director of the Government Publishing Office” for “Public Printer”.
