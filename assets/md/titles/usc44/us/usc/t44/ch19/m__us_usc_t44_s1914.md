## § 1914. Implementation of depository library program by Public Printer

    The Public Printer, with the approval of the Joint Committee on Printing, as provided by [section 103 of this title][/us/usc/t44/s103], may use any measures he considers necessary for the economical and practical implementation of this chapter.

([Pub. L. 90–620][/us/pl/90/620], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1287][/us/stat/82/1287].)
