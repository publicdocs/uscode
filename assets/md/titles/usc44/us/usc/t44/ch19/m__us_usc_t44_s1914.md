## § 1914. Implementation of depository library program by Director of the Government Publishing Office

    The Director of the Government Publishing Office, with the approval of the Joint Committee on Printing, as provided by [section 103 of this title][/us/usc/t44/s103], may use any measures he considers necessary for the economical and practical implementation of this chapter.

([Pub. L. 90–620][/us/pl/90/620], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1287][/us/stat/82/1287]; [Pub. L. 113–235, div. H, title I][/us/pl/113/235/dH/tI], § 1301(c), Dec. 16, 2014, [128 Stat. 2537][/us/stat/128/2537].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 81c ([Pub. L. 87–579][/us/pl/87/579], § 10, Aug. 9, 1962, [76 Stat. 356][/us/stat/76/356]).

 __Amendments__ 

    2014—[Pub. L. 113–235][/us/pl/113/235] substituted “Director of the Government Publishing Office” for “Public Printer” in section catchline and text.
