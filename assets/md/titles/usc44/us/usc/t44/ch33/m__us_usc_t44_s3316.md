## § 3316. Establishment of Commission

    There is established a commission to be known as the National Study Commission on Records and Documents of Federal Officials.

(Added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202][/us/pl/93/526/s202], Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1699][/us/stat/88/1699].)

 __Termination of Commission__ 

    For provision that Commission is to cease to exist sixty days after transmitting its report, see [section 3323 of this title][/us/usc/t44/s3323].
