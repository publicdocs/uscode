## § 3324. Authorization of appropriations

    There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out section 3315 through [section 3324 of this title][/us/usc/t44/s3324].

(Added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202][/us/pl/93/526/s202], Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1701][/us/stat/88/1701].)
