## § 3323. Termination

    The Commission shall cease to exist sixty days after transmitting its report under [section 3322 of this title][/us/usc/t44/s3322].

(Added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202][/us/pl/93/526/s202], Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1701][/us/stat/88/1701].)
