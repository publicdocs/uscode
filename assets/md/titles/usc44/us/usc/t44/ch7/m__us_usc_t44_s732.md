## § 732. Time for distribution of documents by Members of Congress extended

    Reelected Members may distribute public documents to their credit, or the credit of their respective districts in the Interior or other Departments and bureaus, and in the Government Publishing Office, during their successive terms and until their right to frank documents ends.

([Pub. L. 90–620][/us/pl/90/620], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1253][/us/stat/82/1253]; [Pub. L. 113–235, div. H, title I][/us/pl/113/235/dH/tI], § 1301(b), Dec. 16, 2014, [128 Stat. 2537][/us/stat/128/2537].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 159 ([June 4, 1897, ch. 2][/us/act/1897-06-04/ch2], § 1, [30 Stat. 62][/us/stat/30/62]).

 __Change of Name__ 

    “Government Publishing Office” substituted for “Government Printing Office” in text on authority of [section 1301(b) of Pub. L. 113–235][/us/pl/113/235/s1301/b], set out as a note preceding [section 301 of this title][/us/usc/t44/s301].
