## § 3104. Certifications and determinations on transferred records

    An official of the Government who is authorized to certify to facts on the basis of records in such official’s custody, may certify to facts on the basis of records that have been transferred by such official or such official’s predecessors to the Archivist, and may authorize the Archivist to certify to facts and to make administrative determinations on the basis of records transferred to the Archivist, notwithstanding any other law.

([Pub. L. 90–620][/us/pl/90/620], Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1298][/us/stat/82/1298]; [Pub. L. 98–497, title I][/us/pl/98/497/tI], § 107(b)(21), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2290][/us/stat/98/2290]; [Pub. L. 113–187][/us/pl/113/187], § 8(10), Nov. 26, 2014, [128 Stat. 2012][/us/stat/128/2012].)

 __Historical and Revision Notes__ 

    Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 396(d) ([June 30, 1949, ch. 288][/us/act/1949-06-30/ch288], title V, § 506(d), as added [Sept. 5, 1950, ch. 849][/us/act/1950-09-05/ch849], § 6(d), [64 Stat. 583][/us/stat/64/583], and amended Feb. 5, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–265][/us/pl/88/265], [78 Stat. 8][/us/stat/78/8]).

 __Amendments__ 

    2014—[Pub. L. 113–187][/us/pl/113/187] substituted “such official’s custody” for “his custody” and “such official or such official’s predecessors” for “him or his predecessors”.

    1984—[Pub. L. 98–497][/us/pl/98/497] substituted “Archivist” for “Administrator of General Services” and “Archivist” for “Administrator” wherever appearing.

 __Effective Date of 1984 Amendment__
