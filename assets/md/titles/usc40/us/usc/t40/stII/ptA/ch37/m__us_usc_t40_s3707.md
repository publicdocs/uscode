## § 3707. Contractor certification or contract clause in acquisition of commercial items not required

    In a contract to acquire a commercial item (as defined in [section 103 of title 41][/us/usc/t41/s103]), a certification by a contractor or a contract clause may not be required to implement a prohibition or requirement in this chapter.

([Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1174][/us/stat/116/1174]; [Pub. L. 111–350, § 5][/us/pl/111/350/s5](l)(21), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3852][/us/stat/124/3852].)
