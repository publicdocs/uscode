## § 6923. Compensation and expenses

    Members of the National Visitor Facilities Advisory Commission who are not officers or employees of the Federal Government or the government of the District of Columbia are entitled to receive compensation under [section 3109 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s3109] and expenses under [section 5703 of title 5][/us/usc/t5/s5703].

([Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1203][/us/stat/116/1203].)
