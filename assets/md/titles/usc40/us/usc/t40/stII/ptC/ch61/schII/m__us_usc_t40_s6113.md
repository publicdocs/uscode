## § 6113. Duties of the Superintendent of the Supreme Court Building

    Except as provided in [section 6111(a) of this title][/us/usc/t40/s6111/a], all duties and work required for the operation, domestic care, and custody of the Supreme Court Building shall be performed under the direction of the Marshal of the Supreme Court. The Marshal serves as the superintendent of the Building.

([Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1181][/us/stat/116/1181].)
