## § 8162. Ailanthus trees prohibited

    Ailanthus trees shall not be purchased for, or planted in, the public grounds.

([Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1209][/us/stat/116/1209].)
