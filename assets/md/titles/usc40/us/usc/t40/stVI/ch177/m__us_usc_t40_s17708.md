## § 17708. Nonapplication

    This chapter does not modify in any manner the Communications Act of 1934 ([47 U.S.C. 151][/us/usc/t47/s151] et seq.).

([Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1288][/us/stat/116/1288].)
