## § 18101. Definitions

    In this chapter—

    (1) __Federal agency.—__ 

    The term “federal agency” has the same meaning given that term in [section 102 of this title][/us/usc/t40/s102].

    (2) __TTY.—__ 

    The term “TTY” means a text-telephone used in the transmission of coded signals through the nationwide telecommunications system.

([Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1290][/us/stat/116/1290].)
