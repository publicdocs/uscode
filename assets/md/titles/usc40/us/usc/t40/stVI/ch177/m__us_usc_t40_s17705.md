## § 17705. Approval of Federal Communications Commission

    A transfer under this chapter does not require the approval of the Federal Communications Commission except to the extent that the approval of the Commission is necessary under [section 17704(3) of this title][/us/usc/t40/s17704/3].

([Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1288][/us/stat/116/1288].)
