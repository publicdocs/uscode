## § 11317. Significant deviations

    The head of each executive agency shall identify in the strategic information resources management plan required under [section 3506(b)(2) of title 44][/us/usc/t44/s3506/b/2] any major information technology acquisition program, or any phase or increment of that program, that has significantly deviated from the cost, performance, or schedule goals established for the program.

([Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1242][/us/stat/116/1242].)
