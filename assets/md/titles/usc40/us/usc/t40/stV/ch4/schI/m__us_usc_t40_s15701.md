## § 15701. Consent of States

    This subtitle does not require a State to engage in or accept a program under this subtitle without its consent.

(Added [Pub. L. 110–234, title XIV][/us/pl/110/234/tXIV], § 14217(a)(2), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1479][/us/stat/122/1479], and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246], § 4(a), title XIV, § 14217(a)(2), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 2241.)

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246] enacted identical sections. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a].

 __Effective Date__ 

    Chapter effective on the first day of the first fiscal year beginning after June 18, 2008, see [section 14217(d) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s14217/d], set out as a note under [section 15101 of this title][/us/usc/t40/s15101].
