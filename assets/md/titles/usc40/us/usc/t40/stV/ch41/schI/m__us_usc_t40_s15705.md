## § 15705. Biannual meetings of representatives of all Commissions

    (a) __In General.—__ 

    Representatives of each Commission, the Appalachian Regional Commission, and the Denali Commission shall meet biannually to discuss issues confronting regions suffering from chronic and contiguous distress and successful strategies for promoting regional development.

    (b) __Chair of Meetings.—__ 

    The chair of each meeting shall rotate among the Commissions, with the Appalachian Regional Commission to host the first meeting.

(Added [Pub. L. 110–234, title XIV, § 14217(a)(2)][/us/pl/110/234/s14217/a/2], May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1480][/us/stat/122/1480], and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a], title XIV, § 14217(a)(2), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 2242.)

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246] enacted identical sections. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a].
