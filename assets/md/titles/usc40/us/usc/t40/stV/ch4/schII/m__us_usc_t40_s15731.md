## § 15731. Southeast Crescent Regional Commission

    The region of the Southeast Crescent Regional Commission shall consist of all counties of the States of Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, and Florida not already served by the Appalachian Regional Commission or the Delta Regional Authority.

(Added [Pub. L. 110–234, title XIV, § 14217(a)(2)][/us/pl/110/234/s14217/a/2], May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1481][/us/stat/122/1481], and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a], title XIV, § 14217(a)(2), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664][/us/stat/122/1664], 2243.)

 __Codification__ 

    [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] and [Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246] enacted identical sections. [Pub. L. 110–234][/us/pl/110/234] was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246][/us/pl/110/246/s4/a].
