## § 607. Records

    The Administrator of General Services shall maintain an accurate record of the cost of establishing, maintaining, and operating each motor vehicle pool or system established under [section 602 of this title][/us/usc/t40/s602].

([Pub. L. 107–217][/us/pl/107/217], Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1124][/us/stat/116/1124].)
