## § 55115. Supplies on fish processing vessels

    [Section 55102 of this title][/us/usc/t46/s55102] does not apply to supplies aboard a United States documented fish processing vessel that are necessary and used for processing or assembling fishery products aboard such a vessel.

([Pub. L. 109–304][/us/pl/109/304], § 8(c), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1640][/us/stat/120/1640].)
