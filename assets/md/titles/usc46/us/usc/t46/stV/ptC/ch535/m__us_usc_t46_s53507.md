## § 53507. Nontaxation of deposits

    (a)  __Tax Treatment__  __.—__ 

    Subject to subsection (b), under the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 ([26 U.S.C. 1][/us/usc/t26/s1] et seq.)—

        (1) taxable income (determined without regard to this chapter and section 7518 of such Code ([26 U.S.C. 7518][/us/usc/t26/s7518])) for the taxable year shall be reduced by the amount deposited for the taxable year out of amounts referred to in [section 53505(a)(1) of this title][/us/usc/t46/s53505/a/1];

        (2) a gain from a transaction referred to in [section 53505(a)(3) of this title][/us/usc/t46/s53505/a/3] shall not be taken into account if an amount equal to the net proceeds (as defined in joint regulations) from the transaction is deposited in the fund;

        (3) the earnings (including gains and losses) from the investment and reinvestment of amounts held in the fund shall not be taken into account;

        (4) the earnings and profits of a corporation (within the meaning of section 316 of such Code ([26 U.S.C. 316][/us/usc/t26/s316])) shall be determined without regard to this chapter and section 7518 of such Code ([26 U.S.C. 7518][/us/usc/t26/s7518]); and

        (5) in applying the tax imposed by section 531 of such Code ([26 U.S.C. 531][/us/usc/t26/s531]), amounts held in the fund shall not be taken into account.

    (b)  __Condition__  __.—__ 

    This section applies to an amount only if the amount is deposited in the fund under the agreement within the time provided in joint regulations.

([Pub. L. 109–304, § 8(c)][/us/pl/109/304/s8/c], Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1594][/us/stat/120/1594].)
