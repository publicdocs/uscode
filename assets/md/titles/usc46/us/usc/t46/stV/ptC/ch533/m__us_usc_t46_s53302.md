## § 53302. Authority for construction reserve funds

    (a)  __General Authority__  __.—__ 

    An eligible person under [section 53303 of this title][/us/usc/t46/s53303] may establish a construction reserve fund for the construction, reconstruction, reconditioning, or acquisition of a new vessel or for other purposes authorized by this chapter.

    (b)  __Application of Certain Laws and Regulations__  __.—__ 

    The fund shall be established, maintained, expended, and used as provided by this chapter and regulations prescribed jointly by the Secretary of Transportation and the Secretary of the Treasury.

([Pub. L. 109–304, § 8(c)][/us/pl/109/304/s8/c], Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1587][/us/stat/120/1587].)
