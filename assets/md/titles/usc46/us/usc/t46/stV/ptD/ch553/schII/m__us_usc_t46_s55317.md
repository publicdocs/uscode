## § 55317. Termination of subchapter

    This subchapter terminates 90 days after the date on which a notification is made under [section 55316(f) of this title][/us/usc/t46/s55316/f], except for shipments of agricultural commodities and their products subject to contracts made before the end of that 90-day period, unless within that 90-day period the Secretary of Transportation proclaims that funds are available to finance increased freight charges resulting from the requirements of [section 55316(b) of this title][/us/usc/t46/s55316/b]. On the termination of this subchapter under this section—

    (1) this subchapter does not exempt export activities from, or subject export activities to, the cargo preference laws; and

    (2) the 50-percent requirement in [section 55305 of this title][/us/usc/t46/s55305] remains in effect.

([Pub. L. 109–304, § 8(c)][/us/pl/109/304/s8/c], Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1648][/us/stat/120/1648]; [Pub. L. 112–141, div. F, title I, § 100124(c)(3)][/us/pl/112/141/s100124/c/3], July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 915][/us/stat/126/915].)
