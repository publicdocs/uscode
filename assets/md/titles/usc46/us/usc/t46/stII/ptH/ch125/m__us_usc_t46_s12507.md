## § 12507. Penalties

    (a)

     A person shall be fined under title 18, imprisoned for not more than 2 years, or both, if the person with the intent to defraud—

        (1) provides false information to the Secretary of Transportation or a State issuing authority regarding the identification of a vessel under this chapter; or

        (2) tampers with, removes, or falsifies the unique vessel identification number assigned to a vessel under [section 12502 of this title][/us/usc/t46/s12502].

    (b)

     A person is liable to the United States Government for a civil penalty of not more than $10,000 if the person—

        (1) provides false information to the Secretary or a State issuing authority regarding the identification of a vessel under this chapter;

        (2) violates [section 12502 of this title][/us/usc/t46/s12502]; or

        (3) fails to comply with requirements prescribed by the Secretary under [section 12505 of this title][/us/usc/t46/s12505].

    (c) A vessel involved in a violation of this chapter, or regulation under this chapter, and its equipment, may be seized by, and forfeited to, the Government.

    (d) If a person, not an individual, is involved in a violation of this chapter, the president or chief executive of the person also is subject to any penalty provided under this section.

(Added [Pub. L. 100–710, title I, § 101(a)][/us/pl/100/710/s101/a], Nov. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 4737][/us/stat/102/4737].)
