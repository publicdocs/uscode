## § 7116. Examinations for merchant mariner credentials

    (a)  __Requirement for Sample Exams__  __.—__ 

    The Secretary shall develop a sample merchant mariner credential examination and outline of merchant mariner examination topics on an annual basis.

    (b)  __Public Availability__  __.—__ 

    Each sample examination and outline of topics developed under subsection (a) shall be readily available to the public.

    (c)  __Merchant Mariner Credential Defined__  __.—__ 

    In this section, the term “merchant mariner credential” has the meaning that term has in section 7510.

(Added [Pub. L. 114–120, title III][/us/pl/114/120/tIII], § 315(b)(1), Feb. 8, 2016, [130 Stat. 62][/us/stat/130/62].)
