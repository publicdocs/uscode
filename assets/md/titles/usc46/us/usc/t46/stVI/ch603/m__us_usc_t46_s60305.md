## § 60305. Vessels in distress

    A vessel is exempt from tonnage taxes and light money when it enters because it is in distress.

([Pub. L. 109–304, § 9(b)][/us/pl/109/304/s9/b], Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1678][/us/stat/120/1678].)
