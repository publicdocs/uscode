## § 31113. Reports

    The Attorney General shall report to Congress at each session thereof all claims settled under this chapter.

([Pub. L. 109–304, § 6(c)][/us/pl/109/304/s6/c], Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1523][/us/stat/120/1523].)
