## § 80303. Speed of vessel in ice region

    (a)  __Requirement__  __.—__ 

    The master of a vessel of the United States, when ice is reported on or near the vessel’s course, shall proceed at a moderate speed or change the course of the vessel to go well clear of the danger zone.

    (b)  __Civil Penalty__  __.—__ 

    A master violating this section is liable to the United States Government for a civil penalty of not more than $500.

([Pub. L. 109–304, § 11][/us/pl/109/304/s11], Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1693][/us/stat/120/1693].)
