## § 70124. Regulations

    Unless otherwise provided, the Secretary may issue regulations necessary to implement this chapter.

(Added [Pub. L. 111–281, title VIII][/us/pl/111/281/tVIII], § 820(a), Oct. 15, 2010, [124 Stat. 3001][/us/stat/124/3001].)
