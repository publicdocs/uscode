## § 111. Numbered vessel

    In this title, the term “numbered vessel” means a vessel for which a number has been issued under chapter 123 of this title.

([Pub. L. 109–304, § 4][/us/pl/109/304/s4], Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1487][/us/stat/120/1487].)
