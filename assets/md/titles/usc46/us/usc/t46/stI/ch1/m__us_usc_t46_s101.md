## § 101. Agency

    In this title, the term “agency” means a department, agency, or instrumentality of the United States Government.

([Pub. L. 109–304][/us/pl/109/304], § 4, Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1486][/us/stat/120/1486].)
