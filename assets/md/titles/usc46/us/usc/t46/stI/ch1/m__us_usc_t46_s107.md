## § 107. Exclusive economic zone

    In this title, the term “exclusive economic zone” means the zone established by Presidential Proclamation 5030 of March 10, 1983 ([16 U.S.C. 1453][/us/usc/t16/s1453] note).

([Pub. L. 109–304][/us/pl/109/304], § 4, Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1487][/us/stat/120/1487].)
